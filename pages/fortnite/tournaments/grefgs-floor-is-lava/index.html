Overview

“
The rundown of the tournament? It’s all about going up. Once the dangerous lava starts rising, try your best to stay on the high ground! As you’re avoiding the lava, take your best shot at opponents by throwing what you find. When you eliminate your opponents, you’ll Siphon their Health.
”
— In-Game Description

The Grefg's Floor is Lava was a tournament that gave players the chance to earn TheGrefg Outfit, the Balls of Power Back Bling, the Staff of Control Pickaxe, and the Controller Crew Emote for free. There was 2 sessions for 2 playlists - Squads and Solo - taking place on January 14th and 15th 2021 respectively. The rewards for placing in the allocated ranks (different per region) for winning prizes are different per session. Only 12 matches can be played during the tournament. Upon playing 12 in the playlist disables it for you.

The official rules for the Solo and Squads sessions can be read for more information.

Gameplay

Gameplay[]

Similarly to the Floor is Lava Limited Time Mode, lava rose from the ground. The only Weapons that were available were Harpoon Guns, Mythic Goldfishes, and Explosive Weapons. When equipping their Pickaxe, players could walk and run faster to avoid the lava like in the Lachlan's Pickaxe Frenzy Tournament. When a player eliminated another player, they would siphon their Health and Shield. Players would receive Materials over time until they reached the limit of Materials which was 500 per each Material.
