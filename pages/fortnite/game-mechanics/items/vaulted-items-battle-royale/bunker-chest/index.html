Bunker Chests are a type of Container in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced in Chapter 2: Season 6.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Bunker Chests contain the same loot table as a Standard Chest, but contain much higher tier loot.

Bunker Chests contain a Weapon, Ammunition for said weapon, 30 of one type of material, a Utility Item, Miscellaneous Item or Healing Item, and Bars.

Bunker Chests could have been found in a variety of different locations, each with an 80% spawn rate:

  • Sharky Shell.
  • Pleasant Park.
  • Near Pleasant Park.
  • Sweaty Sands.
  • Retail Row.
  • Retail Row Bridge.
  • Near Slurpy Swamp.
  • Near Misty Meadows.
  • Camp Cod.
  • Shipwreck Cove.
  • Craggy Cliffs.
  • Weeping Woods Cabin
  • Stack Shack.
  • Near Boney Burbs.
  • Steamy Stacks.
  • Near Steamy Stacks.
  • Pristine Point.
  • Near Lazy Lake.
  • Near Logjam Woodworks.
  • Between Gas N’ Grub and Green Steel Bridge.

They can be interacted with by the player to open them, granting some XP. They take a standard amount of time to open. Chests can be located by the distinctive ambient noise they make, with a Rare Chest making a different sound to the normal one.

Loot

Loot[]

Bunker Chests will drop five items, that being a Weapon, Materials, Ammo, an extra item and Bars. Bunker Chests will only drop Weapons of Epic or Legendary rarity, and will not weapons of Rarities lower than those.
