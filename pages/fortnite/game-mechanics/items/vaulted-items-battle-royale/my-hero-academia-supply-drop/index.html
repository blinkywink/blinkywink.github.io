My Hero Academia Supply Drops are Legendary Containers in Battle Royale. They contain the Mythic Deku's Smash, several consumables and a good amount of materials. They spawn high up in the sky, in the Eye of the Storm. Unlike the balloons of usual Supply Drops, this one's completely indestructible.

Loot Pool

Loot Pool[]

My Hero Academia Supply Drop grant 1 Mythic Deku's Smash, along with its Ammunition. Additionally, 30 of Wood, Stone, and Metal will drop, alongside some consumables.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Lots of players will try to collect the Supply Drop due to the Mythic Deku's Smash. Be cautious of your surroundings if you decide to try and get one. Protect yourself by building around you if needed. Or watch from a distance of somebody else attempting to loot, and be ready to ambush them if needed.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In the Fortnite X My Hero Academia blog post, they were referred to as "All Might Supply Drops".[1]
  • The idea of an All Might themed Supply Drop bringing the Deku's Smash to players could be an allusion to the passage of the One for All.
