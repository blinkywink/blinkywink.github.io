Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets is an Uncategorized Item in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Chapter 5: Season 4.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets allow the player to shoot small blasts of explosive green energy, which are affected by gravity and explode on impact. Hitting multiple opponents in close range will deal equal damage to both of them.

Alternatively, it has a second attack that launches a Mystical Bomb which deals 90 damage to players, but in exchange has a 11 second cooldown between uses.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets are useful for dealing damage to opponents hiding behind cover.
  • Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets are not recommended for building playlists due to their low structure damage.
  • Even though Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets may not grant fall damage immunity, you can still save yourself by using the Mystical Bomb attack in the air.
  • Try not to use Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets at anything farther than medium range due to this weapon's slow projectile speed and bullet drop.
  • Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets can be used in conjunction with a Shockwave Grenade to deal damage from an unexpected angle.
  • Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets can be especially useful in early-game scenarios due to their unlimited ammo reserve.
  • You cannot fish with Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets with the normal projectiles. But the added Mystical Bomb can.
  • Using the Mystical Bomb can disable Shield Bubble Jrs on impact. This does not work with the Arcane Blast.
