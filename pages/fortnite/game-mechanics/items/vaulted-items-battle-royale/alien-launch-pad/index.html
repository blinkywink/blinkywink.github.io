The Alien Launch Pad is a device in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were added in Chapter 2: Season 7.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Alien Launch Pad launches the player strongly in its direction, regardless of the side it was accessed from. It grants a low-gravity effect.

Locations

Locations[]

All the Alien Launch Pads were environmental, which means they were pre-placed on the map when the game starts and they couldn't be destroyed.

They were available in the following locations:

  • Inside the Mothership.
  • Slurpy Swamp (during the abduction process)
  • Coral Castle (during the abduction process)
  • Corny Complex (during the abduction process)
