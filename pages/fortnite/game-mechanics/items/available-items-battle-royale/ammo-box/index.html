Ammo Boxes are a type of Container in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced in the Pre-Season, and expanded upon in Chapter 2: Season 1, Chapter 2: Season 7 and Chapter 3: Season 3.

Description

Description[]

Ammo Boxes contain mostly Ammunition and come in various types. They can be interacted with by the player to open them, granting some XP. They take a standard amount of time to open. Ammo Boxes do not make an ambient noise.

The currently available types of ammo that an Ammo Box can drop are Light Bullets, Medium Bullets, and Shells.

Types

Types[]

Ammo Box []

Ammo Boxes will drop two stacks of any type of Ammo available.

Rare Ammo Box []

Rare Ammo Boxes have a 20% chance of replacing a Ammo Box. They will drop eight stacks of any type of Ammo available.

Vaulted Types

Vaulted Types[]

Toolbox []

Vaulted in TBA
Toolboxes have a 30% chance of replacing a Ammo Box. They will drop TBA stacks of any type of Ammo available.
As of Chapter 3: Season 2, Toolboxes also always drop one stack of the following items:

  • Vehicle Mod: Cow Catcher
  • Vehicle Mod: Off-Road Tires
  • Repair Torch

Grapple Glove Toolbox []

Vaulted in Chapter 5: Season 1
Grapple Glove Toolboxes will drop one to four stacks of Ammo, along with a guaranteed Grapple Glove .

Trivia[]

  • Ammo Boxes will drop their loot when destroyed. They provide Metal when harvested.
