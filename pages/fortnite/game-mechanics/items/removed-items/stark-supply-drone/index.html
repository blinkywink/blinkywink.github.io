Stark Supply Drones were a type of Object in Fortnite that were introduced in Chapter 2: Season 4. They are a remixed version of Loot Drones.

Overview

Overview[]

Stark Supply Drones were found flying above Quinjet Patrol Sites. They functioned similarly to standard Loot Drones, remaining in motion until shot down by a player or lodged into an object. When destroyed, they dropped a random weapon of Rare rarity or higher, including Mythic Superpowers, from their internal loot pool. If caught in the Storm, Supply Drones would be destroyed without dropping any loot.
