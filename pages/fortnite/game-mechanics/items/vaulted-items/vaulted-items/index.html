Vaulted Items

Overview

This article is about items that can return. For the location, see The Vault. For Vaulting itself, see Vaulting. For concepts that were never implemented, see Unused Features. For concepts that were removed from the game, see Removed Features.

Vaulted Items are items that cannot be found in the current weapon rotation. This list does not contain LTM specific weapons, or Spy Games weapons. On top of this, when a weapon is adjusted to change its rarity for balance reasons (for example, the removal of the Legendary Suppressed Pistol, as its stats were transferred to the Epic Suppressed Pistol), it is not included on this list, as the rarity itself may have been vaulted, but the statistic equivalent remained.

This list also contains Removed Items, even if they were not classed as Vaulted by the game, for consistency's sake. However, this list does not contain Disabled Items, where an available/unvaulted item is temporary disabled because of a glitch or exploit.
