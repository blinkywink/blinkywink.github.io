Playset Grenades

Overview

This page is about an item that is present in the files but cannot be accessed through legitimate means.

Playset Grenades are either Consumables or Utility Items that were used to create buildings on the spot so that the player didn't have to spend time making the building themselves.

They were first featured in Playground in Update v5.41 with the addition of the Spiky Stadium and later with the Playground Spawnables. They were later introduced to creative as another way to spawn Prefabs, Galleries, and Devices and to be used as a type of resource for the islands. They were almost all removed in Update v16.30 due to becoming outdated.
