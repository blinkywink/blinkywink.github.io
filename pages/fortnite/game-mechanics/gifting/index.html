Overview

Gifting is a Mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was introduced in Season 6. It allows players to purchase in-game content with V-Bucks and real-money purchases for the different passes and send it to their friends as gifts.


Functionality

Functionality[]

Requirements[]

  • 2FA must be enabled. More details here.
  • Players must reach level 2 or higher.
  • The recipient must be on the sender's Friends list for at least 2 days.
  • Battle Pass gifting will be turned off towards the end of the season.
  • If the Battle Pass gift token is not sent by the end of the season, it will be converted to 1,000 V-Bucks.
  • After a gift is sent, the recipient must log in to receive it.

Usage[]

Players can select a specific cosmetic item from the Item Shop and choose to send it to another player. Players can personalize the appearance of the Gift Box for the recipient to add a personal touch to the gift.

In addition to cosmetics, it is possible to gift the Battle Pass, OG Pass, Music Pass, and LEGO® Pass, but this will cost the player real money instead of V-Bucks.

Limitations[]

  • All gifts are non-refundable.
  • To ensure a gift is sent to the right friend, search for their Epic Games display name.
  • Gifting is limited to 5 gifts a day for every 24 hours, based on the time of the sent gift.
    • For example, if a gift is sent at 9:00 PM, 1 gift reset occurs, allowing another gift to be sent at 9:00 PM the next day.
  • The following cannot be gifted:
    • Battle Passes using V-Bucks (only via real-money transactions)
    • V-Bucks
    • Battle Pass Levels
    • Real-money Offers (except Battle Pass)
    • Items that are not currently in the Item Shop
    • Some additional offers such as Battle Pass Level bundles and Fortnite Crew
    • Event Pass
    • Dynamically Priced Bundles where the receiving player already owns 1 or more Items.

Season 6[]

Update v6.31[]

  • Introduced Gifting as a limited-time experimental feature.
  • December 4th 2018: Disabled Gifting.

Season 7[]

Update v7.40[]

  • February 18th 2019: Enabled Gifting for a limited time, to celebrate Share the Love.

Season X[]

Update v10.00[]

  • The Battle Pass can now be gifted.
  • August 1st 2019: Disabled the option to gift the Battle Pass on PC.
  • August 5th 2019: Re-enabled the option to gift the Battle Pass on PC.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

Unknown Update[]

  • Removed the option to insert custom text messages when Gifting.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Update v15.30[]

  • February 5th 2021: Disabled the option to self-gift.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Update v16.50[]

  • All Bundles sold for V-Bucks can now be gifted.
    • Dynamically-priced Bundles can also be gifted, meaning only the items not currently owned will be sent, with a discount applied to the price in V-Bucks.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Update v17.00[]

  • Disabled the option to gift the Battle Pass on all platforms.
  • June 9th 2021: Re-enabled the option to gift the Battle Pass on PC.
  • June 10th 2021: Re-enabled the option to gift the Battle Pass on PS4/PS5 and Android.
  • June 11th 2021: Re-enabled the option to gift the Battle Pass on all other remaining platforms.

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

Update v31.40[]

  • Dynamically-priced Bundles can no longer be gifted if one or more items are already owned.
