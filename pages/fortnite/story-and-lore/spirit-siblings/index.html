The spirit siblings[1] are three siblings who are deeply interconnected with the Spirit Realm. They serve among the protagonists of Chapter 6.

The siblings are the descendants of the family who created Masks of Power and supplied masks to the Fox Clan prior to the Shattering War. The family was blamed for the incident, with all masks being destroyed across Oninoshima.

History

History[]

The Shattering War[]

The ancestors of the spirit siblings created Masks of Power and supplied them to the Fox Clan, causing a war to break out which would later be known as the Shattering War. Masks were shattered and magic was banished from the island, with the Fox Clan being blamed.

At some point before the start of Chapter 6: Season 1, Daigo, as the eldest son, inherited the family mask shop, The Thousand Faces, from his late grandfather. Daigo soon grew disappointed with the limitations of ceremonial masks, and began seeking the ability to create true masks of power.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

Demons attack the island from the Spirit Realm after magic was awakened by a meteor shard hitting the island. Kendo and Jade soon start defending the island from the demons, though the latter becomes trapped within the Spirit Realm. With the help of The Looper, Kendo discovers that Daigo had gifted Jade a rudimentary mask in order to protect her from a curse she had received from the Spirit Realm. Daigo theorizes that the mask amplified her curse, allowing her to enter the Spirit Realm, but not enough to let her exit.

Both Daigo and Kendo begin to attempt to save Jade, albeit through different means. Kendo works alongside The Looper, Jones, Hope. Daigo blames himself for Jade's predicament, and attempts to save her himself. Kendo and his allies soon free The Night Rose, a demon warrior, using Splinter Blades forged out of parts of the shard, which she then entrusts to Daigo, believing they'll help him rescue Jade from the Spirit Realm.

The Confrontation Event[]

Daigo begins to utilize the Splinter Blades to open a portal to the Spirit Realm in an attempt to save Jade, using chaos magic powered by nearby conflict. He soon begins to feel the malice of the dark presence in the Spirit Realm, hearing it's whispers. Daigo successfully drags Jade back onto Oninoshima, though Shogun X emerges from the portal to fight Daigo. Daigo defeats the Shogun and magical splashes of gold land around the island, turning mountains into solid gold.

Jade warns that while the demons failed to break her, a darkness lurks in the Spirit Realm that now possesses the shard. She also explains that the darkness intends to claim everybody on the island, particularly Daigo, and advises that the gold around the island will bring greed, conflict, and chaos. After defeating Shogun X and seeing the darkness, Daigo believes it is his duty to protect the island from it, and that he is the only one strong enough to do so.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

Jade's warnings come to fruition as Baron Kane arrives on the island, hoarding gold across it. Daigo grows increasingly obsessed with the darkness in the Spirit Realm and later makes an alliance with the Baron, seeking his wealth in exchange for powerful masks. Upon obtaining a wealth of gold for himself, Daigo repairs an ancient mask of power from the Thousand Faces shop with the traditional kintsugi method, making him powerful enough to enter the Spirit Realm and collect the shard.

Daigo's Fate[]

Daigo continuously attempts to open another portal to the Spirit Realm to face the darkness himself. Upon his success after many attempts, he abandons Kane, believing that he and he alone can enter the Spirit Realm. Daigo encounters a powerful sentient mask, similar to the Puppeteer that bound The Night Rose, and is quickly corrupted by it.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

Daigo, now the darkness' champion[3] returns to Oninoshima along with his lieutenant Kor and Kintsugi Demon Guards and establishes Demon's Domain where his mask shop was formerly located. Kendo meanwhile focuses on Daigo, observing his brother while recalling Daigo's prior betrayal and his own encounter with The Night Rose. Hope figures that in order to free Daigo, they'd have to confront and defeat his captor directly.

In the meantime, Kendo and Jade, alongside their existing allies, create the Supernovas, a team of superheroes on the island, to defend against Daigo. Superman soon begins to aid with the academy while Midas funds their ventures. Jade and Kendo continue to hold a passive role in the academy, while the heroes, along with Hope, Jones, and The Looper, lead the group in their offensive against Daigo.

Daigo's forces later launch a surprise attack on the Academy, with his Spires corrupting the nearby land. While every hero made it out safely, the building was left in ruins, forcing the heroes to scatter across the island. Despite this, Daigo fails to defeat the Supernovas, as they soon rebuild under the leadership of Morgan Myst.

LEGO Fortnite: Expeditions[]

Both Kendo and Jade, alongside the other members of the Supernova Academy, recruit The Looper to assist in their attack on Daigo and his army. While on missions for the Supernovas, the Looper fights Kor, Daigo, spires, demon guards, and other creatures, include beasts of the Storm King, signifying a partnership between Daigo and The Last Reality.

Super Showdown[]

At Demon's Domain, Daigo initiates a ritual using his Spires to summon the dark presence directly. Upon the creature's arrival, it knocks Daigo away, leaving the Supernovas as the only line of defense against the force. Upon Superman's arrival, he defeats the creature, successfully freeing Daigo from it's control. The creatures tentacles remain lifeless where Daigo's domain formerly sat.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

Now with the Swarm attacking the island, the newly-freed Daigo can be found defending O.X.R. Base Alpha from the invasion alongside The Night Rose. Daigo remarks about his siblings, hoping they'll forgive him, explaining how while Jade welcomed him back with open arms, Kendo has been more cautious.

Despite this, Kendo claims he isn't unwelcoming of his brother, instead stating that the Swarm has given no time to look back for redemption, saying that Daigo needs to just pick up a weapon and fight.[4] Jade remarks on her past with her brothers, and says that the ongoing fights have made it feel like they are more aligned, and that if the three of them work together they'll win the battle.

Mizuki, while allied with the O.X.R., blames Daigo's hubris for sending the island into chaos, showing she remains unfriendly to Daigo. Daigo asks his siblings to forgive him for his actions[5] and warns Hope that the weight he felt to protect everyone nearly destroyed him.[6] All 3 siblings choose to ally with the O.X.R., with Daigo telling Onyx Winter he'll do whatever he can to help save the island.
