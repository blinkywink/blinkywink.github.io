Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #5 is a Comic introduced in Chapter 3: Season 4 of Fortnite: Battle Royale, as part of the Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War. It is the final issue in the Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War series.

Summary

Summary[]

The Foundation gives alert to The Paradigm that the Imagined Order were preparing for an evacuation, which suggested that The Collider is near completion. On the Ice Moon, The Paradigm (suited up in new gear from The Scientist) prepares to pilot the Mecha Strike Commander and notifies that she’ll be ready for launch once said. Jones arrives to battle with the IO, stating that he has fighting shape.

Spider-Man arrives in his Zero suit, and Iron Man arrives in his new Hulkbuster-like suit respectively, with The Foundation telling the latter that the suit will remain on Reality Zero permanently. Wolverine, The Imagined, and Shuri arrive and discuss their battle plans with Jones, and resolve their adventures on the Savage Land.

A moment later, Triggerfish delivers a message to The Scientist, which happened to be Imagined Order communications from Geno intended for Doctor Slone involving The Collider, warning that it will reach critical mass than expected. The Mecha lands on the island, and Jones, The Foundation, Spider-Man, and Iron Man act as ground team dealing with the Imagined Order’s shield generators.

Once the shield generators were disabled, The Collider’s shield is downed; Jones notifies The Paradigm about this, and she prepares to strike it with the Mecha’s Light Blade. Before she could hit a blow, an earthquake occurs, and the Mecha falls into the ground below.

The Foundation notices the Zero Point being extracted from the ground, and orders everyone to take on The Collider due to the fact that if the Zero Point reached its crystals, everything will end. Iron Man flies to the top of The Collider, but he is blasted before he could reach it, having his suit damaged. Spider-Man comes to his aid, and Iron Man describes the threat as “Thanos-level power.”

During the same second, Wolverine and The Imagined notice a strange figure on The Collider, with the latter recognizing it to be Geno, immediately calling The Order for help. Meanwhile, Doctor Slone and Gunnar attempt to take down The Seven, but complain about them losing forces. Gunnar asks about Doctor Doom (who was last seen getting weapons from the Ice King’s castle), and sets off to find him. Gunnar finds Doom at The Bridge preparing to leave, and Gunnar is blasted by one of Doom’s stolen weapons. After aligning a portal to his castle, Doom bids farewell to the Imagined Order and admits defeat. Back at The Collider, The Imagined, The Order, and Wolverine fly to the top and prepare to strike Geno. Geno doesn’t kill The Sisters and rather shows mercy, attempting to send them somewhere else. The Imagined takes it a step further and sacrifices herself to send herself and Geno into the Zero Point, which will scatter them across the Omniverse. Wolverine mourns The Imagined’s death, while The Order confronts him. Geno emerges from the Zero Point and orders the Imagined Order to wipe everyone out of existence.

Nearby, Jones and The Foundation arrive at Slone’s tank. Jones distracts Slone with a speech, only for The Paradigm to crush her using the Mecha’s fist, then cupping the two in its hand. The Zero Point focuses on Geno, allowing Jones and The Foundation jump in it in an attempt to find him, while Shuri and the team work on destroying The Collider’s crystal detonators.

In Genō’s reality, The Foundation and Jones witness both Geno and The Imagined getting scattered in the molecular level. Before dying, Geno explains that his attempts weren’t to destroy reality, but to protect and perfect all realities using the Zero Point although people believed that it would put reality in danger, and adds that The Foundation was correct that failure means the end of everything.

Geno admits defeat and tells The Foundation to master the Zero Point any way he could, and asks about Jones (whom he doesn’t recognize) before fading away with The Imagined. Jones and The Foundation return to the island, only to find The Collider destroyed and the Imagined Order defeated, and they fist-bump.

Moments later, The Scientist calibrates the Zero Point to Thor’s Bifrost, allowing all Marvel heroes to use it to return to Reality 616. Spider-Man comforts Jones, who has been thinking about his family, and Spider-Man states that Jones’ family would be proud of what he’s turned out to be. He then goes off to the Bifrost and returns to New York in pursuit of J. Jonah Jameson. Shuri also bids farewell to Jones, and tells him that if he can control the Zero Point now, he might be able to see her again in Wakanda. Wolverine shares his final moments with The Order, and blames her for the death of The Imagined. This angers The Order, and promises Wolverine that she will kill him if he ever returns.

The Foundation explains his plans to Iron Man about sticking to the natural order of things since the Zero Point is now set free, and attempting to find a way to send the Loopers home. Iron Man names his ally with The Foundation “Armor Bros”, which the latter dislikes. Iron Man then returns back to Reality 616.

Meanwhile, the molecular remnants of Geno and The Imagined travel through Reality 8992. They arrive at Reality 35, winding up to a singular spot and reforming Geno.
