Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 6 is a Comic introduced in Chapter 2: Season 7 of Fortnite: Battle Royale, as part of the Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point.

Summary

Summary[]

With Deathstroke gone, Batman and Catwoman are unable to return to their homeworld without a third to increase the omniversal energy. Catwoman was able to retrieve the Portal Device from Deathstroke which was synced up with their homeworld. However, it is slightly damaged and can only allow them to go back into The Loop. Catwoman feels defeated but Batman then remembers encountering Harley Quinn in The Loop and felt like she was familiar, along with Catwoman. They then decide to go back into The Loop and retrieve Harley to go back home. Catwoman hangs onto Batman as he grapples the Battle Bus to scan the area. They then locate Harley fighting off against a group of Loopers and try to convince her to join them. Harley instead fights them and as Batman says if only there was a way to make her understand they want to take her home, Harley surprises them by telling them that she doesn't want to leave. Batman tries reasoning with her but she quickly grabs a Pistol out of Brite Bomber's hand and continues fighting them. Harley explains that she likes being in The Loop since you fight, dress up, die and do it non-stop while looking cool. She then says The Loopers are here people since they are always fighting and flossing while hitting Batman in his groin, which finally urges him to knock out Harley. Just as Batman and Catwoman try to bring back Harley to The Bridge, a Batarang almost hits Batman, revealing that it was thrown by a snapshot of Batman along with another Catwoman. Batman and Catwoman attack their duplicates, realizing that The Loop creates duplicates of beings that escape from the battle. Since the snapshots are just as skilled as them, Batman throws a bomb to slow them down and return to The Bridge. Batman throws a remote detonated Batarang to make sure the portal seals from the bridge so no one else enters. Just before they leave, Catwoman suggests that maybe they should but out of nowhere, Harley wakes up and attacks her before returning to The Loop. With her gone, Batman asks Catwoman why she'd want to stay she states that it's because she's scared since she thinks they are enemies in their home and would rather stay on the island now that they can be happy together and simply doesn't want to lose what they have. However, Batman tells her that people back at home are counting on them and that the dimensional breach is dangerous and could happen again in the future. Catwoman changes her mind and they jump into the Zero Point, with the Remote Batarang sealing off the portal. They have now returned to Gotham City and Catwoman asks why she doesn't remember anything. Suddenly, Batman and Catwoman regain all of their memories. Batman tries to reason with Catwoman but she leaves him, stating that they should have stayed at the island. Batman meets up with Commissioner Jim and lets him know that everyone is safe now that the crack in the sky is gone. Batman doesn't feel the same way and claims that Deathstroke may have had help from someone powerful in this world. Now in Metropolis, Deathstroke tells Lex Luthor that the crack in the sky is gone and that he planted the Anchor Device in the Zero Point chamber with Lex telling him that they have they ability to return to Zero Point and use it's power. The Batman Who Laughs then reveals that they needed Batman on the island to uncover the secrets and sent Catwoman as an "emotional engine" to drive him. Lex says that they succeeded in being able to return to the Zero Point, allowing The Imagined Order to get everything they wanted. Doctor Slone lets him know that he delivered precisely what he promised with Lex asking if it's time for her to hold up her end of the bargain. Slone tells him to look outside and that they've already begun, revealing another rift forming in Metropolis, ending the issue and series.
