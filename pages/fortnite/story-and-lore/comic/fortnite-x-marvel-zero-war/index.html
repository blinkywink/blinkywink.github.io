”

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War is a series of comic books initially released from June[1] to September[2] 2022 in Chapter 3: Season 3 of Fortnite: Battle Royale. There are 5 issues, released throughout Chapter 3: Season 3 with the final issue on September 28, 2022 in Chapter 3: Season 4.

These comics each came with codes redeemable for various Marvel cosmetics in Fortnite.[3] All 5 issues were also released in a single Hardcover Collection on November 22, 2022.[4]

It follows the story of The Seven before the Collision event.
