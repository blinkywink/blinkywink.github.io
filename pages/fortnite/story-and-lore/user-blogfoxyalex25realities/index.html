Fortnite: Battle Royale Reality

Fortnite: Battle Royale Reality[]

There are two parts of this reality known, the loop and what's outside. Within the loop, time resets itself every 22 minutes and residents are forced to battle it out till only one is left. It's possible to escape the loop, but it's not easy. The world outside the loop may be Fortnite: Save the World.
