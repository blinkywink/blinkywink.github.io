InnoLoop is a high-tech company that was introduced in Chapter 7: Season 1. They are responsible for maintaining the Zero Point Shard and own the particle accelerator below the island, which also serves as a teleportation system between 5 corridors.

Overview

Overview[]

InnoLoop is an organization residing within Reality: 266. Currently, The Zero Point Shard is within their possession, but Carina does not trust them, stating that despite the fact they proclaim themselves "the frontier of innovation", the company has always been shady. Curiously, it would appear the company has been aware of The Zero Point, the other realities and The Seven for a long time, with Carina finding several redacted files about each of them. The Dark Voyager took over InnoLoop Labs for himself very quickly.

History

History[]

Chapter: Seven Prologue[]

InnoLoop emerges as the widely known "frontier of innovation" for Reality: 266.[1]

Carina, a mystery hunter and livestreamer, seeks to uncover the secrets behind InnoLoop Labs because of the company's shady history despite its polished public image. She hacked their servers long ago and discovered confidential files related to multiple realities, The Zero Point, and The Seven. However, without additional context, she had no way of understanding what the information truly meant.

Later, the The Dark Voyager and his spaceship arrive on the Golden Coast, frightening everyone on the Island, including InnoLoop's own allies.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

After receiving Jones' distress signal while she was investigating the aftermath of the battle on Reality: 783, The Order arrives in Reality: 266 inside a meteor, just like her former comrades once did. She confirms that the Loop is stable but altered, likely due to the Zero Point shattering into shards and affecting its structure, which has led to instabilities in The Storm. The Order was interested in InnoLoop's technology in order to enact her plan.

While investigating the Dark Voyager, Carina repeatedly encounters Hope and Jones, who are currently associated with InnoLoop, no matter where she searches for clues. Hope and Jones push her away, warning her of the danger involved.[2]

The Attack from Above[]

The Dark Voyager and his Cradle make their way over to InnoLoop Labs and alongside their Henchmen, they attack the laboratory and seize it for themselves.

The Order, after settling in on this new Island, was interested in InnoLoop's technology in order to enact her plan to send a signal to her old friends. Along the way, she’s attacked by a swarm of The Dark Voyager's henchmen, but even so, she feels like there's more going on here than meets the eye. Gathering her technological pieces she needed, she goes to alert the remaining members of The Seven, until she aborts the mission and alerts Jones of someone that she sees, before being attacked.

Dark Innovations[]

After sending his troops to claim InnoLoop Labs, The Dark Voyager pilots his Spaceship on top of the newly-claimed Lethal Labs. Successful, the minions take over all their technology and place their logos across the facilities. The Spaceship begins to scan The Zero Point Shard as it spins in place, hovering in the air. Void Walker Lynx, as Voyager's new Void Visionary, wields a Zero Point Splinter of her own, and by their ideas of innovation, they will transform The Omniverse into what they desire. To do so, they begin scanning for other Zero Point energy signatures that The Order had previously scanned to capture them into their possession.

Meanwhile, Lynx would have her own personal missions and examinations in Reality: Unknown, a "strange Island" of Unknown surroundings. She strikes fear into the Loopers of this land, expressing her dexterity with terrifying Runic power. Even so, she keeps a close eye on the Snow Biome, likely due to The Ice King's and Lt. Winter's arrival.
