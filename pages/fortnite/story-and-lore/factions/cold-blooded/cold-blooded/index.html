The Cold Blooded are a syndicate in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that was first introduced in Chapter 4: Season 1. They are a theft-based criminal organization.

Overview

Overview[]

The Cold Blooded syndicate is a heist group mostly made up mostly of members from the Getaway Gang, and some former GHOST members.

Members under the orders of Cold Blooded Ace took control of the island, and sealed their equipment alongside their fortune gained during robberies in Vaults. After all their Vaults were robbed and ransacked by the Most Wanted, they deserted their bases. Later, other members under the leadership of Wild Card, returned during Chapter 4: Season 2.

History

History[]

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

The presence of the Cold Blooded syndicate had been previously teased by some characters throughout the season, but the faction officially debuted during the Most Wanted event.

They took control of Faulty Splits, Brutal Bastion and Shattered Slabs and established their respective Vaults. However, several robbers attacked them with the intention of heisting their loot - thus the Cold Blooded put a price on their heads. This resulted in the robbers banding together and forming the faction: Most Wanted - which became the Cold Blooded's main opponent.

After all their Vaults were robbed and ransacked during the Most Wanted event, the Cold Blooded deserted their bases.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

Under the orders of Wild Card, they returned, reclaiming the Brutal Bastion and Shattered Slabs vaults and establishing a new one at MEGA City, where Faulty Splits was formerly located.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

They took over Eastern Watch, Meadow Mansion and Secluded Spire and established new vaults.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

The Wild Card Suits and Highcards have vanished, and were replaced by Dealers and Low Cards - Eclipse agents that work for Kado Thorne. No further explanation was given.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Cold Blooded are the only faction to have both Bosses and faction chests with Exotic weapons.
  • A different Boss was planned instead of Chaos Double Agent - their code name was "Double Agent Rogue". However, it was scrapped.
  • Cold Blooded is the first faction where Bosses and Guards have no voice or music.
