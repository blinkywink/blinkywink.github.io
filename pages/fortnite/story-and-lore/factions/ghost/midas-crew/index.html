Midas' crew[1] (also known as Midas' gang[2] and formerly known as The Agency) is an interdimensional criminal, espionage and mercenary organization in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

The organization is large and powerful enough to travel and operate in different realities.

They are a major organization of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main antagonists of Chapter 2's first half, a supporting force during Chapter 2's second half, Chapter 3, Chapter 4 and Chapter 5 as well as main protagonists of Chapter 6: Season 2.

Overview

Overview[]

Founding[]

The organization was founded by Midas and became an international armed criminal group operating as contractors / mercenaries, although this is not their sole activity. SHADOW and, later,[3] GHOST were established as divisions[4][5] of the organization.

Spy War[]

However, a rivalry between the two factions led to war, forcing neutral members to choose sides. SHADOW won the war after The Device, but GHOST wasn't disbanded and continued to resist. The war ended after both factions withdrew for unknown reason.

Reconciliation and enlargement[]

Eventually, after the conflict between GHOST and SHADOW, the two factions reconciled to some extent, unifying the organization once again.[6] Later, in Chapter 6: Season 2, an additional subdivision known as the Outlaw faction formed by talented Outlaws opposing the reign of Fletcher Kane joined the organization.

Inner working[]

The whole crew has a common way of operating; first of all, regardless of the faction, an agent is not allowed to leave the group, as revealed by Skye.[7] However, they are free to switch allegiances between GHOST, SHADOW, or Midas himself.[8] They're also allowed to create their own businesses,[9] or joining other groups when they are not on missions.[10]

However, beyond that the two factions of Midas' Crew operate differently. To begin with, GHOST acts directly under Midas' authority without an intermediate leader.[11] It exclusively handles contracted operations.[12]

On the other hand, SHADOW is led by Meowdas[13], Chaos Director[14], and formerly Chaos Agent[15][16], who was granted authority by the latter.[14] The presence of an intermediate leader grants SHADOW greater autonomy compared to GHOST, allowing the faction to operate on a much larger scale. Their activities include arms dealing[17], scientific experimentation[18][19], and affiliations with major corporations such as Slurp Co.[20] and Kevolution Energy[21]. Additionally, like GHOST, they also undertake contracted operations.

Another key difference is the existence of a secret unit within SHADOW known as The First Shadows. This elite group consists of The Burning Wolf, Chaos Origins (later known as Chaos Agent), and Sierra, who serve as Midas' earliest and most secret operatives.

The neutral faction, is composed of agents who swore allegiance neither to GHOST nor SHADOW, but to Midas himself. This includes some of Midas' close associates such as Brutus and Marigold but also temporary associates under Midas' commands, such as the Outlaw Faction.

Before Chapter 2[]

Midas founded his crew at an unknown time in an unknown reality[57]. Through his covert skills, he set his sights on building an empire of espionage, intrigue, and conquest. For this he recruited three people with great potential to his cause:

  • The first was The Burning Wolf, a former knight believed to be a legend by soldiers, and who attacked Midas' crew[58]; known as Midas' first enforcer.
  • The second was Chaos Agent, then known as Chaos Origins, a mad scientist who once defeated The Burning Wolf,[59] and tried to dominate the world before being stopped by Midas himself; known as Midas' first redeemed.
  • The third was Sierra, a fighter who lost her family and seeks revenge on the perpetrators; known as Midas' first pardoned.

Together, they formed The First Shadows: the very first agents of the newly formed faction named SHADOW. This marked the official beginning for the organization beyond Midas' personal conquest.

In Reality: 783, Midas became a notorious criminal, to the point of being titled the Ultimate Criminal. His reputation made him a legend whose story continued to be told in this Reality.[60]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

After an unknown period of time, a new faction appeared in Midas' crew, named GHOST, which quickly became rivals with SHADOW. The Imagined Order recruits the services of GHOST to study and map Apollo.[61] Unbeknownst to GHOST at the time, SHADOW had already begun engaging in various operations on the island.

  • A.L.T.E.R. team members present on Apollo before E.G.O.'s interference are involved in the trade and marketing of weapons at Dirty Docks[62], and have established links with the companies Slurp Co.[63] and Kevolution Energy.[64]
  • Chaos Agent watches and masterminds everything from his secret lair on the goings-on between A.L.T.E.R./SHADOW and E.G.O./GHOST.
    • He is interested in the REDACTED Bunker[65].
    • Chaos Agent also experiments with Sludges, and builds an army to hunt down the E.G.O. team across Apollo.

The E.G.O. team from GHOST and the A.L.T.E.R. team from SHADOW began entering into conflicts which triggered infighting among Midas' crew. A.L.T.E.R. agents started to oppose the members of E.G.O. and deliberately chased them down, with Rippley being one of their main targets.

Later in the season, Midas selects Eye Land as the new grounds for the main base of GHOST, likely aware of the Zero Point stationed underneath it. Simultaneously, construction on five SHADOW Safe Houses around the island begins.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

Midas founded The Agency, his employment organization for spies from both the factions of GHOST and SHADOW. The organization has its headquarters of the same name built on Eye Land. Conflicts between SHADOW and GHOST escalate, and grow into an all-out Spy War.

  • Several spy bases were in operation across Apollo at this time: The Agency, The Grotto, The Shark, The Rig, and The Yacht. Besides the safehouses, however, there were no major compounds with public allegiance to SHADOW.

With The Agency now built, and tension arisen between the two, the war between GHOST and SHADOW begins in full. While Meowscles controlled The Yacht for GHOST, there were three members of Midas' crew who had not yet chosen their allegiance and remained neutral at the beginning: Brutus, TNTina and Skye.
However, throughout the season, the balance of power between GHOST and SHADOW changed enormously:

  • Brutus was the first operative to choose SHADOW, and it became their Headquarters.
  • TNTina joined SHADOW and was primarily responsible for the ownership and destruction of The Rig, spilling oil into the ocean.
  • Unexpectedly, Meowscles decided to betray GHOST for SHADOW, causing GHOST to send in Contract Giller and Deadpool to reclaim The Yacht for them. Meowscles, now a SHADOW operative, was then sent to the Box Factory.
    • Deadpool was granted authority for GHOST over The Yacht in Meowscles' absence.
  • Skye was the last member of Midas' main crew to choose a side, ultimately deciding to stick with GHOST. The Shark was eventually transformed into a prison HQ for GHOST.


During the war, Midas gradually worked on a Doomsday Device with his daughter Jules made to break free from The Storm. This Device was located in his room, directly underneath The Agency, and possibly right above The Zero Point. Midas' room housed several things that hinted at his involvement behind the scenes:

    • A journal could be found in his room containing a drawing of Oro's scepter, as well as a sketched symbol representing the Storm.
    • A suit similar to that of Tempest's was also spotted in the room, its purpose likely being to defy the Storm, and either giving Midas control over it, or immunity to its effects.
      • This suit, that would later become Cyclo, could have also been found at The Rig.

Near the end of the season, Renegade Shadow storms The Agency for SHADOW's conquest. Countdowns for the activation of Midas' Device began appearing in the Agency and in Midas' own room.

The Device[]

The Device was activated on June 15, 2020. Upon its initiation, Midas' voice and several other Henchmen voices could've been heard as it executed its functions to try and push The Storm back, destroying much of The Agency.

  • When The Looper was temporarily brought out of the loop, files concerning Midas, Jules and another agent Lynx were spotted in Agent Jones' office, meaning that IO had long since been investigating him and his associates.

The Device's plan ultimately failed, and the Storm attacked The Device in retaliation, and turned into a giant Wall of Water. After the event, Midas publicly reappears aligned with SHADOW, and controls what's left of The Agency for the now opposing faction - though he remains the de facto leader of GHOST.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

The giant Wall of Water created by The Storm collapsed, flooding the entire Island. The headquarters of SHADOW are now The Authority, a larger version of The Agency re-constructed by Jules, and implemented a large anti-flood wall that surrounds the compound. Midas was attacked by a Loot Shark.

Due to the flooding of the island, The Grotto & The Rig are no longer fit for use as a bases for SHADOW and are therefore abandoned. All former SHADOW bases and safehouses, save for The Authority, have also become defunct. Brutus defects to GHOST,[66] initiating bad blood between him and Jules.[67] The Box Factory, formerly under Meowscles' jurisdiction, has turned into Catty Corner under control of his son, Kit.

SHADOW continues the war against GHOST, who built The Fortilla as their new headquarters. GHOST's operatives, such as Ocean, refused to give up the fight against SHADOW, and tried to make a comeback after they lost The Agency, but the war is overall less aggressive.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

Both The Authority and The Fortilla have been completely abandoned, and the war between GHOST and SHADOW seems to have ended, with many members now working for themselves,[68] waiting to return online[69] again, pursuing other goals. The last remaining SHADOW Henchman on Apollo spends much of the season parading about with a newfound friend, an equally lone GHOST Henchman- becoming the Henchbros.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

SHADOW, still inactive, is investigated by detective Sleuth, who seeks to hunt down all the agents of both SHADOW and GHOST[70]. Sleuth is ultimately unsuccessful.
Marigold, a member of Midas' crew, arrives on the island, and just like Midas, she is cursed with the Golden Touch. In her quests, she mentions that someone was spending Midas' gold, and recruited the services of The Looper for revenge.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Marigold along with several GHOST and SHADOW henchmen went to collect something.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Marigold is in on a secret plan with Midas and Jules,[71] as she looks for a getaway driver.

For Cosmic Summer, Midas reappears with other agents of his crew, including Jules and Brutus, for an undercover mission.

  • After this, Midas handed over his Crew to Brutus[72], and went offline for then unknown reasons. Marigold has tried to find him since then.


We also learn that SHADOW agents infiltrated and sabotaged The Bridge of the Imagined Order.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

The First Shadows reappeared for unspecified reasons.

During the War against The Seven, several SHADOW members have joined the ranks of the Imagined Order:

The IO is then defeated by The Resistance, leaving SHADOW agents who joined them to find new employers or a new 'job'.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

Many former and current members of SHADOW have joined one of two organizations: Cold Blooded or Most Wanted. The former faction guards vaults all over the island, and the latter is wishing to rob them.

  • The Double Agents joined Cold Blooded while Renegade Shadow, Zadie, Metal Mouth, Stingray, Abstrakt and SHADOW Enforcer(s) joined the Most Wanted.
    • The Double Agents may have been double agents for the Most Wanted, as they have been replaced after the vaults they protected were robbed.

Jules decided to join Oathbound in order to learn about their technology.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

Because of his loss of income, Riptide teamed up with his Alter-ego from long ago: Turk, to capture the Coral Buddies and sell them. This caused them to come into conflict with the people of Atlantis.[73]

Shortly after Turk and Riptide fail, Stingray, under orders from Chaos Agent, contacts Meowscles to tell him they have work to do.[74]

  • Stingray later relocates to Lonely Labyrinth, the place he keeps his loot, as well as Stellan's Refined Kinetic Ore schematic. The schematic was later stolen from him by The Looper.


Chaos Agent enjoys some outdoor activities and exploration.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

Renegade Shadow, who has become an expert as a heist crew leader, is looking for a new crew to rob the Eclipse Hotel.[75] Other SHADOW agents are also involved in the heists, including:

  • Meowscles
  • Abstrakt
  • SHADOW Enforcer(s)
  • and Stingray, who was forced to relocate anyway after Lonely Labyrinth disappeared.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Upon finding clues as to the whereabouts of Midas, Marigold calls former agents and friends of his break him out of The Underworld. Jules, TNTina, Skye, Meowscles and Brutus - the current leader of Midas' crew - reform the old gang with the help of Marigold and her enlisted ally The Looper, search for Midas' underworld cell, only to discover he had already broken free himself. With the forces of The Society dwindling, and the new back together, SHADOW Agents take back control over The Marigold as their ticket out, and the tentative new home base for Midas and his crew.

  • Midas has suspicions that Brutus, has betrayed him, so he sends the Looper to confirm Brutus' loyalty.[72] The Looper finds that Brutus has remained loyal, and so he hands control of the Crew back to Midas.[76]
  • Marigold has also asked Jules to study the Chains of Hades with The Looper to help Midas to seal his Golden Touch. She discovers a mysterious "Essence" in the Underworld's gates, and uses it to start work on new "Gauntlets" which allow Midas to seal and unseal his Golden Touch at will.[77]

Midas sets his sights on escaping the island with The Marigold, wary of Hades' wrath and retaliation. Midas' goal is a fruitful voyage on his yacht to expand his empire somewhere else outside of the island for his mighty ambitions. While Brutus prepares The Marigold to leave with Midas[78], his top agents create distractions across Helios that allow his escape mission to succeed.[79] Midas hides out on Spawn Island, away from Hades, waiting for the plan to succeed.
Following the successful heist, Midas' Crew all collectively set forth on their voyage into the ambitious unknown, and have - for the foreseeable future - left the world of Fortnite: Battle Royale. However, some operatives of SHADOW remain on the island, with assignable SHADOW Briefings continuing to be offered, even around newer locations as the chapter progresses.

Between Chapter 5: Season 2 - Chapter 5: Season 5[]

During the journey, the crew split into several groups, assigned to different missions or occupations. Midas's group, consisting of himself, Brutus, Meowscles, Kit, Marigold, and Jules, departed for Reality: 783, with Jules separating from the group to pursue his own interests. TNTina and Skye were tasked with going to Reality: UN-0-C2R alongside Meowdas, Chaos Director, 1-Ball, and other agents.

Chapter 5: Season 5[]

Arriving in an alternate version of Apollo, Midas' Crew settled on the island and have taken back many of the bases originally under their control. However things were different, as the Island was taken over by legendary musical artists Snoop Dogg, Eminem, Ice Spice and Juice WRLD. Snoop Dogg took over The Agency compound for his company and recruited Eminem, Ice Spice, Juice WRLD and contracted both GHOST and SHADOW.

  • This situation led to major changes in the bases owned by both faction.
    • SHADOW regained control over The Rig operated again by TNTina as well as SHADOW Safe Houses. They also took over The Yacht, formerly owned by GHOST. Lastly in the season, Kit regained control over the Box Factory, protecting it from intruders.
    • GHOST regained control over The Agency (now known as The Doggpound) as they were contracted by Snoop Dogg to protect it as well as E.G.O. Outposts. They also took over The Grotto, formerly owned by SHADOW, as Eminem also contracted them to protect it. Later on, Ice Spice would transform The Shark into Ice Isle, a neon-lit shopping mall. All three of these locations serve as Headquarters for GHOST operations.


In the meantime two high-ranking operatives of SHADOW appear:

  • The first is Chaos Director, the boss of Chaos Agent and Director of SHADOW, has taken back control of SHADOW after realizing it was a mistake to give Chaos Agent the reigns.[80]
  • The other is Meowdas, Boss of SHADOW, now operating from The Yacht with SHADOW Henchmen instead of GHOST Henchmen.


Other old and new agents also appear, such as 1-Ball, a GHOST Agent who meets up with his legend and fellow GHOST Agent, 8-Ball, to test who truly is the best.[81] Skye also returned although this time, she has no base nor known faction, however she did tease the arrival of Juice WRLD.[82] Chaos Director, Meowdas, 1-Ball, Skye and TNTina were all contracted by The Doggpound and sit on the board alongside Snoop Dogg, Rap Boy Reloaded, Ice Spice and Juice WRLD.[83]

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

Meowdas's group arrived in Reality: 783, at Oninoshima. Once there, Meowdas meets up with a new friend named Nyanja in Canyon Crossing.[84] Desks belonging to GHOST Agents, featuring Agency paintings and GHOST mugs can be found around Oninoshima.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

Midas arrived in Reality: 783, again and began his criminal activities, notably against one of his old acquaintances, the Baron Fletcher Kane, who seized all the Island's gold and became the Island's mob don. However, he was noticed to have a scheme in mind as suspected by Vengeance Jones and Skillet. Cassidy Quinn, one of The Outlaws as well as one of the biggest fans of Midas' criminal story finds out her idol is real. Midas, teaming up with The Looper, united The Outlaws into a combined unit for the sole purpose of taking down Alpha. During this, he became known as, including adopting the name of, "The Golden Gunslinger". He utilized Dill Bits to crash the market to put Kane in a panic, all while trying to get to the Zero Point Shard with help from The Looper. Cautious of the Mask Maker that's been helping Fletcher Kane, Midas realizes he made need to team up with Hope and her hero friends.

Meanwhile, a few SHADOW agents start to make some moves. Kit, likely inspired by Katt, upgrades his mecha suit. However, he embraces his darker side, whilst retaining his allegiance to SHADOW, unlike his pal, Cate. Additionally, Kit creates his own personal logo to match his new look.[85] Then, Zadie founds The Elites, a group of the best warriors she can find. They aim to use the power of siphon to conquer their opponents.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

Midas funds the Supernova Academy with his SHADOW team after the Death Star Sabotage to grow a team of superheroes. Together with Hope, Midas creates a Vault capable of storing Zero Point Shards for safekeeping whenever they're capable of doing so. Meanwhile, he keeps in touch with the Outlaws, still working alongside Joss, Cassidy Quinn and Skillet.

      • A covert operative engages in clandestine operations for a government or organization, with both the operation and the sponsor's identity concealed. They conduct secret missions such as sabotage, reconnaissance, and direct action, often in hostile or sensitive environments. Their goal is to achieve strategic objectives while maintaining plausible deniability. They are highly trained and part of special operations or intelligence units.
      • A secret agent gathers information, engages in espionage, and conducts other covert activities on behalf of a government or organization. Their work includes intelligence gathering and potentially recruiting other agents, often under diplomatic cover or without any official cover. Trained in espionage and affiliated with intelligence agencies, their objective is to obtain and transmit information without detection.
      • A spy collects and reports information on the activities, movements, plans, or strategies of an enemy or competitor. They infiltrate organizations or governments to gather intelligence through surveillance and other methods. Spies can be professionals or recruited individuals, possibly without formal training or official backing, and they operate at great personal risk to deliver secret information.
