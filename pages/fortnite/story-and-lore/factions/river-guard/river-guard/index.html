Overview

“
The River Guard welcomes you. If you do not try our patience.
”
— Mizuki

The River Guard is a clan that was first was introduced in Chapter 4: Season 1 through the Cipher Quests. They are one of the syndicates from the reality of MEGA City.

They are a group specialized in water-based abilities and katana swordsmanship. They can be easily recognized through their accessories related to oceanic tides. The clan's ideal is "Flow like water, cut like ice."

Overview

Overview[]

The River Guard is an ancient clan most known for their water-centric beliefs. They were formerly the long-time historic rival of the Fox Clan, until the events of Chapter 4: Season 2, where the clan had to unite with the other syndicates in order to protect MEGA City from outside threats.

History

History[]

Unknown Period of Time[]

The River Guard was founded at an unknown time period in Reality: 783, with their domains residing in Oninoshima. After the Fox Clan's mask artisans—the ancestors of Daigo, Kendo and Jade—created dangerous Masks of Power with dark magic that threatened the entire island, the River Guard united Oninoshima to wage war against the Demon Warriors and the dark masks they used.[4] At the end of the conflict, Demon Warriors and magic were banished, and the incident became known as the Shattering War. From that point on, the River Guard became sworn enemies of the Fox Clan, blaming them for their suppliers' mistake.[5]

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

MEGA City and its surrounding areas are rifted onto the island of Asteria, bringing the River Guard and various other syndicates with a presence throughout the city alongside it.

Among those brought are Mizuki, the current River Guardian Empress, and her estranged brother, Stray. Through their interactions, it's revealed that for unspecified reasons, prior to Chapter 4: Season 2, Stray left both his home and the River Guard to join the Fox Clan after being recruited by Drift,[6] much to Mizuki's disliking. His exit only fueled the prolonged fued between the River Guard and their rivals.[7][8]

Despite the threats posed by Triarch Nox and some of The Unseen to MEGA City's safety,[9] The Looper, working for the Peace Syndicate, later manages to unite all the syndicates in MEGA City, including the River Guard. Through negotiations with the Fox Clan organized by The Looper, their historical conflict was resolved, and they were brought together as allies.[10]

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

Mizuki alongside the River Guard return to Reality: 783, establishing Lost Lake and settling there.[11] Though they have sustained their mended relationship with the Fox Clan (as Vi appears to greet Mizuki with no hostility upon her entering Rolling Blossoms Farm, and Mizuki reassures Vi that the guard seeks no trouble, as well as seemingly no longer carrying resentments towards her brother), the re-emergence of magic and elemental masks on the island prompts reflection on their history with the Fox Clan—and the reminder of why the River Guard now tend to keep to themselves.

Although the Fox Clan has their own doubts about their alliance, both groups appear to be keeping the peace and seem open to continuing to do so—should neither overstep their boundaries.[12]

Notably, Mizuki expresses a disliking towards both Daigo[13] and his ancestors, likely in relation to the events of the Shattering War.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

Lost Lake is taken over by Fletcher Kane, and turned into his Lonewolf Lair. Nevertheless, the stone face statue once residing with the lake's waters can still be found exposed in the lair's basement floor.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

With Mask Maker Daigo freed from his master, The River Guard steps up to quell the chaos left behind from Daigo's arrogance. Successfully holding little to no grudges residing from their ancient rivalry, the clans have nearly made up. Empress Mizuki sends the Fox Clan, her brother included, out on their way out of Oninoshima to a much safer space out in the metaphorical vast sea of realities, far away from a land fueled by ancient hunger. In the meantime, the River Guardians team up with The O.X.R. and The Supernova Academy for the safety of the Island.
