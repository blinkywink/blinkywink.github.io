Overview

The Gnome Empire is a recurring neutral faction hidden in Fortnite: Battle Royale. Its spies can usually be found in every nook and cranny of the Island. Oftentimes, interacting with them will result in the completion of a secret puzzle set up by the trickster Grimbles. They are usually fighting against the Teddy Bears unless a greater danger or threat forces them to form a temporary truce.

Update v11.00[]

  • Gnomes can be found as props in various locations on the island.
  • A single gnome hides as part of Overtime Challenges.

Update v12.20[]

A single gnome has been imprisoned by The Ted Offensive, and espionage-based subsidiary of the Teddy Bears, whom they have now set conquest against.

Update v12.30[]

Several gnomes scout Weeping Woods with telescopes.

Update v12.50[]

The gnomes launch an assault against the Teddy Bears, who are doing the same thing. The Looper can interact and cease the battle should they choose to do so.

Update v12.60[]

Close to GHOST House, representatives from both the Gnomes and the Teddy Bears sign and celebrate a truce between them.

Update v13.00[]

They formed a truce with the Teddy Bears. Despite not being a faction at war anymore, the Gnomes remain a people with notable camaraderie among the other Fortnite mainstays.

  • Previous gnomes on the island are no longer present.

All of the events this season could have been interacted with as part of Secret Challenges that would provide players with Season XP if they chose to do certain tasks.

Update v14.10[]

A small group of Gnomes meet up southwest of Apres Ski for "The GNOM Talk", a small conference with one unique gnome showcasing an alternative Slurp product: "Super Slurp Juice". The meeting was promoted by multiple mini posters and tiny billboards around the island. This "Super Slurp" seemed to be popular with the gnomes, but the event ended abruptly when a chemical reaction caused the unique gnome to blow up with its product.

Update v14.20[]

Shortly after the incident, "Super Slurp Juice" was looked downed upon. The posters and billboards previously displaying the conference were now sprayed over with graffiti. In Hydro 16, group of gnomes are found "discussing" the event behind closed doors, while the unique gnome (now a cyborg) hides and schemes in the basement of GHOST House. The lair is filled with multiple "Super Slurp Juice"-related objects, as well as mini three robot army, and a map with five points marked. The Cyborg Gnome's goal, it seems, was to use Super Slurp Juice as weaponized materials to blow up mapped locations around Apollo.
The Looper can intervene at said locations to disarm the aforementioned traps.

Update v14.30[]

The Cyborg Gnome has left many of their inventions hidden in Camp Cod. This is also likely around when said gnome began experimenting with the Slurp vats at Slurpy Swamp.

Update v14.40[]

The Looper may interfere with these plans as well, and "purify" the vats that have turned green from the Cyborg Gnome's experimentation. Confrontation afterwards led to them trying to blast off in a pseudo-escape attempt, which ultimately resulted in them perishing.

Update v14.50[]

A memorial is enacted to the Cyborg Gnome, and the Looper, as well as the other gnomes, can pay respects to the statue.


The memorial is later removed due to unknown means.
