Alexander Joseph Luthor, better known as Lex Luthor is a character from Reality: NF-1935, who is a rich bussinessman and the arch nemesis of Superman.

He is a supporting antagonist in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as one of the overarching antagonists of The Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point comics, and a major antagonist of The Batman/Fortnite: Foundation comic.

Personality

Personality[]

Luthor is commonly seen as a power-hungry and sadistic villain of pure evil. His sole ambition in life is to destroy Superman so that he can become humanity's rightful champion. Despite his crimes, Luthor has established an image as a wealthy philanthropist and manages to maintain a notable political popularity.[1]

Timeline

Timeline[]

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point[]

He works with The Imagined Order to help attach an Anchor Device to The Zero Point to use its power remotely, in return Doctor Slone opens a Sideways Rift on Metropolis.

Batman/Fortnite: Foundation[]

He leads the villains to the Sideways Rift, but gets attacked by The Foundation and his armor gets cracked. He flies away, and after Batman closes the Sideways Rift, he moves to "Plan B".
