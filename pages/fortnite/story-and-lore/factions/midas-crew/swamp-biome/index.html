Overview

The Swamp Biome was a Swamp in the southeast corner of Athena during Chapter 1 and in the southwest corner of Apollo during Chapter 2.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • Moisty Mire was added.

Season 2[]

  • A Swamp formed around Moisty Mire.

Season 5[]

  • The Swamp was replaced by a Mesa Biome.

Season X[]

  • Moisty Palms was added.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Moisty Palms was removed however,A new swamp formed in the southwest.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • The Swamp was temporarily flooded.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Slurpy Swamp was abducted and turned into Sludgy Swamp.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • The Swamp was removed.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • In the Fracture Live Event, the remaining Swamp biome was destroyed by The Herald offscreen.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

  • Reintroduced the Swamp biome as a result of Slurp Rush.
