Overview

“
An assassin-class autono-bot from Kata Tech's secretive R&D lab.
”
— Felina's In-game description

Kata Tech is an organization in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was first introduced during Season 9. They are an organization specialized in the construction of combat robots and come from MEGA City's Reality.
