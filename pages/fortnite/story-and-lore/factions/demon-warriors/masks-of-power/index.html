Overview

“
These magical masks stink of old, dangerous magic. The very magic we waged a war to destroy.
”
— Mizuki

Masks of Power are magical masks crafted by the ancestors of Daigo, Kendo, and Jade, who served as the Fox Clan's former mask makers centuries ago. These masks were created using the Fox Clan's secret magical mask-making technique. They were introduced during Chapter 6: Season 1.

Overview

Overview[]

Masks of Power were dangerous tools crafted by a family of mask artisans after they became overambitious. The creation of these hazardous masks of ruinous properties resulted in a historical event known as the "Shattering War". After the war, magic was banished, and most masks were shattered. The Fox Clan was blamed for the incident, as the masks were created by their suppliers.

Ages later, when this story was thought to be no more than myths and legends in most eyes of Oninoshima's inhabitants, Demons returned alongside the Masks of Power.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Although similar to the Masks of Power, the Fox Clan's masks are not classified as such.
    • Their notable difference with the Masks of Power is that they don't use dark magic.
    • The Fox Clan's masks served, however, as templates to create the Masks of Power.
