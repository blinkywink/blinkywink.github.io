Kor's Arena is an Unnamed Location and a mid-match objective in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that was added in Chapter 6: Season 3 to the island Oninoshima. When the 1st Storm circle closes in, Kor's Arena would appear at a random preset location through a Rift.

Trivia[]

  • Kor's Arena is the same as Shogun's Arena from chapter 6: season 1-2 or Loot Island from chapter 4 and chapter 5.
  • Players will not take fall damage by falling from Kor's Arena as the location itself is surrounded by a wind volume, similar to the Floating Island.
