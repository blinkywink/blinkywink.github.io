Boss[]

Behavior

Behavior[]

At any of the five infested locations, three Queen Icons will appear in the locations, but the Queens themselves are not present initially and will only appear once players clear the area of the infestation. This can be achieved by:

  • Eliminating Swarmers and Bombers.
  • Destroying their nests.
  • Opening Hive Stashes.

While the meter is being depleted, roars of the Queen will be heard from deep below the surface, and once the requirement is met the Queen will emerge from the ground.

Weakspots[]

  • Each Queen has a glowing weak point right under their jaw. Hitting it will deal critical damage to the Boss.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Queens' codename is TidalCrane_Swarm_Boss.
  • While using the same model, each Queen has slightly different coloration, which is reflected by their Medallions.
  • Initially, the Update v37.00 patch notes state that the Ranger's Ruin Queen drops a Mythic Tactical Shotgun instead of the Sweeper Shotgun. This was corrected shortly after.
    • Additionally, the Demon's Debris and Ranger's Ruin Queens have swapped Medallions in the original version of the patch. This was also fixed.
  • The Queens reuse the roars of the Storm King when they spawn.
  • The Queens are the first Creature Bosses to drop Medallions.
    • They are also the first Medallion Bosses to get their Medallions vaulted within the season they were introduced.

Rita's Monster, also known as Giant Queen is a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 6: Season 4. It can be found battling the Megazord.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Upon the Megazord being activated, Rita Repulsa will summon the Giant Queen in an effort to destroy the Power Rangers. With its gigantic size, Rita's Monster can only be damaged by the Megazord pilots.

Rita's Monster will prioritize attacking the Megazord whilst ignoring other players that are close but still knocking them away when collided with. If the Megazord pilots fully depletes its Healthpool, Rita's Monster will become stunned and vulnerable to a Finishing Strike. If the Finishing Strike has not been dealt, Rita's Monster will recover 3,000 Health after 20 seconds pass.

When Rita's Monster lands a hit on the Megazord, it will corrode its exterior in a purple substance. Upon the Megazord being defeated before the players piloting could defeat it, the Giant Queen will burrow back into the ground and disappear for the rest of the match.

Attacks

Attacks[]

  • Burrow
    • During the fight, Rita's Monster will periodically descend into the ground, visibly tunneling underground before resurfacing in a different spot.
  • Headbutt
    • Rams its body forward if the Megazord gets too close. Dealing 120 damage to players, and 7,000 damage to the Megazord. This attack will briefly stagger the Megazord if it hits.
  • Claw Swipe
    • Swipes its long claws forward if the Megazord gets too close. Dealing 80 damage to players, and 4,000 damage to the Megazord. This attack will briefly stagger the Megazord if it hits.
  • Acid Bomb Volley
    • Rita's Monster spits out multiple balls of acid at the Megazord. Dealing 75 damage to players, and 1,000 damage to the Megazord per acid bomb.
