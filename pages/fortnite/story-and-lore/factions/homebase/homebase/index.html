Homebase is an organization in Fortnite: Save the World that lead the fight against The Storm in their reality.

Overview

Overview[]

Homebase is located on a hill in Stonewood and use any remaining Vindertech Schematics to push back the storm. Their fight against the storm starts to progress when the Commander arrives at Homebase after outrunning a group of Husks.

Their primary source of power is BluGlo and is what is used to power their storm fight product. They power Storm Shields so that they can protect survivors and continue their fight.

History

History[]

2017[]

  • Stonewood
    • The Storm arrives. The Original Commander has been lost but a survivor arrives and takes charge as a new commander, helping Ramirez against a horde and creating the first successful rocket launch.
    • Homebase set up the Storm Shield and find the schematics of the ATLAS.
    • They meet Lars and Dennis.
    • Major Oswald , Clip, and Director Riggs are taken into Homebase and are given positions to help fight the storm.
    • Dennis goes missing and ends up with the Storm King, but Lars saves him.
    • Pop goes missing in Plankerton and Homebase decides to head into Plankerton.
  • Plankerton
    • Homebase expands to Plankerton
    • Pop is found, but has a mean attitude, later fixed by Lars.
    • SEE-BOT is created to help during Repair The Shelter missions.
    • Dark Rifts are discovered and researched. Their purpose is then revealed.
    • Ray and Homebase create a Bomb capable of detonating the Dark Rifts, in the Deliver the Bomb mission.
    • After hearing about the citizens of Plank Harbor, Homebase sets up a Category 3 ATLAS Mission at Plank Harbor to completely wipe the storm from that area.
    • A Vindertech Lab is found and tons of data on the storm is able to be retrieved, along with a message from Doctor Vinderman to Ray after he died trying to stabilise a rift.
    • Lars’s past is revealed. The rest of the Steel Wool band wanted to be with Doctor Vinderman to work on saving the world but Lars didn’t, which caused the band to come to an end.
    • Homebase goes to Canny Valley.

2018[]

  • Canny Valley
    • Homebase expands to Canny Valley.
    • They send SEE-BOT into the desert as he is annoying Homebase.
    • Lars find Steel Wool Syd.
    • Incriminating evidence is discovered about a theory that the storm may have been arrived on Earth much earlier and defeated according to ancient drawings.
    • They defeat Blackboot Ayers.
    • Steel Wool Anthony is found.
    • Desiree is found and has been working with another group of people including Steel Wool Carlos.
    • A plan is now in the works to destroy the Storm King using a remote bomb.
    • Steel Wool is reunited.
    • Dennis and Anthony find SEE-BOT.
    • the Vinderman AI is unpacked from SEE-BOT at the Vinderman Mansion.
    • The Storm King uses mind-control on the surviors at Homebase and Ray helps clean the mess up.
    • Power Chord joins to help defeat the Storm King.
    • Homebase delivers the final blow against the Storm King as the commander defeats him and Desiree sacrifices herself to blow up the portal.
  • Twine Peaks
    • Homebase expands to Twine Peaks.
    • They finally defeat the Mythic Storm King.
