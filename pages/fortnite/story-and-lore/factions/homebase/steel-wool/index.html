Steel Wool is a rock band in Fortnite: Save the World. The band members are also characters that players can unlock as Heroes in the game.

Lore

Lore[]

Steel Wool is also a science organization, with members having a significant role in helping Homebase fight against the Storm. Their music and scientific expertise are intertwined in the narrative, and each member has a unique background and contribution to the game's story and mechanics. The band is composed of four members.

Members

Members[]

This section is a stub. Help the Fortnite Wiki by expanding it!
  1. Lars - The lead guitarist and a prominent character in the storyline. He is a founding member of Steel Wool, which eventually broke up.
    • In addition to being a guitarist, Lars is a meteorologist. He assists Homebase by collecting data related to the Storm, and relaying this information for tactical use. Lars reunites with Steel Wool when Homebase starts to expand their operations in Canny Valley.
  2. Syd - The vocalist of the band, who is also a Constructor hero in the game. Syd can be unlocked by completing the Gravestone Quest in Canny Valley.
  3. Carlos - The bassist of the band. He is also a Soldier hero that players can unlock by completing the Train In Vain Quest.
  4. Anthony - The drummer of Steel Wool and an Outlander hero in the game, who can be unlocked by completing the Battle Royal Quest.
    • Anthony is a scientist. He is an expert in chemistry and was responsible for creating BluGlo, an important source of energy that Vindertech uses to power their inventions, and is found all throughout the world now.
Discography

Discography[]

Their known songs include "Hypertonic BluGlo", "O Vinderman", "Cyclone of Your Love", and "Stand and Fight". None of these songs have actually been heard start-to-finish apart from Stand and Fight. Later in Canny Valley, it is revealed that these are important to the storyline.
