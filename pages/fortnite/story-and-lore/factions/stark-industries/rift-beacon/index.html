Overview

“
Shuri thinks she can reverse the energetic flow of the Rift beacons with Rift Recalibrators.
That would give us the power to send Doom through a controlled Rift, and away from the Island.
”
— Chapter 5: Season 4 Story Quests

Rift Beacons are objects that can be used to bring stuff from other reality to the beacon. The beacon are powered by Power of Creation from the The Zero Point.

They were first introduced in Season X, when The Scientist used them to open several Rifts which by side effect, have accidentally altered many locations into Rift Zones.

History

History[]

Season X[]

Rift Beacons appeared in random locations every week, knowing that specific location will change. When new challenges came out, that specific location would have changed into a Rift Zone. The final rift beacon would eventually ended up as a base for The Rocket.

The purpose of the beacons was revealed in The End event. The rifts they formed were used by The Seven as a way to get onto the island and begin the event.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

In Chapter 2: Season 4, they were repurposed by Tony Stark to bring multiple locations from his reality to the island. When Stark Industries was created as a result, they powered down.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

Stark Industries Rift Beacons were bought to the island by Magneto in Magneto Quests. He used them to send reinforcement from Reality: 616 to help the island deal with an impending doom.

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

Hope and Marvel heroes plans use rift beacons against Doom in Absolute Doom Event to rift him away from the island. Doom managed to destroy all rift beacons except for one that the heroes used to rift him away.
