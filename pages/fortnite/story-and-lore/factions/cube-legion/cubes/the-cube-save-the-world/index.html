Cube is an entity appearing in Save the World after destroying the Storm King and during several seasonal questlines. It is a form of the Corruption Cube as well as the true identity of Storm King.

Lore

Lore[]

The Cube came to STW's Reality along with Motherships of The Last Reality.[1] For unspecified reason, those crashed in Frozen Fjords. The Corruption Cubes of the Motherships invoked The Storm after an unspecified time period in the past, an event whose traces have remained until the present, with multiple ancient frescoes found in the caves of Canny Valley depicting the Husks. However, they eventually failed for unknown reasons.

At some point, before or after the first Storm. He and his kind transformed into several Storm Kings.

Ages later, Dr. Vinderman somehow got information on the Storm King and The Nothing before their arrival, potentially through discovering the cave drawings. Vinderman began creating weapons to counter the threat before it happens or fight it if they couldn't prevent it, however, he lost the trust of his associate Desiree who abandoned him.

Later, the Storm Kings summoned The Storm once again, wiping 98% of the world's population, turning them into zombies known as Husks, and taking over Save the World Reality's Earth. The Storm also created Mist Monsters, the strongest form of Storm Monsters.

As the Cube, he first appeared to Homebase after the defeat of Storm King in Canny Valley, although they didn't notice. Later, The Cube appeared several times, notably during Tales of Beyond, where Homebase first encountered him, and he gave them a cryptic warning: "THE SIX SIDES OF CHANCE CAN TURN IN AN INSTANT. THE DIE IS CAST."

After The Timeline was broken in during The Long Road Home and that Ray disappeared over time, Commander and Director Riggs looked for a way to fix it during Timeless, unfortunately without success: the only solution was to destroy it before endangering the multiverse. But Ray, who had traveled through time, finally made a pact with The Cube to repair the timeline in exchange for something.

A few seconds after the timeline collapsed on itself and that everyone ceased to exist, The Cube partially restored it and explained to Homebase that it was necessary to perform 4 Dark Rituals to restore it completely, but it was also a trick to help him regain all of his powers.

As of now, only 2 of the 4 Dark Rituals have been carried out.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Storm King[]

After defeating the Storm King in the mission Enter the Portal, the Cube can be seen dissolving in the arena's central pond, similar to what occurred in Season 5 of Battle Royale. It is not acknowledged through dialogue by any character.

Tales of Beyond: Monster Hunters[]

Throughout the questline of Lok's Tales of Beyond, Lok tells of a terrifying monster he encountered outside of Homebase. It is described as making everything feel 'dark and sad.' At the end of this questline, it appears and gives Homebase a cryptic warning: "THE SIX SIDES OF CHANCE CAN TURN IN AN INSTANT. THE DIE IS CAST."

Timeless[]

At the end of the questline Timeless: Vroo-Over, Ray makes a deal with the Cube and requests that it fixes the rapidly deteriorating structure of the timeline. It complies, leading into the next questline in which you must complete a four rituals as your part of the deal.

The Door To Darkness[]

The Cube sent a message to Homebase to tell them about the four dark ritual and that the first would start directly at Oakdirge Hollows Stonepantcantwine's third most haunted hotel, where they had to survive seven nights. Then, they arrived in a mysterious underground crypt hiding the Door to Darkness, the source of the evil of the Hotel. The Cube ended up absorbing it, completing the first ritual.
