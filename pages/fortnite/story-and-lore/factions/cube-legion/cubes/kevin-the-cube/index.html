Kevin the Cube is a Corruption Cube that first appeared in Season 5. It is a sentient being born from The Last Reality with malicious intent to corrupt everything.

It is a major antagonist in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main antagonist of Season 5 and Season 6.

Overview

Overview[]

Kevin is a sentient entity that appeared first on August 24, 2018, during Season 5. It appeared near Paradise Palms, being created from several lightning strikes from the Sideways Rift, closing it in the process. The Cube is an object of immense power, as it was capable of destroying objects in its path as it rolled towards Loot Lake, printing powerful runes which reduced gravity, and generated Cube Monsters from its fragments.

The Cubes are the power sources of The Last Reality, an evil and malicious faction affiliated with the The Sideways with an intention to corrupt and destroy realities. The Cubes are all controlled by The Cube Queen. Thousands of dormant cubes are stored in a dedicated room in the Motherships, used to power the gigantic alien ships.

History

History[]

Season 5[]

As the Sideways Rift continued to rapidly close up, it suddenly turned purple. Within the next few days, purple storm lightning bolts began shooting out of the crack, all targeting a hill near Paradise Palms, containing a large stone rock surrounded by a ring of cacti. Each lightning strike picked off one cacti one-by-one, until a larger lightning bolt destroyed the large rock. The Crack Closure event occurred on 24th August 2018, with a gigantic storm lightning bolt striking out of the rift, lasting for around 10 seconds. When it was over, the Crack in the Sky closed. then The Cube was formed. Though it first appeared to be a stationary object, it began to move at a rate of one revolution every 1 hour and 43 minutes.

During its journey around Athena, The Cube printed seven runes with anti-gravity domes surrounding.

After finishing this process, The Cube destroyed one of the buildings in Tilted Towers and then proceeded to the edge of Loot Lake on September 19, 2018. The Cube appeared to dissolve, turning Loot Lake in a purple-pink gelatinous state with properties akin to The Cube itself, such as becoming bouncy.

Season 6[]

With the beginning of Season 6, the Cube—now fully energized—rose from the waters of Loot Lake, lifting the cabin and the island at its center with it to form the Floating Island. This ascent created a massive hole in the lake, exposing the bridge leading to The Zero Point, which appeared as a vortex at its center.

Just two days after the island began to rise, the Cube started moving the Floating Island to each of the runes it had imprinted during Season 5. After absorbing energy from all the runes, the Cube and the island descended back to Loot Lake. Kevin then began to power The Storm itself by channeling energy into the sky, forming a portal-like phenomenon that continued to expand until it enveloped the entire island.[1] On October 24, 2018—the first day of Fortnitemares 2018—the Cube released a massive burst of energy, causing the Floating Island to explode into multiple fragments. In the aftermath, the Storm began forming Cube-like monoliths across the island and spawning Cube Monsters.

However, the Loopers repeatedly and relentlessly attacked the Cube, causing it to begin cracking. After sustaining heavy damage, the Cube started leaking its dark nectar into the Vortex, drip by drip, until the final drop fell. On November 4, 2018, the Cube began spinning rapidly before erupting in a blinding white flash, splitting itself apart.

Following the flash, the Loopers were transported to The In-Between. Shortly afterward, a rift formed and manifested as a butterfly, which approached The Looper and landed gently on their finger. The butterfly then opened a Rift, returning the Loopers to Athena and restoring Leaky Lake.

Kevin the Cube is a Corruption Cube that first appeared in Season X. It is a Kevin from an alternative timeline of Reality Zero brought to this reality by The Scientist.

It is a major antagonist in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a minor antagonist during Season X and one of the main antagonists during Chapter 2: Season 8 alongside the other Cubes, The Cube Queen and the rest of The Army of the Last Reality.

Overview

Overview[]

An alternate timeline version of Kevin where the Floating Island was a fusion between the Motel and Loot Lake house. Rifted objects believed to have disappeared or changed places such as the Durrr Burger tagged by Drift, the Lonely Lodge sign etc... during World Collide are also on this Island, strongly suggesting that it comes from another timeline where the events, although similar, happened a little differently. In The End, the Island blew up but Kevin itself lived and became the power source of Steamy Stacks. When it managed to escape, it joined the rest of the Cubes at The Convergence.

History

History[]

OG Chapter 1: Season 6[]

Kevin mysteriously took Revin the Cube's place when the latter was melted in Loot Lake. With this change, all of the runes Revin printed reverted into the Kevin's rune. Revin's influence wasn't completely gone however, as instead of Kevin's Shadow Stones, they were now red and known as a Havoc Stone, giving people the power of the Nitro instead of transforming them into Shadows. With each rune Kevin went to, the floating island became more corrupted, and pieces of cubes appear throughout, most being purple with additions of red. After Kevin absorbed all the energy from the runes, they collapsed to the ground and the floating island returned to Leaky Lake, where it unleashed its energy to The Storm.

The floating island then exploded into several pieces, and Cube Monsters were unleashed across the island. From that day onward, as Looprs were fighting back, Kevin began to crack more and more, until it eventually shed it outer shell completely and began to drip nectar into the Vortex.

The Butterfly Event[]

After the last drop of nectar leaked from Kevin, the Cube began to spin, gradually accelerating in speed. It eventually exploded into pieces, sent the Loopers to the In-Between, and sent the chunks of the Floating Island down to Leaky Lake, patching up the whirlpool in the process. While in the In-Between, Revin the Cube was seen altering the location in some form, having turned part of it red. After The Looper touched a Rift Butterfly, they were rifted back to the Island, now gazing upon what Leaky Lake had now become.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kevin's name's origin was due to the name "Cube" being banned on a megathread on the Fortnite Subreddit.
    • The nickname "Kevin" originated from the title of a post by Reddit user u/6acee.
    • The first time "Kevin" was officially used in the game was in the final Fortnitemares 2018 reward, the Lil' Kev back bling.
  • When rolling over, Cubes deal 10000 damage, thus destroying any player or vehicle underneath.
    • If players get eliminated by a Cube, the kill feed will say "[player name] Got Pancaked by", followed by flickering Cube Runes.
  • Counting all of Kevin's alternate timeline counterparts (SX Kevin, C4OG Kevin, OGS6 Kevin and Revin the Cube, "Kevin" is the only antagonist in Fortnite: Battle Royale to have returned after its death thrice.
  • OG Chapter 1: Season 6 version of Kevin the Cube mysteriously took the place of Revin the Cube under Loot Lake under unknown circumstances while Revin the Cube went into The In-Between.
    • Despite being two distinct cubes, both OGS6 Kevin and Revin the Cube are the counterparts of Kevin, each playing the role of one half of Kevin's existence, Revin playing that of Season 5, and OGS6 Kevin playing that of Season 6.
    • It was initially believed that OGS6 Kevin and Revin were once a single Cube altered by the Red Rift before splitting into two separate entities, Kevin as the original Cube, and Revin as a manifestation of the Red Energy forming a new Cube. However, Dark Voyager (Reality Redacted)'s Void Voyager style later revealed that red-colored Cubes occur naturally.
