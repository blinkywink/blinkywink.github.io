Super Human Intelligence: Extra-Legal Division also referred as S.H.I.E.L.D. (Formerly Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division) is an organization in Fortnite: Battle Royale from Reality: 616, first appearing in Chapter 2: Season 4.

Overview

Overview[]

In its original iteration, it was international peacekeeping, law enforcement, and counter-terrorism agency sanctioned by the United Nations. However before Chapter 2: Season 4 its original iteration fell during Secret Empire event, before being secretely recreated as an organization to deal with superhumans by Maria Hill as Super Human Intelligence: Extra-Legal Division inside the CIA.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

Thor brought the Helicarrier 64 to Apollo, a former Helicarrier of the S.H.I.E.L.D. now under the control of Tony Stark.

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

Doctor Doom summoned a part of The Raft on the Island to free like minded super villain and make them join his army.
