The First Galactic Empire, simply known as the Empire is the dictatorship government that replaced the Galactic Republic within the Star Wars Universe.

They are major antagonists of Fortnite, serving as reoccuring antagonists in Fortnite: Battle Royale as well as LEGO Fortnite: Odyssey.

Overview

Overview[]

The Galactic Empire is a fascist dictatorship led by Emperor Palpatine and his apprentice, the Commander-in-Chief Darth Vader. They train many ranks and special corps of Stormtroopers to protect and expand their territory, as well as to fight against rebel forces. After their defeat, several of their remnants attempted to overthrow the New Republic, notably the alliance of Imperial remnants led by the Shadow Council, which was awaiting the return of Grand Admiral Thrawn. Other imperial remnants hid in unknown regions, while the cult of Sith Eternal went on Exegol of those same region. These Imperial remnants later became the First Order while those on Exegol became the Final Order Fleet, which later worked alongside the First Order before both being destroyed at the same time.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

A Star Destroyer detects a Sideways Rift on Tatooine. They send 2 Imperial Stormtroopers down to scout it out only to be encountered by Loopers.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

The Empire sends a Star Destroyer to the Island to search for Obi-wan. The IO makes an alliance with the Empire and recruits Stormtroopers to increase their ranks and eliminate The Seven The IO recruit to use them as a force against.[1] The Star Destroyer stays in the air, visible at the sky as the Stormtroopers set up Stormtrooper Checkpoints.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

Darth Vader and his Stormtroopers are sent to Reality Zero to recover a Sith Holocron brought to the island by the Zero Point. They set up Imperial Docking Bays around the island. After retrieving the Sith Holocron Darth Vader is confronted by The Looper who was ordered to retrieve the artifact and has been defeated.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

Darth Vader and Stormtroopers are back in search for Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa and Han Solo. They set up Imperial Docking Bay Eclipse and new Stormtrooper Checkpoints.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Darth Vader and Stormtroopers arrive on Helios. They set up Imperial Docking Bay Eclipse and new Stormtrooper Checkpoints with a captured Chewbacca.

LEGO Fortnite: Odyssey[]

In the Lego Galaxy, in a village from the world The Looper and other inhabitants of Helios settled, a Sideways Rift cracks open in the sky and an Imperial Star Destroyer emerges from it, accompanied by a fleet of X-Wings engaged in a dogfight with Tie Fighters.[3] The Empire establishes many outposts throughout The Looper's LEGO world and try to invade it.[4] The Looper joins the rebels to help them, building the Rebel Village.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Galactic Empire and the First Order are the only factions that appear in a chronologically inconsistent manner in Fortnite.
    • The First Order from Star Wars: Episode IX – The Rise of Skywalker is the first of the two faction to appear, during Chapter 2: Season 1. Then, the Galactic Empire of the Obi-Wan Kenobi show appeared during Chapter 3: Season 2 and Chapter 3: Season 3, followed by the iteration of Star Wars: Episode VI – Return of the Jedi movie. However, during Chapter 4: Season 2 it is the iteration of the Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith that appeared, when Chancelor Palpatine proclaimed the Order 66, and when Anakin Skywalker became Darth Vader. In the end, during Chapter 5: Season 2 the Galactic Empire from Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back appeared in both Fortnite: Battle Royale and LEGO Fortnite:Odyssey.
