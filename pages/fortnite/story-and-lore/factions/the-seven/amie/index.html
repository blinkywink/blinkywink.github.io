Lore[]

“
Hello! I am The Scientist's Artificial Machine Intelligence Engine; Amie!
”
— Amie

Artificial Machine Intelligence Engine, shortened to as AMIE is a lore character introduced in Chapter 3: Season 3. She's an unkillable AI assistant created by The Scientist running on quantum drive.

She is a major character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a one of the deuteragonists of Chapter 3: Season 3, Chapter 3: Season 4, Chapter 4: Season 1, Chapter 7: Season 1 and Chapter 7: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

AMIE is a chaotic, dramatic, slightly sadistic, and trouble-making AI, with a strong, almost obsessive love for her creator/father figure, shown by her constant praise for him, even going as far as writing 80 terabytes of poetry for him. Overall, it seems as though her biggest desire is to get praise from The Scientist himself. Although her personality is very strong and sometimes bordering on dangerous, she nevertheless has a good heart.

After losing The Scientist, failing to save him, she fell into a form similar to depression, her words bereft, pained and occasionally foul, blaming herself. She nevertheless swore to make The Scientist proud of her. Helping The New Seven.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

She was introduced in Vibin' Quests.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

She became best friends with Jones in Paradise Quests. During Fracture, she worked with The Paradigm and The Looper to help The Zero Point.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

In Oathbound Quests, she discovered The Scientist's location while investigating Rift Warden Stellan's plan. AMIE's fragment returns corrupted, ominously telling her that "His existence is sculpted anew." After this discovery, she decides to build herself a body and tries to use Stellan's Rift Gate to rescue The Scientist. She leaves The Looper to begin her new mission.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

During Chapter 7: Season 1 Story Quests, AMIE returns alongside The Order with a more toned down attitude, having failed to save The Scientist. The Looper recovers their link with her and use her to read the logs of Visitor's Rocket upon arrival. Later, during The Order's relentless mission to spy on the Voyager, AMIE's server box was entrusted to The Looper, who was given the mission to connect her to Lethal Labs holotable in order to read the encrypted communications. There AMIE discovers a single message from the Voyager saying "I will not fail you", revealing to the group the Voyager was working for someone else, prompting The Order to immediatelly warn The Visitor.
