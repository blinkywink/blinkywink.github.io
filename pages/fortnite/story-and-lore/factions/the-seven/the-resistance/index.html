Overview

This article is about The Resistance faction seen in Chapter 3: Season 2. For The organization of the same name from Galactic Battle, see The Resistance (Galactic Battle). For the Back Bling of the same name, see The Resistance (Back Bling).

“
It doesn't matter who you are or where you came from. If you fight for us, you are one of us. Right? It is important for all of us, including The Seven, to remember what we're fighting for! The Island. Reality itself. And most importantly...you, the Loopers.
”
— The Imagined

The Resistance was an army mainly composed of Loopers founded and lead by The Seven to fight against The Imagined Order and act as a resistance. They were formed between Chapter 3: Season 1 and Chapter 3: Season 2 before being disbanded when most of The Seven got Chromed during Chapter 3: Season 4.

Overview

Overview[]

The Resistance was a sub-faction of The Seven made up of the Loopers that have been freed from The Loop and rallied to their cause. Just like the War Effort, they use Donation Stations to finance weapons and various equipment. They were later joined by forces from Reality: 616, dividing leadership between The Foundation and The Origin for Reality Zero's force, and Iron man for Reality: 616 forces.
