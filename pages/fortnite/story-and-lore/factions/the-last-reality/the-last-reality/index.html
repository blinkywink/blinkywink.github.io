The Army of the Last Reality, also referred to simply as The Last Reality, is an ancient, inter-reality empire originating from the Last Reality in Fortnite. First introduced indirectly in Season 5 and directly in Chapter 2: Season 7, this army comprises aliens of various species, monsters, fiends, artificial intelligences, supernatural entities, and cosmic beings. They share a single goal: to destroy all realities across the Omniverse and revert everything to Nothing. The Last Reality operates under an imperialist regime with multiple rulers.

The Army of the Last Reality are the main antagonists of Fortnite, serving as the main antagonists of Fortnite: Save the World, LEGO Fortnite and Fortnite: Battle Royale: Season 5, Season 6, Chapter 2: Season 7, Chapter 2: Season 8, Chapter 3: Season 3, Chapter 3: Season 4, Chapter 4: Season OG, Chapter Seven: Season 1 and also one of the two main antagonists (alongside The Ice Kingdom) of Chapter Seven: Season 2.

They also serve as the overarching antagonists of Chapter 2: Season 6 and Chapter 4, the secondary antagonists of Chapter 2: Season 1 and minor antagonists of Season X.

Overview

Overview[]

The Last Reality is driven by its ultimate goal of annihilating all realities in the Omniverse. While its members may have differing personal motives, they are unified in this purpose. Recruitment spans several sub-factions, though some members are chosen by The Nothing, the primordial entity that existed before the birth of The Zero Point. Their reasons for targeting The Zero Point remain unclear, but as of Chapter 6: Season 1, it is revealed that The Zero Point has been destroyed, though it's uncertain if The Last Reality is responsible.

The Invaders[]

The Last Reality's alien forces consist of countless species all ruled by The Cube Queen, along with entire alien ecosystems under their control. They wield advanced technology powered by the dark energy of The Cubes, enabling them to create weapons, vehicles, and soldiers. This near-limitless energy coupled with a vast armada grants them an unparalleled military force capable of conquering hundreds of realities. Even a single Mothership can overwhelm organizations like the Imagined Order. The aliens also conduct experiments on captured prisoners aboard their ships.

The Cube Legion[]

A major part of The Last Reality is the Cube Legion, powered by Corruption Cubes and Golden Cubes created by The Cube Queen and the Cube King. These Cubes serve as energy sources for the alien forces and also act as soldiers by summoning monsters from The Sideways. When an alien Mothership fails or crashes—such as in Chapter 2: Season 7—the Cube Legion takes over. Given enough energy, they can open portals to summon the entire alien armada or evolve into Storm Kings capable of summoning The Storm.

The Cubes can also corrupt individuals, turning them into allies like the Cube Assassin, or create dark reflections of people. Some Cubes, such as Kevin the Cube, can operate independently to destroy realities. Despite their destructive efforts, the Cube Legion's attempt to annihilate Reality Zero ultimately failed during The End (Chapter 2).

Storm King's Acolytes[]

The Storm King's Acolytes or the Storm King's realm are a sub-faction led by Storm Kings. They consist of highly evolved Cube Monsters and individuals empowered by Storm Kings. These acolytes are fanatics granted special abilities by their leaders and act as an elite extension of the Cube Legion. Other creatures of the Storm are also considered as part of this faction.

The Nothing's envoys[]

A sub-faction of The Nothing's chosen led by The Herald. Each member possesses the power of Chrome in different variations and is given a title. The two known members being their leader titled the Herald and Bytes the Warrior. The envoys succeeded in destroying Reality Zero during Fracture.

\\REDACTED\\[]

A sub-faction of the alien army led by the Dark Voyager. Their members are Void Walker Lynx and Trespasser Unknowns. They seems to be tied to void exploration. Their primary mission appears to be research and investigation rather than the direct annihilation of worlds, although they annihilated countless. They are currently searching for the remaining Seven members as well as the Zero Shards.

Although The Last Reality has multiple divisions, it is not uncommon for members to be part of several of them at once:

  • The Trespassers Elite are part of the alien army but are also Cubes, making them members of the Cube Legion.
  • The Cube Queen and Cube King lead both the alien forces and the Cube Legion.
  • The Storm Kings lead the Storm Acolytes but are also Corruption Cubes and technically part of the Cube Legion.
  • The Herald is likely a Pluxarian, part of the alien army, but works directly under The Nothing.
  • Dark Voyager and Void Walker Lynx both use the powers of the Cubes and a ship reminiscent of those of the Cube's Cradle of the Invaders. However, they operate independently of both Cube Legions and Invaders, employing technologies that differ from those of the latter.

History

History[]

The Nothing[]

In the beginning, there was Nothing. Then, in a burst of pure creation, the Zero Point came into existence. Sometime afterward, at an indeterminate point in time, the Nothing gathered various beings and formed the Army of the Last Reality, with the goal of destroying the Omniverse created by its nemesis, the Zero Point. Over time, this army grew immensely large, becoming one of the greatest threats the Omniverse has ever faced.

The Cube King and the Cube Queen[]

The Cube Legion, alongside the alien forces of the Last Reality, annihilated thousands of worlds since the beginning of time under the leadership of the Cube King. They were feared across realities, spreading misery and destruction wherever they went. However, after witnessing the end of countless worlds, the Cube King came to believe that their crusade served no true purpose. He ultimately chose to abandon the Last Reality in search of a better path.

He was eventually captured by the Imagined Order, imprisoned, and subjected to torture. Pretending that his mind had been broken, he waited for the moment their vigilance weakened, then incinerated his guards and escaped. Later, he was discovered by The Seven, who offered him a chance at redemption. Accepting their offer, he joined them as their field general and tactician, taking a new name: The Origin. In his absence, the Cube Queen assumed full control of the Cube Legion and the alien empire of the Last Reality.

The Storm Kings[]

The Storm Kings are the rulers of monsters and fiends. It is unknown when or how they first appeared, or whether the different Storm Kings are distinct individuals or manifestations of a shared consciousness inhabiting separate bodies. Certain Corruption Cubes created by the Cube Queen and or the Cube King have proven to be more powerful and intelligent than others, possessing unique abilities that allow them to create their own monsters rather than summoning them from the Sideways.

When a Corruption Cube reaches a sufficient level of power, it transforms into a massive and intelligent entity known as a Storm King. In this form, it gains the ability to create and control the Storm, which either invades realities through incursion points known as Alpha Rifts or manifests directly within an invaded reality under the Storm King's influence.

Save the World[]

In Reality: UN-STW-1, one or more purple Motherships from the Last Reality were shot down under unknown circumstances and crashed at Boreal Base in the Frozen Fjords during a distant past. The surviving Cubes gradually gathered their strength over time and appear to have attempted at least one invasion of Earth. These events were later depicted in cave drawings found in Canny Valley. At some point, the Storm mysteriously vanished, suggesting that the invasion ultimately failed for unknown reasons.

In an era similar to the present, Dr. Vinderman somehow acquired knowledge of the Storm King and The Nothing prior to their return, possibly through the discovery of the cave drawings. Anticipating their arrival, he developed weapons and formulated a plan to stop them, but ultimately failed. The Cubes launched another offensive, with several of them having evolved into Storm Kings. Upon the arrival of the Storm, approximately 98 percent of the world's population was annihilated and transformed into Husks, allowing the invaders to take control of the planet. In response, a group of survivors formed Homebase and began fighting to reclaim their world and ensure humanity's survival.

The Loop[]

Under unknown circumstances, several realities such as Reality Zero and Reality: 266 came with a a Loop, each incorporating The Storm. The Imagined Order later used it as a critical component of their altered Loop System in Reality Zero, a mechanism powered by the Zero Point that forced a repeating time loop. This system forcibly abducted warriors from across realities and compelled them to fight to the death every 22 minutes, erasing their memories and stripping them of their ability to speak.

Season 5[]

A Corruption Cube was sent by the Army of the Last Reality to the Island through a Sideways Rift. Upon arrival, it began rolling across the Island, imprinting the ground with Glyphs. Eventually, the Cube reached Loot Lake and dissolved into its waters.

Season 6[]

After recharging itself with energy, the Cube rose from Loot Lake, lifting a chunk of land into the air and exposing a mysterious Vortex beneath it. The Floating Island then traveled above Athena, drawing power from the Glyphs the Cube had previously created. Once fully charged, the Island returned to Loot Lake, where the Cube injected its energy into The Storm,[6] triggering a massive explosion that unleashed hordes of Cube Monsters onto the Island. Following this event, the Loopers fought back and repeatedly attacked the Cube, slowly weakening it. During a final confrontation between the Loopers and the Cube Monsters, the Cube ultimately exploded.

Season X[]

The Scientist activated a Rift Beacon at Mega Mall, which brought in a version of Retail Row from an alternate reality overrun with Cube Monsters. He later deployed another Rift Beacon at Fatal Fields, transporting an alternate reality version of Kevin the Cube contained within a different Floating Island.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

During Fortnitemares 2019, a Storm King discovered[7] the Island. It corrupted the Eye Land, transforming it into Isle Of The Storm, seized control of The Storm, and summoned Cube Monsters. The Loopers eventually defeated the Storm King, after which the Eye Land reverted to its original state.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Long after the construction of The Spire, it became corrupted by an unknown force. It began killing those who ventured too close or granting them power while simultaneously corrupting them by exploiting their weaknesses. Raz became corrupted through a Spire Artifact, which was a fragment of the Spire's heart. His growing power drew the attention of the Last Reality, leading him to unknowingly guide a Mothership directly to the Island. Around this time, an unknown type of Kymera ship began randomly abducting players. Crop circles bearing the symbol of the Army of the Last Reality appeared in Colossal Crops.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

The Army of the Last Reality arrived aboard The Mothership and destroyed The Spire in an attempt to abduct The Zero Point. They deployed their forces and flying Saucers across the Island as the Mothership began its operations. It abducted Slurpy Swamp, followed by Coral Castle, and later Corny Complex. Meanwhile, Trespassers seized control of Holly Hedges, transforming it into Holly Hatchery as a breeding ground for Alien Parasites.

Operation: Sky Fire[]

Doctor Slone assembled a strike team to infiltrate the Mothership during the abduction of Corny Complex. The team navigated through the Mothership while avoiding Trespassers before being locked inside the Abduction Chamber Window. During the operation, a Corruption Cube rose from the water and struck the Loopers with energy. Slone temporarily shut it down and abandoned the Loopers after the Countermeasure Devices were armed.

The Loopers reactivated the Cube, which restored power to the elevator system. As they ascended, they witnessed multiple Cubes acting as the Mothership's power source, including the Golden Cube. Shortly afterward, the explosives detonated, causing the Mothership and all of the Cubes aboard to crash onto Apollo.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

The Army of the Last Reality continued their invasion of Reality Zero. Following the events of Operation: Sky Fire, the Cubes crashed onto the Island, along with the Cube Queen and the Rebooted Cube. During these events, Sideways Anomalies began appearing across the Island, converting POIs into the Sideways and releasing Cube Monsters led by Cube Assassins.

The aftermath of Operation: Sky Fire was visible on the new season's map. The wreckage of the Mothership could be seen on the horizon, while debris from destroyed Abductors littered the Island. The Golden Cube began rolling across the Island to awaken the other Cubes. Once they all reached The Aftermath, they formed The Convergence, which later transformed into The Pyramid.

The End[]

The Cube Queen launched her attack by opening a portal to the Cube's Cradle. She summoned Caretakers to defeat the Loopers and abducted their weapons using Saucers. The Rebooted Cube eliminated several Caretakers, but was ultimately destroyed by a Golden Abductor. Golden Abductors began destroying locations as the Loopers were rescued by The Scientist and The Visitor.

The Cube Queen then sent Caretakers underwater, with one emerging above The Bridge before shattering its glass ceiling and flooding the area as Apollo flipped over, revealing Artemis. The flipping of the Island caused energy from the Pyramid to erupt, seemingly destroying the Cube Queen and closing the portal.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

After the Last Reality's repeated defeats, The Nothing sent its Herald to spread a living substance that consumed everything: the Chrome. As Loopers celebrated their victory over the Imagined Order, The Herald began observing the Island, specifically the Reality Tree, which drew energy from the recently released Zero Point. As Reality Zero faced impending doom, the Zero Point began pulling powerful artifacts from various realities in an attempt to warn those on the Island of the approaching danger.

Through a stealth assault, The Herald and the Chrome spread across the eastern part of the Island, silently consuming several Loopers and members of The Seven, including The Foundation and The Order. The remaining members of The Seven: The Scientist, The Paradigm, The Visitor, and The Origin attempted to study the Chrome, with The Origin devising battle plans against his former colleague.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

During their research, a sample of Chrome broke free and consumed The Origin, The Scientist, and The Visitor. The Herald then used it to construct her own body, mocking Paradigm as she was cornered by the Chrome. However, The Paradigm managed to escape by flying toward The Zero Point.

Meanwhile, The Herald established a chrome fortress above the abandoned Sanctuary, known as Herald's Sanctum. From this base, she continued observing the Reality Tree, directing Chrome from the eastern part of the Island toward it while simultaneously causing Chrome to emerge from all the Reality Saplings spread across the Island. At the same time, after selecting Bytes as their Warrior, The Nothing granted him chromed hands and waited for him to make contact. Jones later contacted The Looper and briefly summarized the situation, asking them to reactivate AMIE. He then sent The Looper to the Reality Tree, where a message was recovered through the roots and recorded. AMIE decoded it, revealing it was a warning from The Paradigm about The Nothing.

After further investigations, Jones informed The Looper that The Origin had planned an assault on The Herald. AMIE considered the plan reckless but decided The Looper should carry it out regardless. The Looper infiltrated Herald's Sanctum and defeated The Herald in battle. However, this did not halt the spread of Chrome, as The Herald could endlessly reconstruct her body due to being made entirely of Chrome.

Later, The Paradigm explained to Jones, AMIE, and The Looper that victory was impossible and that Reality Zero was doomed, but she revealed she had a plan to save the people.

Once Bytes was ready, he contacted The Nothing on his own through a Chromed television. The Nothing demanded he prove himself through a series of trials, gradually stripping him of his identity and humanity while rewarding him with increasing power. Though initially frightened, Bytes endured every trial. Upon completion, he became the Warrior of The Nothing, who warned him that his journey was only beginning and that he would be summoned when needed.

Fracture[]

As the remaining Loopers constructed a base atop the Reality Tree in preparation for the final battle, The Herald used a Chrome Tornado to transport herself there. She withstood heavy gunfire before her chrome body was destroyed by a barrage of rockets. As the Loopers celebrated, The Herald reformed into a larger chrome form and rapidly chromed both the Loopers and the Reality Tree by striking them.

After eliminating the Loopers, she fused with the Reality Tree and destroyed Reality Zero, leaving the Zero Point isolated. Following the destruction, The Paradigm instructed AMIE and The Looper to locate Zero Fragments to restore the Zero Point's energy and initiate the Zero Fusion. During this process, the Reality Tree merged with the Herald's giant form and was absorbed and compressed by the Zero Point. Once its energy was restored, The Paradigm activated the Zero Fusion, creating a new Reality Zero and a new Island: Asteria.

As a result, it never placed runes and followed a completely different plan from its original counterpart.
