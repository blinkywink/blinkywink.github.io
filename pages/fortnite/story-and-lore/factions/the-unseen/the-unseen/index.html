The Unseen is a neutral Syndicate that was first was introduced in Chapter 4: Season 2 through the Syndicate Quests.

Overview

Overview[]

The Unseen are an unpredictable faction that do not care when innocent people get hurt. Some former members attempted to incite a war between all of MEGA City's Syndicates, sabotaging them with the help of Triarch Nox while working with The Last Reality. However, not all of the faction was aware of this, and Highwire alongside Renzo put a stop to their plan upon learning of it.

They later joined the Syndicate alliance in order to protect MEGA City.

History

History[]

Unknown Period Of Time[]

The Unseen is formed, and establish their presence within MEGA City.

After the unexpected collapse of Triarch Nox's kingdom alongside his realm, he fled into The Omniverse, and eventually arrived in MEGA City. There he joined The Unseen, notably meeting Dahlia.

At some point, Triarch Nox collaborates with Dahlia and Styx[2] to discreetly contact The Last Reality and begin plotting with them.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

Following the arrival of MEGA City on Asteria, tensions begin to raise between the various factions residing in it. Unbeknownst to the rest of The Unseen,[3] Dahlia's sabotage of the Peace Syndicate's servers (specifically their data on The Last Reality)[4] is one of several attempts to start an inter-syndicate war during the Syndicate Quests, by causing trouble for one group, and blaming it on another.

At some time during their crime spree, Dahlia and Styx also worked together to cause chaos against the Cold Blooded's members.[5]

Thanks to investigations done by The Looper for the Peace Syndicate, Evie and Thunder conclude the string of incidents throughout MEGA City were caused by The Unseen— though further investigation reveals it to have been part of Triarch Nox, Dahlia, and Styx's untold plan with The Last Reality. Upon being informed by The Looper, Highwire and Renzo put an end to their schemes, with Highwire heading off to confront Dahlia,[6][7] and The Unseen allies itself with the rest of MEGA City's syndicates.[8]
