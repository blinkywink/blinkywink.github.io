The Onimorph eXtermination Rangers[2], better known as O.X.R. is an organization in Fortnite: Battle Royale first introduced during Chapter 6: Season 4. It is Oninoshima's anti-bug military defense force.

They are major protagonists of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main protagonists of Chapter 6: Season 4.

Overview

Overview[]

The O.X.R. is an ancient military organization founded generations ago as The Island's anti-bug military defense force. It came out of hiding following the re-emergence of The Swarm, otherwise known by their species name, Onimorphs, between Chapter 6: Season 3 and Chapter 6: Season 4 to deal with the infestation, the organization is currently led by General Onyx Winter.

Although considered a military organization, the O.X.R. is also considered an agency,[3] possessing special agents like their Elite team which are responsible for investigations[4] and law enforcement[5] as well as espionage and other covert operations.[6] This makes O.X.R. both a military organization and a security, intelligence and law enforcement agency.

History

History[]

Before Chapter 6[]

At an unknown point in time, The Swarm emerged and led to the founding of the O.X.R. to fight back. After their disappearance, O.X.R. assets hid for generations, awaiting the Swarm's re-emergence.

Between Chapter 6: Season 3 & Chapter 6: Season 4[]

Following the infestation, the O.X.R. came out of hiding in order to fight back the invasion. Several bases such as O.X.R. HQ, Swarmy Stash and a O.X.R.'s Weapon Lab were established. However, the last two fell due to the infestation and their Weapons Lab became Ranger's Ruin, one of the three nests of the swarm, the two other being The Hive and Demon's Debris where once stood Lonewolf Lair and Demon's Domain respectively.

The O.X.R. are not the only forces prepared to fight back, as they gained the support of The Supernova Academy with Hope Jones and Midas developing a plan, while The Night Rose and The Supernovas fought back The Swarm. The Spirit Siblings, Kendo, Jade and even Daigo also aligned with O.X.R., Daigo teaming up with The Night Rose to defend O.X.R. Base Alpha while Kendo and Jade defend O.X.R. Base Theta. Seeing chaos and conflict once again unleashed by a mask maker's hubris, repeating the history of the Shattering War, Mizuki and the River Guard allies themselves with the O.X.R. as well,[23] with the River Empress defending O.X.R. Base Epsilon alongside Yoo-mi.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

Welcome to the Infestation[]

The Looper gets in contact with O.X.R. Commander and Leader, General Onyx Winter. After noticing their moves against target dummies, she assigns them with a task of eliminating a chunk of The Swarm to test them. Upon the the test's successful completion, Onyx Winter complimented them by expressing how breathtaking it was to her and invite The Looper at O.X.R. HQ as she got an offer for them.

Once they met, General Onyx Winter formally introduced herself, before complimenting The Looper once again, noting that they were similar to a young Ripp Slade. She then offered them the chance to join the O.X.R. as they needed The Looper's abilities on the battlefield. The Looper accepted the invitation.

Officially ranked among the O.X.R., The Looper was sent in a recon and recovery mission, consisting of defending one of the bases of the O.X.R. from The Swarm's assaults and put it back online. Once done, The Looper was assigned to minimize the threat posed by the enemies located at Ranger's Ruin and The Hive.

Taking a detour from O.X.R. commands, they head over to their friends Hope and Jones at Supernova Academy to talk about the piece of the tentacle that was cut off by Superman in their laboratory. Hope and Jones reveal to them that their intel gathers that the Tentacle and The Swarm accessed the Island from the Spirit Realm without using Spirit Realm regular portals, however they need more data for further study, notably the location of that mysterious access point.

Therefore, The Looper's mission was to find how they could access the Island and from where. After some research, The Looper locates The Swarm's access portal anomaly just outside the perimeters of Foxy Floodgate, north of Demon's Debris. After The Looper analyzed the anomaly, the access point changed aspect and became dark red and Jones noted it was creepy but helpful nevertheless.

Dystopia[]

Following in the footsteps of Mask Maker Daigo who once sought to destroy Utopia City, Demon's Debris's Queen assaulted the city and destroyed three-quarters of it before installing a new nest. Fortunately, O.X.R. and The Supernovas managed to protect a part of the city, which remains more or less intact and continues to resist the infestation the best they can. Long before the invasion, Midas had closed access to The Vault, ensuring its safety from the threats of the Spirit Realm.

As for the O.X.R. Bases Alpha, Epsilon and Theta, The Swarm continues to relentlessly attack all of them, with several overruns and becoming completely infested, with their protectors (The Night Rose, Daigo, Jade, Kendo, Mizuki and Yoo-mi) imprisoned within stashes if not fighting.[24] Through outside assistance if not their own willpower, each of them breaks free and continues the fight against the infestation, attempting to restore their control over their respective bases.[25] Triggerfish, Meowscles, Cuddle Team Leader and Peely are also in the same situation.

Extermination Time![]

“
O.X.R. Battalion, the bugs are on their last legs. Repel the Menace. Hold the line at all costs. And above all—finish the fight.
”
— O.X.R. High Command[26]

As the campaign to exterminate The Swarm continues, O.X.R. struck fear into insect horde and drove them into disarray. Their numbers has been decimated with over 1 Billion bugs squashed[27] and the remaining swarms scattered, completely disorganized and fleeing against O.X.R. advance. All infested and conquered outposts : O.X.R. Base Alpha, O.X.R. Base Epsilon and O.X.R. Base Theta were reclaimed and cleared out with their protectors (The Night Rose, Daigo, Jade, Kendo, Mizuki and Yoo-mi) all freed from their goo pods permanently. To ensure the complete eradication of The Swarm O.X.R. High Command transmitted a message to all their battalions announcing the deployment of the powerful Shockwave Hammers.

“
Your campaign has been exemplary, striking fear into the Insect Horde and driving them into disarray. Enemy numbers have been decimated, and the remaining swarms are scattered, disorganized, and fleeing before your advance. To ensure their complete eradication, High Command has authorized deployment of Shockwave Hammers to your arsenal to wield them with precision and finality. Let no pest escape Humanity's Justice.
”
— O.X.R. High Command[28]

Egg-cellent Observations[]

As The Swarm begins to regain ground, invading Big Bend and creating a new nest Hive Hideaway, Hope asked The Looper to take out Swarmer Nests, to thin them out as much as they can. However, The Swarm appears to emerge directly from the Spirit Realm at an alarming rate and will soon overrun the Island. Seeking to know exactly how The Swarm came from, Hope asked The Looper to investigate its source, hoping to find the means The Swarm uses to get here from the Spirit Realm. Observing that the demonic energy is off the charts, The Looper decides to tune up sensors at Hope's request, thinking the nest must be close to their access point.

However, instead of discovering the access point, The Looper discovered some eggs with strange readings. Noting that the eggs were tainted with something, Hope asked The Looper to collect some and bring them to her to study them. She theorizes that the eggs are Queen eggs laid in the Spirit Realm and brought here, and that their energy signature could weaken the access point or even allow them to use the access point to their advantage to access the Spirit Realm without a portal. To confirm her theory, Hope sent The Looper to fight and destroy a Queen to retrieve data.

Megazord Rising[]

In order to help fight The Swarm, the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers team intervened on the Island. Blue Ranger started to study the effectiveness of Precision Air Strike on The Swarm, Yellow Ranger started to use her Zord as transport to first aid, Black Ranger was training reinforcements, Pink Ranger was in aerial combat while Red Ranger was tied up fighting Queens. After getting some help from The Looper and other soldiers, they managed to assemble. However, before the Red Ranger could be summoned, Lord Zedd cursed Oninoshima and empowered The Storm. Then, when the Megazord was finally assembled, Rita Repulsa summoned a Queen and her army in an attempt to destroy it.

The Swarm's disappearance[]

With the power of the Dino Megazord, the O.X.R. finally turned the tide of the war against the Swarm. They reclaimed all their lost bases—including Ranger's Ruin and Swarmy Stash—and eradicated the remaining nests across the island after a final decisive battle at The Hive.[29]

With the Swarm believed to be defeated for good, the island entered a period of peace. This respite allowed the Supernovas to restore both the Supernova Academy and Utopia City. Meanwhile, the O.X.R. undertook extensive reconstruction efforts, rebuilding their ruined bases. Ranger's Ruin and Swarmy Stash were transformed into Ranger's Rest and Harmony Hills. The organization also terraformed the former large "Swarm" biome, replanting trees, rebuilding paths and roads, and converting what was once The Hive into Bugswatter Beach, a museum commemorating the war against the Swarm. On display at the museum is the Dino Megazord's Power Sword, serving as both a tribute and a reminder of the battle that saved the Island.

However, doubts soon began to surface regarding the island's fragile peace. Hope and Jones prepared to confront the Dark Presence, mobilizing the O.X.R. assets, which had been placed under their command by General Onyx Winter. The general maintained that the Swarm could never truly be defeated as long as their master still existed. Lieutenant Ripp Slade warned that the peace was nothing more than the calm before the storm, while Doctor Theodore Flyesbee theorized that the Swarm would not vanish on its own—unless something "much higher in the food chain" was approaching.

Afterward, a summit was held between the Island's leaders and allies to establish a definitive strategy for counterattacking the Spirit Realm and the Dark Presence.[30]

Prepare to Fight[]

Following the preparations for the assault on the Spirit Realm, Hope tasked The Looper with consulting The Night Rose, Jade, and Daigo. The Night Rose admitted that the plan was far from ideal, but also that there was no better alternative. Jade, on the other hand, compared the idea of attacking the Dark Presence with the Splinter Blades to "using a toothpick against a Great Turtle," though she conceded that luck might still favor them.

In the end, The Looper met with Daigo, who seemed to already know why they had come—to retrieve the Splinter Blades. Acknowledging his past failure, he handed the daggers to The Looper and asked them to finish what he could not: to pierce the veil and save everyone from the demonic incursion.

With the Splinter Blades recovered, Hope assigned The Looper a new mission—to rally the troops, secure ground support, and gather as much intel as possible. Once everything was in place, Hope warned that they were about to strike the access point and instructed The Looper to prepare their weapons. After running final tests, The Looper used the Splinter Blades to weaken the access point, before signaling to Hope that the path was clear.

Hope then declared that with the veil between realms now weakened, they had everything they needed to cross over—the blades, the Swarm tunnels, and a small army—before rallying everyone with a final message: they would need to stay focused, stand together, and make every action count.

The Anomaly[]

However, a mysterious anomaly taking the form of a tree appeared on the northwestern corner of Oninoshima, eventually growing into a large tree outfitted with a treehouse. Over time, a mysterious dark aura appeared over the treehouse, progressively worsening the instabilities of the veil separating the Island from the Spirit Realm.

While Hope sent The Looper to study the anomaly, she and Jones prepared a countermeasure to permanently seal the portals prior to their planned invasion of the Spirit Realm, with Jones enhancing his Portal Device to do so. When the instabilities reached their critical point, Hope and Jones rifted to a control center that had been set up nearby and proceeded to seal the breach and prevent the Dark Presence from invading the Island. The portal was closed for a moment before reopening into a larger portal that allowed the demonic creature to cross over.

As a battle erupts, The Looper is hit by one of the Dark Presence's tentacle. Meanwhile, Kang and Kodos became captivated by the sight of the Dark Presence as they hope to find a reproductive mate. They abduct Hope and Jones to gain the demon's favor, but Hope overpowers them and gains control of their saucer to attack the Dark Presence. Her attack is parried and the saucer is thrown into an inter-reality wormhole, but Jones notices the Zero Point Shard in the Dark Presence's chest, and Hope is able to use the tractor beam to steal a piece of it and take it into the wormhole.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 2[]

Zero Hour[]

The O.X.R. rifted back to the Island alongside the allies across the Omniverse, lending their Choppas to ground and allied troops to enable an air assault against the Dark Presence. After the Dark Presence was finally defeated and Oninoshima restored, the status of the O.X.R. became uncertain following the apparent extinction of the Onimorphs.
