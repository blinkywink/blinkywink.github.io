War Effort was a group founded by J.B. Chimpanski to fight against The Cube Queen's army and act as a resistance. They use Donation Stations to collect fund from Loopers.

Overview

Overview[]

The War Effort place their Donation Stations in Named Locations, and let the Loopers make a choice on what to unvault. The first and the 5th rounds didn't have a Weapons or Items, but had Turret Stations and Salvaged B.R.U.T.E.s per location.

It is unknown if War Effort is related or is allies to the Imagined Order or The Seven.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

War Effort is founded by J.B. Chimpanski to fight against The Cube Queen's army. They first deploy Turret Stations around the Island which require Bars to construct. On October 12th, The final Turret at Craggy Cliffs was successfully funded and new ones have been placed for Shockwave Launcher and Rift-To-Go. The Shockwave Launcher wins, and new ones for the Combat Assault Rifle and Combat SMG get placed. The Combat Assault Rifle wins and new ones for the Boogie Bomb and the Combat Shotgun get placed. The Combat Shotgun moves, and as a final push J.B. Chimpanski decides to unvault the B.R.U.T.E.s from salvaged parts. They also construct Guava Fort.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

There are no signs of War Effort in Artemis after their failure to defend Apollo in The End.
