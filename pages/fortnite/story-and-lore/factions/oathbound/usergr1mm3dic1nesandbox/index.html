Phases

Phases[]

The Shadow Queen does not immediately attack the player. Instead, she wanders around Shadow Sanctuary; waiting for the player to attack her first.

The Shadow Queen wandering.

The Shadow Queen's fight is split into three phases upon triggering the fight:

Phase One: DEFEAT THE SHADOWS![]

“
A mortal? How obscure. Let us dance, my darlings!
”
— The Shadow Queen

The Shadow Queen teleports to her throne, summoning a protective shield and watching over the battle. She summons five non-player Shadows to attack the player. During this phase, a maximum of five Shadows can appear at a time and more will spawn to replace any that have been eliminated. Five of them need to be eliminated to progress into the next phase.

The Shadow Queen summoning a protective shield.

Phase Two: RESIST THE DARKNESS![]

“
A strong warrior always has a strong mind. Now prove it..
”
— The Shadow Queen

The Shadow Queen summons even more Shadows during this phase. This time summoning 10 non-player Shadows with even stronger health. If a player is eliminated by The Shadows, they will be rebooted as a Shadow themself, playing the rest of the match as one.

If the player is successful in defeating the first 10 Shadows, the Shadow Queen will summon 5 more. During this phase, a maximum of 15 Shadows (including Shadowified Players) can appear. 13 of them need to be eliminated to progress into the final phase.


Phase Three: DEFEAT THE QUEEN![]

“
Impressive.. now let's see how you do against a monarch.
”
— The Shadow Queen

The Shadow Queen enters the battle and attacks the player while alternating between her Shadow Rifle and Scythe to defeat the player. During this phase, The Shadow Queen has the following abilities:

Summoning Shadows

The Shadow Queen summons 3 Shadows to aid her in the beginning of the battle, and towards the ending of the battle. The maximum Shadows that spawn during this phase is 6.


Shadow Magic Health Siphon


The Shadow Queen unleashes spider-like tendrils; Latching onto nearby players, stealing 50 Health in increments of 5 over 10 seconds.

Once she is defeated, a giant shockwave is sent through Shadow Sanctuary. Eliminating all Shadows still alive within the sanctum.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Shadow Queen is the largest out of all the bosses on the island. Having similar height to Cube Assassin.

The Rebels is an organization in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was first introduced in Chapter 5: Season 4.

They're a group of vigilantes against The Shadow Empire before being disbanded after the Shadow Empire collapsed and scattered. They have several scattered hideouts around the Island as well as a secret camp on the Island. They are the main protagonists of Chapter 5: Season 4.

Overview

Overview[]

The Rebels was originally The Unseen; a group of vigilantes led by Highwire. The group was ambushed after the arrival of The Shadow Empire at the beginning of Chapter 5: Season 4, and Highwire and Renzo the Destroyer went missing. They reformed under a new alias - The Rebels - and set a new goal: to take down The Shadow Empire. The Rebels caused various chaos and mayhem across the island as a distraction from their real plan.
