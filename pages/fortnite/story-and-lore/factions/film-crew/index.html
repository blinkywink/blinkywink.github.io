The Film Crew refers to the organization in Fortnite: Battle Royale making movies for Risky Reels.

Overview

Overview[]

The Film Crew is composed of actors, directors, camera operators, stage hands and more. They produce movies on the Fortnite map to be played at Risky Reels.
