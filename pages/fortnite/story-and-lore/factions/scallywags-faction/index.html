The Scallywags is a feared crew of pirates led by the dreaded captain of the stormy seas: Blackheart. They were first introduced during Season 8.

Overview

Overview[]

The Scallywags is a famous crew of pirates composed of Blackheart the captain, at least 5 other members in addition to several pirate Fishsticks. The crew first arrived on Athena during Season 8, in search of treasure, but were opposed by both The Ice King and The Prisoner. They became recurring characters throughout the seasons and chapters.

History

History[]

Season 8[]

  • Blackheart's Crew arrive at Athena, seeking a treasure and turn Lazy Lagoon into a pirate town. They set up Pirate Camps around the Island. However they were opposed by The Ice King and The Prisoner.

Season 9[]

  • The crew decided to remain on The Island, still at Lazy Lagoon.

Season X[]

  • A wanted poster for Blackheart can be found at Tilted Town.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Blackheart takes over Viking Vessel and has a rivalry with Ragnarok.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Following the destabilizations created by Reality Tree, The Daily Bloomgle sapling transformed The Daily Bugle into Lazy Lagoon, making Blackheart, his crew along with their Driftwood ship to return on the Island.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • The Driftwood was lifted as Chrome took over Lazy Lagoon. And moved all over the Island, near Flutter Barn then to the bay in the southwest corner of the Island, to the east of Fort Jonesy before moving to the east of Herald's Sanctum, along with The Flairship, to which the ship was attached by ziplines. However during Fracture Live Event, The Driftwood along with Lustrous Lagoon were destroyed by The Herald.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

  • The Scallywags arrived at Helios Island, docking The Driftwood near a cave in the east of the Island, which they transformed into a secret base to hide their treasure. It seems they also helped Jack Sparrow.

OG Chapter 1: Season 8[]

  • Alternate universe versions of The Scallywags appear in Reality: Unknown alongside The Brood under the command of Pyreheart as apart of his Crew while taking the role of The Fire Kingdom.
