The Lockjaw is a Vehicle in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced in Chapter 5: Season 3 through Car Vaults at Nitrodrome and Redline Rig, and patrolling around the Island. It is prefixed with mods and cannot be Modded.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Lockjaw is a 4 wheeled, high-speed car with 2 seats, 2 windows and a windshield. It is fueled with Nitro, granting it infinite fuel.

It features a quick drifting allowing it to perform rapid 360s. Its high acceleration can be combined with its fuel boost; when activated, the Lockjaw can surpass its standard speed limit.

The vehicle spawns with 1,330 HP (Solo only). Once its health bar has been emptied, it will enter the vehicle death state and explode after a few seconds (or instantly, if damaged during the death state), leaving a wreckage prop and starting a Fire. It can be healed via Campfire, Slurp Cactus, Service Station or by using a Repair Torch.

It has access to a honk and Radio Stations.

Obtained

Obtained[]

As of Update v30.00, the Lockjaw can be found in both stationary locations and around the Island, depending on where The Machinist spawned. Ringmaster Scarr's Lockjaw will always spawn at Nitrodrome's Car Vault. A Medallion is required to access the car if it has spawned inside the vault.

History

History[]

  • C5S3

Update v30.00[]

  • Introduced the Lockjaw and its variants.
  • May 28th 2024: Lockjaw no longer is indestructible while the Car Vault isn't opened.
    • This is a fix to an issue where the garage gate could unintentionally be opened with a War Bus. By doing so, it was possible to take the car in an indestructible mode.
      • With this resolution, however, another problem has arisen. It is now possible to damage the bosses' vehicles from outside the cage using the Pickaxe.
  • May 30th 2024:
    • Lockjaw's Machine Gun Turrets accuracy reduced.
    • Lockjaw's health reduced from 1,615 to 1,330 in Solos.
    • Lockjaw's health don't automatically regen anymore.

Update v30.10[]

  • Lockjaw is now marked on the player's map when nearby.

Update v30.20[]

  • July 4th 2024: Vaulted the Lockjaw in Ranked Mode.
