Rezz is a type of pure light energy in Fortnite that was first witnessed in Save the World before being properly introduced in other modes. This energy allows matter to be decomposed, altered or recomposed via morphite.

Overview

Overview[]

The Rezz is light energy appearing as blue cubes that can reassemble or disassemble any form of matter, including living beings at the molecular level. When an object or person is in the process of disassembling or completely disassembled into morphite, this energy is also called De-Rezz, same when it is used offensively to disassemble something or someone forcefully.

The Rezz energy is the truth behind the "resurrection" system of the Reboot Vans as well as the energy used by the Spawn Drone, in order to deploy or retrieve people from an area by Homebase and the Imagined Order. This energy was one of the main component of The Loop, allowing to resurrect all the killed Loopers.

Known Users

Known Users[]

Certain characters such as The Looper, Geno and Lightrider have acquired the ability to use this energy for various purposes.

User Information
The Looper 		Along with the Sky Fire team, they used the Rezz energy to reboot Bluevin the Cube, and they are also able to use Rezz energy to manipulate a vehicle's appearance to their whim.
Geno 		After being discorporated by The Zero Point with every molecule in his body sent to a different reality, Geno was able to recompose himself, teleporting and reassembling every molecule in his own body until he reformed entirely.
Lightrider 		She possesses the intrinsic ability to generate pure Rezz Energy which she uses to form objects including a board made of pure Rezz energy, allowing her to move at dizzying speeds and in any direction.
Reboot Man 		As the name suggests, he can act as a Reboot Van, i.e he can restore any deceased individual. This process also applies to himself. His body can take a partially or completely "digitized" form.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Before being given a proper name, this power was called Reboot by the community.
  • The name "Rezz" is derived from the long-standing gaming slang "rez", a clipped form of revive or resurrect used when a knocked player asks teammates to revive them "rez me".
  • When something is restored by Rezz energy, it is said to have been "Rebooted" or "Reloaded".
  • The use of Rezz energy when knocking out or killing a character in-game or in cinematics serves as a censorship to avoid corpses and blood.
    • However, this use is not canon to the story as revealed in Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point, Batman/Fortnite: Foundation and Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War, where corpses and blood remains.
