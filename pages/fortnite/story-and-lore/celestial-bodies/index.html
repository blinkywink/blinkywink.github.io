The Ice Moon:[]

The Ice Moon is a moon outside Reality Zero and the home of The Ice King. It is an extremely hostile environment covered in Ice Storm cast by The Ice King to make it inhospitable to living and unliving beings, all of this to isolate it completely for all time.

Astroworld:[]

Astroworld was a dystopian dwarf planet that was seen in the sky coming closer and closer to the island in Chapter 2: Season 2.

On April 23rd 2020 Astroworld made it to Apollo. A purple comet-looking thing came out of it's core, summoning a giant Travis Scott, starting the Astronomical event

The Moon:[]

The Moon is a celestial object created by The Zero Point and is in a constant cycle with the sun

In Chapter 4: Season 4, Kado Thorne used his sionic powers to trigger Eclipses despite them being impossible, as both the Sun and Moon within Asteria were fake.

In-Game during the day, The Eclipse was only visible at 9AM-11AM. During the night, the red moon is always visible.

Meteors:[]

The first Meteor was an object was first spotted in the sky during Season 3. struck Dusty Depot during Season 4 and returned as an unnamed POI located just above the Factories near Dusty Depot in Season X.

Meteors like this one, the one back in Season 4, and the one that would come in the Zero Crisis Finale are used as vehicles for The Seven.

Save The World Earth:[]

A long time ago, multiple purple Motherships of the Army of the Last Reality crashed on the planet. Later, the cubes from the crashed motherships summoned The Storm. And one day, The Storm mysteriously vanished; the plan seems to have failed for some unknown reason.

Most, if not all of the Motherships' Cubes evolved into Storm Kings and summoned The Storm once again, wiping 98% of the world's population, turning them into zombies known as Husks, and taking over Save the World Reality's Earth.
