Power of Creation (also known as Zero Point Energy) is a lore concept in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It is a type of pure energy originating from The Zero Point and considered the most dangerous force in all of existence. In Reality: 783, it is considered as a form of magic, being the force behind it.

Capabilities

Capabilities[]

  • Reality Warping: Users can produce something either out of nothing or from existing sources, bending reality to their will. This ability can take many forms, from simple elements and substances to complex materials and objects, whether conceptual or sentient.
    • Energy Generation: Users can create energy of presumably every type.
    • Soul Creation: Users can fabricate entirely new souls, like The Zero Point did after forming The Omniverse.
    • Life Creation: Using the Power of Creation, users can create entirely new lifeforms.
    • Reality Creation: Users can construct entirely new realities.
    • Creation & Destruction: This power is equally capable of creating and destroying, reducing everything to nothing.
    • Gigantification: When within people, the energy can transform people into giants.
  • Loop Immunity: This power can also protect users from the effects of The Loop.
Limitations

Limitations[]

Too much exposure to power of creation could lead to losing consciousness or death. After Zero Crisis Finale, The Foundation almost died inside the Zero Point but his suit saved him. If this power is left unchecked or misused, it can wreak havoc, potentially destroying entire realities.
