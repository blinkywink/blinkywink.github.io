Overview

Slurp is a blue substance best known for its healing capabilities and shield conjuring effects. It is a recurring substance in Fortnite.

Lore

Lore[]

Slurp comes in many forms, from juicy drinks to buff humanoid god. Man-made slurp usually comes from Slurp Co. factory. Natural slurp seems to be related to The Zero Point since Bouncy Slurpshrooms can be found growing near the its flowing energy. There is also an eons years old god named Poseidon who is made of slurp.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Slurp is usually seen in glowing blue color with a mix of light green.

Capabilities

Capabilities[]

  • Healing injuries.
  • Shield conjuring.
  • Growing and sustaining plants.
  • Vessels for lifeforms.
