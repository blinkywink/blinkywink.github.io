Overview

A list of organizations, groups and companies that appear in Fortnite: Battle Royale and Fortnite: Save the World's Lore.

Lore Organizations

Lore Organizations[]

Organization Leader Status Description
Imagined Order Disbanded An organization that controls The Loop from both outside and inside. They want at all costs found the perfect organizational system for the perfect civilization. They were defeated by The Seven and The Resistance.
The Seven Active A very powerful group of 7 people with the aim of liberating The Zero Point and taking down the entirety of the I.O. After suceeding in doing so, most members were destroyed by The Last Reality. 4 years later, the surviving ones started to slowly regroup, becoming The New Seven.
The Last Reality Active A chaotic alien organization that invade and destroy realities so only The Nothing remains. They managed to destroy Reality Zero during Fracture, but it was rebuilt through Zero Fusion.
The Government Inactive An agency that investigates the strange sightings on the island and fixes messes after various disasters.
Expedition Active A military and arctic research organization that set up Expedition Outposts around Athena. One of their major labs later ended up on Asteria due to Zero Fusion. They developed the first A.I.M. robots.
The Ice Kingdom Active A kingdom led by The Ice King originating from The Ice Moon, they played a very important role in the events of late Chapter 1, and later tried to establish a rulership in Reality: 266.
The Fire Kingdom Disbanded A kingdom ruled by The Prisoner that brought the Volcano and the Jungle Biome to Athena.
Scallywags Active (Left the island) A group of Pirates that somehow found their way onto the Island.
Midas' Crew Unknown (Presumed Disbanded) Formerly known as The Agency, it's a spy and military criminal organization divided into two factions lead by Midas. They act as mercenaries although it's not through their sole activity.
Spire Cult Inactive A cult of people that revere the Spire. They were manipulated by the I.O so that no one else takes control of Zero Point.
War Effort Disbanded An army led by J.B. Chimpanski that tried to defend the Island. They were wiped out by the The Last Reality, with the four remaining loopers were saved by The Scientist and The Visitor.
The Resistance Disbanded A large faction composed of the loopers freed from the Loop by the Seven, a division of The Seven founded to fight back against the Imagined Order.
Oathbound Unknown An empire led by a young Snapshot of Geno. The earliest version of the Imagined Order.
The High Stakes Club Active A group of teenage Vampire Hunters who consider themselves family.

Cold Blooded
(formerly the Getaway Gang) 		Unknown Group of skilled criminals with a network of vaults stashed full of powerful weapons and bars in MEGA City, Shattered Slabs, Brutal Bastion and formerly Faulty Splits.
Most Wanted Disbanded An alliance of criminals and former operatives with the aim of robbing all of Cold Blooded's vaults.
Peace Syndicate Unknown (Presumed Active) A powerful and well-known Syndicate made to keep the peace, lead by X.
Fox Clan Unknown (Presumed Active) A Syndicate of skilled trackers and hunters operating in MEGA City and historical rivals of the River Guard.
River Guard Unknown (Presumed Active) A Syndicate of well-trained warriors operating in Kenjutsu Crossing and historical rivals of the Fox Clan.
The Luminaries Unknown (Presumed Active) Syndicate of intelligent engineers operating in MEGA City, allies with the Peace Syndicate.
The Unseen Unknown (Presumed Active) A hostile Syndicate causing chaos wherever they go, regardless of hurting innocent people.
The Triarchs Disbanded The Triarchs are the triarchy rulers of a unnamed peaceful kingdom from a realm that stretched across the stars.
The Explorers Active Allies with The Luminaries and Operating in Creeky Compound.
The Ancients Unknown A mysterious organization of people who once lived in the jungle that suddenly disappeared.
Eclipse Unknown (Presumed Disbanded) They are an estate agency owned by the rich and wealthy vampire Kado Thorne.
The Heist Crew Unknown (Presumed Disbanded) The Heist Crew is a crew of robbers brought together by Nolan Chance.
The Society Disbanded (As a criminal organization, Active as a company) A group of corrupt wealthy elites lead by their founder Montague and bosses Valeria, Nisha, Oscar and Peter Griffin. The group uses healing medallions that harnesses ancient power from The Pantheon.
The Underground Disbanded The Underground is a group of rebel founded and lead by Hope. They wanted to completely disband The Society by defeating all of their bosses, take down Zeus for misusing his power, stop Megalo Don from taking over the Island, and stop Doom from turning the island into his new Latveria.
The Pantheon Disbanded The Greek Pantheon from the myths and legends, lead by Zeus as their King before being dethroned by the combined efforts of the gods, mortalkind and The Oracle.
Wasteland Warriors Unknown (Presumed Disbanded) A group of marauders powered by Nitro within a sandstorm biome. Lead by Megalo Don, a tyrant that wishes to raze the Island with his Leviathan ship.
Demon Warriors Defeated The main antagonists of Chapter 6, they are a group of demonic oni from The Spirit Realm who arrived to Reality: 783 after a shard of The Zero Point landed on Oninoshima awakening magic on the island, while also opening portals to The Spirit Realm setting the demons free. They were led by Shogun X in Chapter 6: Season 1 but later it was revealed that a strange malevolent Dark Presence is their actual ruler. He, along with the rest of the demons was defeated during Zero Hour.
Demon Hunters Disbanded The main protagonists of Chapter 6, an unnamed and unofficial alliance formed by people investigating about the return of magic and Demons, other supernatural threats and strangers in Reality: 783, which later evolved into The Supernova Academy.
Alpha Disbanded An organization of criminals lead by the wealthy kingpin Fletcher Kane.
The Elites Active An organization of mercenaries made up of elite fighters from various realities, the best of the best defined as "Fast, sharp, able to face a hundred foes and come out on top".
The Supernovas Unknown (Presumed Active) An alliance of super-powered individuals under the guidance of Hope and Jones, and mentors, The Night Rose, Robin and Superman.
O.X.R. Unknown (Presumed Defunct) An anti-bug military organization under the guidance of Onyx Winter who defend Oninoshima from the bug invasion.
InnoLoop Active A high-tech company capable of making particle accelerators. Powers the Golden Coast island with energy from the Zero Point Shard.
