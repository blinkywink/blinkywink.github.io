The Omniverse

Overview

This page may contain speculation and/or fan interpretations.
Due to the lack of official information, some info on this page may therefore be partially incorrect.

“
Each its own universe... Its own Multiverse.

The Omniverse is the connection of the various Realities and Universes connected together by the Zero Point, within the story of Fortnite: Battle Royale. The existence of other Universe in connection to Battle Royale was first mentioned in Season 4, with the Blast Off Live Event.

Description

Description[]

The Omniverse is the connection of the various and infinite Realities connected together by The Zero Point. Different Realities have shown to be accessible via The Zero Point, as well as the Portal Device and other forms of interdimensional travel, such as Rockets. As previously mentioned, The Omniverse was first mentioned with the Blast Off Live Event, in which The Visitor's rocket caused a Sideways Rift, linking other dimensions to Fortnite: Battle Royale's world.

The Omniverse was later expanded upon Season X with Rift Zones, as well as in Chapter 2: Season 5 with Agent Jones linking various Realities together to obtain Hunters and to stop Loopers exiting The Loop. Other Seasons such as Chapter 2: Season 4 also had ties to the Omniverse. Ever since, The Omniverse has continued to grow, with an increasing amount of Collaborations between Fortnite and other media.

Known Realities

Known Realities[]

Below is a list of all known Realities/Universes which directly link to Fortnite: Battle Royale. These Realities are both media which exist in the real world, the real world itself, and fictional Universes created solely for Fortnite itself.

It is important to note that very few Universes connected to Fortnite actually show the characters being removed from their respective Universes. Only the Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Comic Book Series, and the Nexus War characters are physically taken from their world into Fortnite: Battle Royale, in their respective media. As such, all other versions are listed as "Alternate" Universes.

Reality Zero[]

Reality Zero[1] is the Reality in which Fortnite: Battle Royale takes place in. The game has always taken place here, in three Chapters of the game (the Athena, Apollo and Artemis Islands). This Reality acts as a hub world, with most crossovers taking place here, courtesy of The Zero Point.

The In-Between[]

The In-Between is a hub-like Reality that connects to multiple different Realities in The Omniverse. It was first seen during the The Butterfly Event, and has made one appearance afterwards, in The Devourer of Worlds Live Event.

The Sideways[]

A Reality where The Last Reality originated from, which houses The Cube Queen who destroys Realities with her army. The Cube's Cradle is a planet-like ship from The Sideways that is currently located in the Space.


Save The World[]

Save the World is a Reality taken over by The Storm and corrupted by The Storm King.
