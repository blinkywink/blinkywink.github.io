Pure Portals are a type of Rifts in Fortnite linked to and created by The Zero Point that allow characters to travel to any realities.

Overview

Overview[]

Pure Portals are cracks through reality allowing travel in space, time or other realities. Pure Portals are a method of travelling realities by directly or indirectly using The Zero Point, which open a portal tied to all realities. Unlike Sideways Rift, which take time to heal, Pure Portals can be closed at the source directly, like Batman did with Gotham City's Rift.

Pure Portals, often cause significant space-time problems, notably by randomly opening smaller Sideways Rifts taking and bringing objects, places or people and making them travel to other Realities.

Much like Sideways Rifts, Pure Portals have a gravitational pull.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Pure Portals usually appear as large crack, breaking the fabric of reality exactly like Sideways Rift. They usually comes in bright blue and pink color. However, they can also appear under the form of Rift Gates, notably when opened by Portal Device.

Season X[]

The Scientist's Rift Beacons opened several Pure Portals. Those created Rift Zones.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

They appeared around the map after Agent Jones used his Portal Device to 'hire' Hunters from other realities and put them in The Loop.
