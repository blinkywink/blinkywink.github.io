Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Brazillian version of the game also features voice actors that voice characters outside of the main storyline such as:
    • In Ana Paula Cadamuro, Carlos Seidl and Wellington Lima voice trailer narrators, as well as Beatriz Meinberg and Francisco Júnior, as their respective characters;
    • Fred Mascarenhas voices Claptrap;
    • Ana Paula Cadamuro, Rebeca Zadra, Emicida, Marcelo Salsicha, Marco Antônio Abreu and Vany Américo voice the broadcasters for the Party Royale, Radio Yonder, Beat Box, Power Play, Radio Underground, and Rock & Royale radio stations, respectively.
    • Bianca Lua voices the reporter seem in the trailer for Chapter 2: Season 7;
    • Reginaldo Primo voices Guy in the Free Guy ATM video pop-ups;
    • Felipe Zilse and Marcos Jardym voice The Mandalorian and Obi-Wan Kenobi in the This Is The Way and Obi-Wan's Message emotes, respectively.
