Overview

This article is about the quest item from Chapter 4: Season 3 and Chapter 4: Season OG. For the quest item from Season X, see Visitor Tapes. For the quest item from Chapter 3: Season 1 and Chapter 2 Remix, see Audio Logs. For the quest item from Chapter Seven: Season 1, see Mission Logs.

“
It's Slone. Day number... (exhausted sigh) Still in the jungle..
”
— Slone

Audio Recorders and Lab Reports are interactable items that can be found in many locations in Chapter 4: Season 3 and Chapter 4: Season OG. It replays Doctor Slone's audio logs.
