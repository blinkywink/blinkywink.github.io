Mysterious Transmission

Mysterious Transmission

Details

Debut

Chapter 4: Season 3

Overview

Mysterious Transmission is a special Radio Station in Fortnite: Battle Royale that plays storyline related voiceover. It was added in Chapter 4: Season 3 and re-introduced in Chapter 5: Season 3.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In June 19 2024, during Update v30.10 Epic Games accidentally added Megalo Don's voicelines for The Wanderer and Pandora Gems early for most international dubs except english.
    • Most of these voicelines were later released in english.
    • However, two of these voicelines ended up going unreleased for some reason, despite their importance regarding The Wanderer[1] and the lore of the Wasteland Warriors and Pandora Gems[2]. With the warning of The Wanderer now being said by Hope.
