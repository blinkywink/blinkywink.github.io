Containment Specialist is an Uncommon Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks or with the Ghostbusters Crew for 2,000 V-Bucks. Containment Specialist was first released in Chapter 2: Season 4 and is part of the Ghostbusters Set.
