The Counting Stars Bundle is an Epic Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,800 V-Bucks. The Counting Stars Bundle was first released in Chapter 6: Season 3 and contains Astra with the Counting Stars Jam Track.
