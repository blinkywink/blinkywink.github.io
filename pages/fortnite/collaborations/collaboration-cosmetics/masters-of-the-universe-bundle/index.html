The Masters of the Universe Bundle is an Epic Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 3,600 V-Bucks. The Masters of the Universe Bundle was first released in Chapter 7: Season 3 and contains cosmetics from the Masters of the Universe Set.
