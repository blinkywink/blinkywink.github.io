Outfit[]

“
You see...a Batman who laughs...is a Batman who always wins. HAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!
”
— In-Game Description

The Batman Who Laughs is a DC Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with The Batman Who Laughs Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. The Batman Who Laughs was first released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and is part of the Dark Multiverse Set.

The Batman Who Laughs can also be obtained by redeeming a code found in a copy of Batman/Fortnite: Foundation for $4.99.

Section

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, The Batman Who Laughs's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Icon Featured

Prior to the release of this LEGO® Style in Update v33.20, equipping The Batman Who Laughs in LEGO Fortnite would instead equip a random default outfit's LEGO® style.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for The Batman Who Laughs's appearances?
The Batman Who Laughs has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Bruce Wayne, commonly known as The Batman Who Laughs and formerly Batman is a multiversal threat and killer from Earth -22. He worked with the IO and Doctor Slone to plant her anchor device, but actually used them to reach Reality Zero and use The Zero Point to corrupt different Realities.

He is a supporting antagonist in Fortnite, serving as one of the overarching antagonists of the Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point comics, and the main antagonist of the Batman/Fortnite: Foundation comic.

Personality

Personality[]

The Batman Who Laughs is maniacal and sadistic, despite being very smart and tactical, just like his Prime Counterpart. He enjoys killing his enemies in brutal ways and always has a smile while doing so.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point[]

The Batman Who Laughs mysteriously returned to life, although without his omnipotence obtained as the Darkest Knight. At an unknown point in time, he, Lex Luthor and Deathstroke would ally with Doctor Slone. The Imagined Order sought to regain control of the Zero Point, and in exchange for their help they would offer them an access to it. Slone, Laughs and Lex would then establish a plan, recruiting other villains in the process such as Harley Quinn. The plan involved using Batman, the "world's greatest detective" by trapping him in The Loop. The plan was for him to escape, in needed time and join Deathstroke, who would be already outside thanks to a special device and who had a special anchor device on himself.

The plan was to use Batman to manage to infiltrate the Bridge, and free the Zero Point from The Spire. Allowing then Deathstroke to plant the anchor and return. However, knowing Bruce Wayne, the Batman Who Laughs added the idea to use Catwoman, to give him an emotional support, knowing that without he may not succeed. Once the time came, they opened a Pure Portal type of Rift in Gotham City, and Deathstroke attacked Batman from behind, trapping him in Reality Zero, while Slone, Lex and Laughs would watch and wait from far away, in Metropolis.

After the success of the mission and the return of Deathstroke, he and the others would discuss the merit and contribution of each, before coming to their reward. Doctor Slone answers them that it already started, with a Sideways Rift leading to Reality Zero being opened in Metropolis.

Batman/Fortnite: Foundation[]

Lex Luthor with his special armor and The Batman Who Laughs, then recruited massive amount of villains, in order to get to the Sideways Rift and join Reality Zero, however The entire Justice League and other heroes (except Batman, who was on his way to Metropolis) got in their way, creating a gigantic confrontation zone between Heroes and Villains.

When Batman arrived alongside an unnexpected "ally": The Foundation, Laughs jammed Batman's comms before taking his appearance, in order to trick The Foundation into leading him to the Rift and managing to enter it, alongside The Foundation.

After a fierce confrontation with Foundation, trying to get him in his worst fears by breaking the sealed environment of his armor thus condemning Foundation to be trapped in The Loop next round, he was ultimately killed, while Foundarion was at the center of The Storm allowing the later to escape The Loop. The next Loop, however, The Batman Who Laughs gloats and compares his acting to that of a soccer player, he then reveals he still has his memories because of his visor made of the World Forge, a metal having forged Realities, and therefore similar enough to the Zero Point to protect him from memory loss of The Loop. The evil Batman then sets his sight to the Zero Point, which he wants to seize in order to corrupt the entire Omniverse, as he got bored of Realities of same multiverses.
