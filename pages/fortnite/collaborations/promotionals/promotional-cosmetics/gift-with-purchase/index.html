Gift-with-Purchase

Overview

Gift-with-Purchase is a system in Fortnite on the Epic Games Store that rewards players with cosmetics, or V-Bucks if they already own the promoted item or bought it from the Item Shop previously. These are typically collaboration-driven.

Trivia[]

  • On January 23rd, 2026, Epic Games general manager Steve Allison announced a plan to expand Gift-with-Purchase to over a hundred collaborations per year.[4] This does not mean that all gaming collaborations will be part of this plan however, as neither Solo Leveling:ARISE, PEAK, or Overwatch were.
