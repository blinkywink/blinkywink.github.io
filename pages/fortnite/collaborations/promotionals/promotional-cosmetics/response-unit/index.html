Response Unit is a Rare Back Bling in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with Reflex for 1,200 V-Bucks. Response Unit was first released in Season 7 and is part of the Counterattack Set.

Response Unit could have also been purchased with the GeForce Fortnite Bundle 1 prior to its release in the Item Shop.
