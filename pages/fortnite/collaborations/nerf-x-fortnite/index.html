Overview

NERF x Fortnite is a series of real world promotional products produced by NERF. The series includes Fortnite: Battle Royale weaponry made into Toy Blasters, as well as Fortnite themed MicroShots, and Super Soakers.

List of Products

List of Products[]

As the series has been produced since very early in Fortnite's lifecycle, many weapons are produced using the Chapter 1 visuals as reference. Many of the following weapons use initials to refer to them, as NERF weaponry does not use weapon classifications due to the products being produced for those under 18 years of age.
