The KPop Demon Hunters Cup was a Duo Build Tournament for Fortnite: Battle Royale.

Points System

Points System[]

For the Victory Royale, 7 points are rewarded. Every Elimination grants a player 2 point, in addition to giving points from position 50th. See all of them on the Official Rules page.
