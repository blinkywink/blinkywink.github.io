Overview

With both Beast Boy and Raven dropping onto the Island, you knew it wasn’t long before Deathstroke arrived…
”
— Official Blog

The Deathstroke Zero Cup was a Solos Tournament for Fortnite: Battle Royale and was part of a collaboration with another DC Comic Character - Deathstroke. It was available on June 1st, 2021.

Points System

Points System[]

For the Victory Royale, 30 points are rewarded. Every Elimination grants a player 1 point. Other placements range from 1 to 25 points. See all of them on the Official Rules page.
