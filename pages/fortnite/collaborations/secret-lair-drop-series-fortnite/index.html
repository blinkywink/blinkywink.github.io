Secret Lair Drop Series: Fortnite is the name of the Collaboration between Secret Lair (the "sub-brand" of Magic: The Gathering), and Fortnite. The collaboration entailed fourteen different cards in two different drops from Secret Lair, with each card being a reskin of a preexisting one.

The two sets released were Fortnite and Fortnite: Landmarks and Locations.
