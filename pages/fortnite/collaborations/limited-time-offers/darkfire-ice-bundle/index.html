Overview

The Darkfire & Ice Bundle is a Legendary Limited Time Offer in Fortnite that can be obtained by purchasing The Physical Card/Box for Nintendo, PlayStation or Xbox for $29.99. Darkfire & Ice Bundle was released in Chapter 7: Season 1.
