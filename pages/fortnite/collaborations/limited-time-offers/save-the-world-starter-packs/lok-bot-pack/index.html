The Lok-Bot Pack is a Rare Limited Time Offer in Fortnite, that can be purchased for $15.99. The Lok-Bot Pack was first released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and contains Lok-Bot with his cosmetics from the Lok-Bot Set along with Save the World and Challenges for 1,000 V-Bucks and Lok-Bot Constructor Hero.
