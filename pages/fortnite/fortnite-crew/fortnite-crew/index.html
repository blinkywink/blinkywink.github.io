Fortnite Crew

Description

Type

Subscription Service

Overview

Fortnite Crew is a monthly subscription service for Fortnite costing $11.99 per month that grants multiple rewards. The offer was first given on December 2nd 2020 - with the start of Chapter 2: Season 5.

Since November 18th 2025, the Xbox Game Pass Ultimate subscription service also included Fortnite Crew.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Overview[]

Items included in the Fortnite Crew package include:

  • The month's featured Crew Series Outfit and their matching cosmetics
  • 1,000 V-Bucks
  • The current Battle Pass (C7S2)
  • The current OG Pass (C1S8)
  • The current Music Pass (S14)
  • The current LEGO Pass (SS)
  • The current Rocket Pass (S22)

The cosmetics are all exclusive to the Fortnite Crew, but can return in future Crew Packs.

Be aware that upon purchase, 1,000 V-Bucks will be instantly provided and upon the next month of Fortnite Crew you will not get your V-Bucks until the day your subscription renews. For all other rewards, they are provided as soon as the season shifts over.

The Rocket Pass began being included with Fortnite Crew in April 2023, while the OG Pass, Music Pass, and LEGO Pass began being included in December 2024.

Subscription System[]

For new subscribers beginning December 1st 2024, at 5 AM GMT/UTC, premium rewards on the Battle Pass will only be available to claim if their Fortnite Crew subscription is active. This means that if a player cancels their Crew subscription, they will be able to claim premium rewards from the Battle Pass until the end of their current billing period but not for the remaining duration of the pass.

For players who are currently subscribed to Fortnite Crew or subscribe before December 1st 2024, there will be no change to how they claim premium rewards on the Battle Pass. If a player cancels their subscription after December 1st 2024, and resubscribes after it has lapsed, they will be considered a new subscriber, and claiming premium rewards on the Battle Pass will require an active Fortnite Crew subscription.

Pass Rewards[]

An active Fortnite Crew subscription is required to unlock and claim premium rewards on the Battle Pass, LEGO Pass, OG Pass and Music Pass for both new and existing subscribers. If a user's subscription ends, they will no longer be able to unlock premium rewards unless they either renew their subscription or purchase the pass seperately.

If a player purchases the Battle Pass, LEGO Pass, OG Pass or Music Pass separately from Fortnite Crew, they will still be able to unlock and claim premium rewards on those passes individually as they normally would.

One-time Refunds[]

If a player subscribes to Fortnite Crew within 14 days after purchasing a pass, they will automatically receive a one-time grant of the V-Bucks paid for the pass back. Note that this will also mean the pass will convert to requiring an active subscription to unlock and claim premium rewards.

If a player buys a pass and subscribes to Fortnite Crew more than 14 days after, they will not receive the one-time V-Buck grant, and that pass will not require an active subscription to unlock and claim premium rewards.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Early concepts of Fortnite Crew were the Annual Pass and The Club. The Annual Pass was going to be an annual subscription service that grants players 7,200 V-Bucks and 7 Outfits per year. The second concept of Fortnite Crew was The Club, which is similar to the current Fortnite Crew but includes exclusive access to "The Club", a game mode similar to Party Royale.
  • Fortnite Crew has known to crash slightly, so be aware of this issue otherwise you may pay twice.
  • 2024 is the only year to not have a new Crew Legacy set released besides the Crew Legacy Styles, besides 2020.
  • As of June 6th 2026, the amount of V-Bucks included with Fortnite Crew will be decreace from 1,000 to 800.
