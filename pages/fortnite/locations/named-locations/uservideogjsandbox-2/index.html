Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

For information on The Location during Chapter 2: Season 4 , see Doom's Domain.

Pleasant Park was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced in Season 1, and returned in Chapter 2: Season 1 on the new island. It was removed with the launch of Chapter 3: Season 1.

Map Changes

Map Changes[]

  • Chapter 1
  • Chapter 2

Season 1[]

Pleasant Park was added to the island.

Season 7[]

The Showtime Event took place at Pleasant Park.

Season 8[]

The white house with a fallen tree has been renovated; in addition, bunkers have been added underground under all the houses.

Season 9[]

During The Final Showdown Event, the modern house was destroyed by The Devourer.

Season X[]

Pleasant Park, along with every other location, was sucked into a black hole during The End event.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

In Chapter 2 - Season 1, despite the old map being sucked into a black hole, Pleasant Park survived, and is now placed in the northwest side of the new map.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

SHADOW Safe House - Alpha was added as a landmark under the soccer field and was initially only accessible through Porta-Pottys; however, later in the season the soccer field itself opened up to reveal the Safe House.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

Pleasant Park has been flooded but is protected by No Sweat Insurance. As a result of this, the houses, gazebo, soccer field and other areas of the POI are now on floaties.

On July 18th (stage 4 of the water level), Pleasant Park is no longer flooded. It is now back to its normal state and the houses are no longer on floaties.

In Week 6 of the 13.30 update, the Pass 'N' Gas Station has been removed and replaced by a much larger one, most likely to accommodate for the cars added in Joy Ride Update.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

Doctor Doom has built a Mansion and replaced the central area with a statue of himself. Additionally, the area that was once SHADOW Safe House - Alpha was opened up and now contains a Vault, renaming the POI to Doom's Domain. Despite this, most of Pleasant is unchanged.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Doom’s Domain has been reverted back to Pleasant Park. Everything that had changed upon the start of the season was now all gone. This includes Doctor Doom's Mansion, Doom's statue, and all Doom Henchmen along with their Disguise booths. The soccer field has also closed up, with no way to get inside once again. Additionally, as this season was the introduction of Characters, there was a chance Doggo would spawn here.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Pleasant Park is initially unaffected by the Island's transformation to go partially primal, but it experienced changes throughout the season. Jonesy The First spawns here as an character. Update v16.10: Some of Pleasant Park has been blocked off. The GAS Station is completely abandoned. Update v16.30: Galactico can be found here

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Content Update v17.10 (Week 5) An Alien Artifact is added to the central building of the park area.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Pleasant Park was flipped and submerged from The End (Chapter 2).

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • In the Fracture Live Event,Videogj/Sandbox 2 was destroyed by The Herald offscreen.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Pleasant Park, along with Retail Row were added to the map right from the start of Battle Royale and existed in some form until the end of Chapter 2: Season 8, making them the oldest POIs in Fortnite history.
    • In Chapter 3: Season 1, Pleasant Park was finally removed, marking the first time it was completely absent from the map.
  • There was usually a chest spawn in the middle of the soccer field.
  • In December of 2019, the location became very dusty. Everybody thought that this was part of the storyline, but it turned out to be a Red Herring.
  • There used to be two SHADOW recruitment posters here.
  • In Chapter 1, the Green house was abandoned until Season 8, when it was restored.
    • In Chapter 2, one of the SHADOW posters was on the side of this house.
