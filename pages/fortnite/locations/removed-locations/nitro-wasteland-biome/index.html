Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

The Nitro Wasteland is a biome in Fortnite: Battle Royale that appeared in Chapter 5: Season 3, in the southern part of the Helios island. It is a mythological desert created by the combustion of massive Nitro energy from the Pandora's Box during the Sandstorm Event.
