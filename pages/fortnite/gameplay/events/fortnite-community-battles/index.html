Overview

Fortnite Community Battles are a series of events that take place bimonthly, and allows participants to battle for their chosen team in order to earn rewards such as Sprays, V-Bucks, Emoticons, Banners, Wraps, and Harvesting Tools.

The winning team in each battle is determined based on the amounts of points earned by each team. Team members can earn points by completing in-game actions, which are different for each series of Community Battles. Players are removed as participants if they are inactive for the event.

There have also been similar community events/challenges that had occurred before the official introduction of the Community Battles, these include the Autumn Queen's Quest and the Fishing Frenzy.

Fortnite Community Battles: Bootcamp

Fortnite Community Battles: Bootcamp[]

The first series of Community Battles went live on August 19th 2020 and lasted until August 23rd 2020. This consisted of 2 Daily Challenges, as well as one additional "Creator Challenge" each day.

To progress, players simply had to complete the 2 Daily Challenges, but didn't have to complete the Creator Challenge because it was optional. The faster you completed a Challenge, the more points you received for the leaderboard.

Fortnite Community Battles: Loot Fight

Fortnite Community Battles: Loot Fight[]

The second series of Community Battles went live on September 24th 2020 and ended on September 29th 2020. Players chose between 4 Teams with the Captains being: Fixx, Hey Stan, Standart Skill, and Syou.

Players collected points by completing in-game tasks. The more points they collected, the more Loot became available for their Team's Captain in the Show Match, which is a series of 4 matches.

Fortnite Community Battles Series 3

Fortnite Community Battles Series 3[]

The third series of community battles went live on October 28, 2020, and ended on DATE. The teams consisted of one original Fortnite outfit VS an outfit based on a Marvel character.

The points earned by each team member were determined by the amount of eliminations, Victory Royales and minutes played by each team member.

Rewards[]

The following rewards were available:

  • Be a member of a winning team at least once - I Sits Spray
  • Top 5000 - 200 V-Bucks
  • Top 1000 - 350 V-Bucks
  • Top 100 - 1,000 V-Bucks

Fortnite AFL Community Battles

Fortnite AFL Community Battles[]

The fourth series of community battles went live on January 27, 2021, as a collaboration with the AFL. It lasted until January 31st, 2021. The teams available to choose were the 18 teams from the AFL.

As usual, players were removed as a participant for inactivity - not playing a match within 24 hours of signing up to the tournament.

Points were earned through 2 ways:

  • Completing daily tasks called "fan tasks", which are similar to in-game Quests.
  • Performing in-game actions in any core gamemode (Solo, Duo, Squad), which gave points for each action
    • Make a Victory Royale - 100 Points
    • Eliminate an Enemy - 20 Points
    • Play for a minute - 1 Point

Rewards[]

The following rewards were available:

  • Complete at least one daily task - Punt! Spray
  • Be a member of the team that takes 3rd Place - 200 V-Bucks
  • Be a member of the team that takes 2nd Place - 400 V-Bucks
  • Be a member of the team that takes 1st Place - 800 V-Bucks

Community Battle: Tower Defense

Community Battle: Tower Defense[]

The sixth series of Community Battles went live on January 30th 2023 and ended on Ferbruary 5th 2023. It only featured German content creators and the event took place on a creative map: 7125-8952-9143. However you were able to join from any region.

Players were separated into two teams: one team fought zombies and the other focused on collecting reccources to craft weapons, ammo and traps.

Points were earned by defending the fortress from zombies:

  • 1st Zombie-Wave: +1 Point
  • 2nd Zombie-Wave: +1 Point
  • 3rd Zombie-Wave: +1 Point
  • 4th Zombie-Wave: +2 Points
  • 5th Zombie-Wave: +2 Points
  • 6th Zombie-Wave: +2 Points
  • 7th Zombie-Wave: +3 Points
  • 8th Zombie-Wave: +3 Points
  • 9th Zombie-Wave: +3 Points
  • 10th Zombie-Wave: +5 Points

Players who earned atleast one point would get the Winning Spray. It didn't matter which team or creator you played for.
