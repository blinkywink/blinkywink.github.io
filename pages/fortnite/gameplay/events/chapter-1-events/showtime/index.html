”
— Marshmello

Showtime was a Concert Live Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale that aired on February 2nd 2019 at 11AM PST and the encore on the same day at 11PM PST.

As the songs cycled, the stage got different special effects (anti-gravity, skydiving, etc). It had its own set of challenges.

Buildup

Buildup[]

After the effects of Ice Storm wore off and snow started melting a stage was started being built on the field in Pleasant Park.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Showtime was the first Concert Live Event to take place in Fortnite.
  • Marshmello was the second player-sized character to be featured in a live event. First being The Ice King in Ice Storm.
  • Marshmello himself stated all the lines he said during the concert were not pre-recorded and instead were broadcasted live to the concert attendees during the event.
  • Showtime is the first Live Event to not be in Fortnite OG.
