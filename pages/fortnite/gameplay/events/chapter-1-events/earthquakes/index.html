Ice Storm OG Chapter 1: Season 8
The Earthquakes were a series of Story Moments that took place in OG Chapter 1: Season 7. Many cracks formed in the ground over a period of time, mostly focusing around what is now the Jungle Biome, probably from the pressure building up from the forming Volcano on Wailing Woods. Eventually, a huge crack swiped through Tilted Towers destroying many cars and props. It made its way over to the new office building and destroyed it, creating its annual destruction for Season 7.

Buildup

Buildup[]

Pyreheart frees himself from the Ice King's Prison by melting the ice, and escapes. He travels to the northeast side of the island to regain his power as the Fire King and casts a spell to create the Volcano.

    • Active after the 1st storm circle formed.

  • South-East of Pleasant Passage
    • Active during the shrinking of the 1st storm circle.
  • South-West of Hidden Henge
    • Active a minute after the shrinking of the 1st storm circle.
  • East of Hitches and Ditches
    • Active after the 2nd storm circle formed.

The Earthquakes were Story Moments in Chapter 3: Season 1 caused by the Imagined Order's big underground drilling. There are 2 types of earthquakes, a major one that is moving everyday and smaller ones that happen across the island every game after the first Storm circle formed.

Major Earthquake

Major Earthquake[]

Major Earthquake will start in any matches after the first Storm circle formed. It will destroy certain locations on specific dates. It moves everyday at 4:00 AM ET.

Random Earthquakes

Random Earthquakes[]

Random Earthquakes are the smaller ones that can happen randomly. It will destroy buildings and the environment around it. Earthquakes will get stronger towards the end of the season.
