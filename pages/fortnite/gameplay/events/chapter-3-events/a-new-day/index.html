A New Day was a Solo Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was the opening cutscene for Chapter 3: Season 1.

Summary

Summary[]

After the Drift To Artemis event, The Looper washes ashore on a small isle east of Sanctuary. There, the Looper awakens and passes by Ronin, sitting on a rock sharpening the Blade of the Waning Moon, and Harlowe, sitting on a fence. After passing by them, the Looper finds more people around a campfire. The Looper sits near the campfire, and The Foundation explains to them that while the Imagined Order still has possession of The Zero Point, they will always face threats such as The Last Reality, and possibly even worse. He continues telling them that as long as the IO keeps exploiting the Zero Point, everything is in danger. This is why he needs their help to destroy the Imagined Order to set the Zero Point free.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The event’s code-name is "Pumpkin".
  • It is the second event to happen at the start of a season, the first being Zero Crisis Finale.
  • It is also the first event to happen at the start of a new chapter.
