The Devourer of Worlds was the end of Season Live Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale for Chapter 2: Season 4. It featured Galactus, Tony Stark, Wolverine, Thor and other characters. The Playlist went live at 3:35 PM EST and the event started at 4:10. The event ended ten minutes later at 4:20 PM.

Event Summary

Event Summary[]

Before the Live Event started, every player that attended it was on Helicarrier 64, which had moved from its previous spot as the Spawn Island to above the Apollo Island near Dirty Docks. The Live Event began with music similar to Herald's Warning, which may have been unique to the Live Event, that started playing when the Live Event’s timer reached 2 minutes left.

Shortly after the Live Event’s timer hit zero, Galactus began to come closer to the Apollo Island and grew in size. Galactus then hit Helicarrier 64 with his fist and knocked all the people off it, luckily, Iron Man gave The Looper a Stark Industries Jetpack. Galactus then reached for The Zero Point (located under The Ruins), he then proceeds to extract the Zero Point from deep underneath the middle island. Due to the Zero Point attempting to fight back Galactus, almost everything was sucked into it like in The End. Instead of another Black Hole, they were sent into The In-Between and were inside multiple Upgraded Battle Buses in front of Galactus.

Iron Man explained to The Looper that he had created upgraded Battle Bus clones with cannons and Gamma Cells to use them as bombs. He then directed the The Looper to shoot Gorgers and Gatherers coming from behind of Galactus with their Battle Bus’ cannons, and to head towards Galactus so he could consume as many Battle Buses as possible. If all went to plan, Galactus would be sent back to where he came from. Throughout time, The Looper got closer to Galactus and started to drive along his right arm with the help of Thor and Wolverine. Finally, The Looper left their Battle Buses and watched as Galactus consumed the Battle Buses while having Gamma energy coming out of him, and was then sent back to where he came from with a Rift.

Then, back to the mysterious office seen during The Device, Agent Jones is seen laying on the floor waking up in panic, with an alarm and a phone ringing heard in the background.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Iron Man mentions hacking our "time loop thingy" to create the billions of Battle Buses. This is referring to The Loop.
  • The song played by Iron Man on the player's Battle Bus stereo during the Galactus Arm Trench run was Demon Fire by AC/DC.
  • After exiting Galactus's arm, Astroworld from Travis Scott's Astronomical Live Event could be seen in the distance of space.
  • In the climax of the event, She-Hulk, Groot, and Storm were seen with Iron Man and Thor as Galactus was consuming the Battle Buses, although they did not have any dialogue.
  • If the player manages to eliminate 100 or more Gorgers during the event, the monitor behind the bus's driver's seat will display "Tony Royale".
  • The Rift Galactus goes through looks similar to a butterfly.
  • Kevin Feige shared a clip of the event to the crew of ‘The Fantastic Four: First Steps’ as inspiration for the film.
  • Gwenpool's Outfit description claims that she participated in this live event.
