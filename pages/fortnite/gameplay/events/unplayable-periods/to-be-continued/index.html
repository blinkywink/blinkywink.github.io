To Be Continued! was a Mini Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale that occurred directly after Operation: Sky Fire. It was an Unplayable Period of server downtime that lasted around 12 hours and 43 minutes, and ended at 5:00 AM EST September 13th 2021.

The Devourer of Worlds Chapter 2: Season 5

To Be Continued! was a mini event in Fortnite: Battle Royale and is the aftermath of The Devourer of Worlds event. It was an Unplayable Period similar to that of The Blackout. In total, it was present for 7 hours and 40 minutes.

After the countdown, there was still the server downtime until Chapter 2: Season 5 launched.
