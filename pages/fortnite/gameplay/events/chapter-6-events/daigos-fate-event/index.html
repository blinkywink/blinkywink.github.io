Daigo's Fate was a Story Moment in Chapter 6: Season 2. It was the direct continuation of Outlaws Story Quests - Wanted: Baron as part of The Baron's Big Deal.


Build-up

Build-up[]

After The Confrontation Event, Daigo became overly obsessed with the dark presence. He partnered up with Fletcher Kane to repair a powerful ancient Mask of Power that will be used to enter the Spirit Realm.

Trivia[]

  • The Live Event's codename is "GiftedTraveler".
  • The Phase 1 music reuses the Stage 1 Countdown music from The Confrontation Event, though with the gong at the start being absent.
  • Unlike other Mini Live-Event, this one is repeatable because it occurs in every game, once the player defeats Fletcher Kane.
