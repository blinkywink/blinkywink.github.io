The Confrontation Event, also known as Daigo vs. Shogun X was a Story Moment in Chapter 6: Season 1. It was a direct continuation of Story Quests: The Spirit Realm.

Build-up

Build-up[]

A comet landed on Oninoshima, awakening ancient magic across the island, alongside several strangers from another reality. Hope, Jones, and The Looper meet Kendo and Jade.

They begin to work together to defeat the demons that have came to Oninoshima from the Spirit Realm following the reawakening of magic. Daigo, Kendo and Jade's older brother, gifts Jade a magical mask, which leads to Jade getting trapped within the Spirit Realm due to a curse placed on her by the demons that was accidentally amplified by the mask.

The Looper investigates the crater left behind by the comet, forging Splinter Blades with parts of the comet that had broken off. They hand these splinter blades to The Night Rose, a warrior who has been forcefully bound by a powerful demon. She promises she knows who to give them to and ends up giving them to Daigo.

Hope enlists the Looper's help once more, urging them to find where the comet went. She deduces it's somewhere in the mountains, and later discovers it has been taken to the Spirit Realm. Kendo realizes that Daigo is using the splinters and the portal to try to save Jade from the spirit realm, hoping to undo the damage he's done.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Live Event's codename is "GrillMoon".
    • Spirit Realm Portal's codename is "Moon", so it could indicate that the meaning behind the Event's codename is "Gate Spirit Realm".
  • Daigo's Magic Shield can instantly destroy any vehicles.
  • On February 15th 2025, the regular Storm was put on hold for the duration of the event.
  • 30 minutes before the event, there was a safe zone in the area that disabled weapons and enabled respawning.
  • This is the first event to contain a hidden quest that can be completed for XP just for attending the event
  • This is the second story moment to make players teleport into the sky with the first being Sandstorm event.
  • The event music contains leitmotifs from Break The Curse, Shogun X's boss theme and The End.
  • Some servers unintentionally ended the event early, likely an unforeseen bug.
  • Players could get eliminated during the floating fight scene by flying towards the sky barrier or getting shot by players with working weapons.
  • The Kanji 鬼 that appears after Shogun X attacks Daigo translates to "Oni" or "Demon".
  • The fight scene was choreographed by Alfred Hsing with him also doing the motion capture for Shogun X. The motion capture for Daigo was performed by Ryan Tremaine Klarenbach[1]
  • The Voice Actors for the event are:
    • Daigo - Brent Mukai[2]
    • Shogun X - Tetsuya Fujita[2]
    • Jade - Leader Looi
      • The Night Rose Boss's voicelines were reused for Jade.
