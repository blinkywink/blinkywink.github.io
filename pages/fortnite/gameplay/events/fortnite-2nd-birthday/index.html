Fortnite 2nd Birthday

Overview

Fortnite 2nd Birthday was an event that celebrated Fortnite's 2nd birthday, which lasted until the start of Season X. Players were tasked with a short set of challenges that rewarded a Wrap, Lobby Music, Spray, Emoticon, and after completing all four challenges, you would earn a Pickaxe.

Even if not directly participating, players could eat the cake slices scattered around the map to receive +5 health and +5 shield instantly when consumed. Also, the Battle Bus sported a birthday theme for a limited time, and the Save the World mode recieved a new Hero, plus special birthday tickets.

Birthday Challenges

Birthday Challenges[]

Complete all four Challenges to earn the Birthday Slice Harvesting Tool.

  • Play 10 matches - Wrap
  • Dance in front of 10 different Birthday Cakes - Music
  • Outlive 500 Opponents - Spray
  • Gain Health or Shield From Cake (50) - Emoticon
