Lantern Fest was an Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that began on April 20th 2022 and ended on April 24th 2022 during Chapter 3: Season 2. This event coincided with the start of Ramadan, an Islamic tradition.

Are you ready to share the light? Lantern Fest 2022 runs throughout April with illuminated activities including Lantern Trials, bundles and more!

Fortography: Share the Light for Lantern Fest

Fortography: Share the Light for Lantern Fest[]

There are many ways to celebrate Lantern Fest. One that delights us is to decorate rooms, buildings, and streets with lanterns. For this illuminated Fortography, we invite you to visit select locations on the Island featuring beautiful lanterns.

When you arrive to one of these locations, make sure to capture your favorite Lantern Fest moment with a screenshot! Share it using #Lantography2022 on Twitter by April 8 at 11:59 PM ET.

We’ll be highlighting some of our favorite screenshots in a blog post, and their submitters will receive a code to redeem the delightful Cameo Needs Loot! Emoticon.
