“
From now until July 5, 2021, at 11:59 PM ET, the Alien enthusiasts’ excitement is escalating to a full-on Welcome Party. In this extraterrestrial celebration, enjoy fan-favorite Creative LTMs, unlock summer-themed rewards, and put on your best beach attire with new and returning Outfits.
”
— Cosmic Summer Blog Post

The Cosmic Summer Celebration was a limited-time event that began on June 22, 2021 at 9:00 A.M ET and ended on July 5th 2021 at 11:59 PM ET. The event mostly affected Believer Beach: a concert stage appeared there with music playing all over the location. Several Creative LTMs also became available for the duration of the event.
