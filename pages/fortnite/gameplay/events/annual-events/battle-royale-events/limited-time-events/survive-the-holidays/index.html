Survive the Holidays! was an event in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The event introduced the Grenade Launcher, and holiday-themed Battle Bus.

Overview

Overview[]

  • New holiday-themed cosmetics were released in the Item Shop.
  • The Grenade Launcher was changed to the Snowball Launcher for the duration of the event.
  • Christmas lights were added to the foliage on the island.
  • The Battle Bus was changed to have a festive look.
