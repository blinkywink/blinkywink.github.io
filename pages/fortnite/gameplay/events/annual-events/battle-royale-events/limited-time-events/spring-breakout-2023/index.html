Spring Breakout 2023 is an Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale that celebrates Easter and the Spring Break. It began on March 29th 2023, and ended on April 11th 2023.

It is the second Spring Breakout event, following Spring Breakout 2021.

Overview

Overview[]

  • Returning Egg Launcher
  • New Augments
  • Spring Chickens
  • 3 interactable eggs
    • Heal Eggs: These green eggs restore your Health and Shield slowly over time.
    • Hop Eggs: These blue eggs temporarily give you a low gravity effect, and also give you Health.
    • Golden Eggs: You can’t technically eat these eggs, but interacting with one grants you Bars!
