Godzilla

Godzilla[]

  • A Godzilla Portal has a chance to spawn in matches. The first player to jump into it becomes Godzilla.
  • When Godzilla spawns, special Monarch Supply Drops appear, always dropping a Ranger Assault Rifle or Rail Gun, and Shield Bubbles.
  • Other players must deal damage to Godzilla to drive him off the island, the player who does the most damage wins bonus rewards.
    • The winning player receives Godzilla's Medallion and the Exotic Burst Quad Launcher.
