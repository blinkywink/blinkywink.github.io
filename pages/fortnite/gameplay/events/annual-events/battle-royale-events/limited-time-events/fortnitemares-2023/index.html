How did it start?

How did it start?[]

Chapter 4: Season 3 / The Prophecy of the Eclipse and the Vampire[]

Initially, it was assumed that the message in Rumble Ruins was connected with The Last Reality, but as it turned out, the omen was something else...

A message was found in the ruins about a certain vampire who will come to the island on the eve of the Eclipse.

Chapter 4: Season 4 / Arrival of Kado Thorne on the island[]

The prediction came true in Chapter 4: Season 4, a collector named Kado Thorne arrived on the island with a Time Machine, and began robbing the island of its wealth in the past and present.

The Eclipse[]

But it was in Fortnitemares 2023 that Kado Thorne decided to reveal his Vampire Form to everyone and implement his plan.

Battle Royale[]

Introduction

Introduction[]

Kado Thorne decided to put into action his plan related to the time machine by revealing his vampire form to everyone. But in order to stop him, you will need a new weapon or the help of dead ghosts...

New Weapons and Unvaulted Weapons

New Weapons and Unvaulted Weapons[]

New weapons in Fortnitemares 2023 consists of two dangerous weapons: an Anti-Vampire shotgun to subdue Kado Thorne - Wood Stake Shotgun, as well as the collector's katana, Thorne's Vampiric Blade.

  • Wood Stake Shotgun
  • Thorne's Vampiric Blade

Other items from previous Fortnitemares have also returned!

  • Pumpkin Launcher (Rare-Legendary)
  • Witch Broom
  • Candy Corn
  • Jelly Bean
  • Pepper Mint
  • Hop Drop
  • Candy Bucket
New mechanics

New mechanics[]

Ghosts of Pirates[]

Ghosts of pirates appear on the island, pointing to their buried treasures in the form of buried chests.
