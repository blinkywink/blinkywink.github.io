Cursed Sails was a Limited-Time Event and Collaboration in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that began on July 19th 2024 and ended on August 6th 2024.

Introduction

Introduction []

Blimey! Disney’s Pirates of the Caribbean has raised the cursed sails and docked into Fortnite as part of the Battle Royale v30.20 July 19 hotfix (“Cursed Sails”). Cast off with the ghostly Ship in a Bottle, explore Shipwreck Shallows, and hunt for buried treasure. Cursed Sails runs until August 6, 2024, at 4 AM ET!

Taking part in nautical antics will help you complete Cursed Sails Quests, unlocking a treasure trove of in-game rewards buried in the Cursed Sails Pass.

Plunder the Pass’ free reward track to unlock items from the world of Pirates of the Caribbean, such as Jack’s Cutlass and Bane Pickaxe and Jack’s Prize Back Bling. The Premium Reward Track offers a hoard of extra goods that can be unlocked at the same time as the treasures in the free reward track. When you purchase this upgrade, you'll instantly unlock the Jack Sparrow Outfit!

Ship-“Wrecked”

Ship-“Wrecked”[]

Cursed Sails will have you aboard different kinds of vessels than what you’re used to in Wrecked. Use them in your adventure for buried treasure!

Ship in a Bottle
Unleash the spectral fury of the seven seas! Among other places, you can find a Ship In a Bottle inside Chests scattered in Shipwreck Shallows’ pirate ship. Crack it open to bring forth a cursed ship from the ghostly depths that can dive into opponents with a devastating crash. You can also take aim at landlubbers from the crow’s nest! After a short distance, the ship will dissipate.

Unvaulted: Pirate Cannon
Chart a course for the skies! Jump into one of the Pirate Cannons located around Shipwreck Shallows to launch yourself avast distance. Or roll it to a prime destination to launch a teammate into uncharted territory.

Unvaulted: Buried Treasure
Buried Treasure awaits on the Island! Hunt for treasure like the Flint-Knock Pistol and other loot using one of the Buried Treasure Maps in Chests and on the ground across the BR Island. X marks the spot, matey.

A Buried Treasure Map.

(Yes, the Flint-Knock is unvaulted too! You can also find this Pistol on the ground and from normal Chests.)

Of Bounty and Doubloons

Of Bounty and Doubloons[]

If you’re setting sail on your cursed ship and in pursuit of pirate plunder, then we’ve got some good news for ya: yer not just sailin' across the BR Island, ye'll be conquerin' Cursed Sails Quests too!

Completing Cursed Sails Quests will line your pockets with Cursed Gold. The more Cursed Gold you have, the more goods you’ll unlock from the Cursed Sails Pass! Keep track of your gold stash (and remaining Pass rewards) by heading to the Cursed Sails tab in the Lobby.

Your timbers sufficiently shivered? The Cursed Sails Pass has a free reward track and Premium Reward Track upgrade available for 1,000 V-Bucks. The free reward track contains 11 unlockable rewards, including the Skull and Crossed Swords Back Bling and Jack’s Cutlass and Bane Pickaxe. Complete your voyage to the end of this reward track and you’ll unlock the Jack’s Prize Back Bling!

With the Premium Reward Track, you can unlock extra rewards without having to complete any additional Quests. As an added bonus, you’ll instantly unlock the Jack Sparrow Outfit after purchasing this upgrade! Take “Captain” Jack Sparrow with you into LEGO® Fortnite and LEGO islands with his included LEGO Style.

The Premium Reward Track adds more unlockable rewards like the Jack Sparrow’s Sword Pickaxe, Jack’s Compass Back Bling, and Jar of Dirt Emote (Guess what’s inside it?). If you reach the Premium Reward Track’s end, you’ll unlock the Cursed Jack Sparrow Outfit which also comes with a LEGO Style!

Codes of the Pirate Brethren
It’s time for the ultimate pirate treasure hunt! The Cursed Sails Quests are divided into pieces of eight known as “Pirate Codes,” each going live sequentially and available ‘til August 6 at 4 AM ET. By completing four Quests in each Code, you’ll unlock a Map Piece…

  • The Pirate Code One Quests are live now.
  • The Pirate Code Two Quests go live July 21 at 9 AM ET.
  • The Pirate Code Three Quests go live July 23 at 9 AM ET.
  • The Pirate Code Four Quests go live July 25 at 9 AM ET.
  • The Pirate Code Five Quests go live July 27 at 9 AM ET.
  • The Pirate Code Six Quests go live July 29 at 9 AM ET.
  • The Pirate Code Seven Quests go live July 31 at 9 AM ET.
  • The Pirate Code Eight Quests go live August 2 at 9 AM ET.

Collect all eight Map Pieces to unlock the Jack’s Ship Glider! The sky is your sea.

Please note that Cursed Sails’ free reward track items, Premium Reward Track items, and Jack’s Ship Glider are not exclusive to Cursed Sails, as they may be purchasable in the Shop at a later date.

In the Shop: Elizabeth Swann, Barbossa, and Davy Jones!

In the Shop: Elizabeth Swann, Barbossa, and Davy Jones![]

There be pirates aplenty. In case you missed it, the Elizabeth Swann, Captain Barbossa, and Davy Jones Outfits entered the Shop a few days ago! Now’s your chance to walk the plank in style.

The Elizabeth Swann Pack
Pirate King and captain of her fleet. The Elizabeth Swann Pack contains her Outfit (including a LEGO Style), the Dead Man’s Chest Back Bling, and the Elizabeth’s Favor Pickaxe.

The Captain Barbossa Pack
Master pirate and mutineer! Purchase the Captain Barbossa Pack and you’ll plunder his Outfit (which has a LEGO Style) and the Barbossa’s Sword Pickaxe.

The Davy Jones Pack
Add Davy Jones… to your Locker. Containing the infamous captain of the Flying Dutchman himself, the Davy Jones Pack includes the Davy Jones Outfit (has a LEGO Style), Lil Kraken Back Bling, and Norrington’s Sacrifice Pickaxe!

Looking to stock up on the finest pirate provisions? Everything in the three Packs, plus the Davy’s Pipe Organ Emote, is included in the Pirates of the Caribbean Bundle! The Packs and Bundle will be in the Shop all the way ‘til 8 PM ET on August 6.
