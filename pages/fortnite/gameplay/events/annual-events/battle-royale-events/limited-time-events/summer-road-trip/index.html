Summer Road Trip was an Fortnite: Battle Royale event. It began on July 23rd 2024 and ended on July 31st 2024.

Quests

Quests[]

The Summer Road Trip Quests were released on July 23rd 2024. A new quest would release each day for 9 days and can be completed to earn multiple free rewards, including the Holiday Jam Track, Underworld Camo Wrap, Sun Sign Back Bling, and the Tesla Cybertruck Bundle. The quests were available until August 6. Other Quest Rewards include road trip-themed Sprays.
