April Fools was an Event in Fortnite that celebrates April Fools being the main focus of Update v40.10 for Battle Royale, Blitz Royale and Reload. It started on April 1st 2026 and ended on April 2nd 2026.

Description

Description[]

  • Big Heads - Players have enlarged heads. How does that work on Peely? We had to get a bit creative.
  • Finger Guns - Special finger guns go pew pew.
  • Shoulder Riding - Players can ride on teammates' shoulders with multi-stacking capability (infinite, if your balance is good enough?).
  • Llama Riding - Rideable Llamas have appeared around the Island. Yeehaw!
  • Fall Damage Immunity - Instead of taking fall damage, players make a splat sound when landing.
