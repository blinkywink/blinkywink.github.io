”
— Update v5.10 Patch Notes

Fortnite 1st Birthday was an event in Fortnite: Battle Royale, celebrating Fortnite's 1st anniversary. The event added Birthday Cakes around the map, along with consumable cake slices, and it added the first event challenges, the 1st Birthday Challenges. When completed, the challenges will reward you with 3 free cosmetics.

Overview

Overview[]

  • 10 Birthday Cakes scattered across the island
    • Fatal Fields: Middle
    • Flush Factory: Beneath giant toilet sign
    • Greasy Grove: Center, between Durrr Burger and the basketball court
    • Lazy Links: South, road near sand trap
    • Lonely Lodge: West, near vehicles
    • Paradise Palms: North, across from welcome sign
    • Pleasant Park: East, behind gas station
    • Retail Row: Middle, near court
    • Tilted Towers: Above waterfall toward Loot Lake
    • Wailing Woods: On hill toward Risky Reels
  • Consumable Birthday Cake slices which grant health and shield
  • A free Back Bling, Emoticon, and Spray for completing challenges
  • A birthday-themed Battle Bus with a new jingle

“
The Save the World storyline continues in the Canny Valley campaign. Get in-game and complete the Fortnite Birthday questline to unlock a special birthday reward!
”
— Update v5.10 Patch Notes

Fortnite 1st Birthday was an event in Fortnite: Save the World, celebrating Fortnite's 1st anniversary. The event added a new hero and brought back items from past limited-time events through the Birthday Llama, along with 2 new themed Quests.

Overview

Overview[]

  • The Birthday Llama, bought using Road Trip Tickets, contains heroes and schematics from previous events.
  • New Quests
  • New Birthday Brigade Ramirez Hero
