Lantern Fest 2024 is an Event in Fortnite that observes the islamic month Ramadan. It started on March 10th, 2024 and ended on April 10th, 2024.

Celebrate Fortnite’s Lantern Fest 2024![]

The lanterns never faded! Celebrate the return of Lantern Fest with the Lantern Fest Oasis island and Outfits whose inner flames will never go out. Lantern Fest 2024 runs from March 10 at 12 PM ET to April 10, 2024, at 12 PM ET — it’s time to share the light and keep the lanterns glowing.

Into the Oasis

Into the Oasis[]

Lantern Fest 2024 returns with a completely new island by Illusion Creative: Lantern Fest Oasis! Various challenges such as the Arena, Setting the Dining Table, Find the Falcon, Kashta Parkour, and more await.


These challenges will be unlocked one-by-one over the course of Lantern Fest. Ready to eat? Setting the Dining Table will reward you with XP!


The island code for Lantern Fest Oasis is 5629-9147-3382. Your presence is a light of its own.

New Outfits in the Shop[]


May the moon shine upon you.  These Outfits of the new Radiant Moon Set will appear in the Fortnite Shop later during Lantern Fest:

  • Noorah Outfit (includes the Lunar Lantern Back Bling)
  • Anwar Outfit (includes the Moonlit Blades Back Bling)

SirSANX Locker Bundle[]


Fortnite content creator SirSANX is participating in the Lantern Fest festivities with his new hand-picked Locker Bundle! Get your hands on it when it appears in the Fortnite Shop later during Lantern Fest.

SypherPK Returns[]

SypherPK is celebrating Lantern Fest once again! You can find SypherPK’s Icon Series Outfit in the Shop when it makes its appearance later in March.
