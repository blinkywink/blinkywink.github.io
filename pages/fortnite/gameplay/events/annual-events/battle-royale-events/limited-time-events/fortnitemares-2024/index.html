New and Returning Weapons

New and Returning Weapons[]

New weapons in Fortnitemares 2024 include Jigsaw's Boom Billy and the brand new Chainsaw!

  • Boom Billy
  • Chainsaw
  • Wood Stake Shotgun
  • Pumpkin Launcher
  • Witch Broom
New POIs and Bosses

New POIs and Bosses[]

Brawler's Patch has replaced Brawler's Battleground. Ultima Carver spawns here as a Boss and, when eliminated, drops a Wood Stake Shotgun and a Pumpkin Launcher.

Freaky Fields has replaced Reckless Railways. There is an interactable TV here that spawns Billy the Puppet as a Boss. He drops a Boom Billy when eliminated.

Challenges

Challenges[]

Multiple sets of challenges were released throughout Fortnitemares: the Fortnitemares 2024 Quests and the Horde Rush 2024 Quests.

Fortnitemares 2024 Quests[]

Coming out not immediately, but gradually during the event. They force you to use added/returned items from previous Fortnitemares, as well as complete certain tasks in the new POIs.

Horde Rush 2024 Quests[]

They came out completely at the beginning of the event.
