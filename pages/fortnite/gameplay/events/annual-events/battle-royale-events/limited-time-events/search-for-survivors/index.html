Search for Survivors was an event during June and July 2017 in Fortnite: Save the World. It allowed players to select one of several YouTube content creators to support while recruiting friends to join Save the World. Recruiting friends granted the player increasing rewards, and each YouTube content creator had a Banner Icon that would be granted to players upon selecting them as their "Commander". [1]
