“
Battle Royale gets in the Fortnitemares spirit! Participate in this limited-time event and get your hands on the frightening new Pumpkin Rocket Launcher. Earn season rewards and equip your favorite skin this Fortnitemares.
”
— Fortnitemares Announce Trailer

Limited-Time Events are events in Fortnite: Battle Royale, usually themed around a season or occasion, that last for a limited amount of time. They can last anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, and tend to come with Quests (formerly Challenges), new items and new themed cosmetics. The first event in Battle Royale was Fortnitemares 2017, in Season 1.

Season 5 introduced the first event to feature challenges, Fortnite 1st Birthday.

Chapter 1 History

Chapter 1 History[]

Season 1[]

Fortnitemares 2017[]

  • Update v1.8: Fortnitemares 2017 begins, with the addition of the Item Shop, Pumpkin Launcher, and Slurp Juice
  • November 29, 2017: Fortnitemares 2017 ends, and the Pumpkin Launcher is removed.

Survive the Holidays![]

  • Update v1.11: The Survive the Holidays! event begins, 1 day before the launch of Season 2. It brings with it the Snowball Launcher, a festive Battle Bus, and new holiday-themed cosmetics.

Season 2[]

  • Update v2.2.0: The Survive the Holidays! event ends, and the Snowball Launcher is removed.

Valentine's Day 2018[]

  • Update v2.42: Valentine's Day 2018 begins, adding Cupid's Crossbow and Valentine's-themed cosmetics.

Season 3[]

  • Update v3.00: Valentine's Day 2018 ends, Cupid's Crossbow changed to have its default appearance, and the name is changed to "Crossbow".

Season 4[]

  • Update v4.00: Vaulted the Crossbow.

Infinity Gauntlet Limited Time Mashup[]

  • Update v4.10: The Infinity Gauntlet Limited Time Mashup begins, adding the Infinity Gauntlet limited-time mode.
  • Update v4.20: The Infinity Gauntlet Limited Time Mashup ends, and the LTM is removed from playlists.

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.10: Fortnite's 1st Birthday event begins, adding free cosmetics as rewards for challenges and consumable Birthday Cake items.

  • Update v5.40: The High Stakes event begins, along with The Getaway LTM and challenges.

Wild Weeks/The Crossover[]

  • Content Update v16.30

    • May 13th, 2021: Wild Week #2, Fish Fiesta, begins. Fishing Holes now persist for longer, rare fish are more common, all fished weapons are Rare or above, and Pro Fishing Rods are more common.

    • May 19th, 2021: The Crossover begins, and the Fortnite x NBA Team Battles become available.

    • August 19th, 2021: Wild Week #3, High Tech Hijinks, begins. Alien and IO tech is made more common, and in addition, Abductors will only carry the Kymera Ray Gun, Grab-itron, Prop-ifier, and Alien Nanites. Likewise, IO Chests will only give the Pulse Rifle, Rail Gun, Plasma Cannon, and Inflate-A-Bull.
