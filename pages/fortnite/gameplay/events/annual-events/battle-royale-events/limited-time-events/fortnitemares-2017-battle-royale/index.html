”
— Fortnitemares Announce Trailer

Fortnitemares 2017 was a Limited-Time Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale, and was the first annual Fortnitemares event. This event featured the Item Shop with new Halloween-themed cosmetics and Lobby background, and a reskin to the Rocket Launcher.
