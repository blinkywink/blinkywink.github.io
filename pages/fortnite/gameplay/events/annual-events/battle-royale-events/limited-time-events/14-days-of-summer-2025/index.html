14 Days of Summer was a Fortnite: Battle Royale summer event that began on June 24th 2025 and ended on July 8th 2025.

Quests

Quests[]

Each day, a 14 Days of Summer Quest got released dedicated to an unvaulted item. The quest only remained available for 24 hours, after which both the item and the quest got removed.
