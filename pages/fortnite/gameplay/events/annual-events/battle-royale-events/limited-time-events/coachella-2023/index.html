Overview

The media represented on this page has been created by a second/third party.
This content is not originally part of the game but has been introduced through a special collaboration.
This article is about a limited-time event in Battle Royale.
The following page details content that is no longer available, or will soon become unavailable.

“
The Coachella Music and Arts Festival is back in Fortnite, bringing even more melodic festivities and cosmetic items for you to enjoy! Visit Coachella Island to earn XP and cosmetics. Jumping into Battle Royale? Listen to some of what Coachella 2023 has to offer by tuning into Icon Radio!
”
— Coachella 2023 Blog

Coachella 2023 is an Event and Collaboration in Fortnite: Battle Royale that celebrates the Coachella festival. It began on April 13th 2023 and ended on April 28th 2023.

Overview

Overview[]

  • New cosmetics for the Coachella Set.
  • New songs added to Icon Radio.
  • Coachella Island (5449-4207-1280) Creative Island linked to Quests.

Creative Island

Creative Island[]

“
Created by Alliance Studios in collaboration with Epic Games, grab your friends and find more to explore on Coachella Island, available in the Discover from April 14th 2023 at 3 PM ET to April 28th 2023 at 12 PM ET. From the Art Park to the team vs. team minigames, take part in an artistic adventure as you discover everything the island has to offer. You may even uncover some secrets during your exploration.
It wouldn’t be a Coachella experience without music: a variety of songs by Coachella 2023 performer Porter Robinson will be playing on the island! You’ll be able to enter Coachella Island via Discover or island code 5449-4207-1280. Coachella Island will remain active after the Coachella 2023 festival ends!
”
— Coachella 2023 Blog
