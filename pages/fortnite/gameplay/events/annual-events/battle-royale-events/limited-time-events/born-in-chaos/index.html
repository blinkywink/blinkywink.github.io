Born in Chaos was a Limited-Time Collaboration Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale that began on January 9th 2026 and ended on February 5th 2026.

Overview

Overview[]

For the announcement post, see Update v39.20

Additions[]

  • Cartmanland POI added, replacing Wonkeeland.
  • Quints 5-person playlist added.
  • South Park Outfits available in the Shop:
    • Stan
    • Kyle
    • Cartman
    • Kenny
    • Butters

New Items[]

  • Stick of Truth
    • Allows players to control the Storm.
  • Cheesy Poofs
    • Primary attack: heals the user.
    • Secondary attack: throws them at enemies, damaging them.
  • Kenny's Respawn Token
    • Automatically rebooted players upon elimination.

Born in Chaos Pass[]

  • Born in Chaos Pass available in the “Born in Chaos” tab.
    • Entirely free, no V-Bucks required.
  • 13 South Park-themed rewards unlocked by completing Born in Chaos Quests:
    • Backpack of CRED Back Bling
    • Terrance and Phillip Pickaxe
    • Cheesy Poofs Rocketship Emote
  • Imaginationland Airship Glider unlocked by completing all Quests.
