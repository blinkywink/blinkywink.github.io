Love and Legends was an Event in Fortnite that celebrated Valentine's Day, the start of Ramadan, and Friday the 13th. It started on February 5th 2026 and ended on March 5th 2026. Once the event went live, a new occasion occurred every few days.

Roadmap

Roadmap[]

On February 4th 2026, Epic Games revealed the Roadmap for Love and Legends:

  • February 5th (Update v39.40):
    • KPop Demon Hunters items and HUNTR/X return to Battle Royale.
    • The Storm turns pink.
  • February 6th:
    • KPop Demon Hunters Wave 2, including the Jinu, Golden Mira, Golden Rumi, and Golden Zoey outfits.
  • February 7th and 8th:
    • Battle Royale Power Hours.
  • February 13th:
    • Nitemare Island returns to Reload for the weekend.
  • February 14th and 15th:
    • Battle Royale Power Hours.
  • February 15th:
    • The Ice Storm Live Event in Fortnite OG.
  • February 19th (Update v39.50):
    • The Visitor from an unknown reality arrives to the Chapter 7 timeline in a Live Event.
    • The Back to the Future Time Machine was unvaulted in Battle Royale.
    • KPop Demon Hunters items are vaulted in Battle Royale.
    • Lantern Fest event in Reload, celebrating what might be the start of Ramadan, the Islamic Month, depending on the moon.
      • From February 19th to 25th, Unranked Reload was locked to Teams of 8.
  • February 21st and 22nd:
    • Battle Royale Power Hours.
  • February 23rd:
    • A new transmission from The Seven.
  • February 28th and March 1st:
    • Battle Royale Power Hours.
