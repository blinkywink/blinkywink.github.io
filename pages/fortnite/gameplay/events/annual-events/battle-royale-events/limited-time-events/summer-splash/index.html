Summer Splash is an Event that ran from the 30th of June, 2020 until it ended simultaneously along with Fortnite: Splash Down on August 27th 2020 (the start of Chapter 2: Season 4). It shares many similarities with 14 Days of Summer but did not include daily Unvaultings or challenges, and lasted for longer.

A new selection of LTMs were chosen. Jump the waves with LTMs both new and old. Sneak around in Fog Of War or trigger a sense of nostalgia with Unvaulted!

New Outfits, Back Blings, and more cosmetics will be released as well. Join Drift, Fishstick, Jonesy, Fable, and more with their brand new summer variants: Summer Drift, Atlantean Fishstick, Dad Bod Jonesy, and Summer Fable. Or, use brand new outfits released, from Safari, Par Patroller, or Bryce 3000.

Swing your Hook Slicers, mount your Sail Sharks, and buckle up!
