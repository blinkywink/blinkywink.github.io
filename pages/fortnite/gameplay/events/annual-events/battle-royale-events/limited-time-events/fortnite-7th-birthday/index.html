“
Where does the time go?! From now until October 1, 2024, at 2 AM ET, we’re celebrating Battle Royale’s seventh birthday with a heaping helping of party favors. At this birthday party, the guests get gifts — unlock three new Quest rewards as you play!
”
— Content Update v31.20 Patch Notes

Fortnite 7th Birthday was a Limited-Time Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale that lasted from September 27th 2024 to October 1st 2024 to celebrate the 7th anniversary of the release of Battle Royale.

For the occasion, the island was covered with Birthday Cakes, a set of quests were added, items were unvaulted, and a new medallion was added. The theme for the seventh birthday was Galaxy.

Overview

Overview[]

  • 10 Birthday Cakes scattered across the island:
    • Doomstadt
    • Castle Doom
    • Reckless Railways
    • Restored Reels
    • Grim Gate
    • The Underworld
    • The Raft
    • Grand Glacier
    • Mount Olympus
    • Redline Rig
  • A free Spray, Pickaxe and Back Bling for completing quests.
  • A birthday-themed Battle Bus.
  • New Birthday Medallion.
  • Unvaultings: Firework Flare Gun, Birthday Cake and Birthday Presents.
