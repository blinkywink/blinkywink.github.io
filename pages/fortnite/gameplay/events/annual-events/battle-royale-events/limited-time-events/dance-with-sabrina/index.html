Dance With Sabrina was a Limited Time Mode and Concert Event for Fortnite Festival. It was introduced on May 30th 2025 and ended on June 18th 2025, with the conclusion of Fortnite Festival Season 8.

Gameplay

Gameplay[]

Dance With Sabrina replaces Festival Jam Stage through the duration of the event. Lobbies consist of up to 24 players, and a party of five players can queue up for the mode.

During the concert, Tour-Ready Sabrina Carpenter will appear on stage to perform four of her hits from her album Short n' Sweet (2024). Players standing near the stage during each song will be able to join a dance challenge, where they will be prompted to hold either left or right before their respective buttons reach the center of the screen to fill their "Heart Meter". Players that have gained the most Heart at the end of a dance challenge will be selected to join Sabrina Carpenter onstage as either the Dance Leader, Special Effects Pro, or Video Artist.

  • Dance Leaders share the floor with Sabrina Carpenter alongside her backup dancers. As the leader, players can Emote on their own accord.
  • Special Effects Pros control stage flair. They can choose between confetti, hearts, or pyrotechnics to add spectacle to Sabrina's performances.
  • Video Artists control the appearance of the large video wall. They are able to select between three different backgrounds, and can adjust their saturation levels for a dramatic effect.

Players not chosen for either role will remain in the crowd to dance along using the song's associated Emote, and will receive a consumable in their inventory to play with. Although players will not be able to join the stage more than once, the top three performers at the end of the concert will be included in a photograph with Sabrina Carpenter.

There is a five-minute intermission between shows. During this time, players can start Jam Loops, collect XP Coins, or change their character's appearance with a Changing Booth.
