Overview

This article is about Summer Road Trip of 2025. For the 2024 event, see Summer Road Trip.

Party Up - Summer Road Trip is a Fortnite: Battle Royale Limited-Time Event. It began on July 22nd 2025 and ended on August 7th 2025.

Quests

Quests[]

The Party Up - Summer Road Trip Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite and Rocket League, available from July 22nd 2025 to August 6th 2025. The Party Up - Summer Road Trip Quests were released in Chapter 6: Season 3 and rewarded players with The Zephyr Song Jam Track, the Appeeling Drive, Sushi Scuba and Jelly Jammin' Sprays, the Sunset Vibes Wrap, The Quack Axe Pickaxe, the Cool Sun Emoticon, the Chevrolet Corvette ZR1 Car Body, the ZR1 Circuit Decal, the Rubber Duck Banner Icon, the Hairpin Outfit, the League Balloon Glider, and the Chevrolet Corvette ZR1 Wheels.
