Fortnitemares 2025 was the 9th Fortnitemares Event in Fortnite that started on October 9th 2025, and ended on November 1st 2025.

This edition of Fortnitemares introduced the largest collection of Halloween collaboration cosmetics yet, with 14 Outfits from 9 media properties being released. Freaky Fields, a fan-favorite location from the previous event, was brought back.

A week before Fortnitemares 2025 began in Battle Royale, Horde Rush was brought back as Demon Rush with a KPop Demon Hunters theme.
