Reality Rebirth (unofficial naming) was an Unplayable Period in Fortnite: Battle Royale that occurred right after The Big Bang event, and is the 7th Unplayable Period.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Reality Rebirth was the first Unplayable Period that could be exited without closing the game. Allowing the player to still play different Creative maps before servers temporarily shut down.
