Remix: The Finale was a Concert Live Event in Fortnite: Battle Royale that aired on November 30th 2024 at 2PM ET, with an encore showing at 8PM ET.

Remix: The Finale was the concluding Live Event for Chapter 2 Remix and Chapter 5 in its entirety.

Buildup

Buildup[]

After Doctor Doom's defeat, a Rift Butterfly merged Helios with New York City before opening a peephole into the real-world Times Square, where Snoop Dogg and Ice Spice performed on the TSX Broadway stage. The Rift Butterfly then returned to the in-game universe and sent the island's inhabitants back to an alternate Apollo, with Snoop Dogg replacing the Agency with his enterprise, which consisted of Ice Spice and Eminem. At the start of the season, only Snoop's base of operations — The Doggpound — was active.

On November 7th, Eminem's base of operations, Spaghetti Grotto, was opened.

On November 14th, Ice Spice's base of operations, Ice Isle, was opened.

On November 21st, Juice WRLD began watching over the Island across the ocean from the southwestern corner, overlooking a serene cliffside. The countdown to Remix: The Finale appeared in the lobby and at The Doggpound.

On November 23rd, Juice WRLD rose from the ocean, holding a mysterious object in his hand that radiated a blue energy.

On November 25th, a mysterious aura began to surround Juice WRLD as he further exposed his hand to view.

On November 29th, the lobby background for Battle Royale and Zero Build was updated to feature an oddly familiar set of electrical towers rising from the moat surrounding The Doggpound.

The Event

The Event[]

Intro[]

In a reprise of The Device event, warning sirens blare out as five electrical towers began to rise from the moat surrounding The Doggpound, all the while The Storm intensified, followed by a mysterious device that emerged from the top of the complex. It is revealed to be a gigantic microphone after the warning sirens transitioned into "Drop It Like It's Hot" and the towers channeled electricity into the device, which Snoop Dogg then picked up.

Concert[]

Character Song Description

Drop It Like It's Hot
(Snoop Dogg) 		The Doggpound was transformed into a theme park with bouncing Cadillac Devilles, dancing trees and dog statues. LBC '93 Dogg emerged from a portal in the sky and joined Snoop Dogg on stage.

In front of The Doggpound are dance floors that make the player do the Snoop's Walk emote when stood on.


Another Part Of Me
(Snoop Dogg & Dr. Dre ft. Sting) 		In a cosmic setting, LBC '93 Dogg sings to the Loopers on a ring of a planet rides a rocket-powered bus across the cosmos.

The Monster
(Eminem ft. Rihanna) 		At Flopper Pond, Slim Shady raps against the backdrop of a fiery wormhole, as Choppas fly around him. Eminem is suddenly summoned and he is soon confronted by The Mecha.

On the helipads are dance floors that make the player do the Rap Villain emote when stood on.


Houdini
(Eminem) 		In a segment stylized after the music video, Eminem and the Mecha engage in a lyrical and physical battle, with a microphone being stolen from one another as they exchange hits. Soon after, the Mecha was unmasked as Slim Shady, with the fight becoming more one sided in Eminem's favor.

Players are granted Jetpacks as they are forced into the air during the battle.


In Ha Mood
(Ice Spice) 		On top of a skyscraper in New York City, Ice Spice dances across multiple themed billboards.

On the rooftops are dance floors that make the player do the In Ha Mood emote when stood on.


Oh Shhh...
(Ice Spice ft. Travis Scott) 		Rap Princess Ice Spice raps as the city begins to crumble. Holograms and a purple comet representing Travis Scott appear alongside her.

Empty Out Your Pockets
(Juice WRLD) 		At WRLD's Point, Slayer Juice WRLD dances before soaring into the sky and slamming into the water with his fist, changing the scenery.

At the beach shore are dance floors that make the player do the Lucid Dreams emote when stood on.


Lucid Dreams
(Juice WRLD) 		The Looper is transformed into a Rift Butterfly for the encore. Juice WRLD creates fireworks and other effects that illuminate the sky.

Aftermath[]

Juice WRLD then disappears into the stars, which spell out "999" in tribute to the late rapper. In a wormhole The Looper transforms back into their normal form, who is then joined by Hope, Jones and the Battle Bus as they travel to a new Island. Behind them a mysterious object follows them out of the wormhole, which falls towards the Island's surface.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Remix: The Finale is codenamed "Quail".
  • Remix: The Finale was the world premiere for "Another Part of Me" by Snoop Dogg and "Empty Out Your Pockets" by Juice WRLD.
    • "Empty Out Your Pockets" was added to Juice WRLD's final album The Party Never Ends following the event. The instrumental for the song was teased at WRLD's Point in the buildup to the event.
  • More than 14,340,000 players watched the event, making it the most-watched Fortnite live event in history, surpassing the previous record of 11,600,000 players that viewed The Big Bang.
  • Interscope Records collaborated with Epic Games to release a series of vinyl albums to commemorate Chapter 2 Remix and Remix: The Finale. Four editions of the vinyls feature exclusive portraits of the rappers' Fortnite outfits; a fifth compiles them all. Eminem's and Ice Spice's covers are sold on their respective websites, while Snoop Dogg's, Juice WRLD's, and the compiled covers are sold on Interscope's website.
    • This marks the second time an exclusive record was produced as part of a Fortnite collaboration; the first being Fortnite X Metallica, which featured the 14 Metallica songs released as Jam Tracks for Fortnite Festival's fourth season in addition to "Hit the Lights", a song that was only heard in Metallica: Fuel. Fire. Fury. This record was issued exclusively to Epic Games employees.
  • The Ghostly Director Loading Screen featured panels that alluded to the event.
  • The whole soundtrack of Remix: The Finale was leaked around two days before the event actually happened.
  • While in the playlist before the event, players were able to walk on the moat surrounding The Doggpound with modified exterior, but were unable to access the interior of the building.
  • Remix: The Finale makes many callbacks to previous Live Events in Fortnite's lifespan.
    • The Butterfly Event: During Juice WRLD part, a Rift Butterfly lands on the player's finger, reusing the same animation from the Event, before turning the player into a Rift Butterfly.
    • The Final Showdown: The Mecha clashes with Eminem during the "Houdini" segment. Eminem takes the role of The Devourer, hinted with "The Monster" playing prior.
    • Travis Scott's Astronomical: Travis Scott's purple comet from Astronomical returns before his verse in the "Oh Shhh..." segment as a callback to the concert's beginning. However, Travis Scott's Outfit itself does not appear. The sky near the end of the song also mimics how it appeared during the "Stargazing" segment of Astronomical.
    • The Device: The Doggpound took the place of The Agency as Midas' Doomsday Device was changed into a microphone.
    • Zero Crisis Finale: During Juice WRLD part, the player turns into a Rift Butterfly like Agent Jones was in Zero Crisis.
    • Rift Tour: The wormhole the player, Jones, and Hope go through at the end of the event is very similar to the one seen at the start of Rift Tour when The Last Reality scans The Looper's memories.
    • The End (Chapter 2): During the moments LBC '93 Dogg utilized portals for transport, the way they appear is somewhat similar to The Last Reality's massive invasion Portal summoned by The Cube Queen atop The Pyramid.
    • The Big Bang: The red wormhole above Slim Shady was the same that was above Marshall Never More during The Big Bang's "Godzilla (Ft. Juice WRLD)" segment.
  • The Zero Point is revealed to been fractured off-screen following Reality Zero's collapse after Chapter 4: Season 4.
    • This event is the only time it physically appeared in Chapter 5.
