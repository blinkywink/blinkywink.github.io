Drift To Artemis (unofficial naming) was an Unplayable Period in Fortnite: Battle Royale which was the aftermath of The End event and the 4th Unplayable Period.

It starts out with the Looper drifting at sea unconscious on a log. While the game is active the camera will zoom into them very slowly, and then slowly pan towards Artemis as the sun rises. After drifting in the ocean for approximately 18 hours, A New Day begins.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The event code-name is "Coconut".
  • This is the first Unplayable Period not to involve interstellar or extraterrestrial items, this just being The Looper on a log in a massive body of water.
  • Various props can be seen floating to and away from The Looper. These range from Debris to other things like a The Burger.
  • Unlike The End (Chapter 1), The End (Chapter 2) transitioned to Drift To Artemis immediately after the event ended.
    • The End (Chapter 1) Also had a playable minigame unlike the newer one.
  • If IOS players changed their Outfit whilst being adrift, the Outfit would change on whatever other platform they'd view the drifting on.
