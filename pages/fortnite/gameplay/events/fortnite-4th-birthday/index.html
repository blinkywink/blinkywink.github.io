Fortnite 4th Birthday

Fortnite's 4th Birthday

Overview

Fortnite's 4th Birthday was a mini in-game event that took place from 9:00 AM EST on September 24th 2021, to 2:00 AM EST on September 28th. The small celebration commemorated the fourth anniversary of the release of Fortnite: Battle Royale. 4th Birthday Quests were released and given by the Character Birthday Brigade Ramirez for rewards that could have been obtained. POIs around the map received birthday decorations in small areas, along with large cakes with slices around them that could have been eaten to receive both 10 Health and Shield per slice.
