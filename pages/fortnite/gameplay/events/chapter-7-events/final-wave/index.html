The Final Wave was a Story Moment in Fortnite: Battle Royale that took place on March 14th 2026 at 3:30 PM ET.

It is the concluding Live Event of Chapter 7: Season 1.

Buildup

Buildup[]

Main Article: The Reassembly Events

Throughout the season, the Dark Voyager gradually acquired Zero Shards, the first being the one already present at Lethal Labs, by taking over the facility on December 11, 2025.

The second was acquired by traveling to an unknown reality on January 11, 2026, before returning on January 31, 2026, and placing the second Shard alongside the first over the following two days.

The third Shard was recovered on March 12th 2026, in Reality: 7021, after forcing Fishstick to betray his world under duress.

The Event

The Event[]

At the end of the countdown, the Dark Harvester began gradually dispensing all of its Golden Cube energy into the Zero Point Shards. Leading to the partial re-awakening of The Zero Point as it tethers Rift Gates to different Realities.

Having expended all the energy of the vessel, the Dark Harvester temporarily hovered slightly tilted over the Zero Point Shards, unresponsive as its lights flickered until it reactivated shortly after the event.
