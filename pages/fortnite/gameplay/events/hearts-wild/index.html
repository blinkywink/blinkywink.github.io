Overview

“
Love is in the air on the Island and things are gonna get wild... Hearts Wild!

Find love with Fishstick, Compete in the Hearts Wild Cup to earn an Outfit early, join in on some Team Battles, create your own Papercraft and more 💘

”
— Fortnite Twitter

Hearts Wild is an event in Fortnite: Battle Royale, similar to Love and War and Share the Love, that celebrates Valentine's Day. It began on Tuesday, February 8th 2021 and ended on February 17th 2021.

Over the course of the week, it will bring a tournament, another Community Battle, themed Quests, new and returning cosmetics, and more!

Hearts Wild Cup

Hearts Wild Cup[]

Main Article: Hearts Wild Cup
“
Whether you’re a couple or just any dynamic duo, assemble your Duos team for the Hearts Wild Cup on February 9, 2021.
”
— Hearts Wild Blog

Hearts will flutter at the chance to earn the Lovely Outfit before it hits the Item Shop. You can do so with a Duos partner with the Hearts Wild Cup!
The top-performing Duos in each server region will earn the Lovely Outfit and Heartblast Back Bling.

Team Battles

Team Battles[]

Main Article: Hearts Wild Team Battles

Community Battles return for Hearts Wild with a new event: Hearts Wild Team Battles. Between February 10-17, 2021, Fortnite players will have an opportunity to team up with their favorite Creators to help them earn points in unique Challenges.

By placing highly enough, participants competing against other teams within their regional group can win in-game rewards.

Themed Quests

Themed Quests[]

Main Article: Chapter 2: Season 5 Epic Quests
“
With so many fish in the sea, surely Fishstick is capable of finding a Valentine.
”
— Hearts Wild Blog

This week’s [[Chapter 2: Season 5 Epic Quests|Epic Quests]], going live on February 11, 2021, involve Valentine's-themed tasks.

With these, you will help Fishstick get a date, and oversee Big Chuggus and Lovely's mission to spread the spirit of love throughout the island. Doing these Characters these favours will grant you some XP!

New and Returning Outfits

New and Returning Outfits[]

New Outfits will make their debut in the Item Shop throughout Hearts Wild, including the bursting-with-love Lovely and Cuddle Team Leader’s #1 fan Cuddle King. Outfits from years past, like the tough-loving Lovethorn, will be making their way to the Item Shop as well!

February 8th - Lovethorn
February 9th - Cuddle King
