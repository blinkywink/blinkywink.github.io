Daily Jam Tracks

Description

Type

Music Library

Mode

Fortnite Festival

Introduced

Update v29.01

Overview

Every day, a selection of up to 40 Jam Tracks can be selected for free in the Music Library while on the Main Stage and Battle Stage in Fortnite Festival. This rotation refreshes daily at 0:00 UTC.

Contents

Contents[]

Featured Jam Tracks[]

Featured Jam Tracks are tracks that are selectable for free throughout the day. They rotate every day at 0:00 UTC. Jam Tracks that are featured may appear back-to-back on different days and are free for all players to use that day. They are marked with a Featured icon and will appear at the top of the Music Library by default.

New Jam Tracks[]

New Jam Tracks are tracks that release in the Item Shop on the same day they are added to the rotation. They typically release at the same time as their addition, and will stay in multiple rotations before moving to the sporadic Featured rotation. They are marked with a New icon and will appear alongside the Featured Jam Tracks at the top of the Music Library by default.

Shared Jam Tracks[]

Shared Jam Tracks are tracks that other players in your lobby own but you do not. They can be selected like any other Jam Track in the rotation. They are marked with a Shared icon and will appear with all Jam Tracks not currently in the rotation.

Spotlight Jam Tracks[]

Spotlight Jam Tracks are tracks that usually correspond with new Jam Track rotations featuring new songs by artists, certain quests to earn or are tracks featured in the Spotlight Setlist. They will sometimes replace already featured Jam Tracks. They are marked with a Spotlight icon and will appear above the Featured Jam Tracks by default.

Server Downtime/Shutdown, What Happens?[]

Updates/Downtime[]

When a new update releases or downtime occurs, the game (and by proxy the Daily Jam Tracks) are inaccessible until servers come back online.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Prior to Week 10 of Fortnite Festival's fourth season, the Jam Track rotation refreshed daily at 0:00 UTC, with 20 songs in rotation.
  • To celebrate Fortnite Festival's one-year anniversary, all 285 Jam Tracks in the Item Shop that were in the game up to that point were added to the Weekly Rotation for the weekend of December 12-15th 2024.
