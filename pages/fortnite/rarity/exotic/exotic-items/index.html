Overview

Exotic Items are a special rarity in Fortnite that was introduced in Chapter 2: Season 5. They are often attributed for upgraded versions of existing weapons with unique attributes.

Description

Description[]

Exotic items are unique with no other existing rarities, designed to enhance an existing weapon with a unique attribute or modifier. They usually, but not always, have a unique icon and model. With the exception of the Storm Scout, all Exotic items feature a distinct trait setting them apart from their base weapon.

Rules[]

  • Most exotic weapons share stats with their Legendary counterparts, if applicable.
  • They have distinct names, often reflecting their special trait.
  • They are obtained through unique methods, typically by purchasing from Characters or malfunctioning Vending Machines.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Exotic weapons are very similar to Mythic weapons in terms of their function.
    • There are several weapons that have unique attributes that are dropped by Bosses, and as such are Mythic
      • These include Deadpool's Dual Hand Cannons, TNTina's Ka-Boom Bow, Kit's Shockwave Launcher, Thorne's Vampiric Blade, RG's Minigun, Ice Spice's Rifle, Ice Spice's Grappler, and Baron's Double Down Pistol.
  • When viewed in the inventory, Exotic items’ rarity can be indicated with varying number of stars, ranging from 5 (e.g. Shadow Tracker) to 7 (e.g. Slap Splash).
  • Because of it being based off the unreleased transcendent rarity, it will have a red shine in creative item placers unlike most rarities that use their actual inventory color.
  • Several Exotic weapons belong to set groups, each having their own unique color schemes.
    • These include the Heisted exotics from Chapter 4: Season 1, the Lawless exotics from Chapter 6: Season 2, the Unstable exotics from Chapter 6: Season 3 and Eradicator exotics from Chapter 6: Season 4.
  • There have been 6 Exotic healing items in Battle Royale, including 3 cannons and 2 splashes.
    • 3 of them are related to Slap, with the Slap Splash being the only one that's not a Lawless exotic.
