Meowscles' Purr-Fect SMG is a Mythic Submachine Gun That was added in Chapter 3: Season 3.

It is theMythic Variant of the Submachine Gun.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Meowscles' Purr-fect SMG is a fast firing, short range, high hip-fire accuracy weapon, in line with most SMGs in the game. Its purpose is to deal high amounts of DPS in a close range environment. The weapon can function at longer ranges, but it suffers from high damage falloff and low accuracy.

Stats

Stats[]

Meowscles' Purr-fect SMG uses Light Bullets and has a headshot multiplier of 1.6x.
