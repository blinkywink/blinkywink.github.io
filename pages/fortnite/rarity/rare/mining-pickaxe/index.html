Overview

This article is about the Save the World Harvesting Tool. For the Battle Royale Cosmetic of the same model, see Hydraulic Wrecker.

“
Pickaxe: A harvesting tool that deals heavy damage against the environment, but is not very effective in combat.
”
— In-Game Description

The Mining Pickaxe is an Uncommon and Rare Pickaxe in Fortnite: Save the World, that was introduced in Pre-Online Test.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Mining Pickaxe functions as a harvesting tool used to damage objects for resources. When the damaging an object, a Weak Point can form on the object which can then be hit to deal additional damage.

  • The Mining Pickaxe damage is dependent on what is being hit and the "team" it's on relative to the player:
    • 100% damage to hostile and neutral objectives and props (I.E. trees, rocks, and cars), but 50% damage to friendly objectives and props.
    • 70% damage to hostile and neutral players[unused] and enemies, but 35% damage to friendly players[unused] and enemies.
    • 140% damage to hostile and neutral Weak Points, but 70% damage to friendly Weak Points
    • 200% damage to hostile and neutral prop Weak Points, but 100% damage to friendly prop Weak Points
      • Props refer to objects and structures, such as trees (wooden props), rocks (stone props), and cars (metal props)
  • The Mining Pickaxe range is dependent on what is being hit:
    • 2.96m range for enemies
    • 2.88m range for buildings on the xy axix
    • 3.84m range for buildings on the z axis
    • 4m range for Weak Points

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Prior to Online Test 6, pickaxes would lose durability when used and could be crafted[3]. This includes the following: Handmade Pickaxe, Pickaxe, Mining Pickaxe, Hydraulic Pickaxe, Vindertech Pickaxe, and all upgraded variants. Although the schematics were removed before Online Test 6.5, durability still exists internally (being 125 and 220) but is never depleted.
  • In Online Test 6.5, the Mining Pickaxe was tied to two ability kits, "Kit_HarvestWeapon_T05" and Kit_HarvestWeapon_T06[Upgraded], which were removed between Online Test 6.5 and Update v1.0.
  • In Update v1.6, the Mining Pickaxe model from Online Test 6.5 was reused for the Basic Hoe.
