Overview

“
Jump in & explore the world of Fortnite Competitive at Legends Landing! Your one-stop-shop for all things competitive w/ games, broadcasts & much more
”
— In-Game Description

Legends Landing is an island in Fortnite: Creative, introduced in Chapter 3: Season 1. Players can watch the FNCS on a big screen, practice shooting and play Box Fights. Players in Legends Landing can earn free FNCS themed cosmetics by being on the island for a certain amount of time. Prior to Legends Landing, in-game FNCS watch parties were on Party Royale.
