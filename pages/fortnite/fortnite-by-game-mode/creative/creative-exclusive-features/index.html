Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Prop-o-Matic was semi-released in Battle Royale in the form of the Prop-ifier.
  • The first Mythic item exclusive to Creative was the Striker Burst Rifle.
  • There were alot of weapons that were originally exclusive to Creative, but were added to Battle Royale or other modes.
    • The Mythic Prime Shotgun was added to Battle Royale in Update v22.00.
    • The Octane was added to BR in Update v22.40.
    • The Legendary Lever Action Rifle was added to the Comeback LTM.
    • The Mythic Ranger Assault Rifle was added to Battle Royale on January 7th 2025 during Update v33.11.
    • The Uncommon Tactical Assault Rifle was added to Reload in Update v34.20.
    • The Rare Fortnite OG Pump Shotgun was added to Fortnite OG in Update v34.40.
    • The Uncommon Ex-Caliber Rifle was added to Battle Royale on June 25th 2025 during Update v36.10.
    • The Mythic Heavy Sniper Rifle and the Mythic Explosive Repeater Rifle were added to Blitz Royale on August 2nd 2025 at 3:58pm ET during Update v36.30.
    • The Common Deadeye DMR was added to Battle Royale in Update v37.00.
    • The Uncommon Falcon Eye Sniper was added to Battle Royale on September 2nd 2025 during Update v37.10.
    • The Uncommon Hunting Rifle was added to Demon Rush in Update v37.40.
    • The High Stakes' Shotgun was added to Battle Royale in Update v37.50.
    • The Shiva Shotgun and Lucille were added to Blitz Royale on October 28th 2025 during Update v37.51.
    • The Epic and Legendary Pump Shotgun was added to Fortnite OG on October 24th 2025 during Update v37.51.
    • The Legendary Six Shooter was removed from the Content Browser in Update v39.10 and then added to Battle Royale in Update v39.11.
    • Yule Trooper's Holo Rush SMG was added to Battle Royale in Update v39.11.
    • The Drum Gun was added to Reload in Update v39.30.
    • The Mythic Flex SMG was added to Blitz Royale with Update v39.30.
    • The Common Tactical Assault Rifle was added to Reload on February 13th 2026 at 7:00 AM EST during Update v39.40.
    • The Ice King's Gauntlets, Seven Power Gloves, Foundation's Power Gloves, Slapperoni Pizza and the Slapperoni Slice were removed from the Content Browser with a Hotfix on March 19th 2026 (Update v40.00) before being released to Battle Royale and the later re-added to Creative with Update v40.10.
