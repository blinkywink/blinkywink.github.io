Overview

“
The Arena: where champions rise. Drop into a competitive world of close-quarters combat in Arenas Boxfights, a 16-player, 1v1 or 2v2 Ranked mode where skills are tested and legends are forged. Emerge victorious in a series of build-only boxfights against a lineup of 15 other players or teams. The first player to reach the score limit will be victorious! Each match drops you into a small map with full health and shield, along with a weapons loadout that matches your opponent’s. After each round, you’re queued instantly, so the faster you finish, the more rounds played.
”
— In-Game Description

Arenas Boxfights is a Ranked PvP (Player versus Player) mode in Fortnite that was released on April 9th 2026. Players fight in a 1v1 or 2v2 format in a Build mode inside small boxes. The player who first reaches 20 wins or in which 2v2 mode 15 wins becomes the match winner.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Arenas Boxfights' codename is SquareClub.
  • This is the first mode created by Epic Games to feature a voice announcer.
  • Despite the existence of many creator-made maps, this is the first Box Fight map officially released by Epic Games.
  • This is the second Core Gamemode by Epic Games to be released as a Fortnite: Creative island, with the first being Legends Landing.
