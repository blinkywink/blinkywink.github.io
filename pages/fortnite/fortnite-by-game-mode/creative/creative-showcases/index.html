Creative Showcases

Overview

For information on spiritual successor in the Discover tab, see Discover and Playlists.

“
Queue up solo or with a party. You will be automatically matched with other players to fill a lobby.
”
— In-Game Description

The Creative Showcases were featured, limited-time gamemodes of popular community made games in Fortnite: Creative before the introduction of the Discover menu. Content was rotated in regularly, so players could check back every few days for new experiences.
