Star Wars Brand Template

Star Wars Islands

IP Owner

Lucasfilm

Initial Release

March 19th 2026

Overview

This Content is Available in the Star Wars Brand Template.


Creators can build entire Fortnite islands inspired by the Star Wars universe,.

The Star Wars Brand Template allows creators to design custom gameplay experiences using assets, items, and mechanics from the Star Wars Series, such as Lightsabers and Blasters.

Please note, creators must share 15% of their revenue gained from Star Wars Islands with Disney.
