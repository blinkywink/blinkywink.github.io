Devices (Creative)

Devices

Overview

This page is under construction.
Please refrain from editing the page if another user is actively doing so as to avoid edit conflicts.

Devices are the main mechanic in Creative, that adds functionality to your island; These devices have a wide array of capabilities that allow you as a creator to customize your island your way. Most devices will have a list of customizable options, functions and or events. These are key to creating your experience.

Some Devices are UEFN or Creative exclusive; these are tagged respectfully below.

Beta or Experimental devices; These devices will be tagged and subjected to change and or removal from the game with or without notice.

  • Contextual Filtering : Some devices are affected by a feature called contextual filtering. This feature hides or displays options depending on the values selected for certain related options.
  • Upgraded Devices  : As of Update v26.30, all islands made with Fortnite Creative will transition to upgraded devices automatically, and all new islands will use upgraded devices by default.
    • These devices include many upgrades such as:
    • Direct numerical input,
    • Color pickers,
    • Additional features on some devices,
    • Trap Devices replaced with props that can be copied and pasted.
  • Direct Binding : As of Update v25.00, this is the new system that replaced Channels that allows you to directly bind a function or event to other devices.

While this is a complete list of all devices in Creative, not all devices have a completed page of settings and information; View the Official Documentation from Epic Games to see all values and functions of each device.
