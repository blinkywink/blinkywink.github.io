Galleries

Overview

This page is a stub.
Some content on this page may be missing or incomplete, you can help by expanding it!


Galleries are groups of props and/or buildings in Fortnite: Creative that instantly create a selection of tiles and objects for building in creative. They were introduced in Update v7.00, along with the release of Fortnite: Creative and Season 7. Galleries are continuing to be added to creative with almost every new update. They include BR Locations and Landmarks, STW Props, and Creative-Exclusive Galleries.
