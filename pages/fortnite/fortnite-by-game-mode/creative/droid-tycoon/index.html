Overview

“
Start with a small workshop and build a droid empire. Craft authentic Star Wars droids, send them out on missions, set them to work for credits, and keep your favorite creations by your side. In this Star Wars tycoon experience, your path to mastery is built one droid at a time.
”
— In-Game Description

Droid Tycoon is a mode in Fortnite that was released on May 1st 2026 with the Unleash the Force event. Players must expand their workshop and build Star Wars droids.

The Base

The Base[]

Main base of player's operations. It gets upgraded with each rebirth.

Shipyard Missions[]

Each pad offers missions to different planets. Time and location may vary but rewards stay consistent.

There's 5 available pads in a fully upgraded base.

  • #1 - Common 40%, Gold 10%, Credits 50%
  • #2 - Common 20%, Gold 70%, Diamond 10%
  • #3 - Gold 20%, Diamond 70%, Rainbow 10%
  • #4 - Diamond 20%, Rainbow 80%
  • #5 - Rainbow 15% , Beskar 5%, Credits 80%

Updates

Updates[]

v1.03[2]

May 3rd 2026

  • Added mutation labels for Droids on the Rebirth screen
  • Improved Upgrade Chip drop rates for Daily Quests and World Activities
  • Improvements to base loading
  • Fixed issue where unfinished Daily Quests were showing as completed
  • Adjusted Credits materials to reduce blur + Fixed a tutorial softlock
  • Droids now walk away sadly when sold at Crafting Stations

v1.05[3]

May 5th 2026

  • Selling Gold / Diamond / Rainbow Droids now give upgrade chips
  • Droid rarity is now shown on message feed
  • Fixes for offline earnings
  • Adjusted weapons wall
  • Your workshop now only makes you invincible, other players can be eliminated
  • Lucky Pit Droid now supports legendaries
  • Increased Companion follow distance
  • Mission rewards have been rebalanced
  • Fixed rare issue where Droid Collection could be reset
  • Added Matchmaking Portal in the Cantina

v1.06[4]

May 6th 2026

  • Pickup Upgrade Chips can now give 1-3 instead of always just 1
  • Selling a Gold / Diamond / Rainbow Droid now grants significantly more Upgrade chips
  • The Legendary & Mythic Astromech Missions have had their time reduced
  • Astromech Mission Rewards have been buffed (better chance for variants)
  • Some Legendary Droids have had their stats increased
  • Adjusted start time for BB-8 Event: 10:00 AM ET Saturday 10:00 AM ET Sunday

v1.07[5]

May 9th 2026

  • Added confirm button for selling Droids
  • Added Upgrade Chips amount to Droid sell button
  • You can now use your controller’s back button to close most menus
  • Missions have been updated to make rewards more relevant
  • Fixed Daily Mission text not appearing correctly for some languages

v1.08[6]

May 12th 2026

  • Updated requirements for Rebirth 11–18 to scale across all Legendary Droid variants, not just Rainbow variants.
  • Removed “Sell a Rainbow Legendary Droid” from the Daily Quest.
  • Fixed several UI localization issues.

v1.09[7]

May 16th 2026

  • Scrap Station now provides Credits to you and the owner if you hit someone else’s Station.
  • Building a Droid on the correct Depot now provides a 10% speed bonus.
  • Increased Companion follow distance.
  • Adjusted Astromech Mission rewards to increase Blueprint variety.
  • Added Droid Icon to the Sell Confirm UI so you know what droid you’re selling.
  • Increased Upgrade Chip pickup collision size.
  • Swapped Slap Juice with Nitro Splash in Firing Range.
  • Removed 16:9 constraint on cinematics
  • Droidex buttons now work on mobile

v1.10[8]

May 16th 2026

  • Fixed a visual issue during the Mister Bones event where the event icon and 2x Credits / 2x Upgrade Chips indicators could disappear after leaving and rejoining an event lobby.
  • Fixed an issue where Credits could not be collected from some worker stations.
  • Increased Credits earned from Astromech Missions.
  • Increased Mono-WLKR’s blueprint cost to the intended amount.

v1.11[9]

May 23rd 2026

  • Flawless Droids have entered Droid Tycoon. When crafting Droids, there’s a small chance they will produce a Flawless Droid. Crafting a Flawless Droid permanently unlocks a new, cosmetic effect for that Droid type.
  • NEW DROID: Gunrunner has been added to the Sandcrawler Shop.
  • Fixed a localization issue for Droid types on server callouts.
  • Optimization improvements.

v1.12[10]

May 28th 2026

  • Added Player names to the holograms at the base
  • Added countdown for BESKAR EVENT
  • Added "Crafted Flawless" Message feed
  • Updated Diamond and Rainbow Droid materials for a cleaner look. Reduced glow / bloom for a more uniform look across platforms.
  • Fixed Droidex saying "MYTHIC" for Iconic Droids

v1.13[11]

May 30th 2026

  • Added 3 new Rebirth Levels
  • New look, who dis? Try the Changing Booth!
  • Astromech Mission and Daily Quest Rewards now have more variations
  • Using Upgrade Chips on a Crafted Flawless Droid retains it's Flawless status
  • Astromech Companions have been made slightly better
  • Cantina Quests now provide more credits and no longer give Droids
  • Cantina Quests no longer have a cooldown
  • Removed "Rebirth 1 Time" from the Daily Quests
  • Updated portraits for Variants

v1.14[12]

June 1st 2026

  • "Upgrade Chip" Astromech Mission updated to "Beskar" Astromech Mission
  • 12 minute Base Mission Time
  • 80% Credits
  • 15% Rainbow Blueprint
  • 5% Beskar Blueprint
  • Rainbow Droids can now be upgraded into Beskar using Upgrade Chips
  • Beskar 20min timer now spawns 1 Blueprint per player
  • Daily Quests expire after 22 hours instead of 24
  • Daily Quests now correctly weigh your progress when giving a blueprint
  • BB-8 Perk updated to "100% Upgrade Chips"
  • Upgrade Chip Pickup reduced to 1
  • 3x Epic / Legendary Rarity Droid Upgrade Chip sell amount
  • Fixed Missing Weapon Wall slots
