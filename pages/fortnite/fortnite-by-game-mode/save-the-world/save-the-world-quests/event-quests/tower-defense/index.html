Mission[]

“
Trap up and defend the objective while fortifying it with your B.A.S.E to showcase what a Constructor can really do!
”
— In-Game Description

Tower Defense is an Event Quest in Fortnite: Save the World. It is the 5th Quest of the Guide to Saving the World Questline, and unlocks the Check Your Radar Quest.

Objectives

Objectives[]

  • Eliminate 20 Husks with traps in any zone
  • Use your B.A.S.E 3 time in any zone
  • Activate Kinetic Overload 3 times by staggering or knocking back Husks with a melee weapon
