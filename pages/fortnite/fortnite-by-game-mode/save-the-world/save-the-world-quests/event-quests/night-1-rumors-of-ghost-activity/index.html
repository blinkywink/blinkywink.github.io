Night 1: Rumors of Ghost Activity is a Special Defense Missions in Fortnite: Save the World that is a part of The Door To Darkness Event Questline.

Overview

Overview[]

Night 1: Rumors of Ghost Activity requires players to locate the Haunted Hotel and place sensors in various locations inside of it. After every two sensors are placed, Takers will spawn nearby and must be eliminated to proceed. The final sensor is placed in the hedge maze, giving players three minutes to build defenses before a four-minute defense begins.
