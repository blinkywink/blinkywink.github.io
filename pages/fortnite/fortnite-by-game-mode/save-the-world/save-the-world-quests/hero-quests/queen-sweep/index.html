Queen Sweep is a Campaign Questline in Fortnite: Save the World that can be unlocked after completing Plankerton Storm Shield Defense 1.

It rewards players with 14K Hero XP, 50 Training Manuals, 15 Pure Drop of Rain, 150 Gold, and an Epic Autumn Queen Hero.
