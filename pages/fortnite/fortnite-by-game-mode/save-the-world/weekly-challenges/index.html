Overview

Weekly Challenges are a set of Quests in Fortnite: Save the World that refresh each week on Wednesday at 8 PM EST.

Weekly Challenges allow players to earn rewards such as Gold and Battle Pass XP upon completing them. They are typically more time-consuming than other Quests.

Progress for regular Weekly Challenges does not reset, except for the Supercharger and Mythic Storm King Weekly Challenges, which do reset. If the week ends while a weekly quest is partially complete, its progress will carry over to the next week instead of restarting with a new quest.

The quest difficulty requirements and reward quantities will scale based on the player's Storm Shield progression.[1]
