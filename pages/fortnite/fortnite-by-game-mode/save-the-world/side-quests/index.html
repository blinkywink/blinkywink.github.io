Side Quests

Description

Mode

Save the World

Introduced

Pre-Season

Overview

Side Quests are a set of Quests in Fortnite: Save the World that are usually granted in a chain after completing a Storm Shield Defense. They reward players with varying items to help with progression throughout the game. Most Side Quests after Plankerton require the player to complete any of the 3 listed Missions three times, similar to the Main Quests in Twine Peaks.
