Overview

The Nurse are Husk Monsters in Fortnite: Save the World.

Behavior

Behavior[]

The Nurse has an AoE bubble around it that constantly heals herself and other Monsters. Nurses are mostly for support as they mostly head towards Husks that is low on health. Their only attacks are melee attacks, which deal the same amount of damage as Lobbers.

Encountered

Encountered[]

They begin to be encountered in harder levels of Stonewood.

Nurses are found:

  • Patrolling or sleeping
  • During objective defense
  • Spawned from Husk Encampments
Abilities

Abilities[]

  • Healing Pool - The Nurse always has a healing pool around herself, healing other Monsters and herself within a 1.536 tile radius. Nurses cannot heal each other.
  • Dodge - The Nurse has the ability to dodge to the left or right when getting attacked, possibly preventing itself from taking massive damage. This dodge can only be performed every 3.5 seconds.
  • Melee Attack - The Nurse can also swipe at players if they get too close, dealing damage. If a building blocks their path, The Nurse will use this ability to break builds.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Nurses are the newest type of Husk Monsters, being released during Chapter 2: Season 1, but only during Frostnite 2019. It was then added to the main zones at the start of Chapter 2: Season 2.
  • Along with Lobbers and Mimics, the Nurse is one of the three feminine husks.
  • The Nurse shares the same sounds with the Lobber.
