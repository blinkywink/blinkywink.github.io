Overview

Husks are the most common type of Monsters in Save the World.

Characteristics

Characteristics[]

Husk Monsters are similar to zombies from other horror media. Their body is slightly decayed with green skin and their backbone exposed. The hair and skin in the head has been ripped, exposing their skull, the ripped skin takes the form of a "hoodie". Their eyes glow orange.

Husks are formed from a corpse of humans killed by The Storm or by other Monsters, reanimated into an undead being by The Storm. Ray describes the Husks' body when the ATLAS is being attacked as "cold, (un)dead", confirming that Husks are once living humans.

Their usual behavior is attacking humans or Wildlife when there are no objectives. When an objective is placed, Husks will try to destroy them and the defenses surrounding it. Husks has some form of intelligence in that they can find weak points of a fort and exploit them, such as breaking a stone wall when the rest of the fort is metal or finding an opening. When Husks touches a mission objective, they are instantly eliminated but the objective takes damage.

When Husks are eliminated, their body disintegrates.

Encountered

Encountered[]

Husks are found in many ways:

  • Spawned around the map, sleeping
  • Randomly spawning Storms or Storm patrols.
  • Husk Encampments
  • Rescuing a Survivor (RV roof, Car roof, or on foot Survivors)
  • Opening a Storm Chest
  • Spawned during objective defense.
  • Spawned by the Flinger Mist Monster
  • Spawned by the Storm King
  • During Storm Shield Defenses
  • Dungeons, Survive the Horde, Hit the Road, and Frostnite events
