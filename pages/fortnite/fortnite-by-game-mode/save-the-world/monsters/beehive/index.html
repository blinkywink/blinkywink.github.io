Beehive Husks are a type of Husk Monster in Fortnite: Save the World. They are introduced in Stonewood Storm Shield Defense 2.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Beehive Husks appear as with a beehive on their head, surrounded by a swarm of bees. These bees will linger on any tile where the Beehive Husk has walked past, dealing damage per second to players within the tile. Shooting the beehive off the head of a Beehive Husk causes the hive to break, and turns it into a regular Husk.

Stats

Stats[]

The Beehive Husk has a movement speed of 0.60 TPS (Tiles per Second). It also has Knockback Threshold of 399.

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • A group of Beehive Husks are capable of doing a high amount of damage to a player.
  • Beehive Husks can be lured away from the objective by getting their attention.
  • AOE weapons can clear out groups of Beehive Husks quickly.
