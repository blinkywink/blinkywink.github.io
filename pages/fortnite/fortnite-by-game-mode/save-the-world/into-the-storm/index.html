Into the Storm

Overview

“
Accompany Lars and Ray on their journey into Lars' past in the brand new questline, ‘Into the Storm’!
”
— In-Game Description

Into the Storm is an Event in Fortnite: Save the World, that ran from April 11 to May 1, 2018. It introduced the Neon Weapon Set and Cyberpunk Heroes.
