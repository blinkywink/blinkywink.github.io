Overview

This article is about the Mechanic in Save the World. For LEGO Fortnite Gamemode, see LEGO Fortnite:Expeditions.

“
Send Heroes out on expeditions to find valuable resources.
”
— In-Game Description

Expeditions are a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Save the World that allow players to send their Heroes on missions to gather Crafting Ingredients, Materials, and even Survivors, Heroes, and Schematics. There are three categories of expeditions: Land, Sea, and Air expeditions.

Expeditions are unlocked upon completing the Van or Astro-van? Main Quest on Page 8 of the Stonewood Questline.

Expedition Slots

Expedition Slots[]

Expedition Slots can be unlocked by completing parts of the Main Quests, and Storm Shield Defenses.

Expedition Boosts

Expedition Boosts[]

By assigning Heroes with the matching hero class bonus, you’ll receive boosts. The effectiveness of these boosts can vary significantly depending on the Rarity of the Hero used.

Completion

Completion[]

Expeditions are automatically completed after the designated duration. Expeditions can be completed/failed depending on the percentage of success that's affected by the Heroes slotted. The tougher the expedition, the more boost is needed and the better reward will be granted.
