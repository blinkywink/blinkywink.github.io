Six Shooter Pistol Schematics

Overview

Six Shooter Pistol Schematics are a fast and precise sidearm that deal low damage. They also have a small magazine. These weapons use Light Bullets.

Obtain

Obtain[]

  • Six Shooter Pistols can be found in loot chests within a match.
  • Six Shooter Pistol Schematics can be found in the Item shop.
  • Six Shooter Pistol Schematics can be rewarded from missions.
  • Six Shooter Pistol Schematics can be researched.
  • Six Shooter Pistol Schematics can be evolved into the next rarity.

Heroes

Heroes[]

  • Six Shooter Pistols are recommended with Soldiers, Outlanders, Ninjas and Constructors.
Is It Worth Using?

Is It Worth Using?[]

  • Six Shooter Pistols in this category are some of the most basic weapons in Save The World. These pistols are especially useful for newer players of the game.
