Overview

“
Sniper Rifle: Bow - Neon. Fires an arrow that drops over time. Damage, range, and velocity increase with charge time. Arrow pierces through targets.
”
— In-Game Description

The Xenon Bow is a Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Energy Ammo and is a part of the Neon Weapons Set.

Function

Function[]

Like all other bows, the Xenon Bow can be charged to increase its damage (to a max of 1.75x), range and bullet velocity, allowing it to shoot through multiple enemies. The Xenon Bow has the special unblockable ability to pierce through all terrain and enemies, hitting many enemies lineup at once with its significantly larger hitbox.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • The Xenon Bow's projectile travels through the entire map, damaging everything in its path, essentially destroying smaller objects. This is especially helpful for Daily Destroy Daily Quests, and Dennis' Destroy Venture Quests.
  • The Xenon Bow's projectile can be ridden, and as such, it can be used to push Survivors off cars and RVs.
    • These Survivors stay invincible as long as their vehicle isn't destroyed, so it's an easy way to protect them.
    • Be wary not to mindlessly fire the weapon too much in a Rescue the Survivors mission though, as its projectiles can destroy Survivor tents and shopping carts, essentially blocking them from spawning, and soft-locking the mission, and forcing to wait the entire 20-min evacuation timer.
