Overview

“
Shotgun: Burst. Fires an extremely rapid three shot burst of nature damage that is effective against water enemies, but weak against fire enemies. Can stun enemies, but won't knock them back.
”
— In-Game Description

The Vacuum Tube Shotgun is an Epic and Legendary Shotgun in Fortnite: Save the World, that is currently only available from Birthday Llamas during Birthday Events.

The Vacuum Tube Shotgun has a Burst fire mode, meaning the weapon fires shots in groups. This weapons fires in 3-round bursts.

It had previously been available in Holiday Llamas during the Fortnite Guide to Holiday Survival event.
