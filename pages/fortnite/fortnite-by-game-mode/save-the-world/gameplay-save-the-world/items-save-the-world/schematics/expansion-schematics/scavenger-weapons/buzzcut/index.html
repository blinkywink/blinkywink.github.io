Overview

“
Assault Weapon: Semi-Automatic. A versatile rifle that rewards careful aim with high headshot damage, but can be fired very rapidly in a pinch. Reduced durability, but much cheaper to craft.
”
— In-Game Description

The Buzzcut is an Epic and Legendary Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World. It is a part of the Scavenger Weapon Set.

Function

Function[]

The Buzzcut is a hitscan, semi-automatic, and medium to long ranged weapon with a decent fire rate and deals a moderate amount of damage. It is an accurate weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.
