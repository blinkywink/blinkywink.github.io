Overview

“
Sniper Rifle: Bow - Vacuum Tube. Fires an arrow that drops over time. Damage, range, and velocity increase with charge time.
”
— In-Game Description

The Vacuum Tube Bow is a Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Heavy Bullets and is a part of the Vacuum Tube Weapons Set.

Function

Function[]

Like all other bows, the Vacuum Tube Bow can be charged to increase its damage (to a max of 1.75x), range and bullet velocity.

Spread Weapon Has 0 Spread
Horizontal Recoil 3.5
Vertical Recoil 0.5

History[]

Update v11.40

January 29th 2020

  • The Vacuum Tube Bow can now be obtained by completing the Final Frost Frostnite Challenge.

Update v12.00

February 20th 2020

  • Changes to the Vacuum Tube Bow's Chain Lightning:
    • Now triggers on hit instead of on elimination.
    • Has increased range.
    • If only two targets are within range, the lightning will bounce between them multiple times.

Update v12.30

  • March 31st 2020: The Vacuum Tube Bow can now be obtained from Llama Pinatas.

Update v12.60

  • May 20th 2020: The Fire Rate perk now decreases the amount of time it takes to fully charge the bow.

Update v23.40

February 14th 2023

  • The Vacuum Tube Bow can now be researched with 100 Legendary Flux and 1,500 Weapon Designs instead of a Weapon Research Voucher.
