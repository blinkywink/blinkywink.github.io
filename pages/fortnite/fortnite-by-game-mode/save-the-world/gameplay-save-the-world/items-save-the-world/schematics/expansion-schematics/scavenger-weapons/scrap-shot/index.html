Overview

“
Sniper Rifle: Bow - Scavenger. Fires an arrow that drops over time. Damage, range, and velocity increase with charge time. Reduced durability, but much cheaper to craft.
”
— In-Game Description

The Scrap Shot is a Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Medium Bullets and is a part of the Scavenger Weapons Set. It is nearly identical to the Knightfire, but has a lower crafting cost and durability.

Function

Function[]

Like all other bows, the Scrap Shot can be charged to increase its damage (to a max of 1.75x), range and bullet velocity.
