Overview

“
Semi-Automatic. Fast-firing shotgun with a large magazine. Good for dealing with multiple targets at short range. Reduced durability, but much cheaper to craft.
”
— In-Game Description

The Husk Buster is an Epic and Legendary Schematic in Fortnite: Save the World. It was introduced in Update v1.7 and is a part of the Scavenger Weapon Set.

It is the Scavenger version of the Stampede.

Function

Function[]

The Scavenger is a hitscan and semi-automatic weapon with a fast fire rate and deals a high amount of damage, just like its Core Game counterpart.
