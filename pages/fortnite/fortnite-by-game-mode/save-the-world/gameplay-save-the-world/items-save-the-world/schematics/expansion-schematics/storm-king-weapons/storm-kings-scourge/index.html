Overview

“
Assault Rifle: Storm King. The Storm King’s Arm, forged into an assault rifle with excellent precision and damage.
”
— In-Game Description

The Storm King's Scourge is a Mythic Assault Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World uses Energy Ammo and a is part of the Storm King Weapons Set. It was first introduced in Update v11.20.

Function

Function[]

The Storm King's Scourge is a fast-firing, accurate and long-range weapon that deals moderate to high amounts of damage. It lacks a magazine; therefore, with each shot the weapon has heat added to it and after a certain number of shots, the Storm King's Scourge will overheat and take 2 seconds to be usable again. The Storm King's Scourge also has an Ammo Cost of 1, meaning that one shot consumes 1 Bullet.

Obtained

Obtained[]

The Storm King's Scourge can be obtained by completing a Mythic Storm King Quest then beating the Mythic Storm King in Twine Peaks to obtain a random Mythic Storm King Weapon Schematic.
