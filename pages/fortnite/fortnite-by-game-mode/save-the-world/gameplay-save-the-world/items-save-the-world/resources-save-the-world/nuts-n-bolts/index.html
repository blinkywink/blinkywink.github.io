Overview

This article is about the Fortnite: Save the World resource. For the Fortnite: Battle Royale version, see Nuts and Bolts.

“
Crafting ingredient. Found in toolboxes, crates and by destroying mechanical objects.
”
— In-Game Description

The Nuts 'n' Bolts are a Resource in Fortnite: Save the World used for Crafting.

Obtaining

Obtaining[]

Nuts 'n' Bolts can be found in all Regions. Resource can be obtained by opening Tier 3 Chests and toolboxes, and by destroying encampments. Nuts 'n' Bolts and can be obtained by breaking cars, electrical towers, and mechanical objects.

Usage

Usage[]

Nuts 'n' Bolts are used to craft Ammo and Traps.

  • Medium Bullets
  • Shells 'n' Slugs
  • Light Bullets
  • Heavy Bullets
  • Explosive
  • Energy Cell
  • Anti-Air Trap
  • Boost Pad (Straight)
  • Ceiling Electric Field
  • Ceiling Gas Trap
  • Ceiling Zapper
  • Defender Post
  • Player Jump Pad (Directional)
  • Player Jump Pad (Up)
  • Retractable Floor Spikes
  • Wall Dynamo
