Overview


Ore is a type of Ingredient in Fortnite: Save the World. It can be found throughout all zones, and is used for crafting Weapons and Traps. It is found in Caves and on Mountains.

Drops

Drops[]

Each zone will drop the most common ore for all missions within it's normal level range (ex. stonewood, 1-14 = Copper), however: the missions that are above the normal level range have a medium to high chance to spawn the resources of the tier above (example: stonewood missions 15 to 19 on the edges of the map = Silver).

Recycling

Recycling[]

A more reliable way to earn these resources is to complete things like Storm Chests or finding Weapon Chests and recycle the weapons that are given as a reward from them. Depending on durability, this can yield quite a lot of ore.
