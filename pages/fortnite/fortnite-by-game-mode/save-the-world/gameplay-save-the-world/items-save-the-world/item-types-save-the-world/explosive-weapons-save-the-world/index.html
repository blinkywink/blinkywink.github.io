Explosive Weapons (Save the World)

Explosive Weapons

Details

Class

Ranged Weapon

Overview

Explosive Weapons are a subclass of Ranged Weapons in Fortnite: Save the World. The purpose of these weapons is for big crowd busting, high damage and ease in demolition at close to medium ranges. Most Explosive Weapons use Explosive, while some use Energy Cells.
