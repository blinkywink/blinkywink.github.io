Overview

Assault Weapons are a subclass of Ranged Weapons in Fortnite: Save the World. The purpose of these weapons is to be flexible in all ranges of combat. Most Assault Weapons use Light Bullets and Medium Bullets, but also often use Shells 'N' Slugs and Energy Cells.
