Overview

This article is about a Schematic in Save the World that is no longer obtainable.

“
Sword: High Tech. Energized blade with a fast sing speed for great sustained damage. Deals fire damage that is very effective against nature enemies. Cut and Thrust: A series of heavy attacks that cut twice and thrust once while moving forward.
”
— In-Game Description

The Founder's Blazing Masamune is a Rare, Epic and Legendary Sword in Fortnite: Save the World that could've been obtained by purchasing the Limited Edition () or the Ultimate Edition Founder's Pack. It is a part of the Founder's Weapons Set.

Function

Function[]

The Founder's Blazing Masamune is a fast-swinging sword that deals a moderate amount of damage.
