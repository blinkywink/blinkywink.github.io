Overview


“
Scythe: Flintlock. A slow weapon that deals high damage and moderate impact in a wide arc. Whirlwind Attack: Heavy attack that spins around several times damaging and knocking back nearby foes.
”
— In-Game Description

The Fishing Hook is an Epic to Legendary Scythe Melee in Fortnite: Save the World that is a part of the Pirate Weapons Set.
It is available in the Pirate Llamas during the Scurvy Shoals Venture Season.

Function

Function[]

The Fishing Hook is a slow-swinging Scythe weapon that deals high damage with moderate impact in a good range.
