Overview


“
Club: Heavy - Flintlock. A heavy club with a slow swing speed and large knockback potential. Home Run: Heavy attack focused on impact and a knockback that will send enemies flying.
”
— In-Game Description

The Sir Hootie is an Rare to Legendary Club Melee in Fortnite: Save the World that is a part of the Pirate Weapons Set.
It is available in the Pirate Llamas during the Scurvy Shoals Venture Season.

Function

Function[]

The Sir Hootie is a slow-swinging Club weapon that deals high damage with moderate impact in a good range.
