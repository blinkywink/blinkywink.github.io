The Third Rail is a Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Heavy Bullets and is a part of the Fortnitemares Weapon Set. It was added in Update v22.30.

Function

Function[]

The Third Rail can be fired either by fully charging it for 2.5 Seconds, or charging for a small amount of time to deal much less damage. While the Rail Gun is charging, it emits a red beam that can be seen by the user and other players. This beam will show where the bullet will hit hurting enemies through a single structure wall, or another wall if there's no enemy.

Firing the weapon from the hip will always shoot from the center of the screen. Aiming down the weapon grants a much increased zoom, removes the sway and displays an overlay with a charge bar.
