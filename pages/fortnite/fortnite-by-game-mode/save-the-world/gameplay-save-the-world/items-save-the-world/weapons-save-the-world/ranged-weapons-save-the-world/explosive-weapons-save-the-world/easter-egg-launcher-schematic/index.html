Overview

This article is about the Save the World Weapon. For the Battle Royale Weapon, see Easter Egg Launcher.


“
Explosive Weapon: Lobs eggs that explode on contact with enemies. Maximum of 6 eggs active at a time.
”
— In-Game Description

The Easter Egg Launcher is a Legendary Explosive Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Rockets and is a part of the Holiday Weapons Set.

Function

Function[]

The Easter Egg Launcher shoots out an egg projectile which is affected by gravity and sticks to surfaces, but not Monsters. After a short time the egg explodes dealing explosive damage to Monsters and environmental structures. It has an Ammo Cost of 1, unlike most other Explosive Weapons, which means it can shoot 6 times before needing to be reloaded.

Spread Weapon Has 0 Spread
Horizontal Recoil 1
Vertical Recoil 7
