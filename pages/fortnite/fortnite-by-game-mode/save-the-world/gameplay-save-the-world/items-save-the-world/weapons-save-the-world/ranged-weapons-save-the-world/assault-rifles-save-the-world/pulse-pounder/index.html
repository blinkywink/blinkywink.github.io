Overview

“
Fully Automatic when hip-fired, slower shots when aimed down sights.
”
— In-Game Description

The Pulse Pounder is an Epic and Legendary Assault Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Medium Bullets and is a part of the Fortnitemares Weapon Set. It was added in Update v22.30. It can be obtained through the Eye of the Brainstorm Questline.

Function

Function[]

The Pulse Pounder is a semi-automatic, fast firing, medium ranged weapon, which excels at medium ranged combat, but had the ability to perform decently at long ranges. It also had first shot accuracy enabling longer ranged engagements to be useful.

This weapon has two different firing modes which are switched between by aiming down the sights of the weapon and hip-firing. Aiming down the sights of the weapon makes it fire at 50% speed, but deal greater headshot damage with a 4x bonus multiplier added on its base multiplier, whilst hip-firing makes it fire faster, but deal less headshot damage with the base 1.50x multiplier.

The Pulse Pounder has an Ammo Cost of 1, meaning that each time the weapon is shot, 1 Bullet is consumed.

Bonus Perks[]

  • Each hit applies a stack of slow. When an enemy reaches 10 stacks, they are frozen for 3 seconds
  • If this weapon hits an enemy, your shield is replenished for 3% of the damage dealt and you gain 20% max shields for 8 seconds
  • Damage dealt to enemies below 15% health will immediately eliminate them.
