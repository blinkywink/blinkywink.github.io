Overview

“
This alien weapon emits a star-fueled laser blast.
”
— In-Game Description

The Shooting Star is an Epic and Legendary SMG in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Energy Ammo and is a part of the "Invasion" Set. It was added in Update v22.30. It can be obtained through the Eye of the Brainstorm Questline.

The Shooting Star currently has an Automatic firing mode, meaning that the trigger can be held down to shoot continuously.

Bonus Perks[]

  • Each hit applies a stack of slow. When an enemy reaches 10 stacks, they are frozen for 3 seconds
  • If this weapon hits an enemy, your shield is replenished for 3% of the damage dealt and you gain 20% max shields for 8 seconds
  • Damage dealt to enemies below 15% health will immediately eliminate them.
