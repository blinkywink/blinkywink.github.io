Overview

This article is about the Save the World Weapon. For the Battle Royale Weapon, see Proximity Grenade Launcher.

“
Explosive Weapon: Grenade Launcher - Spec Ops. Uses explosive ammo to shoot proximity grenades that damage groups of enemies in a moderate radius when they explode.
”
— In-Game Description

The Potshot is a Legendary Explosive Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Rockets and is a part of the Spy Weapons Set. It can be obtained from Spy Llamas for 300 X-Ray Tickets.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Potshot shoots out a projectile which is affected by gravity and sticks to surfaces, but not Monsters. This projectile functions similar to a grenade, in that it will bounce off of surfaces. When the projectile comes within a small radius of a monster, it will detonate and cause damage.

History

History[]

Content Update v12.60

March 24th 2020

  • Introduced The Potshot in Legendary.
  • March 25th 2020: The Potshot can now be purchased from the Weekly Store for 1,680 Gold.
  • April 1st 2020: The Potshot has left the Weekly Store.

Unknown Update

  • Added The Potshot to the Spy Llamas pool.

Update v23.40

February 14th 2023

  • The Potshot can now be researched with 100 Legendary Flux and 1,500 Weapon Designs instead of a Weapon Research Voucher.

Update v36.20

July 15th 2025

  • Fixed an issue causing the Xenon Bow, Plasmatic Discharger and The Potshot to incorrectly deal damage multiple times to a single target.
    • The Potshot was previously dealing damage twice from each shot, with each hit capable of critting on its own, causing larger-than-intended damage.[1]
    • This was patched, but its base damage was increased by +46.5%, along with its base Critical Hit Chance being increased by 5% to compensate.
    • Made eligible for an Item Reset.
