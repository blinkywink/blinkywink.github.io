Overview


“
Pistol: Semi-Automatic. Versatile sidearm with moderate damage, good rate of fire and large magazine.
”
— In-Game Description

The Vigilante is a Common - Rare Pistol in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Light Bullets and is a part of the Core Game Weapons Set. The High-End Variant of this weapon is the Jackal.

Function

Function[]

The Vigilante is best used in a close combat space. It deals moderate damage with a high fire rate and has a large magazine.

Trivia[]

  • The Vigilante is based on the American Colt M1911 with altered iron sights.
    • The real life Colt M1911 has an unmodified magazine size of 7 or 8 rounds, making the 16 round magazine of the Vigilante impossible for the size of its magazine.
    • The weapon also has an incorrectly modeled trigger, as the real M1911 does not have the standard curved trigger of most weapons, but uses a sliding mechanism to fire the weapon.
    • The Chapter 1 version of the Vigilante used comically large and stylized iron sights for the pistol, much larger than they would be in real life. These sights were scaled down for the Chapter 2 version of the Pistol in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The size of the end of the barrel was also reduced for its remodel.
      • The model change was reflected in Save the World as well, resulting in the old model not being present anymore.
      • As such, the Vigilante uses the Chapter 2 model, but with the Chapter 1 icon.
    • The Vigilante's slide is much more open than a traditional M1911, making it appear like a Beretta-style pistol despite all the M1911 design elements.
    • The Common, Uncommon, and Rare Pistol from Fortnite: Battle Royale share their model with the Vigilante.
