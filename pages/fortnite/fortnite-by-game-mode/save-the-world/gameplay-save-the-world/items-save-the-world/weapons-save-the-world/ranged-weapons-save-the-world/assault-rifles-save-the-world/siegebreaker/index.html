Overview

“
Assault Weapon: Fully Automatic. Flexible assault rifle capable of handling all types of combat scenarios. High rate of fire with solid accuracy when aiming down sights and firing in controlled bursts.
”
— In-Game Description

The Siegebreaker is an Epic and Legendary Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that is part of the Core Game Weapons Set. It is the High-End variant of the Breacher.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Siegebreaker is a hitscan, automatic, and medium to long ranged weapon with a fast fire rate and deals a moderate amount of damage. It is an accurate weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Siegebreaker is modeled after the Belgian FN SCAR-L.
    • The real SCAR-L uses a larger caliber bullet ( Medium Bullets) than its Save the World counterpart.
    • This is reflected in its Battle Royale version.
      • However, the Wraith, which is also based on the SCAR-L but with a suppressor, uses the proper caliber bullets.
  • The Siegebreaker and Vigilante are the only two weapons in Save the World to gain updated models on Chapter 2: Season 1's introduction.
  • A Tier 6 Ore Version of the Legendary Siegebreaker currently exists in the files but is unused.
    • The Tier 6 Ore Version (Epic) and the Crystal versions of the Siegebreaker (Equalizer) used to exist in previous versions but has since been removed.
