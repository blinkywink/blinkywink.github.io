Overview

This article is about the Save the World Weapon. For the Outfit of the same name, see Breacher (Outfit). For the Battle Royale Weapon of a similar name, see Heisted Breacher Shotgun.

“
Assault Weapon: Fully Automatic. Flexible assault rifle capable of handling all types of combat scenarios. High rate of fire with solid accuracy when aiming down sights and firing in controlled bursts
”
— In-Game Description

The Breacher is a Common - Rare Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that is part of the Core Game Weapons Set.

The Breacher has a high-tier variant called Siegebreaker.

Function

Function[]

The Breacher is an accurate, fully automatic, fast firing medium to long ranged weapon, which uses Light Bullets and has a headshot multiplier of 1.5x. The Breacher is a hitscan weapon with first shot accuracy enabled.

History

History[]

Update v1.0

Update v1.0

  • Added the Assault Rifle in Common rarity.
  • Added the Striker in Uncommon rarity.
  • Added the Breacher in Rare rarity.
Update v11.00

Update v11.00

  • All weapons that share a model are now variants of a single weapon, causing "Assault Rifle" and "Striker" to be renamed to "Breacher".
  • The Breacher now uses the Assault Rifle (v11.00 - Low-Tier) model in-game, but still uses the old Breacher icon.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Breacher is modeled after the American M4 Carbine. Its front sight is mistakenly modeled backward and the receiver lacks it's dust cover and forward assist.
    • The real M4 Carbine uses a larger caliber bullet ( Medium Bullets) than its Save the World counterpart. This is reflected in its Battle Royale version.
      • However, the Grave Digger, which is also based on the M4 Carbine but with a Halloween paint scheme, uses the proper caliber bullets.
  • The Handmade Breacher is obtained during the tutorial when teaching the player where to salvage Crafting Materials. It cannot be recycled, nor slotted into the Collection Book.
    • The Handmade Breacher was later converted into Common Rarity and replaced the existing previous Handmade Breacher.
  • The Common variant of the Breacher used to be called Assault Rifle.
  • The Uncommon variant of the Breacher used to be called Striker.
  • The Epic variant of the Siegebreaker can be unlocked after evolving the Rare Breacher.
  • Before Update v11.00, the Low-Tier Assault Rifle in Battle Royale used to share the same image and model as the Breacher, particularly due to the mode being originally built as a PvP Side of the game.
  • The Breacher was used at some point by the Cube Assassin during Chapter 2: Season 8.
    • Its ID was WID_Assault_Auto_R_Ore_T03.
