Overview


“
Sniper Rifle: Bolt-Action - Flintlock. Accurate, high-powered rifle with a single round per magazine that kicks like a mule. Her excellent damage and impact make her a prime choice for those who can handle her temper.
”
— In-Game Description

The Ol' Betsy is a Rare to Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that is a part of the Pirate Weapons Set.
It is available in the Pirate Llamas during the Scurvy Shoals Venture Season.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Ol' Betsy is a strong Sniper Rifle that deals high damage and impact, although at the cost of a slower reload time and heavy recoil.
