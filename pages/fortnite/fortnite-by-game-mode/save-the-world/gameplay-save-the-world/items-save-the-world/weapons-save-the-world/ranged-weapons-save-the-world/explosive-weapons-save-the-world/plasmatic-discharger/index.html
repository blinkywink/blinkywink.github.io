Overview

“
Launch a slow moving energy ball that zaps nearby objects and destroys any buildings in its path.
”
— In-Game Description

The Plasmatic Discharger is a Legendary Explosive Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Energy Ammo and is a part of the Fortnitemares Weapons Set. It can be purchased from the Event Store during the Hexsylvania Venture Season for 1,680 Gold.

Function

Function[]

The Plasmatic Discharger is Launcher that must be charged up to be used. After a charge-up, the Plasmatic Discharger shoots a plasma ball which flies slowly for 9 seconds and can only cover a max distance of 10 Tiles. The Plasmatic Discharger's energy ball is surrounded by a force field which deals 20% of its damage per tick.

The Plasmatic Discharger has an Ammo Cost of 30, meaning that each time the weapon is shot, it consumes 30 Energy Cells.

Update v22.30

November 1st 2022

  • Added the Plasmatic Discharger to the files.

Update v22.40

  • November 15, 2022: Fixed an issue where the Plasmatic Discharger was not assigned a class, allowing Perks to not function correctly and the weapon being able to damage the Storm King Horns.

Update v23.10

December 13th 2022

  • The Plasmatic Discharger is now eligible for an Item Reset due to the following changes:
    • Very slightly reduced damage.
    • Slightly reduced impact area.
    • Decreased the time its projectile lasts after hitting a player-built structure.  
    • Increased ammo cost per shot from 3 to 30.

Update v36.20

July 15th 2025

  • Fixed an issue causing the Xenon Bow, Plasmatic Discharger and Potshot to incorrectly deal damage multiple times to a single target.
    • Made eligible for an Item Reset.
