Assault Weapon: Burst Semi-Automatic. Fires a highly accurate two bullet burst, capable of dealing good damage at long range. Can throw lead downrange very quickly, but rewards careful aim with increased headshot damage.

The Ranger is an Epic and Legendary Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that is part of the Doubleshot Rifle Schematics.

Function

Function[]

The Ranger is a hitscan, semi-automatic, 2-round burst, accurate and fast-firing weapon that performs best at medium to long ranges.
