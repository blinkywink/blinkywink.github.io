Overview


“
Sniper Rifle: Bolt-Action. Accurate and slow firing air rifle that has modest damage but a massive headshot bonus. Remember to always wear your eye protection!
”
— In-Game Description

The Ralphie's Revenge is a Rare to Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Heavy Bullets and is a part of the Holiday Weapons Set. It is available in the Holiday Llamas during the Frozen Fjords Venture Season.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Ralphie's Revenge deals moderate damage and with low impact.
