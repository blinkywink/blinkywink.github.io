Overview


“
Pistol: Handcannon - Flintlock. Piercing single shot pistol with a powerful kick that deals heavy headshot damage and knocks back targets. Most effective when used with a sword in close to mid-range combat.
”
— In-Game Description

The Jack's Revenge is a Legendary Pistol in Fortnite: Save the World that is a part of the Pirate Weapons Set.
It is available in the Pirate Llamas during the Scurvy Shoals Venture Season, and obtainable from the Yarrr! Questline.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Jack's Revenge is a handcannon pistol that deals heavy headshot damage with high impact.
