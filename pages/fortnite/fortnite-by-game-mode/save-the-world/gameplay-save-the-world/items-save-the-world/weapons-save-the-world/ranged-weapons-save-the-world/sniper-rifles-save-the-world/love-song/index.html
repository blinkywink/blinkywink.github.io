Overview

“
Sniper Rifle: Bow. Fires an arrow that drops over time. Damage, range, and velocity increase with charge time.
”
— In-Game Description

The Love Song is a Legendary Sniper Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Heavy Bullets and is a part of the Holiday Weapons Set. It can be purchased from the Event Store for 1,680 Gold during the Flannel Falls Venture Season.

Function

Function[]

Like all other bows, the Love Song can be charged to increase its damage (to a max of 1.75x), range and bullet velocity. The Love Song has the special ability to make the Husks Lovestruck with this bow. Keeping the bow drawn past max charge will fire an arrow that stuns the enemy for 10 seconds. Weapon damage will break this effect. It does not affect Bosses or enemies that have recently been Lovestruck.
