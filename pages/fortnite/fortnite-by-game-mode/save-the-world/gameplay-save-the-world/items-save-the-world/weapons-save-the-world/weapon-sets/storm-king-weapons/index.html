The Storm King Weapon Set is a Weapon Set in Fortnite: Save the World that was introduced in Update v11.20.
Schematics from this set can only be obtained by defeating the Mythic Storm King.

It contains 2 Melee Weapons and 3 Ranged Weapons.

Requirements

Requirements[]

Your Power Rating needs to be a minimum of 122. If your Power Rating is lower than 122, you will not be able to fight the Mythic Storm King or get a Mythic Schematic Cache.
It is still possible to get carried in the mission, in case you are underpowered.

First Time[]

  1. Complete Twine Peaks Storm Shield Defense 5.
  2. Complete the King Me Quest.
    • It will unlock the Regicide one-time Quest.
  3. Defeat the Mythic Storm King in the Enter the Portal mission at the end of Twine Peaks.

Weekly[]

  1. Complete the Scions of the Storm King Weekly Quest.
    • It will unlock the Defeat the Mythic Storm King Weekly Quest.
  2. Defeat the Mythic Storm King in the Enter the Portal mission at the end of Twine Peaks.
Functionality

Functionality[]

  • After the Mythic Storm King is defeated, and if the weekly requirements are fulfilled, he will drop a Mythic Schematic Cache along with other caches.
  • Upon picking it up and returning to Homebase, you will receive one random Mythic Schematic from the Storm King Weapons Set.
    • If you have not already unlocked all five Mythic Schematics, you will be granted one of the missing Schematics. Duplicate schematics will not be given.
    • This is called dupe protection.
  • If you have already unlocked all five Mythic Schematics, you will receive one random duplicate Schematic of the five Mythic weapons.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Storm King Weapon Set was added in Update v11.20.
  • The Storm King Fist Pickaxe shares its appearance with the Storm King's Fury.
  • Items from this set cannot be researched using a Weapon Research Voucher.
  • Items from this set are apparently forged from the Mythic Storm King's body parts by Clip.
