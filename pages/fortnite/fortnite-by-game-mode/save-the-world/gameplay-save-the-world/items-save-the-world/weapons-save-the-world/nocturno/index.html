Overview

This article is about a Schematic in Save the World that is no longer obtainable.

“
Assault Weapon: Fully Automatic. Flexible assault rifle capable of handling all types of combat scenarios. High rate of fire with solid accuracy when aiming down sights and firing in controlled bursts.
"Don't worry, we'll give you a running start." - Ramirez
”
— In-Game Description

The Nocturno is a Legendary Assault Rifle in Fortnite: Save the World.

Function

Function[]

The Nocturno is a hitscan, automatic, medium to long ranged Assault Rifle with a fast fire rate that deals a moderate amount of damage. It is an accurate weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled. The Nocturno also has an Ammo Cost of 1, meaning that each shot consumes 1 Bullet.

Obtained

Obtained[]

The Nocturno could only be obtained by purchasing the Ultimate Founder's Pack for $99.99 USD. However, as of Update v13.20, Ultimate Founder's pack was removed from purchase, so it is currently impossible to obtain the Nocturno.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Nocturno's model is a Chapter 1 Siegebreaker with a black finish, Duct tape wrapped around the stock and a skull painted on the right-side front end of the gun with a very impressive amount of tally marks next to it.
    • Just like the Siegebreaker and Fortnite: Battle Royale's High Tier Assault Rifle, it is modeled after the Belgian FN SCAR-L.
  • During the E3 Gameplay Trailer for Fortnite, the Nocturno was known as Ramirez's Assault Rifle.
