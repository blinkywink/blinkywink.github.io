Overview

This article is about the Exchange. For the Pinata Packs, see Llama Pinatas.

The Exchange tab, formerly known as the Llama, Llama Shop, Items, Items Shop, Gold Store, is a Shop in Fortnite: Save the World where players can purchase a variety of different items. The shop has three different sections: X-Ray, Loot, and Items.

X-Ray

X-Ray[]

The X-Ray section is where players can buy X-Ray Llamas. These Llamas cost anywhere from 50 to 1.5K X-Ray Tickets to purchase them. They also showcase the Llama's contents on the right-hand side, allowing the player to know what they will get before buying the Llama.

Loot

Loot[]

The Loot section is where players can claim Mini Reward Llamas, or purchase Event Llamas.

Items

Items[]

The Items section is where players can purchase a variety of items with Gold. There are two subsections to this: Event Items and Weekly Items.

Event Items

Event Items change at the start of each Ventures season, while more are made available for purchase each week during the season. They include Event Schematics and Heroes, special Account Resources and a few core game items:

  • Mythic
  • Legendary
  • Epic
  • Rare

Pre-Season

  • Introduced the Llama Shop with the launch of the game.

Update v1.11

December 13th 2017

  • Introduced the Event Store.

Update v4.00

May 1st 2018

  • Loot Llama Price Reduction!
    • V-Buck prices on several of the Loot Llamas have been reduced!
    • Upgrade Llama: 100 to 50
    • Weapon Llama: 350 to 200
    • Ranged Weapon Llama: 350 to 200
    • Melee Weapon Llama: 350 to 200
    • People Llama: 350 to 200
    • It’s a Trap Llama: 350 to 200
  • Bundles containing multiple Llamas have been reduced in price as well.
  • Players who have purchased these Llamas in the past will receive an equivalent value in free Troll Stash Llamas they can redeem in the Loot tab.

Update v5.10

July 24th 2018

  • Changed countdown timers to display more useful information.
    • Shows hours left instead of days when under 72 hours.
    • Shows hours:minutes: seconds left when below 13 hours.
Bug Fixes
  • The pop-up to select to open one or all unopened pinatas no longer appear if you only have one to open.
  • Fixed the issue where a Llama from your inventory queue could be opened rather than a purchased or claimed Llama you just obtained.
  • Moved the button to open unopened pinatas to be next to the unopened pinata count indicator.
  • Fixed Llama count displayed while opening Llamas to correctly show the number of unopened Llamas.
  • Fixed an issue where the unopened pinatas indicator was behind other UI elements.

Update v7.30

January 25th 2019

  • Introduced X-Ray Llamas, along with the reworked X-Ray section in the Llama Shop.

Update v20.00

March 20th 2022

  • Reworked the currency system.
    • Llamas no longer cost V-Bucks for anyone, but X-Ray Tickets instead.

Update v33.20

January 14th 2025

  • Renamed the Llama Shop to Llamas.
