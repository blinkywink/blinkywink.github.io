Rad Llamas are a type of Seasonal Llama in Fortnite: Save the World that are available during the Blasted Badlands Venture Season, and can be purchased for 500 Road Trip Tickets.

They contain the following items:

  • 2 Boombox Melee Weapons
  • 3 Boombox Ranged Weapons
  • 4 Rad Heroes
