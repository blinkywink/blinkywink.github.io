Lunar Llamas are a type of Seasonal Llama in Fortnite: Save the World that are available during the Flannel Falls Venture Season, and can be purchased for 500 Lunar Tickets.

They contain 2 Melee Weapons, 6 Ranged Weapons and 9 Heroes.
