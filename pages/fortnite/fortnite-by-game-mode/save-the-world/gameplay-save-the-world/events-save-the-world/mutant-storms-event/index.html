Mutant Storms

Overview

This article is about the Save the World event that features special missions with modifiers. For the special missions with modifiers, see Mutant Storms.

Mutant Storms was an Event in Fortnite: Save the World that was introduced in Update v1.9.1.

Brief

Brief[]

  • The event's primary feature was Mutant Storms, missions with special modifiers that appeared randomly and for a limited time that were first seen in the Survive the Storm event.
  • It introduced Vindertech Weapons set.
  • Mutant Storms were reprised in Update v2.30 and brought with it another chance to obtain the Storm Zone Heroes and Hydraulic Weapons sets.
Tickets and Llamas

Tickets and Llamas[]

Main Article: Llama Pinatas

Players earned Storm Tickets while playing during the Mutant Storm events. They were used to purchase Vindertech (first event) or Storm (second event) Llamas at the price of 1000 tickets per Llama.

Mutant Storms

Mutant Storms[]

Main Article: Mutant Storms

Mutant Storms, first introduced in Survive the Storm, were special Missions that appeared at random on the Mission select screen. They featured a number of player and monster buffs that forced the player to adapt new strategies while attempting to complete the mission. Each Mutant Storm offered a reward of Storm Tickets, and players received tickets for the first 10 missions that they successfully completed each day.

Following the success of the Mutant Storms events, Mutant Storms were introduced as a standard feature to Fortnite: Save the World.
