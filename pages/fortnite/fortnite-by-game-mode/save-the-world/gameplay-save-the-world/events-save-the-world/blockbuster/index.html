Overview

This article is about the Save the World Questlines. For the Battle Royale set of challenges of the same name, see Blockbuster Challenges.

“
Ray has gone missing. Investigate the comet and gear up to recruit a team of superheroes. Find Ray and bring her back home!
”
— In-Game Description

Blockbuster is an Event Questline in Fortnite: Save the World that was first added in Update v4.00. It is available during the Flannel Falls Venture Season.

It has a total of 11 Pages with 32 Quests total. It is the biggest Event Questline so far.

Lore

Lore[]

Ray goes missing and the Commander along with Lok and Spitfire investigates a mysterious comet. Ray returns and Spitfire is brought back home after he discovered a vulnerability about himself and left. The Commander helps Ray make a suit for Spitfire to protect him from the radiation, making his Superhero dream come true. Chrome Huskies are taken down, and the comet is destroyed with the help of the Commander.

History

History[]

  • C2S6

Update v4.00[]

  • May 1st 2018: Added Part 1: Origin Story of the Blockbuster Questline, with Crossbow, Bald Eagle and Silenced Specter as the main choice reward.

Update v4.30[]

  • May 30th 2018: Added Part 2: A Hero Is Born to the Blockbuster Questline, with Carbide as the main reward.

Update v4.40[]

  • June 11th 2018: Added Part 3: The Cloaked Star to the Blockbuster Questline, with The Cloaked Star as the main reward.

Update v4.50[]

  • June 27th 2018: Added Part 4: The Finale to the Blockbuster Questline, with Ambush Buzz, Deadly Lotus Luna, Saboteur Bull and Undercover Vaughn as the main choice reward.

Update v16.10[]

  • April 3rd 2021: Blockbuster has returned along with the introduction of the Scurvy Shoals Venture Zone.

Update v23.30[]

  • January 31st 2023: Special missions in Blockbuster are now locked to the Mild Meadows Venture Zone.

Update v28.10[]

  • January 25th 2024: The Mild Meadows Venture Season has been removed, and Flannel Falls has taken its place.
    • As such, special missions in Blockbuster are now locked to the Flannel Falls Venture Zone.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Raven was introduced with Blockbuster in 2018 and was sold in the Event Store, but he was pushed back and got his own Questline in 2019.
  • Lok gets his ability to speak during this Questline.
  • The old-man Survivor is named "Jack". This is revealed in the "Jack of All Trades" Quest.
