Horde Bash

Description

Introduced

October 5, 2017

Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.
“
You’ll have unlimited time, but limited resources to build a portable horde fort. Once you've designed the perfect fort, exit the staging area and party with up to three friends. Your forts will be ready for action when your team hits the ground. As you survive, you’ll receive more resources and more rewards to Challenge The Horde.
”
— In-Game Description

Horde Bash was an event in Save the World that was released in Update v1.7. It featured the debut of the Challenge the Horde mission type, two new Defenders, and the Scavenger Heroes and Weapons sets.

Tickets and Llamas

Tickets and Llamas[]

Players earned Scavenger Tickets while playing during the Horde Bash event. They were used to purchase Scavenger Llamas at the price of 1000 tickets per Llama.

Image Name Cost Caption
Scavenger's Choice Llama 1000 Scavenger Tickets "Guarantees at least one Epic or better limited time Scavenger Hero or Scavenger Weapon."
Challenge the Horde

Challenge the Horde[]

The Horde Bash event debuted the Challenge the Horde mission type.

Wave Modifiers[]

Husk Modifiers[]

Normal[]

Normal waves consist of Husks, Husky Husks, Pitchers, Beehives, and Lobbers, with the occasional Mist Monster and Shielder.

Lobber[]

Lobbers waves have more lobbers than usual, destroying weak bases.

Smasher[]

Smasher waves contain many more smashers than normal, and multiple can spawn at the same time.

Event Defenders

Event Defenders[]

Horde Bash added two new defenders, the Female Shotgunner and Male Sniper to the regular llama reward pool. They became available during the event and continued to be obtainable after the event ended, dropping from any regular llama that can contain defenders.
