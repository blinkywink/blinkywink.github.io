Love Storm

Overview

This article is about the Save the World event. For the Questline, see Love Storm (Questline).

“
Love is in the air - everybody run! Homebase has fallen victim to a virulent love outbreak, and it’s up to Ray and company to break it off before things get too serious.
”
— Update v8.00

Love Storm was an Event in Fortnite: Save the World that was introduced in Update v8.00.

Overview

Overview[]

Love Storm's primary addition was the Love Storm Event Questline which rewarded players with Fallen Love Ranger Jonesy and Anti-Cuddle Sarah. It also introduced Love Lobbers for the first time.

Tickets and Llamas

Tickets and Llamas[]

Main Article: Llama Pinatas

Players earned Spring Tickets while playing certain missions during this event, as well as completing Quests. Spring Tickets were used to purchase Legendary Troll Stash Llamas at the price of 1,000 tickets each.
