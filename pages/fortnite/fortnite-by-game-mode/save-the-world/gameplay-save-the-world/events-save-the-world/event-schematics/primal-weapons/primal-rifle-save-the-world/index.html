Overview

This article is about the Save the World Schematic. For the Battle Royale version, see Primal Rifle (Battle Royale).

“
High capacity, wild firing rifle. While this weapon is held, wildlife will not attack unless provoked.
”
— In-Game Description

The Primal Rifle is an Epic and Legendary Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Save the World that uses Medium Bullets and is a part of the Primal Weapons Set. It can be purchased from the Event Store for 600 Gold during the Blasted Badlands Venture Season.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Primal Rifle is a hitscan, automatic, and medium to long ranged weapon with a moderate fire rate and deals a moderate amount of damage. It is an accurate weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled. Holding it makes Wildlife passive to the player by default, unless provoked.

Stats

Stats[]

The Primal Rifle has an Ammo Cost of 1 and a headshot multiplier of 1.5x.

  • The Primal Rifle deals aprox. 70% Damage at Mid Range (5 Tiles)
  • The Primal Rifle deals aprox. 29% Damage at Long Range (8 Tiles)
  • The Primal Rifle deals aprox. 4% Damage at Max Range (15 Tiles)
Notice: These Stats take the maximum amount of Offense Points in certain missions and the Schematic Level into consideration to attempt make an accurate statsheet for each tier. Actual stats may vary based on your Offense Points and your Level.
