Overview[]

“
Take part in new and unique Stormshield mission Simulations to put your skills to the test!
”
— Update v9.00

Wargames is a Game Mode in Fortnite: Save the World, that was added in Update v9.00. It is a Storm Shield Simulation that involves defending the Storm Shield and an Amplifier with unique modifiers.

Simulations

Simulations[]

Each Simulation requires the players to defend their base for seven minutes. A random Amplifier is then selected to defend for another seven minutes.

List of Simulations
Icon Name Description
Torn Apart A tornado is wreaking havoc on your fort! This simulation causes destruction to your structures, flinging players and enemies into the air.
Hot Spots Volcanic ruptures plague this simulation as lava pools sprout from the ground, damaging structures, and converting enemies into the Fire element.
Denied During the course of this simulation, more and more anomaly rifts appear around the defense point. Standing too close to these hazards will sap your health!
Safety Zone With the main Storm Shield down, you will have to rely on portable shield generators. Stay inside one of these protective bubbles or feel the effects of the Storm. Enemies will prioritize destroying the shield generators first so keep them protected!
Mist Pods Husk Pods appear around the map, and players must destroy them as soon as they appear. If the Pod hatches, an Epic Mini-boss is spawned.
Space Rocks Meteors will fall from the sky and damage players, husks and buildings. They need to be shot down to prevent them from doing any Damage.
Hexed Your character becomes cursed and your health starts draining. You will need to locate Aztec Statues around the Storm Shield to remove this curse.
Meltdown The storm shield emits a cloud of radiation that expands over time, damaging players.
Quantum Countdown A bomb that teleports every few minutes spawns in. Defend it from Husks or it'll explode and destroy your buildings when destroyed!
Invasion A UFO invades the sky above your Storm Shield, blasting your defenses, and beaming down an Epic Zapper or Gunslinger Mini-boss. Destroy that spinning saucer to give your defenses a breather… but like every bad sequel, it will return!
Double Trouble Two Invulerable Smashers spawn in and periodically switch between targeting you and your fort.
Shortcut Doors that act like teleporters periodically appear. They teleport husks way too close to your base. Destroy them!
Challenges

Challenges[]

If the simulations aren't challenging enough, players can apply one of the Challenge modifiers for bonus rewards.

List of Challenges
Icon Name Description
Rolling Fog A heavy fog obstructs your sights and enemies won’t appear on your mini-map.
Close Quarters Enemy spawn locations appear much closer to your base.
No Building Zone No building, no repairs, and no pickaxe.
Heavy Explosives Huskies explode when eliminated. Eliminate them before they get too close!
Trapped Out All of your traps are disabled and none can be placed in this challenge.
Full Time Time Cheats don’t appear in this simulation challenge.
Surprise The Wargames simulation is randomized and will swap to a different one in the second wave.
Head Games Damaged enemies must be finished off with a headshot!

Event: Wargames Boot Camp[]

Note: These Quests may or may not return in the future. Since the removal of the Mild Meadows Venture Zone in 2024, the seasonal LTM changed from Wargames Boot Camp to Dungeons. This change followed the introduction of Survive the Horde the previous year. Before Survive the Horde was added, Dungeons was the seasonal LTM for Scurvy Shoals. Therefore, Wargames had an open slot and returned to the Mild Meadows Venture Zone. Wargames Boot Camp's fate is currently unknown.

History[]

Season 9

Update v9.00

  • Introduced Wargames with the following Simulations and Challenges:
    • Modifiers:
      • Torn Apart
      • Hot Spots
      • Denied
      • Safety Zone
    • Challenges:
      • Rolling Fog
      • Close Quarters
      • Husk Swarm
      • No Building Zone
      • Heavy Explosives
      • Trapped Out
      • Full Time
      • Surprise
      • Head Games

Update v9.01

  • Introduced the Mist Pods Simulation.

Update v9.10

  • Introduced the Space Rocks Simulation.

Content Update v9.10

  • Introduced the Hexed Simulation.

Update v9.20

  • Introduced the Invasion Simulation.

Update v9.21

  • Introduced the Quantum Countdown Simulation.

Update v9.30

  • The “Assist Wargames” and “Assist Wargames (Repeatable)” Quests have been updated to count hosts who utilize the “Wait for Assistance” functionality in addition to players who use the Wargames Assist feature of Community Lookout.
  • Stonewood, Plankerton, Canny Valley, and Twine Peaks will now all reward the same quantity of Event Tickets for Wargames.
  • Players will now be rewarded any of the four evolution materials at all difficulties in Plankerton and Twine Peaks Wargames.
  • PERK-UP! Rewards in High level Twine Peaks Wargames will now only be of Epic or Legendary quality.
  • In the Hexed Simulation, statues no longer pop-out of visibility if the player moves far enough away from the statue.

Content Update v9.30

  • Introduced the Shortcut Simulation.

Content Update v9.30 - 2

  • Introduced the Meltdown Simulation.

Content Update v9.30 - 3

  • Introduced the Double Trouble Simulation.

Update v9.40

  • Time Cheats will now spawn more reliably in Wargames.
  • Traps no longer visually persist after their durability runs out in Wargames.
  • The Hexed statues in the Hexed Simulation no longer pop out of visibility.
  • Wargame Simulation and Challenge boxes now have scrolling sounds when selecting them while using a controller.

Season X

Update v10.00

  • Adjusted Wargames Daily Quests and Mission Rewards:
    • Stonewood:
      • Summer Tickets and 100 Gold
      • ↳ 250 Gold
    • Plankerton:
      • Summer Tickets and 100 Gold
      • ↳ Choice of 60, 35, 15, or 10, and 100 Gold
    • Canny Valley:
      • Summer Tickets and 100 Gold
      • ↳ 250 Gold
    • Twine Peaks:
      • Summer Tickets and 100 Gold
      • ↳ 250 Gold
  • Removed the Husk Swarm Simulation.
  • In the Hexed Simulation, the statues no longer leave shadows behind when they disappear.

Trivia[]

  • In Twine Peaks, it is possible to eliminate the Invulerable Smashers in the Double Trouble Simulation.
    • Lead the Smashers near a geyser and make them fall in the lava.
    • They will simply just respawn however.
