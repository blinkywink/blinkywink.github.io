Deliver the Bomb

Overview

“
Build tracks from the Armory to the Launcher, then protect the Bomb until it reaches the Launcher and gets fired at the Rift.
”
— In-Game Description

Deliver the Bomb is a mission where a bomb needs to be delivered from an Armory on tracks to a stationary Launcher. The Launcher will then use that bomb and launched it to destroy a nearby rift.

Tips

Tips[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • The first 2 things to do is to find the Armory and the Launcher. The Launcher can be found near the Alpha Rift in a grey/blue holo.
  • You will need to deposit 4 BluGlo into the armory to deploy the bomb.
  • Upon deploying the bomb, tracks will be found in the trap section of your inventory. You need to connect the Armory to the Launcher, and fortify the launcher in the meanwhile.
  • Use the lands surfaces to pin-point shortcuts. You may need to build up or down surfaces.
  • Tracks cannot be built on structures.
Objectives

Objectives[]

Your mission is:
Deliver the Bomb
Build Tracks
Craft tracks from your backpack, then build a connection between the Ammo Depot location and the Launcher location. Avoid storm twisters if possible.
Protect the Bomb
Enemies will attempt to destroy the Bomb once activated.
Complete Delivery
Launch the Bomb into the Rift to complete the delivery

Trivia[]
