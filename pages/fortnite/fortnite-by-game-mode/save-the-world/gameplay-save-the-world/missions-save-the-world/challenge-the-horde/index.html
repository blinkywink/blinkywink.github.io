Challenge the Horde

Overview

Challenge the Horde is a survival Mission in Fortnite: Save the World. It was first released on October 5th as part of the Horde Bash event and was re-released as the Road Trip (Event) with modified mechanics. Challenge the Horde mission has the following key points:

  • Select Difficulty - Decide as a team which difficulty and select it from the control panel.
  • Prepare to Fight - Use resources received before each wave to craft weapons, repair fort, places traps and deploy defenders.
  • Defend Forts - Survive, but watch out for modifiers that make things more challenging.
Challenge the Horde (Road Trip)

Challenge the Horde (Road Trip)[]

On Patch 5.0 version of Challenge the Horde, the map takes place in a unique desert biome which focuses on a tighter experience with less running from base to base between waves, and more time to focus on building and item-crafting.

Challenge the Horde can be unlocked based upon the Storm Shield Defense progression or Horde Quest progression in the Campaign. This means that players who are currently playing in Twine Peaks can expect the entire Challenge the Horde map to be unlocked. However, individual challenges within a Horde mission must still be unlocked in order. It also introduces new Storm behavior that beat back waves of enemies as the storm closes in (resets every wave) and while in the Storm, players take damage.

Resourcing[]

  • Challenge the Horde missions now grant a starter, first tier, uncommon Assault Rifle and Sword to help players hit the ground running in early Horde missions.
  • The initial ammunition grant has been increased to match the increased difficulty of the first Horde mission.
  • Building materials, ammunition, and crafting materials granted to players per skill tree upgrade have been reduced throughout the Horde Skill Tree.
Tips

Tips[]

  1. As you continue to challenge the horde, your inventory and plot size will increase.
  2. Attacks could come from any direction.
  3. Head to your console to get started.
  4. Prepare for the next wave.
  5. Resources may be scarce. Prepare accordingly.
  6. When your design is complete, exit the staging area.
  7. You and your fort will have been teleported to our combat testing facility.
  8. You can level up your building plot and inventory.
  9. You may want to focus on weapons with a low crafting cost.
  10. Your progress will be saved automatically.

Rewards[]

Mission Alerts in Challenge the Horde are available with a maximum limit of 10 per day.

  • Award Road Trip Tickets used to purchase Road Trip Event Llamas.
  • Award Horde Skill Points used to purchase nodes in the Horde Skill Tree.
  • Perk Recombobulator materials are now distributed in Challenge the Horde including RE-PERK! or PERK-UP! in larger quantities than found in Campaign missions.

Road Trip Quests[]

Horde Progression quests appear under the Event: Road Trip quest journal category and grant various rewards: Road Trip Tickets, V-Bucks, PERK currencies, a choice of Epic and Legendary Wild West Heroes, Horde Exclusive Banners. Repeatable quests for completing Horde content award a choice of Hero XP, Schematic XP, or Survivor XP.

Weekly Challenge Mission[]

Each week starting on Patch 5.20, during the run of the Road Trip event, there will be a new Weekly Challenge mission in the Challenge the Horde zone. These zones include specially crafted challenges to push player’s skills using a variety of modifiers and wave compositions.

  • Weekly Horde Challenge 1: Mini Bosses Challenge - 3 Mini Bosses spawn each wave.
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 2: Bullet Proof Husks Challenge - Bulletproof enemies that must be defeated with melee weapons, traps, or Hero abilities.
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 3: Endurance Challenge - 16 waves of shorter wave phases, less time to prepare and faster enemies.
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 4: No Trap Challenge - Husks will not trigger traps when walking over/near them.
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 5: Buff Husks Challenge - Enemies are hard to defeat, and bat players away with strong knockbacks.
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 6: Assassin Husks Challenge - Players have no shield.
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 7: Crowd Control Husks Challenge - Stuns, knockbacks and slowing fields does not affect husks.

Changes

Changes[]

Patch 5.30 (August 28, 2018)

Missions + Systems
  • Weekly Horde Challenge 4
    • This week, prepare your forts for a no-trap challenge: Husks will not trigger traps when walking over/near them.
    • Win a Legendary Redline Ramirez for the first-time completion of Weekly Horde Challenge 4.
    • The Horde Weekly Challenge quest is also repeatable for Event Tickets.

Patch 5.21 (August 15, 2018)

Missions + Systems
  • Weekly Horde Challenge - Bullet Proof Husks
    • Features bulletproof enemies that must be defeated with melee weapons, traps, or Hero abilities.
    • One time completion Quest Reward
      • 1500 Gold
    • Repeatable quest reward
      • 75 Tickets
  • Removed the Power Rating restrictions on Challenge the Horde missions.
    • These restrictions were blocking some players from accessing the Weekly Challenge Missions even though their Power Level was at the necessary level.

Fixed an issue where the modifier between Challenge the Horde missions wasn’t appearing between waves.

    • These zones include specially crafted challenges to push player’s skills using a variety of modifiers and wave compositions.
    • Weekly Horde Challenge 1: Minibosses
