Mission[]

Survive the Night is a Mission in Fortnite: Save the World that is exclusive to the Hexsylvania Venture Zone.

Overview

Overview[]

Survive the Night is nearly identical to Eliminate and Collect, where the objective is to eliminate Monsters. Additionally, 4 Vampiric Takers will spawn and are marked on the map. Eliminating them is optional, though they pose a high threat.

There is a hotel to keep safe while the storm closes in. Outside of the hotel is a large maze.

There are a select few Encampments that can spawn in the mission, as well as Survivors on the rooftop, and on top of a car right outside the hotel.

Towards the end of the mission, more waves of Husks spawn in to provide a chance to get the objectives completed faster.
