Overview

“
Participate in Beta Storms as a way to test new content and experimental ideas. Let us know what you think about this limited time mission alert.
”
— v6.10 Patch Notes

Beta Storms or Beta Storm Missions are a type of limited time Missions in Fortnite: Save the World that usually remain available for a short amount of time. They are an avenue for testing all kinds of new content, which gives the dev team a chance to gather player feedback over new content and experimental ideas.[1] A Beta Storm Mission may or may not be made available in the main pool of Missions depending on the feedback received.
