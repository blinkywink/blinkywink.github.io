Overview

Storm Zone Modifiers are a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Save the World that alter how Missions are played. Missions containing these Modifiers are called Mutant Storm Missions.

Monster modifiers have a red background, and feature buffs to Monster strength and abilities, or debuffs to Player strength and abilities.

List of Negative Modifiers

Frenzied Deathburst When Monsters die, they grant nearby Monsters a brief movement and attack speed increase. If Wildlife is nearby, they will also receive this buff.

Slowing Attacks Whenever a player is struck by a melee attack, they move more slowly for a short time.
Slowing Pools Monsters have a chance to leave a slowing pool on the ground when they die.
Smoke Screens Monsters have a chance to create a smokescreen when they die, granting enemy damage resistance to ranged attacks.
Uncharted Enemies Monsters don't show up on the minimap.
Wall Weakening Buildings become increasingly vulnerable to damage for a short time after they are attacked.
Life Leech Attacks Enemies restore health each time they deal damage.

Main Article: Wargames
  • Metal Corrosion: Metal structures take periodic damage from melee attacks.
  • Quickened: Husks gain speed after taking damage.
  • Ricochet: Mini-bosses reflect ranged damage back at attackers (rare).
  • Slowing Attacks: Melee hits slow players.
  • Torn Apart: Tornadoes destroy buildings and launch players/enemies.
  • Hot Spots: Moving lava pools damage players, buildings, and buff enemies to Fire element.
  • Denial: Anomaly Rifts hurt players who get too close.
  • Safety Zone: Players must stay in mini-shields; husks target them first.
  • Mist Pods: Pods spawn, hatching Epic Mini-bosses if not destroyed.
  • Space Rocks: Meteors fall, requiring players to shoot them down.
  • Hexed: Curses players, requiring activation of Aztec statues to remove.
  • Rolling Fog: Heavy fog obscures vision; enemies hidden from the mini-map.
  • Close Quarters: Enemy spawns are much closer to the base.
  • No Building Zone: No building, repairing, or pickaxe use allowed.
  • Heavy Explosives: Eliminated husks explode; stop them before they get close.
  • Trapped Out: All player traps are disabled.
  • Full Time: Time Cheats (e.g., extra time) don't appear.
  • Surprise: Wargames simulation randomly changes in the second wave.
  • Head Games: Damaged enemies must be finished with a headshot.

Main Article: Survive the Horde
  • Quickened: Enemies gain movement speed after taking damage.
  • Slow Burn: Husks leave slowing puddles or apply slow to players on hit.
  • Melee Mayhem: Ranged attacks bounce to players; traps and melee are buffed; ranged ammo limited.
  • Hasty Husk: Horde moves faster when damaged and heals near players.
  • Unflappable Husk: Husks ignore crowd control; increased damage to/from certain weapons.
  • Windswept: Creates damaging tornadoes.
  • Storm Surge: Smaller safe zones, frequent lightning strikes.
  • Low Gravity: Higher jumps, faster movement, disabled hoverboards, melee knockback.
  • Smog of War: Ash clouds reduce visibility; radar malfunctions.
  • Floor is Lava: Lava erupts under players.
  • Not Space Rocks: Volcanic debris falls from the sky.
