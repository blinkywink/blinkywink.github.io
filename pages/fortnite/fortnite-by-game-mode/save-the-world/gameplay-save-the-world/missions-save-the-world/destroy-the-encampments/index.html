Mission Details

Duration

Overview

“
Destroy all the Encampments!
”
— In-Game Description

Destroy the Encampments is an objective mission to destroy spawns of Monsters. There are a total of 5 encampments needing to be destroyed. Each encampment destroyed progressively increases its difficulty.

Tips

Tips[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Prepare before entering into an encampment as there will be hoards of Monsters spawning.
  • If you are unable to locate the encampment from the map, you can look into the sky for a bright vertical purple beam. Encampments are found consecutively.
  • An active encampment with have a purple hue going through it. Surrounded by the encampment are sleeping Husks.
    • An inactive encampment will have no purple hue and no sleeping Husks.
Objectives

Objectives[]

Your mission is:
Destroy all the Encampments
Strike back!
Check your map for Husk Encampments.
Strike hard!
Difficulty increases as each Encampment is destroyed.
Strike fast!
Destroy 5 Encampments in 20 minutes.
Successful Mission

Successful Mission[]

  • The mission can be completed by destroying all 5 encampments. You must wait for the remaining timer of the mission to end.
  • Bonus: By destroying all 4 super encampments regardless of the remaining mission timer, the mission will end sooner in its completion.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Unspeakable Horrors Achievement and Banner can be acquired by killing 20,000 Mist Monsters in successful missions.
