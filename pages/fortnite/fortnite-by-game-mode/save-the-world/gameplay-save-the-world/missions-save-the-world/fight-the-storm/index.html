Overview

“
Find the target. Deploy the ATLAS. Fight the Storm!
”
— In-Game Description

Fight the Storm is a type of mission in Fortnite: Save the World that requires players to defend an ATLAS against hordes of Monsters.

Objectives

Objectives[]

Your mission is:
Fight the StormFight Category 2 StormFight Category 3 StormFight Category 4 Storm
Find the Target.
Ideal storm attack points are marked in the zone. Locate the target.
Place the ATLAS.
Deploy an ATLAS device on the target. Search for Bluglo.
Fortify & Defend
Construct a fort around the ATLAS device. Defend while ATLAS laser charges.

You have to deposit 1–4 BluGlo into the ATLAS depending on the amount of ATLASes in the mission. You do not have to deposit BluGlo into each individual ATLAS.

Tips

Tips[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Floating ATLASes are the best to defend, because they just need lower structural support. After doing this, you can almost ignore the ATLAS unless there are Flingers/Lobbers. Sometimes a roof is good to have with a defender inside.
  • If a floating ATLAS falls because of the loss of structural support, the ATLAS will fail. You will then need to restart the entire defense.
  • If at anytime 1 or more ATLASes fails, the entire mission must be restarted.
  • Be aware of Wildlife and random husks breaking your builds. As a result, do not build until you are ready to defend.
  • Don’t take too long to complete the mission. You’ll fail if you take 2 days longer than the Speed Run badge.

Category Types

Category Types[]

  • Category 1 Fight the Storm is defending 1 ATLAS.
  • Category 2 Fight the Storm is the same mission but you have to defend 2 ATLASes. This is recommended to be completed with 1 - 2 players.
  • Category 3 Fight the Storm is with 3 ATLAS. Recommended 3 - 4 players.
  • Category 4 Fight the Storm is with 4 ATLASes. It also gives players an excessive amount of rewards if you're up for a challenge!

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Fight the Storm is the only mission where you can redo the mission if you fail to defend it. (HP hits 1)
  • It requires 6 Wood and 1 BluGlo to reboot the ATLAS.
  • The Category 4 Fight the Storm also has a "x4" mission type. This is an increase in the power of husks and rewards.
  • Category 4 Fight the Storms in 160 zones will give the most rewards.
  • Fight the Storm was one of the first missions available during the Alpha phase of Save the World. Back then, it was known as "Close the Gate".
