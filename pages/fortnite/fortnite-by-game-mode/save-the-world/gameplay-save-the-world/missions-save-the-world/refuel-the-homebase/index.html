Objectives

Objectives[]

  • In Refuel the Homebase, players must find and deploy Siphons on raw BluGlo deposits in order to fill up Homebase's BluGlo reserves within 20 minutes.
  • Once a Siphon has been deployed it must then be activated using the console to begin the extraction sequence, you must then protect it from the Husks for a minute.
    • Each Siphon can be activated up to three times before the deposits must be collected.
  • Once the extraction is complete you can interact with the valve at the front of the Siphon to collect every extracted drop.
    • Beware that all extracted BluGLo will be lost if the Husks manage to destroy the Siphon.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Due to the time limit of 20 minutes and downtime between finding and placing Siphons it is recommended that you split your squad over multiple Siphons to ensure you earn the platinum Medal.
  • If a defense has left a Siphon at particularly low health it is better to collect whatever tanks have been filled rather than starting another and risking losing them all; better safe than sorry!
