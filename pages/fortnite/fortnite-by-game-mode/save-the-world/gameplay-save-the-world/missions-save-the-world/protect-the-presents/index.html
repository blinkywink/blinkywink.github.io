Mission[]

“
Defend gifts dropping from the sky!
”
— In-Game Description

Protect the Presents is an objective mission exclusive to the Frozen Fjords Venture Zone in Fortnite: Save the World. It requires players to defend presents. There are a total of 5 presents that drop from the sky overtime.

Objectives

Objectives[]

Your mission is:
Protect the Presents
Find Drop Location
Build defenses around the landing zone.
Defend Presents
Defend the presents from husks!
Collect the Present
Unwrap the gifts you've defended!

    • Number of Drops: 1 Present
    • Preparation Time: 1 Minute

    • Time Until Drop: 30 Seconds
    • Number of Drops: 2 Presents

  3. Third Drop
    • Time Until Drop: 1 Minute

Successful Mission

Successful Mission[]

  • Defend at least one Present to win.
Notes

Notes[]

  • This mission is similar to Retrieve the Data.
  • Sleeping Husks spawn in huge numbers in this mission.
    • They can be of any element which makes the mission great for the Ventures: Elementary quest that requires the elimination of Nature and Fire Husks.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • This mission uses the same music as Frostnite.
  • This is the second mission to feature Dennis as the narrator, first one being Survive the Night.
  • At the start of the mission, Dennis mentions that he "signed us up for endless presents".
    • He signed us up "three-ish times" which is apparently why multiple presents can drop at once.
Update v27.11
  • Before the Update v27.11, "Crackshot's Neighborhood" was its own independant map in the Map tab before being renamed Protect the Presents and folded into the Frozen Fjords Venture Zone in 2023.
    • As such, the mission would share your base game Backpack instead of the Ventures Backpack before this change.
  • With the addition of the Frozen Fjords Venture Zone, almost all the old foliage & props, buildings and roads were replaced with Chapter 2 - 4 ones.
    • This change, however was not included in Frostnite for an unknown reason which still uses the old assets.
    • Another oversight is that Vending Machines and Repair Benches were not added to this mission, despite it being in the Venture zone.
  • There are a lot of searchable present/gift box containers in this mission.
    • This is the same reason why the community used to deem this mission as the 'best' Coal farm.
    • There is a small chance of getting Active Powercells from these containers.
