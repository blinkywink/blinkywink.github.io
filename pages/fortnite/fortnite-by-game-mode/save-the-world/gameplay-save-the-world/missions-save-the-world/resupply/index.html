Overview

“
Time to replenish your inventory!
”
— In-Game Description

Resupply is a Mission in Fortnite: Save the World that was introduced as a Beta Storm in Update v8.20, and permanently reintroduced in Update v9.30. It involves harvesting multiple objects and searching containers, and is one of the few missions to feature the Storm in it.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • It is recommended to use an Outlander for faster resourcing.
  • If you are at max Building Materials, transfer them to your Storage before playing the mission.
  • Sleeping Husks and Encampments spawn in huge numbers in this mission. Watch out!
History

History[]

  • S8
  • S9
  • C6MS2

Update v8.20

March 27th 2019

  • Introduced Resupply as a Beta Storm Mission.

Update v8.30

  • April 10th 2019: Replaced Resupply with Lost in Transit.

Update v8.50

  • April 25th 2019: Re-added Resupply.
    • Added new primary Objectives:
      • Harvest Rocks (0/15)
      • Retrieve Supply Crates (0/10)
  • The "Collect Mushrooms" and "Collect Barrels" tasks are now Bonus Objectives instead of main Objectives.
  • Increased the minimum Storm Shield radius to allow for safer harvesting.
  • Fixed an issue that caused mushrooms in Plankerton to not count.

Update v9.00

May 9th 2019

  • Removed Resupply as Beta Storm testing has ended.

Update v9.30

  • June 18th 2019: Re-added Resupply as a permanent primary Mission.

Update v38.10

November 11th 2025

  • The Storm no longer closes in during the Mission.
    • The “Retrieve the Crates” objective and Bonus Objectives (including their Badges) have been removed
    • Badges now match the number of objects you gather/search (50 = Bronze, 100 = Silver, 200 = Gold, 300 = Platinum).
