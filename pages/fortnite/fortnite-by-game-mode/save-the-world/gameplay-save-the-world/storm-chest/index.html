Overview

Storm Chests are a Side Objective in Fortnite: Save the World. They are introduced in the Air Quotes Quest in Plankerton.

Overview

Overview[]

Storm Chests only spawn in Missions of Power Level 28 or above.

Storm Chests can appear in all normal missions except for Resupply and Eliminate and Collect. Destroy the Encampments and Build The Radar Grid missions can have up to 5 Storm Chests spawns. 4x missions except for Evacuate the Shelter do not contain Storm Chests.

Storm Chests' appearance is that of a purple-tinted Level 6 Chest surrounded by a purple smoke.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Storm Chests are most commonly found along the edges or outskirts of the maps, similar to Anomalies, but can sometimes appear in the central area of a map, even near or inside of buildings. Once near them, they will be marked on the Map and Minimap with a blue icon.

Objectives

Objectives[]

Upon activating a Storm Chest, an atonal sound effect can be heard, and Husks will begin to spawn in the vicinity of the chest. The amount of rewards granted by the Storm Chest depend on the completion of the waves.

If at any point during the Waves that the number of targets were not eliminated, the Storm Chest will close and disappear for the entirety of the mission.

Rewards

Rewards[]

Rewards are granted only to players within a specific tile range at the end of the objective. This means that players outside this range may miss out on them if the Storm Chest is completed without their participation. Storm Chests grant valuable rewards due to the amount of effort required to complete them. Rewards generally include a good quantity of crafting Materials and Ammunition, and one to two Weapons. The Tier of the crafting material and weapon rewards scales with the Power Level of the mission— Silver Ore and Malachite Ore-tier weapons are likely in mid-Plankerton, for example.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Depending on the mission and Power Level of the enemies, you may need a teammate or two to help complete the Storm Chest waves.
  • Outlanders and Ninjas are most effective against the hordes.
  • Hover Turrets are extremely effective for the Storm Chest.
  • Explosive Weapons are really useful, because they can take down large hoards of enemies in one shot.
  • To ensure a considerate experience while farming Storm Chests, it is recommended to set "Team Fill" to "No Fill" before repeatedly joining and leaving missions.
  • In public missions, consider informing your teammates and invite them to join if a Storm Chest is discovered, ensuring everyone has the chance to take part and earn rewards.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Storm Chest play the same ambient loop and atonal sound as Encampments.
  • Storm Chests were used as the Cube Spawner Chests in the Horde Rush LTM in Season 9.
    • From Chapter 2: Season 8 onwards, those Chests were replaced by Level 3 Chests with a purple glow.
