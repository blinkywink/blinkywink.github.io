Overview

Power (previously called as Power Level and Homebase Power) is a measure of the strength and a direct reflection of your personal progress. It is primarily influenced by the player's Commander Level, the player's Homebase, the player's Hero Loadout, Weapons, Traps, Defenders, Heroes, Survivors squads, and the top three highest-level weapons in your inventory. It is also measures the difficulty of Missions and Expeditions.

Item Power

Item Power[]

  • The maximum power level is 145.
  • All inventory items have a Power that depends on their rating, type, Rarity and level.
  • The Power of all people and items can be increased by leveling, evolving, and supercharging it.
  • Higher rarity people and items have higher Power than others of the same type and level (with the exception of Mythic Heroes).
  • On Heroes, Weapons, Traps and Defenders, the Power influences difficulty game mechanics.
  • Survivors and Lead Survivors increases the player's F.O.R.T. Stats proportionally to their Power when placed in Survivor Squads.

Calculation[]

An approximate formula to calculate power for an individual people and item is as follows:[1] 

Power = Round( Base Power + ( (LVL - 1) * LVL Constant ) + ( (Star LVL - 1) * EVO Constant ) )

LVL is the current level of the item, and Star LVL is the current Evolution Tier of the item. Base Power, LVL Constant, and EVO Constant in the above formula depend on the type and rarity of the item, and are as follows:
