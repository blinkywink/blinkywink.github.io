Strategy

Strategy

  • Use Play with Others, available beside the Storm Shield in the Map tab, to maximise Quest and Trophy progress.
  • Eliminate as many Mist Monsters as possible to facilitate the Unspeakable Horrors achievement.
  • Activate the Hoverboard and traverse across the entire zone when in a mission to uncover the fog. Doing so grants the "Exploration Badge", which is required 1,500 times for the World Explorer achievement.
  • Despite the challenging progress of the Talented Builder achievement, refrain from overbuilding in missions as it will make the zone harder for both players and the Husks to navigate. Save that achievement for last, it can be completed in a Storm Shield.
  • Rescue every Survivor encountered to facilitate the Guardian Angel achievement.
