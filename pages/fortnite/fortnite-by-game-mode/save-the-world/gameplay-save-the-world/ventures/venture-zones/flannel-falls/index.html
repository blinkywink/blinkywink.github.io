Overview


“
Bundle up for a lakeside storm battle in the chilliest Venture yet!
”
— In-Game Description

Flannel Falls is a Venture zone in Fortnite: Save the World. It was first added in Update v14.60, and introduced on November 20, 2020. It takes place throughout spring, ending in early April.

Flannel Falls used to be the Winter Venture zone and would take place after Hexsylvania, but was replaced by Frozen Fjords in 2023. As such, it was moved ahead and replaced Mild Meadows as the Spring Venture zone in 2024.

A few special quest missions like Scan the Comet from the Blockbuster Questline are available in this zone.

Trivia[]

  • As a result of Event Flags ending a day earlier than intended, the 2025 Flannel Falls Season departed a day early, along with Blockbuster.[1]
    • The pre-game Lobby was also affected, with the 3D, environmental version being replaced by the 2D version from Battle Royale as a fallback.[2]
    • Event Heroes and Schematics likewise left the Event Store a day early.
    • The "Loot" section in the Llamas tab also appeared blank.
    • The Repeatable Quests Killer Storms and Danger in the Mist also disappeared from players' Quest Logs.[3]
