Overview

“
Bundle up for an arctic storm battle in the chilliest Venture yet!
”
— In-Game Description

Frozen Fjords is a Venture Zone available during Winter in Fortnite: Save the World. It was first added in Update v27.11, and released on November 22nd 2023. It takes place throughout fall/winter, ending in late January. It replaced Flannel Falls as the winter Venture Zone starting in 2023.

There is 1 mission exclusive to this zone: Protect the Presents.

Frozen Fjords is comprised of three snowy zones; Chilly Cove, Boreal Base, and Crackshot's Neighborhood.

The common type of materials found are Stone and Metal.
