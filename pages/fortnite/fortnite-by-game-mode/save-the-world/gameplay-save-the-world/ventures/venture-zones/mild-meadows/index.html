Mild Meadows

Mild Meadows

Beginning Date

Late January

Ending Date

Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.

Mild Meadows was one of the Venture Zones in Fortnite: Save the World. Mild Meadows is similar to Stonewood as it contains the basic and simple zones of Save The World: Suburbs, The City, Forest, Industrial Park, and Grasslands.

Mild Meadows got replaced as the Spring Venture Zone by Flannel Falls.
