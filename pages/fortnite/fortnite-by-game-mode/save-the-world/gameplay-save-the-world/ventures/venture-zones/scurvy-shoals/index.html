Overview


“
Batten down the hatches for Venture in the remote islands of Scurvy Shoals!
”
— In-Game Description

Scurvy Shoals is a Venture zone available during spring in Fortnite: Save the World. It was first added in Update v16.10, and introduced on April 3rd, 2021. It takes place throughout spring, ending in late June.

Scurvy Shoals is a tropical archipelago consisting of small towns and ancient Mesoamerican-like ruins. It is mainly comprised of the Tropical biome. Metal is scarce in this zone.
