Overview


“
Level up in the Venture theater! Build a hero team to take on the new "Rage" seasonal modifier, and scavenge for weapons using Vending Machines and Repair Benches.
”
— In-Game Description

Blasted Badlands is a Ventures zone available during summer in Fortnite: Save the World. It was first added in v13.30 patch, and released on July 27th 2020. It takes place throughout summer, ending in early September. It was the first ever Ventures zone and season.

Blasted Badlands is comprised of a portion of Canny Valley's Desert, Thunder Route 99 and Ghost Town zones, along with a few Suburbs, City and Industrial Park zones appearing throughout.

The common type of materials found are Wood and Stone.
