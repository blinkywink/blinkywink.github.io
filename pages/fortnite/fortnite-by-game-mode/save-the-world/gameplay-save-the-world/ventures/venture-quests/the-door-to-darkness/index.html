Overview

The Door To Darkness is an Event Questline in Fortnite: Save the World that was first added in Update v11.01. It is available during the Hexsylvania Venture Season, and rewards players with 200 Gold, 1,500 Candy, and a Legendary Willow.

The Questline is the second installment in a trilogy of Questlines, serving as a direct sequel to Timeless and preceding Eye of the Brainstorm. It has a total of 2 Pages with 8 Quests total.

Lore

Lore[]

After the events of Timeless, Ray made a deal with The Cube to restore Homebase's time. In return, Homebase is required to do four dark rituals. Lok, who speaks The Cube's language, translates to the rest of Homebase that the dark rituals musk be completed at Oakdirge Hollows, Stoneplancantwine's third most haunted hotel.

During their exploration, Lok and Dennis encounter a ghost named Willow, who only they can see. The rest of the Crew is initially skeptical of their claim, dismissing it as a bluff, but Willow eventually reveals herself. She explains that she was trying to scare everyone away to protect them from the impending danger of the Door of Darkness opening soon. Dennis finds Willow's diary, where she reveals that her death was surprisingly ordinary, not the dramatic end one might expect from a ghost. Despite the risks, the Crew remains determined and assist Willow in preventing the Door of Darkness from opening.
