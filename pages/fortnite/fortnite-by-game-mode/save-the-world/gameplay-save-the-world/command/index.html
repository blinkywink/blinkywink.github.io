Overview

The Command tab is a menu in Fortnite: Save the World that serves as an overview of account progress, prominently displaying the current Hero Loadout. It functions as a hub, offering various options that unlock as players advance through the campaign. It provides quick access to change the Hero, view stats, and manage all Heroes and Survivors that contribute to increasing a player's Power Level. By successfully completing missions, a player's Commander Level will increase, resulting in profile stats improving and Gadgets leveling up.

Heroes

Heroes[]

Main Article: Heroes

Unlocked after completing Stonewood Storm Shield Defense 1, the Heroes section is dedicated to heroes and the Commander. In this area, players can:

  • Manage and upgrade their Heroes
  • Customize Hero Loadouts
  • Dispatch and manage Expeditions
Survivors

Survivors[]

Main Article: Survivor

Unlocked after completing Stonewood Storm Shield Defense 1, the Survivors section is staffed by Director Riggs, and is the place where players can:

  • Manage and upgrade their Survivors
  • Organize Survivor Squads to boost Power Level and F.O.R.T. stats
Gadgets

Gadgets[]

Main Article: Gadgets

Unlocked after completing Stonewood Storm Shield Defense 3, the Gadgets section, managed by Kevin, allows players to enhance Gadgets as their Commander Level increases

Profile

Profile[]

Main Article: Profile

The Profile section is where players can spend their standard and teammate XP Boosts. Their Commander Level and upcoming rewards in the track are displayed here as well. It should be noted that since Update v39.00 Commander Levels now upgrade F.O.R.T. stats instead of Research Points.

Collection Book

Collection Book[]

Main Article: Collection Book

The Collection Book is a feature that details all Heroes, Defenders, Survivors, and Schematics, except for a select few.

Defenders

Defenders[]

Main Article: Defenders

The Defenders section, administered by Major Oswald, is where players can manage their Defenders.

  • In Update v25.20, all of Homebase members disappeared from the Frontend menus, except for Kevin in the Upgrades tab. [2]
    • This was fixed a few days later.
