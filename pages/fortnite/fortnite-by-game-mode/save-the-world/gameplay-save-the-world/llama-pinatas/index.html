Llama[]

Llamas, also known as Llama Pinatas, are the main 'loot boxes' in Fortnite: Save the World. They are also Fortnite's primary mascot.

There are many types of llamas. These include Mini Reward Llamas, Upgrade Llamas, Melee Llamas, Ranged Weapon Llamas, Hero Llamas, People Llamas, and more.

Overview

Overview[]

  • Players can open the X-Ray and Loot tabs in the Exchange, which contains options to purchase Llamas.
  • All Llamas in the X-Ray tab display the items they contain on the preview panel on the right.
  • If the player has Llama Tokens available, they will immediately be able to open them in the Loot tab, with the exception of Basic Upgrade Llamas, which will still show up in the X-Ray tab.
  • Basic Upgrade Llamas have a chance to appear in either normal, silver, or gold variants.
  • Upgrade Llamas and Mini Reward Llamas obtained through mission rewards may sometimes also upgrade to silver, with a lower chance of upgrading to gold.
Since Update v20.00, only X-Ray Tickets can be used to purchase llamas for all Non-Founders and Founders. Prior to v20.00, it was possible to spend V-Bucks on Llamas.
