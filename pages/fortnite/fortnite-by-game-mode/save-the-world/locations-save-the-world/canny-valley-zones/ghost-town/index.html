Overview


Ghost Town is a Zone in Fortnite: Save the World. It is available in mid Canny Valley, throughout Twine Peaks, and in the Blasted Badlands Venture Season.

Overview

Overview[]

Ghost Town consists of western themed towns with establishments, tourist attractions, and a large ravine with a river running through it, with the outer area of the map mainly consisting of scattered cabins and houses, with a railway snaking around the map in one big loop.

Landmarks

Landmarks[]

Western Amusement Park[]

A western themed amusement park located in Ghost Town. This landmark consists of many smaller restaurants and carnival games with a wooden roller coaster and acting theater.

Exposed Catacombs[]

A catacombs that has been exposed by a sinkhole. This landmark mainly consists of a small chapel, a graveyard, and a subterranean catacombs filled with cobwebs and coffins that has been exposed to the surface by a sinkhole.

Old Catacombs[]

An old cemetery with underground catacombs. This landmark consists of an old cemetery, with a derelict stone building in the center that leads into a catacomb filled with coffins and sometimes valuable ores.

Plains[]

A large flat area with a river flowing through it. This landmark consists of a large plain with a river flowing through it, a camper parked near the river, and a large spire jutting out into the sky.

Church[]

A moderately sized church. This landmark consists of a church located in a western themed town. Interestingly there seems to be a gnome who has been sacrificed on the altar.

NOMS[]

A western themed branch of NOMS. This landmark consists of two connected 2-story buildings with a NOMS and unnamed shop represented by a bust of a husk on the ground floor and another unnamed shop and an apartment on the second floor.

Bridge[]

A bridge that crosses a ravine made out of the natural terrain.

Oddly Colored House[]

An oddly colored western themed house. This landmark mostly consists of a 2-story house with a kitchen, two bedrooms, a shed attached to the house, an underground bathroom, and an inaccessible room filled with a gnome, several teddy bears, and lots of toys. Best for Daily Destroy type Daily Quests.

Small Farm[]

This landmark consists of a small plot of farmland and a small shed.

Small Pond[]

This landmark consists of a small pond with rubber ducks floating in it and a wagon half submerged in it.

Special Landmarks

Special Landmarks[]

Joel's Pub[]

A western themed branch of Joel's Pub. The Side Quest of the same name requires the player to visit Joel's Pub twice in a 40+ zone.

Spas[]

These landmarks usually consist of a large pueblo building with an outdoor pool in the center courtyard, various lounge chairs, bathrooms, locker rooms, and staff only areas. Every Ghost Town map is guaranteed to have one spa. The quest Retreat! requires the player to visit three spas in a 58+ Ghost Town zone.

Forts[]

These landmarks usually consist of a fortified compound usually made of wood or adobe, with cannons that the player can fire, have sometimes been converted into a museum, and always have a tall wooden watch tower. Every Ghost Town map is guaranteed to have one fort. The quest Mercerado! requires the player to fully explore three forts in a 58+ Ghost Town zone.

Train Stations[]

These landmarks usually consist of a wooden or adobe building with a platform at the back to board trains. Every Ghost Town map is guaranteed to have one train station. The quest Train In Vain requires the player to explore four train stations in a 64+ Ghost Town zone.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The whistle in the Ghost Town's music track was reused as a sound effect that plays when you entered the Tilted Town Rift Zone in Fortnite: Battle Royale.
  • A lot of "Wild West" Prefabs and Galleries in Fortnite: Creative are based on Ghost Town buildings and landmarks.
