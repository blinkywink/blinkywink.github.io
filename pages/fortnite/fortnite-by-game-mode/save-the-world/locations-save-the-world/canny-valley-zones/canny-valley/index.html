Canny Valley is the third location in Fortnite: Save the World that can be unlocked after completing the Onward to Canny Valley! Main Quest in Plankerton. It has a Recommended Power Rating of 40 to 70.

Malachite is the primary ore in Canny Valley, although you can occasionally find Silver in low-end Canny Valley zones, and Shadowshard and Obsidian in higher-end Canny Valley zones. Players are able to craft 3-Star Weapons and Traps

Canny Valley is a desert-themed region, with western towns and abandoned mineshafts that go fairly far down. It is also one of the first areas to contain natural hazards such as sandstorms that randomly appear.

Upon completing the Canny Valley Questline, players can unlock the Storm King mission.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Canny Valley was named after a failed expedition to the lost city of Tin, which resulted in cannibalism.
  • It was wishfully theorized by historians that Canny Valley's name was derived from a can.
  • Canny Valley is a play on the phrase “uncanny valley”.
