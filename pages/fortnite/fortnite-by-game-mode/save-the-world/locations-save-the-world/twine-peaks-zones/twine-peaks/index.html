“
Weather alert! These missions will challenge even the best Commanders.
”
— In-Game Description

Twine Peaks is the fourth location in Fortnite: Save The World. It has a recommended Homebase Power level of 70-160. It is the only location to not have a storyline. Its questlines consist of clearing specific types of missions multiple times.

Upon completing the Twine Peaks Storm Shield Defense 5, reaching Power Level 122 and killing 2 Minibosses in a 140+ zone, the Mythic Storm King will become available to fight.

The Twine Peaks 160+ powered level missions will give you 5x reward upon completion. Weekly quests that require 10 missions to be completed in a 160+ zone will reward players with Superchargers.

It has all the biomes from previous locations and introduces the Tropical Island biome.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Prior to Chapter 2: Season 1, the lava textures in the Twine Peaks Storm Shield were of significantly worse quality, thus earning the nickname "Twine Beans".
  • Some textures in in the Twine Peaks Storm Shield map were actually reused leftovers from Limbo, a scrapped dimension from Fortnite's earliest versions.
