Tougher enemies and greater rewards await in Plankerton.
”
— In-Game Description

Plankerton is the second location in Fortnite: Save the World that can be unlocked after completing the Van or Astro-van? Main Quest in Stonewood. It has a Recommended Power Rating of 15 to 46. 

Silver is the primary ore in Plankerton, although you can occasionally find Copper in low-end Plankerton zones, and Malachite in higher-end Plankerton zones. Players are able to craft 2-Star Weapons and Traps

Wildlife is more prominent in Plankerton, than it is in Stonewood.
