Penny is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World that is first introduced in the Stonewood's questline. Penny is a Constructor as a hero in-game. She was introduced in Online Test 6.5.

Description

Description[]

Penny is a female with pink/blond hair and has a British Accent.

Lore

Lore[]

Penny is a skilled constructor at Homebase who happens to have a friendly rivalry with Kyle in terms of Building Skill. She also has a sister named Lianne.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Penny's hair is inspired by the Marvel comics character Gwenpool, according to Gwenpool co-creator Heather Antos.
    • Coincidentally, the character would later be added as part of Battle Royale's Chapter 5: Season 4 Battle Pass.
  • Penny's dialogue icon uses the appearance of Rare Tank Penny.
