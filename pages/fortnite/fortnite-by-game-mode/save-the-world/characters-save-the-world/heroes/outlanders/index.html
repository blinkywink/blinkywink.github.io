Overview

As of May 4th 2026, there are 56 Outlanders In-Game

Outlander is one of four classes in Fortnite: Save the World. It is associated with gathering materials and exploring.

Description

Description[]

The Outlander is a lone-wolf whose abilities specialise in survival. however they have very little damage bonus and have lower health than other classes and so are not a practical class for engaging in combat. An Outlander typically relies on external support, like using a T.E.D.D.Y. or shields.

Class Abilities

Class Abilities[]

Name Description

Seismic Smash 		Smash the ground, creating a fissure of energy which deals 114 energy damage to enemies.

Phase Shift 		Rapidly shifts 1.562 tiles in the direction currently moving.

Shock Tower 		Consumes a Charge Fragment to place a Shock Tower that pulses every 0.5 seconds, and lasts for 6 seconds. Causes Chain lightning, doing a base of 18 energy damage to enemies within 1.367 tiles, stunning small enemies for 1 second. Can stun large enemies after multiple hits.

T.E.D.D.Y. 		Consumes a Charge Fragment to deploy T.E.D.D.Y. for 15 seconds. T.E.D.D.Y. will blast enemies within 4 tiles for a base of 10 physical damage, 4 times per second.
Class Perks

Class Perks[]

Name Description

Anti-Material Charge 		Punch forward, destroying structures and harvesting their materials. Deals X Damage to enemies. Activate Heavy Attack with your pickaxe equipped.

In the Zone 		After 5 hits in a row with a pick axe, gain In the Zone which increases pick axe damage by 24%.

Loot Llama 		Consumes a Llama Fragment to deploy a Loot Llama. Whack the Loot Llama with a harvesting tool to get building materials and crafting ingredients before it disappears.
