Overview

Team Perks are a type of Perk in Fortnite: Save the World that are unlocked after completing Stonewood Storm Shield Defense 4. Team Perks are powerful abilities that activate upon meeting certain requirements in your Hero Loadout.

Usage

Usage[]

Team Perks are activated upon reaching the required amount of Heroes or types of Heroes needed. Match the exact requirements, or take as many heroes as you can that match the rules in order to gain the most benefit.
