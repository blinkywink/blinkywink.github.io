Overview

B.A.S.E.
Cost: 50 energy
Cooldown: 120 seconds
Place B.A.S.E. to reinforce attached structures with 60 Armor. Extends 4 segments from placement. Placed from trap wheel.

B.A.S.E. (Blu-Glonium Actuated Structural Enhancer) is a Constructor Class Perk in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

B.A.S.E. can be accessed in Build Mode as any Constructor by selecting Floor Traps. It can only be placed on unedited and unoccupied Floors much like regular Floor Traps.

When a B.A.S.E. is placed the device will strengthen all nearby structures' Armor by 60 in a 4 tile radius, which is indicated by yellow hydraulic segments latched onto the corners of a building piece and connected together via a blue field.

When a B.A.S.E. is destroyed or moved all previously reinforced structures will revert back to normal.

All hero perks interacting with B.A.S.E. abilities counts towards Husk Extermination (Traps) Daily Quests and Trap-Vulnerable Mini-Bosses.

Notes

Notes[]

  • B.A.S.E. takes Priority on the Floor Trap selection-wheel (i.e. at the top center) when placing floor traps.
    • Unfortunately, Fortnite's history of patches and updates periodically introduces (and re-introduces) a game-breaking bug where B.A.S.E. is given LOWEST priority; thereby preventing the deployment of the B.A.S.E..
      • Game Developers' suggested workarounds are:
        1. Easiest and best solution is to always maintain less than 8 Stacks of Floor Traps.
        2. Switch the trap icon next to pyramids until it lands on B.A.S.E.
        3. Try to place a Floor Trap on an edited floor to get it to show up.

Update v3.40

  • Improvements:
    • Improved the functionality of the 'Recycling' Perk:
    • 'Recycling' will now take into account the level of the buildings near BASE.
    • Level 1 buildings are worth 1 building piece.
    • Level 2 buildings are worth 3 building pieces.
    • Level 3 buildings are worth 6 building pieces.
    • 'Recycling' now requires 3 building pieces of the same material (reduced from 5) to generate 1 of the appropriate resource.
  • Fixes:
    • Fixed issue with the 'Recycling' of the Constructor's BASE, where it could reward Wood, even when there were no Wood pieces in range of BASE.

Update v4.00

May 1st 2018

  • Fixed an issue with BASE's tooltip showing incorrect segment extensions with Heavy B.A.S.E. Kyle.

Update v4.20

  • May 16th 2018: Fixed a tooltip issue which could cause all of the Constructor BASE perks to not display correctly.

Update v6.30

November 14th 2018

  • Renamed BASE to B.A.S.E.

Update v7.10

December 18th 2018

  • Fixed an issue that allowed players to place a B.A.S.E. inside of BluGlo Pylons.
    • Converted B.A.S.E. to use Armor instead of Damage Resistance.

Update v8.00

February 28th 2019

  • B.A.S.E. re-worked:
    • B.A.S.E. is now placed like a trap, to allow constructors to use more abilities.
    • Available from the Trap Wheel.
    • Connects to structures up to 4 tiles away.
    • Increases the Armor of connected structures by 50.
    • No longer reflects damage by default.
  • Increased stability when placing Tracks and B.A.S.E. in the Builder Pro configuration.

  • March 12th 2019: B.A.S.E. can now be auto placed, just like Traps.
    • Fixed an issue where BOOM B.A.S.E. buff was not being removed after B.A.S.E. was destroyed.

Update v9.10

May 22nd 2019

  • B.A.S.E. no longer applies its effects to destroyed building pieces.

Update v9.20
