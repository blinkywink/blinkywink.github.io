Overview

For the article on the Icon Series Outfit of the same name, please see Ninja (Outfit)

Ninjas are one of the four classes in Fortnite: Save the World. They are primarily associated with Melee Weapons and close combat.

Description

Description[]

Ninja class is a Long Sword focused class, which has much better Speed and Mobility, versus the other classes in the game. They have less HP and Shield as a trade-off, but can escape easily from Husks or Wildlife attacks. Ninjas cannot take fall damage and can jump one additional time in the air. If they are using the Soaring Mantis team perk they can jump an additional two times, which can be very useful to dodge attacks from monsters.

Abilities

Abilities[]

Name Description

Crescent Kick 		A quick kick that does a base of 54 damage, 800.0 impact, and stuns for 3 seconds.

Dragon Slash 		Ninja springs forward 2 tiles, dealing a base of 95 edged weapon damage to all enemies within 0.5 tiles.

Smoke Bomb 		The Ninja throws down a smoke bomb, creating a cloud that persists for 5 seconds. The cloud slows all enemies inside by 30%, dealing a base of 40 damage every second.

Throwing Stars 		The Ninja hurls throwing stars in a straight line. Throws 1 star three times in quick succession, dealing a base of 35 edged weapon damage with each star.

Kunai Storm 		Vault into the air and hurl a barrage of Kunai at your enemies. Each Kunai deals 184 damage.
