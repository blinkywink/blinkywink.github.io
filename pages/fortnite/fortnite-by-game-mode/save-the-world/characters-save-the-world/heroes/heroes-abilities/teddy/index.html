Overview

T.E.D.D.Y.
Cost: 30 energy
Cooldown: 60 seconds
Consumes a Charge Fragment to deploy T.E.D.D.Y. for 15 seconds. T.E.D.D.Y. will blast enemies within 4 tiles every 0.25 seconds, for a base of 13 physical damage.

T.E.D.D.Y. is an Outlander Ability in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

T.E.D.D.Y. is a sustained single target (SST), damage over time (DOT) ability in the Outlander family. The T.E.D.D.Y. is a deployable auto-sentry ability. Basically a big metal bear with machine guns for arms, that fights all on his own. Only T.E.D.D.Y. may be summoned at a time. They will target anything within four tiles and even further with the right perks. T.E.D.D.Y. has a lot of damage increase perks.

Using a Fragment Charge will consume the charge, reduce the cooldown of the T.E.D.D.Y. by 30%, from 60 seconds, to 42 seconds and make it cost no energy to use.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Battle Royale outfit Metal Team Leader and Save the World hero Metal Team Leader are based off of T.E.D.D.Y.'s design.
  • T.E.D.D.Y. stands for Tactical Electronic Destruction Device... Yeah.
  • Playing as Metal Team Leader, Jilly Teacup, or Madcap as the Commander alters the appearance of T.E.D.D.Y. with a unique character model.
