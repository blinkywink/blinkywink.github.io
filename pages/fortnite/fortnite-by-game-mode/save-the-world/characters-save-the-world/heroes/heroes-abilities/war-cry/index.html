Overview

War Cry
Cost: 50 energy
Cooldown: 1 minute 30 seconds
Give a War Cry, affecting friendly characters within 4 tiles for 10 seconds. Grants 40% more damage and 40% attack speed for ranged weapons and 40% more damage and 16% attack speed for melee weapons.

Frag Grenade is a Soldier Ability in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

War Cry temporarily increases the weapon damage, fire rate and melee attack speed for the triggering player and their nearby teammates, for a limited duration.
