Overview

Lefty and Righty
Cost: 50 energy
Cooldown: 1 minute and 40 seconds seconds
Equips two pistols that fire 6 times per second, dealing 57 base physical damage per shot, for 10 seconds.

Lefty and Righty is a Soldier Ability in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Lefty and Righty allows the player to akimbo powerful hand cannons for a limited duration, firing 6 shots per second. While the ability is active, movement speed is heavily reduced. The ability also has its own Crit Rating and Crit Damage stats that can be buffed by perks.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Lefty and Righty uses the appearance of the Haywire Storm for most Heroes.
    • When using Carbide the appearnce changes to the Space Invader.
    • When using Dashing Hawk the appearance remains as the Haywire Storm but a flower design gets added.
  • This is the second ability to be a weapon added to Battle Royale, taking the form of the Dual Pistols, the first ability being Goin' Commando!!! taking the form of the Minigun in Battle Royale.
