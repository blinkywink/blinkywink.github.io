Overview

This article is about the Ability. For the LTM, see Shockwave (LTM).
Shockwave
Cost: 30 energy
Cooldown: 25 seconds seconds
The Soldier emits a shockwave, knocking back enemies within 1 tile range and dealing a base of 75 energy damage.

Shockwave is a Soldier Ability in Fortnite: Save the World.
