Overview

Phase Shift
Cost: 15 energy
Cooldown: 8 seconds
Quickly dash through space, gaining 30% increased movement speed for 3 seconds.

Phase Shift is an Outlander Ability in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Phase Shift causes players to dash in the direction of their momentum through the air, and enemies. Allowing for very quick mobility with a wide range while also granting a brief movement speed boost after dashing.
