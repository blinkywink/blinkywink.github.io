Overview

Shock Tower
Cost: 30 energy
Cooldown: 20 seconds
Consumes a Charge Fragment to place a Shock Tower that pulses every 0.5 seconds, and lasts for 6 seconds. Causes Chain Lightning, doing a base of 18 Energy Damage to enemies within 1.365 tiles, Stunning small enemies for 1 second. Can stun large enemies after multiple hits.

Shock Tower is an Outlander Ability in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Shock Tower is a highly damaging burst area of effect (AOE), damage over time (DOT) ability in the Outlander family. It lasts a few seconds, dealing Chain Lightning damage every second to targets around it within an expanding radius. It can deal a moderate amount of Impact on hit, jolting Husks to be stunned in their place momentarily. It can give you a small reprieve from being attacked when being swarmed or interrupt enemies from using ranged abilities.

Using a Fragment Charge will consume the charge, reduce the cooldown of the Shock Tower by 30%, from 20 seconds, to 14 seconds and make it cost no energy to use.
