Obtained[]

  • MEGA B.A.S.E. Kyle can be obtained:
    • from Llama Pinatas.
    • by completing quests.

Quests that offer a Legendary Hero as a reward may grant MEGA B.A.S.E. Kyle and other Mythic Heroes.
