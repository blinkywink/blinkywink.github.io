Lars is an Epic and Legendary Constructor in Fortnite: Save The World that could have been obtained by purchasing the Lars Pack. He is a part of the Starter Pack Heroes Set.

Lars (short for Larson Helmsgate) is a member of Homebase. He is a founding member of Steel Wool, a rock band which eventually broke up.

In addition to being a guitarist, Lars is a meteorologist. He assists Homebase by collecting data related to the Storm, and relaying this information for tactical use. Lars reunites with Steel Wool when Homebase starts to expand their operations in Canny Valley.

Stonewood

Stonewood[]

Lars joins Homebase after being found in the vast desert alone. He is given a place at Homebase, and when the Commander is recruited, Lars asks the commander to search for lab equipment, allowing him to research the storm further and analyze it.

Canny Valley

Canny Valley[]

Lars reunites with his old bandmates, who blame him for breaking up the band. As all of the members of the band are scientists, they all contribute in the fight against the Storm. Lars helps them to search for Anthony, who has gone missing.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Lars' name is a reference to Metallica drummer Lars Ulrich, while his design is a reference to Metallica's lead singer James Hetfield.
    • Coincidentally, both members (along with the rest of the band) would receive outfits as the featured artist of Fortnite Festival Season 4.
