Overview

Mist Monsters are one of the two types of Monsters in Fortnite: Save the World. They are the stronger form of Monsters. Similar to Husk Monsters, Mist Monsters have an anthropomorphic appearance. However, their bodies are usually twisted past recognition, barely resembling humans, with elongated, oversized or stick-like body parts or appendages, alongside numerous eyes and swirls surrounding their bodies. Their color scheme is similar to that of The Storm.

The Taker and Blaster can spawn in Shadow Orbs at cave entrances. Eliminating them grants you loot.

Types

Types[]

There are currently 10 Mist Monsters in Save the World, 8 of them are commonly found:

Name Image Intro Text AI Type Description
Taker Dive Bomb! Assassin A Mist Monster that only attacks players. Takers take on players by diving towards them, dealing large damage on contact. Can create portals to pass through structures and environmental obsticles.
Blaster N/A Assassin A Mist Monster that blasts players by firing laser beams from its mouth. Also has a melee attack if players come close.
Smasher No Wall Is Safe! Demolition A Mist Monster that can smash through fortifications guarding an objective. Will charge towards a fortification which can break or severely damage the fort on impact. Can also attack players if they come within melee range. Smashers are the only Mist Monster that can be Elemental.
Flinger It's Raining Husks! Objective A Mist Monster that can summon and fling Husk Monsters towards an objective. Can also attack players if they come within melee range.
Trolls N/A N/A A small Mist Monster that resides near BluGlo. Appears when a players tries to pick up BluGlo.
Mimics N/A Assassin A Monster with the body shape of a Lobber that take on the appearance of blue level 3 chest. They are dangerous, having High Speed, Health and dealing High Damage, but killing them grants rewards for the whole team.
Shielder N/A Defenseless A small Mist Monster that acts as a support by providing shield to Monsters. There are also super shielders that can create a shield bubble around Monsters.
Super Shielder N/A Defenseless A more powerful version of the Shielder, the Super Shielder's shield is much more resilient, and comes in the form of a wide dome that provides coverage for more than one monster. Larger versions of regular Shielders that act similar to them, floating above some Husks and Mist Monsters to shield them from damage. The biggest difference however, is that the Super Shielder creates a big ball-shaped shield allowing anything inside of the circle to be shielded from damage, along with the Super Shielder itself. The shield can be shot down, or a player can enter the shield to kill anything inside it without penalty.
Storm King N/A Assassin The final Boss in the Campaign.
Mythic Storm King N/A Assassin A significantly stronger version of the Storm King. The final Boss in Save the World.
Event Variants

Event Variants[]

Name Image AI Type Description
Krampus Smasher Objective

A Winterfest Smasher that is exclusively available during the Frozen Fjords Season. It has a beard and horns.

Its Miniboss variant of the same name is exclusive to Frostnite. It has the Vortex Modifier by default.

Vampiric Taker Assassin

A Taker that is exclusively available in the Survive the Night Mission, available during the Hexsylvania Season. It is vampiric by default, causing it to get healed whenever it melee-attacks players and Wildlife. Ocassionally, it enters a life-sucking state after hitting a target, causing them to take affliction damage over time, and increasing its own damage resistance by a significant amount.

Its Miniboss variant is Vlad, who is exclusive to the Crypts Dungeon which is available during the Flannel Falls and Hexsylvania Seasons.

Stretegy Guide

Stretegy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • If you are playing as a Ninja or Outlander, using or Mantis Leap will help you get out of the way of Takers, Blasters, and Smashers.
  • When playing as a Constructor, you can use to cancel Smasher charges. This also works for the Outlander and abilities.
  • When a Blaster starts firing, building a wall or a stair using any material will prevent the its lasers from damaging the structure or passing through it.
  • When a Taker is charging towards you, you can place a structure in front of you to block it from hitting you.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Blaster is the only Mist Monster in the game to have an appearance change.
    • Before being updated in Update v9.00, Blasters were big purple Husk with glowing purple eyes and more teeth on its lower jaw. They also emitted different noises.
  • Before Update v7.40, Flingers used to have high Ranged Weapon resistance, which was balanced out by having low health. This is no longer the case, and they now have more health but can also be effectively fought with all weapon types.
