Hero Perk[]

STANDARD PERK
Phased and Confused

Increases Phase Shift maximum charges by 1.

Passive bonus granted if this Hero is slotted in a Hero Loadout.
 
COMMANDER PERK
Phased and Confused +

Increases Phase Shift maximum charges by 3.

Replaces the Standard Perk if this Hero is slotted as the Commander.

Team Perk[]

Shifting Gears
Phase Shift has 1 additional charge(s) and Range is decreased by 0.2 tile(s).

Obtained

Obtained[]

  • Phase Scout Jess can be obtained:
    • from Llama Pinatas.
    • by completing quests.

Quests that offer a Legendary Hero as a reward may grant Phase Scout Jess and other Mythic Heroes.
