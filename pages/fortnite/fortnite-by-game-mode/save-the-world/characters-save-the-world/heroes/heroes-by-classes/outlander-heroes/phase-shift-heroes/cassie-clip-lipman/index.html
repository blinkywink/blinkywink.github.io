Overview

This article is about the Save the World Hero. For the Battle Royale Outfit of the same name, see Clip (Outfit).

“
Let's take things to the next level.
”
— Clip

Cassie "Clip" Lipman is a Legendary Outlander in Fortnite: Save the World. She can be unlocked by completing the Clearing the Air Quest in the Clip's Nightmare Questline, available during the Hexsylvania Venture Season. She is a part of the Homebase Heroes Set.

  • October 26th, 2021: Cassie "Clip" Lipman can now be purchased from the Event Store for 2,800 Gold instead.

  • September 6th, 2023: Cassie "Clip" Lipman can now be obtained by completing the Clip's Nightmare Questline again.
