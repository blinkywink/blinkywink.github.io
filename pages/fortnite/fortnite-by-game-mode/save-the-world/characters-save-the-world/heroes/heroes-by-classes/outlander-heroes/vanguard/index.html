Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.

“
Phase Shifts across the battlefield delivering unexpected Anti-Material Charges to enemies.
”
— In-Game Description

Vanguard is a Specialty Outlander available in the game. The number of perks and abilities depends on the hero's maximum evolution and level.

Maximum evolutions

Maximum evolutions[]

  • Rare: 4
  • Epic and Legendary: 5.
Perks

Perks[]

Name Unlocked Description
Energy Regen 18 Regenerate energy 25% faster.
Fist of the South Moon 5 Reduces the cooldown of Anti-Material Charge by 13 seconds.
Focused Acquisition Evo Increases chance to find double loot by 6%.
In the Zone Evo After 5 hits in a row with a pick axe, gain In the Zone which increases pick axe damage by 24%.

Abilities

Abilities[]

Name Unlocked Description
Anti-Material Charge 1 Punch forward, destroying structures and harvesting their materials. Deals X Damage to enemies. Activate Heavy Attack with your pickaxe equipped.
Loot Llama 2 Consumes a Llama Fragment to deploy a Loot Llama. Whack the Loot Llama with a harvesting tool to get building materials and crafting ingredients before it disappears.
Phase Shift Evo Quickly dash through space, gaining 30% increased movement speed for 3 seconds.
Shock Tower 2 Consumes a Charge Fragment to place a Shock Tower that pulses every 0.5 seconds, and lasts for 6 seconds. Causes Chain Lightning, doing a base of 18 Energy Damage to enemies within 1.365 tiles, Stunning small enemies for 1 second. Can stun large enemies after multiple hits.
Squad Bonuses

Squad Bonuses[]

When put into a squad slot on the Hero screen, an outlander can give either a passive support or tactical bonus. Some require a certain Primary Hero to use. Abilities that have percentages change depending on the star level.

Tactical[]

Name Description
Stun Baton Melee critical hits do increased impact and add 1.5 seconds of stun when the enemy is interrupted.
