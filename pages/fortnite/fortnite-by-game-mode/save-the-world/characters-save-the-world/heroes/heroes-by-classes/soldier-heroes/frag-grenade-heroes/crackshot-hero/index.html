Overview

This article is about the Save The World Hero. For the Battle Royale Outfit, see Crackshot.

“
Get out there and... crack some nuts.
”
— In-Game Description

Crackshot is a Legendary Soldier in Fortnite: Save the World. He can be unlocked by completing the "A Hopeful Song" Holly Jolly Ransom Quest during the Frozen Fjords Venture Season. He is a part of the Holiday Heroes Set.

Perk[]

STANDARD PERK
Mad Tidings

Firing a ranged weapon grants 1 stack of Holiday Spirit, up to 50 stacks. Each stack of Holiday Spirit increases Ranged Weapon Damage by 1% but decreases Weapon Stability by 0.2%. Buff removed on weapon swap or reload

Passive bonus granted if this Hero is slotted in a Hero Loadout.
 
COMMANDER PERK
Mad Tidings +

Firing a ranged weapon grants 1 stack of Holiday Spirit, up to 50 stacks. Each stack of Holiday Spirit increases Ranged Weapon Damage by 2.9% but decreases Weapon Stability by 0.7%. Buff removed on weapon swap or reload

Replaces the Standard Perk if this Hero is slotted as the Commander.
