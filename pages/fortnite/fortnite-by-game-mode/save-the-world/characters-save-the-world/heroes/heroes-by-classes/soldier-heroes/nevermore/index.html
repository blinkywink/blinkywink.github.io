Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.

“
Shadowy figure who screeches deafening War Cries before eliminating prey with his dual wielded pistols.
”
— In-Game Description

Nevermore is a Mythic Soldier available in the game. The number of perks and abilities depends on the hero's maximum evolution and level.

Maximum evolutions

Maximum evolutions[]

  • Mythic: 5
Perks

Perks[]

Name Unlocked Description
Advanced Tactics Evo Ranged weapon damage increased by 10%. Health increased by 10%.
Bang and Pow 25 Dealing damage with Lefty and Righty causes an explosion that deals 383.6 Energy Damage in a .5 tile radius.
Debilitating Shots 2 Dealing ranged damage applies one stack of Vulnerability, increasing damage taken from all sources by 5% on the target for 15 seconds. Stacks up to 3 times.
Dual Debilitation 5 Lefty and Righty now applies Debilitating Shots.
Explosive Rounds 15 Killing 10 enemies with a ranged weapon deals X damage in a 1 tile radius. Count resets after 15 seconds without a kill.
Kneecapper 30 Increases the effectiveness of Debilitating Shots Vulnerability stacks to 9%.
Lingering Pain 8 The Vulnerability effect from Debilitating Shots can now stack up to 5 times.
Practiced in Combat 12 Increases the duration of War Cry by 5 seconds.
Where's Lefty 18 Eliminating enemies with pistols reduces the cooldown of Lefty and Righty by 1 second.
Abilities

Abilities[]

Name Unlocked Description
Frag Grenade Evo The Soldier throws a frag grenade detonating after a few seconds, dealing a base of 101 energy damage in a 0.5 tile radius.

Squad Bonuses

Squad Bonuses[]

When put into a squad slot on the Hero screen, a soldier can give either a passive support or tactical bonus. Some require a certain Primary Hero to use. Abilities that have percentages change depending on the star level.

Support[]

Name Description
Armored Gives 8%/11%/14% damage resistance.

Tactical[]

Name Description
Explosive Rounds Killing 10 enemies with a ranged weapon deals 1,357.2 damage in a one tile radius. Does not stack with other Explosive Rounds perks.
