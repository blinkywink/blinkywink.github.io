Rifle-focused soldier who gets bonuses for successful headshots.

Urban Assault is a Specialty Soldier available in Fortnite: Save the World. The number of perks and abilities depends on the hero's maximum evolution and level.

  • Epic and Legendary: 5

Squad Bonuses

Squad Bonuses[]

When put into a squad slot on the Hero screen, a soldier can give either a passive support or tactical bonus. Some require a certain Primary Hero to use. Abilities that have percentages change depending on the star level.

Support[]

Name Description
Improved Headshots Increases headshot damage multiplier of ranged weapons by 13%/20%/27%.

Tactical[]

Name Description
Lucky Stars Throwing Stars will cause 40% extra headshot damage.

Requires Ninja as Primary.
