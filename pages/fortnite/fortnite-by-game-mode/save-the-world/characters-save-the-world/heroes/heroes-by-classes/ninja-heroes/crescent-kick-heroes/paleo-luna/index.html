Overview

“
I have more backbone than a Spinosaurus.
”
— Luna

Paleo Luna is a Legendary Ninja in Fortnite: Save the World. She can be unlocked by completing the Self Care Quest in the Tales of Beyond Event Questline during the Blasted Badlands Venture Season. She is a part of the Historic Heroes Set.

History

History[]

  • S9

Update v9.00

May 9th 2019

  • Introduced Paleo Luna in Legendary.
    • She can be obtained by completing the Self Care Quest in the Tales of Beyond Questline.

Update v11.30

December 12th 2019

  • Decreased the buff from Paleo Luna's Saurian Claws Perk.
    • Before: Adds 5.5% (16.5%) of Current Health to Melee Weapon Damage.
    • After: Adds 3% (9%) of Current Health to Melee Weapon Damage.
    • This change was made because Homebase’s max level Heroes were updated with more ability damage (up to +72% Ability Damage), and their Health and Shields were reinforced (up to +42%).
      • This does not 'nerf' Paleo Luna in a drastic manner.
