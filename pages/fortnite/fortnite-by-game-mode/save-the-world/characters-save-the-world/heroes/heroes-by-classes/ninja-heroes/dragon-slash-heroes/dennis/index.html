Hero[]

“
Who ordered the Dennis?
”

Dennis is a Legendary Ninja in Fortnite: Save the World. He can be unlocked by completing the Hostile Ventures Quest, in the Imposter! Event Questline, available during the Blasted Badlands Venture Season. He is a part of the Homebase Heroes Set.

STANDARD PERK
Burger Buff

Eliminations have a 8% chance to drop a Burger. Burgers grant 53 base Health and 5 Energy every 1 second for 3 seconds.

Passive bonus granted if this Hero is slotted in a Hero Loadout.

Replaces the Standard Perk if this Hero is slotted as the Commander.

Dennis is a former cook for Durrr Burger. He used to work there before the Storm came and took it over.

He is a major supporting character in Save the World.

Save the World

Save the World[]

Dennis appears to be a teenager between the ages of 17 and 19. He is thin and likes to keep his hair shaggy. He usually does not catch on to things right away, causing him to be clueless about situations or explanations and is prone to getting into troubling situations at first. Despite this, he is usually dependable and occurs in most of the story quests. His training at Homebase has even caused him to become a hero and take up arms towards the frontlines against the Storm.

Main Article: Imposter!

Dennis had a girlfriend named Summer, whom he still sees even after she stopped appearing herself. However, in The Long Road Home questline, it is revealed that he is destined to have a child with Sarah, who will be named Dennis Jr., who is also a playable hero.

Dennis and Sarah's relationship is expanded upon in the Imposter! and Eye of the Brainstorm Questlines.

Battle Royale

Battle Royale[]

Though Dennis only makes an appearance in the Foreshadowing Quests, it can be heard through his voice that his character remains mostly the same as he supports Mari when discussing the matter of alien sightings on the radio.
