Overview

This article is about the Save the World Hero. For the Battle Royale Outfit of the same name, see Snowstrike (Outfit).

“
Strike swiftly, and always leave your mark.
”
— In-Game Description

Snowstrike is a Legendary Ninja in Fortnite: Save the World. She can be unlocked by completing the Hunter Hextraordinaire Quest in the Hunt the Titan Questline, available year-round. She is a part of the Holiday Heroes Set.

Snowstrike could also been obtained as a reward from the Save the World Community Goal.

Perk

Perk[]

STANDARD PERK
North Star

Throwing Stars Slow enemies for 4 seconds instead of dealing damage. Enemies slowed by North Star explode for 167 base Water Damage if they receive melee damage, or damage from another North Star explosion. Additional Throwing Star effects and damage will be applied during North Star explosions.

Passive bonus granted if this Hero is slotted in a Hero Loadout.
 
COMMANDER PERK
North Star +

Throwing Stars Freeze enemies for 4 seconds instead of dealing damage. Enemies slowed by North Star explode for 167 base Water Damage if they receive melee damage, or damage from another North Star explosion. Additional Throwing Star effects and damage will be applied during North Star explosions.

Replaces the Standard Perk if this Hero is slotted as the Commander.
Crescent Kick and Dragon Slash trigger North Star explosions, since the game mechanics treat both as melee.
