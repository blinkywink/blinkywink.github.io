Ken is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World who was first introduced in the Stonewood questline.

Ken is a Ninja as a Hero in-game.

Description

Description[]

Ken is a slim male with black hair, wearing a flick-dagger on his right breast and an arm plate on his right arm.

Lore

Lore[]

Ken is a member of Homebase as a highly skilled ninja. Prior to the Storm, he was an actor who starred in many films, including his leading role in Batquake, a film about a bat scientist and a quake engineer.

Trivia[]

  • Ken is not a fan of his role in Batquake and gets annoyed when people ask him to say his iconic line: "We gettin' bat to business."
  • Ken is used as the Character Model for the Cloaked Star Outfit.
