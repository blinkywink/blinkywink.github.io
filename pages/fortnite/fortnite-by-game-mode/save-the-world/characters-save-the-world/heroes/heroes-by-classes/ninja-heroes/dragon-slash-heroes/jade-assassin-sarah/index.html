Overview

This article is about a Hero in Save the World that is no longer obtainable.

“
Green life, only life!
”
— Sarah

Jade Assassin Sarah is a Rare, Epic and Legendary Ninja in Fortnite: Save the World. She was one of the exclusive Heroes that was released to celebrate the inclusion of Xbox One X in Update v1.11. She could be unlocked by purchasing a Founder's Pack on Xbox One.

Perk[]

STANDARD PERK
Assassination

Dealing melee weapon damage grants 1 stack of Assassination (up to 5 stacks). Assassination lasts 5 seconds and increases melee weapon damage by 4.5%.

Passive bonus granted if this Hero is slotted in a Hero Loadout.
 
COMMANDER PERK
Assassination +

Dealing melee weapon damage grants 1 stack of Assassination (up to 5 stacks). Assassination lasts 5 seconds and increases melee weapon damage by 18%.

Replaces the Standard Perk if this Hero is slotted as the Commander.
