Jess is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World that is first introduced in the Stonewood questline.

Jess is an Outlander as a Hero in-game.

Description

Description[]

Jess is a blunt Outlander, typically seen wearing a scarf with a signature Outlander jacket with a light-gray turtleneck underneath and long boots.

Lore

Lore[]

In Tales of Beyond, Jess is revealed to be pragmatic, direct and possibly a bit cynical. She expresses frustration and concern about the risks involved in finding Lok, whom she refers to dismissively as "a thing." Her critical tone and the rhetorical question about the ethics of creating a child-like robot in a warzone suggest a more skeptical and blunt personality.

Jess has a more hard-edged and questioning demeanor, if compared to Luna, who is kind-hearted and empathetic.
