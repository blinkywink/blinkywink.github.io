Sarah is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World that is first introduced in the Stonewood questline.

Sarah is a Ninja as a Hero in-game.

Description

Description[]

Sarah is a skilled and confident young late-teenager who dons a no-nonsense attitude, albeit with a caring nature for members at Homebase.

Lore

Lore[]

Initially, Sarah was in a relationship with Jonesy, in the Love Storm questline, but they have a falling out.

In the Timeless questline, it was revealed that Sarah and Dennis end up having a child together in the future. She is conflicted at first, but eventually accepts the truth after their DNAs match.

In the Imposter! questline, she supports Dennis through his self-doubt, helping him defeat his impostor.

During the Eye of the Brainstorm questline, her sharp wit and determination are showcased, as the crew work on pursuing Brainstorm.
