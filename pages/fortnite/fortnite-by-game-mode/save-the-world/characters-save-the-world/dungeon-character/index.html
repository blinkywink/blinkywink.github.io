Dungeon is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World who runs four of the five main Dungeons. It is actually implied that he is The Dungeon itself as he sometime refer to the first person when talking about the Dungeons.

Overview

Overview[]

Dungeon is a very expressive Dungeon which took four forms in total over time: the Crypts, Inferno, Grotto, and Labyrinth dungeons. He constantly commentates the events that transpire within whilst giving insights on each dungeon's mechanics, backstory, and future threats that lurk in the lower floors.

Despite being the only other being present besides the husks, dungeon-goers inside each dungeon and other Dungeons, Dungeon has a boss whom he is extremely fearful of, although we never see the boss' face.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • All images of Dungeon feature him made of stone.
  • Dungeon refers to his supervisor as a "sentient cloud of pain and tormant".
    • This description is reminiscent of The Storm, who is the only known being fitting that description in Fortnite: Save the World.
  • Dungeon refers to his Boss as a being that's "two mountains tall" and one who "invented pain".
    • This description is reminiscent of Storm King, who is the only known being fitting that description in Fortnite: Save the World.
      • Dungeon's Battle Theme is a remix of Storm King's Battle Theme.
    • Dungeon fears his Boss, fearing that upon failure, he would be banished to the Nether Realm or have his soul devoured.
      • In The Labyrinth, upon seeing The Pusher being defeated by the players, he begs them not to escape the Dungeon.
  • Dungeon implied that he is not the only one existing.
    • He also revealed that every Dungeon's dream is to become a grotto.
