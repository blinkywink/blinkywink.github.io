Kevin is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World. He is a member of the Commander Support Crew and is one of the automaton companions that helps drive the storyline further.

Overview

Overview[]

Kevin is revealed to be a gadgeteer and can be found in the Upgrades tab, assisting the Commander with upgrading their Gadgets and tools. Much like Pop, he does not speak in any understandable human language, but in one consisting of beeps, clicks and other buzzing sounds.

In Canny Valley, Kevin is revealed to be working with Desiree to create a Rift Stabilization device. Ray is shown to dislike him because he seemingly wants to 'take her job'.


Kevin later on joins the fight along with the rest of the Commander Support Crew nicknamed Robo-Kevin.
