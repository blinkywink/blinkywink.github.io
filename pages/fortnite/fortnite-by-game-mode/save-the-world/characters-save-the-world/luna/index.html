Luna is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World that is first introduced in the Stonewood questline.

Luna is a Ninja as a Hero in-game.

Description

Description[]

Luna is a brave Ninja, typically seen wearing a bandana and dressed in a ninja-like outfit, characterized by her distinctive hair bun.

Lore

Lore[]

Luna is revealed to be caring and empathetic in nature in the Tales of Beyond Questline. She feels guilty for unintentionally shunning Lok, an action that led him to lose his way and encounter the Monster. To make amends, Luna takes turns caring for Lok, ensuring he feels supported. She asks the Commander to gather arcade games for Lok to help distract him from the dark memories of the Monster and also offers him advice on self-care.
