This article is about the Character from Save the World. For the Outfit and NPC of the same name, see Kyle (Battle Royale).

“
I'm sorry I ever denied you rhythm!

Kyle is a Character in Fortnite: Save the World that is first introduced in the Stonewood questline.

Kyle is a Constructor as a Hero in-game.

Description

Description[]

Kyle is a strong, large built man with tattoos on his chest and arms and a heavy strong accent. He is commonly seen wearing a lumberjack-like outfit.

Lore

Lore[]

Kyle is a skilled constructor at Homebase who happens to have a friendly rivarly with Penny in terms of Building Skill.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Sometime during Season X, Kyle's model, alongside Penny, was added to Battle Royale to test plus-sized Outfit models, which paved the way for Outfits such as The Scientist. Penny would then go on to become her own Outfit while Kyle still remains unused in Chapter 2: Season 3.
    • It is uncertain why Kyle's Outfit is still unused despite having updated Icons and an implied description via his entry in the Character Collection Book.
