Overview

Season 3 was the third season of Fortnite Festival. It began on April 23rd 2024, and ended on June 12th 2024, making it 51 days long. The featured artist was Billie Eilish.

Updates[]

Update v29.30[]

April 23rd 2024

  • Season 3 began with Billie Eilish as headliner.
    • Pro Lead was added.
    • Pro Bass was added.
    • Guitar controller support was added.

Update v30.00[]

May 24th 2024

  • Keyboard/controller support for Pro Lead and Pro Bass were added.
  • Jam Tracks can now be used as Lobby Music.

Season 3 Update Notes
New Additions
  • 2 new song parts added to all Jam Tracks: Pro Lead and Pro Bass.
  • Added support for various guitar controllers, head to fn.gg/FestivalSeason3 for more info.
  • More stats on the Results screen after each performance. See the percentage of notes hit and missed and the average from previous performances to see how you've improved.
  • New Wristband Quests and rewards have been added for Season 3.

Improvements and Fixes

  • Updated the Overdrive color for all song parts from bright orange to silver-white. - The Music Library now shows an expanded set of stats, showing your selected Instrument type's best performance in each Jam Track row.
  • The Music Library is now also ordered with new and featured Jam Tracks at the top by default for easy access to Quest-relevant Jam Tracks.
  • Spawn closer to the organ when arriving Backstage, no more sprinting to make your setlist!
