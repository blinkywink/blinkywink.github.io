Overview

Season 1 was the first season of Fortnite Festival. It began on December 9th 2023, and ended on February 21st 2024, making it 75 days long. The featured artist was The Weeknd, and it had the slogan "Opening Night".

Updates[]

Update v28.01[]

December 9th 2023

  • Season 1 began with The Weeknd as headliner.
    • Added Fortnite Festival with Main Stage and Jam Stage, along with the Festival Pass and Jam Tracks.

Update v28.20[]

February 16th 2024

  • Premiere of the music video for "Popular" took place at the Festival Jam Stage.
