Overview

Season 8 was the eighth season of Fortnite Festival. It began on April 8th 2025 and ended on June 18th 2025, making it 68 days long. The featured artist was Sabrina Carpenter.

Updates

Updates[]

Update v34.30[]

April 8th 2025

  • Season 8 began with Sabrina Carpenter as headliner.
    • New calibration settings were added.
    • Favorited outfits in the player's Locker can now appear as bandmates on stage.

Update v34.40[]

April 22nd 2025

  • Players can now start a Jam of four instruments by themselves.

Update v35.20[]

May 29th 2025

  • Replaced Jam Stage with Dance With Sabrina.
