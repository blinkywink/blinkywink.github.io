Overview

Season 7 was the seventh season of Fortnite Festival. It began on January 14th 2025, and ended on April 8th 2025, making it 84 days long. The featured artist was Hatsune Miku.

Updates

Updates[]

Update v33.20[]

January 14th 2025

  • Season 7 began with Hatsune Miku as headliner.
    • Local multiplayer was added for PlayStation and Xbox platforms.
    • Music Pass was updated to have a single rewards track to be in line with other Fortnite Crew passes.
    • The song's information was added to the bottom left of the screen.
    • Instruments can now be equipped as pickaxes and back blings.
    • Single-instrument modes for Battle Stage were added.
