Overview

Season 6 was the sixth season of Fortnite Festival. It began on November 2nd 2024, and ended on January 14th 2025, making it 73 days long. The artist featured was Snoop Dogg.

Updates

Updates[]

Update v32.00[]

November 2nd 2024

  • Season 6 began with Snoop Dogg as headliner.
    • Festival Pass was rebranded to Music Pass, with progression being relegated to XP instead of Festival Points.
    • The song's timer was added at the bottom left of the screen.

Update v33.00[]

December 1st 2024

  • Music Pass was included in Fortnite Crew.

Update v33.10[]

December 13th 2024

  • First anniversary of Fortnite Festival took place. Platinum versions of 4 headliners were released in the Item Shop and all shop Jam Tracks were made available to play for free for the whole weekend.
