Grottos are a type of Cave in LEGO Fortnite. Grotto entrances can be found scattered across the Lost Isles.

Overview

Overview[]

Grotto are caves found within the Lost isles. It contains valuable resources, such as Gold, and Emerald, and contains many pirate enemies. Unlike other caves that typically lead to a dead end or another exit, Grottos have whirlpools that can transport you back to the start and vice versa. The whirlpool does not appear in the water near the start until you've reached the whirlpool at the end.

Loot

Loot[]

The resources found within Grottos are very useful for creating Epic and Rare tools

Name Overview Visuals Mining Requirenments
