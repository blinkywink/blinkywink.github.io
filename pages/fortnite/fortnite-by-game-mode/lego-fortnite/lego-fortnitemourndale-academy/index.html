Mourndale Academy is a Named Location in LEGO Fortnite Brick Life, introduced in Update v33.10 alongside the city of Brick Bay.

Overview

Overview[]

The Academy is a gothic-structured school campus, renowned as the home of all things mysterious within Brick Bay.[2] It appears to specialize in the supernatural - notably spellcasting,[2] and seemingly offers on-grounds housing through student dormitories.

Raven serves as the headmaster and may hire the player as an Academy Professor - meanwhile, Isabelle attends the school and can grant the role of Academy Student upon being interacted with.

When a role at this location is listed under Trending Jobs, the amount of cash rewarded by it is increased by 25%.
