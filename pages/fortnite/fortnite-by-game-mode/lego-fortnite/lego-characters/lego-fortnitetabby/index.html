Overview[]

“
You can see a lot of the world in nine lives -- just ask Tabby! Fierce, friendly, and a little world-weary, this adventurous tiger woman has done it all... except settle down in a Village she could call home. Might it be time for the tiger to kick up her paws for a bit?
”
— Village Residents Page

Tabby is one of the Characters in LEGO Fortnite, that the player can recruit to their Village.
