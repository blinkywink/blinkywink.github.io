Overview[]

“
Tenacious hiker Silas is all about life on the trail! He knows everything about his favored vocation, from how to find a trail in an overgrown forest to how to make the perfect trail mix. (No peanuts, please!) If you're looking to wear in your hiking boots, Silas is your new best friend!
”
— In-Game Description

Silas is one of the Characters in LEGO Fortnite, that the player can recruit to their Village.
