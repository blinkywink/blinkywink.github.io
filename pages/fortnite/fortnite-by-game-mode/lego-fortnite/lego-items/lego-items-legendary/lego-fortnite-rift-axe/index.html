Overview

The ★ Rift Axe is a Legendary Tool in LEGO Fortnite, that was added in Update v33.10.

Obtaining

Obtaining[]

A Rift Axe can be unlock by using a  Legendary Forge on a  Gem Axe and then using the newly rifted axe until it's full potential is unlocked.

Usage

Usage[]

Chopping[]

Pressing attack while holding a Rift Axe inflicts 11 damage on any wooden-based material, such as trees or bushes. Upon damaging a wood-based material, the Rift Axe's durability decreases by 1.

Weapon[]

Pressing attack while holding a Rift Axe inflicts 11 damage on creatures. Upon damaging a creature, the Rift Axe's durability decreases by 1.

Runes[]

A Rift Axe has two Rune slots.
