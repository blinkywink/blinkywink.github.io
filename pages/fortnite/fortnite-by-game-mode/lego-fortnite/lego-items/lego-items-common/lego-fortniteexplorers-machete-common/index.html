Usage

Usage[]

Chopping[]

A explorer's machete is used to break bushes, cacti, and dangerous plants in the Lost Isles faster. Breaking a material with a explorer's machete consumes one use (one durability point).

This explorer's machete or a better one can be used to chop the following materials quicker

  •  Flexwood
  •  Vines

The explorer's machete can also disarm the following plants

  • Bomb Flowers
  • Stink Flowers

Weapon[]

Explorer's Machete can also be used as weapons but with less efficiency than swords dealing 2 damage.
