Usage

Usage[]

Mining[]

A pickaxe is used to break stone and metal-based materials faster. Breaking a material with a pickaxe consumes one use (one durability point).

This pickaxe or a better one is required to mine the following materials:

  •  Iron
  •  Rough Sapphire
  •  Malachite

Weapon[]

Pickaxe can also be used as weapons but with less efficiency than swords dealing 4 damage.

Essences[]

A pickaxe can receive the following essence:

Name Description
 Essence of Damage Increase the damage of your weapons and tools.
 Essence of Durability Increase the durability of your weapons and tools.
 Essence of Health Increase the chance of creatures & enemies dropping healing items when using weapons and tools.
 Essence of Luck Increase the chance of creatures & enemies dropping extra loot when using weapons and tools.

Once applied, essences cannot be removed or modified.
