Usage

Usage[]

Mining[]

A pickaxe is used to break stone and metal-based materials faster. Breaking a material with a pickaxe consumes one use (one durability point).

This pickaxe or a better one is required mine the following materials:

  •  Iron
  •  Rough Sapphire
  •  Malachite

Chopping[]

A forest axe is used to break wood and wood-based materials faster. Breaking a material with a forest axe consumes one use (one durability point).

This forest axe or a better one is required to chop the following materials:

  •  Frostpine

Digging[]

A shovel is used to obtain Soil from the ground through digging. Performing excavation with this shovel will allow you to obtain one soil and consumes one use (one durability point).

It also allows, with a certain probability, the acquisition of seeds from the crops native to the biome in which you are located.

Weapon[]

Pressing attack while holding a sword inflicts 3 damage on creatures. Upon damaging a creature, the sword's durability decreases by 1.

In order to use a crossbow, the player must have at least one arrow in their inventory. Pressing attack while holding a crossbow an arrow will be loaded in 1.33s and pressing attack again will launch the arrow dealing 3 damage or 10 headshot damage.

Pickaxe can also be used as weapons but with less efficiency than swords dealing 4 damage.
