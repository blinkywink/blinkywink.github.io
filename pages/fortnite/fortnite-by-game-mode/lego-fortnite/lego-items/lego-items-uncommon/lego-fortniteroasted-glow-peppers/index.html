Overview

Roasted Glow Peppers is an Uncommon Food in LEGO Fortnite, that was added in Update v31.20.

Obtaining

Obtaining[]

Unlocking Recipe[]

The recipe for Roasted Glow Peppers is unlocked once the player collects a  Glowing Pepper.

Crafting[]

Roasted Glow Peppers can be crafted through the  Grill.

Eating[]

To eat Roasted Glow Peppers, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores  +30 health and grants fifteen minutes of an aura of light around the player.

Before Update v37.50, eating one would have restored  +30 health, +3 satiety, and granted two minutes and thirty seconds of cold resistance.

History

History[]

  • Update v31.20:
    • Roasted Pepper was added.
  • Update v33.10:
    • Roasted Peppers rarity was changed from Rare to Uncommon.
  • Update v37.20:
    • Health gained was increased from   to  +30 health.
  • Update v37.50:
    • The Roasted Pepper was renamed to the Roasted Glow Peppers.
    • Description was changed from "Eat to heal and reduce Hunger."
      to "Eat to temporarily give off an aura of light. Does not stack with other carried light sources."
    • The crafting recipe was changed from 1  Spicy Pepper to 5  Glowing Pepper.
    • The consumption time was increased from 0.6 seconds to 2 seconds.
    • Satiety gained was remove with the removal of the hungry system.
    • Instead of gaining 2 minutes and 30 seconds of cold resistance, Roasted Glow Peppers now grants 14 minutes and 59 seconds of an aura of light around the player.
  • Update v38.10:
    • Instead of gaining 14 minutes and 59 seconds of an aura of light around the player, Roasted Glow Peppers now grants 15 minutes.
Trivia

Trivia[]
