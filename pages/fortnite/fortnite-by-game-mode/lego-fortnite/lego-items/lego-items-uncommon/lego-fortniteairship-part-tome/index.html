Overview

Airship Part Tome is an Uncommon Tome in LEGO Fortnite:Odyssey, that was added in Update v40.10.

Obtaining

Obtaining[]

Fishing[]

The Airship Part Tome can be obtained by throwing  Flintlocke's Bait Bucket into water near the Misty Isle and then using a fishing it up.

Chest Loot[]

The Airship Part Tome can be obtained by by defeating the The Thing From Beneath and opening the chest spawned in after.

Usage

Usage[]

Reading[]

Airship Part Tome upon reading will give the player new Building Parts and Toys.

Building Parts & Toys[]

Zeppelin Deck
Zeppelin Wing Keel
Zeppelin Thruster
Zeppelin Nav Computer
Zeppelin Balloon
Zeppelin Cannon Figurehead
Zeppelin Steering Helm
Steering Thruster
