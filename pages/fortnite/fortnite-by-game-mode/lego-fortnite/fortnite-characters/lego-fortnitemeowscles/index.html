Overview[]

“
Swole cat Meowscles is always looking for a new exercise buddy! He knows everything about stretches, endurance training, and chasing the red dot, but also knows the value of a good nap in the sun.
”
— In-Game Description

Meowscles is one of the Characters in LEGO Fortnite, that the player can recruit to their Village.
