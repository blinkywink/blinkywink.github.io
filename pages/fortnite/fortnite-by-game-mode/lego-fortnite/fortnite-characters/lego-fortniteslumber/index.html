Overview[]

“
Life's a dream for Slumber, a sweet little star who's adventuring across the universe hunting for a cozy new home! They could use your help getting the lay of the land, and maybe making a friend or two.
”
— In-Game Description

Slumber is one of the Characters in LEGO Fortnite, that the player can recruit to their Village.
