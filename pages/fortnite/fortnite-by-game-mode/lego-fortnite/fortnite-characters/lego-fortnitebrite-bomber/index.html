Overview[]

“
The world is always a lot briter when Brite Bomber's in town. She's a vibrant, adventurous, and stylishly spirited friend to all.
”
— In-Game Description

Brite Bomber is one of the Characters in LEGO Fortnite, that the player can recruit to their Village.

She is one of the first Characters that the player may encounter when they enter a world for the first time after the world is initially created, however there is a chance that she will be swapped out with Cuddle Team Leader for that world. She spawns near the player when the world is created and she provides tips and tutorial for player.
