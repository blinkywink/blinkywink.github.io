Overview

“
Cook more potent foods used for healing and reducing Hunger.
”
— In-Game Description

The Oven is a Station in LEGO Fortnite, that can be used for cooking different types of foods.

Obtaining

Obtaining[]

Unlocking Recipe[]

The Oven recipe is unlocked once the player collects either Brightcore, Copper Bars or Obsidian Slabs.

Building[]

The Oven requires: 8 Brightcore, 15 Copper Bars, and 6 Obsidian Slabs. The Oven can be built instantly and placed anywhere.

Usage

Usage[]

Crafting[]

The Oven can be accessed by pressing the "Interact" keybind. It cannot be accessed if it is exposed while it is raining. Once opened, the player can choose an Item he wants to craft. By pressing the "Craft" Button in the bottom right, the Item can be crafted, given the player has the necessary items needed for crafting the Item.

Assigned Villagers[]

Once the player has built a Oven, a Villager job under the Food Jobs category is unlocked, titled Cook food!. which will take the Villager 1 in-game day to complete.
