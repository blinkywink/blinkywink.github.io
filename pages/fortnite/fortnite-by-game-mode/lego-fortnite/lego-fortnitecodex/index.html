Overview

The Codex is an informative list of every type of item, rune, resource, creature, and NPC in LEGO Fortnite Odyssey.

Usage

Usage[]

The primary function of the Codex is to inform the player of what you’ve discovered, get hints on what they have not yet found, and track recipes you're working on.

The Codex is found in the inventory.
