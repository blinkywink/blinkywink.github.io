The Juicer is a Station in LEGO Fortnite used to produce various drinks.

Productions

Productions[]

Once placed, the Juicer can be accessed by pressing the "Interact" keybind.

Using the Juicer, the player can choose between different recipes. To refine items, the player can insert Food either by either pressing the "Arrow Up" button, which inserts one Item at a time, or the "Quick Deposit" button, which instantly inserts as many items needed to make one item. The refining takes roughly 10s.
