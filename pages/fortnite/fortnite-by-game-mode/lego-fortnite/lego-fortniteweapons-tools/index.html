LEGO Fortnite:Weapons & Tools

Overview

Weapons & Tools are the primary ways to deal Damage and harvest materials in LEGO Fortnite. Weapons & Tools are craftable throughout the game using the Crafting Bench.

Overview

Overview[]

Weapons are a strictly defined group of items only used for combat, dealing more damage compared to Tools. They can not be used to harvest a certain material quicker. There are both Melee weapons used for close combat, and projectiles used for long-ranged combat.

Tools are also a strictly defined group used to either harvest materials quicker than normal, or catch and attract fish.

An item is classified as a Weapon or Tool if they are in that tab in the Sandbox version of LEGO Fortnite.
