Lava Cave is a type of Cave in LEGO Fortnite. Lava Cave entrances can be found in various places of the Dry Valley biome.

Overview

Overview[]

Lava caves are very difficult to roam around in. You'll need strong tools and lots of health to survive. You need a Rare Pickaxe to mine the goods in lava caves. You might also need to bring a villager with you to distract enemies and shields to block ranged and explosives.

A lava cave is the hottest place in LEGO Fortnite, meaning you are required to have items to cool you down. You either need 3 Uncommon Cool-Headed Charms) or 2 Rare Cool-Headed Charms and heat resistance to survive.
