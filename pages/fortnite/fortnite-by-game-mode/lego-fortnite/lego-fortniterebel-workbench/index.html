The Rebel Workbench is a Station in LEGO Fortnite, that can be used to create different Star Wars weapons.

Obtaining

Obtaining[]

Unlocking Recipe[]

The Rebel Workbench recipe is unlocked once the player collects either  Plastoid.

Building[]

The Rebel Workbench requires: 5  Wood and 8  Plastoid. The Rebel Workbench can be built instantly and placed anywhere.

Usage

Usage[]

Crafting[]

The Rebel Workbench can be accessed by pressing the "Interact" keybind. It cannot be accessed if it is exposed while it is raining. Once opened, the player can choose an Star Wars weapon they want to be made. By pressing the recipe on the left side of the menu and pressing the plus button in the top section of the menu, the weapon can be created, given the player has the necessary resources needed for creating the weapons.
