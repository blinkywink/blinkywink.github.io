Overview

“
Not everything you need can be found on the surface. Explore Caves discover resources like Knotroot, Copper, or Brightcore in order to craft stronger Tools and Weapons!
”
— In-Game Description

Caves are generated structures in LEGO Fortnite Odyssey. Cave entrances can be found in various places of the world.

The interior of caves is much different from what you see outside. Instead of trees and grass, you'll find stone and ores. Caves are dangerous, much more dangerous than being outside. They are really dark, so without a torch, it's hard to see mobs and ores. They have mobs harder than those in the biome that cave lies in. Although they are risky and have extreme temperature issues, the rewards (like ores and stones) are crucial to beating LEGO Fortnite Odyssey. Inside caves, you can also find characters who have taken shelter inside. You can invite them to venture with you elsewhere, or bring them to your village for them to stay.
