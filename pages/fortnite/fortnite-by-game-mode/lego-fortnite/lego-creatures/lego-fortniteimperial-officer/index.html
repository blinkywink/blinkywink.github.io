Imperial Officer is an Epic Hostile Creature in LEGO Fortnite, added in Update v29.40. Imperial Officer takes the appearance of an Imperial Crew Member.

Spawning

Spawning[]

Imperial Officers can naturally spawn with other Stormtroopers and Imperial Officers in any biome in either a Random Encounter or Empire Bunker.

Attacking

Attacking[]

Once an Imperial Officer spots either a Player, Villager, Skeleton, Scoundrel, or Docile Creature, the Imperial Officer will begin to chase and attack the entity from a range with an  DL-44 or will punch the entity if they get too close, until the Imperial Officer is destroyed. Each attack from an Imperial Officer deals 0.1 damage.

Drops

Drops[]

On death[]

Imperial Officer drops a DL-44 and has a chance to drop Durasteel or Durasteel Scrap.
