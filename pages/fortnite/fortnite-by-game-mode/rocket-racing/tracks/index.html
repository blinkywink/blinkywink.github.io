Overview

Tracks are the main maps players race on in Rocket Racing. They were introduced in v28.01. Tracks come in three difficulties, Novice, Advanced, and Expert.

A Track can be themed after anything and take place anywhere, but most take place in Convergence Canyon. Some Tracks are remixed versions of easier Tracks, these remixed tracks add more obstacles to increase difficulty. Most remixed Tracks add "2" to the end of the orignal Track's name, but that isn't always the case (E.G. K2 Raceway Duex).
