Overview

The Metallica Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite, available from June 13th 2024. Metallica Quests were released in Chapter 5: Season 3 and rewarded players with Season XP and themed cosmetics.

The Poster

The Poster[]



Reveal the Metallica image to unlock it! Complete Metallica Quests to fill in the band name letters, and use Encore Sessions to collect Metallica Notes. Jam at Metallica Statues to start an Encore Session. While the Encore Session is active: Damage opponents or search containers for a chance to make Metallica Notes appear!

Metallica Quests
Complete Metallica Quests in Battle Royale, Fortnite Festival, and Rocket Racing to earn cosmetic rewards.

Battle Royale

- Collect Metallica Music Notes to earn the Metallica Rises Loading Screen.
- Complete 20 Metallica Quests to earn the Parade Riffs Emote.

Fortnite Festival
- Complete 7 Metallica Quests to earn James's ESP Snakebyte Guitar.
- Complete 14 Metallica Quests to earn Robert's Warwick M72 Bass.

Rocket Racing
- Complete 5 Metallica Quests to earn the Chasing Light Wheels.
- Complete 10 Metallica Quests to earn the Backfire Vehicle.

June 13th 2024
Metallica Quests
Crank up the volume and complete 5 Metallica Quests to earn the Chasing Light Wheels. Complete 10 Metallica Quests to earn the Backfire Vehicle and complete more Quests to unlock additional rewards including Metallica themed Backfire decals! 		June 13th 2024
