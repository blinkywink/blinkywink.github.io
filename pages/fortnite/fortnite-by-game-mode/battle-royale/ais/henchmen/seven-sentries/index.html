Guards[]

Seven Sentries were neutral Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced during Chapter 3: Season 1.

Description

Description[]

They could have been found in The Seven controlled POIs and would only shoot at the player if they attack them or The Foundation.

When their health was drained, they could have been knocked. While knocked, they could have been revived by other Seven Sentries or The Foundation.

Seven Sentries can be either male or female. This determines their appearance and voicelines.

Spawn Locations

Spawn Locations[]

  • Chapter 3: Season 3: Sanctuary, Rave Cave, Synapse Station, The Daily Bugle, Condo Canyon, Coney Crossroads, Rocky Reels, and Tilted Towers
  • Chapter 3: Season 2: Help The Seven Reclaim, The Daily Bugle, Condo Canyon, Coney Crossroads, Rocky Reels, and Tilted Towers
  • Chapter 3: Season 1: Sanctuary, Seven Outposts

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Seven Sentries are added.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: Removed Seven Sentries for later use.
  • Update v20.10: Readded Seven Sentries for the Help The Seven Reclaim Battles.
  • Hotfix v20.10: After the battle, Seven Sentries replaced the IO Guards at The Daily Bugle.
  • Update v20.20: After the battle, Seven Sentries replaced the IO Guards at Condo Canyon.
  • Content Update v20.20: After the battle, Seven Sentries replaced the IO Guards at Coney Crossroads.
  • Update v20.30: After the battle, Seven Sentries replaced the IO Guards at Rocky Reels.
  • Content Update v20.40: After the battle, Seven Sentries replaced the IO Guards at Tilted Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: Seven Sentries now appear in quantities of 2, given new lines when approached, and became guards at Sanctuary, Synapse Station and Rave Cave. Oddly enough now bearing the combat music of the IO Guards.
  • Update v21.30: Removed their Idle voicelines.
  • Update v21.40: Added Seven Sentry Sven and Seven Sentry Svenja to Seven Outpost V and Seven Outpost VII. Currently the only two Seven Sentries who can be talked to.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00: All other Seven Sentries on the map have vanished. Except for a Female sentry titled "Seven Guard" who can be seen watching the Henchbros from a distance. Seemingly wishing to join their squad.
  • Update V22.10: The Seven Sentry joined the Henchbros. No longer titled "Seven Guard".
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Their music is a very intense version of The End music with leitmotifs of The End (Chapter 2) music when The Scientist guides The Loopers to The Bridge.
  • Seven Sentries use the same model as Chapter 3 Recruits, though with Seven specialized helmets. The helmet model itself originates from the Vanishing Point Set, and its exterior visor display matches that on The Foundation's Mantle.
    • There are 7 variants of Seven Sentry for skin tones.
    • The helmets themselves are likely made and serviced to prevent the guards from being looped.
    • The Seven Sentries' Outfit IDs are CID_NPC_Athena_Commando_M_Prime and CID_NPC_Athena_Commando_F_Prime.
    • An upgraded version of the Seven Sentry design was found in the files during Chapter 3: Season 2. Where they have one set of arm protection, upgraded helmets with a unique interface and four different vest color styles for both genders. This design can be seen in the Turning Point Loading Screen.
      • Their Outfit IDs are CID_NPC_athena_Commando_M_PrimeOrder and CID_NPC_athena_Commando_F_PrimeOrder.
      • Currently, only Svenja is released with this upgraded design. The male design still unused.
  • The Male Seven Sentries' voicelines utilize the same samples used by the Heavy Marauders during Chapter 2: Season 3.
    • These samples were also used by the IO Guards during Chapter 2: Season 5.
  • They are hostile towards IO Guards and IO Patrols.
  • Seven Sentries are invulnerable to player damage in the Help The Seven Reclaim Battles. But can still be eliminated by any fire source and fall damage.
  • Despite their appearance and affiliation, Seven Sentries will fight Seven Sentry Sven and Seven Sentry Svenja if they accidentally shoot each other.
