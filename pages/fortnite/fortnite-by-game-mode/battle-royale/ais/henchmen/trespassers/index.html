Henchmen[]

Trespassers were hostile Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced during Chapter 2: Season 7.

Description

Description[]

Trespassers used to pilot Saucers and hover around Invaded Locations, picking up random objects and sometimes attempting to abduct players. When piloting, Trespasser Pilots would not attack players unless they dealt damage to their saucers. When their Saucer gets disabled the Trespasser will exit the Vehicle and shoot at the player.

Trespassers could have also been found in Holly Hatchery and will shoot at the player if they notice them without an Alien Parasite. They could have been knocked down and Shaken Down to reveal and mark nearby chests, Trespassers, Alien Parasites, and players in the area.

Impostor Characters

Impostor Characters[]

Starting from Update v17.10, Trespassers would randomly replace some Characters each start of the game by taking on their appearance. But when their covers are blown either by interaction or attacking them, they become hostile. Disguised Trespassers can revive knocked Trespassers without blowing their cover.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00: Trespassers are added.
  • Update v17.10: Trespassers can now randomly replace various Characters on the Island.
  • Update v17.10 Hotfix: Trespassers received Guard music; they now patrol Holly Hatchery.
  • Update v17.20: Increased disguise probability from 0.05 to 0.3.
  • Operation: Sky Fire: Trespassers can be seen constantly as the Loopers infiltrate The Mothership. Patrolling halls, running in groups, and firing at the Loopers with Alien Knockgun Launchers.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00: Trespassers no Longer Spawn at Holly Hatchery or replace Characters. After Sky Fire one Trespasser can be found at one of the abandoned houses at Pristine Point. For more details, please see the Henchbros and Trespasser Defector pages.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Trespassers' Epic Outfit ID is CID_NPC_Athena_Commando_M_AlienRobot.
    • The Trespassers (Henchbro included) had a unique footstep sound that is absent from the Trespasser Defector Outfit. This sound was removed in Chapter 4: Season 1.
  • The Trespassers are the first Guards capable of piloting Vehicles.
  • The Trespassers' Guard layer music is very slightly off sync, causing it to not line up properly with the Base layer.
  • The Disguised Trespassers can also replace the Passive Healer Characters found around Apollo. This way, their disguise can only be broken by attacking them.
  • If a Trespasser and an IO Guard meet, they will attack each other.
  • Although they belong to the same faction, the Trespassers can’t revive or be revived by Zyg and Choppy.
  • The Trespassers that spawn piloting Saucers will usually be eliminated instead of knocked, though on the player's screen in will say that they knocked them down.
  • Talking to a Trespasser disguised as a Character while doing the Week 8 "Converse with characters to identify an Infiltrator" Legendary Quest will fully complete it.

The Trespassers are low ranking soldiers for The Last Reality. They pilot the Saucers and patrol The Mothership, also the locations they took control of.

There are stronger versions of them called "Trespasser Elite" powered by The Cubes. They can shapeshift into the Characters on the Island. One of them was sent to infiltrate Imagined Order but he was unmasked by The Looper under the command of Doctor Slone to find the infiltrator in Week 8.
