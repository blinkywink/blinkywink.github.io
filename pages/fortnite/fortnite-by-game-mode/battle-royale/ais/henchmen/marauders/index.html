Marauders[]

Marauders were hostile AIs that appeared in Chapter 2: Season 3. They spawned randomly from Marauder Capsules that came out of Rifts. They are similar to Henchmen, however they spawn in much lower concentrations and aren't tasked with guarding one specific location.

Skills and Behavior

Skills and Behavior[]

Marauders spawn from Marauder Capsules coming out of Rifts during the match in groups of five. After landing, Marauders will start moving around the Island instantly, looking for players to fight.

Marauders, just like players (only when playing with squadmates), Henchmen, and other AIs can be shaken down to reveal the positions of all other Marauders in their group. They are also capable of reviving knocked down Marauders.

When left undisturbed, Marauders might build simple structures, cover, and Emote to gain Health, or even revive fellow Marauders who have been knocked down.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Marauders are the only AIs who can build.
    • Their builds are made out of a distinct white-tinted Wood.
  • The unreleased Epic Outfit names for the Marauders are called Marauder, Marauder Elite, and Marauder Heavy on developer accounts.
  • Marauders and Henchmen will attack each other if they see each other.
  • Eliminating a total of 100 Marauders grants the Legacy Achievement Marauder Slaughter.
  • Getting eliminated by a Marauder grants the Legacy Achievement Marauder Fodder.
  • As of now, it is still unknown to which faction they abide to.
