Henchmen[]

Stark Robots were special Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced during Chapter 2: Season 4.

Description

Description[]

Stark Robots were initially hostile and will shoot at the player if they spot them. When a Stark Robot is knocked down it can be Hacked instead of shaken down. The hacking process takes 3.5 seconds to complete.

Once hacked, they will join your team and follow you until they get eliminated, their hacker gets eliminated, or they get the Victory Royale. You can have no more than 3 Stark Robots hacked at a moment.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: Stark Robots were added, spawning at Quinjet Patrol Sites.
  • Update v14.10: Stark Robots can now spawn at Stark Industries.
  • Update v14.40: Nerfed the quantity of Stark Robots the player can hack from 3 to 1.
  • Update v14.50: Reverted the previous quantity nerf now that Fortnitemares has ended.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: Stark Robots were removed.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • If a player hacks a Stark Robot and started emoting, all hacked Stark Robots will do The Robot with them. This trait is later adapted onto Hired Characters.
  • On August 27, 2020, a glitch was discovered where if the player dies before their hacked Stark Robot does, and keeps spectating until the end of the game, they will share the Victory Royale with the winning player.
    • As such, Epic Games removed the Stark Robots from competitive modes until this is patched.
  • The unreleased Epic Outfit is known as Stark Auto-Defense Unit on developer accounts.
  • When breaking obstacles, the Stark Robots wield the Mark 85 Energy Blade as their Harvesting Tool.
