Henchmen[]

Ghosty GHOST Henchmen were hostile Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale exclusive to Fortnitemares 2020. They were introduced during Chapter 2: Season 4.

Description

Description[]

They could have been found in The Ruins and will shoot at the player if they spot them. They could have been knocked down and Shaken Down to reveal and mark nearby chests, henchmen, players, and Shadow Midas if he was present.

Ghosty GHOST Henchmen are passive to players who are Shadows. However, if the player attacks them or Shadow Midas, they will become hostile against them.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Ghosty GHOST Henchmen are the first kind of Henchmen to be featured in Fortnitemares.
  • They are referred to as Reawakened Henchmen from Epic Games' YouTube channel, Ghost Henchmen in-game and Ghosty GHOST Henchmen in the game files.
  • The Outfit used for the Ghosty Ghost Henchmen is the same one as the GHOST Henchmen from Chapter 2 Season 2 complete with the earpiece. They have not changed physically at all unlike the GHOST Henchmen from Chapter 2: Season 3.
