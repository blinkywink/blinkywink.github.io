Henchmen[]

Kit's Henchmen were hostile Guards in Fortnite. They were introduced during Chapter 2: Season 3.

Description

Description[]

They could be found in Catty Corner before Update v14.00 and will shoot at the player if they notice them undisguised. Doing small visual reactions for getting suspicious and spotting a target.

When knocked down, they can be shaken down to reveal nearby chests, henchmen, cameras, and Kit if he was present.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kit's Henchman's Epic Outfit ID is CID_NPC_Athena_Commando_M_Scrapyard.
  • In-game Kit's Henchmen were simply referred to as Henchmen when knocked.
  • Kit's Henchmen had one of their Full Alert voicelines removed in Update v13.20. Due to it being commonly interpreted as the Henchmen swearing.
  • Phone Booths originally made for Kit's Henchmen were added back in Update v32.00 at the Box Factory. However likely due to an error, the Phone Booths would turn the player into Kitbash instead of the original Guard Outfit.
    • Its possible the Outfits were mixed up during development. As Kitbash and Kit's Henchman both have Scrapyard as their CID Codename, and no additional word was added to set them apart unlike the Grotto Henchmen, Rig Henchmen, Shark Henchmen, and IO Guards.
