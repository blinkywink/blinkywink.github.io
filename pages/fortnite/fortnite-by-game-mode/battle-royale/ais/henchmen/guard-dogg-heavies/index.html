Henchmen[]

Guard Dogg Heavies were hostile Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced during Chapter 2 Remix.

Description

Description[]

Two Guard Dogg Heavies could have been found in The Doggpound working with the GHOST Henchmen and will shoot at the player if they notice them undisguised.

They can be knocked down and Shaken Down to reveal and mark nearby chests, henchmen, cameras, players, and Snoop Dogg if he were still neutral.

Though they can't be disguised into, they can be used on GHOST ID Scanners if carried to one.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Following the Guards present in Chapter 5, Guard Dogg Heavies reuse the base model of the Legion Heavy Guards. With a grey hound mask, and a chain that resembles the Dogg Tag Emoticon.
    • Guard Dogg Heavy's Outfit ID is Character_FirthNyx_NPC.
  • Guard Dogg Heavies reuse the voice of Brutus with an added Full Alert Idle voiceline set and no Interrogation set.
  • Guard Dogg Heavies, along with the GHOST Henchmen and SHADOW Henchmen are the first Guards since the IO Guards in Chapter 2: Season 8 that can be shaken down.
  • The Guard Dogg Heavies, along with the Latverian Guards, are the only Legion Guard derivatives to not have been made into proper Outfits in Update v34.10, likely due to their ties to Snoop Dogg's brand.
