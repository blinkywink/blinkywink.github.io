Overview

This article contains unreleased content that may be available in the future.
The content on this page is currently present in the game's files but is not accessible by normal means at this time.

Unreleased AIs are AIs in the game files that have been surfaced and obtained by data-miners, from the in-game files of Fortnite: Battle Royale. They are not yet available in-game so these act as spoilers as to what gameplay is to come.
