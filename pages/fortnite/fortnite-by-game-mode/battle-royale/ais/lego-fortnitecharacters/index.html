Overview

Characters (also known as Friendly NPCs) are a type of AI and Gameplay Mechanic in Odyssey, Brick Life, and Expeditions. They were introduced in Update v28.00.

Description

Description[]

In Odyssey, characters are a unique type of AI which will spawn in every worlds, at various locations. Unlike other Creatures, they can be talked to by the player, offering dialogue and various functions for the player to interact with. There are three types of character in Odyssey, each of them with a unique purpose and features:

  • Travelers: Found in various locations.
  • Visitors: Will come to visit your villages and stay for a night.
  • Villagers: Can help with tasks in your villages.

In Brick Life, the characters are citizens of Brick Bay that can be interacted with. Some characters are job recruiters that let the player work for them.
