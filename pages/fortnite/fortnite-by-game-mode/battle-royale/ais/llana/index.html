Overview

“
It's me, Llana! Your personal Island assistant! How can I help you today?
”
— In-Game Description

Llana is an AI Assistant built by Lana Llane that operates the Vending Machines around the Battle Royale Island. Llana was removed with the release of Chapter 4: Season 4, but was added back in Update v26.30. She was once again removed in Chapter 6: Season 2, but was added back in Chapter 7: Season 1.
