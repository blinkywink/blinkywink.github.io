Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In most matches during Chapter 5: Season 1 Vengeance Jones is armed with only a Pickaxe and would drop nothing. However on rare occasions he will drop a Rare Modular Frenzy Auto Shotgun and Modular Enforcer AR.
    • Eliminating Vengeance Jones was initially the only way to obtain the Modular Enforcer AR in-game before it was enabled.
  • While the Character Collection Book preview during Chapter 5: Season 1 displays one of Vengeance Jones' interactions as selling the Modular Enforcer AR, the option never appears in-game.
  • Vengeance Jones has the most re-occuring Character appearances out of any Character in Battle Royale. Appearing in 9 seasons since Chapter 5: Season 1.
