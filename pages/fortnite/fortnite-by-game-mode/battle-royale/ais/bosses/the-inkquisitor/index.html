Outfit[]

“

The Inkquisitor is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during November 2022, as part of the November 2022 Fortnite Crew Pack. The Inkquisitor was released in Chapter 3: Season 4 and is part of the The Inkquisition Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, The Inkquisitor's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Signature
Base

The Inkquisitor received a higher-detailed style in Update v30.20, which replaced the original one.
Players have the option to choose between both.

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, The Inkquisitor's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Inkquisitor was a hostile Boss exclusive to Fortnitemares 2022 introduced during Chapter 3: Season 4. He could have been summoned in Grim Gables.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Inkquisitor dropped the following items when eliminated. He also dropped two Healing Items and Bars.

Inkquisitor's Suppressed SMG Pumpkin Launcher Firefly Jar

Inkquisitor uses an Uncommon Pumpkin Launcher to attack players. He will not drop the Explosive Weapon when eliminated.

Pumpkin Launcher
Behavior

Behavior[]

Inkquisitor is not initially patrolling Grim Gables. Instead, he will only appear a moment after a player touches the octopus mural located under the manor. Causing the six torches circling it and the mural itself to light up, calling upon him to appear.

Inkquisitor alternated between his weapons and throwables in combat. He could only be summoned once per game.

Boss Trivia

Boss Trivia[]

  • Inkquisitor's Boss Outfit ID is Character_Imitator_NPC and his codename is Freaky.
    • The Boss Outfit itself differs quite a bit from his release design. Lacking the smiley face and digital texturing on his visor, his back tentacles are tied in a sort of ponytail while the two front hanging ones are curled, and his back straps are completely absent.
  • According to the Fortnitemares 2022 Challenges, Grim Gables should have become a chromed location when Inkquisitor was added.
