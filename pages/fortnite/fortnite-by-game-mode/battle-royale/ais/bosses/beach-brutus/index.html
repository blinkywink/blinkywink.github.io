Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Beach Brutus's appearances?
Beach Brutus has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Beach Brutus is a neutral Boss introduced during Chapter 7: Season 1. He can be found at various locations around the Golden Coast.

Beach Brutus drops the following items when eliminated. He also drops a Healing Item and Bars.

Random Weapon Dynamite
Behavior

Behavior[]

Beach Brutus has a chance to spawn all around the Golden Coast at one of his locations, surrounded by two Henchfans praising him. He will not attack the player on sight unless he or his Guards are attacked, they aim their weapon at him for too long, or they get too close to him to slowly build up suspicion.

While in combat, he will occasionally throw Dynamite at his target.

In matches where players use the Battle Bus, Beach Brutus can be seen jumping out to where he will spawn.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Beach Brutus' codename is PAVBoss_Alpha.
  • Beach Brutus reuses his original form's voice with many new voicelines added with or replacing old ones, a new Full Alert Idle set, an Idle set that lacks the Walkie Talkie filter, and shortened Defeated set.
    • Some of these new voiceline takes were first heard from the GHOST Henchmen and Guard Dogg Heavies from Chapter 2 Remix.
    • The new voicelines also feature higher quality audio compared to his original voicelines.
  • Beach Brutus' line "Don't make me tell ya twice." is a reference to his original Outfit's description.
  • Beach Brutus, along with Human Bill and Hush, are the first Bosses to both drop out of the Battle Bus and thank the Bus Driver. As their names can be seen before jumping.
