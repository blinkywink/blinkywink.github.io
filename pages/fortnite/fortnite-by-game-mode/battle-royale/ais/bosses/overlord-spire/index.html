Boss[]

Overlord Spires are hostile Spire Bosses introduced during Chapter 6: Season 3. They can be found in Named Locations.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

When eliminated, the Overlord Spires drop a random Exotic Weapon (listed below)

  • Unstable Frostfire Shotgun
  • Unstable Yoink Shotgun
  • Unstable Voltage Burst Pistol
  • Unstable Thunderclap DMR
  • Unstable Bounce Grenade

and 2-3 Legendary or Epic Weapons + 1 or 2 Chug Jugs.

Behavior

Behavior[]

To eliminate the Overlord Spires, you need to destroy 2 weak points before damaging the Spire itself.

Special Abilities

Special Abilities[]

The Overlord Spires can attack the player in 3 different ways:

  • Scorch Beam
  • Explosive Screaming Masks
  • Energy Drain Wave
