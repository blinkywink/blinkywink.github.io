Boss[]

The Puppeteer was a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 6: Season 1. It can be found puppeteering The Night Rose in Demon's Dojo before The Confrontation Event.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Phase 1: BATTLE THE NIGHT ROSE![]

During this phase, The Puppeteer is invulnerable as it watches The Night Rose battle the current target.

The Night Rose strung up by The Puppeteer
The Night Rose waking up as a player gets near

Phase 2: DEFEAT THE NIGHT ROSE![]

Once The Night Rose's health is depleted, she will be knocked down, but then pulled back in by The Puppeteer shortly after. While strung up by him, The Night Rose will have 100% damage resistance until she is back on her feet.

Weakspots: DESTROY THE EYES![]

  • The Puppeteer's eyes will glow brightly while holding up The Night Rose, with 100/150/200/250 Health each. Attacking them enough times will cause them to stop glowing, and destroying both will stun The Puppeteer, thus forcing him to drop The Night Rose. This enables her to attack the player and become vulnerable again. She will be quickly picked up again if she is brought to 50% health or 15 seconds has passed after she is released.
The Night Rose getting knocked then pulled into the air
The Night Rose being dropped after The Puppeteer is stunned
Note: The Night Rose and The Puppeteer constantly teleport around unintentionally in the Replay Client. Causing syncing issues

During the time when The Puppeteer is active, he will use the following abilities:

  • Demonic Spirit Warp
    • Vanishes and re-appears on another corner of the arena.
  • Explosive Screaming Masks
    • Fires a slow 3-round burst of Fire Oni Masks that fly towards the target, dealing 20 damage in a small AoE.

The Puppeteer is a type of gigantic semi-sentient Mask of Power created by Demon Warriors. Puppeteer masks are used by powerful demons such as Shogun X to bind and control their puppet remotely.

It is a major antagonist in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the secondary antagonist of Chapter 6: Season 1.

Appearance

Appearance[]

The Puppeteer is a giant floating Oni mask with two burning void magic eyes, two floating hands and dark smoke emitted from its back. On both of its hands are magical strings tied to its puppet.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

Spirit Realm
Body manipulation
The Puppeteer can manipulate The Night Rose like a puppet using its strings and a curse. The Puppeteer is also is capable to heal the Night Rose should she be injured. However, this ability does not properly work if the Puppeteer is injured as well.
Fire Manipulation Magic
The Puppeteer can launch fire oni masks.
Void Manipulation Magic
The Puppeteer can open Void Tears to warp around.
Nigh-Invulnerability
The Puppeteer is invulnerable everywhere except for its eyes, and the Night Rose can only be harmed when the Puppeteer's eyes are already damaged.
