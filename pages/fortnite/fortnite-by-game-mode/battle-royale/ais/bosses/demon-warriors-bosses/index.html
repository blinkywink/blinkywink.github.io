Void Demon Warriors[]

Void Demon Warriors were neutral Bosses introduced during Chapter 6: Season 1. They could have been found wandering around Oninoshima's Spirit Realm Portals.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Void Demon Warriors dropped the following items when eliminated. They also dropped a Shield Potion.

Void Boon Surgefire SMG Void Oni Mask
Behavior

Behavior[]

Three random Demon Warrior types will always appear on Oninoshima, typically near a Spirit Realm Portal. While active they are marked on the map by a Kabuto icon.

Void Demon Warriors will not attack the player on sight unless they or a Demon Grunt are attacked, they block their path for too long, or they aim their weapon at them for too long.

In combat, they will alternate between using their Surgefire SMG and brandishing their Void Oni Mask to conjure a portal to Void Warp towards whenever the player is looking right at them.

History

History[]

  • C6S1
  • C6S2

Update v33.00[]

December 1st 2024

  • Added the Demon Warriors as Bosses. Two can be found near Oninoshima's Spirit Realm Portals per match.
    • The Fire Demon Warrior was in the files, but weren't enabled in-game at the time.
    • Though he won't appear on the map unless nearby, an additional Wind Demon Warrior will always appear alone while battling Kendo and Jade in Sakura Plunge. But cannot deal damage to each other.

Update v33.10[]

December 10th 2024

  • Added the Fire Demon Warrior variant.
  • Increased the amount of Demon Warriors on the map from 2 to 3 (3 to 4 if counting the Wind Demon Warrior battling Kendo).
  • Switched the Wind Demon Warriors' drop loot chance from the Epic Sentinel Pump Shotgun having the higher chance of dropping to the Typhoon Blade.

Update v33.20[]

January 14th 2025

  • Replaced the Wind Demon Warrior battling Kendo with 2 Demon Grunts.

Update v33.30[]

January 31st 2025

  • A Wind Demon Warrior now appears on the third wave when helping Doughberman battle Demon Grunts in Masked Meadows.

Update v34.00[]

February 21st 2025

  • Removed Demon Warriors.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Demon Warriors' Boss Outfit ID is Character_UnluckySinge_Staff_NPC and their codename is LesserDemons.
    • The Outfit's appearance is very similar to Shogun X. Though with a different helmet and a few details either simplified or stripped down.
      • The TBD Outfit itself has three alternate Styles. The Default style being used by the Fire Demon Warriors, Mage being used by the Void Demon Warriors, and Angst being used by the Wind Demon Warrior.
      • As seen in the Me and Bro Promotional Trailer, the design of the Demon Warrior that fought Jade was slightly different. Having horns on his kabuto and visible glowing eyes, which do not appear in the in-game model.
      • In Update v34.00, the TBD Outfit was given an official Icon and style render. Likely indicating their release soon.
    • In blogposts, the Demon warriors are also referred as Lesser Demons.
  • The Demon Warriors' size is much larger than other Bosses. Being as large as Cube Assassin and Hades.
  • All types of Demon Warriors are simply referred as Demon Warrior in-game.
  • Like Shogun X and The Night Rose, the Demon Warriors have a unique red crumbling effect when defeated.
