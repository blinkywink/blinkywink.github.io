Dark Voyager Drones are hostile AIs in Fortnite: Battle Royale and Fortnite: Save The World. They were introduced during Chapter 7: Season 2.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Battle Royale[]

Dark Voyager Drones only appear in waves of three when battling a Dark Voyager Echo or while the Stabilize the Anomaly Rift Anomaly is active, where they will be observing a golden rift and are required to be destroyed in order to claim the rewards. Summoned Drones will instantly despawn when their host Dark Voyager Echo is defeated or when an anomaly is stabilized.

Dark Voyager Drones hover around an area and slowly chase any target they find, locking onto them and firing a beam that deals 15 damage to the target. When a drone is destroyed, they will drop Ammunition.

Save the World[]

Dark Voyager Drones only appear in a mission when a player is currently doing the Drone-Be-Gone Quest of A Voyager's Arrival Questline, attacking players on sight.

Their laser attacks will instantly destroy any objects or structures struck by it, along with dealing ??? damage.
