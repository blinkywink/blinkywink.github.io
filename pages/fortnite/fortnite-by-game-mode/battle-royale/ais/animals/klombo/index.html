Klombos are a type of Wildlife in Fortnite: Battle Royale and a special type of Creature in LEGO Fortnite that were initially introduced in Chapter 3: Season 1.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Klombos would slowly move across the map. Players could stand on top of one and it moves along with it. Klombos could have been fed with Klomberries, which will sometimes make it shoot an item out of its blowhole on the back of its head. This item will bounce a few times before stopping. Standing on the blowhole would launch the player very high into the air, and initiate Glider Redeploy. Klombos would eat up to two Klomberries at a time. Klombos would consume all loot they stumble upon and destroy all structures they go through. When a player is near a calm Klombo, soothing music plays.

Klombos could not be eliminated, but Klombos can take damage and all damage sources only deal 1 damage per attack. After taking 10 damage, it will become hostile for 30 seconds.

Attacks

Attacks[]

Attacking a Klombo would cause it to become aggressive and the music becomes more similar to a boss battle (like Gunnar or The Foundation). Klombos will rear onto their hind legs, before shooting a ball of damaging acid at the player, or charging and stomping on them if they are close enough.

Klombos deal 15 damage on an Acid Ball attack, and 20 on a Stomp Attack.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • When feeding a Klombo an Exotic Item, the Klombo will give a Legendary Item in return.
  • Don't drop Mythics next to it as it can eat them.
  • Klombos only take 1 damage from any damage source, so it is not advised to ever attack them.
  • When feeding a Klombo a Victory Crown, the Klombo will not give anything (including the Victory Crown.) back.

Klombo is a species of a herbivore dinosaur-like creature traveling The Omniverse. They are native to Lost Isles' reality and used to inhabit Reality Zero at one point before the war between IO and the Seven broke out. After a long period of time, during an unspecified event, parts of Lost Isles arrived in Reality: UN-85 and Klombos now live there.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Klombos can be found on Artemis island. They were hibernating in the snow during the winters. They woke up and started to roam around the island. They will consume anything that Loopers bought for them.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

Klombos have disappeared.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

A Klombo skull can be found near Behemoth Bridge. Haven mentioned that they all have rifted away somewhere.

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

The Oracle mentioned Klombo in one of her weird analogy.

LEGO Fortnite[]

Haven arrived in Reality: UN-85 in preparation for the big Klombo migration.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Its codename is "Butter Cake".
  • Its name originates from r/FortniteBR where the user u/Graphenegem dubbed the name on December 6, 2021.
    • Original Comment.
  • It is the second Wildlife with boss music, the first being Loot Shark.
  • Klombos will change color when fed. They will also become dark red and glowing when aggressive.
  • It is impossible to eliminate a Klombo in normal games. This is due to Klombo being able to regenerate 1000 health per second while only taking 1 damage per shot.
    • A Klombo was eliminated by a modded Save the World shotgun by a user in the community that shoots grenades which deals about 9,000 damage. When it "died" it vanished due to it having no death animation in the files.
  • It is the second wildlife that can eat/absorb items other than meat, fish, and vegetables, the first being the Loot Shark.
  • The promo image for Klombos seems to be referencing the film Kong: Skull Island (2017).
  • There are 5 different color variants of Klombos.
