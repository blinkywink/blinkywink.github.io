Overview

This article is about the Wildlife in Save the World. For the Battle Royale version, see Wildlife (Battle Royale).

Wildlife are NPCs in Fortnite: Save the World that were first introduced in Update v19.40.

Since Update v22.30, Wolves and Boars can be ridden, making them function as Vehicles.

Since Update v26.20, Crows are no longer the only Wildlife that can carry loot. In addition to the loot Crows; loot Chickens, Frogs, Boars, Wolves, and Raptors can also appear.

Functionality

Functionality[]

General[]

Wildlife can be found roaming across a zone during a misison. They can potentially attack or flee from the player when they get close. Hostile Wildlife can be tamed and be used to kill Monsters, helping the player and their team. Players can only have one tamed Wild Animal at a time. Taming a new one will eliminate the previous one. Wildlife can not be elemental.

Wildlife appears in every mission except Storm Shield defenses, Dungeons, Frostnite, Survive the Horde, and when fighting the Storm King.

Taming[]

Select Wildlife can be tamed using Meat, or by jumping on top of them, which will tame them and cause them to become friendly.

Riding[]

Players can ride certain Wildlife, which enables them to control the animal as if they were a vehicle. Riding an Animal allows the player to move in any direction, jump and sprint. Animals have a stamina bar which stops them moving if depleted. This bar must be refilled with Meat. Riding on an animal allows the player to use Weapons and Consumables, but doesn't allow the use of Gadgets or Abilities, and building whilst riding.

Elimination[]

Unlike untamed Wildlife, when a tamed Animal's health bar reaches zero, it will be knocked down instead of instantly being eliminated. Players can revive them. If a Player gets knocked down and chooses to Respawn instead of using a life to revive themself, their tamed Animal will despawn from the zone.

Animal Dens[]

Multiple Animal Dens can be found in a mission. Going near one and interacting with the associated objects will cause an Animal to come out and be instantly tamed:

  • Wolf Dens have dry skulls.
  • Boar Dens have red mushrooms.
  • Raptor Dens have eggs.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Monsters will engage on any Wildlife they see.
    • In Eliminate and Collect and Destroy the Encampments missions, this can be a hindrance, as monsters may chase Wildlife and stay away from players while the timer runs out.
    • In siege and defence based missions, Wildlife can be a benefit as they may serve as a distraction.
  • Taming Wildlife does not take up a Defender slot, so it is possible to have 3 Defenders and 1 tamed Animal to assist in a mission.
  • Tamed Wildlife will periodically teleport near the player if stuck or too far away.
  • Do not leave a fortification unattended and without traps for too long because wildlife can break builds.
  • Knocked Wildlife never disappears, unless the taming player is eliminated, or another animal is tamed.

Update v19.40[]

  • Added Wildlife
    • Added 6 Different Types: Wolves, Chickens, Crows, Raptors, Frogs and Boars.

Content Update v19.40[]

  • Chickens will now attack players.

Update v21.30[]

  • Chickens' damage now scales with zone power, instead of a fixed damage of 1.

Update v22.30[]

  • Added Zombie Chickens.
  • Wolves and Boars can be ridden.
  • Tamed wildlife will be knocked down instead of fully eliminated when health reaches 0.

Update v25.30[]

  • Added the Primal Weapons Set.
  • Primal Weapons have a core perk which causes even aggressive Wildlife to not attack the player when holding the weapon, unless provoked.

Update v26.20[]

  • Chickens, Frogs, Boars, Wolves, and Raptors can also be found glowing now, and will drop loot when eliminated.
  • Dens and Nests will now appear around the edges of the map where you can get tamed wolves, boars, and raptors without danger!

Update v27.00[]

  • Lowered the spawn rate of Zombie Chickens significantly in Regular Missions, affecting quest-completion for Eye of the Brainstorm.
  • Zombie Chickens can still be found in the Hexsylvania Venture Zone. Best found in Survive the Night.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The floating health bar is the same as that of Wildlife in Battle Royale, rather than the one used for Husk Monsters. As a result, Wildlife do not display a power level. This is due to Wildlife being introduced in Battle Royale first.
  • Wildlife will be affected by the Healing Deathburst Modifier.
  • While riding a tamed Animal, it can be noticed that the font used for the Animal's health bar is Burbank instead of Noto Sans, which appears to be an oversight.
    • The shape and colors of the Animal health bar also resemble those from Battle Royale Chapter 3: Season 2.
    • In contrast, Save the World still uses the shapes and colors from Chapter 2: Season 1 for player health bars.
