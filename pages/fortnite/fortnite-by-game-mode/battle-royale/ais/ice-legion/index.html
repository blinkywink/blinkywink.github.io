Overview

Ice Legion were Winter variants of the husk-like AIs that are Cube Monsters in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were added immediately after the Ice Storm Event as a result of a shard of The Cube being used by The Ice King to unleash the Ice Storm.

They spawned from Ice Shards (similar to Spawn Obelisks) randomly around the map.

The Fiend Hunter Crossbow dealt 4x more damage to Ice Legion monsters.
