Overview

Creatures are AIs in LEGO Fortnite. They were introduced in Update v28.01. Damage taken is calculated based on the number of hearts lost (1 damage = 1 heart, 0.5 damage = 1 half-heart), additionally, the damage taken is received with 0 shield.
