Sprites are a type of Item, Gameplay Mechanic, and AI in Fortnite that were introduced in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Description and Behavior

Description and Behavior[]

Sprites are a type of gameplay mechanic which will roam around certain areas of the map, Some Sprites are AIs and can potentially flee from the player when they get close. Sprites have a wide range of uses, from mobility, to unlocking loot to aid in battle.

Water and Air Sprites[]

Water and Air Sprites will flee from the player if approached or attacked and can be picked up and carried, which enables the player to access shrines. When a carried Sprite is not the currently held item, it will take the place of the player's Back Bling. Depositing a Sprite into a Sprite Shrine will free the Sprite and grant the player Epic tier loot including a Boon corresponding to the element of the Sprite. When picked up, Water, Air, Dash and Superman Sprite Sprites are of Epic rarity. When dropped, Sprites will go back to being Wildlife.

Carried sprites could have been given to Bushranger in Nightshift Forest during Chapter 6: Season 1 to shift the time to day and grant the player a Solunar Key.

Earth Sprites[]

Earth Sprites can be found in various locations, unlike other sprites they will not flee from the player and cannot be picked up or carried, meaning they cannot be used to access Sprite Shrines or given to Bushranger. When a weapon is given to an Earth Sprite, it will eat the weapon, giving back a random Legendary weapon, most commonly a Shotgun. Earth Sprites have an Icon on the map that shows where they are.

Sprites are a species of magical spirits found in various realities. They are minor deities of Oninoshima following the return of Magic on the Island. Before the Shattering War, Sprites were worshiped to the now forgotten Sprite Shrines.

Personality

Personality[]

Sprites are described as innocent, endearing, helpful creatures. Water and Air Sprites can sometimes be fearful of strangers depending on the situation, while Earth Sprites remain much calmer. Moreover, while the first two are unable to speak, the latter are able to do so and are endowed with a great intelligence. Dash and Superman sprites loves to follow and interact with basically everyone that's not demons.

The only known people that can communicate and understand sprites are Bushranger.[3] and Jade.

Sprites are known to be afraid of the Dark Presence and his magic hungry Spires.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Air Sprites: Soft, glowing, cloud-like creatures with a creamy white body. It has a swirl design on its chest denoting its element and expressive, large black eyes with sparkling highlights. Its rounded, marshmallow-like shape gives it a gentle and cute demeanor.

Water Sprites: Bubbly, water-inspired creatures with a translucent, gradient body transitioning from blue to teal. It features tiny bubbles rising from its head. Like the Air Sprite, it has adorable black eyes, a circular mark on its torso denoting its element, and a playful, cheerful appearance.

Earth Sprites: Robust, nature-themed entities resembling a humanoid tree trunk laid and surrounded by mossy rocks. It has glowing pink eyes, moss growing on its shoulders, and leafy branches sprouting from its head.

Dash Sprites: A glowing orange creature with soft edges, wearing a blue superhero mask with bright white eyes. It has small arms, Supernova emblem on its chest, and a blue cape flowing behind. Surrounded by a fiery red aura with sparkles, it radiates intense energy and heroic power.

Superman Sprites: A vibrant blue, chibi-style creature with big sparkling eyes and a small smile. Dressed in a red superman costume with a yellow belt and the iconic S emblem on its chest, it also wears a matching red cape. A swirling purple and pink aura gives it a charming yet powerful presence.

History

History[]

Unknown time ago[]

Prior to the Shattering War, When magic still existed on the island, Sprites were roaming the island, deified, with multiple Sprite Shrines to honor them. However, upon the end of the Shattering War, they disappeared along with magic, which was banished from the island.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

Water, Earth and Air Sprites have re-emerged on the island, following the crash of the Zero Point Shard which reawakened magic on the Island. Jade, one of the Spirit Siblings, discovered that she had a special connection with the Sprites. When she was trapped in the Spirit Realm, Jade would be protected by the Sprites from the Demons.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

Jade started to study her connection to Sprites, revealing she could understand them and what they feel and the same way they understand her and listen to her. She also reveals she feels the heartbeat of the Island itself in her chest.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

Reacting to the changed Island energies following the Reality Wave from Predator Peak Portal and the invasion of the Demon Warriors, some Water and Air Sprites reacted and evolved respectively in Superman Sprites and Dash Sprites. The former obtaining Superman's powers, the latter obtaining the Zero Point's power. Jade sent The Looper investigate on them to see if they were alright.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

The Swarm emerged from the Spirit Realm and attacked the Sprites on the Island, leading to their disappearance.
