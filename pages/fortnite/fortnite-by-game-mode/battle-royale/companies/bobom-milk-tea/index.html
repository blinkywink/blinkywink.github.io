Overview

This article is about the company. For the LEGO Fortnite Brick Life location, see LEGO Fortnite:Bobom Milk Tea.


Bobom Milk Tea, also known simply as Bobom, is a boba café franchise existing within multiple realities in Fortnite. They appear in Battle Royale and LEGO Fortnite Brick Life.

Franchise History

Franchise History[]

Overview[]

Bobom Milk Tea is a boba café originally from Reality: 783, the same as its monster hunting Bobarista: Helsie.[1] The status of the original location(s) on Reality: 783 or elsewhere is unknown. The franchise currently has a newer location in Brick Bay.

Unknown Time Prior to Chapter 4[]

Bobom Milk Tea exists on Reality: 783. At some point before, during, or after the founding of The High Stakes Club and friendship breakup between Joni the Red and Minato Hakaru, Helsie is hired to Bobom. Following the split, Joni makes new best friends in Helsie, and soon after Lucien. She brings them into the club, and the three become chosen family.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

The club, besides Minato, are pulled to Asteria. Helsie’s hunting attire includes Bobom merchandise. She even manages to have a Bobom uniform while away from her reality, suggesting the possibility of Bobom being brought with them or her starting a new location.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

Helsie and her chosen family are seen resting, drinking Bobom brand boba on a building in MEGA City during MEGA City Swing.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

As a part of her concert outfit, Huntress Helsie has a Bobom keychain hanging from her Cerulean Clawpack.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

The full club is reunited in Reality: 783, but no Bobom locations are present on the island as they were prior to Chapter 4.

LEGO Fortnite Brick Life[]

At some point after Chapter 4 and before Chapter 6, the company has established themselves in Brick Bay. Helsie runs the local Bobom Milk Tea and serves as a bobarista, while Joni and Lucien reside within the lower floor. Beneath Bobom, the club has a secret space featuring four chairs at a round table, suggesting Minato’s presence. To unlock the room, Lucien’s plushie “Lil Louie” must be brought to the break room of the café.

Raven holds a vampire experiment at Mourndale Academy. Bobom rewards any surviving students with a Vamp Champ Trophy.
