Ship It! is a shipping company in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

Update v9.00[]

  • Ship It! was added to Neo Tilted.

Update v10.00[]

  • Ship It! was removed due to Neo Tilted's disappearance.

Update v12.00[]

  • Ship It! has a new installation on Apollo, the Box Factory.

Update v13.00[]

  • The Box Factory has been renamed to Catty Corner, now under the control of Kit, and now a Named Location. A gas station and nearby cafe have been added to the location.

The End[]

  • With the flipping of Apollo, Catty Corner and Ship It! are flipped as well.

Update v28.00[]

  • Ship It! has three new installations on Helios, being the Landmark named Ship It! Station, or the two shops in Hazy Hillside and Ritzy Riviera.

Update v29.00[]

  • The shops in Hazy Hillside and Ritzy Riviera are removed due to the POIs’ disappearances.

Update v32.00[]

  • Ship It! Station is removed alongside Helios. The Box Factory has returned along with Apollo.

Update v32.11[]

  • The Box Factory has been upgraded into to Catty Corner once again.

Update v33.00[]

  • Catty Corner has been removed along with Apollo once again.
