Overview

This article is about the Restraurant Chain. For the Save the World Starter Pack, see Cuddle Buns Pack.


Cuddle Buns is a Restraurant Chain in Fortnite: Battle Royale and Fortnite: Ballistic that was introduced in Chapter 6: Season 1.

History

History[]

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

  • Update v33.00: Cuddle Buns was added to Oninoshima: the bakery within Canyon Crossing and the three food trucks.
  • Update v33.10: Cuddle Buns advertisement poster can be found in Skyline 10.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

  • Update v36.00: A new Cuddle Buns food pod was added to Utopia City.
