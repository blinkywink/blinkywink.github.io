Overview


Cap'n Carp is a fishing equipment shop in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Cap'n Carp shop was added to Lazy Lake, Craggy Cliffs, Misty Meadows and Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00: The Cap'n Carp shop was added to The Fortilla and Boat N' Tackle.
  • Update v13.20:
    • July 1st: Boat N' Tackle was positioned a different way to fit in it's spot.
    • July 11th: The Cap'n Carp shop was removed due to Boat N' Tackle's disappearance.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: The Cap'n Carp shop was removed due to The Fortilla's disappearance.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00:
    • The Cap'n Carp shop was removed due to Sweaty Sands's disappearance.
    • The Cap'n Carp shop was added to Believer Beach.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The four Cap'n Carp shopes were turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: The Cap'n Carp shop was added to Camp Cuddle and in the Outdoor Equipment Store in Greasy Grove.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: The Cap'n Carp shop was removed due to Camp Cuddle's disappearance

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

  • On a small island on the north eastern corner of the island Helios, there are many references to Chapter 2, including a Cap'n Carp sign.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

  • An abandoned storefront has been added to the south of Ripped Tides.
