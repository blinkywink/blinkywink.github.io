Overview


Pass 'N' Gas is a gas station chain in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • Four Pass 'N' Gas stations were added to Loot Lake, Pleasant Park, Greasy Grove and Tomato Town.

Season 2[]

  • Update v2.2.0: Two Pass 'N' Gas stations were added to Sports Center and Convenience Store.

Season 3[]

  • Update v3.00: Two Pass 'N' Gas station was added to Lucky Landing and Salty Springs.

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.00: A Pass 'N' Gas station was removed due to Convenience Store's disappearance.
  • Update v5.30:
    • A Pass 'N' Gas station was removed due to Tomato Town's disappearance.
    • A Pass 'N' Gas station was added to Tomato Temple.

Season 7[]

  • Update v7.00: A Pass 'N' Gas station was removed due to Greasy Grove's disappearance.

Season 8[]

  • Update v8.00: A Pass 'N' Gas station was removed due to Tomato Temple's disappearance.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.10: The Pass 'N' Gas station at the Sports Center was destroyed by the Rift Beacon's explosion.
  • Update v10.30:
    • Two Pass 'N' Gas stations were added back to Convenience Store and Greasy Grove.
  • The End: The Pass 'N' Gas stations were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00:
    • Two Pass 'N' Gas stations were added back to Pleasant Park and Salty Springs.
    • A Pass 'N' Gas station was added to Blue Steel Bridge.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.30: The Pass 'N' Gas stations were replaced by GAS stations.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: Part of a Pass 'N' Gas station was added to The Pizza Pit.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00: The part of Pass 'N' Gas station at The Pizza Pit was removed.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The name of the company is a joke based on the term "passing gas" (i.e. farting). This is further supported by the logo on the sign, which depicts a smoke cloud coming out of the bottom of a Gas Can.
  • As time progressed, The company started losing parts of its chain... By Chapter 2 Season 5 There was only one remaining station by Pizza pit.
  • According to the history section, They were replaced by GAS stations. A possible theory is that they were bought out by the company.
  • The signs look similar to the gas cans that are scattered around the map, This could indicate they were actually created by Pass 'N' Gas.
