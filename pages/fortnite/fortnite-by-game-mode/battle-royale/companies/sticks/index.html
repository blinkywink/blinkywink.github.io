Craggy Cliffs Sticks Restaurant

Overview


Sticks is a restaurant company in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It might have been owned by the late Fishstick who died in the Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point.

History

History[]

Season 9[]

  • Update v9.00: Sticks restaurant ads were added to Neo Tilted and Spawn Island.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.00:
    • The ads were removed from the Spawn Island.
    • The ads were removed due to Neo Tilted's disappearance.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Sticks restaurant was added to Craggy Cliffs.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00:
    • The Sticks restaurant in Craggy Cliffs was flooded.
    • The Sticks restaurant was added to The Fortilla.
    • A small Sticks restaurant was added to Pleasant Park.
    • The Sticks restaurant was added to Float Fillet, a replica of the Craggy Cliffs restaurant to sail around the map.
  • Update v13.20:
    • July 11th: The Pleasant Park restaurant has vanished.
    • July 18th: The Float Fillet has vanished.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00:
    • The Sticks restaurant was removed due to The Fortilla being half gone.
    • The water drained from the area and the Sticks restaurant in Craggy Cliffs has turned back to normal.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Sticks restaurant was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00:
    • The Sticks restaurant was added to Sleepy Sound.
    • A small Sticks restaurant was added to Sunburned Shacks.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00:
    • Fishstick is added to the Sleepy Sound restaurant.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.20: The Sticks restaurant was lifted and is on top a sky platform in Shiny Sound.
  • Fracture: The Sticks restaurant was destroyed along with the island.

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

  • Update v40.00: The Sticks restaurant was added to Golden Coast, northwest of Humble Hills and southwest of Squibbly Shores.
  • Update v40.20: A new sign displaying 99 sold meals was added.
  • Update v40.30: The sign now displays 999,999,999 sold meals.
