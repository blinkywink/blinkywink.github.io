McGuffin's

Overview


McGuffin's is a bookstore company in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were first introduced during the Pre-Season.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • The McGuffin's bookstore was added to Retail Row.

Season 2[]

  • The McGuffin's bookstore was added to Tilted Towers.

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.00: The McGuffin's bookstore was added to Paradise Palms.

Season 6[]

  • The McGuffin's bookstore was in Paradise Palms turned into a Keyboard King cybercafe.

Season 8[]

  • Update v8.00: The McGuffin's bookstore was added to Happy Hamlet.
  • The Unvaulting Event: The McGuffin's bookstores in Tilted Towers and Retail Row were destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.10 The McGuffin's bookstore was added back to Retail Row.
  • The End: The McGuffin's bookstores were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00:
    • The McGuffin's bookstore was added back to Retail Row.
    • The McGuffin's bookstore was added to Misty Meadows.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The McGuffin's bookstores were turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00:
    • A McGuffin's Bookstore is on the Artemis Island. It is located at The Daily Bugle.
  • Update v19.10:
    • With the snow covering Tilted Towers being melted, the McGuffin's Bookstore located at the Castle Building is now accessible again.
