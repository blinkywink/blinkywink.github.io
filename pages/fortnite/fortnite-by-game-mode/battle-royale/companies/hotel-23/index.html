Overview


Hotel 23 is a hotel company in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.00: The Hotel 23 was added to Paradise Palms.

Season 6[]

  • Update v6.00: Halloween decor has been added at the hotel.

Season 7[]

  • Update v7.10:
    • A Christmas tree was added along with string lights.
    • January 2nd: The Christmas tree and the string lights have been removed.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.30:
    • The Hotel 23 was removed due to Paradise Palms's disappearance.
    • The Hotel 23 was added to Moisty Palms.
    • The pool is filled with lily pads, dark blue and dirty water.
  • The End: The Hotel 23 was sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Hotel 23 was added to Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00: No Sweat Insurance saved the Hotel 23 from flooding by installing floats.
  • Update v13.30:
    • July 30th: The water drained from the area and the Hotel 23 has turned back to normal.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00:
    • The Hotel 23 was removed due to Sweaty Sands's disappearance.
    • The Hotel 23 was added to Believer Beach.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Hotel 23 was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.
