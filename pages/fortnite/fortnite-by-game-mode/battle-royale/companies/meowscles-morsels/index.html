Meowscle's Morsels is a company in Fortnite: Battle Royale, introduced in Chapter 2: Season 7 on the island Apollo. The company is known for making cat food.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.10 Hotfixes
    • July 6th: The central building at Holly Hatchery is encased in a perpetual Alien Nanite; At least two shipping containers for Meowscle's Morsels can now be found.
  • Update v17.30: The Last Reality put up Meowscle's Morsels billboards on the map, located in different named locations. When close enough, the billboard will change from the Meowscle's Morsels logo to text that says "SUBMIT."

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: A billboard is present in The Daily Bugle, giving the implication that the company is still active.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

  • Update v23.00: Boxes of Meowscle's Morsels can be found in various places on the island.
