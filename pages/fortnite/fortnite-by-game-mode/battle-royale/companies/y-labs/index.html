Overview


Y-Labs is a company in Fortnite: Battle Royale from Reality: 982. It was first introduced during Chapter 2: Season 5.

Overview

Overview[]

Y-Labs is a mysterious laboratory owned by Lexa and Orin's father. The research topic of this lab appears to be Mechafusion, as well as the creation of cybertronic suits, one of which has been built for Lexa, so that she could pursue her occupation as a Bounty Hunter.
