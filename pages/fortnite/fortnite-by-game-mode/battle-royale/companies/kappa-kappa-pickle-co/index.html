Overview

Kappa Kappa Pickle Co. is the company that produces pickles in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

  • Update v33.00: Kappa Kappa Pickle Co.'s Kappa Kappa Farm along with food trucks and stores were added to Oninoshima.
  • Update v33.20: Kappa Kappa Pickle Co.'s main installment, Kappa Kappa Factory was added to Oninoshima.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

  • Update v34.00: BURDs convince store section was removed.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

  • Update v36.00: Pickles & Paddles is built in place of Dashi Dash.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kendo (and Tomoki) may be fans/regular customers of Kappa Kappa Pickle Co. because Kendo wears two bottles of their pickles on his waist and the two are seen enjoying them alongside Shadow Blade Hope in the Kendo Loading Screen.
