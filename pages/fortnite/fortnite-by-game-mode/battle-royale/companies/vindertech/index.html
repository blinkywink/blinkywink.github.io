Vindertech is a technology company in Fortnite: Battle Royale and Save The World. Vindertech was named after its founder, Doctor Vinderman. Vindertech is the manufacturer of many weapons and vehicles such as the Battle Bus and the Storm Shield.

Overview

Overview[]

Vindertech was founded by Dr. Vinderman as a technology company. Its goal was to prevent The Storm from ever happening, but the goal was ultimately failed. Ray took the leadership, forming Homebase to rescue Survivors from the Storm. Vindertech somehow has access to other Realities, including Reality Zero.
