Overview


Slap Co. is an energy-drink company in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was first introduced in Chapter 4: Season 1 and creates Slap products around Asteria.

Their main factory is in Slappy Shores, which was later purchased and acquired by Eclipse and by Kado Thorne.

Overview

Overview[]

Slap Co. is a beverage production company having speed and endurance enhancing properties. It was created at an unknown time period, but after Skye and Guff showed an employee the Slap Berry they discovered from the Medieval Biome, they turned it into a drink: Slap Juice, and became a rival to Slurp Co..[1]

History

History[]

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

After Fracture, Slap Co. is introduced as a new company. After Skye and Guff's discovery of the Slap Berry, Slap Co. became a new direct competitor to Slurp Co. with their new Slap Juice, and have spread their products across the island. They have locations in Slappy Shores and Slap 'N Go.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

Earthquakes have destroyed the Spillexx Gas Station across the Slap store, turning the location into Slap N' Gone.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

Kado Thorne arrives and purchases their Factory. He uses it as a base for the Eclipse.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

A Slap Juice poster can be seen in Seaport City.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

A new slap product, the Lawless Slap Cannon is released by Slap Co. Later, they release the Slap Jug.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

A new slap product, SlapBerry Fizz, which is a spin off of FlowBerry Fizz, was released by Slap Co. It was to sponsor O.X.R..

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Members of the Oathbound often use Slap Co. drinks, as evidenced by their presence in The Citadel, and other like locations.
    • This is not surprising as Slap Juice is made out of Slap Berry that are found in the Medieval Biome.
