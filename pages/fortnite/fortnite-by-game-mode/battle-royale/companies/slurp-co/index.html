Slurp Co. also known as Slurp Corp is a production company in Fortnite: Battle Royale that creates the Slurp products. Although their products were introduced during Season 1, the company was properly introduced during Chapter 2: Season 1, with their main factory at the time: Slurpy Swamp.

Overview

Overview[]

Slurp Co. is a beverage production company having protective and/or regenerative properties. However, secretly, it is an organization associated with SHADOW conducting experiments on humans in order to create super-soldiers.

History

History[]

Before Chapter 1[]

Slurp Co. was founded at an unknown time period, in an unknown place of The Omniverse. Under unknown circumstances, they were brought to Athena and their product became used by the inhabitants of the island.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

Slurp Co.'s main factory, Slurpy Swamp, was revealed. However, human testing grounds were found, and slurp super soldiers were supplied to A.L.T.E.R. under the supervision of Chaos Agent, a high-ranking SHADOW officer who appears to be secretly running the factory.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

During on of his experiments, Chaos Agent manage to transform Jonesy The Secondst, Bandolette and Leviathan into perfect Slurp Soldiers, who were dubbed as the Slurp Legends.
