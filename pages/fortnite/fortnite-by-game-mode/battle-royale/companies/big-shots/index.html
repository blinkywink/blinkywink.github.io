Overview


Big Shots is a coffeehouse chain in Fortnite: Battle Royale and Fortnite: Save the World. They produce expressos.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • Update v1.6: The Big Shots branch was added to Retail Row.

Season 2[]

  • Update v2.2.0: The Big Shots branch was added to Tilted Towers.

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.00: The Big Shots branch was added to Paradise Palms.

Season 7[]

  • Update v7.00: The Big Shots branch was added to Happy Hamlet.

Season 8[]

  • The Unvaulting Event: The Tilted Towers and Retail Row Big Shots branches were destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Season 9[]

  • Update v9.00: The Big Shots branch was added to Mega Mall.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.10: The Big Shots branch was removed due to Mega Mall's disappearance.
  • Update v10.20: The Big Shots branch was added back to Retail Row.
  • Update v10.30: The Big Shots branch was removed due to Paradise Palms's disappearance.
    • The Big Shots branch was added to Moisty Palms.
  • The End: The Retail Row, Moisty Palms and Happy Hamlet Big Shots branches were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Three Big Shots branches were added to Craggy Cliffs, Lazy Lake and Misty Meadows.
    • Big Shots was added back to Retail Row.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00: The Big Shots branch was added to Catty Corner.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: The GHOST Big Shots branch was added to the map.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The six Big Shots branches were turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: The Big Shots branch was added to Condo Canyon and Sleepy Sound.
  • Update v19.10: The Big Shots branch was added back to Tilted Towers.

Chapter 7 season 1[]

  • Update v39.00: The Big Shots company headquarters is sighted in Latte Landing, but the franchise has also taken over a lot of the island with many of their coffee vans and coffee shops being around the Golden Coast.
