History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Logjam joinery is found in Logjam Woodworks.
  • Update v11.30:
    • The location has been renamed to The Workshop.
    • Christmas decor and gingerbread men and presents have appeared in the workshop. Gingerbread men work at the control panels and presents have replaced logs on the conveyer belt.
    • A truck near the workshop has crashed down a hill, presents falling out all over the ground.
  • Update v11.31:
    • The Gingerbread men and presents have suddenly vanished from the workshop.
    • All presents have been gathered around the remains of the crashed truck near the workshop.
  • Update v11.40: The location has been renamed back to Logjam Woodworks.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00: Logjam Woodworks has been completely flooded over, a huge pile of rubble has completely covered it.
  • Update v13.20:
    • July 1st: The water level decreased and the pile of rubble has been cleaned up, revealing a small amount of Logjam Woodworks.
    • July 11th: The water level lowered a bit and now more of Logjam Woodworks is accessible, however the lower levels are still underwater.
    • July 18th: The water levels have decreased further, and the last large land of rubble has been cleaned up, revealing more of Logjam Woodworks.
  • Update v13.30:
    • July 24th: The water lowered even further, revealing the rest of Logjam Woodworks.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: Half of Logjam Woodworks was half destroyed by the Durrr Burger restaurant.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Logjam Woodworks was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Logjam joinery is found in Logjam Lumberyard.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.10: Logjam Lumberyard was destroyed by the Reality Tree's Roots and replaced by Logjam Lotus. Logjam set up a new location: The Chop Shop.

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

  • Update v40.20: Treechopped Timbermill is a sawmill built in the ruins of Tiptop Terrace.
