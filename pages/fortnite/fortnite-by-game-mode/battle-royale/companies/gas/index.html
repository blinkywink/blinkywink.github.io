Overview


GAS is a gas station chain in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Three GAS stations were added to Holly Hedges, Frenzy Farm and Gas N’ Grub.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.00: An underground hideout has been constructed underneath a gas station east of Frenzy Farm also only accessible through hideouts. It is called SHADOW Safe House - Beta.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00:
    • Five GAS stations were added to Lazy Lake, Catty Corner, east of Misty Meadows, west of Steamy Stacks, south of Weeping Woods.
    • The SHADOW Safe House - Beta disappeared.
  • Update v13.30:
    • Multiple gas stations have been upgraded around the map.
    • July 24th: A GAS station has appeared south of Weeping Woods, next to the Purple Steel Bridge.
    • July 30th: The construction at Sweaty Sands has been completed into a gas station.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: The GAS station south of Weeping Woods disappeared.
  • Update v14.10:
    • The GAS station south of Weeping Woods reappeared.
    • The Frenzy Farm GAS station branch was crushed by Upstate New York.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00:
    • Upstate New York was removed and the Frenzy Farm GAS station branch was re-added.
    • Salty Springs was replaced by Salty Towers the GAS station survives.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00: Salty Towers was replaced by Boney Burbs, the GAS station is still intact.
  • Update v16.20: The Catty Corner GAS station branch have been upgraded.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00:
    • The GAS station was removed due to Sweaty Sands's disappearance.
    • A GAS station was added to Believer Beach.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The GAS stations were turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Six GAS stations were added to Sleepy Sound, Coney Crossroads, The Joneses, Rocky Reels, Frosty Fields and Camp Cuddle.
  • Update v19.10:
    • A GAS station was added to Tilted Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: Two named GAS Stations are added as Landmarks - Shroom Station and 'Stools Fuels.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

  • Update v23.00: Three GAS Stations are added - Beep 'n Bounce, near Hitches and Ditches and in Slappy Shores.
