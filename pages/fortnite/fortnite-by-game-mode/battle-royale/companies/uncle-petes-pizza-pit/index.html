Overview

For information on the Landmark, see The Pizza Pit.


Uncle Pete's Pizza Pit is a restaurant chain existing in multiple realities in Fortnite. They appear in Battle Royale, Save the World and Party Royale. They are the rival to Durrr Burger.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Unlike Durrr Burger, Pizza Pit is not in Save the World's storyline.
    • The restaurant appears in random places in several zones in Stonewood.
  • It is Farmer Steel's favorite restaurant.
  • The company seems to run a secret Tomato cult, judging by Tomato Temple, Tomatohead's Crown style, and the Praise The Tomato emote, which shows some sort of ritual in which you throw a tomato in the air so it lands on your head.
    • The cult manages to convert Grill Seargent in the Tomato Temple loading screen.
