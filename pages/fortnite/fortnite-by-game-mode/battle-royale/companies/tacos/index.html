Overview


Tacos is a fast food chain existing in multiple realities in Fortnite. They appear in Battle Royale, Save the World and Party Royale.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • Two Tacos store were added in Retail Row and Tomato Town.

Season 2[]

  • Update v2.2.0: Three Tacos stores were added to Tilted Towers, Sports Center and Outskirts.

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.00: The Tacos restaurant was added to Race Track.

Season 8[]

  • Update v8.00: The Tacos store in Tomato Temple was destroyed.
  • The Unvaulting Event: The Tacos store in Tilted Towers was destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Season 9[]

  • Update v9.00:
    • The Tacos store was removed due to Retail Row's disappearance.
    • The Tacos store was added to Mega Mall.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.10:
    • The Tacos store was removed due to Mega Mall's disappearance.
    • The Tacos store was added back to Retail Row.
  • Content Update v10.10: The Tacos store at the Sports Center was destroyed by the Rift Beacon's explosion.
  • Update v10.30: A Rift Beacon has brought Greasy Grove to the island where the Tacos restaurant has replaced the Durrr Burger restaurant.
  • The End: The Tacos stores were were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00:
    • The Tacos store was added back to Retail Row.
    • The Tacos store was added to Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.30: Two Tacos stores were added to The Yacht.
  • Update v12.50: The Tacos store was added to The Plaza.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00:
    • A 2nd Tacos store was added to Sweaty Sands.
    • The Tacos store was added to Motorboat Mayhem.
  • Update v13.00:
    • July 30th: The 2nd Tacos store was removed from Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: The Tacos store was removed due to Motorboat Mayhem's disappearance.
  • Update v14.40: The Plaza Tacos store was decorated for Halloween.
  • Update v14.60: The Plaza Tacos store was back to normal.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: The Tacos store was added to Salty Towers.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00: The Tacos store was removed due to Salty Towers's disappearance.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00:
    • The Tacos store was removed due to Sweaty Sands's disappearance.
    • The Tacos store was added to Believer Beach.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Tacos stores were turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00:
    • The Tacos restaurant was added back to Greasy Grove.
    • The Tacos store was added to Rocky Reels.
  • Update v19:10: The Tacos store was added back to Tilted Towers.

Galactic Battle[]

Update v35.00: A Tacos food truck appeared in eAT-eAT.
