Overview


Hey Boo! is a Halloween shop company in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Season 6[]

  • The Hey Boo! shop was added to Retail Row.
  • The Hey Boo! shop was abandoned after Halloween ended.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • The Hey Boo! shop was added back to Retail Row.
  • The Hey Boo! shop was abandoned after Halloween ended.
    • It was later converted into a Christmas shop.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.20: The Hey Boo! shop was added to the vacant store next to Holly Hedges.
  • Update v14.30: The Hey Boo! shop was under staging.
  • Update v14.40: The Hey Boo! shop was finished, and became a named Landmark called Hey Boo! Megastore.
  • Update v14.50: The Hey Boo! Megastore had returned to the staging phase.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • The Hey Boo! Megastore was turned into Viking Vessel.
