Overview


Knepley's Pawn Shop is a pawn shop in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Season 2[]

  • The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added to Tilted Towers.

Season 8[]

  • The Unvaulting Event: The Knepley's Pawn Shop was destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added to Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00:
    • No Sweat Insurance saved the Knepley's Pawn Shop from flooding by installing floats.
    • The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added to Pawntoon.
  • Update v13.30:
    • July 30th: The water drained from the area and the Knepley's Pawn Shop has turned back to normal.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: The Knepley's Pawn Shop was removed due to Pawntoon's disappearance.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00:
    • The Knepley's Pawn Shop was removed due to Sweaty Sands's disappearance.
    • The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added to Believer Beach.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Knepley's Pawn Shop was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added to Condo Canyon.
    • The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added back to Pawntoon.
  • Update v19.10: The Knepley's Pawn Shop was added to Tilted Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00:
    • The Knepley's Pawn Shop was removed due to Condo Canyon and Pawntoon's disappearance.
    • Two Knepley's Pawn Shops were added to Cloudy Condos and 'Loon Pawntoon.
  • Update v22.40: With the arrival of Tainted Towers, The Knepley's Pawn Shop was destroyed and affected by Chrome.
    • In the Fracture live event, Two Knepley's Pawn Shops were destroyed.
