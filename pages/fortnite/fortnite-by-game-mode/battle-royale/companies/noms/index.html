Overview


Noms is a grocery store franchise in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The Shopping Cart vehicle is owned by Noms. They usually own apples, mushrooms, and peppers inside their stores.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • The Noms shop was added to Retail Row.

Season 2[]

  • The Noms shop was added to Tilted Towers.

Season 4[]

  • Update v4.30: Noms Shopping Cart added.
  • Update v4.50 The Noms sign at Retail Row disappears by a rift.

Season 5[]

  • Update v5.00: The Noms sign reappears at east of Risky Reels.

Season 7[]

  • Update v7.00:
    • The Noms shop was added to Happy Hamlet.
    • The Noms sign was removed due to Risky Reels's disappearance.
    • The Noms sign reappears at east of The Block.

Season 8[]

  • Update v8.00: The Noms sign was removed from the addition of the Volcano.
  • The Unvaulting Event: The Tilted Towers and Retail Row Noms shops were destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.20:
    • The Noms shop was added back to Retail Row.
    • The Noms sign has returned to it's original location.
  • Week 5: The Floating Island has returned carrying a Noms sign on it.
  • Update v10.31: The Noms shop was added to Gotham City.
  • The End: The Noms shops were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Three Noms shops were added to Craggy Cliffs, Misty Meadows and Sweaty Sands.
    • The Noms shop was added back to Retail Row.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: The Noms shop was added to Salty Towers.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00:
    • The Noms shop was removed due to Sweaty Sands's disappearance.
    • The Noms shop was added to Believer Beach.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The five Noms shops were turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Two Noms shops were added to Sleepy Sound and Condo Canyon.
  • Update v19.10: The Noms shop was added back to Tilted Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: Two rappelling lines have been added to the premises of the Noms shop, both of which lead up to IO Airship - Tilted.
  • Update v20.30: The Noms shop from Tilted Towers has been destroyed.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00:The Condo Canyon Noms shop was lifted into the air as the location becomes Cloudy Condos.
  • Fracture: The Noms shops were destroyed along with the island.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

  • Update v23.00: The Noms shop was added to Faulty Splits.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

  • Update v32.00: The Ice Isle mall was added. It features NOMS's logo outside.
