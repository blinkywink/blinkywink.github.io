Overview


Garden Center is a plants and gardening equipment shop company in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Garden Center was added to Holly Hedges.
  • Update v11.10:
    • October 29th: Halloween decorations have been added to the Garden Center for the Fortnitemares event.
    • November 5th: The Halloween decorations have been removed from the Garden Center.
  • Update v11.31:
    • December 24th: The Garden Center has been covered in snow.
  • Content Update v11.31 - 3
    • January 7th: The snow melted from the Garden Center.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: A challenge was added that involves finding a Sapling Groot in the External Shop.
  • Update v14.20:
    • September 26th: Birthday Decorations were added to the Garden Center.
    • October 1st: The Birthday Decorations were removed.
  • Update v14.30: Halloween Decorations were added to the Garden Center.
  • Update v14.50: All Halloween decorations were removed.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00: Holly Hedges has been transformed into Holly Hatchery also transforming the Garden Center.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00: The alien biomes are gone, which brought everything back to normal.
  • The End: The Garden Center was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00:
    • The Garden Center was added to Coney Crossroads.
