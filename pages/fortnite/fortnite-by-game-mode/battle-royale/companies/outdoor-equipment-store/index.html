Overview


Outdoor Equipment Store is a fishing equipment store company in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • Two Outdoor Equipment Store branches were added in Greasy Grove and Retail Row.

Season 5[]

  • The Outdoor Equipment Store branch was added to Tilted Towers.
  • The Outdoor Equipment Store branch in Tilted Towers was destroyed by The Cube.

Season 7[]

  • Update v7.00:
    • The Outdoor Equipment Store branch in Greasy Grove is partially frozen.
    • The Outdoor Equipment Store branch was added to Happy Hamlet.

Season 8[]

  • The Unvaulting Event: The Outdoor Equipment Store branch in Retail Row was partially destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Season X[]

Chapter 2[]

  • Update v10.10:
    • The Outdoor Equipment Store branch was added back to Retail Row.
    • Week 7: Greasy Grove was added back to the map with the Outdoor Equipment Store.
  • The End: The three Outdoor Equipment Store branches were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Outdoor Equipment Store branch was added back to Retail Row.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Outdoor Equipment Store branch was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: The Outdoor Equipment Store branch was added back to Greasy Grove.
