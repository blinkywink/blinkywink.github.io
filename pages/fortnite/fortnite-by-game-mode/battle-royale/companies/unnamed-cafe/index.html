History

History[]

Season 3[]

  • Update v3.10: Unnamed Cafe was added to Lucky Landing.

Season 7[]

  • Update v7.00: Unnamed Cafe was added to Happy Hamlet.

Season X[]

  • The End: The Unnamed Cafe were sucked into a black hole.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Two Unnamed Cafe were added to Sweaty Sands and Misty Meadows.
