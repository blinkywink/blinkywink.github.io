Lore

Lore[]

Reality 1009 is a Aquatic planet, 100 Years ago a meteor had landed and caused massive floods.

Factions

Factions[]

The Hidden[]

Hidden is a Secret Organization that nobody knows. only a few members are chosen. This faction led by Joey who was a Formerly Last Reality Lifeform.

No Sweat Insurance[]

No Sweat Insurance is the main Faction and controls the most part of reality 1009. they are also the ones who supplies each faction within Reality 1009.

The Council[]

The Council is the current client for No Sweat Insurance, The Council is now actually a Sub-faction for No Sweat Insurance.

Command Blimp[]

Fort Blimp is an Old IO Blimp that was reformed as an Supply Blimp by No Sweat Insurance, This Blimp has been moving 13 Years, After the great battle of the Mountain Regions, Fort-Blimp was the last standing Blimp of No Sweat Insurance, They repurposed it naming it "Command Blimp"

Fort Blimp currently has 30 Crewmembers and 2 Generals.
