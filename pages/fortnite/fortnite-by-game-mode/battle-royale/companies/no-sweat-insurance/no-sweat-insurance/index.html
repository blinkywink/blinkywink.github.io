No Sweat Insurance (also known as All Sweat Insurance) is an insurance Company in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were first introduced during Season 8. They are known for having notably protected town from flood during Chapter 2: Season 3, took charge (more or less) of the repair of the island during Chapter 3: Season 3 after the war against IO as well as having protected numerous locations threatened by Chrome during Chapter 3: Season 4.

Overview

Overview[]

No Sweat Insurance is an insurance company for almost everything, vehicles, buildings, gliders against almost any kind of situations. However, they are known for wanting to avoid paying as much as possible, encouraging their customers to pay as much as possible for their services, going as far as using criminal methods as well as taking revenge on customers who no longer pay by sabotaging them, as they did with The Society. Considering their actions, they are in truth a criminal organization disguised as insurance.

History

History[]

Season 8[]

  • Their first branch is built at Tilted Towers. After The Fire Kingdom's attack during The Unvaulting Event, it is the only surviving building.

Season 9[]

  • The same branch kept operating at Tilted Towers' upgraded variant, Neo Tilted.

Season X[]

  • The Neo Tilted branch has been replaced by the Gotham City branch.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • A new branch was opened at Lazy Lake. No Sweat Insurance signs are being put at Pleasant Park, Holly Hedges, Frenzy Farm and Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • No Sweat Storage appeared near Frenzy Farm, and was a floating storage site. Following the flood, buildings insured by the company were raised above the new water level, while those that were not were submerged beneath the water. Prior to the introduction of driveable Cars, No Sweat Insurance recalled all of the existing cars on the island. The company also seemed to team up with Spillexx to build Gas Stations in preparation for the arrival of the Cars. After the water levels want back to normal, all their locations were removed.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • No Sweat Insurance was added back to Tilted Towers once it thawed out.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Their Tilted Towers branch has been taken over by the Imagined Order.
  • The branch became independent of the Imagined Order after the IO loses control of Tilted Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • No Sweat Insurance sponsors a summer event titled "No Sweat Summer" from July 21st to August 9th 2022.
  • No Sweat Insurance then orchestrated the rebuilding of 3 buildings in Tilted Towers, designed from The Block 2.0.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • No Sweat Insurance raised buildings using balloons and jets in order to prevent Chrome spread. This includes Cloudy Condos, the Pirate Ship in Lustrous Lagoon, and their Tilted Towers branch, making a large city at Cloudy Condos, No Sweat Insurance Moved their Tilted Towers Branch to Chromejam Junction attempting to also Raise the building.
  • Fracture: All of No Sweat Insurance's branches on Artemis are destroyed during the event.

Before Chapter 5: Season 1[]

  • The Society has taken out an insurance contract with No Sweat Insurance. Valeria and Hope's family was responsible for paying the insurance costs.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

  • After The Society's defeat by The Underground, then by the gods who left them barely alive, Montague and Valeria both fled the island. The Society's insurance costs therefore stopped being paid.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

  • As a revenge against The Society, The Underwriter recruited some enforcers of the Wasteland Warriors thanks to Ringmaster Scarr and took down the train of The Society.
