Overview


a Farmers Market is a market selling locally grown food in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: The Farmers Market was added to The Orchard.
  • Update v11.21:
    • December 7th: Multiple posters have appeared around The Orchard, having the same information as the Risky Reels movie screen of Star Wars.
  • Update v11.30:
    • December 15th: All posters about the Star Wars event have been taken down.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.40:
    • April 17th: Multiple posters have appeared around The Orchard and the Farmers Market relating to music, a planet, and Sweaty Sands.
  • Update v12.41: The Travis Scott's Astronomical Event posters hung around The Orchard and the Farmers Market have been updated, showing the time and dates of the Travis Scott's Astronomical Event.
  • Update v12.50: All posters about the Travis Scott's Astronomical Event have been taken down.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.10: The Orchard and the Farmers Market were crushed by Upstate New York.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: The Orchard and the Farmers Market have been added back. Some part of them had been covered or destroyed by sand.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00: The Orchard and the Farmers Market have been repaired.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The Farmers Market was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.
