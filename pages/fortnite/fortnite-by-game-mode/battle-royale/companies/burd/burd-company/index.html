Burd is a oil manufacturing, Gas Station, convenience store, and fast food chain in Fortnite: Battle Royale introduced in Chapter 6: Season 1. The company originates in Reality: 783 on the island Oninoshima and in Reality: 857a on Skyline 10.

An alternate version of BURD called Burd-E-Mart exists in Reality: UN-149.

History

History[]

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

  • Update v33.00: Burd is introduced as a gas/oil/fuel company that is providing gas as well as other items around the island and serving fried chicken. All of the five BURD To Go Gas Stations, the BURD complex, the small convenience store in Seaport City,and the restaurant in Masked Meadows are available. A small branch is also present in the Skyline 10 map in the Fortnite: Ballistic gamemode.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

  • Update v34.00: BURD’s flagship gas station, fried chicken restaurant and convenience store - BURD - has been demolished by Alpha. A new BURD store was added alongside Crime City. A Rouge Repairs Black Market had replaced the BURD To Go gas station near Rolling Blossoms Farm and the BURD store at Masked Meadows has been replaced by a bank.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

  • Update v36.00: A tiny BURD store was added to Demon's Domain.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

  • Update v37.00: The Swarm stole the mascot statue from the roof and moved it from the gas station, creating The Burd King. In Update v37.40 An identical version of the gas station was consructed in the place of the landmark, with only a foundation remaining in the place of the old one.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 2[]

  • Update v38.00: Added Burd-E-Mart gas stations across Springfield Island, including a landmark of the same name.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In Chapter 6: Season 2, Alpha was at hand for Burd's stock ratings decreasing dramatically, following their flagship location's demolition.
  • The company’s name is a mashup of the words Bird and Turd.
    • This is backed up further by judging the companies mascot, Burd, which appears to be a yellow bird in the shape of cartoon feces.
  • The voice actor for BURD is Rejekun.
  • Fletcher Kane hates BURD.
  • Daigo seems to love BURD.
