Rift Gates are a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale that were introduced in Chapter 4: Season 2 for the Find the Force event. They allow the player to gain a new weapon and various Force abilities if applicable.

Overview

Overview[]

Jedi and Sith Holograms teleport via rift onto the map shortly after the beginning of the game. If interacted with, they offer their training to the player, and, if accepted, grant them new abilities after passing through a Rift Gate.

Every hologram provides the player with a unique Lightsaber which enables double jumping and faster sprint when held. However, these abilities come at the cost of increased energy consumption.

Galactic Battle

Galactic Battle[]

Darth Sidious []

Darth Sidious's hologram grants a Sith Apprentice Lightsaber and the Force Lightning ability, allowing the player to shock opponents with lightning, dealing 15 damage per tick. The ability has a minimum cooldown of 12 seconds and a maximum of 18 seconds.

Darth Vader []

Darth Vader is different from the other Rift Gates, where he needs to be defeated first before it can be accessed. Once defeated and accepted he grants a Sith Apprentice Lightsaber and the Force Saber Throw ability, allowing the player to throw any Lightsaber in the direction they are aiming, dealing 70 damage. This ability has a cooldown of 2 seconds.

Rey []

Rey's hologram grants a blue Jedi Padawan Lightsaber and the Force Push ability, enabling the player to push objects and enemies away. The ability has a cooldown of 12 seconds and deals no fall damage.

Mace Windu []

Mace Windu's hologram grants a purple Jedi Padawan Lightsaber and the Force Pull ability, enabling the player to pull objects and enemies towards you. This ability can also pull opponents through walls, has a windup time of 1 second, a cooldown of 20 seconds and deals no damage.

Darth Maul []

Darth Maul's hologram grants a Sith Apprentice Lightsaber and the Force Throw ability, enabling the player to lift objects from the environment and hurl them at opponents, dealing 30 damage per object thrown. The ability has a cooldown of 15 seconds.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Picking up a Lightsaber after eliminating an opponent will not grant Force abilities, as well as the double jump. These can only be obtained from Rift Gates.
    • Additionally, picking up a different Lightsaber will not switch the Force ability.
  • The Forsaken Vow Blade is the first Rift Gate to send enemy AIs against the player, though the battle is completely optional.
    • The music that plays for it is Battle Without Honor Or Humanity.

Rift Gates are one of the two possible appearance of Rifts in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were first introduced during Chapter 2: Season 5.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Rift Gates appear as "doors" opened by the energy of the Zero Point. They differ greatly from the classic appearance of rifts in that there are no cracks and the arrival point is clearly visible.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Both Sideways Rift and Pure Portals can take the form of Rift Gates.
    • Pure Portals mostly appeared as Rift Gates, especially when opened by Jones's Portal Device.
    • Rift Warden Stellan's Rift Gate, powered by Kinetic Ore, was meant to open Sideways Rift as Rift Gates. However, following sabotage and destabilization of the gate, the Rift took the form of a space-time crack.
