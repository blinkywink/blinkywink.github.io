Credits were the main Currency in Fortnite: Ballistic that could be used to purchase Weapons and Items.

Players started each match with 800 Credits.

Obtained

Obtained[]

Credits could be earned by completing rounds, scoring eliminations and planting the Rift Point Device.

  • Win a round: + 3,100 Credits
  • Lose a round and get eliminated: + 2,300 Credits
    • This value increases after each consecutive round lost by 400 Credits, capping at 800 Credits. It resets back to its orginal value after winning a round.
  • Lose a round but survive: + 1,000 Credits
  • Plant the Rift Point Device: + 200 Credits
  • Eliminate players: + 200 Credits

Players would be reset back to 800 Credits after switching sides.

Uses

Uses[]

Credits can be spent within a match at the beginning of every round on additional weapons and items.
