Overview

This item is currently vaulted in Battle Royale.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.

Turret Stations are static Mounted Turret emplacements, found within several Locations and Landmarks. Turret Stations were first introduced in Chapter 2: Season 7.

Description

Description[]

Turret Stations are locations in which a static Mounted Turret is placed. The Turret is Common rarity, but maintains the same stats at the Legendary Mounted Turret. When destroyed, the Mounted Turret will explode, and disappear. Mounted Turrets appear at most named locations, and Satellite Stations.

Turret Stations in Chapter 2: Season 8 were funded via Donation Stations, and were each added to game sequentially.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00: Mounted Turrets can be found at Satellite Stations. They are Common and spawn already placed.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00: You can fund Turret Stations by using your Bars at a Donation Station.
    • September 16th 2021: The Turret at Boney Burbs was successfully funded.
    • September 17th: Turrets at Weeping Woods and Pleasant Park have been successfully funded.
    • September 18th: Turrets at Misty Meadows, Stealthy Stronghold and Corny Crops have been successfully funded.
      • Second Turrets at Corny Crops and Pleasant Park have both been successfully funded.
      • The Turret at Catty Corner has been successfully funded.
    • September 19th: The second Turret and Weeping Woods has been successfully funded.
    • September 21st: A Turret at Holly Hedges was successfully funded.
    • September 22nd: Both Turrets at Retail Row have been successfully funded.
    • September 23rd: A Turret at Believer Beach was successfully funded.
    • September 24th: The Turret at Sludgy Swamp was successfully funded.
    • October 2nd: A Turret at Dirty Docks was successfully funded.
    • October 3rd: The Turret at Steamy Stacks was successfully funded.
      • The second Turret at Holly Hedges has been successfully funded.
    • October 10th: The second Turrets at Believer Beach and Dirty Docks have both been successfully funded
    • October 12th: The Turret at Craggy Cliffs was successfully funded.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: No Turret Stations appear on the new map.
