Overview

Radio Stations are a feature that was added in Chapter 2: Season 3 which gives players the option to listen music while in vehicles. Currently they are only useable in Cars, but were formerly accessible in Motorboats and Choppas. Radio Stations include copyrighted music, that players can choose to switch off and only play music free of rights via the Game Settings or switching to Party Royale (Radio Station).

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Motorboats and Choppas temporarily had Radio Stations, but the feature was removed just before Chapter 2: Season 3 ended.
  • Choppas have the feature to play Richard Wagner's "Ride Of The Valkyries" upon operation, via the 'Loudspeaker' function. This is a separate function to Radio Stations however, and is closer to a horn like other Vehicles.
  • On the Party Royale station, LazarBeam could have been heard at certain intervals to promote his Cosmetic Set.
  • Radio Underground was removed on June 14, 2024 but came back on October 8, 2025
  • Songs including references to drugs, alcohol, or swearing are edited to fit Fortnite's Age Rating Guidelines. Due to this, some lyrics may have been replaced or completely removed from featured songs.
  • Upon the release of the Radio Stations, several songs would play on stations which they didn’t belong to. This issue was since patched on the new radio U.I Update on September 27, 2023.
  • During representation months, station's playlists will feature multiple artists from each represented community.
  • DJ Yonder runs most radio stations, apart from Party Royale, Rock & Royale, and Radio Rocket League, which are ran by DJ Bop, Power Chord, and the IRL DJ Kaskade, respectively.
  • Party Royale is the only station available in Creative.
  • Ever since February 14, 2024 Radio Stations started using songs from Fortnite Festival. Rock and Royale was the first Radio Station to Adopt this.
  • Radio Underground's February 14, 2024 to June 14, 2024 Rotation was the last radio rotation not to feature Fortnite Festival songs.
  • Radio Underground's February 17, 2023 Rotation Featured Bandcamp Artist.
  • Destruction, by Joywave, Found in Power Play was the song used in the original Fortnite: Battle Royale Trailer back in 2017.
  • Drive, By Gretel, Found in Power Play was recently used in "The Crash (2026)"
