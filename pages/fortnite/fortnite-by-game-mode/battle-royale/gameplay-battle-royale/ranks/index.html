Overview

This article is about the mid-match Gameplay Mechanic for Battle Royale. For the competitive gamemode, see Ranked.

“
So you wanna be a hero? You’ll need to earn your stripes first! Every time you perform a heroic deed in each match, you’ll progress towards raising your Hero Rank.
”
— v36.00 Release Notes

Ranks are a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite that were introduced in Chapter 6: Season 3.

They were known as Hero Ranks during Chapter 6: Season 3, O.X.R. Ranks in Chapter 6: Season 4, and Blitz Level in Blitz Royale.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Ranking Up[]

Rank is shared through a squad, and all players start each match as Rank C. Ranking up involves gaining points from accomplishing certain objectives, including eliminating other players or guards, surviving storm phases, and taking a sprite to a shrine. In Chapter 6: Season 3, every time the player ranked up, they would gain Effective Health based on their rank, 25 at Rank B, 50 at Rank A, then 100 at Rank S and above.

Rewards for Ranking Up

Rewards for Ranking Up[]

Every rank grants rewards, depending on the season; rewards usually take the form of supply caches, such as Drop Pods that drop near the player, or access to Hero Caches, with improved rewards for each rank. They guarantee a consumable and a weapon of a specific rarity, going from Uncommon to Legendary, depending on the specific rank.

Reaching Rank S+ provides significant rewards, including powerful Boons and Mythic weapons. For example, in Chapter 6: Season 3 the player would have received all Boons (except the Surging Speed Boon) that they hadn't yet obtained.

History

History[]

  • C6S3
  • C6S4

Update v36.00[]

  • Introduced Hero Ranks to Battle Royale.

Update v36.10[]

  • Blitz Levels were added alongside Blitz Royale.

Update v37.00[]

  • Renamed Hero Ranks to O.X.R. Ranks.
  • Ranking up now spawns a Drop Pod.
  • Removed Effective Health gained from ranking up.

Update v37.40[]

  • O.X.R. Ranks were vaulted.
