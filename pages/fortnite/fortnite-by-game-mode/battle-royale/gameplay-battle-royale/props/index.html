Overview

Props are environmental objects in Fortnite Battle Royale, Save The World, Creative, Reload, Blitz Royale and LEGO Fortnite. They make up nearly the entire game and form everything from trees, rocks, buildings, landscapes, and more. Most props can be destroyed with a Harvesting Tool and will give off Materials, though there are few that are “indestructible.”

Destructible

Destructible[]

Destructible props are props with a Health bar that can be damaged and destroyed using any weapon. Hitting the prop with Harvesting Tool will make it drop Materials.

Hideouts

Hideouts[]

Hideouts are a variety of special props which can be interacted with by the player, in order to allow them to hide inside them. When interacted with, the player will enter the prop, and remain hidden until they exit.

Port-a-Potty
Dumpster
Haystack
Snowball
Sneaky Snowman
Scare Crow
Llamataun
