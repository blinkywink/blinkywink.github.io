Overview

Zero Point Dash is a gameplay mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was added in Chapter 2: Season 5.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Zero Point Dash allows the player to perform a short teleport when pressing the Jump Button whilst already in the air.

Players can identify how much Zero Point Dash charges they have left based on the purple cystals around them, the player can have a maximum of 3 Zero Point Dash charges at a time.
