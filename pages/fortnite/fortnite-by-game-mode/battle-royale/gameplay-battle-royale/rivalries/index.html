Overview

This page is about a mechanic that has been removed from the game.

“
Go head-to-head with another player as your rival. Win rivalries in a match to earn points for your side and Rival Credits to buy Perks, powerful weapons, and XP.
”

Rivalries are a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite that was introduced in Battle Royale Chapter 7: Season 2. Rivalries focus on one-on-one combat with other players on opposing global teams.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Rivalries are shared across teams, and can be started by three ways :

  • from Rivalry Screens, that are scattered all across the map ;
  • when a player has dropped a Rivalry Contract when eliminated by somebody other than their rival and/or their team ;
  • if the Rivalry Rumble Rift Anomaly starts for all players of the match.

When a Rivalry has been started, it gives the player (or their team) 5 minutes to eliminate an opponent of the triggering player's opposing faction (e.g., a player on Team Foundation will have a player on Team Ice King chosen as their rival, and vice versa). If no opposing faction players exist in the match, no rivalry will be started.

Once a rivalry is started, both the aggressor and their opponent will have their approximate location marked on each other's map in the form of a large circle. These circles shrink over time until they almost reveal each player's exact location.

Successfully eliminating the opponent within the time limit will grant the player/their team 10-20 Rivalry Credits. If the opponent is eliminated by somebody other than their rival and/or their team, they drop a Contract which can be used to claim the Credit reward; however, if somebody else picks up the Contract, then they become the new Rival who must be eliminated. Stealing other people's contracts in this way does not grant credits.

If a player fails to eliminate their opponent within the time limit and lets them escape, there is a chance that they can challenge each other again via a rematch.

A player's overall Rivalry wins are tracked on a leaderboard with their friends list, which can be seen under the Showdown tab in the Lobby or by accessing the Map screen while players are in a match. The global amount of Rivalry wins contribute towards each team's progress in unlocking items for the loot pool as well as for other in-game and real life rewards.

Rewards

Rewards[]

Rivalry Credits can be spent at Rivalry Screens for special loot and other rewards. As the player wins more rivalries over several matches, more "slots" for items on sale become available, depending on the type of gear:

  • For weapons:
    • 20 wins will unlock the third item for sale.
    • 60 wins will unlock the fourth and final item for sale.
  • For miscellaneous gear and Perks:
    • 2 wins unlocks the Rarity Upgrade
    • 10 wins unlocks 20,000 (formerly 5,000) XP (purchasable once per day).
    • 40 wins unlocks the Mark On Damage Perk
    • 80 wins unlocks the Infinite Slap Perk

Credits carry over across matches, and some of the available items are random:
