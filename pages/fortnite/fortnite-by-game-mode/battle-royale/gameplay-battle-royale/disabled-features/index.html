Disabled Features

Overview

When an item or gameplay mechanic causes an issue in Fortnite: Battle Royale, developers are able to disable it, removing player's abilities to use said item or mechanic to prevent abuse.

Description

Description[]

As Fortnite: Battle Royale is a huge game with many players and item interactions, oftentimes game-breaking bugs can slip through the cracks and enable players to have an unfair advantage in the game, or accidentally lock themselves out of normal gameplay. In order to combat this, items or mechanics can be disabled, stopping players from accessing them and enabling the game to return to normal whilst the issue is removed.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Hurdling currently holds the record for longest period of being disabled at once in all modes, and total.
    • It was disabled for 65 days in a row (this is still ongoing), and disabled for 89 days in total due to two separate issues.
  • The Boundless set holds the record for the longest period of being disabled in one mode, being Arena.
  • Shadow Stones although not disabled for the longest time, hold the record for the most disablings / bugs related to their inclusion, at 4:
    • The item was disabled twice, as listed in the table above.
    • A bug existed for a short time where players could use a Harvesting Tool whilst in Shadow Form via the Hand-Forged or Snikt! emote, leading to said emote being permanently removed from use with shadows.
    • Shadow Bombs were disabled on launch in Chapter 4: Season 1 for an unknown reason.
  • The reason Glitched is present on the list despite being permanent is because it was intended to be fully functional, and if possible, Shadow Stones would have been present in its rotation.
    • This is unlike other items which are permanently altered or removed, as they are changed due to community feedback or general development changes.
  • J.B. Chimpanski Quest Line was likely intentionally disabled, as the developers understood Donation Stations were not going to be available for the whole of the season.
    • Despite this, FortniteStatus still reported the change.
  • Despite Dragon Rune Lance and Riot Control Baton being disabled for Footstep audio being muted, Erisa when equipped with Guardian Daggers also makes no footstep noise, with the issue yet to be resolved.
