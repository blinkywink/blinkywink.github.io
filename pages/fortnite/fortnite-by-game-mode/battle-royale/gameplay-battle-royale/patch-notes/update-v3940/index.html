Fortnite goes to the Pink Pony Club on February 5 as GRAMMY-winning Chappell Roan kicks off Festival Season 13! Chappell’s adding “Pink Pony Club” to her collection of Jam Tracks, with “Good Luck, Babe!”, “HOT TO GO!”, and “The Giver” returning for an encore.

Hey everyone! Update v39.40 is live! Dive into the notes to see what’s new and which issues we’ve fixed.

Odyssey

Odyssey[]

➕ Additions[]

  • New worlds will now start on the mainland. A monk named Tiago will spawn with you and give you the opportunity to teleport to NINJAGO Island at your leisure.
  • From today till February 8, additional Steel Ore and Dragon Opals are dropping from Elite ELEMENTAL enemies such as Bears, Wolves, Rollers, Dragons, and Lost Masters.

⚒️ Bug fixes[]

  • After defeating a Spire, the Golden Reward Chest will sometimes appear in midair.
  • Sandbox quest “Deal Damage” and Survival quest “Closing Time” now progress correctly.
  • Resolved an issue where the Bait Buckets couldn’t be unlocked in Codex or in Mixer.
  • Fixed an issue where the players weren’t dealing any damage to the Spire when using Spinjitzu.
  • Mitigated an issue where renaming of the villages was not available after rejoining the world.
  • Resolved the low-resolution issue for generic prefab icons.
  • The Processing queue works as intended and doesn’t automatically start when the controller is on.
  • Fixed an issue where the players couldn’t sprint when their stamina was low.
  • Fixed a visual issue with Spinjitzu tornado form displaying after the minifig was flying or jumping.
  • Resolved an issue with Mask Maker Diago not spawning in players’ worlds.
  • Fixed a small inconsistency with the Palace of Oni build and its foundation.
  • Resolved a visual issue where the minifig could appear invisible when knocked out of Spinjitzu.
  • Fixed a small inconsistency with the quest “A Gem for Jay”.
  • Relics can now be placed in the Recycler.
  • Fire dragon now drops the Ritual Oil upon defeat.
  • Fixed an issue where the Drill Spinjitzu couldn’t be cancelled using the controller.
  • Resolved an inconsistent behaviour of villagers when attacking the dragon.
  • Mitigated an issue where a player would spawn on a roof even though they were assigned a bed.
  • Resolved an issue where the Lost Master would glitch when spawning on the ground.
  • Mitigated an issue where the players could walk on water longer than intended when the ICE Glide ability was active.
  • Resolved an issue where some players were unable to use the Bus Stations.
  • Addressed the issue where some craftable items were not recyclable.
  • Fixed the issue where the map marker for Ninjago Island would not appear if players walked to the Monastery.

Save the World v39.40 Update Notes[]

Howdy all! This update includes progression tweaks based on your feedback, along with a set of bug fixes. Read on for details!

Updates, Changes and Improvements

Updates, Changes and Improvements[]

Power Rating Progression Update[]

We’ve heard your feedback on the recent Power Rating changes and made more adjustments to better reflect your progression. Since update v39.00, Power Rating has been calculated using:

  • Total F.O.R.T. stats
  • Survivor Squads
  • The rating of the Heroes in your active Hero Loadout
  • The top three highest-rated weapons in your Backpack

With this update, Power Rating will now also factor in your top three Weapon Schematics alongside the weapons currently in your Backpack. The game will then automatically select the top three highest-rated weapons across both sources when calculating your Power Rating.

This means your Power Rating should now better reflect your total investment and progression, even when your strongest weapons aren’t actively carried.

ICYMI: Ventures remain unchanged and will continue using the previous Power Rating calculation based solely on F.O.R.T. stats.

Bug Fixes

Bug Fixes[]

  • Wall Launcher Traps can once again send Husks flying. Yeet!
  • The mantling/hurdling sound effect volume has been adjusted to match other experiences.
  • Fixed an issue where some abilities could be repeatedly activated while on cooldown, sometimes consuming Energy without fully activating.
  • Interact/Reload will no longer fail when a weapon mag is not fully loaded.
  • Hotwire’s Overcharge (+) Perk now consistently triggers lightning damage when swinging the hammer.
  • Inspecting certain Heroes on the Manage screen will no longer result in missing head textures.
Known Issues

Known Issues[]

  • Players may notice an obsolete Research tab has reappeared in the Command menu. Pressing the Research button within the tab has no effect and does not impact F.O.R.T. stats.
    • This will be fixed in the next major game update.
  • Player-built structures may not finish their build animation - Trello
  • Certain weapons' firing animations may continue after shooting - Trello
  • Players are currently unable to purchase multiple Armory Slots in the Llamas tab - Trello
  • The Ancient's Launcher fires instantly after aiming - Trello
  • The “X has joined the team” notification still displays Power Level and F.O.R.T. stat increase text, even though these stats no longer change when players join a party.
  • [Simple Edit] UI does not update to reflect the reset after editing a structure.
  • [Xbox One] Directional Jump Pads won’t activate when Phase Shifting onto them.
  • The Hero Commander Buckle+ Perk currently provides reduced effective damage as weapon level increases.
  • The player character may sometimes use an incorrect arm-sweep animation when interacting with world objects.
