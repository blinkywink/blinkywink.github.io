v40.40 Act III: Battle Royale Update Notes[]

May 14th 2026
🔗 Source: Communities

Reinforcements have arrived! Overwatch heroes have joined the fight in Battle Royale, an overhauled Zero Build, Reload and Blitz to answer The Foundation’s call for reinforcements against the Ice King. Read on for more details, including the latest fixes and improvements.

Fortnite x Overwatch

Fortnite x Overwatch[]

Busan Sanctuary, Hanamura Courtyard, King's Row Tower, and bases inspired by Watchpoint: Gibraltar are now on the Island. At King's Row Tower, a Payload spawns each match after it unlocks in the Showdown, rewarding Overwatch loot to everyone who escorts it home.

Tracer and Mercy also bring their signature weapons to the loot pool:

  • Pulse Pistols puts you in first-person mode, lets you rewind back a few seconds to your previous position and undo damage taken, and lets you Blink to zip short distances.
  • Caduceus Staff latches a healing beam onto teammates to restore their health and shields, and while it's equipped you can glide through the air.
Competitive Notes

Competitive Notes[]

With the FNCS Major Summit LAN coming up, we’re keeping the Competitive Battle Royale meta consistent by maintaining a loot pool similar to the previous game update. This means:

  • Tracer's Pulse Pistols and Mercy's Caduceus Staff are disabled
  • The Payload Rift Anomaly is disabled
  • Skyline Deployers and FlowBerry Mist Grenades will remain in the Competitive loot pool

Win Rivalries for your team in the Act III Showdown to unlock new updates on the Island and items for your Locker:

  • Overwatch Payload Rift Anomaly
  • Orca MV-261 Dropship Landmark
  • Overwatch Cache Chests
  • Hero Cache Chests
  • Overwatch Mercy's Crest Spray
Trialing Major Zero Build Changes

Trialing Major Zero Build Changes[]

From infinite tactical sprint while your Pickaxe is out, new Pocket Items, and more… Zero Build just got a major refresh for the remainder of Chapter 7 Season 2

  • Everyone now drops in with a Pistol, a Small Shield Potion and a Pocket Item
  • Pocket Items are unlimited-use items with cooldowns that boost mobility and survivability
    • Choose from Shockwave Grenades, Med Mist Grenades and Shield Bubbles
    • Swap your equipped Pocket Item at Vending Machines around the map
  • Reach the ground faster after dropping from the Battle Bus. Don’t forget to redeploy your Glider in time!
  • Lethal fall damage now leaves you at 1 HP and removes all shields instead of eliminating you in Unranked matches
  • Redeploy your Glider whenever you’re high enough in the air to travel further
  • Unlimited Stamina while holding your Pickaxe
  • Elims now restore your Overshield
  • Environmental structures and objects now provide stronger cover

Be sure to check out the full breakdown here and tell us what you think about all of the new changes!

✅ BR Bug Fixes & Improvements

✅ BR Bug Fixes & Improvements[]

General[]

  • Fixed an issue where Quests currently available in the Quests tab incorrectly appeared as completed
  • Laufey’s dress moves more consistently, and won’t block your visibility as much while playing
  • You can again join other players’ group Emotes (e.g. Shock Grip, Death Rockers)
  • Kafka’s Sword no longer creates a bright flash when hitting objects at high FPS in Performance Mode
  • Fixed Ohm’s Perch Back Bling animations not playing after gliding for the first time in a match

Gameplay / UI[]

  • Being eliminated from DBNO while self-reviving no longer causes the camera to get stuck after rebooting
  • You can again sprint out of a crouch after hurdling through a window
  • You can again scroll through Quest categories without the Quest bar collapsing automatically

Note: Shoulder Riding will remain disabled for now, but we'll let you know if it returns in the future.

PC[]

  • Performance Mode now automatically chooses the best DirectX rendering path for your device to improve stability and performance
  • Slightly improved game startup times on some NVIDIA video cards by reducing DLSS initialization time
  • Auto Super Resolution now scales correctly at more fullscreen resolutions on supported ARM laptops
  • Improved texture rendering on the Intel HD 5500 video card in Performance Mode

Mobile[]

  • Improved aim assist on touch input so it’s easier to track and hit distant or fast-moving targets while aiming
  • You can now tap the Exit button instead of needing to hold it to leave Vehicles
  • Tapping the Self-Throw button now throws items at your feet instead of showing the throwing arc
  • Auto-Interact now correctly opens chests while running toward them
  • You'll no longer accidentally re-enter Ziplines after hopping off when Auto-Interact is enabled
  • Resolved a file corruption error that appeared when updating the game on some iOS devices
  • Graphics quality settings no longer reset after dropping into a BR match
  • Resolved an issue where the FPS setting wouldn't save after a game update on some iOS devices
  • Reduced Megara’s dress clipping when previewing the Outfit in the Locker
  • The Omnitrix Pick's Ampfibian Style now displays consistently after emoting on some Android devices
  • Resolved a delayed text chat resizing when sending long messages quickly
  • The Back button now appears correctly after reaching the end of a conversation, no longer skipping the last dialogue line
  • Added a missing Confirm button when a player has 0 currency and no items selected

For other fixes, improvements and known issues in v40.40, check out this post.

We’re keeping an eye on reports across social media and community spaces - please keep them coming as we work to fix them.

Introducing the Hover Brick!

Introducing the Hover Brick![]

  • A brand-new toy inspired by the Star Wars universe! Snap it onto your creations and watch them glide effortlessly across the landscape. Land traversal will never feel the same again.
  • The Hover Brick counteracts gravity, letting your builds glide effortlessly above the ground. Flip the switch and watch your creations rise!
  • Track down The Mandalorian and strike up a conversation. He'll show you the ropes of the brand-new Hover Brick! Once you've chatted with Mando, you'll unlock your very first Hover Vehicle Guided Build, putting you on the fast track to crafting your own Star Wars-style ride.
  • Then it's time to build, boost, and blast off into your very own galactic adventure!
  • You'll need Durasteel and Plastoid,, the essential ingredients for getting your hovercraft off the ground. Lucky for you, the Star Wars-themed enemies invading the Main Island are packing exactly the ingredients you need, so suit up and start farming!
  • Complete Din Djarin and Grogu's vacation requests to earn brand-new vehicle Blueprints designed specifically to take advantage of the Hover Brick. From sleek speeders to rugged skiffs, the galaxy's coolest rides are yours to claim.
  • Tackle Din Djarin's quest line, and you'll be rewarded with the resources you need to get your whole fleet hovering. Helping the Mandalorian pays off, literally!
  • We've reshuffled the Mobility tab in the Contraptions menu, making it easier to find what you need!
Pro Tips for Hover Brick:

Pro Tips for Hover Brick:[]

  • Place the Hover Brick and use a wrench to interact with it. Then set a channel that can be activated with a driver's seat or switch.
  • Hover Bricks lift to a set distance from the ground. If they’re placed too high in a build, they won’t lift.
  • Hover Bricks have a weight limit.
  • For a smooth, stable ride, try placing four Hover Bricks at opposite corners of your build.
  • Pair them with Thrusters to add steering and propulsion.
Lightsaber Overhaul

Lightsaber Overhaul[]

  • Three New Blade Colors have arrived! Choose from White, Yellow, and Purple — joining Blue, Green, and Red for a total of six color options.
  • Your blade color now reflects how you fight — choose wisely!
    • Every Lightsaber now comes with one of three distinct combat styles, each with its own signature attacks and special abilities:
      • Defense (Blue & White) — Block incoming strikes and fight back enemies with devastating counterattacks.
      • Aggression (Red & Purple) — Go on the offensive with relentless combos. Just keep an eye on your stamina!
      • Fluidity (Green & Yellow) — Soar into battle with acrobatic aerial combos and unmatched mobility.
  • Craft Your Own at the Rebel Workbench
    • Lightsabers are no longer one-and-done legendary finds — you can now forge your own at the Rebel Workbench in Tier 4, 6, and 8 rarities. Heads up: crafted Lightsabers are durable but can break with heavy use, so keep a backup handy!
  • Hunt for Kyber Crystals
    • A brand-new resource has entered the galaxy. Kyber Crystals are the heart of every Lightsaber. Track them down to craft your own.
  • What About My Old Lightsabers?
    • All existing Lightsabers, including those gifted by Captain Bravara, looted from Imperial Bunkers, or earned through the Codex, will be converted to Tier 4 Lightsabers with durability. Your collection isn't going anywhere!
  • Power Up with Runes
    • Lightsabers can now be enhanced at the Rune Forge. Apply Runes to customize your blade and tailor it to your playstyle.
  • Arm Your Allies
    • You can now hand off Lightsabers to your NPC followers. Build a saber-wielding squad and storm the battlefield together!
Kyber Crystals — The Heart of Every Lightsaber

Kyber Crystals — The Heart of Every Lightsaber[]

  • A legendary new resource has arrived in your world! Kyber Crystals are the key ingredient for crafting your very own Lightsaber, and hunting them down is half the adventure.
  • Kyber Crystals come in all six Lightsaber colors: Blue, Green, Red, Purple, White, and Yellow. The crystal you find determines the color (and fighting style!) of the blade you craft, so collect them all to master every combat form.
  • Crystals come in Tier 4, 6, and 8 varieties. The rarer the crystal, the more powerful the Lightsaber you can forge with it.
  • Where to Find Them
    • On the Main Island — Take down Star Wars-themed enemies, crack open special chests, and complete event rewards to score crystals during the limited-time event.
    • On the Rebel Island — Battle Star Wars-themed enemies, loot chests, and earn rewards both during and after the event, making this your go-to crystal-hunting spot once the celebration ends.
New Creatures from a Galaxy Far, Far Away

New Creatures from a Galaxy Far, Far Away[]

  • Porg — These adorable little birdlike critters are as charming as they are harmless. Lead one back to your barn to tame it and add a feathered friend to your village.
  • Bantha — Big, woolly, and surprisingly mellow. These gentle giants can also be tamed by guiding them home to your barn, perfect for any farm in need of a Tatooine touch.
  • Nexu — This vicious predator boasts bone-crushing jaws, a deadly pouncing leap, and absolutely zero chill. Approach with caution and a weapon.
  • Rancor — A towering nightmare of muscle and rage. The Rancor lives for one thing: leveling everything in its path. Bring your bravest squad…and maybe a Lightsaber.
  • These creatures will roam the Main Island for the duration of the Star Wars event. Once the celebration ends, they'll relocate to make their permanent home on Star Wars Rebel Island, so if you want to encounter them on the Main Island, the clock is ticking!
  • Any Porgs or Banthas you tame on the Main Island during the event will stay in your village permanently, even after the event wraps up. Don't miss your chance to lock in these galactic companions while they're still around!

Additions:[]

  • We tracked down and fixed the issue causing stutters and frame rate drops while building, especially on lower-end hardware.
  • Starting with this update, Bus Stations will now glow while Fast Travel is in use.
  • Look up — the skies have changed! For the duration of the Star Wars event, your world's skybox has been transformed:
    • Twin suns now blaze overhead, Tatooine-style; the moon has been replaced by the Death Star, looming ominously above, and keep your eyes peeled for X-Wing flyovers zooming across the sky!
  • We've redesigned the channel modification screen for vehicle toys from the ground up! All 10 channels are now laid out in a cleaner, more intuitive format, making it way easier to understand how the channel system actually works and dial in your vehicles exactly the way you want.
  • We've majorly improved the flip input for vehicles; instead of just spinning you in place, it now does a much better job of getting your ride back on its wheels (or hover pads) where it belongs. Smaller vehicles, especially wheeled ones, will feel the biggest difference. Less time stuck upside down, more time tearing across the landscape!
