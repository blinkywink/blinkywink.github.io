Overview

Update v14.30 is an Update that released on October 13th, 2020 for Chapter 2: Season 4.


Battle Royale[]

Weapons + Items

Weapons + Items[]

  • Added Black Panther's Kinetic Armor
    • Added in Mythic Rarity
    • Damage: 40
  • Vaulted Birthday Presents
  • Combat Shotgun Balance Changes
    • The Combat Shotgun damage increased from 50/53/55 to 57/60/63.
    • Made the Combat Shotgun more effective at longer ranges.
  • The Slurpfish now heals 40 HP/Shield.
  • Unvaluted Rocket Launcher in Legendary.

This Week - Clip's Nightmare[]

Starting Friday, October 1Ath at 8pm. A nauseating spell overtakes Hexylvania. A gunsmith is haunted by a figure from her past. Help Homebase dispel the curse before it's too late! Complete this brand new questline to unlock Homebase’s favorite blacksmith, Clip!

Dire Leads the Pack!

Dire Leads the Pack![]


"Howwwwwwwwlll !" - Dire

  • Standard Perk: Night Stalker
    • During evening and night, increases movement speed by 25%
  • Commander Perk: High Caliber Harvest +
    • During evening and night, increases movement speed by 50% and decreases energy cost for abilities by 50%
  • Unlocks Team Perk: Hunter's Instinct
    • FOR EACH: Hero of Epic or higher rarity with at least 4 stars Eliminations during evening or night grant 0.24% damage and 0.08% Melee Life Leech, up to 40 Stacks. These bonuses last until night ends.
