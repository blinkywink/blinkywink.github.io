New update, new weapon! v36.20 brings a new Launcher into the mix, accompanied by a whole bunch of buffs, adjustments and bug fixes. Let's get into it:

Updates, Changes and Improvements

Updates, Changes and Improvements[]

Introducing the Ancient's Launcher![]

Recovered from the remnants of a storm-consumed civilization, this powerful new weapon launches an explosive orb of magic and brings an all-new mechanic to Save the World.

Weapon Perk: Ancient Curse[]

Mark up to eight enemies by aiming down sights (ADS) and hovering your crosshair over them to apply a mystical Glyph that lasts for five seconds. Then, release ADS and unleash single-target damage on all marked targets in the order they were marked. Bear in mind - the Ancient's Launcher is mighty, but can overheat! Aim carefully, mark wisely, and watch your enemies fall one by one!

Weapon Balance Update

Weapon Balance Update[]

This update brings bug fixes and targeted buffs to a variety of weapons, especially those that are situationally useful but often outclassed in general play.

  • Most ranged Vindertech weapons (Pulsar, Blazer, Burster, Jolter, Blaster, Disintegrator) have received stat buffs. These weapons' old 5-headshot Perk (minus the Disintegrator) has been replaced with the fixed version of the following Perk:
    • Damage dealt to enemies below 15% health will immediately eliminate them. Does not affect Mist Monsters or Bosses.
  • Tiny Instrument of Death now deals higher Damage, has stronger Knockback, faster Reload Time, less Recoil, and uses slightly more ammo per shot.
  • Shooting Star now overheats less quickly and has faster Reload Time.
  • Potshot's details now display the full extent of its previously hidden base damage, which has been reduced as part of the balance changes.
  • In addition to the changes above, the following weapons are eligible for an optional one-time full reset - all invested resources will be refunded, and the weapons will return to level 1:
    • Xenon Bow
    • Plasmatic Discharger
    • Potshot
    • Vindertech Pulsar
    • Vindertech Blazer
    • Vindertech Burster
    • Vindertech Blaster
    • Vindertech Jolter
Blakebeard’s Stash

Blakebeard’s Stash[]

We've updated the Blakebeard’s Stash Team Perk to reduce confusion and prevent interruption during fast-paced moments. It now opens instantly when the Perk owner walks over it - no interaction required! To reduce visual clutter, only the Perk owner can now see and collect the Stash and its loot.

Mission Vote Notification

Mission Vote Notification[]

To ensure players don’t miss vote prompts, the Mission Activation / Object Difficulty Vote notification now appears at the top center of the screen.

Bug Fixes

Bug Fixes[]

  • Flutter's Wisp damage now scales more effectively.
  • Chests and other containers no longer appear as if already opened or empty in Hit the Road.
  • The correct amount of Battle Pass XP will be now be earned after the weekly limit has been reached, instead of earning 0 XP.
  • The default keybinds for Pet, Sprint and Dismount commands no longer appear as Unbound.
  • Opening the Emote Wheel no longer stops the player from opening the Main Menu.
  • Fixed a Perk Recombobulator issue where the focus could randomly jump to a different Perk slot.
  • Players are no longer prevented from jumping while in the Grasslands lake.
  • Corrected Major Oswald’s dialogue volume in Hunt the Titan.
  • Cursor Mode has been restored, and releasing the [Alt] key after holding it now closes the Inventory as intended.
  • Fixed an issue causing the Xenon Bow, Plasmatic Discharger and Potshot to incorrectly deal damage multiple times to a single target.
  • Fixed an issue where certain weapon Perks were incorrectly applied to other equipped weapons.
  • Resolved various UI and input issues that affected multiple menus.
  • Added several missing voice lines to the "Imposter!" Questline in Blasted Badlands.
  • Quest reward envelopes no longer cover the completed Quest's name on PS4 Pro.
Known Issues

Known Issues[]

  • Players may be able to target more than eight enemies with the Ancient's Launcher while aiming down sights.
  • After firing and aiming down sights, some visual and audio effects on the Ancient's Launcher may persist after overheating.
    • Overheating the weapon again and waiting for it to cool down will reset the effects (e.g. Overheat Bar, ADS Crosshair).
  • Banner icons may not update to the currently selected one upon entering or leaving a Mission.
Thanks for reading, Commanders! We’ll be eagerly awaiting your feedback on these updates. 🫡

Hey all! The v36.20 update goes live today with a number of additions and bug fixes.

Expeditions

Expeditions[]

Additions[]

  • A new map that players and more expert ones can explore is called Demon's Domain.
  • You can now upgrade your Hero Classes up to 50.
  • Enemies are growing stronger.
  • The new boss, Mask Maker Daigo, will try to stop you from completing the missions.
  • New mission, “Eliminate Captains”, and a new community quest are available.
  • Be on the lookout, more weapons of different rarities are dropping.

Bug Fixes[]

  • Mitigated an issue where the Superior Healing Runes equipped on weapons would block the Healing Cristal skill update.
  • Fixed an issue where the players lose specific abilities as the Hero Class Shadow Caller after its Shadow form ends.
  • Fixed an issue where players who chose Shadow Caller as their Hero Class couldn’t deploy shadow bombs or fly.
  • Fixed an issue where recycling was giving an incorrect amount of essence.
Odyssey

Odyssey[]

Additions[]

  • Mask Maker Daigo has released evil forces:
    • Spires have awakened. Players will be able to obtain different rewards by defeating various Spires, and the Spires will regenerate after a certain period. Note the Spires vary in difficulty – the ones close to your base are easier to defeat whereas others spread around your world will vary depending upon your game progression, all of which will affect the wave of enemies spawned by the Spires.
    • Numerous enemies will start to spawn in your world:
      • Masked minifig and Titans
  • New Rune is now available: Spire Bane III, which deals extra damage to any Spire, creature, or boss.
  • You can now craft a weapon with the new Corrupted Crystal material.

Bug Fixes[]

  • Weapons of high rarity now deal the corresponding damage.
  • Resolved an issue where a couple of Journal quests could not be completed.
Brick Life

Brick Life[]

Additions[]

  • There are now XL-sized lots on the map that players can claim and place larger builds such as Durrr Burger Flagship Restaurant, Main Stage, Ruin of Rue Royale, Knightly Large Keep, Grand Manor, Mos Eisley Large Garage, First Order AT-AT, First Order Base, and Supernova Academy Training Facility.
  • A new house, the Victorian Villa, was added that players can unlock.

Bug Fixes[]

  • Fixed an issue where players were not able to complete the bounty “Claim your Daily Stipend”.
  • Mitigated an issue with HUD layout failure on mobile when launching the game.
  • Fixed an issue where players were not able to use the Boba station.
  • Resolved an issue where a visitor could access the house even though the ‘Refuse Visitors’ function was enabled.
  • Resolved an issue where Dim Sum carts would float in the air.
  • Fixed a few inconsistencies when placing different decor items.
  • Addressed the issue with the taskbar showing as completed when players redo the previously done task.
  • Fixed a visual issue with the Cat Tree when players interacted with it.

Happy Tuesday Speedsters! Here's a quick overview of the latest changes and fixes in v36.20, including this week’s track rotation:

Updates & Changes

Updates & Changes[]

  • [Mobile/Touch Input] The Toggle Throttle setting now includes auto-acceleration by default after race start. You can toggle it off by going to Settings > Game > Rocket Racing > Toggle Throttle.
  • [Mobile/Touch Input] Players no longer need to press the “Accelerate” button after demolition when Toggle Throttle is enabled.
Bug Fixes

Bug Fixes[]

  • The original Battle Bus Vehicle has now been granted to all eligible players.
  • Repeatable Ranked Quests no longer display the incorrect end times.
  • The menu is no longer unresponsive when opened on the post-race screen while using a controller on Nintendo Switch or Mobile.
  • Vehicle lighting now displays correctly when driving through tunnels on various tracks.
  • The Text Chat key binding icon now shows up right away after joining a Casual race.
  • Updated the Black Painted Kill Screen Wheels to match the Style used in Rocket League.
  • The Dueling Dragons Wheels no longer clip through several Vehicles’ hubs.
  • [Nintendo Switch] Fixed several texture gaps on Breakout X.
  • Flames, Lightning, Wings, and Stripes Decals for Breakout X now appear correctly in the Locker.
  • [Nintendo Switch] Fixed a few texture issues seen on the Battle Bus.
Known Issues

Known Issues[]

  • When Rocket Racing is selected in the Lobby, some Battle Royale UI elements are incorrectly displayed (Career, Compete, BR Ranked Mode Information tabs).
  • The auto-acceleration option is missing when using a controller on Mobile.
  • [PC/Nintendo Switch] Players need to press the “Accelerate” button after demolition when Toggle Throttle is enabled.
