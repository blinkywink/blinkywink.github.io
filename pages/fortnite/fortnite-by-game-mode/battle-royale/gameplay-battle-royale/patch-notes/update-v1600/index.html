Overview

Update v16.00 of Fortnite was released on March 16th, 2021. It started Chapter 2: Season 6 and brought many new features.


What's new in Battle Royale?

What's new in Battle Royale?[]

Chapter 2: Season 6 - Battle Pass[]

New Season, new Battle Pass. 100 levels, over 100 new rewards. Available in-game right now for 950 V-Bucks. Find out more information on this Season's Battle Pass here.

Crafting[]

Don't just find your weapons. Make them. Using loot from wildlife and technology untouched by the Pulse, craft your own weapons anywhere.

New Locations[]

Visit new points of interest affected by the Pulse like Boney Burbs and Colossal Crops or visit the newly emerged location, The Spire.

Gameplay[]

  • Added Spire Towers
    • Going to the top of the either one of the 6 Spire towers on the edge of the Primal biome will launch you off, allowing you to redeploy your glider.
  • Added Crafting
    • Crafting allows you to make your own weapons.
      • With a Makeshift Rifle you can craft a:
        • Assault Rifle (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Mechanical Parts.
        • Primal Rifle (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Animal Bones.
      • With a Makeshift Shotgun you can craft a:
        • Pump Shotgun (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Mechanical Parts.
        • Primal Shotgun (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Animal Bones.
      • With a SMG you can craft a:
        • Submachine Gun (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Mechanical Parts.
        • Primal SMG (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Animal Bones.
      • With a Makeshift Bow you can craft a:
        • Mechanical Bow (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Mechanical Parts.
        • Primal Bow (Uncommon - Epic) with 4 Animal Bones.
      • With a Primal Bow you can craft a:
        • Epic Primal Flame Bow with a Gas Can or a Firefly Jar.
        • Epic Primal Stink Bow with a Stink Sac or 3 Stink Floppers
      • With a Mechanical Bow you can craft a:
        • Epic Mechanical Shockwave Bow with 2 Shockwave Grenades.
        • Epic Mechanical Explosive Bow with 6 Grenades.

Creative[]

Improved Barrier Device[]

The improved Barrier device is here and includes some updated artwork! Previously, the Barrier device was tied to the build grid, now it can be placed anywhere without needing to be anchored to prebuilt structures. The new Barrier device can be cut, copied, pasted and rotated, and is added to the quick bar rather than the trap inventory. Additionally, the Barrier provides improved scalability selections. If you need a small or large barrier, the scalability improvements will allow Creators to alter the size of the barrier before and after being placed.
