Save the World v37.40 Update Notes[]

Reddit

Howdy folks! v37.40 has landed with a batch of bug fixes (Mushrooms are back on the menu!). More details below:

Bug Fixes

Bug Fixes[]

  • Fixed an issue where Daily Quests couldn’t be pinned after replacing a previously pinned Quest.
  • Mushrooms can again be consumed, and the consumption animation plays as intended.
  • The progress bar now correctly appears while crafting items during Missions.
  • Collecting recycled materials from the B.A.S.E. now also spawns the same amount of materials for other players to pick up.
  • Removed the incorrect reference to Affliction damage from the Ceiling Gas Trap description.
  • The player map blip animation now plays correctly, making it easier to quickly locate your position during Missions.
  • Adjusted Grotto Dungeon dialogue volume so it can be clearly heard over ambient and combat sounds during gameplay.
  • Players can again view and interact with Edit Styles of equipped Commander Heroes in the Locker.
  • The Items Acquired screen no longer replays in the main Lobby after making a real-money purchase and leaving Save the World.
Known Issues

Known Issues[]

  • Leaving a Mission and returning to the main Fortnite Lobby may prevent the player from selecting other game modes.
  • Some sections of the mansion roof in the Survive the Night Mission are missing, causing invisible collisions that can block players.

LEGO® Fortnite v37.40 Update Notes[]

🔗 Source: Reddit
See Also: X

Hey everyone! Here’s the overview of all additions and bug fixes in today’s update:

Odyssey

Odyssey[]

Additions:[]

  • We know how much you love to build and show off your creations. Therefore, we’ve added generic building elements that you can use for your future artworks. You can also use a generic filter to filter out all these elements.
  • An offset was added to the Builder Tool to improve stud visibility and make prefab placement easier.

Bug fixes:[]

  • Fixed an issue where the build menu was resetting while trying to build or decorate.
  • Fixed an issue where players could get stuck in a cave upon rejoining the world if they were leaving the match while in a cave.
  • Damage that Single Daggers inflict has been increased.
  • Resolved an issue where players couldn’t move the camera when exiting the cave.
  • Fixed an issue where some players may have experienced fall damage when launching with Plasma Pistols.
  • Mitigated an issue where some explosives did not deal damage to Spires.
  • Resolved a minor audio issue when exiting the bus.
  • Resolved an issue where some options were unresponsive in the inventory after moving an item.
  • Resolved an issue in which the grid was not displaying when attempting to Nudge or Snap to the underside of an asset.
Brick Life

Brick Life[]

Additions:[]

  • The Recipes app on your phone has been replaced with the Collections App
  • Collections will track all food items you have crafted
  • Collections will track all the fish you have caught
  • Collections contain hints on finding the items you are looking for!
  • Editing decor has never been easier. You can preview how items will look before you finalize moving them.
  • We’ve added a few features to Fishing.
  • Now you can find different fish in different bodies of water. Many new fish species will be discoverable at varying times of day and weather.

Bug fixes:[]

  • Resolved an issue where some players were unable to use the edit mode while decorating.
  • Fixed an issue with the missing “Collect” button for mobile players.
  • Mitigated a few visual issues with ATMs and vehicles.
  • Fixed an issue with being unable to move or remove decor items when the inventory was full.
We’re Merging LEGO® Fortnite Expeditions with Odyssey by Bringing Hero Classes to Odyssey

We’re Merging LEGO® Fortnite Expeditions with Odyssey by Bringing Hero Classes to Odyssey[]

Posted October 3rd 2025

On November 1 we’re merging LEGO® Fortnite Expeditions with Odyssey by bringing Hero Classes to Odyssey. This merger will focus the team’s development efforts on Odyssey and Brick Life.

While you finish up your Missions, Expedition’s Hero Classes come early to Odyssey on October 9 in the form of Hero Weapons and Ability Equipment. Craft these to use in your world.

Also in Odyssey on October 9, hunger won’t slow you down anymore so you can focus on fighting bad guys and building Villages. Food items will offer powerful buffs. And if you need a Health boost, make potions at the new Potions Cauldron Station.

As a thank you for training in Expeditions, you’ll receive the Mask Maker Decor Bundle as a gift on November 1.

Putting the OG back in OG Season 6[]

🔗 Source: X

We’ve seen your feedback coming in towards the end of OG Season 5, and Season 6 fell a bit short of the classic Fortnite experience we all remember and have been excited to play again. Let’s talk about some things we’re doing:

These changes are now live in OG Season 6:

These changes are now live in OG Season 6:[]

🌪️ Moving End Storms[]

We’re restoring storm values closer to the original Chapter 1 Season 6 experience. That means moving zones and tighter circles in the end game. Rotations should feel similar to 2018 again.

🔁 Nerfed Shotgun Swapping (AKA Double Pumping)[]

We’ve removed the ability to swap between shotguns without a cooldown. We kept shotgun swaps around longer to enable fun Double Pump gameplay, and are returning shotgun fights to feel authentic to the OG experience.

🔫 OG Weapon Balancing[]

  • The Minigun has been unvaulted!
  • We’ve also adjusted Pump Shotguns and Hand Cannons to match the OG feel.
  • Chests were fixed yesterday and should no longer have a chance of missing weapons.
  • Pistol bullet spread was fixed in yesterday’s patch.

🍎 Forageables Rebalanced[]

Apples and Mushrooms now restore 5 HP/Shield instead of 10.

🟥 Havoc Stones Rebalanced[]

We’ve reduced how much damage to structures you deal while under the Havoc Stone effect, and these should feel more fair now. We’ve also reduced the Havoc effect duration from 45 seconds to 30 seconds.

✈ Quadcrashers Unvaulted Next Week[]

We’re bringing back everyone’s favorite unintentional flying vehicle in next week’s 37.50 update

Some of the bugs we’re squashing in future updates:

Some of the bugs we’re squashing in future updates:[]

🪲 Reducing the fog that’s reappeared on the Island after the recent change.
🪲 POI inaccuracies will be fixed.
🪲 Fix incorrect audio effects.
🪲 Correcting the OG S6 Umbrella’s Locker name and description.

We’ll continue listening and adjusting. And we have more unvaults coming ;)

Keep letting us know what feels true to OG.
