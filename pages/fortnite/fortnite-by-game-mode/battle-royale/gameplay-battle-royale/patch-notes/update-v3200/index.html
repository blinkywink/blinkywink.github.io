**Record Scratch**

Welcome to Chapter 2, the Remix. The Island you knew is back, remixed and reimagined, with weekly location and loot drops inspired by the best of early Chapter 2 gameplay and some of the greatest music icons in history. In this time rewind, Snoop D-O-double-G heads up the Agency, the rap god Eminem sets up shop in the Grotto, hip hop princess Ice Spice lays claim to Shark Island, and the remix hits the finale with a celebration and tribute to the legend and icon, Juice WRLD. Push play, turn up the volume, and open up the vault in Fortnite Battle Royale Chapter 2 Remix!

Snoop and Ice Spice dropped the mic in a surprise IRL concert just moments ago to kick off the Season. In case you missed the live broadcast, catch the full performance in-game at Risky Reels in the near future 🎤

This season is kicking off with a special, limited-time quest. Jump in once Chapter 2 Remix is live and earn 140K XP from any experience in Fortnite before November 11 at 12 AM ET to unlock the Midas-inspired Golden Scythe Pickaxe!

Drop In Like It’s Hot

Drop In Like It’s Hot[]

The Chapter 2 map is back! Different locales will enter the scene each week as the featured artists make their debuts:

Week 1 (Starts Nov 2) - Snoop Dogg & The Doggpound
The Agency compound is now The Doggpound, the new headquarters and home base for Snoop’s enterprise.

The Doggfather will gift you Snoop’s Drum Gun Mythic if you can defeat him, and will join you as an ally!

This OG’s also the Icon for Fortnite Festival Season 6! You’ll be able to unlock his LBC ‘93 Dogg Outfit by picking up the Music Pass, and you’ll find the Snoop Dogg Outfit in the Shop.

Also in Week 1 - Meowdas at The Yacht & TNTina at the Oil Rig

Meowdas is swole ‘n gold, and whatever he says goes. Pay a visit to The Yacht and take him out to get the Peow Peow Rifle! And Dynamo TNTina is up to no good at the oil rig, she’ll drop the Ka-Boom Bow if you can manage to eliminate her.

Week 2 (Starts Nov 7) - His Name is Slim Shady (at the Grotto)
Abracadabra! Guess who’s back, bruh? Eminem is reaching into his bag of tricks to make a restaurant appear, Mom’s Spaghetti, in the Grotto cave… an area known for its window shoppers and convenient location.

Showed up to Spaghetti Grotto without a reservation? If you can defeat Eminem, he’ll join your cause and bestow you with his Mythic RG Minigun — a lyrical minigun with a rapid-fire flow.

It’s only natural to be inspired after watching Slim in action. Pick up the new Rap Boy Reloaded Outfit when it hits the Shop in Week 2!

Week 3 (Starts Nov 14) - Ice Spice Headlines Shark Island
Grrah. Bow down, because Bronx baddie Ice Spice has dibs on Shark Island, now dubbed Ice Isle! You’ll love her plans for the place: Ice Isle will be a neon-lit, Y2K-dripped abandoned shopping mall with an ominous aura.

Defeat Ice to add her to your team and get Ice Spice’s Grappler and Ice Spice’s Rifle Mythics!

Coming for the crown each match? The Ice Spice Outfit and Rap Princess Ice Spice Outfit, both featuring Benny The Jeweler’s princess necklace, will be in the Shop starting in Week 3.

Week 4 (Starts Nov 21) - Legends Are Forever
It’s not a lucid dream. Draw your eye to the setting sun at the WRLD's Point landmark arriving in Week 4.

Also in Week 4 - Remix: The Finale

Chapter 2 Remix is ending on a special note — catch the Remix: The Finale live event on November 30 at 2 PM ET! To honor Juice’s legacy and love for Fortnite, all players who log in between 1 AM ET on November 30 and 1 AM ET on December 1 will receive the cel-shaded Slayer Juice WRLD Outfit*. Stay tuned for more details on celebrating Juice WRLD and Remix: The Finale, later this month.

*The Slayer Juice WRLD Outfit will be available for purchase in the Shop at a later date if you can’t make the event or log in during the applicable window.

Motorboats, Choppas, and the ‘66 Cadillac DeVille

Motorboats, Choppas, and the ‘66 Cadillac DeVille[]

Commandeer Chapter 2’s Motorboats starting now and take to the skies when Choppas come back later in the Season! You’ll find the iconic 1966 Cadillac DeVille around the map all season long — it has hydraulics, bounces to music, and can jump in the air!

Ride low and slow with the Snoop Dogg | Cadillac Bundle which includes the 1966 Snoop Cadillac DeVille Car Body, three Snoop-themed decals, and Snoop Dogg White Wall Wheels — available in the Shop all season long.

Introducing Kicks…

Introducing Kicks…[]

The Island isn’t the only thing to be remixed and reimagined. We’re bringing self-expression to a whole new level with the introduction of Kicks (a.k.a. shoes), a new type of cosmetic included as part of the Remix Pass and dropping in the shop on November 12.

The initial lineup of Kicks will feature Nike and Jordan brands alongside Fortnite originals. Look out for other brands to join the lineup in the future! Kicks will be compatible with over 500 of the most popular Fortnite Outfits to start, with more than 95% of Outfits compatible by Spring of next year. While there are a few Outfits that are particularly challenging to style Kicks for, we’re working hard to make sure that as many Outfits as possible can throw on a fresh pair.

Score your first pair of Jordans when they unlock in the Remix Pass on November 12 with v32.10. This is only the beginning for Kicks, more info will hit before they launch in the shop!

Fortnite Original Kicks are not included in the Remix Pass, the first pair will be available in the Shop starting November 12.

Level up the Remix Pass by earning XP across Fortnite Battle Royale, creator-made islands, LEGO® Fortnite, Rocket Racing, and Fortnite Festival. Here are the nostalgically new Outfits in the Pass:

Fortnite Reload Desert Drop: New Map & Solos Mode

Fortnite Reload Desert Drop: New Map & Solos Mode[]

Battle Royale isn’t the only mode to run it back. Fortnite Reload Desert Drop also arrives on November 2 with the all-new Oasis Map and Solo Ranked + Unranked Modes. Featuring new takes on familiar spots like Paradise Palms, Shifty Shafts, and Lazy Links, you can rift, reboot, and reload solo or with your favorite Duo or Squads starting the same day as Chapter 2 Remix.

Starting November 12 with v32.10 the Reload map will rotate every 30 minutes between Oasis and the original Reload Venture map! When you’re in the lobby queuing up, you’ll be able to see which map is currently live and how much time is left until it swaps.

Locker Improvements

Locker Improvements[]

Whether you prefer Battle Royale or another Fortnite experience, we’ve got big news! The v32.00 update (when Chapter 2 Remix goes live) includes improvements to the Locker. Take your fashion combo game to the next level and bundle up Character, Emote, Lobby, Music, Wraps, and Vehicle selections all into one Saved Loadout you can take into different Fortnite experiences.

With all these options to mix and match, it’s only fair you have a lotta loadouts — the number of available category presets and saved loadout will be 200 each! The Locker now displays more of your loadouts at a time, and scroll navigation has been implemented across the Locker to make customizing your look a breeze.


Heads up — randomize/shuffle for saved loadouts will be available soon.

Other Improvements & Fixes

Other Improvements & Fixes[]

  • Controller players can now set a scoped multiplier for aim sensitivity while aiming down sight with scoped weapons.
  • Fixed a bug where Left and Right Stick Dead Zones for PlayStation controllers were not registering when set below 12%.
  • Human-like Aim Assist has been enabled for hitscan weapons across all game modes.
  • Aim assist on controller has been adjusted to be somewhat stronger.

Content Creation - Chapter 2 Remix

Content Creation - Chapter 2 Remix[]

Each week Throughout Chapter 2 Remix, content creators in the Support-A-Creator (SAC) program will be able to monetize VODs of Chapter 2 Remix events, gameplay (including music from POIs, Weapons, Contrails, and Gliders - not including music from Emotes and Radio tracks), and trailer/clip content on YouTube for a seven-day period following their initial release. No takedown notifications should occur for this content during this time.

  • In order to monetize your Chapter 2 Remix content, you need to link your YouTube and Support-A-Creator accounts.
  • IMPORTANT: When uploading the content to YouTube, be sure to upload it as Unlisted or Public. If you upload the video as Private, it's possible you could receive a monetization claim on your video.
  • For uploading the content on other platforms, refer to the channel’s platform policies for tips on how to avoid copyright strikes.
  • To avoid hearing licensed audio during interactions with other players, make sure to set your "Licensed Audio" setting to "Mute Others" in the Creator Options under the Audio Settings in-game.

Although unlikely, if you do receive a claim on any SAC content, there may be a short delay in YouTube processing the release of the claim. During this time, please do not dispute the claim. Not in SAC? Don’t worry, you can still share your highlights from the experience. However, you will not be able to monetize this content on YouTube.

Please note: For Twitch creators streaming Chapter 2 Remix, we cannot prevent your VODs or clips from getting flagged by third-party copyright detection systems. The general recommendation is to disable VODs/clips when streaming the experience. For more information, see Twitch’s DMCA & Copyright FAQs help page.

It’s the Snoop D-O-Double G! With more than 35 million album sales worldwide, Snoop Dogg is one of the biggest rap and hip-hop icons in the world, and now he’s the Icon for Fortnite Festival Season 6.

It's bumpin' in here — Season 6 begins November 2 with v32.00 and comes to a close January 14, 2025, at 12 AM ET.

The Festival Pass Becomes the Music Pass

The Festival Pass Becomes the Music Pass[]

Look right at home and laidback with Snoop Dogg Instruments and the LBC '93 Dogg Outfit in the Music Pass. You can progress in the Music Pass with XP from any experience in Fortnite that grants XP, not just Festival. This means there are more ways to unlock Instruments, Auras, and more to take to the stage! Check out our Level Up Your Fortnite Passes at the Same Time blog post for more info. The Music Pass, which has a free reward track and a Premium Reward Track upgrade, is available for 1,400 V-Bucks.

Free Rewards
Add some bling to your fancy guitar spins. The Flux Silvera is the final reward of the free reward track, perfectly polished and built for an ever-changing style!

Let’s turn it up a notch: the Music Pass includes four new Jam Tracks!

  • Epic Games - “Spies (Marshmello Remix)”
  • Epic Games - “Magenta Benz”
  • Epic Games - “Larry’s Place”
  • Epic Games - “Turn Up”

If you already have the Lobby Track “Turn Up” in your Locker before Fortnite Festival Season 6, you’ll receive a one-time V-Bucks grant equivalent to the amount you paid for it, as well as the Jam Track version.

What’s a Dogg without his diamonds? Let ‘em see you shine with the Diamond Dazzle Aura and start fireworks onstage with the Grand Finale Aura. Surely not a fire hazard, right?

If you thought fireworks were flashy enough, teach the Dogg some new tricks with the Stand Surfer Emote. You don’t need a mic drop when you can ride the mic stand instead!

Premium Rewards
Pick up the Music Pass for 1,400 V-Bucks to access Snoop Dogg-themed Instruments and other unlockable rewards! You’ll automatically get the LBC '93 Dogg Outfit when you purchase the Music Pass, bringing the West Coast vibes to the Main Stage, Jam Stage, and Battle Stage! Of course, you can let the Dogg loose and use this Outfit in some other Fortnite experiences too!

Be the boss with the Masterpiece Mic, deliver a funk-infused flow with the G-Funk ‘93 Guitar, or lay down some paradiddles with the Top Dogg Drum Kit, the final Premium reward in the Pass.

As for the music, the Fortnite Festival stage crew is throwing it back with fresh beats from some of the biggest names in hip-hop. You can unlock “Young, Wild & Free” by Snoop Dogg, Wiz Khalifa, and Bruno Mars as a Jam Track near the end of the Music Pass.

Look out for these other Jam Tracks as Premium Rewards:

  • Soulja Boy Tell 'Em - “Crank That (Soulja Boy)”
  • Big Sean - “Bounce Back”
  • Juice WRLD - “Robbery”

Please note! 🎵 The Music Pass rewards (including the LBC ‘93 Dogg Outfit) are not exclusive to the Pass, as they may become purchasable in the Shop at a later date.

New Features & Improvements

New Features & Improvements[]

Along with Festival Pass becoming the Music Pass and progressing via XP earned from any activity in Fortnite, several other new features and improvements have been added this Season.

Beat Synced Emotes
Feel the flow of the music with new Beat Synced Emotes, which synchronize compatible Emote animations to the beat of Jam Tracks! Compatible emotes will be tagged as “Beat Synced” in your Locker and the Shop. Get grooving!

Take Your Favourite Jams to the Lobby!
Starting November 12, you’ll be able to jam with up to three friends in the Lobby using any Jam Tracks you own! This works in every Fortnite Lobby except Fall Guys and LEGO.

Main Stage and Battle Stage Improvements

  • Improved UI shows the song name and artist, your progress through the song, and where you have a break.

Battle Stage Improvements

  • Characters are now animated when they launch Attacks.
  • You can now vote for the Featured track you'd prefer to play.
  • You can now see a score percentage for individual instruments in each song, helping you to select the highest-scoring parts!

Other Improvements The following Jam Track song parts have been adjusted:

  • Quavo and Lenny Kravitz - Fly: Drums
  • Linkin Park - Numb: Lead/Pro Lead
  • Metallica - One: Pro Lead (Hard)
