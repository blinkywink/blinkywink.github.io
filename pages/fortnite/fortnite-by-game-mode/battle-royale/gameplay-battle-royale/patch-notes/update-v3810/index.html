Updates, Changes and Improvements

Updates, Changes and Improvements[]

Hunt the Titan out of Beta

The Beta version of the Hunt the Titan Mission and its Questline ended on November 11 @ 12 AM UTC (November 10 @ 7 PM ET). With today’s release, the Mission has officially joined the regular roster!

How are Questline rewards affected?[]

The Snowstrike Hero will arrive in the Event Store during the upcoming Frozen Fjords Season. The Primal Flame Bow will make its return in the Badlands Season, along with other Primal weapons for those who like their combat a little more… prehistoric.

Mission Improvements

Mission Improvements[]

We’ve made a few adjustments to the Resupply and Eliminate & Collect Missions. Details below!

Resupply[]

  • The Storm no longer closes in during the Mission, allowing for uninterrupted gathering.
  • The “Retrieve the Crates” objective and Bonus Objectives (including their Badges) have been removed to help you focus on general resource farming.
  • Badges now match the number of objects you gather/search (50 = Bronze, 100 = Silver, 200 = Gold, 300 = Platinum)

Eliminate and Collect[]

  • You’ll now need fewer enemy eliminations and Enemy Data Samples to complete the objectives, ensuring more consistent progression.
  • Enemies will now appear inside and around the Storm circle even when it’s at its smallest size, keeping the action steady throughout the Mission.
Bug Fixes

Bug Fixes[]

  • Husks and other enemies now consistently move down from ledges and similar elevated terrain.
  • Players can again remap their Jump keybind to another custom input without it always defaulting to the Space Bar.
  • Heroes’ Star Levels now display correctly when loading into the Lobby.
  • Ever see your Sidekick’s name randomly appear on world objects? Fixed now!
  • Storm Shield Defense / Endurance Amplifiers again have their intended collision.
  • Storm King’s Onslaught projectiles now correctly hit detached Shielders.
  • Fixed an error with the Ancient’s Launcher’s higher-level ability being unlocked even if the weapon hadn’t reached the required level.
Known Issues

Known Issues[]

  • Ninja Heroes cannot use the Mantis Leap ability consistently.
  • Soldiers’ Shockwave ability may be locked out if interrupted by player-built ramps, roofs or sloped terrain.
  • Bullets from some Assault Rifles and SMGs may appear to fly in the wrong direction. This is visual only - shots still hit where you’re aiming.
  • Constructors' Plasma Pulse grenades may not deploy or damage enemies after the player fires a Bow. Using other weapon types (Melee included) doesn't break the ability's functionality.
  • The Hero Commander Buckle+ Perk currently provides reduced effective damage as weapon level increases.
  • The player character may sometimes use an incorrect arm-sweep animation when interacting with world objects.
  • Some sections of the mansion roof in the Survive the Night Mission are missing, causing invisible collisions that can block players.

LEGO® Fortnite v38.10 Update Notes[]

Hey everyone! The 38.10 update is live. Here’s the list of all new additions and bug fixes included in this update.

Odyssey

Odyssey[]

➕ Additions:[]

  • Buckle up - huge vehicle improvements incoming! 🚗
  • We’ve fixed an issue where vehicles could take off without you, so there will be no more joyrides without the driver.
  • Vehicle stability has also been improved, reducing those moments when cars mysteriously fall underground.
  • We’re continuing to work on stability and server performance to make every ride smoother — more fixes are on the way! 🛠️💨
  • Who says there’s such a thing as too much movement? 🌀 Players can now mount, vault, and slide their way through the action!
  • We’ve added another bar slot, allowing you to carry more items than before.
  • We’ve boosted your stamina! 💪 It no longer drains while you’re swimming or gliding through the air.
  • We’ve also made a few minor improvements to enhance your overall experience:
  • Potion glow-up alert! 🧪 We’ve refreshed potion bottles so they’re easier to tell apart — and they’ll look extra stylish on your shelf now!
  • We’ve made small improvements to the HUD Layout and Hero Mastery Tab.

⚒️Bug fixes:[]

  • Good news! 🎉 We’ve fixed an issue where some weapons weren’t firing when equipped by NPCs on LEGO Fortnite Islands — everything’s back in action! 🔫
  • Improved certain lag instances happening when another player joins the first player’s world.
  • We’ll continue to refine and improve this to ensure your experience is the best it can be.
  • A fix has been deployed to address the issue with the Flying Fish Sandwich. This update resolves the problem preventing players from performing jump-related actions, restoring normal functionality.
  • Resolved an issue where players couldn’t interact with the Rift Shrine.
  • Horde minifigs are now correctly spawning on Lost Isles.
  • Fixed an issue where players would fall through the ground and die if they were teleporting to another player.
  • Addressed an animation issue with teleportation.
  • Mitigated an issue where the Thunderclap Blade’s damage failed to increase tenfold.
  • When consumed, the Robust Pumpkin Pie provides the noted health benefits.
  • Resolved an issue where players could experience different temperatures when flushing back into the hot biomes.
  • Fixed an issue where intentionally dropped items would be automatically picked up after the players were respawned.
  • Resolved an issue where the different Hunter accessories were dealing the same damage.
  • Fixed some discrepancies with the quests “Collect 100 Storm Eyes” and “Collect 100 Rift Crystals”.
  • Adjusted the damage amount of the Rifted Plasma Pistols.
  • Fixed an issue where the player could take multiple damage instances when using the Warp Wrangler or Warp Rider.
  • Fixed small hitches while in the Storm King battle.
  • Resolved an issue where Daigo’s Road Patrol can destroy villages.
  • Fixed an issue where the Spire Wrath IV Rune would be triggered when out of stamina.
  • Glowy Skeletons now drop cursed bones, not regular ones.
  • Fixed an issue with the completion of the quest “Pop five storm buds”.
  • Fixed an animation issue of the NPC when it became stuck after the player became DBNO.
  • Rewards are now being granted accordingly when players upgrade their village square.
  • Fixed an issue where the Battle Bus was not usable for an extended time after being used for Fast Travel.
  • Shadow Caller and Hunter’s abilities are now properly charging.
Brick Life

Brick Life[]

➕ Additions:[]

  • Jump into the four new Festival Job Quests and grab another set of awesome rewards:
  • Simpsons Kids Room's Decor Bundle 🛋️
  • Simpsons Bathroom Decor Bundle 🚽
  • Simpsons Misc Decor Bundle 🎍
  • Something’s changed… The lake is now glowing with eerie green water. ☢️
  • Search the shores and uncover the hidden nuclear rods found nearby.
  • You might discover something new swimming in the waters…

⚒️Bug fixes:[]

  • Mask Maker props are now available.
  • Resolved specific crashing issues for PlayStation 4 players.
  • Resolved a visual issue where the banana peels were displayed even before being placed.
  • Fixed an issue where Festival Tickets would appear in a shop app.
  • Mitigated an issue with the Melon Soda recipe.
  • Fixed an issue where one player would experience a black screen while another player's controller would be disconnected.
  • Custom keybinds are now working properly.
  • Resolved an issue where the total amount of creds was displayed instead of the gained amount.
