Overview

Update v14.00 of Fortnite released on August 27th, 2020 and brought a season of Marvel - Chapter 2: Season 4.


Battle Royale

Battle Royale[]

Highlights[]

  • A brand new Battle Pass with a Marvel Comics Theme, under the slogan "Nexus War".
    • This brings Thor, She-Hulk, Groot, Storm, Doctor Doom, Mystique and Iron Man.
    • It also brings Wolverine, secretly available through the Wolverine Challenges, gradually releasing throughout the Season!
      • Level up your Battle Pass further to unlock the true hero/villain within them.
  • There's new items and Weapons including Superpowers!

Gameplay[]

  • New Legacy Achievements have been added
  • New Punch Cards have been added.
  • Quinjets spawn in at the start of matches and make 2-3 Quinjet Patrol Sites in a possibility of many locations.


Map Changes[]

  • Pleasant Park has been taken over by Doctor Doom and his henchmen! It is now Doom's Domain.
  • A Boogie Boat landmark has been added.
  • Jennifer Walters now has an office in Retail Row.
  • A Sentinel Graveyard has appeared south of The Authority.
  • Helicarrier 64 is now the new Spawn Island.
