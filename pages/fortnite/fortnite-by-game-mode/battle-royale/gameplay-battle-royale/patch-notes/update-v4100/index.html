v41.00 Update Notes: Grab A Sprite. Get Out Alive. Fortnite Runners Is Here[]

June 6th 2026
🔗 Source: Communities

The Zero Point cracked the sky wide open, scrambling and reassembling the Island into the Shattered Coast: a fresh map dropping mid-chapter with twisted takes on familiar POIs, a totally fresh pool of firearms, and a whole new way to win. This Season it’s all about getting in, grabbing power-packing Sprites, and doing whatever it takes to extract them for the next match. Welcome to Fortnite: Runners.

THE NEW ISLAND

THE NEW ISLAND[]

On the new Island you'll wade through the crystalline shallows of Sunken Shores, fight your way up the Shattered Coast, and feel the Zero Point's pull at the center of it all.

SPRITES: EXTRACT IT OR LOSE IT

SPRITES: EXTRACT IT OR LOSE IT[]

In this new reality Sprites are diverse and coveted helpers, with each granting a special Sprite Power ability when equipped. Find a Sprite in a match, get to an Extraction Site, defend your pick up while the timer runs, and you’ll bank it to your Collection for use in future matches. Get eliminated before you bank your Sprite and it’ll be left on the battlefield for the next player to steal.

Be quick to grab these if you find them:

Rare

  • Earth Sprite — You have a chance to find additional rare items when opening chests
  • Fire Sprite — Creates a fiery burst when you deal enough damage to an enemy
  • Water Sprite — Replenish shield while in water for you and your nearby Squad

Epic

  • Duck Sprite — Emoting or Jamming replenishes shields
  • Ghost Sprite — Grants cloak for a duration upon reloading
  • Demon Sprite — Siphon some health and shields when you eliminate an opponent
  • King Sprite — Your Pickaxe deals more damage

Legendary

  • Dream Sprite — Grants a random item at each level, exploding with legendary loot at Max Level
  • Punk Sprite — Possibly nothing... or infinitely something

Mythic

  • Zero Point Sprite — Spawn a Shield Bubble Jr. when you use a healing item on yourself

Successful Extractions also earn you Sprite Dust, the currency that powers your Collection in future matches. Spend Sprite Dust at the start of any match to summon a Sprite you've already Extracted, equipping its Sprite Power before you drop from the bus. The rarer the Sprite, the more Dust it costs to summon. Sprite Dust can also be spent to upgrade your Sprite’s powers and loot.

There are three key ways in which Sprites level up; exploration (finding and opening Chests), eliminations (downing and eliminating opponents) and by extraction (extracting Sprites). Getting a Sprite to max level is no easy feat, so we recommend attempting it across multiple runs, and if you extract a max-level Sprite - you will Master it! It is this Mastery that will unlock progression rewards as you play through the season.

Keep your eyes peeled for rare Special variants of Sprites with a (small) bonus effect available throughout the Season from daily drops to Power Hours and more!

Looking to show off your favorite Sprite? Starting next update, when you level up and master a Sprite it can be put in your Sprite Pod - including the special variants! As more are added each update, you can master and display them in your Spirte Pod Back Bling in the following update and the style is forever unlocked.

NEW EXTRACTION GIZMOS

NEW EXTRACTION GIZMOS[]

There's no wrong way to extract a Sprite. New Gizmo consumable items will be available all season that give you quick ways to shortcut and supercharge your Sprite extractions. Play it safe, or create a high-risk, high-reward hotspot.

These new Gizmos, like the Golden Fireworks that debuted on Community Day, will start in your Pickaxe slot when you jump from the bus. Your Gizmo inventory will persist all season-long, but will expire when the season ends, though, so be sure to use 'em!

  • Portable Extractor: Extract your equipped Sprite from anywhere on the island. Perfect for that rare Sprite you've been searching for!
  • Lucky Locator (coming later this Season): Tracks down a buried Chest that’s guaranteed to have a Sprite you haven't collected.
  • Extraction Site Booster (coming later this Season): Call in a special Extraction Crate that will boost any Sprite extracted at the site. This will get other players' attention!

Gizmos can’t be traded and dropped when eliminated, so no risk of losing one. You can earn more Gizmos by completing Bonus Goals quests, trading in Sprite Dust, and completing your Sprite Mastery progression!

LOCKED, LOADED, AND SLIDING

LOCKED, LOADED, AND SLIDING[]

Four new weapons drop with v41.00, with more to come later in the Season:

  • Extending Focus Shotgun: long range, barrel lengthens as spread tightens across a 3-round volley
  • Surgical Burst Rifle: burst fire, low recoil, minimal damage falloff
  • Chaos Exploder Rifle: the Dark Voyager's heavy AR, firing explosive cubes, especially nasty when playing around cover
  • Lancehead Pistol: John Wick's sidearm, 21-round mag. Throw your magazine at nearby enemies when reloading for additional damage!

Later in the Season look out for the Bank Shot Pistol that bounce shots off surfaces to hit your target with a ricochet. Trickshot!

Joining them are two new movement items: Seven Sliders & Shock Rocks.

Slide faster than your legs can carry you with Seven Sliders, ADS with your equipped weapon mid-slide to slow time for a clean shot, and let the jets launch you skyward. Shock Rocks arrive later this Season. Crush one to perform three midair leaps.

Returning to the loot pool: Striker Pump, Maven Auto Shotgun, Ranger Pistol, Stinger SMG, Hunting Rifle, and Chaos Reloader Shotgun.

LATER THIS SEASON: BUS EXFIL

LATER THIS SEASON: BUS EXFIL[]

Later this Season, matches may end differently. There will be a small percentage chance that in some matches, the Battle Bus will rift back into the map, and you'll have a window to board it. Climb on, grapple up, get at least one teammate to the rift out point, and your team secures the Victory. Multiple teams can win this way per match!

NEW RANKED SEASON + REWARDS

NEW RANKED SEASON + REWARDS[]

Ranks have been reset and new Ranked rewards are waiting to be unlocked in Battle Royale:

  • Gold: Cube's Creche Back Bling
  • Platinum: Voyager's Suit Wrap
  • Elite: Hatchets of Chaos Pickaxe
  • Unreal: Axe of Unreal Pickaxe

This Season introduces the new Unreal Legends rank, for the true masters of Fortnite who reach Unreal, hold at least 2,500+ Skill Rating, and place in the Top 500 on the Unreal Leaderboard at the same time. You can read about the rest of our competitive and ranked changes this season here.

QUALITY OF LIFE IMPROVEMENTS

QUALITY OF LIFE IMPROVEMENTS[]

  • Festival Auras Now Trigger on Victory Royale: We’ve heard your calls for Auras! Your equipped Fortnite Festival Aura now plays during your Victory Royale moment in Battle Royale.
  • Distance-based transparency: Audio Visualizer icons for gunshots and footsteps now fade based on distance. Close threats stay fully opaque, distant ones go transparent.
  • Reduced audio visualizer range for footsteps: Footsteps now surface on the indicator at a shorter range than gunshots, reflecting that footsteps are a close-range cue.
  • Ground Rush Goes Global: Ground Rush, the skydiving mechanic introduced last Season for Zero Build, is now active across Build and Zero Build.
  • Zero Build Ground Rush Glider Redeploy: Ground Rush now activates from Glider Redeploy states, not just initial drops or launch pads. Thanks for the Zero Build feedback everyone!
  • Battle Bus ADS: During the Battle Bus ride into a match, you can now aim down sights (ADS) to get a better view of the map. Also makes pinging and marking your drop site easier.
  • New Controller Setting - Tweak Zone: We’ve made a new way to tweak your aim with those tiny stick movements that are normally ignored in a controller’s Dead Zone. When enabled, these movements will move your crosshair directly instead of turning the camera. Perfect for lining up a long shot or turning a body shot into a headshot. Find Tweak Zone Aiming in Controller settings and let us know what you think!
  • Updated Storm Times: We've made storm circle timing changes to better pace matches and prevent slow endgames. These timing changes are not in Competitive or Ranked.
    • This change is intended to reduce situations where the number of players remaining in final storm phases is disproportionate to the available play area. As part of this, we're making adjustments to improve match pacing and help games reach a conclusion at a reasonable rate. These changes are designed to achieve that without making the storm feel overly aggressive, while still preserving adequate time during the early and mid game for looting, exploration, and engagement opportunities. Tell us how it feels!

Save the World v41.00 Update Notes[]

🔗 Source: Communities

Happy weekend everyone! We’ve got a new round of Save the World updates, including the long-awaited Play With Others in Ventures, custom weapons for Defenders and a bunch of fixes and improvements.

Lend a hand in Ventures, equip your Defenders with weapon Schematics and share your feedback with us!

Play With Others Comes to Ventures!

Play With Others Comes to Ventures![]

Ventures now includes a Play With Others option, letting you queue into Missions with other players and help them complete their objectives. To try it out, head to the World Map, select the Ventures pin and you’ll find it in the currently active Venture zone.

Custom Weapons for Your Defenders

Custom Weapons for Your Defenders[]

You can now equip Defenders with class-appropriate weapon Schematics, giving them custom weapons to use in Missions. If no Schematic is equipped, Defenders will use their class-specific default weapon.

Weapons & Perks[]

Defenders & Wildlife[]

Audio, Environment & UI[]

General[]

  • The Dark Cubic Sneakers' preview may not load on the Passes screen, and the Kicks aren't being granted properly to eligible Battle Pass owners
  • The Health and Shield bar may incorrectly show 82 points even when full
    • Fixed in 41.10
  • Helsie's hair may move unnaturally on DirectX 12 at 60 FPS or higher framerate
    • Fixed in 41.10

PC[]

  • Some players on Windows versions earlier than 25H2 (Build 26200) may not see DLSS options after updating to the latest NVIDIA drivers
    • We're investigating and will update this list as soon as DLSS options are available again

Mobile[]

  • Using the "Tap Anywhere" firing mode may be unresponsive on touch input
    • Temporary Workaround: Use the dedicated Shoot HUD button in the meantime while we work on a fix

Battle Royale & Zero Build[]

  • [Ranked/Competitive] Some chests may appear already opened or drop no loot
  • Fire Sprites may not trigger consistently if your target is airborne or hit by splash damage
  • Fixed in 41.10
  • Your character’s upper body may not follow the camera while aiming or shooting on Wonkeeland Grind Rails
  • Fixed in 41.10
  • In Ranked matches, the stamina bar may remain white instead of turning orange when the Slap effect is active
  • Chaos Exploder Rifle shots may be blocked by an invisible barrier in some Particle Accelerator areas
  • Fixed in 41.10
  • We've disabled the Skydive Leader split screen feature to fix a bug that prevented some players from deploying their Glider after exiting the Battle Bus
    • Skydive Leader will be re-enabled in the v41.10 update

Save the World[]

  • We’re investigating inconsistent weapon damage output, particularly during Mythic Storm King fights
  • [Simple Edit] UI does not update to reflect the reset after editing a structure
  • [Xbox One] Directional Jump Pads won’t activate when Phase Shifting onto them
  • The Hero Commander Buckle+ Perk currently provides reduced effective damage as weapon level increases

LEGO Fortnite[]

  • The health bar will be adjusted to remain visible at all times during combat, even when the player is at full health
  • Exiting a vehicle now requires holding the exit button instead of a single press. Input bugs related to exiting vehicles will be resolved in a future update with a move back to a single press
  • In a future update, Zeppelin Balloons and Hot Air Balloons will be fully customizable per channel using the wrench tool. For now, they'll default to channels 5 and 6 until that update rolls out
  • Sometimes Hover Bricks get confused, and your vehicle might appear to drift slowly upward into the sky - this is visual-only
    • Walk over to where your hovercraft was before it started floating off, hop on, and the position will snap right back into place

Fortnite Festival[]

  • The green fret on the guitar peripherals may stop responding during Local Multiplayer
  • Scrolling the Music Library too fast may cause a delay in loading earned Stars
  • Yellow and blue pads may not be properly detected on Xbox MadCatz drumkits
  • Gold Band Star visual effects may overlap other UI elements
  • Auto-Emote setting may not always apply properly
  • Pro Vocals note tubes may appear distorted on sharp pitch changes
  • Pro Drums Overdrive gems may cause lower-than-intended scores if mistimed against other gems
  • Extra inputs may register when playing Pro Drums with Double Kick enabled if more than one pedal is unpressed at the same time
