Overview

Online Test 6.5 was a build released as part of the online tests that ran from January 26, 2016 to March 1st 2016.

Additions and changes

Additions and changes[]

  • Introduced Penny alongside the rest of the female constructor class.
  • Introduced the Deliver The Goods mission which then became Deliver the Bomb in later builds.
  • Added the ability to change difficulty when starting objectives.
  • Changed the difficulty on the Stonewood Outpost amplifier defense difficulty on the 3rd, 4th and 5th amplifiers.
  • Retrieve the Data now fails if the balloon is destroyed regardless of how much data has been downloaded
  • Reduce Melee Weapon durability by 27.78% whilst still keeping them cheap to craft.
  • Balance changes to elemental enemies.
    • One element per encounter/outpost wave
    • Damage resistance reduced.
      • Elemental Husk vs Normal Weapons: 75% Damage Resist -> 50% Damage Resist
      • Elemental Husk vs Energy Weapons: 50% Damage Resist -> 33% Damage Resist
      • Elemental Husk vs Bad Element: 90% Damage Resist -> 75% Damage Resist
      • Elemental Husk vs Good Element: 0% Damage Resist (Unchanged)
      • Elemental Husk vs Same Element: 50% Damage Resist -> 33% Damage Resist
  • Gadget and abilities loadouts are now kept across all zones.
  • Selecting the Outpost from the Mission Select screen now shows the current level of each Outpost structure and their upgrade cost.
  • Command Center upgrades 2, 3 and 4 now give +5 max level to all schematics and survivors. All other sources of max level increases have been removed.
  • Outpost upgrade information can now be viewed from the mission select screen.
    • Selecting the Outpost from the Mission Select screen now shows the current level of each Outpost structure and their upgrade cost.
  • Other bug fixes and improvments. [1]

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • As of April 2024, this remains the only online testing build to have been found.
  • Within the game files, dataminers have found leftovers of the earliest builds of Fortnite dating back to 2011 and 2012. Whether these files still exist up to at least Season 3 is unknown.
