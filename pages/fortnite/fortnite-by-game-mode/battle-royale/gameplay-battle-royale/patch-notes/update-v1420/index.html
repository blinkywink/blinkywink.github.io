What's New In Battle Royale?

What's New In Battle Royale?[]

Gameplay[]

  • Added Wolverine A.I. to Weeping Woods.
    • Drops Mythic Wolverine's Claws.
  • Added Superstore Landmark West of Holly Hedges in preparation of Fortnitemares 2020.
  • Vaulted Uncommon and Rare Scoped Assault Rifles.
  • Added the Midas Flopper.
  • Unvaulted Birthday Presents with the new loot pool

Bug Fixes[]

  • Heroes Park and Ghost House don’t count as discovered locations after discovering them.
  • Fishing spots can disappear on PC with effects set to low.
  • Inconsistent fishing spot visibility on Nintendo Switch and Android.
  • All Outfits that have the old SHADOW logo have their logo updated.

THIS WEEK - THE DOOR TO DARKNESS RETURNS!

Starting Friday, September 25 at 8PM, make a reservation for terror! Join the Homebase crew for a nightmarish stay at legendary Oakdirge Lodge, Stoneplankentwine’s third most haunted hotel to unlock Willow, the ghoulish Outlander!

WILLOW

"I want your soul... and your candy!" - Willow

  • Standard Perk: Ghoulish Cackle
    • Increase Ability damage up to 37.5% based on the percentage of your missing health.
  • Commander Perk: Ghoulish Cackle +
    • Increase Ability damage up to 37.5% based on the percentage of your missing health. In addition, ability eliminations have a 70% chance to conjure a Phantasm which seeks out nearby foes and explodes, dealing 62 base energy damage in a small radius.

Available from the Door to Darkness questline!

HUSK GRINDER

Sharpen up your Black Metal weapon set with the darkest metal axe around, the Husk Grinder.

Available on September 25 at 8PM ET in the Event Store!

DIRGE SONG

Sing the Husks a song! Firing in a 9-round burst, this Black Metal SMG packs a powerful punch.

Available on September 25 at 8PM ET in the Event Store!

QUIET PLEASE. I AM FIGHTING.

WEEK 3 - THE GRAVE DIGGER IS BACK!

This popular assault rifle returns! Gravedigger fires medium ammo, gaining damage and impact at the cost of accuracy and fire rate.

Available on October 9 at 8PM ET in the Event Store!
