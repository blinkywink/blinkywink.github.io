Fortnite Balance Adjustments and Bug Fixes[]

Posted January 30th 2026
🔗 Source: X
See Also: Communities

Hey everyone, we've been listening to your feedback and working hard to squash bugs and re-balance the loot pool. Here's what we've already fixed, what's coming in v39.40 next week, and what we're still investigating:

Upcoming Battle Royale Balance Adjustments in v39.40

Upcoming Battle Royale Balance Adjustments in v39.40[]

Several weapons will be powered up with new adjustments in the next game update:

🌀 Crash Pad Jr. will be vaulted and Shockwave Grenades will be unvaulted in Unranked Battle Royale in v39.40. We’ll keep evolving the movement meta with fun, healthy, high-energy options that open up exciting plays and clutch moments. And yeah… we’ve got some very cool mobility ideas cooking that we can’t wait to tell you about in a future game update 👀🔥

🔫 Submachine Guns will receive significant buffs across the board. For a while now, Submachine Guns blurred the lines with Assault Rifles in typical player loadouts. With this update, we expect SMGs to perform faster, snappier, and a viable up close option with fun run-and-gun capabilities.

💪 Holo Rush SMG will deal more damage per shot, have a larger magazine, and handle faster; making it snappier to pull out and aim.

💪 Flex SMG will deal more damage, have a larger magazine, and handle faster to match other SMGs in the category.

💪 Dual Micro SMGs will deal more damage and have a larger magazine with slightly faster handling.

We’re continuing to tune and evolve SMG identity in the loot pool, so keep the feedback comin’!

🎯 Tactical AR and Enforcer AR will both deal more damage. We're continuing to close the gap between the Deadeye AR and other weapons to give you more meaningful choices in your loadout.

🔫 Tactical Pistol will deal more damage, retain damage better at range, and aim down sights faster; giving it a fighting chance against the competition.

🩹 Sovereign Shotgun will fire slightly faster while aiming down sights for a smoother feel.

🫥 Stealth Splash will last significantly longer and you'll be able to carry more of them (Lock On Rifles?! 😉).

🚗 All-Terrain-Kart health will be increased to make it more durable across all game modes.

Fixed Bugs Live Now

Fixed Bugs Live Now[]

  • We’ve pushed a hotfix to significantly improve crashes.. For PC, the DirectX 11 rendering mode may still see some instability, and we’re temporarily disabling this setting for older GPUs in the next update. We recommend PC players switch to DirectX 12.
  • Fixed a Deadeye Assault Rifle bug that allowed players to fire early when equipping the weapon.
  • A blank Knocked! pop-up no longer incorrectly appears when in the Storm.
  • Older versions of the HUD/UI no longer persist in Battle Royale after playing a Creative experience.
  • Materials now correctly drop on elimination in Ranked Reload.
  • Ballistic Ranked Quests can now be properly progressed and completed.
More Bug Fixes In v39.40 (alongside new content)

More Bug Fixes In v39.40 (alongside new content)[]

  • Mantling and Hurdling sound cues will be returned to regular volume levels.
  • Audio of weapons firing will correctly play in BR after playing a Creative/UEFN match.
  • Fixed an issue where recoil on some weapons will no longer incorrectly reset your reticle back to the original firing point, causing aim problems.
  • Fixed several Emote, Locker and audiovisual issues that will no longer occur after playing Crown Jam.
  • Fixes to lag when navigating the lobby, locker, and emote wheel. We’re also adding a new Favorites tab to the emote wheel, so you can have quick access to more smooth moves.
  • Vehicle buttons will now be present when an old HUD layout is used on mobile devices.
Issues Being Investigated

Issues Being Investigated[]

  • We've heard your feedback that latency has been feeling higher than normal. This one's proving complicated to pin down, but we're actively investigating and will have more info to share soon!
  • Reload players may suddenly be unable to build or shoot Morphite weapons even when there are still bullets in the magazine, and we’re investigating this for a fix.
  • We've seen community posts showing inconsistencies with Hurdle & Mantle, interaction prompts not appearing when expected, or inputs not registering near windows and objects. We want this to feel smoother and more reliable.
  • We’re looking at tournament players that may have experienced issues loading into tournament matches, as well as a UI scoring placement bug that we’re fixing in a future game update.
  • The Storm Icon next to the Minimap doesn’t communicate whether the Storm is shrinking or holding still. We’re fixing this in a future game update.
  • We’re aware that interactions may become unusable after editing default binds, especially on controller inputs, and we’re working on a fix for this.
Thanks for helping report these issues both out-of-game and in-game so we can fix them - keep it coming. Expect some lovely and legendary updates coming in v39.40 that we’ll share more about soon!

Crown Jam: A Limited-Time Fortnite Mode from the Fall Guys World[]

Posted January 21st 2026
🔗 Source: Blog Post

Get ready to fall for a new 3v3 arcade sport when Fall Guys Crown Jam hits Fortnite. From January 23 to February 9, this isn’t the Fall Guys in Fortnite you’re used to — it’s all about getting slam dunks on the other team’s side of the arena. It’s basketball… meets football… meets obstacle course chaos.

When you queue into Crown Jam, you’ll be put in one of six Crown Jam clubs each time. For Crown Jam up-and-comers, there’ll be a Ranked option so you can earn rewards for your Locker like the Less Fallin’ More Ballin’ Jam Track and Crown Ball Back Bling!

Finish five matches of Crown Jam to unlock the Blue Plushie Bean Back Bling. Earn this Back Bling and the Ranked rewards to show off in other modes.

The Beans in Crown Jam are powered by real physics, so you can feel every jump, tackle, and slam dunk! For more Fall Guys in Fortnite, check out Fall Guys islands where you can play as a Bean Style of many Fortnite Outfits.

Less Fallin’ More Ballin’...The Crown Jam LTM is LIVE 🏀🔥[]

🔗 Source: X | Communities

Fall for a new 3v3 arcade sport when Fall Guys Crown Jam hits Fortnite.

From January 23 to February 9, jump into a whole new experience where the Beans battle for the crown amidst chaos.

Dunk, shoot, tackle and pass your way to victory inside the Crown Jam Arena, full of bouncers, ramps and chances to use new Hype Abilities (more on that later).

Jump into Unranked for a laid-back hoop session or crank it up with Ranked for more competitive play and ballin’ rewards. Unlock rewards and flex them in any other mode in Fortnite.

How To Crown Jam

You’ll be assigned one of six Crown Jam clubs, each bringing its own flavor and splash of color to the arena:

  • Dunktown Dinos
  • Plunderdome Pirates
  • Bean City Birds
  • Fairycorn Stampede
  • Fallhalla Longboats
  • Windy Wood Howlers
HYPE ABILITIES

HYPE ABILITIES[]

What is Hype?

Hype is the energy generated by the crowd! Each successful and daring action you perform: Passing, Shooting, Tackling and Stealing and scoring Goals, hypes up the crowd and in turn builds up your Hype Meter.

Hype Abilities are show-stopping special moves that can turn the tides in a match. When you’re glowing with energy, wait for the right moment to trigger your selected ability.

Before the match kicks off, make your first big call - pick one of three Hype Abilities to shape how you play and give your team the edge:

  • Big Bean - Transform temporarily into an unstoppable giant bean.
  • Bounce Pad - Throw a trampoline to foil your rivals’ plans, or use it as an escape route.
  • Confetti Crash - Target in sight? Blast a confetti projectile at the opposing team to ragdoll them.

Movement in Crown Jam gets an upgrade too 👀 Use ramps to build ramps boost your movement, hop on bouncers to make massive dunks - and choose the right path to maneuver and outplay your opponents when taking it to the hoop!

Knock out every quest, dunk your way to glory, and enjoy the chaos in the Arena before February 9th! 👑

Save the World v39.30 Update Notes[]

🔗 Source: Reddit | Communities

Welcome back to Flannel Falls! The Venture Season returns, along with Dungeons, Lunar Llamas, restocked Event Store and more.

Updates, Changes and Improvements

Updates, Changes and Improvements[]

Flannel Falls Ventures Season[]

Spring into the Ventures zone and take on the Escalation Modifier! Every 30 eliminations, you’ll trigger an elemental burst, damaging nearby enemies and granting a stacking 10% damage boost (capping at 100%).

Elimination Streak Effects[]

🍃 30 Eliminations - Nature Burst (damages and stuns nearby enemies)
🔥 60 Eliminations - Fire Burst (applies a damage-over-time effect)
🪩 90 Eliminations - Dance Party (forces Husks to dance and refills your Energy)
❄️ 120 Eliminations - Ice Burst (freezes nearby enemies)
⚡ 150 Eliminations - Energy Burst (nearby enemies take increased damage)

Heads up: Losing your streak resets the above bonuses, so be sure to stay on the move and see how far you can go!

The Flannel Falls Venture Season runs from January 22 (12:00 AM UTC) to April 1, 2026.

Dungeons[]

The Dungeons have reopened! Fight through monsters and avoid nasty traps as you descend into the depths. Your inventory is limited and crafting is disabled, so don’t forget to search for treasure lying around - it might just give you the edge to slay the Boss at the end!

Complete Dungeons and related Quests to unlock a whole bunch of rewards, such as:

  • Heroes: Mermonster Ken (Ninja), Swamp Knight (Soldier), Chaos Agent (Soldier)
  • Cosmetics: Spray, Banners, Loading Screen, Lobby Music

Lunar Llama[]

Lunar Llama in, Holiday Llama out! With Frozen Fjords behind us, Lunar Llamas have stepped in to replace Holiday Llamas.

The Lunar Llama is packed with seven Dragon Weapons (both ranged and melee) and a roster of eight Lunar New Year Heroes, available in the Llama Shop for 500 Lunar Tickets.

Event Store Arrivals[]

Wukong makes his return to the Event Store! This Mythic Soldier Hero specializes in chaining ranged eliminations into explosive bursts, dealing damage to nearby enemies.

The Dragon's Fist is a melee weapon that trades speed for raw power, dealing fire damage and knocking enemies back with dragon-fueled force.

Blockbuster[]

The Blockbuster Questline returns for the entire Ventures Season! Completing all four parts will net you:

  • Mythic Heroes: Carbide (Soldier) and The Cloaked Star (Ninja)
  • Choice of Legendary Military Weapons: Crossbow, Silenced Specter, Bald Eagle
  • Choice of Legendary Shadow Ops Heroes: Saboteur Bull (Constructor), Ambush Buzz (Outlander), Deadly Lotus Luna (Ninja), Undercover Vaughn (Soldier)
  • Lunar Tickets and Gold
Bug Fixes

Bug Fixes[]

  • Fixed a matchmaking issue where some players couldn't load into Missions following today's release.
  • Players can now join Ventures Missions with the Fill option enabled.
  • Soldiers’ Shockwave ability should no longer be locked out for the remainder of the Mission if interrupted by player-built ramps, roofs or sloped terrain.
  • [Xbox One] Mitigated stuttering (i.e. rubberbanding) while sprinting.
  • [Xbox One] Fixed a lighting issue on the Command and Mission Launch screens.
  • Placing R.O.S.I.E. next to Traps no longer destroys them.
  • [Xbox/PlayStation] Fixed swapped Simple Build button inputs.
Known Issues

Known Issues[]

  • Players are currently unable to purchase multiple Armory Slots in the Llamas tab.
  • The “X has joined the team” notification still displays Power Level and F.O.R.T. stat increase text, even though these stats no longer change when players join a party.
  • Some abilities may be spammable while on cooldown, sometimes consuming Energy even though the ability is not actually activated.
    • Fixed in next major game update.
  • [Simple Edit] UI does not update to reflect the reset after editing a structure.
  • When inspecting Heroes on the Manage screen, certain pieces of head textures (e.g. hair, beard, hats or full head) may not be visible.
  • [Xbox One] Directional Jump Pads won’t activate when Phase Shifting onto them.
  • Hotwire's Overcharge Perk may not trigger lightning damage consistently.
    • Releasing and pressing the Attack button again will restart lightning damage.
  • The Hero Commander Buckle+ Perk currently provides reduced effective damage as weapon level increases.
  • The player character may sometimes use an incorrect arm-sweep animation when interacting with world objects.
