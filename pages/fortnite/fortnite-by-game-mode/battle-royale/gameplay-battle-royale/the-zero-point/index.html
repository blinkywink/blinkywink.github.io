The Zero Point, also known as Zero,[3][4] is a cosmic being in Fortnite. It is the second primordial entity born after The Nothing. It served as the source of the Big Bangs, the heart of Reality Zero, and the origin of many realities in the Omniverse.

It was originally believed to be a completely unique being, however, other diverging timelines from Reality Zero such as the one Jones traveled to in Chapter 4: Season OG, have been found to possess one.

In Remix: The Finale, it was revealed that Reality Zero's Zero Point was shattered into pieces sometime after Chapter 4: Season OG, implying the destruction of Asteria Island and Reality Zero alongside it. One of its fragments later landed on Oninoshima in Reality: 783 and reawakened the Island's magic, leading to its brief capture by the Dark Presence. The Zero Point was rebuilt throughout the course of Chapter 7 by the Dark Voyager on Geno's orders after traveling across the Omniverse in search of the Shards.

Overview

Overview[]

The Zero Point is a nexus orb composed of pure energy, believed to be the origin of all reality. It constantly cycles through countless realities per second, serves as an infinite power source as well as a gateway to alternate realities. Structurally, the Zero Point appears to be an energy sphere with an outer shell containing a central core made up of tendrils of energy capable of opening portals. These tendrils can become tangled, as demonstrated during the Zero Crisis Finale when The Foundation tried to untangle them to prevent an implosion. Despite this effort, he couldn't prevent the implosion; containing the blast and healing the Zero Point afterward ultimately required the creation of The Spire and The Foundation to seal himself inside.

Reality Waves, Altering Time and Space[]

The Zero Point holds the ability to manipulate reality in limitless ways. When unstable, it emits reality waves that randomly warp reality itself, changing anything into completely other things. These waves also have the capacity to restore specific locations to moments from their past, or rather, bring them from different point in times, mostly from different alternate timelines. For instance, during Season X, it reverted Dusty Divot back to Dusty Depot, before halting time just before The Meteor struck.

Exploitation by the Imagined Order[]

For millennia, the Imagined Order harnessed the power of the Zero Point to access and control various realities, imposing their vision of "order". The Zero Point was also integral to maintaining The Loop, a computer system that sets up 22-minute time loops where kidnapped fighters from countless realities must fight to death endlessly.

Following the events of Chapter 3: Season 2, the Imagined Order's defeat and destruction liberated the Zero Point from their influence, putting an end to The Loop in the process.

Sentience and Galactic Communication[]

The Zero Point has demonstrated signs of sentience, especially during moments of crisis. In Chapter 3: Season 3, it began summoning relics from other realities as a form of communication. The Scientist tasked The Looper and AMIE with recovering these relics, each retrieval triggering a shockwave. These shockwaves were later revealed to be a galactic distress signal calling for assistance against an impending threat, The Herald. Despite this, the Zero Point went dormant.

Restoration and Zero Fusion[]

After the destruction of Artemis and Apollo, the Zero Point reactivated, gathering fragments of the shattered islands and others from fractured realities. With the assistance of AMIE and The Looper, the Zero Point was restored. The Paradigm initiated Zero Fusion, merging fragments from different realities to form Asteria, with the Zero Point hidden beneath Hidden Henge.

Exposure and Petrification[]

During Chapter 4: Season 3, the seismic problem caused by the appearance of MEGA City on the island revealed a hidden rainforest, which was trapped in the heart of Asteria, exposing the Zero Point again. However, it had entered a petrified state, depleted of much of its energy following Zero Fusion.

Alternate Timeline: Reality UN-0-C4OG[]

As the present time Zero Point was unusable, Jones was sent in the past to use a functional version of it in order to ensure Asteria's inhabitants survival. He arrived in Reality: UN-0-C4OG, an alternate past that is already different, but similar enough for Jones to achieve his goals. Jones' mission needed him to remake The End again, but with a different intended aftermath than Reality Zero's. The Zero Point collided with the Meteor, pulling Athena into it as in Reality Zero. However, due to Jones' interference, the Black Hole exploded instead of forming like it should have. This resulted in another Big Bang, creating new galaxies and realities, including Reality: UN-88.

Remix: The Finale and the Spirit Realm[]

During Remix: The Finale, a fragment of the Zero Point followed Jones, Hope and The Looper out of a wormhole, landing on Oninoshima in Reality: 783. This fragment's arrival restored magic to the island and reopened portals to the Spirit Realm, enabling an invasion by Demon Warriors. The Demons seized the fragment and took it back to the Spirit Realm. During Welcome, Our Alien Overlords, the fragment was revealed to be embedded in the Dark Presence, which seized control of Oninoshima while everyone else was on Springfield Island.

The Dark Voyager[]

After the events of Zero Hour, the Zero Point shard captured by the Dark Presence was liberated and purified all of Oninoshima before teleporting itself, Hope, Jones, and The Looper to Reality: 266, where the shard would then reside in InnoLoop Labs. Shortly then, the Dark Voyager took control of the lab—and by extension, the shard—and is now searching for the remaining fragments, leading to the Reassembly Events.

The Zero Point is Reformed[]

During the events of Shattered, the Dark Voyager places the final shard into the Zero Point before then overclocking it, turning the Golden Coast into the Shattered Coast. After the Looper takes out the Dark Harvester, the Zero Point became unstable.

Personality

Personality[]

The Zero Point is a highly enigmatic entity whose intentions are extremely difficult to discern. Although seemingly aware of everything occurring around it, it often allows events to unfold without interference, even when those events directly affect its own condition. For centuries, it tolerated being exploited and controlled by the Imagined Order without openly resisting.

When threats such as Galactus targeted it, the Zero Point again refrained from directly defending itself. Instead, it allowed Galactus to attempt to consume it, an act that would have resulted in the destruction of both Reality Zero and Galactus through the creation of another Black Hole.[5] This course of action appeared to disregard the inhabitants of Reality Zero entirely.

Despite this apparent passivity, the Zero Point has demonstrated signs of sentience and intervention on multiple occasions. It called for help and warned the Islanders of The Herald's impending arrival,[6] and it actively resisted when The Last Reality attempted to capture it.

These actions suggest that the Zero Point generally tolerates most events and rarely interferes, instead allowing individuals and realities to resolve their own conflicts. However, its behavior changes when confronted with threats connected to The Last Reality, The Nothing, or dangers capable of affecting the Omniverse itself.

Although seemingly unwilling to fight directly, it has repeatedly shown a willingness to restore and heal realities after conflicts conclude. This was notably demonstrated by Reality: 783's Zero Point Shard after it was freed from the Dark Presence, when it restored Oninoshima and returned all combatants to their respective realities.[7]

Appearance

Appearance[]

The Zero Point is a large glowing orb that is constantly emitting the power of creation in the shape of plant-like tendrils. The interior of the orb is hollow and big enough to fit a person inside, but it is also the place that contains the raw power of creation.

  • Stable State - It glows a bright blue color.
  • Unstable State - It will slowly change from glowing light blue to dark red. Environment surrounding it will be distorted.
  • Bloom State - Special state that the Zero Point occasionally enters when it becomes extremely unstable. The Zero Point will shape its tendrils into the shape of a gigantic lotus flower. After the flower fully materializes it will release all of its unstable energy across the sky in an attempt to stabilize itself.
  • Dormant State - Dark, but with a faint blue light at the center of the orb. Low power.
  • Petrified State - Silicified, constantly groaning. No power left.
  • Big Bang State - A massive Kaleidoscope explosion with lotus flower layers which resulted in the creation of The Omniverse.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

The Zero Point is a very powerful being wielding the Power of Creation. It can create, alter, and destroy entire Realities and more. Its power is absolutely infinite, unless destabilized. The Zero Point is also the source of magic.

Power of Creation
Big Bang
Bloom into a countless layers of lotus flower to create plenty of new Galaxies and Realities. The Omniverse was created by the Zero Point during its emergence.
Reality Restructuring
Can produce a new Reality by restructuring the matter of an existing Reality. It transformed the first iteration of Reality Zero (Athena) into a new iteration composed of two superimposed islands (Apollo & Artemis).
Reality Annihilation
The power of creation can also be used to destroy reality.
Reality Warp
It possesses the power to alter reality in any way imaginable. It can send out Reality Waves that will alter the fabric of time and space, as well as create snapshots or copies of the matter in Reality Zero.
Zero Fusion
Merging pieces of fractured realities together into one. During the process, The Zero Point will open portals to random realities and uses its tendrils to snatch a chunk of land from it. However, several negative effects are to be expected, such as the sacrifice of the person who initiated this phenomenon, a relatively short lifespan of the created reality, and the petrification of the Zero Point. This power allowedt eh creation of the third and final iteration of Reality Zero, from the remnants of the previous iteration and various other realities.
Infinite Energy
The Stabilized Zero Point is an infinite source of pure creation energy that can be harnessed for many uses. The Bridge, Neo Tilted and The Loop used the Zero Point as their main power source. Mecha Team Leader, as well as Galactus used it to not only replenish them, but also make them stronger.
Reality Travel
Tendrils
Can shoot out Tendrils that have the ability to pull anything from a previous time or different reality. They can also open portals (also known as Rift Gates).
Rift
A crack in spacetime, a type of portal that can lead into random places on the island or other realities.
Reality Portal
The Zero Point itself is a massive portal that cycles through countless realities infinitely. By using vibrational energy attuned to a specific reality, it can lock on to that reality for short amount of time.
Transformations
Reality Bloom
A healing mechanism, it can bloom into a huge lotus like flower to release all unstable energy in an attempt to stabilize itself. Just like Zero Fusion, this phenomenon must be activated by a third party.
Black Hole
When the Zero Point is extremely damaged, imploding over itself or under certain unknown conditions, it will explode, releasing what could have been seen as stars before transforming into a black hole. After a while, the Black Hole appears to shrink and explode, creating a new reality from the matter of the old reality that was sucked out and restoring the Zero Point to it's original state.
Petrification
The Zero Point appears in this state after its power has faded out from unknown circumstances.
Other Abilities
Reality Awareness
It is aware of imminent threats and dangers. The Zero Point was aware of The Herald before her attack on the island. If the threat is linked to The Nothing, it will start showing signs of significant activity, such as bringing back artifacts or creating shock waves to warn people.
Source of Magic
The Zero Point's power is also a source of magic itself, although this aspect is unknown to most characters. This power was revealed during Chapter 2: Season 6 with The Spire and its guardian towers manifesting magical power. Spire Cult and Raz also manifested magical powers from The Zero Point itself. Later in Chapter 6: Season 1, one of the shards of The Zero Point returned Magic to Oninoshima.

Zero Point energy were used by the Rift Beacons to create a controlled rift to send Doom elsewhere. After Doom was weakened enough, the rift violently ripped Pandora's Box power out of Doom first before rifting Doom away from the island.

Chapter 5: Season Remix[]

Remix: The Finale[]

It is revealed that the petrified Zero Point has been destroyed off-screen at an unspecified period of time between Chapter 4: Season 4 and Chapter 5: Season 4. One of the fragments traveled through a rift, alongside Hope, Jones and The Looper to Reality: 783.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

The fragment crash landed near Nightshift Forest, unleashing a Reality Wave across all realms on Oninoshima, reawakening all ancient magics, reopening the gate to Spirit Realm, and allowing the Demons to resurface. Rift Butterflies and an abundance of zero point energy can be found at Battle's End located in the forest. In Story Quests, the Zero Shard is described by everyone as an ancient object with strange but powerful magic. The demons took the Zero Shard and moved it into their Spirit Realm where the Dark Presence plans to do something evil with it.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 1[]

Someone was actively trying to repair a Spirit Realm portal using Zero Point energy. The portal was later struck by a huge debris from Death Star Sabotage, causing a reality waves that transformed the residents of the island into superheroes.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

A mural of the Zero Point can be found in Utopia City.

During Super Showdown, Zero Point energy can be seen flowing through the Dark Presence's tentacle. He converted the energy into barrage of attacks.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

The Zero Point Shard was seen on Dark Presence's heart. Hope and Jones attempted to use Kang and Kodos's saucer to beam the shard off but the Dark Presence utilizes the shard's power to throw the saucer off to other reality along with a very small splinter of the shard. The small splinter ended up in the hands of Homer Simpson where he plans to transform Springfield Island into paradise.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 2[]

After countless misusages of the shard, Homer tried to dispose the shard by throwing it into a nuclear pool in Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. As he rest here, he tries to get a half-eaten candy bar at the edge of the pool, but he accidentally falls into the pool, sinking to his doom. However, a reaction between the Zero Point energy and radiation emits a purple cloudy explosion and clones Homer in large numbers in which they begin to run amok around Springfield.

Lore Trivia

Lore Trivia[]

  • The End's leitmotifs usually plays when the Zero Point is involved.
  • In Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War, it is heavily implied that Vibranium originated from the Zero Point.
  • In Batman/Fortnite: Foundation, The Batman Who Laughs implied that World Forge's creation power is a lesser version of the Zero Point's creation power.
  • In the deleted scene of the Fracture event, The Zero Point is shown to be able to destroy The Mothership on its own and also be able to escape and move on its own.
    • In the final version of Fracture, the Zero Point showed instead the ability to teleport at a much farther location in the Omniverse.
  • In The In-Between, The Zero Point only appears as pure energy.[10][11]
  • Zero-point energy (ZPE) is a real type of energy that is found in objects close to absolute zero, the coldest theoretical temperature any object can reach before it loses nearly all energy (0°K/Kelvin, about -273° Celsius). It keeps these objects stable, and is also part of the reason why Helium can remain a liquid at close to 0°K. This may be linked to the Zero Point, not just by name, but by strength (ZPE is quite strong) and application (Most of space is close to absolute zero, yet particles are still created and it is still stable thanks to ZPE partly).

    The Zero Point is an Environmental Object in Fortnite: Battle Royale, first introduced in Season 9. It has appeared in multiple seasons but functioned as a Gameplay Mechanic only in certain Seasons and Live Events.

    Functionality

    Functionality[]

    Chapter 7: Season 2[]

    • Touching any of the Zero Point Shard would cause the player to get Rifted into the sky.

    Chapter 7: Season 1[]

    • Coming into contact with the Shard would rift the player to a random place near the Shard.

    Chapter 2: Season 5[]

    • Coming into contact with the Zero Point would pull the player in, granting a small amount of Health and Shield before ejecting them.

    Season X[]

    • Touching the Zero Point would cause the player to get Rifted into the sky.

    Trivia

    Trivia[]

    • The Zero Point Object appeared ingame in the following seasons: Season 9, Season X, Chapter 2: Season 5, Chapter 2: Season 6, Chapter 3: Season 3, Chapter 3: Season 4, Chapter 4: Season 3, Chapter 4: Season 4 and Chapter 4: Season OG.
      • It also appeared ingame in the following events: The Butterfly Event, The Unvaulting Event, The Final Showdown, The End (Chapter 1), The Devourer of Worlds, Zero Crisis Finale, The End (Chapter 2), A New Day, Collision, Fracture and The Big Bang.

      • Chapter 1: Season 8's version has an extra texture called "T_ButterflyStarField_D" for the starry effects inside the orb.
      • Chapter 2 cracked version utilizes the World Position Offset Material Functions for the unstable shaking.
    • Since the Zero Point in Chapter 2: Season 5 could give you full health, players used this to their advantage to get free wins by making a sky base inside the Zero Point, so they don't fall down. Luckily this was patched, now that players don't have enough health to survive the storm.
