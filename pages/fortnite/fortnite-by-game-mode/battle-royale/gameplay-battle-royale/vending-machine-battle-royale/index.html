Vending Machines are an Object and a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale that were introduced in Season 3, and reworked in Chapter 2: Season 7.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Vending Machines offer different goods depending on the type of machine visited, which is displayed by a white weapon class icon on the machine's screen. Each set of goods is for sale for a cost of an amount of Bars. Interacting with a Vending Machine is identical to a Characters, in that a radial menu appears, offering services in exchange for Bars. Vending Machines also provide dialogue like many Characters do.

Before Update v34.00, Llana would appear on the screen of a Vending Machine.

Types

Types[]

  • All Outlaw Vending Machines sell Thermite and Plasma Burst Laser.
  • Weapon-O-Matic Vending Machines sell two weapons of the same type, plus some corresponding ammo.
  • Mending Machines sell two healing items, plus the Patch Up Service.
  • Malfunctioning Vending Machines sell a random weapon or item for each purchase.
Location

Location[]

Vending Machines are found at set spawn locations across the map.

Season 3

Update v3.40

  • Introduced.
    • Items can be purchased infinitely using Materials, as long as the player can gather them.

Season 5

Update v5.20

  • Vending Machine changes:
    • Chance for no Vending Machine to spawn at a location reduced from 54.67% to 26.66%.
      • Vending Machines spawn more often, cost less, and have an increased chance to spawn at a higher rarity.
        • Common Vending Machine
          • Spawn chance reduced from 7.27% to 6.67%.
          • Material cost reduced from 100 to 75.
        • Uncommon Vending Machine
          • Spawn chance increased from 14.55% to 20%.
          • Material cost reduced from 200 to 150.
        • Rare Vending Machine
          • Spawn chance increased from 14.55% to 20%.
          • Material cost reduced from 300 to 225.
        • Epic Vending Machine
          • Spawn chance increased from  5.45% to 18.67%.
          • Material cost reduced from 400 to 300.
        • Legendary Vending Machine
          • Spawn chance increased from 3.64% to 8%.
          • Material cost reduced from 500 to 375.

Season 8

Update v8.10

  • Vending Machines reworked.
    • They no longer require Materials, but instead drop a single item of choice.
    • This item can be cycled through by hitting the machine with the Pickaxe.
  • Removed Common and Uncommon Vending Machines
