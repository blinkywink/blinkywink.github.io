Overview

Alien Artifacts are resources in Fortnite: Battle Royale that can be used to unlock Selectable Styles for the Kymera Outfit unlocked at Page 1 of the Chapter 2: Season 7 Battle Pass. If you get close enough to an unclaimed Artifact, it will be marked with a purple exclamation mark on the mini-map. As of week 11, it is speculated that no more alien artifacts will be hidden on the map, as no new alien artifacts were added for week 11, and leakers found no source of new alien artifacts being added.

While Alien Artifacts can be found in Cosmic Chests, each week brings a set of alien artifacts to collect on the island.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The fifth artifact of Week 3 is located in a small house next to Lockie’s Lighthouse. However, it is glitched into the ground and therefore can not be collected.
  • The maximum number of artifacts you can obtain from Cosmic Chests per week is 15. The reset is at Tuesday 4:00PM. This is now no longer true, and as many as you need can be obtained at once from Cosmic Chests, instead of 15 per week.
  • On July 20 2021, a Alien Artifact can be found at Rainbow Rentals. It is not part of any week, and is mostly a bonus related to the LGTBQIA+ due to its location. It is located inside the orange rental.
  • 360 Alien Artifacts are required to unlock all Kymera styles, so even if you collected all of the Alien Artifacts across the map, you would have to open at least 36-100 Cosmic Chests throughout Chapter 2: Season 7.
