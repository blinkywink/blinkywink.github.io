Overview

Fishing Holes, also known as Fishing Spots, are a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite that was introduced in Chapter 2: Season 1. They are visible spaces at the surface of the water where the player can see Floppers swim about. The Fishing Holes can be fished for rare Fishes and a wide variety of weapons.

Types of Fishing Spots

Types of Fishing Spots[]

Common Fishing Spot[]

Has an increased chance of catching rarer Fishes and Weapons compared to plain water.

Common Loot
Item Rarity Description
Flopper 		Common Heals 40 HP.
Jellyfish 		Rare Heals 20 Effective Health.
Shieldfish 		Rare Heals 50 Shield.
Slurpfish 		Epic Heals 40 Effective Health.
Vendetta Flopper 		Legendary Heals 40 HP. Marks a nearby enemy.
Midas Flopper 		Legendary Heals 40 HP. Turns all inventory weapons to Legendary.

Golden Fishing Spot[]

Can be fished twice, granting a guaranteed high-tier weapon and high-tier fish each time. Has a chance to fish up a Chest instead.

Golden Loot
Item Rarity Description
Jellyfish 		Rare Heals 20 Effective Health.
Shieldfish 		Rare Heals 50 Shield.
Slurpfish 		Epic Heals 40 Effective Health.
Vendetta Flopper 		Legendary Heals 40 HP. Marks a nearby enemy.
Midas Flopper 		Legendary Heals 40 HP. Turns all inventory weapons to Legendary.
Spicy Fish 		Rare Heals 15 HP. Gives brief speed boost.
Cuddle Fish 		Rare Deals damage to an enemy when they walk near it.
Chest 		Common A chest with random loot.

Blue Fishing Spot[]

Found in the waters of Squibbly Shores. Grants the Lighthouse Keycard.

Blue Loot
Item Rarity Description
Lighthouse Keycard 		Legendary Keycard for Squibbly Shores's lighthouse vault.
