Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.

Community Choice was a unique feature in Fortnite: Battle Royale's Item Shop. For one or two days, you could vote on one of three cosmetic items to come back or add first, with the cosmetic with the most votes being available for 1 day. A bonus cosmetic item is also added to the item shop depending on which outfit is decided as the winner.
