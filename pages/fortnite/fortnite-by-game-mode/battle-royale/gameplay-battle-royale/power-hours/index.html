Power Hours

Overview

Power Hours are Events in Fortnite that were introduced in Blitz Royale during Chapter 6: Season 3, later introduced to Fortnite: Battle Royale during Chapter 6: Season 4 and then introduced to Fortnite: Save the World during Chapter 7: Season 2.

Power Hours last one hour and activate special modifiers that persist for the duration of the event. Certain items may also be unvaulted, vaulted or have their behaviors temporarily changed.

  • Blitz Royale
  • C6S4
  • C7S1
  • C7S2
  • Save the World
