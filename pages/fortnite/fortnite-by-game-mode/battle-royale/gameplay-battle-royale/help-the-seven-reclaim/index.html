Help The Seven Reclaim was a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced in Chapter 3: Season 2.

Description

Description[]

Each game one Named Location will be marked with red color or red crosshair icon where the battle will begin. When activated, Rifts will appear and IO Guards and Seven Sentries will begin to spawn and fight each other. Seven Sentries will team up with Players and eliminate as many IO Guards/IO Patrols as possible in order to push back the IO, Eventually, IO Hunter will spawn on the final stage and he is required to defeat in order to gain the highest tier loot.

The more enemies the player eliminates, the higher tier loot they will receive.
