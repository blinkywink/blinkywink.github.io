Punch Cards

Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.

Punch Cards were a feature added in Chapter 2: Season 3. They were removed in Chapter 2: Season 5 and returned for one season during Chapter 2: Season 8.

  • Chapter 2: Season 3/4
  • Chapter 2: Season 8

Chapter 2: Season 3

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

In The Hideout, there was a screen where 53 punch cards could have been discovered and completed to earn 14,000 XP each stage. Every card was unique and granted a different challenges, such as getting a certain placement multiple times, winning different modes, and harvesting multiple materials. Each punch card usually had different levels to it, however, there were some exceptions that only had one stage.

Some punchcards had levels that were harder versions of the same challenge, but others had similar objectives as the different stages.

Chapter 2: Season 5

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

At the Start of Chapter 2: Season 5, Punch Cards were replaced by Rare Quests.

Chapter 2: Season 8

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

Punchcards made a return in Chapter 2: Season 8, in the form of Daily, Weekly, and Character Punchcards.

Daily Punchcards replaced Uncommon and Common automatically-given Quests and Quick Challenges, in that they are a set of three quests that refresh daily. Each quest awards 17,000 XP upon completion (meaning a daily total of 51,000 XP), and include goals such as completing quests of a certain rarity or from particular characters (However, daily quests were later changed to 5 quests per day, giving 45K XP each, meaning a total of 225K XP every day, and the types of quest granted by them were also changed). This card resets at 9:00 AM EST/6:00 AM PST/1:00 PM GMT.

Weekly Punchcards work the same as Daily Punchcards, in that they are a set of three quests that refresh once a week. Each quest awards 65,000 XP upon completion (meaning a weekly total of 195,000 XP), and include longer goals such as Placing Top 10 with friends a certain number of times.

Character Punchcards are questlines given by Characters on the Island. They are generally in 5 stages, and award an increasing amount of XP per stage (from 12,000 XP to 20,000 XP, increasing in 2,000 XP increments, meaning a character total of 80,000 XP). These values were later increased to 30,000 XP per stage (for all stages) on newer punchcards.

Chapter 3: Season 1

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

When Chapter 3: Season 1 launched, Punch Cards were replaced by Milestones.
