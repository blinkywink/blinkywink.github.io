Overview

The Elimination Feed, also known as the Message Feed, is a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite that was introduced in the Pre-Season.

Overview

Overview[]

The Message Feed is a string of text in the bottom left side of the player's screen which frequently updates over the course of a match, to show the Elimination of different players within the lobby. The feed updates every time a player is eliminated, and will also update via the usage of certain Toys.

The feed displays who was eliminated and by who, with different types of messages depending on how a player was eliminated, and who eliminated them. Toys will show various feats such as a number of Bottle Flips or longest passes with a Flying Disc.

List of Strings

List of Strings[]

The Message feed has a large amount of unique text strings for Messages, many of which are references to popular culture. Below is a list of all known Message feed strings, and how to obtain them. Many strings are also used interchangeably with the terms 'eliminated' and 'knocked'. These are only mentioned if unique, else the assumption is all uses of eliminated and knocked are interchangeable. If a player eliminates an enemy at long range, the distance in meters is shown. If a player eliminates an enemy while wearing the Victory Crown, the player's name is shown in yellow.

Player 1 refers to the person playing the game, whereas player 2 is referring to an enemy player

Replay Client

Replay Client[]

In the Replay Client, the Message feed is different. Instead of being text based, it is icon based and is located in the upper left corner, below the number of players left. The player being eliminated/knocked is shown in a red background on the right side. If a player eliminates themselves, the name is shown on the left and right side.

The Message feed icon is displayed in a red circle with a white icon:

  • Crosshair Icon: Message
  • Storm Icon: Message by the Storm
  • Down arrow with a bang: Fall damage elimination
  • Cross/health icon: Knocked down
  • Explosion icon: Message by an explosive weapon

Update v5.00[]

  • Streamer Mode no longer just says "Player###", instead it now uses the player's cosmetic character as their display name "Character[###]".

  • Added Toys as a Cosmetic Type.
    • Toy usage will now be displayed in the Elimination Feed.

Season 7[]

Update v7.00[]

  • Added the Elimination Feed to Creative.

Update v7.30[]

  • The Streamer Mode setting has been replaced with two separate settings:
    • Anonymous Mode: displays the player's name as “Anonymous” to all players that are not in their squad.
    • The player's account info will still be attached to reports submitted by other players using the Player Reporting feature.
    • Hide Other Player Names: displays all other player names as “Player” unless they are in the player's squad.
  • Neither of these settings will be functional when playing in a Tournament match.
