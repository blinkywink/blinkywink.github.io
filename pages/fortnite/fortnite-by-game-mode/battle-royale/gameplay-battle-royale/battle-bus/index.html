The Battle Bus is a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite that was introduced in the Pre-Season.

It was replaced by Storm Surfing during Chapter 7: Season 1 until December 4th where it was reinstated in proper working condition.

Overview

Overview[]

The Battle Bus is an aerial vehicle in Battle Royale that transports players to the Battle Royale Island at the beginning of every match. It has a specific path, which is randomized each game, that it follows over the Island to let players exit and land. This path is always a straight line. When the Battle Bus' doors open and players are able to jump out, its horn sounds. Once the Battle Bus has reached the end of its path, players who haven’t exited the Battle Bus will be immediately dropped from it.

By pressing the emote button before exiting the Battle Bus players can thank the Bus Driver with message appearing in the Elimination Feed "[Player Name] has thanked the bus driver."

Appearance

Appearance[]

The default Battle Bus combines a blue-painted school bus with a large hot air balloon, which inflates with a blue flame to allow the vehicle to fly. Wheel clamps on the bus' back tires render it immobile on the ground.

It is very similar to Lars' Van from Save the World, and even includes a BluGlo funnel on the front.

In Chapter 4, the Battle Bus was equipped with rocket engines in order to be able to fly through space. Upon reaching the boundaries of the Island, the engine would shut down, the boosters would be jettisoned, and the hot air balloon would be inflated.

Pre-Season

Update v1.6

  • Introduced the Battle Bus alongside the launch of Battle Royale.
    • A Crashed Battle Bus could have been seen on a mountain in between Shifty Shafts and Dusty Divot in Chapter 1. The balloon of this Battle Bus was deflated and also lost some of its color.

Season 4

Update v4.40

  • All Battle Buses received updated visuals.
    • The Crashed Battle Bus is now destructible along with gaining a proper deflated balloon prop.

Season 5

Update v5.30

  • Players now have the ability to thank the Battle Bus Driver by pressing the Emote key/button when inside the bus. Doing so displays a message in the Elimination Feed that states "[Player Name] has thanked the bus driver."

Season 6

Update v6.20

  • During Fortnitemares 2018, another Crashed Battle Bus could have been seen, as a result of the Floating Island exploding. It could have been found north of Loot Lake, and west of Lazy Links. The model of this Battle Bus is the same as the crashed one outside of Shifty Shafts. At the same time this Battle Bus was added, the old crashed Battle Bus was removed.
