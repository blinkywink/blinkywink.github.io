Star Destroyer Bombardment is a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was introduced during Galactic Battle.

Overview

Overview[]

STAR DESTROYER PORTAL IS OPEN![]

After the first Storm circle closes, a pop up will appear followed by a Rift Gate opening in a random location on the island, marked for all players to see. The first player to reach it will have them, and their teammates be transported aboard the incoming Imperial Star Destroyer.

BOMBARDMENT INCOMING![]

Upon the Rift Gate being entered, a Rift will appear and transport the Imperial Star Destroyer onto a random part of the island going in a stationary path. For 1 minute and 30 seconds, the players inside the Star Destroyer are given an overhead display of the island below with other players and AIs' locations marked, and controls to Fire the capital ship's Heavy turbolaser turrets down on the island to deal high explosive damage in a wide AoE and destroy structures near instantly, Zoom onto a specific location under the targeting reticle, and Exit to simply leave the controls and return onto the island while the Star Destroyer remains on its designated course. Exiting the Star Destroyer will not boot the entire team out of the capital ship.

Players on the ground cannot harm the Star Destroyer in any way, leaving their only courses of action to either run away out of its path and attack radius, or seek shelter under caves or Shield Bubble Jrs. A player will know if they are in the Star Destroyer's attack radius when their screen begins to glow yellow.

Triva

Triva[]

  • Star Destroyer Bombardment's codename is SharpFrog.
  • If no player reaches the Rift Gate within 4 minutes and 30 seconds after it opens, it will close, and the Star Destroyer will still appear but will not be player controlled.
  • Hired Characters such as Chewbacca, Darth Vader, and the Mandalorian Specialists will not be transported off the island and will continue to battle on the ground.
  • During the intro sequence, the controlling player can see Imperial TIE Fighters fly out of the Star Destroyer's hanger bay. These TIE Fighters cannot be seen by other players on the island.
