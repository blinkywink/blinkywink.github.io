Hologram Pads are Devices in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced in Chapter 2: Season 7.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Hologram Pads are devices which, when touched, temporarily apply various effects to the player, normally affecting their movement.

Types

Types[]

There are 7 types of Hologram Pads which differ in their effects:

Image Information Description Locations
Low Gravity Pad Grants a very strong low-gravity effect. Party UFO
Rift Pad Activates a Rift. Party UFO
Ice Slide Pad Grant Frozen Feet effect. Party UFO
Prop Disguise Pad Transforms the player into a prop. Party UFO
Bubble Pad Forms a protective bubble around the player. Risky Reels

History

History[]

  • Update v17.21: A new blue Abductor called the Party UFO appeared above The Aftermath.
    • July 28th: Party UFO activated the low gravity Pad.
    • July 30th: Party UFO activated the Rift Pad. The low-gravity Pad was disabled.
    • August 2nd: Party UFO activated the Ice Slide Pad. The Rift Pad was disabled.
  • Update v17.30: Party UFO activated the Prop Disguise Pad.
    • August 4th: Replaced the Prop Disguise device with the low-gravity Pad. Bubble Pads appeared near Weeping Woods Cabin, Boney Burbs, Green Steel Bridge, Sheriff's Office and in Risky Reels.
    • August 8th: Bubble Pads appeared for PC players.
  • Update v17.40: Removed the Bubble Pads.
