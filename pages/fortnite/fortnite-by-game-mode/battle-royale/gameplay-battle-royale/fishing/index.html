Overview

Fishing is a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite, introduced in Chapter 2: Season 1.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Players can fish using a Fishing Rod to catch fish or other items. Harpoon Guns or Explosives can be used on Fishing Holes to gain different loot. Fishing in holes provides the best loot, whereas exploding them provides regular loot. Players can catch fish, materials, weapons and ammo from Fishing.

Locations

Locations[]

  • Calm water, where items other than Fishing Rods cannot be used to fish, is a slow way of fishing and gives low-tier items.
  • Fishing Spots, using any tools or explosives that give rarer items in greater quantity.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • It is possible to fish in all bodies of water, including the stacks in Steamy Stacks and fountains in The Authority.
  • Players have a 1 in a million chance of obtaining the Mythic Goldfish from any form of fishing except explosions.
