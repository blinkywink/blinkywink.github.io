Community Day

Overview

Community Days are Events in Fortnite that were introduced during Chapter 7: Season 2.

It is implied that more will happen in the future.

Events

May 30th 2026 9:00 AM ET - May 31st 2026 9:00 AM ET

Whole Day[]

  • 5X Rivalry Credits and Double XP up to 50 Levels
  • Returning Twitch Drops

  • Player voted Weapon Unvault:

  • Increased Slapperoni Slice max stack size.
