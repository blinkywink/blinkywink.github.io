Overview

Scanning is a Gameplay Mechanic in Fortnite: Battle Royale, Fortnite: Creative, and Fortnite: Ballistic. It was introduced in Chapter 2: Season 2.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Scanning allows to see opponents and Containers through several layers of structures. While all scanned opponents are indicated with a specific symbol above them, which varies depending on which kind of an opponent is scanned, some items also highlight the outlines of Containers and opponents.

Symbols

Symbols[]

Icon Description Meaning
Opponents
Red Diamond Players and some Guards
Red Crown Boss
Red Triangle Guards, Characters, Animals
Eye Marker Sentry Cameras, Sentry Turrets, Alien Parasites eggs
Containers
White Chest Ice Machines and all kinds of Chests
White Circle Ammo Boxes, Fishing Barrels

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Cold Blooded Suits and their Bosses are marked with the eye symbol when neutral to the player. Their scan symbols change to a triangle and a crown respectively when alerted.
