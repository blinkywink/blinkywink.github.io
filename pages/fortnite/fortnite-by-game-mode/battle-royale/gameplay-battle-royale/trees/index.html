Overview

Trees are one of the many Props found across the map. There are 2 types of Trees: Physics Trees, which fall into a log when cut down; and Decoration Trees give Wood, but do not fall when cut down.

Trees are the main source of Wood on Fortnite. Most Trees give around 50 Wood (when the multiplier is 1.0x for harvesting) in total before being destroyed.

Physics[]

Exclusive to Artemis/Chapter 3, these trees fall and become a log when destroyed.

Once fallen, these logs have 600 HP and show physical damage when hit.

The following Trees are Physics Trees:

  • Tall Pine Trees
  • Tall Snowy Pine Trees
