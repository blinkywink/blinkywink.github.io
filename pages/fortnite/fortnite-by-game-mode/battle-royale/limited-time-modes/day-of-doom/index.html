Day of Doom is a Limited Time Mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced in Chapter 5: Season 4.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

In Day of Doom, players are divided into 2 teams of 16: Doom's Henchmen and Allies of the Avengers.

Players who join Doom's Henchmen must focus on gathering all six Arcane Artifacts. Once all artifacts have been collected, the Avengers will lose their ability to respawn, and the Henchmen must then eliminate every hero to claim victory.

Meanwhile, players on the Avengers team must work together to stop Doom's forces from collecting the Artifacts. Although Doom's Henchmen possess numerous respawns, the Avengers will achieve victory by depleting all of the enemy team's reinforcements.

No matter the team, players will slowly regenerate lost health and shields, take no fall damage, and have infinite ammo on all their weapons. Doom's Henchmen will regenerate health and shield instantly when an Artifact is collected and have 80 lives, once all of them have been depleted, they lose.

Available Items

Available Items[]

In Day of Doom, all players from both teams spawn with a predetermined loadout of items that cannot be dropped. The Henchmen start with only three items, acquiring one more for each of the next two collected artifacts, which also changes what order their items are in. Unlike the opposing team, the Heroes team spawns with all their weapons, receiving all items from one of four possible loadouts, based on Iron Man, Captain America, War Machine and Black Panther. Each respawn by the heroes a loadout will be randomly selected. The Henchmen who collects the first Artifact will transform into Doom's Chosen, replacing their loadout with the Armor of Doom.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Day of Doom is referred to as ToadJam in the files.
  • The gameplay and concept is similar to that of the Endgame Limited Time Mode from Season 8.
  • Players on the Villains team are instantly given the appearance of the Latverian Tactical Grunts after leaving the Battle Bus. Similarly to the Chitauri in the previously mentioned Endgame Limited Time Mode.
    • Though they were prominently shown in the trailer and official artwork, the Latverian Heavy Elites do not appear in Day of Doom.
  • Accolades can still be earned in Day of Doom, making some accolades like Shield Master or Mechanism of War very easy.
  • All currently available Containers still spawn in Day of Doom but can't be opened or marked. Breaking them will also not drop any items.
  • Marking an Arcane Artifact while it is still falling will display the icon for one of the Infinity Stones. This is due to parts of the code from the Endgame Limited Time Mode being directly reused in Day of Doom, and the icons for the Infinity Stones being accidentally left in as a result.
    • This becomes even more evident, as the music stingers for both Doom's Chosen reappearing and the moment when all six Arcane Artifacts are collected are the same as those used in Endgame.
  • There is no "Fill/No Fill" option while queueing, so players are always forced into Squads with others.
