Overview

This article is about a Mode available for a limited time only.

“
Battle Royale gameplay with a limited set of weapons made up entirely of items that are vaulted in the standard modes.
”
— In-Game Description

Unvaulted is a Limited Time Mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced on December 19th 2018.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

In this LTM, weapons and items that are in The Vault are possible to obtain, hence its name, Unvaulted. It mostly contains vaulted items, although healing items, materials and ammo are still present.

Loot Pool

Loot Pool[]

Despite being named Unvaulted, many Vaulted items do not appear in the loot pool, and some items which are not vaulted also appear.

All information is correct as of Chapter 3: Season 1.

Trivia[]

  • The Grenade Launcher is the only standard weapon which is in the Unvaulted LTM at the same time as the current loot pool (This is likely an error).
  • The Uncommon Automatic Sniper Rifle is exclusive to this mode.
  • In previous iterations, the Machine SMG was included in this LTM before its official release in Chapter 3: Season 1.
    • This caused speculation as to its relation to the Pre-Season Submachine Gun.
