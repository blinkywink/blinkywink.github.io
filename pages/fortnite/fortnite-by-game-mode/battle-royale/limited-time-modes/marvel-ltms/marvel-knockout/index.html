Marvel Knockout is a Knockout LTM released in Update v14.10. It uses the standard Knockout LTM preset, except it relies on the player spawning with different Superpowers. It comes in Duos and Trios variants.

Gameplay

Gameplay[]

Marvel Knockout works identically to other Knockout LTMs. It is a tournament of 16 Duos or Trios, with whoever gets more eliminations (or in the case of a draw, whoever got the first elimination) in the given time, and there are 4 rounds, each with the losing team being knocked out, until the final 2 teams play off in the Finals.

Weaponry and Items[]

  • Unlike standard knockout or regular modes, the player cannot find Weapons to use, and is stuck with the Superpowers they spawn with.
  • Each player gets one Superpower with mobility/support, secondary attack, and a primary attack with extra mobility.
  • Players have no inventory and can’t drop Items, but can build.

Health and Shield[]

  • Players start off with 200 Health and 100 Shield.
  • There are no Healing Items for players to use and gain Health or Shield, however players will regain Health over time and can gain even more Health if they have Groot’s Bramble Shield.
Marvel Knockout Super Series

Marvel Knockout Super Series[]

Marvel Knockout Super Series was a series of 5 Cups where players competed in Marvel Knockout for a chance to win a Marvel Series Outfit along with that Outfit's Set or real life money. The 5 Cups were the Daredevil Cup, the Ghost Rider Cup, the Black Widow Cup, and the Venom Cup which all granted Marvel Series Outfits and their Sets, along with the $1 Million Super Cup which only granted large amount of real life money.
