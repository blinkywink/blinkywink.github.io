Overview

This article is about a Mode available for a limited time only.

“
Close Quarters Combat with Shotguns & Jetpacks! To use a jetpack, double-tap the jump button to take off. Keep the button held down to apply thrust. Make sure to land before it runs out of fuel!
”
— In-Game Description

Close Encounters is a LTM in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced on May 25th 2018.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

In this gamemode, the only available form of weaponry are Shotguns, making it almost impossible to attack players from a long range. The Storm moves more quickly and an upper Storm is present which will descend throughout the match.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.

In this LTM, you will have to get within close range to a player. This will result in them taking more damage when you fire your weapon. Also, do not go excessively high on your Jetpack, unless you are above water.

Try landing closer to the middle of the map to make sure you don't have to run too far or at all when the zone closes in.

Try and use the Heavy or Combat Shotgun, due to their range, for longer engagements.
