Overview

“
Classic Battle Royale loot comes to the new island!
”
— In-Game Description

Classic Loot is a Limited time mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was introduced on March 8th 2021.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

The items present in this mode are ones that were present in the early seasons of Battle Royale, alongside some newer ones which are present in most playlists.
