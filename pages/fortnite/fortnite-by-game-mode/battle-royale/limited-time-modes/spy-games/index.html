Overview

This article is about a Mode available for a limited time only.

The Spy Games was an event in Chapter 2: Season 2 introduced in Patch 12.20 where players got to pick the sides of GHOST or SHADOW, Gather intel to level up and get new tech. There were 50 levels for each team. The end of each line of tech was Ultimate Tech: Midas' Drum Gun and TNTina’s Ka-Boom Bow.

On the June 11th, all tech was made available to all players to use.

On June 17th, Spy Games finished with the release of Chapter 2: Season 3.

Tech

Tech[]

Tech consists of weapons, perks and items that were available to choose between in Spy Games modes once unlocked. The method of unlock resembled the Battle Pass, five Tech Points earned a new tech on the Spy Games menu in The Hideout. There were 2 sections of the Tech Tree, one for Shadow and one for Ghost. When the player chose an allegiance to either side, they could earn that side's respective tech by playing Operations and gaining intel in-game. Players could switch allegiances at any time through the Spy Games menu.

Every player started out with 'Starter Tech', and has access to the other side's Starter Tech as well, no matter which side they were on. Players did not gain access to every perk or weapon right away, and could choose between them once in an Operation (choices were not permanent). To unlock Tech during operations, you had to collect intel by doing various things:

  • Operation: Dropzone - Collect Intel Drops
  • Operation: Knockout - Win matches.
  • Operation: Payload - Successfully defend/deliver the payload.
  • Operation: Infiltration - Successfully retrieve the case.

On June 11th, all tech was made available to all players.
