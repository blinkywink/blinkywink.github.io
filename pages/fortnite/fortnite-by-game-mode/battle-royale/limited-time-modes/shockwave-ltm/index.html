Overview

This article is about the LTM. For the Ability, see Shockwave.
This article is about a Mode available for a limited time only.

Shockwave is a Limited Time Mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale that released on December 18th 2020 during the Operation: Snowdown LTM. Teams of 2 fight to win 3 rounds.

Gameplay

Gameplay[]

The Storm[]

  • The Storm will be centered at a specific Named Location or Landmark.
    • When you are in the storm for more than a few seconds, you will be eliminated.
  • The storm will continuously move.

Looting[]

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Its premise is similar to the Super Smash Bros. series in which players must be pushed out of the stage to be defeated, and instead of having a conventional health bar, players become lighter to push based on the amount of damage they've taken.
