Overview

This article is about a Mode available for a limited time only.

Arsenal is a Limited Time Mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced on 27th February, 2019.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

In this LTM, players will start off with a Mythic (Solos) or Legendary (Squads) weapon, with above average stats. Each weapon will downgrade as you gain eliminations, until eventually players are stuck trying to eliminate players with their Harvesting Tool, after getting an elimination with an Uncommon Makeshift Bow.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Make sure to not let any players eliminate you at all costs. When you see a player, you should always try to eliminate them.
  • If possible, try to focus the players that are in the lead. The longer lead players stay dead, the more time you have to catch up to their position.
  • Play defensively when you can; steal kills from fights that are already going on. If you're lucky enough you'll kill both players in a fight and go up two weapon levels.

Changes

Changes[]

  • Legendary Combat Shotgun (6) was replaced with Spire Assassin's Primal Shotgun.
  • Epic Suppressed Sniper Rifle (10) was replaced with the Epic Recycler.
  • Epic Suppressed Pistol (13) was replaced with the Epic Mechanical Explosive Bow.
  • Uncommon Semi-Automatic Sniper Rifle (21) was replaced with the Uncommon Primal Pistol.
  • Common Pistol (28) was replaced with the Uncommon Makeshift Bow.
