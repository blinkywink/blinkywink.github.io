High Explosives is a limited time mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced on December 29th, 2017.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

In this LTM, the only available form of weaponry is Explosive Weapons. Glider Redeploy is enabled, as well as adjustments to loot rates, and the rate at which the storm closes in. Supply drops drop more loot, and chests have a 100% chance of dropping Utility or Explosive Throwable Items.
