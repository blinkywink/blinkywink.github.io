Team Up! Aloy & Lara is a Limited Time Mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale that released on April 23rd 2021.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

Gameplay[]

  • One player will have access to the Makeshift Bow while one player has access to Dual Pistols.
    • You will spawn with the weapon associated with the character, and your Outfit will change to Lara Croft or Aloy.
  • No weapons can be looted. Grenades, Gas Cans and Crafting Items can only be picked up to craft other bows.
