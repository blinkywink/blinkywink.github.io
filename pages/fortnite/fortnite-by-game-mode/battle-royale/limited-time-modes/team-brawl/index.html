Overview

This article is about a Mode available for a limited time only.

“
Two teams face off in this fast-paced brawl! Battle to earn your team the most eliminations using weapon loadouts that change every time you respawn.
”
— In-Game Description

Team Brawl is a Limited Time Mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale similar to Team Rumble. It is a fast-paced playlist in which the team that reaches the target elimination wins the game.

LTM Rules and Gameplay

LTM Rules and Gameplay[]

The Storm[]

  • The Storm sets in 1 location and encompasses the playing field. It does not move.

Looting[]

  • Each player starts out with a unique loadout of 3 Items/Weapons and 100 Wood.
  • Supply Drops can give Exotic and Legendary Loot.
  • Players drop Chug Splashes upon elimination.

Gameplay[]

  • There are 2 teams: Red and Blue.
  • Elimination goal is set according to the number of players on each side.
  • The elimination goal is usually 60.
  • Players spawn after crashing from a Marauder Capsule.
  • The leading team is marked if they are way ahead of the opposing team.
Loot Pool

Loot Pool[]

  • The Loot Pool for the mode contains most standard weapons and items which have been present in the game's past seasons.
    • Weapons such as the Pulse Rifle or Makeshift Rifle are not included.
    • Sideways Weaponry is present.
    • Exotic Weaponry is present, including weapons which are currently not available.
