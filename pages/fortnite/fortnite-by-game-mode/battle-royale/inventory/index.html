Overview

This article is about the Inventory used in some Fortnite modes. For the Save the World inventory, see Backpack (Save The World).


The Inventory stores the items that you are currently holding. The Inventory menu also displays the Statistics, such as damage, of some of the items in your inventory. You can also rearrange items that are in your hotbar slots.

It can be accessed by pressing the Backpack Icon (Mobile), D-Pad Up or Left Menu Button/Thumb-pad (Controller), or 'I' (Default Keybind) on PC.

Below is a list of items that can be stored in the inventory:

Hotbar (Items)[]

In most modes, you can carry up to 5 in total of any Weapons or Items in your hotbar. An exception is Fortnite Ballistic, where you can only carry 1 Weapon and 1 Pistol.

Traps[]

You can also carry various Traps (such as Launchpads). Each Trap has its own limit for how many can be carried in the Inventory. For example, you can only carry 15 Armored Walls.
