Overview

Trees are one of the many props found across the map. There are 2 types of Trees: Physics Trees, which fall into a log when cut down; and Decoration Trees give Wood, but do not fall when cut down.

Trees are the main source of Wood on Apollo, Athena, and Artemis. Apollo and Athena only contained Decoration Trees, but Artemis contains the first Physics Trees. Most Trees give around 50 Wood (when the multiplier is 1.0x for harvesting) in total before being destroyed. Decoration Tree are trees that are made for getting materials. Physics Trees can fall down and be moved.
