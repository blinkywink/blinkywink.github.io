Overview

Healing Items are a type of item in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The purpose of these items is to increase the player's hitpoints or HP. HP is a combination of Health and Shield.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Healing Items increase players HP, be it Health or Shield. The majority of healing items are Consumables, meaning they are used by standing still for a period of time to gain the benefits. However, some healing items are thrown such as Chug Splash, and others are placed such as Cozy Campfires. Either way, all healing items increase the player's HP.

Healing Items

Healing Items[]

HP refers to both Health and Shield combined. An item that heals 100 HP heals 100 Health and / or Shield.

Environmental Healing

Environmental Healing[]

Health and Shield can be applied when entering specific locations and destroying certain props:

  • The waters at Slurpy Swamp could have been used to regain 1 HP per tick (2 if you crouch inside the Slurp pipes).
    • Destroying Slurp Barrels grants 10 HP.
    • Destroying Slurp Co. Trucks grants 100 HP.
    • Destroying Slurp Silos grants 50 HP.
  • If you landed in the Zero Point, you gained 48 HP.
  • Touching the Rebooted Cube at Friendly Forest would grant 20 Shield over time.
  • Cozy Campfires are stationary environmental objects on Artemis, which heal more than 50 HP. These campfires can burn out, and be re-lit with 200 Wood. They can also be stoked with 30 Wood to increase the duration and amount of health healed per tick.
    • They cannot be stoked in Zero Build.
  • Players who entered Pandora would passively gain Shield whilst in the location.
  • Bouncy Slurpshrooms heal the player 15 HP per use.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Storm and fall damage will ignore Shield and deplete only HP.
  • If you miss a shot with a Bandage Bazooka, it will turn into a Bandage upon impact.
  • Many items such as Wolverine's Claws and Peppers give extra abilities aside from just being healed.
  • In Galactic Battle, healing items are the only items that haven’t been vaulted, aside from Water and Air Sprites."
