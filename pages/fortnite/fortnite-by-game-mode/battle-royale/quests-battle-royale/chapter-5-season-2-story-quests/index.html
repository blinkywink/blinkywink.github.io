Overview[]

The Story Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from March 12th 2024 to May 24th 2024. Story Quests were released in Chapter 5: Season 2 and rewarded players with Season XP, the Artemis Insignia, Aphrodite Insignia, Cerberus Insignia, Medusa Insignia Sprays and the A New Olympus Loading Screen.
