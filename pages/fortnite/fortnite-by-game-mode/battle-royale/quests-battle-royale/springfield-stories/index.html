Overview[]

The Springfield Stories are a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from November 1st 2025 to November 29th 2025. The Springfield Stories were released in Chapter 6: Mini Season 2 and rewarded players with Season XP.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Description for the last quest of Part 1 was changed somewhere during the season from "We're running out of time here, we need Homer to undo his work. - Hope" to "We're running out of time, we need Homer to understand how serious this is. - Hope".
    • Probably during Update v38.10.
  • A large portion of the dialogue spoken by The Simpsons cast does not match the subtitling for them.
  • Springfield Stories' The Incredible Bulk marks the first time since Chapter 4: Season 2 where The Seven phone sound when story dialogue begins is used.
