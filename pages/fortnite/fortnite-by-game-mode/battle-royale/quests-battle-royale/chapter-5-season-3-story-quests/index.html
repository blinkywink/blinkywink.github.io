Overview[]

The Story Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from May 28th 2024 to August 16th 2024. Story Quests were released in Chapter 5: Season 3 and rewarded players with Season XP, the Assemble the Gems, Leviathan's Folly Loading Screens and the Hope's Oracle Spray.
