Overview[]

The Story Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from August 16th 2024 to November 2nd 2024. Story Quests were released in Chapter 5: Season 4 and rewarded players with Season XP and the Hopeful Llama Spray.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In Part 2 of Gwenpool's Mission, when you input a password in the computer, the options are VALERIA, CYNTHIA, and SUE.
    • VALERIA refers to a character from Marvel Comics named Valeria who was a teenage love interest of Doom or his "goddaughter" Valeria Richards that he named, rather than the Outfit Valeria.
    • CYNTHIA refers to Cynthia von Doom, the mother of Victor Von Doom.
    • SUE refers to Susan "Sue" Storm, the Invisible Woman of the Fantastic Four, whom Doom also married a Variant of.
  • In Part 2: Heroes Assemble, a subtitles mention an additional dialogue about X-Men, but that was not added as a voiceline.
    • Additionally, this dialogue does not appear in subtitles of other languages.
  • As of Update v31.10, it is no longer possible to start Gwenpool's Mission.
