Overview[]

The Syndicate Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from March 10th 2023 to June 9th 2023. Syndicate Quests were released in Chapter 4: Season 2 and rewarded players with Season XP, a Banner Icon, the Stray Spectre Emoticon, the Undersea Oni Spray and the Syndicate Slide, Syndicates Unite and River Guardians Loading Screens.
