Overview[]

The Resistance Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from March 20th 2022 to June 4th 2022. The Resistance Quests were released in Chapter 3: Season 2 and rewarded players with 20,000 Season XP for each completed challenge.

Starting from the beginning of the season, only one week quests were unlocked. However, for every week that passed, another quests would unlock to be completed.
