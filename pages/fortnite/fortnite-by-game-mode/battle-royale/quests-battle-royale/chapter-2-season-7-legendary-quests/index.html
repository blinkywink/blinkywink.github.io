Chapter 2: Season 7 Legendary Quests

Overview

  • Overview
  • Dialogue

The Legendary Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from June 8th 2021 to September 12th 2021. Legendary Quests were released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and rewarded players with Season XP for completion of each quest.

Starting from the beginning of the season, only one week quests were unlocked. However, for every week that passed, another quests would unlock to be completed. Each week was completable within 7 days of the release date.
