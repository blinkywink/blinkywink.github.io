Adrenaline Rush Quests

Overview

The Adrenaline Rush Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale available from February 21st 2023 to March 8th 2023. Adrenaline Rush Quests were released in Chapter 4: Season 1 and rewarded players with Season XP.
