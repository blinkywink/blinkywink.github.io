The Outlaw Keycard Quests

Overview

The Outlaw Keycard Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite, available from March 11th 2025. The Outlaw Keycard Quests were released in Chapter 6: Season 2 and rewarded players with the Outlaw Keycards.

The Outlaw image

The Outlaw image[]



Complete Outlaw Quests to reveal sections of the Outlaw image. When enough quests have been completed, the Outlaw Keycard will increase in rarity and gain access to new areas and services. Complete all the Outlaw Quests to reveal the whole image.
