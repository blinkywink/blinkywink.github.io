Overview[]

The Snapshots Quests were a set of Quests in Fortnite: Battle Royale, available from June 9th 2023 to August 25th 2023. Snapshots Quests were released in Chapter 4: Season 3 and rewarded players with Season XP, the Aura of Discovery Emoticon, the Coral Commando Spray and the Slone's Reinvention Loading Screen.
