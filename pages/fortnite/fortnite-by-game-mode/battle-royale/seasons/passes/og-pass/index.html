OG Pass

Overview

This article is about the pass tied to Fortnite OG. For the Battle Pass for Chapter 4: Season OG, see Chapter 4: Season OG#OG Pass.

The OG Pass is a seasonal cosmetic item bundle in Fortnite OG, which grants the player rewards in exchange for XP. Each OG Pass costs 800 V-Bucks (formerly 1,000 V-Bucks) and you do not get any from completing it. V-Bucks can only currently be earned through the Battle Pass.

OG Pass items are not exclusive to the season in which they are offered and are likely going to be added to the Item Shop sometime after the season ends. However, players are unable to access OG Pass from past seasons.
