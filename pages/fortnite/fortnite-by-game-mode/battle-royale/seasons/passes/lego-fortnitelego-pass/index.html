LEGO Fortnite:LEGO® Pass

Overview

The LEGO® Pass is a seasonal cosmetic item bundle in LEGO Fortnite, which grants the player rewards in exchange for XP (formerly Studs).

LEGO® Passes can be purchased for 1,200 V-Bucks (formerly 1,400 V-Bucks) or unlocked with Fortnite Crew.

The LEGO® Pass is structured similarly to the Battle Pass and other Fortnite Crew passes, with a single rewards track that contain both free and premium items. Like the other passes, LEGO® Pass items are not exclusive to the season in which they are offered and may be added to the Item Shop sometime after the season ends, though players are unable to access LEGO® Passes from past seasons.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • LEGO® Passes prior to Chill & Thrill used the same layout as Event Passes.
  • Prior to December 1st, 2024 and the release of Update v33.00, the resource used to progress the LEGO® Pass was Studs, which worked in a similar manner to Event Passes. Studs being the resource that was used for unlocking items in the LEGO® Pass may be a reference to how many LEGO® video games use Studs both as a form of completion and as a form of currency for use in unlocking rewards such as additional characters or cheats.
    • After December 1st, the resource used to progress the LEGO® Pass was changed to use XP rather than Studs, requiring 80,000 XP per tier rather than 1,000 Studs.
      • The Brick or Treat LEGO® Pass was affected by this change as it was the LEGO® Pass that was currently running before this change was made.
      • For the Chill & Thrill Pass onwards, this was changed again to 160,000 XP.
  • LEGO® Passes, starting with Chill & Thrill, now only have a single reward track with both free and paid rewards, similar to the Battle Pass. LEGO® Passes also have become included as part of Fortnite Crew. Access to the Premium reward track is only available while a subscription is active if the pass is obtained through Fortnite Crew.
  • Goldrush Gallery was the only LEGO® Pass to include more than one Outfit.
