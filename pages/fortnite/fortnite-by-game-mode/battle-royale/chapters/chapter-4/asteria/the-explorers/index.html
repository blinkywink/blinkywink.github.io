The Explorers are an exploring and research-based faction first was introduced in Chapter 4: Season 2. They are composed of various people from several different factions.

History

History[]

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

The Explorers set up excavation camps at cracks site to study The Rumbles. The Luminaries investigate them, and team up to study the seismic activity together.[7]

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

After the Jungle appeared, The Explorers enacted Creeky Compound and began investigating the various ruins around the area, chiefly The Apparatus, which they send a Convoy to.

  • They continue to be allied with The Luminaries to carry out research, with Trace serving as an intermediary between the two Factions. They can be found at The Apparatus as a Character.
  • Slone and Guardian Amara joined them at an unknown time period and collaborate with Trace. They study the Kinetic Ore found in the Jungle.[8]
  • Aura teams with The Looper to study field notes written by The Explorers on the Chalice of Cubetastrophe, as part of the season's Snapshot Quests.

Update v25.10[]

The convoy reaches The Apparatus, and a rudimentary research camp is set up there, fit with various machinery, equipment, and supplies.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

The research has slowed down on the island with the sudden introduction of Kado Thorne and his faction.

  • Trace no longer spawns at The Apparatus.
  • Slone has shifted her focus to stealing the Time Machine and thus does not appear to have continued work with The Explorers.

Chapter 4: Season OG[]

A Teddy Bear can be found with The Explorer's notes on the Chalice of Cubetastrophe with a jailed Gnome in Wailing Woods.

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

The new primary installation of The Explorers lies within the mountainous region of Helios, named Research Rock.

  • A small research operations on the Coral Buddies warrants a small outpost and campsite.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

New research camps were made near Lovely Lodge to monitor the arrival of Wasteland Warriors.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

The Explorers established a new research complex on the southeastern shore of Oninoshima, named Lakeside Lab.
