Chapter 4: Season OG,[1] also known as Season 27, Chapter 4: Season 5 or just simply Season OG/OG Season, was the twenty-seventh season (and first Mini-Season) of Fortnite: Battle Royale. The season started on November 3rd 2023 and concluded on December 2nd 2023 with the The Big Bang Live Event.

The season was themed around traveling back to Chapter 1, starting in Chapter 1: Season 5 and ending with Season X. The slogan is Fortnite: OG.

It was the fifth and final season of Chapter 4.

Previously, in Fortnite...

Previously, in Fortnite...[]

In Chapter 4: Season 3, The Ancients, as well as Optimus Prime, tried to warn Dr. Slone about the arrival of formidable enemies and an impending event that would collapse Reality Zero for good. However, Slone and the The Explorers rebuilt the Apparatus, which prophesied the eminent arrival of The Eclipse.

The first enemy to arrive, tied to the Eclipse, turned out to be a man known as Kado Thorne, a revenant vampire with a Rift-powered Time Machine who brought his estates (Eclipsed Estate, Sanguine Suites, and Relentless Retreat) and Guards to the Island. Kado used his Rift-powered Time Machine to collect artifacts from different times and events throughout the seasons of Fortnite: Battle Royale such as the Golden Egg, Mythic Goldfish, Midas' Drum Gun, The Foundation's MK-Seven Assault Rifle, Oro's Skull, as well as other items such as Kit's Shockwave Launcher or Ocean's Bottomless Chug Jug.

Optimus Prime's words made Slone come to a conclusion: She can use the Time Machine to return to the past of an alternate timeline version of Reality Zero (in other world a Reality very similar to Reality Zero) and to provoke The End once more. But this time, to control The Black Hole in order to generate a Big Bang creating new realities in the year 2018, so that Asteria's inhabitants in the year 2023 can escape into these new realities before Reality Zero collapses. Slone called for a professional robber and Heist Crew leader named Nolan Chance, who she thinks can help her steal the Time Machine from Kado. Slone also managed to recruit The Looper to assist with the Eclipsed Estate Heist. Nolan and The Looper managed to get Antonia, Fish Thicc, Piper Pace, Mae and Khaby Lame to also join the crew for the Eclisped Estate Heist.

Around two months later, during Fortnitemares 2023, the Eclipsed Estate Heist ended in failure, with the crew being ambushed by Kado in his Revenant form. Fortunately, the crew was able to successfully steal the Time Machine from Kado, due to The Looper and the Vampire Hunter Shimmerdusk taking him out.

They stashed the Time Machine at Frenzy Fields, just outside of The Getaway, where Slone and Jones started to study and experiment on it. They used the machine to bring many items from Season 5 as a test, such as the Tomato Temple head, Durrr Burger head, Dusty Diner Sign and the Truck N' Oasis sign. Eventually these items disappeared from Frenzy Fields with Jones and the Time Machine itself, with the Season 5 items appearing in the real world.

Leadup to the OG

Leadup to the OG[]

Jones used the Time Machine to travel in the past of an Alternative Timeline of Reality Zero at the beginning of Season 5 for an extraction mission. However this past seems to have been already altered by Thorne's time travels as there are notable differences and anachronisms, a sign of several time travels. Certain iconic characters don't exist and their roles are filled by other characters:

  • Here the Renegade Raider is none other than Lynx.
  • Ragnarok is the actor who played the role of the super villain Omega.
  • The master of SoFDeeZ's ice cream is not Lil Whip but Peely instead.
  • Raven as well as Rex do not exist and their roles were given respectively to Cuddle Team Leader, Brite Gunner and Aura. They are respectively known as Raven Team Leader, Brite Hunter and Saura.

Other elements have subtly changed and anachronisms are present but any change in the past of this Reality similar to Reality Zero will affect the future of the Omniverse, so what does the future hold?

Will Jones succeed in his mission to save the inhabitants of Reality Zero from The Island's collapse?

The End of Chapter 4

The End of Chapter 4[]

Main Article: The Big Bang

The Rocket at Dusty Divot is launched and the events played out just as it did in The End. However, some reality glitches happened to the rocket and the meteor due to the time machine being attached to it and affecting it. Athena was sucked into the black hole created by The Zero Point. However, unlike last time, it violently blooms and an explosion occurs that create plenty of new Galaxies each containing new worlds, allowing the inhabitants of Asteria to escape in one of the new worlds before The Island collapses.

This marks a new beginning...

Season OG Story

Season OG Story[]

Jones, after using the Time Machine receives messages from Slone. She informs Jones that he isn't there to change the past but to do an extraction, and Slone tells Jones that whatever Jones does will ripple forward to Slone's exact moment in the present. Slone sends another message to Jones, but she doesn't remember if she sent a message before or not. The Past changes even if Jones didn't mean for it to happen, The Cube does not implode, instead staying above Loot Lake where the Zero Point is located. More changes have also happened with the Ice Moon coming onto the island much earlier than originally, but this time The Ice King never arrived on the Island, meaning The Prisoner is also absent, which prevents him from creating the Volcano along with the Jungle on the northeastern side of The Island, although The Devourer had already broke out from the Ice Moon chunk. Slone sends another message to Jones and she tells Jones that they should say goodbye to the current versions of themselves, as they're heading into the unknown. The Rocket had finished construction at Dusty Divot by this time, with the Time Machine attached to it.

One big plot hole within the story is why The Cube (alongside the Floating Island) isn't present during The Big Bang.

OG Pass

OG Pass[]

The Battle Pass, for this season named the OG Pass, costs 950 V-Bucks, with an additional 25-Level Boost offer for 750 V-Bucks available via the Item Shop, only providing 10 levels instead of 25. If you own a Fortnite Crew subscription, you will unlock the OG Pass for free.

Battle Pass levels require 80,000 or up to 50 levels can be bought for 150 V-Bucks each.

Map Changes[]

Update v27.00

November 3rd 2023

  • Agent Jones utilized the time machine to journey back to 2018, returning to the original island for an extraction mission.
  • The original Island from Season 5 has returned, bringing all old locations;
    • Dusty Divot
    • Fatal Fields
    • Flush Factory
    • Greasy Grove
    • Haunted Hills
    • Junk Junction
    • Lazy Links
    • Lonely Lodge
    • Loot Lake
    • Lucky Landing
    • Paradise Palms
    • Pleasant Park
    • Retail Row
    • Risky Reels
    • Salty Springs
    • Shifty Shafts
    • Snobby Shores
    • Tilted Towers
    • Tomato Temple
    • Wailing Woods
  • At Pleasant Park's football/soccer field, Agent Jones arrived with the time machine. The broken seat lay beside it, accompanied by the Battle Bus balloon, a recording from Doctor Slone, and a truck on the other side.
  • Ziplines and Rifts have been added around the Island for easier access.
  • Reboot Vans have been added around the Island.

November 9th 2023

  • The Cube emerged from Loot Lake's waters, lifting Loot Lodge on its back to form the Floating Island.
  • The truck at Pleasant Park has begun to move, but slipped on a banana peel and crashed at the shore on the north of Loot Lake.
    • Jones crossed the lake, establishing a makeshift camp on the opposite shore of Loot Lake with the Time Machine and another Slone recording.
  • A crane and trucks have appeared at Dusty Divot, beginning to move out furniture and paintings to make way for a construction.
    • The ceiling and a pit have been opened up at the center of Dusty Diner, a second floor was also built on the outside of the building.

Update v27.10

November 16th 2023

  • The Iceberg has once again collided with the Island, creating a snow biome on the southwest corner of the Island, adding Frosty Flights to the Island.
    • Polar Peak is already in it's destroyed state.
  • Blackheart's crew has once again invaded the Island, creating several pirate outposts around the Island.
  • Jones has arrived at Dusty Diner with the Time Machine and another Slone recording.
  • Furniture has been further packed and moved out of Dusty Diner's red warehouse, as the interior has been transformed from a diner into a lab.
    • The Rocket was built at the center of Dusty Diner, as more scaffolding was built around it and a second floor was added inside the building
  • A blue glow and waves started coming out of the vortex underneath the Floating Island in Loot Lake.

Update v27.11

November 21st 2023

  • An 11-day countdown has appeared above the rocket at Dusty Divot.

November 23rd 2023

  • The Meteor has returned, suspended over the island.
  • Everything that was packed within Dusty Diner has been moved out and placed inside containers outside of the building.
    • The gift shop in Dusty Diner became a laboratory as a result. The roof was transformed into a platform.
  • Another Slone recording can be found within Dusty Diner.
  • The Time Machine has been attached to the rocket.
  • The Zero Point has emerged at Loot Lake, underneath the Floating Island.

    • Dog tags with Chapter 1 outfits imprinted on them.

  • The Chapter 4: Season OG keyart is a reference to the Season 1 keyart, as well the Battle Royale Loading Screen.
    • However, it can be noted that the Season 1 Keyart appears to loosely take place between Dusty Depot and Retail Row, while the Season OG Keyart takes place inside Pleasant Park.
    • The keyart showed Chapter 1 models of weapons, though in-game the weapons' current icon and models are used.
    • In the Season 1 keyart, Jonesy was seen behind a wooden wall, while in the Season OG keyart, Jonesy is actually shown peeking out of said wall.
