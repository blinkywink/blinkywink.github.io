Blast Off Fortnite 1st Birthday
Season 5, with the slogan Worlds Collide, was the fifth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale. It started on July 12th 2018. It was scheduled to end on September 24th 2018 but was extended after Epic Games had extended the season to end with the 1 year anniversary of Fortnite being Free to Play.

The theme revolved around world collision, following the aftermath of Blast Off, which resulted in things being taken from other realities and put into Reality: Zero.

Previously, in Fortnite...

Previously, in Fortnite...[]

The Crack in the Sky created by the Blast Off event connected Reality Zero directly to other realities, and within days before Season 5 began, rifts began forming around the island, rifting away objects from the island and bringing in new ones from other realities.

At the beginning of the season, entire locations from different realities were brought to the island through the Crack in the Sky. Moisty Mire was replaced with a giant desert, along with a large resort town, a mexican-like town, scrapyard, and a cafe. The dirt racetrack to the east of Retail Row was revamped and upgraded to a full-scale concrete motorcourse track. Two more major changes occurred to the island. The farm Anarchy Acres was replaced with a luxury golf club called Lazy Links, and a large Viking Village was dropped on top of a mountain near Snobby Shores, complete with a large viking ship. Dusty Divot became overgrown with vegetation, and the research facility there started to fall apart and decay, same with the Hero Mansion and Villain Lair. The Durr Burger head that was transported into the real world was, again, sucked through a rift and transported back to the island, and could have been found on top of a hill near Pleasant Park. Around each of the affected areas, rifts could have been found, which transported loopers high up into the sky when entered.

The Season 5 Storyline

The Season 5 Storyline[]

After the Crack in the Sky brought the locations from different realities into the Island, it started shrinking and closing, and in the process, becoming purple and sending lightning strikes in a mountain east of Fatal Fields in the Desert. When the Crack in the Sky was nearly done closing it sent it's last lightning strike, which was the longest, bringing in a Cube, then finally closing.

This Cube started rolling around the Island, burning runes into the soil of the Island with the purpose of gathering power, then sinking into the Loot Lake, where the Zero Point was, so it could start the next phase of it's plan in Season 6.

At the same time, a group of Enforcers likely working for the IO, started retrieving objects previously rifted away back to the Island, and closing those rifts.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass cost 950 V-Bucks, with an additional 25-Level Boost offered for 2,800 V-Bucks available via the Item Shop.

Season 5's Battle Pass introduced a new cosmetic type: Toys. Since this season, you can complete outfit challenges even after the season ends. The Omega and Carbide challenges were locked, however.

Map Updates[]

Update v5.00

July 12th 2018

  • Moisty Mire was replaced by the Desert, bringing in a new city with it, Paradise Palms, located at the place of the Stagecoach originally rifted into Moisty Mire.
  • Added new Unnamed Locations:
    • Dino Park
    • Junkyard
    • Pueblo
    • The Burger
    • Cacti Forest
    • Trucker's Oasis
    • The Birthplace
    • Roadside Diner
    • Calamity's Residence
  • Prison, Convenience Store, Circle of Hedges, Wooden Crab, and Truck Coast were removed along with Moisty Mire.
  • The Race Track southeast of Retail Row was revamped with a proper concrete race track.
  • A fancy golf club named Lazy Links was rifted onto the Island, replacing Anarchy Acres.
  • The abandoned Dusty Divot was overgrown with grass, trees and small ponds.
  • Viking Village was rifted to the summit of a mountain near Greasy Grove.
  • Open Soccer Stadium was removed.
  • Brick Fox was removed
  • Missing NOMS sign appeared north east of Risky Reels.
  • The missing Durrr Burger head from Greasy Grove's Durrr Burger Restaurant got rifted back in. It can be found on a hill east of Pleasant Park.
  • Goodbye signs were put out on the roof and the parking lot of the Pizza Pit in Tomato Town.
    • Similar signs can be found on the roof and sidewalk of the Durrr Burger in Greasy Grove.
  • 7 Moai Heads were added around the Island.
  • Most of the movie sets were removed.
  • Ramps were added in many places around the Island.
  • The Fork Knife food truck moved to Paradise Palms.

Update v5.10

July 24th 2018

  • The drive-in section has been lined up with cars. Flipped cars and destroyed houses' roofs were fixed.
  • A tombstone was added east of Paradise Palms to pay homage to the "greatest rescue mission in Fortnite[1]".
  • Trucks have appeared between Salty Springs and Dusty Divot.
  • Birthday Cakes have appeared around the map in celebration of Fortnite's 1st Birthday:
    • Fatal Fields
    • Flush Factory
    • Pleasant Park
    • Loot Lake
    • Lazy Links
    • Greasy Grove
    • Lonely Lodge
    • Paradise Palms
    • Northern Houses

Update v5.20

  • The truck has moved to the western side of Dusty Divot.
  • The Crack in the Sky has shrunk a bit. Parts of it are less bright and one of the trails is barely visible.
  • Birthday Cakes were removed.

  • The Motel sign was rifted back in. (As shown in the Signing Back In Loading Screens)

Crack Closure
