Overview

This article is about the third season of Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 3, see Season 3 (disambiguation).

Season 3, with the slogan Meteor Strike, was the third season of Fortnite: Battle Royale. It started on February 22nd 2018 and ended on April 30th 2018. The season's theme revolved around space exploration.

The game's userbase increased[Citation needed] during this season as Battle Royale was publicly released for iOS on April 2, 2018.[1]

Storyline

Storyline[]

About halfway through the season, a blue object appeared in the sky above Athena Island, which was later revealed to be a meteor approaching the island. As time went on, telescopes were set up on high vantage points, such as hills and mountains, to observe the meteor. Players began to theorize that the meteor would eventually strike Tilted Towers.

As the season started coming to a close, small camps appeared on a building in Tilted Towers, followed by televisions around the island broadcasting static with unintelligible audio. For the last few days of the season, small meteor fragments would crash into the island starting towards the middle of matches, progressively getting worse as the match went on.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass cost 950 V-Bucks, with an additional 25-Level Boost offered for 2,800 V-Bucks available via the Item Shop.

Season 3's Battle Pass introduced 3 new cosmetic types: Contrails, Back Blings, and Loading Screens.

  • Happy Chinese New Year! Props were added.

March 20th 2018

  • The Meteor has appeared in the sky of the island.
    • It gets closer to the island as time passes

Update v3.40

March 28th 2018

  • Vending Machines were added across the map.

March 29th 2018

  • Telescope camps appeared around the island, looking towards the meteor.

Update v3.50

April 11th 2018

  • Dancefloors were added in 3 points on the Island:
    • Pleasant Manor
    • Flush Factory
    • RV Park

Update v3.60

April 24th 2018

  • A small camp appeared on a building in Tilted Towers with signs with a UFO drawing and text that read "Today Tomorrow".

April 25th 2018

  • Televisions around the island began broadcasting a test-pattern graphic of a Llama and unintelligible audio.

April 26th 2018

  • Meteorites began falling from the sky during matches.
    • The amount of meteorites would increase as the match progressed.
New Features & Changes

New Features & Changes[]

As of Content Update v3.40 (April 4th, 2018), Epic Games introduced non-downloadable updates which released every other week which were referred to as Content Updates. These were introduced to give Epic Games more time to fix more complex bugs, with the first Content Update releasing on April 4th, 2018.
