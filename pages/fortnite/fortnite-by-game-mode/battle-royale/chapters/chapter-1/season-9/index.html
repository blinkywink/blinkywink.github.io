Season 9, with the slogan The Future Is Yours, was the ninth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale. It started on May 9th 2019, and ended on July 31st 2019.

The theme revolved around the futuristic aesthetics and reconstruction of various locations that were destroyed by the Volcano during The Unvaulting.

How did it Start?

How did it Start?[]

The explosive eruption of the Volcano brought forth devastating damage to locations on the Island, causing the Volcano to collapse on itself and become a caldera, leaving Tilted Towers and Retail Row in ruin.

However Singularity, the Guardian of The Zero Point, helped rebuild the locations that previously faced destruction. They re-emerged from the ashes as Neo Tilted and Mega Mall, powered by The Zero Point itself. The Imagined Order took over the remains of the Volcano, building a Thermonuclear Power Plant known as Pressure Plant within it.

The Season 9 Storyline

The Season 9 Storyline[]

The damage from the impact of the Volcano's volcanic rock to the Polar Peak seemed minor at first, but as time passed, the crack in the iceberg worsened, and within the ice awakened something dangerous. Over the course of the next few weeks, the ice at Polar Peak began cracking apart and melting even further, eventually revealing a giant eye belonging to a beast known as "The Devourer", a powerful monster locked away and frozen by The Ice King.

After a few weeks, the starving Devourer broke free from Polar Peak and walked towards the ocean, leaving giant footprints behind. Bearing the Polar Peak castle on its back, it dove into the waters and started circling around the Island. It occasionally stepped back onto the Island, eating buildings from Pleasant Park and Loot Lake to try and satiate its hunger, once again leaving giant footprints behind.

Following these destructive events, Singularity partnered with the Imagined Order to use Pressure Plant to construct the Mecha Team Leader to fight the monster. Pressure Plant was soon taken apart, and a new layout built for the construction of the Mecha. Meanwhile, around the Island, holographic propaganda signs began appearing in areas to help promote support for the construction of the Mech.

Mecha Team Leader had been completed after two weeks of construction. The mech was painted in a range of colors, and the countdown for The Final Showdown appeared.

The Final Showdown

The Final Showdown[]

When the countdown for The Final Showdown reached zero, The Devourer stepped back onto the Island once more from the north of Lazy Lagoon, sweeping a pirate camp out of its way and blasting its surroundings with a huge beam of energy radiating from its mouth in a show of power. The monster proceeded to Loot Lake, where it attempted to punch through the Vault to get its hands on the orb of power beneath. It was at this moment that the mech came to life, piloted by The Paradigm, a member of The Seven, and distracted the monster from The Vault. After the robot hit the Devourer with a series of missiles, the creature retaliated by blasting the mech with an energy beam. After recovering from the strong retaliatory attack by the monster, the robot sprinted towards the monster and boosted into it, lifting it into the air and off the island into the water. After a short time, the robot emerged from the water and landed back onto the map, seemingly victorious. However, the moment was short-lived as the beast leapt out of the water and tackled the robot, forcing it to the ground. In the ensuing carnage, the Mecha managed to throw the Devourer off of it, but was extremely damaged with its left arm severed, causing it to collapse onto Loot Lake. As the monster got back on its feet, Paradigm realized that if she failed, The Devourer would break into the Vault and consume the Zero Point, causing an end-of-the-world scenario. In a desperate last resort, the Mecha broke through the Vault's hatch with a punch, tearing the Zero Point from containment. It then proceeded to crack its outer shell, absorbing its energy. When the Devourer gathered the strength to strike once more, the mech set the Zero Point down above the Vault, and delivered a hard punch powered by Zero Point Energy, sending it tumbling backwards. With its newfound power, Mecha approached the Singularity statue at Neo Tilted and gripped it, pulling it out. As the monster slowly recovered and got back on its feet, the statue was revealed to be the handle of a sword called The Sword of Singularity. Now, the Mecha powered the sword with The Zero Point energy and faced the monster in a final showdown. As the two launched at each other, the mech stabbed the sword through The Devourer's head, finally defeating it once and for all. The monster's corpse quickly decomposed leaving behind its skeletal remains. Mecha Team Leader, victorious, Flossed on the monster's skeleton in a moment of celebration. It saluted the Loopers, and then boosted into the sky and disappeared along with The Paradigm, later crashing into the Ice Moon.

However, the battle left behind a major problem. The Zero Point was now growing unstable above the heavily damaged Vault. Within the next few days, the Zero Point began cracking further, starting to pulse and radiate with immense energy. This energy altered time for a tree nearby, causing its leaves to change from green to an autumn yellow.

Teasers[]

The first cinematic trailer features Peely and Jonesy fleeing from the erupting Volcano towards a bunker, just as the doors were closing, the duo lunged inside. The bunker lit up, revealing an arcade. The two hung out for a while before noticing that the bunker had no food. After seemingly a lot of time, Vega, Sentinel, and Rox arrive. Sentinel pries open the bunker's doors, and the trio is met with Jonesy, now Bunker Jonesy, still inside with an overgrown beard and torn clothes, holding Peely who he made into a banana smoothie to survive. The trio introduce Peely and Jonesy to the new world as Jonesy steps outside in awe.
