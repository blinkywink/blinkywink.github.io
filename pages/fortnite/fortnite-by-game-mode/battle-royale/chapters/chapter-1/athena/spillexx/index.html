History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.00: Spillexx is introduced as a gas/oil/fuel company that is involved in the creation of The Rig, as well as providing fuel for it.
  • Update v12.20: The Rig has blown up and been converted to a SHADOW-operated base. The oil Spillexx has provided has spilled into the ocean.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00:
    • The Rig has become Rickety Rig. It is unknown if the location is still operated by Spillexx but the company's logo remains regardless.
    • Containers being the Spillexx logo have begun appearing around the map, likely following the destruction of The Rig and the flooding of the island.
  • Update v13.40:
    • The Gas Stations around Apollo use gas provided by Spillexx, as the pumps bear the name of the company.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: Rickety Rig has disappeared.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Four gas stations were added to the map - one west of Logjam Lumberyard, one northwest of Condo Canyon, one adjacent to Butter Barn, and one east of The Daily Bugle.
    • Advertisements may be found at Chonker's Speedway as well.
  • Winterfest 2021:
    • The Gas Station near Logjam Lumberyard was decorated for Christmas and is the site of Santa's Workshop - a location quoted by a Winterfest Quest.
  • Content Update v19.01 # 1: All Christmas decorations have been removed from the Gas Station near Logjam Lumberyard.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: The Condo Canyon station is under occupation by the Imagined Order.
  • Update v20.20: Seeing as how Condo Canyon was liberated by The Seven, the station, too, is also now under their control.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.10: Butter Bloom replaces Butter Barn; a Spillexx gas station is found here. The station remains here when the location is converted to Neo Butter Barn.


Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: The entire island is destroyed, along with every other Spillexx installment on the island.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

  • Update v23.00: Several new installments of Spillexx gas stations can be found around the newly constructed map of Asteria. One such large gas station can be found near the center of the map.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

  • Update v24.00: A new biome with MEGA City is rifted into the island, and with it comes another gas station connected to the city. Fallow Fuel is also added as an additional station on the map, and advertising can be found at Drift Ridge.


Chapter 4: Season 3[]

  • Update v25.00: Following Earthquakes at the end of the previous season, a Jungle Biome emerges, destroying Slap 'N Go into Slap N' Gone.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

  • Update v30.00: added Redline Rig - a Nitro refinery owned by Spillexx and run by The Machinist.
