Fork Knife Food Truck is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. It is located inside the co-ordinate F5 - North of Lazy Lake.
It is a Fork Knife Food Truck, previously seen on Athena, along with some outside decor.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00
    • A single Fork Knife Food Truck was added to the map as a Landmark, and was named appropriately.
  • Update v11.31
    • December 25th 2019: The Truck was covered in snow for Christmas day.
    • January 9th 2020: The snow at Fork Knife Food Truck has melted.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.10
    • The area around the Truck is almost covered in snow.
    • January 6th 2021: There is no more snow.
  • Update v15.00
    • A Bounty Board was added near the Truck.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00
    • The truck and area has been surrounded by "red soil" - The Primal Region.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00
    • The Primal Region has disappeared.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00
    • The Brat spawns here as a Character.
  • Content Update v18.40
    • November 29th: The Cube Queen's corruption of the island has covered the landmark.
  • The End
    • Fork Knife Food Truck, along with the entirety of Apollo, is flipped - thus removing the landmark from the game.

Fork Knife Food Truck is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Season 4 to the island Athena. They all are food trucks from the company Fork Knife, and can be found in many locations around the map.

History

History[]

Season 4[]

  • Update v4.00: Fork Knife Food Trucks have appeared in many places on Athena as Unnamed Locations.

Season 6[]

  • Week 4: A Fork Knife Food Truck appeared in Fatal Fields.

Season 8[]

  • The Unvaulting Event: The Fork Knife Food Truck at Retail Row was destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Season X[]

  • Update v10.00: All Fork Knife Food Trucks disappear inside the Black Hole as a result of The End Event.
