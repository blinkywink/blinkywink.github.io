Lazy Links was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that was added in Season 5 to the island Athena. Lazy Links was a luxurious golf course, characterized by vast green lawns, pool, and elegant buildings.

  • Halloween decorations have been added to Lazy Links for the Fortnitemares event.

Update v6.21[]

  • Halloween decorations been removed from Lazy Links.

  • January 31st 2019: The snow melted.
