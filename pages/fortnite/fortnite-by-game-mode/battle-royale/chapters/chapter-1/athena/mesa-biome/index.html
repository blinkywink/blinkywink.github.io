Overview

This article is about the Battle Royale biome. For the Save the World biome, see Desert.
The subject of this article currently doesn't have an official name.

The Mesa Biome, usually known as the Desert Biome, is a biome in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Season 5 in the southeast side of the map. It is a large desert.

History

History[]

Season 5[]

  • A Rift summoned the Mesa Biome with a New Named Location, which is Paradise Palms, replacing Moisty Mire. Later The Cube was summoned on a Mountain in Mesa and later left.

Season 7[]

  • An Expedition Camp was added.
  • Sofdeez was added replacing Roadside Diner.
  • Snow appeared temporarily for Christmas and the Ice Storm Event.
  • An Earthquake struck next to Sofdeez.

Season 8[]

  • A Pirate Camp appeared.
  • The Expedition Outposts were removed.

Season 9[]

  • The Pirate Camp was removed.
  • A Sky Platform was added.

Season X[]

  • A Cube Memorial appeared.
  • The Sky Platform was removed.
  • Pandora was added temporarily.
  • Moisty Mire merged with Paradise Palms and became Moisty Palms.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • The Mesa was removed.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • The Mesa returned at the southern part of Artemis, partially covered with snow that is now melting to reveal the rest of the biome.
  • Three New Named Locations can be found: Rocky Reels, Chonker's Speedway and Condo Canyon.
  • Butter Barn has also returned from the Zero Point Desert to the Mesa Desert. Mancake can be found as a character and his Barn has been revamped and has now more buildings.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • The Seven built Synapse Station.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • The Butter Barn has transformed into Butter Bloom.
  • A bit of the Mesa has transformed into the Reality Bloom Biome.
  • Butter Bloom later transformed into Neo Butter Barn.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • The Butter Barn has been protected by No Sweat Insurance and is in the sky along with Cloudy Condos.
  • Chrome has spread to the Mesa.
  • Neo Butter Barn has transformed into Flutter Barn and is now a Named Location.
  • In the Fracture Live Event, The Mesa biome was destroyed by The Herald and put back together.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

  • The Mesa Biome has returned.
  • Primo Pumps is now part of the Mesa Biome.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

  • The Mesa Biome has been removed.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Sand Biome or the Zero Point Desert Biome added in Chapter 2: Season 5 is similar to the Mesa Biome but with pure Zero Point matter sand.
  • The Nitro Wasteland Biome added in Chapter 5: Season 3 is extremely similar to the Chapter 3 desert biome, having similar geographical features and vegetation around the middle and being located in the same area of the map.
  • Despite appearing in Fracture, it never showed up on the Chapter 4 Island.
