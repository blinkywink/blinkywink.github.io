Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.
Assets from this POI are available in UEFN/Creative
Assets from this POI are available in UEFN and Fortnite Creative inside of Prefabs and Galleries.

Mega Mall was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale, that was added in Season 9 to the island Athena. Mega Mall was a mall area with stores and a residential area.

History

History[]

  • S9
  • SX

Update v9.00[]

  • Mega Mall was added to Athena.

Update v9.30[]

June 20th 2019

  • Icecream Cones were added to Mega Mall.

July 3rd 2019

  • Many portals from Stranger Things appeared at Mega Mall.

July 6th 2019

  • Propaganda and warning holograms have appeared at Mega Mall.

July 8th 2019

  • Stranger Things' portals disappear.

Update v10.00[]

  • The propaganda and warning posters have been removed.

Update v10.10[]

August 11th 2019

  • A Rift Beacon is being constructed.

August 12th 2019

  • The Rift Beacon has been activated, and a small rift has been opened in the sky above Mega Mall.

August 14th 2019

  • Mega Mall was turned into Retail Row.
