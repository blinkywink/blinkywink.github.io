Tilted Town was a location in the west-midlands of the Fortnite map. It replaced Neo Tilted by the first Rift Beacon in Season X. Entering the location restricts the ability to build or break anything to match Wild West technology in the past.

The 8th Rift Beacon was placed there. On September 21, 2019, the beacon transformed Tilted Town into Gotham City at the beginning of the Fortnite X Batman crossover.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Tilted Town is a wild-west themed location with dirt roads, an l-shaped river, and stables. It is located inside its own sphere of influence like the orb in Loot Lake. In this sphere of influence, you cannot build or break structures. Any item that can destroy structures will not work here.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Tilted Town is assumed to be the past of the original Tilted Towers, as this season seems to be about time travel.
    • However, there was nothing there before Tilted Towers was originally added, so Tilted Town could have been created by a Reality Wave.
  • Sounds related to Tilted Town are codenamed Western.
  • Once you enter the bubble around it, your Outfit is automatically given a cowboy jacket like Calamity and Deadfire. This also works for Recruits.
    • Though if you were a outfit such as Catalyst or Drift you would be turned into calamity (female) or Deadfire (male) insted of just getting the cowboy jacket.
  • Wild West weapons could have been found such as the Six Shooter and the Double Barrel Shotgun.
  • Buildings from Canny Valley Ghost Town were used for Tilted Town.
