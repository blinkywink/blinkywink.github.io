History

History[]

Season 8[]

  • The Jungle Biome was added.

Season 9[]

  • The Volcano was replaced by Pressure Plant.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • The Jungle Biome was removed.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • The Jungle Biome returned enclosed within Stealthy Stronghold to contain the Predator, a hunter lured into Stealthy Stronghold by Agent Jones.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Deep Woods Dish was added.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • The Jungle Biome was Flipped and Submerged.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • In the Fracture Live Event, Stealthy Stronghold's Jungle biome was destroyed by The Herald offscreen along with Apollo.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

  • A Rainforest was added to Asteria.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 1[]

  • A Soft Jungle was added to Oninoshima.
