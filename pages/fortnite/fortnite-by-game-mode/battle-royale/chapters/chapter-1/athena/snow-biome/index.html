Overview

The Snow Biome refers to areas covered with snow in Fortnite: Battle Royale. In addition to the areas covered with snow temporarily due to an event, there are three other subcategories:

  • The Ice biome which appeared for the first time on the southwest side of Athena added in Season 7, it's a large area of ice and snow, there are notably frozen lakes.
  • The Snowy Mountain biome which initially appeared very minimally at the Viking Village added during Season 5 and appeared completely on the southeast side of Apollo added in Chapter 2: Season 1. This biome is a classic mountainous area simply covered with snow, without ice, the rivers and lakes remain normal.
  • The Boreal Snow and Boreal Tundra biomes which were added to the island of Helios in Chapter 5: Season 1.

Season 5[]

  • The mountain where the Viking Village is located is covered in snow.

Season 6[]

  • Polar Peak Iceberg was seen to the south of Flush Factory, getting closer and closer to the island.

Season 7[]

  • The Iceberg crushed Flush Factory turning the South West side of Athena into an Ice Biome with snow.
  • The snow gradually spread beyond the Ice Biome.
  • The snow covered entirely twice and melted afterwards.

Season 8[]

  • More snow has melted, but much of it still exceeds the ice extents.

Season 9[]

  • The Devourer destroyed Polar Peak and escaped.

Season X[]

  • Greasy Grove returned removing more snow.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Snowy Mountain biome is added on the top of mountains.
  • Snow covered the map for Winterfest 2019

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • All the snow melted exept on the top of mountains.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Snow covered the entire mountain biome on the South East during Operation: Snowdown, it was removed after the event ended.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • A large part of Artemis was covered in snow during Winterfest 2021.
  • The biome was reduced to the top left corner of Artemis, becoming simple Snowy Mountain biome.
  • Covert Cavern is added.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Covert Cavern became Command Cavern.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Command Cavern transformed into Rave Cave.
  • Due to Logjam Lumberyard transforming into Logjam Lotus, the snow around it has vanished.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Chrome arrives in the snow biome.
  • Shifty Shafts gets replaced with Grim Gables.
  • In the Fracture Live Event, both Snow biomes from Apollo and Artemis were destroyed.

Chapter 4[]

  • The Zero Point pulls in a large part of the snow and ice biome.
  • The Jungle has destroyed part of the Ice Biome, exposing Cold Cavern
  • Sanguine Suites has been added, removing more of the snow, Lonely Labs and a few landmarks

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

  • The Boreal Forest and Boreal Tundra is convered with snow during Winterfest 2023
  • After Winterfest 2023, the snow has melted making Rebel's Roost, Lavish Lair, and parts of Classy Courts and Hazy Hillside no longer covered with snow.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

  • The Olympus biome has been added, thus removing a large part of the snow biome and Hazy Hillside entirely

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

  • The Forged Forest biome has been added, removing a small part of the snow biome with Castle Doom.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

  • Added Oninoshima along with the snow biome.
  • A huge portion of the map has been covered with snow for Winterfest.
  • The snow has been reverted to its original state.

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

  • A small part of the Snow Biome has been removed due to the addition of the Golden Hills Biome.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 2[]

  • The Snow Biome has been removed due to the removal of Oninoshima.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

  • Added Golden Coast along with the Snow Biome.
  • A portion of the map has been covered with snow for Winterfest.

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

  • Added the Ice Biome.
  • The Snow Biome now invades more of the map.
