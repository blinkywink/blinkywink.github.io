Overview

This article is about the second season of Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 2, see Season 2 (disambiguation).

Season 2, with the slogan Fort Knights, was the second season of Fortnite: Battle Royale. It started on December 14th 2017, and ended on February 21st 2018. The season's theme revolved around knights and medieval times.

New in the Season

New in the Season

Season 2 introduces Victory Umbrellas and the Battle Pass which replaced the Season Shop. The Battle Pass has a free row of tiers which give players rewards every few levels, or the player could spend V-Bucks to purchase the premium Battle Pass to unlock premium rewards which are given every level.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass

The Battle Pass cost 950 V-Bucks, unlocking up to 65+ rewards worth 12,000 V-Bucks.

Update v1.11

December 13th 2017

  • Christmas Trees have appeared in few Named Locations:
    • Retail Row
    • Fatal Fields
    • Pleasant Park (Replaced gazebo)
    • Loot Lake
    • Anarchy Acres
    • Greasy Grove
    • Wailing Woods
    • Spawn Island
  • Bushes were covered in christmas lights.

Update v2.2.0

January 18th 2018

  • The Island went through a major revamp.
  • Added 5 new Named Locations:
    • Tilted Towers
    • Shifty Shafts
    • Snobby Shores
    • Haunted Hills
    • Junk Junction
  • Added new Unnamed Locations:
    • Convenience Store
    • Starry Suburbs Ruins
    • Sports Center
    • Motel
    • Umbrella Mine
    • Metal Llama
    • Yonder Yard
    • Outskirts
    • Blurry Billboards
  • Added new biomes:
    • Boreal Forest
    • Northern Houses
    • Forest
    • Swamp
  • Giant Chair is now bigger.
  • Tomato Tunnel now includes an emergency staircase.
  • Added some more huts around the Island.
  • Pleasant Manor was updated.
  • Christmas Trees and bush christmas lights were removed.

Update v2.50

February 13th 2018

  • Shrines have appeared across the map.
