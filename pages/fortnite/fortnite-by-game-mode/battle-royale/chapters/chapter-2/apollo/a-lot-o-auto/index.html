Overview


A Lot O' Auto is a car and boat dealership in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Lazy Lake (along with A Lot O' Auto branch) was added to the map.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00: The Drop Shop A Lot O' Auto branch was added to the map.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: The Drop Shop A Lot O' Auto was slightly damaged from a rock created by The Zero Point.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.20: The A Lot O' Auto branch has upgraded with a garage for the new Off-Road Tires.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • The End: The A Lot O' Auto branch was turned upside down during the flipping of the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.10: The A Lot O' Auto branch was added to Tilted Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.40:
    • The A Lot O' Auto branch was removed due to Tilted Towers' disappearance.
    • The A Lot O' Auto branch was added to Tainted Towers.
  • Fracture: The A Lot O' Auto branch at Tainted Towers was destroyed by The Herald.
