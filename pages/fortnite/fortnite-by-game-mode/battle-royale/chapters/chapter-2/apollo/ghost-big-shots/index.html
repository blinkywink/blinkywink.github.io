Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.
The subject of this article currently doesn't have an official name.

GHOST Big Shots is an Unnamed Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 2 to the island Apollo. It was a branch of the coffee chain Big Shots, notably littered with white mugs donning the GHOST logo on them.
The building itself had begun construction in Season 2 of Chapter 2, and was completed in Season 5. It was the only branch of the coffee chain's buildings to have said mugs, making this unique.

It is located west of Catty Corner, yet is not to be confused with the Big Shots on the premises of Catty Corner.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • GHOST Big Shots bears similarities with another unnamed location, Hut Watch, which was given its unofficial name by a series from the YouTube channel PlaystationGrenade. It bears the similarity because the host, Adamaru, would check the watch tower to see if it had been changed or updated in any way every update. Like Hut Watch, this location also was changed almost every update.
    • This would later lead to GHOST Big Shots also being dubbed "Hut Watch" as both a spiritual successor to the original location, and because the file names for the location were named "Hut Watch" in reference to the original Chapter 1 location.
  • This became one of the final locations on Apollo with any GHOST faction props present, with the others being Sharky Shell, Crash Site and Defiant Dish.
