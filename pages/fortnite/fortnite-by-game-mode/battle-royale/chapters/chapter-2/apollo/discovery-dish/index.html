Overview

This article is about the Chapter 2 Landmark. For the Season 8 Challenges, see Discovery Challenges. For the Glider, see Discovery.
This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Discovery Dish was a Landmark that was added in Chapter 2: Season 7. It can be found near Fort Crumpet to the west of Believer Beach. It is the 1st of 7 Satellite Stations.
