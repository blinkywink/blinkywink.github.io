The Vault of Doom was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale, added in Chapter 2: Season 4, on the island Apollo. It is located in co-ordinate D3, in Doom's Domain. It is one of the many Vaults.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.20
    • SHADOW Safe House - Alpha has a secret opening under Pleasant Park's soccer field. This area will later be used for the Vault of Doom.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00
    • The Safe House has vanished, and the soccer field has been resealed.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00
    • Vault of Doom has been enacted where the previous SHADOW Safe House's secret opening once was. Now, the landmark is in the middle of Doom's Domain. Doom Henchmen patrol the area and guard the vault, which can be opened with Doctor Doom's Keycard, dropped by Doctor Doom.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00
    • All traces of Doom's Domain are gone from Pleasant Park, including the Vault of Doom, which has since been resealed.
Loot

Loot[]

Inside the vault itself are approximately 5 Doom Chests that spawn, alongside 1 normal chest, approximately 4 Ammo Boxes and 1 Supply Drop.
