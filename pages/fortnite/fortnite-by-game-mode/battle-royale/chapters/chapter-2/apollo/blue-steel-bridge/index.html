Overview

Blue Steel Bridge was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale, added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. It is a large blue bridge that crosses a river leading to The Aftermath. The location can be found south of Craggy Cliffs, and east of Pleasant Park, inside coordinate E2. It is one of five Steel Bridges around the map.

Update v11.00[]

  • Blue Steel Bridge was added to the map.

Update v13.00[]

  • Blue Steel Bridge was partially flooded over

Update v13.20[]

  • July 1st, 2020 - The water level decreased and the upper portion of the bridge has been uncovered. The bottom part of the bridge, however, is still covered in water.
  • July 11th - The water level decreased again and the bottom part of the bridge can now be walked on.

Update v13.30[]

  • The Pass 'N' Gas Gas station was upgraded to fuel the newly added Cars.

Update v14.00[]

  • Most of Blue Steel Bridge was destroyed due to Upstate New York appearing. Only the west side remains.

Update v15.00[]

  • Upstate New York was teleported and all of Blue Steel Bridge is visible again

Update v16.00[]

  • The café of Blue Steel Bridge has been covered in red soil due to the primalization of the island.

Update v17.00[]

  • All red soil has been removed from the map, including the area around the café of Blue Steel Bridge.

Content Update v18.40 - 1[]

  • (November 29th 2021): - The Cube Queen's corruption of the island has covered the landmark.

  • In the Fracture Live Event,Blue Steel Bridge was destroyed by The Herald offscreen.
