Overview

This article is about the Landmark. For other uses, see Durrr Burger (disambiguation).

The Durrr Burger was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 5 to the island Apollo. It features the Durrr Burger restaurant from Greasy Grove that had combined and thus destroyed Logjam Woodworks. It is located South of Holly Hedges and West of Weeping Woods. Currently, much of the landscape here is corrupted to an orange color.

The restaurant appears to be quite run-down. There is lots of litter on the floors and it is quite poorly kept. The various posters on the walls indicate that the restaurant wasn't in a good state initially due to the fact that they are advertising their "special sauce' that contains unknown substances". It is unknown whether the restaurant is currently operational.

The Durrr Burger was removed after The End (Chapter 2). It did not made a return in Chapter 3 despite Greasy Grove returning because it has the Tacos restaurant instead.

After the events of The End, The Durr Burger alongside Apollo were flipped and removed.

Update v15.00[]

  • The Durr Burger is added to the island.
  • Beef Boss is found here as a Character.

Update v15.30[]

  • February 11th 2021 - A Leviathan head was temporarily appeared at one of the tables.

Update v16.00[]

  • Gutbomb now spawns as a Character here. You could have dueled him for an Epic Pump Shotgun.

Update v17.00[]

  • An Imagined Order camp was added alongside a Saucer in the Durrrive Thru area called Food Fighter.

Update v17.10[]

  • The IO camp is removed from Food Fighter, leaving only the Saucer. Alien Parasites are introduced and their eggs spawn in the basement of the main Durrr Burger building.

Update v18.00[]

  • Following the events of Operation: Sky Fire, destroyed Abductor pieces crashed near The Durrr Burger, as well as a Purple Cube, and likely pieces of The Mothership. All these factors contribute to the landscape at and around the Landmark becoming almost completely corrupted to an orange color. The crashed Abductor produces a Slipstream above much of the Location.
  • Food Fighter and the Alien Parasites are also gone.
