Overview

GHOST House is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale, added in Update v12.00, on the Apollo island. It is located in co-ordinate D5, East of Weeping Woods. It is building, first owned by GHOST that seemed to be a training center for GHOST recruits. It had then later been vandalized by SHADOW and taken over by them in Chapter 2: Season 3. The building remained under the occupation of SHADOW until it had been removed.

In Chapter 2: Season 5, it was a part of the sand region, only to be removed in Update v15.30, being replaced with Kit's Cantina.


Overview

Overview[]

As of C2S5, there were not many chest or floor loot spawns to be found here, so the location had gotten worse than it used to be; it could still be a viable drop spot be used to start a game with a mediocre set of loot.

Previously, the GHOST house contained many chest and floor loot spawns so was a good location to drop if you were worried about nearby locations. It had two floors, as well a small basement (which was no longer accessible as of its most recent iteration), as well as some vents that led outside to a bush for cover.
It also had several Porta-Potties you could hide in. However, for some reason, some teleported you to another one close by.

History

History[]

Chapter 2 Season 2[]

  • Added GHOST House as a GHOST training facility.

Chapter 2 Season 3[]

  • GHOST House is taken over by SHADOW.

Chapter 2 Season 4[]

  • GHOST House is completely abandoned and has gone into disrepair. It may have been used as a base by an evil gnome developing Super Slurp Juice.

Chapter 2 Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: GHOST House is covered in sand.
  • Update v15.30: GHOST House is replaced by Kit's Cantina.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

  • Re-added GHOST House to the map, as the Apollo island has returned.
    • The Phone Booth has been moved against the northernmost wall.
    • GHOST Henchmen now roam around the location.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Its name is a play on the phrase 'Ghost House' to refer to a haunted location, but it is literal in this sense, as that was the faction occupying it. In addition, the lack of GHOST Henchmen could make it a small joke.
  • During Chapter 2: Season 2 and Chapter 2 Remix, a chest is booby-trapped - When a player tried searching it, they would drop down to a room with multiple Sentry Turrets.
  • It had barrels of Super Slurp Juice in the basement after being abandoned. The Evil Gnome from Chapter 2: Season 4 may have resided here before his untimely demise in Slurpy Swamp.
  • It has several vents, bushes and dumpsters that can be used to hide from players. After being covered in sand, the bushes disappeared and it gained tumbleweeds.
  • Phonebooths appear at this location, though GHOST Henchmen do not. Though it could be there to show how the Sentry devices would react to disguised players.
    • In Chapter 2 Remix, however, GHOST Henchmen do spawn here.
