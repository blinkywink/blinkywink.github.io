Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Compact Cars was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. It is a small walled/fenced in compound with smashed cars residing inside, with some destruction equipment around, including a magnet crane. Currently, several items from the Alien celebration previously located at Believer Beach can be found here. The Landmark is located west of Dirty Docks, inside the coordinate G4, south of IO Convoy, and directly north of Chair.

Update v11.00[]

  • Compact Cars was added to the map.

Update v13.00[]

  • Compact Cars was completely flooded over by water, making it inaccessible and thus no longer a Landmark.

Update v13.20[]

  • July 11th 2020 - The water level lowered, allowing for part of Compact Cars to be seen above the water line, thus making it a Landmark once again.
  • July 18th - The water level lowered further, revealing more of Compact Cars above the water.

Update v13.30[]

  • The water fully cleared from Compact Cars.

Update v15.00[]

  • Dummy could have been found here as a Character.

Update v17.00[]

  • Dummy is removed as a character altogether, and as such he is not present at Compact Cars anymore, after two seasons of spawning there.

Update v18.00[]

  • Most of Compact Cars is surrounded by an orange landscape created following Operation: Sky Fire when The Mothership was destroyed and multiple Cubes fell around the island. One such Cube landed near here, and its energy in part caused the landscape change.
  • Additionally, the location now houses several decorations and such that were previously found at Believer Beach.

Update v18.40[]

  • Rust Lord could have been found here as a character.

Content Update v18.40 - 1[]

  • (December 2nd, 2021): - The Cube Queen's corruption of the island has added to the corrupted terrain around the landmark.

Compact Cars disappeared from the map because Apollo itself was completely flipped around during the conclusion of The End (Chapter 2) Event.
