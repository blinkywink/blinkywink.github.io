Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Primal Pond was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale, introduced during Chapter 2: Season 6 on the island Apollo. It replaced Hunter's Haven, while previously only existing inside of it. It was a lone pond, with several small primal structures nearby. It could have been found located in the coordinate E6, east of Weeping Woods, northwest of Lazy Lake, and far north of Guardian of the Lake.


History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 5

  • Update v15.00
    • Primal Pond was added as an unnamed location, merely as part of Hunter's Haven.
  • Zero Crisis Finale
    • A building from Hunter's Haven was removed from the top of the location.

Chapter 2: Season 6

  • Update v16.00
    • With the removal of Hunter's Haven, Primal Pond became a Landmark, and had new buildings added around it, as well as now being inside the Primal Biome.
    • Cluck also now spawns as a Character.

Chapter 2: Season 7

  • Update v17.00
    • Cluck has been removed as a Character. Cluck’s house was replaced with an IO Camp and the primal grass around turned green.

Chapter 2: Season 8

  • Update v18.00
    • Kakashi Hatake was added as part of the Naruto Collaboration.
  • Update v18.40
    • Kakashi Hatake no longer spawns here.

The End

  • With the culmination of the event, Primal Pond, and everything else on the island, had completely flipped over.
