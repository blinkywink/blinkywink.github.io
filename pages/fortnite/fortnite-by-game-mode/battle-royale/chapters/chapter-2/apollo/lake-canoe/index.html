Overview

Lake Canoe is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. It is a small lake with a lodge overlooking it, along with two docks and an island in the lake. The location can be found northwest of Retail Row and southwest of Dirty Docks, inside coordinate G5.

Update v11.00[]

  • Lake Canoe was added to the map.

  • Helicarrier 64 has moved above Lake Canoe for the event.

Update v15.00[]

  • Turk may spawn as a Character here.
  • This is also a spawn location for an IO Elevator.

Update v15.30[]

A Portal related to Tron has appeared north of Lake Canoe.

Update v16.00[]

  • Turk has moved locations, and IO Elevators no longer spawn.

Update v17.00[]

  • Dockside Dish is added north of the location.

Update v18.00[]

  • Dockside Dish is replaced by Destroyed Dish following the events of Operation: Sky Fire. A crashed Abductor, a Cube, and likely debris from The Mothership are north of the location, turning much of the landscape in that area a corrupted orange color.

Content Update v18.10[]

  • October 5th 2021, 1:12 EST - the Compact Cars Cube rolled within the vicinity of the Landmark with its cube babies. They soon left, leaving a trail of corruption in their wake.

Lake Canoe disappeared from the map because Apollo itself was completely flipped around during the conclusion of The End (Chapter 2) Event.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Lake Canoe is not a typical drop spot for most players. There aren't many Challenges or Quests that can usually be done here, and is thus rarely populated.
  • That being said, it may serve as a good Solo drop spot both because of the amount of Fishing Spots found here, and the decent amount of loot that be acquired, especially if one were to drop alone.
