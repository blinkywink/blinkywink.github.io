Overview

Hydro 16 is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. It is located east of Slurpy Swamp and west of Misty Meadows. Hydro 16 runs the dam and produces dirty water that flowed down to Slurpy Swamp. It can be found northwest of Misty Meadows and southwest of Lazy Lake - directly west of Lazy Lake Island, inside Coordinate D7.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00: Added Hydro 16.
    • Unknown: Four large windows have been added to the front of the main building.
  • Update v11.50
    • February 16th 2020: A shrine has appeared in Hydro 16 and it features candles, a golden toilet, a gnome and a plinth.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.00
    • Several GHOST Recruitment Posters have appeared around Hydro 16.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00
    • Hydro 16 has been flooded and the plant is now inaccessible. Most of the dam has been submerged underwater, and only the top part with the road is accessible. The buildings on top of the dam have not been flooded. A Whirlpool has also appeared in the area.
  • Update v13.30
    • August 1st: The entirety of the water in the area has receded and Hydro 16 is back to its original state.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00
    • The whirlpool has been removed here, as with everywhere else.
  • Update v14.30
    • Henchbros have visited here.
  • Update v14.40
    • Henchbros have moved to Sweaty Sands.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00
    • Ruckus spawns here as a Hostile NPC.
  • Update v15.10
    • December 19th: A Pure Portal has appeared north of the location leading to the Marvel Reality.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00
    • Ruckus and the Portal have disappeared.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00
  • Anti-Imagined Order Sprays have popped up around the Landmark. An Alien Artifact may be found here as well.
  • Content Update v17.10
    • July 6th 2021: Zyg and Choppy spawned here as a Boss, along with several Alien Parasites.
    • July 13th: They have moved to Weeping Woods.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00
    • The sprays and artifact have been removed.
  • Content Update v18.10
    • October 10th, 1:50 PM EST: The Lazy Lake Island Cube rolls to the vicinity of the area, leaving a trail of corruption in its wake. It soon rolls away with its entourage.
  • Content Update v18.40
    • December 4th: The location has been partially surrounded by corruption.

The End[]

  • Hydro 16 was destroyed by a Golden Abductor, then Apollo was flipped over, removing the location.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture event: The remains of Hydro 16 are destroyed by The Herald offscreen.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

  • Hydro 16 was brought back with the rest of Apollo.
