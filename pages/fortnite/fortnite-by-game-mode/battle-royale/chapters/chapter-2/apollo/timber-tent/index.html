Overview

Timber Tent is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. It is a tent-like structure made of logs, surrounded by other logs that would be used for sitting. It is currently surrounded by remains of The Mothership and a discolored landscape caused by the arrival of Cubes to the island. It can be found south of Believer Beach, west of Boney Burbs and north of Holly Hedges, inside coordinate B4.

Update v11.00[]

  • Timber Tent was added to the map.

Update v11.31[]

  • The location was covered in snow, as part of Winterfest 2019.

Update v11.31 Content Update 3[]

  • The location was rid of snow.

Update v13.00[]

  • Timber Tent was mostly flooded by water.

Update v13.20[]

  • July 1st, 2020 - The water level decreased and the main area with the log structure and logs around it for sitting have been unflooded. The surrounding area, however, remains flooded.
  • July 18th - The water level had lowered again, and now the surrounding area of the Landmark is free from water.

Update v15.00[]

  • Blaze could have been found here as a Characrer.

Update v17.00[]

  • Blaze no longer spawns as a Character, and thus, does not spawn here anymore.

Update v18.00[]

  • The Mothership was blown up due to the events of Operation: Sky Fire, and its remains litter the island. Parts of it landed near the location, and as a result of the energy The Cubes brought to the island, the landscape around Timber Tent is orange.
  • Interestingly, likely due to it currently being mostly damaged, Timber Tent is no longer a named Landmark.

Content Update v18.40 - 1[]

  • (December 2nd, 2021): - The Cube Queen's corruption of the island has completely engrossed the landmark if it wasn't already surrounded by corruption. Interestingly, players could only tell it had been further corrupted because the wood and logs of Timber Tent were now the same color as the terrain.

Timber Tent disappeared from the map because Apollo itself was completely flipped around during the conclusion of The End (Chapter 2) Event.
