Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Defiant Dish was a Landmark, and one of seven Imagined Order Satellite Stations found around the map added in Chapter 2: Season 7. It can be found on a hill south of The Aftermath and east of Weeping Woods, near the former location of Hunter's Haven .

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00: Defiant Dish was added.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00: Defiant Dish received a Reboot Van.
  • Content Update v18.40 - 1 (November 28th 2021): The Cube Queen's corruption of the island has surrounded the forest around landmark, but not the landmark itself.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Defiant Dish was removed after being destroyed by Abductors.
