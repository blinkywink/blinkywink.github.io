Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Gorgeous Gorge was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 1 to the island Apollo. The Landmark a tall waterfall, that leads to a river. There is a ledge on the side of the cliff, and a small tent near the base of the waterfall. At the top of the waterfall, there is a No Swimming sign and a Zipline that leads to a small campsite on a nearby hill. It is located inside the coordinate B6 - Between Lazy Lake and The Pyramid

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • Update v11.00
    • Gorgeous Gorge was added to the map.
  • Update v11.10
    • A memorial of player named IFrost-Origins appeared at the base of the waterfall.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00
    • The water level in the Gorge has increased greatly, and a whirlpool has appeared.
  • Update v13.20
    • July 18th: The water level has lowered to its normal level, causing the whirlpool to vanish.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00
    • Gorguous Gorge was halfway covered in sand.
  • Update v15.50
    • March 5th: Ant-Man's Portal appeared here.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00
    • The red soil/Primal Biome came very close to Gorgeous Gorge.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Bottles of Diamond Blue can be found here to collect the extra style for Toona Fish
  • Content Update v18.40
    • November 30th: The Corruption from The Pyramid has completely engrossed the location.

The End[]

  • Gorgeous Gorge along with the Map Apollo has been flipped after the End event

Chapter 3: Season 4-Chapter 4: Season 1[]

  • Gorgeous Gorge was destroyed by The Herald offscreen during Fracture
