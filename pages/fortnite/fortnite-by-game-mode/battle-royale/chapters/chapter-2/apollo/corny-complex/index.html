Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Corny Complex was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was the futuristic version of Frenzy Farm, replacing Colossal Crops. It was nearly the same as Frenzy Farm, except having IO tech inside barns and shacks, and having two secret bases under farms, one being Surface Hub IO-F4. It started to get abducted by The Mothership in September 5th 2021. Doctor Slone could have been found here as a boss, along with IO Guards.

Overview

Overview[]

Corny Complex was a farm that actually is an IO-Operative Controlled Location, with a Surface Hub beneath the main Barn and another secret base near one of the corn fields. As the name suggests, this farm consisted mainly of corn, alongside cabbage.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Corny Complex was the fifth POI to appear at this location, the others are Frenzy Farm, Stark Industries, Colossal Coliseum & Colossal Crops.
  • This is the only named location to have the letter X in its name.
  • It was the fifth farm named location, the others being Anarchy Acres, Fatal Fields, Frenzy Farm & Colossal Crops.
  • It was the third farm named location on Chapter 2, the others being Frenzy Farm & Colossal Crops.
  • If a Monitor Screen was grabbed with a Grab-itron, the lower screen will show the Stark Industries logo. This is because the monitor screens are reused assets from Stark Industries.
  • Before the abduction, in the Takeover LTM, there was a floating Island that didn't appear in the location in other Gamemodes.
  • When the Mothership was Abducting Corny Complex the ambience in Surface Hub IO-F4 no longer played. However, when under Surface Hub IO-F4, the ambience played again.
