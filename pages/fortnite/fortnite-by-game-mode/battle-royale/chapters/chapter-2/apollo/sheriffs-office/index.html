Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Sheriff's Office is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 5 to the island Apollo. It is a typical western Sheriff's Office fitted with two cells, the southern of which has a hole blown open in its outer wall. It could last be found Northwest of Lazy Lake, inside the coordinates E5, F5, E5, and F6.
It was originally introduced in Chapter 2: Season 5 in a different place, located southwest of Colossal Coliseum inside coordinate E4.

  • The Gnomes break out of the Office.

Update v18.20[]

  • All gnomes have escaped from the location.

The End[]
