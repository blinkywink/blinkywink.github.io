Sentinel Graveyard is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 4 to the island Apollo. The location is a hilly area home to fallen Sentinels, who in Marvel Comics were giant Mutant-Hunting robots. The Landmark is located west of Lazy Lake in coordinates E5 and E6.

The hand blasters of the Sentinels can be jumped on, which can launch the player upward, giving them a chance to use their glider and giving them anti-gravity capabilities, similar to the Corrupted Areas.
