Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Stealthy Stronghold was an Unnamed Location (Formerly Named Location) in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was introduced in Chapter 2: Season 5, and demoted in Chapter 2: Season 7.

Overview

Overview[]

Stealthy Stronghold is a large walled-off biome, with a Jungle inside of it. It is located on the north-western coast of The Island. The location has a complex of ruins inside, as well as an IO Camp, and a river where Fish can be caught. The location previously housed an underground complex, which was later removed. Stealthy Stronghold is surrounded by a tall indestructible wall, with several guard towers on top, and doors inside of it.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: Stealthy Stronghold has been added, replacing the Homely Hills.
  • Update v15.21: Predator has been added as a boss.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00: Surface Hub IO-D1 has been closed and added Lara Croft as a Character. Predator has been removed.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • Update v17.00: Stealthy Stronghold has been turned into an Unnamed Location. Added Deep Woods Dish and IO Guards.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Update v18.00: South of Stealthy Stronghold gets corrupted.
    • 9/18/2021: A Turret Station has been built.
  • Update v18.10: Added Wrath as a Character
    • 10/03/2021: Another Turret Station has been built.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Stealthy Stronghold has been removed after being flipped and submerged.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: Stealthy Stronghold has been destroyed by The Herald

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Several references to Predator (1987) were present at the location, serving as teasers for the Jungle Hunter Set in the Chapter 2: Season 5 Battle Pass.
    • The ambient resembles the Guatemalan jungle and the rebel camp infiltrated and destroyed by Dutch's crew.
    • A truck resembling the dismantled Willys Jeep pickup pushed by Dutch at the rebel camp could also have been found in the area.
    • A crashed helicopter in between the trees, identical to the one seen in the film.
    • The doors for the walls surrounding the area — set up by the Imagined Order — contained the Plasma Caster's reticle on them.
