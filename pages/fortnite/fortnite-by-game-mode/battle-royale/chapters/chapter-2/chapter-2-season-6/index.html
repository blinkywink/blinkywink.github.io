Chapter 2: Season 6, also known as Season 16, with the slogan Primal (stylized as PRIMAL), was the sixteenth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale, that started on March 16th 2021 and ended on June 7th 2021.

The theme revolved around Wildlife as several areas of the island transformed into wild, and plunged into a primal age, changing most of the weapons into primal and makeshift weapons.

Previously, In Fortnite...

Previously, In Fortnite...[]

Following the events of the battle against Galactus, Jones was sent by Slone to fix the Zero Point, reseal The Bridge while not letting anyone escape The Loop. Which Jones does by jumping from reality to reality gathering the best hunters from across all realities. However, each reality jump caused The Zero Point to destabilize, to the point at which characters near it took notice.

Jones, angry that the Imagined Order did nothing to prevent the Zero Point from further destabilizing, brings the leader of The Seven, The Foundation into the Island to fix the Zero Point. However, he fails to do so, and resorts to creating a tower that would contain the Zero Point's final blast and minimize the damage to the island.

How Did It Start?

How Did It Start?[]

After The Zero Point released it's final blast, it created a autumn themed path of destruction on the island, removing the sand from the Desert and erasing all technology in it's path, heavily damaging the remaining Tilted Towers buildings in Boney Burbs and almost completely destroying the Colossal Coliseum, and creating a mini Tower on all of it's ends. A village was then built around the tower created by The Foundation worshipping the tower now known as The Spire, another village was built on the remains of the Colossal Coliseum, and The Spire like outposts were created around the mini Towers.

The Storyline

The Storyline[]

Sometime after The Zero Point had been sealed within The Spire, Tarana reaches to The Looper about a strange device containing a message that she found, asking The Looper to gather artifacts for her in exchange. Once The Looper is done, Tarana tells The Looper that the device was stolen by the thief Raz, which The Looper goes to. Raz tells them about the message, tells The Looper that the message directs them to The Spire, a place of great interest to him, and asks if The Looper want him to decode in exchange for a few favors.
Raz then sends The Looper to steal an Artifact from The Spire, collect an Talisman from a Spire Guardian, and collect a Wolf fang, Boar tusk and Chicken feathers in order to create a disguise for The Looper to go into The Spire and sample the Zero Point Crystals in the location, which The Looper does so, retrieving the samples and the Device to Raz.

Afterwards Raz tells The Looper to take the device to one of the Guardian Outposts due to their strange magic, which The Looper does so and the device starts playing an audio, saying "Jones knows". The Looper then goes after the Jones snapshots in the island for help regarding The Spire, all of them directing The Looper to Jonesy The First. After The Looper confronts Jonesy The First, he agrees on helping, telling The Looper to not allow The Spire to corrupt someone powerful, telling The Looper to go before it attracts any more attention.

Raz then goes to The Spire, and finds the Spire Artifact, which makes him more powerful and at the same time corrupting him and leaving a snapshot behind in Colossal Crops, the Raz snapshot in Colossal Crops realizes he had commit a huge mistake doing that. Jonesy The First then tells The Looper that Raz wasn't helping them as much as he was helping himself. The Looper then talks to other people, which tell them that the longer Raz has the artifact, the more danger they were in and that Raz was bound to attract attention with the amount of power he has, which makes The Looper and Jonesy The First rush to Raz, now as Glyph Master Raz, to defeat him and take away the Spire Artifact from him.

After the fight, The Looper and Jonesy The First manage to defeat Glyph Master Raz and retrieve the Spire Artifact, however Jonesy The First notes that "Anyone who knows what to look for will see how powerful The Spire is.", then telling The Looper "This isn't the end...".

How Did It End?

How Did It End?[]

After the battle against Glyph Master Raz at The Spire, the Imagined Order returns to the Island after escaping during the Zero Crisis, destroying Telescopes and silencing anyone talking about UFO sightings.

Eventually, Crop Circles were created in Colossal Crops and Loopers all over the island start being abducted, and Aliens made their first contact with the Loopers in the form of puzzles. The puzzles were eventually solved, finding out a name: "The Last Reality".

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass could only be purchased for 950 V-Bucks. The Battle Bundle was replaced by a 25-Tier Bundle for 1,850 V-Bucks for those below or at Battle Pass Level 75. Adding the prices gives you the same price as the Battle Bundle. The Battle Pass could have been obtained for free by being in the Fortnite Crew. If you already purchased it with V-Bucks, you would get 950 V-Bucks back.

Map Updates

Map Updates[]

The Zero Point was contained but released a pulse across the Island that erased all technology on it's path, creating the primal Autumn Biome.

Update v16.00

March 16th 2021

  • During the Zero Crisis Finale event, in order to contain the Zero Point, The Foundation created a tower in the shape of a spiral staircase and sealed the Zero Point in it. A village was built around it and the tower is now referred to as The Spire.
  • Salty Towers was changed to it's primal counterpart after a pulse, now named Boney Burbs.
  • The Colossal Coliseum was destroyed by the pulse, and a farm was made on it's remains, this location is now named Colossal Crops.
  • Hunter's Haven was removed and replaced by a landmark called Primal Pond.
  • 6 Spire like towers were created in certain parts of the Island, Guardian of the Bay near Sweaty Sands, Guardian of the Lake north of Misty Meadows, Guardian of the Fields east of The Orchard, Guardian of the Woods in Weeping Woods, Guardian of the Mountains north of Retail Row, Guardian of the Sea northeast of Pleasant Park.
  • Dusted Depot was heavily damaged by the pulse, there are trees growing over it now.
  • The Pizza Pit has been repaired, the Tomato head stopped spinning.
  • Grumpy Greens was turned into stone, it's now called Grumpy Gravel.
  • Grass has grown over the Razor Crest's crash site and the dried river.
  • There's a new house near Fort Crumpet, Raven spawns in there.
  • There's a half opened vault next to the **REDACTED** bunker.
  • There are eggs and bones scattered throughout multiple areas in the map.
  • Deadfire moved into the outskirts of Lazy Lake, he set up a campsite and started re-building his Sheriff Office.
  • All the golden furniture was removed from the basement of a house in Lazy Lake, but there's a hidden room with furniture and a empty cabinet in the middle.
  • A studio was made on the basement of FN Radio, this is due to Stage Slayer spawning in there.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Jones tries to contact you by radio when you go near a Primal biome or a Guardian Outpost in a car. He says "I'm still out here"
  • Chapter 2: Season 6 is the first season to not include an Epic emote as a reward on the Battle Pass.
  • Other than a wild week, Chapter 2: Season 6 is the first season to not include any traps in the loot pool.
  • The season's opening cinematic was co-directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, perhaps best known for their work on the MCU's Avengers films. [1]
    • This would also mark the start of nearly all promotion trailers to be presented in a similar format.
  • Chapter 2: Season 6 is the first season to fully vault Sniper Rifles.


Teasers/Leadup

Teasers/Leadup[]

Teasers for Chapter 2: Season 6 was released on March 13 2021, during the FNCS: Chapter 2: Season 5 grand finals. The teasers tell us Agent Jones is angry with the Imagined Order for not helping him with the unstable Zero Point, and decides he's not going to persue the Order anymore (Although dedicating his whole life to it).
After this, the Imagined Order revokes Jones' credentials as he tries to retrieve all files about The Seven. He decides to take them for himself.

Mark Rein's teaser word for Chapter 2: Season 6 was "Wild."
