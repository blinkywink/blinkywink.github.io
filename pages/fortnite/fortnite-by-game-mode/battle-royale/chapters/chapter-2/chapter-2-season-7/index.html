Chapter 2: Season 6 Cosmic Summer Celebration

Chapter 2: Season 7, also known as Season 17, was the seventeenth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale, and the penultimate season of Chapter 2 that started on June 8th 2021, and concluded on September 12th 2021 at 4:00 PM EST, when the Operation: Sky Fire live event ended, although the next season did not begin until 5:00 AM (EST) September 13th 2021. The period between the event and the start of Chapter 2: Season 8 was approximately 13 hours, known as To Be Continued!.

Its title and slogan was Invasion (stylized as INVASION) and its theme revolved around The Last Reality invading the island.[1]

Previously, In Fortnite...

Previously, In Fortnite...[]

Following The Foundation's attempt to seal off the Zero Point, the Island had gone in a Primal stage, causing parts of the map turn into an Autumn-like biome. Wild animals such as wolves, boars, chickens and frogs, and NPC's like the Spire Guardians, somewhat linked to the Imagined Order, roam around the spire towers.

How Did It Start?

How Did It Start?[]

After Raz obtained a Spire Artifact, he ended up attracting unwanted attention from an alien race known as The Last Reality, which later revealed to be a faction beyond aliens. UFOs began to abduct loopers and send them to different areas of the island. Mari, a woman who is studying aliens, hosts a radio show on them titled "Hot Saucers." Later, the aliens started a full scale abduction against the citizens of Apollo, with them successfully infiltrating the Imagined Order and using their technology to rip the Spire out of the map. The location of the zero point is unknown but The Foundation was seen falling into the ocean, being rifted to the Batman reality, NF-1935.

In the Story Trailer, a series of unusual and strange phenomenon occurred in the Island:

  • Spitfire detects several circle-like objects (UFO) floating and making their way to the Island.
  • Envoy and Contract Giller spot an enormous UFO in a cave-like place.
  • Calamity, Hayseed, and Cobb walk through cornfields quizzically, only to be left in a strange circle in which it turns out to be one of the Alien's signs.
  • A tornado-like phenomenon (Alien's/invaders invading the island) soars through the Island, causing strange winds and taking Rustler's hat in a flash.
  • Mothmando opens the door to their place, only to be caught in a trance after looking at strange lighting, turning into one of the Invaders. (Most likely).
  • UFO's proceed to start abducting people in The Loop, which first happens to Sparkplug.
  • The enormous alien mothership rushes its way through the island, covering the entire island.
  • Rick Sanchez, Dare & the members of the Imagined Order try to make contact with the aliens with music, but in response, the Mothership takes The Spire off the ground, and inserts energy-like waves. On the way, The Zero Point destabilizes once again and sends another wave, removing the Primal biome.
The Storyline

The Storyline[]

Having no choice, IO finally fully reveal themselves to the escaped Loopers in the Extra Loop, to protect Apollo against the Alien Invaders. They set up Satellite Stations and Camps all around the Island to investigate the alien activities and The Mothership's movement. Slone recruits The Looper[2] and sends them to missions to find out more about the aliens and more.

There are rumors of a mole working for the Imagined Order, and Slone tasks the Looper with finding them.[3] Numerous IO agents; including Rick Sanchez, Maven, Rook and Marigold instruct the Looper in their recruitment to the IO. Slone's second task for the Looper has them infiltrate Believer Beach to earn the ringleader's trust.[4] They meet Joey, who knows how to disrupt the mothership's communications[5] by placing alien light communication devices on mountain tops. Slone wants to investigate Sunny and find out if she is the ringleader of the anti-human sentiment on the Island.[6] The Looper goes to earn her trust by making contact with aliens[7] and help her welcome them.[8] Slone wants Hayseed, one of the island's biggest preppers, on the IO's side.[9] To lure aliens, the IO invents Inflate-A-Bulls. Seeing its effectiveness, Slone initiates Operation Fauxvine to handle the Saucers and the abduction crisis.[10]

Rook tasks the Looper to hunt down the traitor Riot, alongside Special Forces' help.[11] After searching the southeast corner of the Island, they eliminate Riot and report to command.[12] A major IO security breach happens and Slone wants to investigate. The Looper finds an access card for her and plant wiretraps to listen to "off the record" IO chatter, confirming the existence of a mole.[13] The mole leaks secret information about the IO to the public[14], and the Looper mindwipes the civillains. An existence of an "infiltratior of alien variety" is also found by Slone and the Looper goes to confront and eliminate them.[15] To learn more about the aliens, Slone and the Looper start monitoring their behavior and presence on the Island.[16] Noticing the rise in abduction numbers[17] and the aliens' interest in Slurpy Swamp, the Looper clears the area so the IO can investigate.[18] Desperate for an insider asset, Slone needs to squeeze intel from Joey or even recruit them.[19] The Looper bribes her with cat food and human gifts, and she agrees to try to discourage the aliens from annihiliating the Island.[20]

The IO comes up with a counter attack, but it has been compromized. Slone and the Looper decide to create a backup plan.[21] They collect Alien Devices to hide the energy signature of the Countermeasure Device at Corny Complex and create hundreds of bombs. Marigold helps the Looper to bait the aliens into abducting their bombs and pressure them into making a big move.[22] She "leaks" the location of their secret base to tempt the Mole into sharing it with the aliens,[23] but share it with locals by herself for insurance.[24] The countermeasure devices are prepared to strike the Mothership, but the area it is expected to crash on is populated.[25] The Looper warns everyone to give them time to leave and prevent them from interfering.[26] The Mole activates the devices early but their plan is quickly spoiled by the Looper.[27] Identifying Maven as the Mole, the Looper is sent to eliminate her and does so successfully.[28]

How Did It End?

How Did It End?[]

Main Article: Operation: Sky Fire

Slone contacts the strike team she made, and place them on the floating parts of Corny Complex that are being abducted to ride along the bombs. The Mothership finally abducts Corny Complex along with Slone's strike team. She guides them through the ship so they can reach the abduction chamber window. The window gets locked and a Cube rises from the water. Slone is shocked by the Cube's return and weaponizes the Looper's Alien Jetpacks to deactivate the Cube. She then leaves the Looper's inside The Mothership, as she is afraid of The Cube returning to the Island. As a last stand the Loopers reboot The Cube and turn it blue. The Cube activates an elevator and brings the team to the Cube Chamber inside The Mothership, where they see numerous Cubes and veins growing from them. They all slowly starting to turn purple, one by one, except of one, that instead of purple, glows gold. Slone then activates the bombs and destroys The Mothership without knowing what's inside of it and ends up bringing all the Cubes to the Island.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass was purchasable for 950 V-Bucks, or if you owned the Fortnite Crew, you would have it on season launch. It had 8 new outfits, including Doctor Slone, Kymera, Rick Sanchez, and Sunny.

The Battle Pass was reworked at the start of the season, in which players unlock a new page of rewards every 10 levels, or by purchasing a certain amount of Battle Pass items, and could purchase them in any particular order with Battle Stars () 5 Battle Stars awarded to players each level. The player also had to reach level 140, instead of the usual level 100, to gain all non-Superlevel rewards.

Teasers/Leadup

Teasers/Leadup[]

During Update v16.50, alien posters and Static TVs started appearing around Apollo, an Imagined Order helicopter crashed onto the island, and most of the crops at Colossal Crops were removed (possibly stolen). A woman named Mari was studying aliens and hosted a radio show discussing them.

Additionally, certain Content Creators, like Loserfruit, received DVD Players containing a CD labeled "They're coming!" and various posters with the same warning. The CD played different footage of Crop Circles in seemingly real-life fields. These Crop Circles showed symbols and likely have something to do with the Foreshadowing Quests. Posters can also be found all around the world, and various people were given postcards with the same symbol.

As well as this, numbers seen on some of these posters were actually phone numbers that you can call to hear sounds that, when put in a spectrogram, show images. One of these images shows Peely running from an alien spaceship, and another with a kangaroo in it. Because of this, it is believed they would be an animal, but instead they would be accompanied by octopuses and platypuses - theorized with some Foreshadowing Quests voice lines.

On June 2nd 2021, UFOs starting appearing, they mostly spawned in Risky Reels and could abduct up to twenty players and would heal players during abduction.

Starting on June 5th 2021, teasers of cosmetics and new items/weapons were released.

  • June 5 2021 teaser: Fishstick Janky, Lac-pack, Grab-itron
  • June 6 2021 teaser: The Fret Basher, Clark Kent, Alien Knockgun Launcher
  • June 7 2021 teaser: Kymera Ray Gun, Butter Robot, Galactic Pack (Sunburnt)

On June 7 2021, Mark Rein, the Vice President of Epic Games, posted an alien emoji (👽) on Twitter as a teaser for Chapter 2: Season 7.
