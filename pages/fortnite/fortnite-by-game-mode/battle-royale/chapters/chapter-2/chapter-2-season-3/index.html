The Device Summer Splash
Chapter 2: Season 3, also known as Season 13 in the game files, with the slogan Splashdown! (stylized as SPLASH DOWN), was the thirteenth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale, which started on June 17th 2020, and ended on August 26th 2020.

Previously the start date was going to be on April 30th 2020, but was delayed multiple times to June 17th 2020

The theme revolved around water, aquatics and summer.

This was the shortest season of Chapter 2, lasting 70 days.

Previously, in Fortnite...

Previously, in Fortnite...[]

Midas had executed his plan to destroy The Storm, activating The Device and pushing back The Storm, however The Storm reacted to it and created a giant Wall of Water held by its barrier.

How did it start?

How did it start?[]

After the failure of Midas' plan, SHADOW took over the destroyed Agency and and rebuilt it, turning it into the The Authority. GHOST members went into exile out of the main Island, creating The Fortilla, a floating island the southeast corner of the Island, as their new HQ. Later The Wall Of Water created by The Storm stopped holding and caused the Island to become flooded.

The Storyline

The Storyline[]

Following the collapse of The Wall Of Water, the fight between GHOST and SHADOW started to cool down, and the water in the Island caused erosion to happen on the northwest corner of the Island, which caused all the water in the Island to get drained by an ancient reign inhabitted by the likes of Fishstick named Coral Castle.

How did it end?

How did it end?[]

During the events of Season X, Thor is crowned the king of Asgard. 2 weeks later, Galactus warns Thor of an impending threat. To stop Galactus from destroying Asgard and the universe, Thor becomes the Herald of Thunder and helps Galactus find 5 special planets to consume in order to stop the Black Winter, however, after Galactus consumed the 4th planet, a Rift opened before him and Thor. He could feel the reorganization of the Island as well as the power of The Zero Point, which attracted him to it. Galactus tries to resist the attraction but is unable to and goes into the Rift, Thor tries to stop him from straying from their path but couldn't, so he follows right behind him to evacuate the Loopers from the Island.

Fast forward to present day, the Herald Thor comes through the Rift into Reality Zero --which he could feel was draining his memories-- to evacuate the Loopers from the Island before Galactus's arrival, but they didn't listen, and started shooting him. He fought back but as he lost his memories, Brite Bomber brings Thor's attention to Galactus's emergence from the Rift, which brought back Thor's memories. Thor sent his ravens to find help in his reality, and in turn, Lady Sif sends several heroes from that reality to Reality Zero, all of which lost their memories.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass cost 950 V-Bucks and the Battle Bundle cost 2,800 V-Bucks as well. Its Tab received a new water-themed hideout, where you could access Weekly Challenges, Style Challenges, the Battle Pass, and a new feature - Build-A-Brella.

Map Updates

Map Updates[]

The large wall of water around the Island stopped holding, and caused the whole Island to flood significantly, making almost half of the map filled with water.

Update v13.00

June 17th 2020

  • New Named Locations, some controlled by Henchmen and Bosses.
    • The Agency turned into The Authority, a SHADOW POI with a large wall around the island to protect it from flooding. The Authority is controlled by SHADOW Henchmen and the boss, Jules.
    • A large GHOST rebellion site has appeared on the southwest corner of the island, called The Fortilla. The Fortilla is controlled by GHOST Henchmen and the boss, Ocean.
    • The Box Factory has turned into a named location called Catty Corner, now including a new gas station and nearby cafe. Catty Corner is controlled by Kit's Henchmen and the boss, Kit.
    • The remains of The Rig were split into three parts extending a distance away. The POI is now called Rickety Rig.
  • Weeping Woods, Craggy Cliffs, Dirty Docks, and Risky Reels have been massively flooded and have become Landmarks.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • This season had the most Jonesy outfits: Scuba Jonesy, Relaxed Fit Jonesy, and Castaway Jonesy, prior to Agent Jones' appearance during the Doomsday event, or better well known as The Device.
  • This season's Battle Pass was the first to include Uncommon Contrails.
  • This is the first season to have a teaser for a secret Battle Pass outfit.
  • On June 1st, Sony/PlayStation accidentally leaked the pre-cover for Season 3.
  • Chapter 2: Season 3 would be the longest-running season on the iOS platform, spanning 1,521 days.
    • Development was paused on the platform as a result of Fortnite's ban from the App Store and the resulting Epic Games v. Apple lawsuit. As a result, iOS clients were not updated past Update v13.40 until August 16th 2024, when Fortnite returned to iOS devices in the European Union.
    • Until Fortnite's return to the App Store, the game was only available through the Epic Games Store for Android and iOS users in the European Union and other territories outside of the United States. macOS players could play Fortnite through cloud gaming services like NVIDIA GeForce Now, Xbox Cloud Gaming, and Amazon Luna.
  • This is the first season since Season 6 that did not give players the chance to earn the showcase art as a Loading Screen.
