Chapter 3: Season 3, also known as Season 21, was the twenty-first season of Fortnite: Battle Royale, and the penultimate season of Chapter 3 which started on June 5th 2022 and concluded on September 17th 2022.

The season's slogan was Vibin' (stylized as VIBIN') and themed around summer recreation and relaxation.

Previously, in Fortnite...

Previously, in Fortnite...[]

The Imagined Order constructed The Collider, a large tower at Loot Lake that could destroy everything in The Loop. It would frequently pulse every day, charging itself up. The Seven rebuilt the Mecha Team Leader to fight against the Collider. In Collision, The Paradigm controlling the mech, the Mecha Strike Commander unleashed onto the island to target the Collider. The Foundation and Agent Jones went into the Zero Point to find Geno.

How Did This Season Start?

How Did This Season Start?[]

The Seven won against the Imagined Order and the Collider was destroyed, the lsland then went into peace after winning the war. All the I.O remains were vandalized by the looper or reformed into party areas.

The Storyline

The Storyline[]

The Reality Tree appeared on the southwest part of the map, Making Reality Roots emerge from locations, Changing them in weird ways. The Scientist did several tests on the Reality Tree and Reality Saplings, and said he thought freeing the Zero Point was a mistake. He built an AI named A.M.I.E and discovered Darth Vader was on the island to look for a relic that had come from his reality. AMIE and The Looper pieced together the relic and The Scientist said that the Zero Point may be trying to communicate with them using the relic. They find the next relic, a shrinking chair, but it's surrounded by a energy field. AMIE and the Looper eventually break the energy field grabbing the relic which releases a Zero Point shockwave of magnitude 8.5. The Paradigm asks the Looper to look for The Foundation, but they find out that he is missing along with The Visitor, and The Sisters. Paradigm and The Looper finds out that The Scientist, The Visitor and The Origin is in a lab researching a weird substance that is spreading across the island and The Scientist asks the Looper and Paradigm to get some gear for them. The Paradigm tells the Looper that the Zero Point shockwaves was distress signals and that they need to help it. The Looper finds a audio log from The Scientist explaining that he is gone. Shortly after The Paradigm goes missing as well. Now it's just The Looper and AMIE.

Map Changes

Map Changes[]

Update v21.00

June 5th 2022

  • The Reality Tree has blossomed in the Island, erasing Camp Cuddle and creating the Reality Falls biome, changing the Island's geography with a new biome full of giant Mushrooms and rivers over it.
    • Around the Reality Tree, several waterfalls, a cave and rivers can be found.
      • Inside the cave area in Reality Falls there are rocks with over 5 trillion health and mining sounds coming from behind it.
    • Greasy Grove has been heavily damaged, with Mushrooms lifting the Tacos restaurant and damaging/destroying multiple houses and flooding the POI.
    • Now known as the Displaced Depot landmark, the blue warehouse from Dusty Depot has returned. The Chair and The Shark structure have returned next to it.
    • Cuddlepool, Quackling and Metal Team Leader have moved to a chalet and landmark known as Shroom Chalet.
    • A small farm was created southwest of Greasy Grove, it is a landmark named Fungi Farm.
    • Reality Falls includes other Landmarks such as:
      • Leafy Reef
      • Shelly Shoals
      • Cappy Cabin
      • Beach Bash
      • Groovy Grove
      • Scratch Pad
      • Peril Pass
      • 'Stools Fuels
      • Spore Shack
      • Hench Hut
      • Shroom Station
    • Several Reality Roots from the Reality Tree can be found over the Island.
  • Command Cavern has been taken over by Loopers and changed into a location with a Cuddle Team Leader statue, roller coasters, Slipstreams and DJ party named Rave Cave.
    • A Peely statue is under construction next to it.
  • The Fortress was destroyed during the Collision and turned into a party landmark known as Drill Hill.
    • Grass has grown over the path of destruction made by The Fortress.
  • The Collider has been destroyed and its remnants were taken over by Loopers and turned into Party locations. The rivers east of Loot lake is flowing with water again.
  • Deep inside the sinkhole in Loot Lake, The Zero Point can be seen exposed, although intact, forming The Glow.
  • Most of The Seven equipment at The Daily Bugle, Condo Canyon, Rocky Reels, Coney Crossroads has vanished.
  • The IO Airship that used to be in Command Cavern has vanished.
  • Reconstruction of the destroyed buildings have begun in Tilted Towers.
  • The yellow house east of Tilted Towers has begun reconstruction as well.
  • The road south of the Sanctuary has begun repairing and reconstruction.
  • A building started construction in Washout Wharf.
  • The craters around the map have been over grown with grass.
  • The beach around the Sanctuary has been expanded and spray painted similarly to Believer Beach.
  • All old IO outposts and crashed IO Airships have been spray painted and taken over by Loopers.
  • The Stone Man and Stone Lady are now resting, drinking slurp and reading a book, while the Stone Kid builds a sand castle and the Stone Dog checks out a crashed boat.
  • The Mighty Monument has been spray painted by Loopers.
  • A piece of Mecha Strike Commander has crashed on a house southeast of Rocky Reels.
  • The skull of a Klombo has appeared in the snow biome.
  • The bus at the car crash site near Coney Crossroads has been held up by a telephone pole.
  • Several spray-painted outposts have been built all around the Island.

June 11th 2022

  • Reality Roots have grown in Logjam Lumberyard.

  • The first building at Tilted Towers has begun construction and the clock tower has been fully repaired.

    • Butter Bloom: Neo, renamed to Neo Butter Barn.

Teasers/Leadup

Teasers/Leadup[]

The first teaser for Chapter 3: Season 3 was during To Be Continued!, where it showed the new Reality biome. Shortly after the unplayable period started, the website fortnitelookbook.com was launched, revealing the season's title and Cosmetic Items for Evie, Adira, and Malik.

To Be Continued screen teasing the Reality biome.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • On May 26 2022, an image of Chapter 3: Season 3's Battle Pass UI was leaked in 4Chan, showing 5 Outfits from the Battle Pass, the Bonus Outfit, and the June 2022 Fortnite Crew Pack. The image was completely true.
    • The only detail that was different in the image was that Mecha Strike Commander's head was bigger than in-game. Much like how Mecha Team Leader was.
  • This was the third Fortnite season to start on a Sunday, the second being Chapter 3: Season 2, and the first, Chapter 3: Season 1.
  • Mark Rein's teaser word for Chapter 3: Season 3 was "Chill."
  • The Herald was teased this season's cinematic trailer, who was holding a view of the island.
  • The music used in the Battle Pass trailer is T.R.I.B.E By LG (TEAM GENIUS) ft. Black Prez.
  • This is the only Chapter 3 season to not feature a Marvel Series outfit in the Battle Pass.
  • This is also the only Chapter 3 season to not have a character that is connected to Spider-Man in the Battle Pass.
  • Season 3 is the only Chapter 3 season to not have a member of The Seven in the key-art, and not have a member of The Seven in the Battle Pass.
  • Chapter 3: Season 3 was the last season Donald Mustard worked on and served as a celebration for his time at Epic Games.[1]
