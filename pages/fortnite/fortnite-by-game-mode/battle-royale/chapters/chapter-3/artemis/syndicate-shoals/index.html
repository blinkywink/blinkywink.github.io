Syndicate Shoals was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 3. It is localised on the east coast of the Artemis island.

Overview

Overview[]

Syndicate Shoals is a fine dining restaurant owned by the Peace Syndicate. This landmark is possibly linked to Midas because it includes references to Chapter 2: Season 2, having items from The Yacht & Midas' Hideout. It also has a boat dock on its eastern end, and the dock is similar to The Agency's old boat docks.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.20: Renamed Cuddle Cruisers to Syndicate Shoals, Evie now spawns here as a Character.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: Syndicate Shoals, like the rest of Artemis, was destroyed by The Herald.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • There are golden M symbols on the menus of the restaurant, similar to those on the carpets from The Yacht.
    • It seems to be inspired by old concept art of the Yacht.
  • The building architecture is similar to that of The Shark, a spy base owned by GHOST.
    • Fans have speculated this ties into either Midas, Marigold, or Meowscles being on the island.
  • The lectern and the book were taken from Midas' Hideout.
