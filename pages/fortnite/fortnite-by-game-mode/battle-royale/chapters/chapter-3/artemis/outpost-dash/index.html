Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Outpost Dash was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale that could have been found north of The Daily Bugle and east of Sleepy Sound. It was added in Update v20.00. And removed in Update v21.00

Overview

Overview[]

Outpost dash was a small, heavily damaged concrete watchtower which was surrounded by a partially destroyed fenced. The higher floors could have been accesed by an exterior staircase going around the tower. It was located on small hill next to Unremarkable House.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: Mole Team: Dash has been replaced with Outpost Dash
  • Content Update v20.10 : Outpost Dash has been destroyed by The Seven and is now smoldering with smoke. The top floor has completely been destroyed.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: Outpost Dash has been replaced with Ocean Outpost.
