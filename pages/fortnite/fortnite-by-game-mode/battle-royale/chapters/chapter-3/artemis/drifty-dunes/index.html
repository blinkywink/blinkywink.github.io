Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Drifty Dunes was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 1 to the island Artemis. It is located at the northwest of Chonker's Speedway and south of Rocky Reels.

Overview

Overview[]

It is a small area of land, composed of multiple buildings and accessories that all give a "western" feel to it.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Drifty Dunes was added to the map covered with snow.
  • Content Update v19.01 - 1:
    • January 7th 2022: The snow has melted from Drifty Dunes.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: Drifty Dunes was destroyed by The Herald, along with the rest of the island.
