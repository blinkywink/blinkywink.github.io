Overview

The Sinkholes were Unnamed Locations added in Update v19.40. They are the results of Earthquakes caused by the Imagined Order.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.30:
    • February 26th, 2022: The Coffee Shop falls into the ground, leaving a Sinkhole behind.
  • Update v19.40:
    • March 6th, 2022: The Red House falls into the ground, leaving a Sinkhole behind.
    • March 13th 2022: Seven Outpost VI falls into the ground, leaving the last Sinkhole behind.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: Seven Outpost VI is turned into Dispatch Depot by the IO. The sinkhole remains.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • In the Fracture Live Event, the Sinkholes were destroyed by The Herald.
