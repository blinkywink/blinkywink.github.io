Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

The Glow was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was located at the east of Loot Lake and the west of Coney Crossroads. It was added in Update v21.00.

Overview

Overview[]

The Glow is the remains of The Collider's surroundings, turned into party grounds. Additionally, The Zero Point stays in the middle, underwater.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: The Glow was created, after The Collider is destroyed.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00: Most of the party decorations have been removed.
  • Fracture: The Glow, like the rest of Artemis, was destroyed by The Herald.
