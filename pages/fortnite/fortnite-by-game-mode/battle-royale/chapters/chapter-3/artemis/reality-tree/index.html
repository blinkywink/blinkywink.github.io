Reality Tree was a Named Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was added as a Landmark in Chapter 3: Season 3 to the island Artemis.

Overview

Overview[]

This used to be a large Zero Point blooming tree located at the center of Reality Falls. There is a spiral stairs leading to the top of the tree. There are also winds current for glider redeploy on the gigantic lotus bloom. Bouncy Slurpshrooms and Ascender can be found around the tree.

Lore

Lore[]

The Reality Tree was a dark ominous tree powered by The Zero Point. With the Zero Point energy flowing through the tree and its roots. It is capable of using the Zero Point's power to alter reality in any locations within its perimeter. The tree's tendrils connected straight to The Zero Point, Continuously feeding energy into it.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: Reality Tree was added to the map.
    • June 11th 2022: Reality Roots began to spread from the tree.
  • Update v21.10: It brought the Star Wars universe onto the island.
  • Update v21.50: Reality Tree has reached hyperbloom state. Causing rainbow aurora and comets all over the sky. Dark Blue Rift Butterflies can now be found all over the tree.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00: Reality Tree became a named location after Reality Falls' leaves fell. The Chrome emerged from the roots of the tree.
  • Update v22.10: The Chrome spread more.
  • Update v22.20: The Chrome spread much more, creating many Chrome Spires. DJ Lyka's Fortnitemares 2022 concert has started. Removed Rift Butterflies. Added Spooky Dome.
  • Update v22.30: The Chrome intensifies. Re-added Dark Blue Rift Butterflies. Removed all Fortnitemares decorations.
  • Fracture: Reality Tree became fortified by Loopers. The Herald arrives at the Reality Tree and fully Chromes the location, causing the destruction of the island. After the reorganization of Reality Zero, the remains of Reality Tree becomes The Herald Tree in Asteria island.
