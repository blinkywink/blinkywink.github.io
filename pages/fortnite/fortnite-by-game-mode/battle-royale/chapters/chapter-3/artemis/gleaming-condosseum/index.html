Overview

Gleaming Condosseum is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 4 to the island Artemis. It is a Chromed-over version of The Condosseum

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00: The Condosseum is turned into the Gleaming Condosseum after The Herald arrived and Chromified much of the island, including this location.
  • Update v22.10: The Chrome spreads further and creares more Chrome Spires.
  • Update v22.20: The Chrome spreads more.
