Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Ridgeline Ranger Station was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Update v19.00 to the island Artemis. It is located between Loot Lake and Coney Crossroads.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Ridgeline Ranger Station was added as an Unnamed Location. It is covered in snow.
  • Update v19.10: Ridgeline Ranger Station became a Landmark and the snow around has melted.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: Ridgeline Ranger Station, like the rest of Artemis, was destroyed by The Herald.
