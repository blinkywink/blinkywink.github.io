Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Horse Shoe Hideout is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 1 to the island Artemis.

Overview

Overview[]

It consists of a small dock near a turn in the stream shaped like a horseshoe next to a couple buildings that appear to be used as hideouts - hence the name "Horse Shoe Hideout". It can be found southeast of Rocky Reels.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Horse Shoe Hideout was added to the map.
  • Content Update v19.01 - 1 January 7th 2022: All the snow melted around Horse Shoe Hideout.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: During the events of Collision, one of the arms of Mecha Strike Commander was blown off. Following the event, it can be found here, crushing the houses.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: Horse Shoe Hideout, like the rest of Artemis, was destroyed by The Herald.
