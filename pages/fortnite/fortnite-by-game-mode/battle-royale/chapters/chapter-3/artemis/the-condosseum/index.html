Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

The Condosseum is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 3 to the island Artemis. It is Bungalow Blooms after remaining in a static appearance, in the style of Colossal Coliseum.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.50: Bungalow Blooms is renamed into The Condosseum, and stays in the form of Colossal Coliseum.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00: With the arrival of The Herald and the Chrome, the Landmark has been turned into the Gleaming Condosseum.
