Overview

5 Power Towers were a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 1 to the island Artemis. The landmark is two large electrical towers with ziplines connecting the two, with the southern tower leading into Turbine, and the northern leading into Greasy Grove. It is located directly south of Greasy Grove.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Power Tower was added to the map.
  • Content Update v19.01 - 2 January 13th 2022: Snow melted around Power Towers.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: Power Towers was removed from the map with the inclusion of the Reality Bloom Biome.
