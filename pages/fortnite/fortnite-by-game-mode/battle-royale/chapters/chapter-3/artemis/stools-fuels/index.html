Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

'Stools Fuels was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 3 to the island Artemis. It is a small Gas Station/Outpost owned by GAS surrounded by decaying fauna, and found northwest of Reality Falls.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00
    • 'Stools Fuels is added to Artemis.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00
    • The decaying of the Reality Tree has caused much of the Reality Bloom Biome to die, including the area around 'Stools Fuels.
  • Fracture
    • 'Stools Fuels, like the rest of Artemis, was destroyed by The Herald.
