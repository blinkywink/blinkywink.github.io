Overview

Tilted Towers Battlefield was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale that is currently unnamed. It can be found at the east of Tilted Towers and south of The Glow.

Overview

Overview[]

Tilted Towers Battlefield is a huge crater field with the remains of a Yellow House and IO buildings.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: Tilted Towers Battlefield was added to the map.
  • Update v20.30: Tilted Towers Battlefield became a Landmark. The amount of craters have risen by a lot and a few buildings have been destroyed, most notably the Yellow House.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: Tilted Towers Battlefield and 3 other similar craters have been removed due to the land slowly healing.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.30: The Chrome reaches Tilted Towers Battlefield.
  • Update v22.40: The Chrome spreads further.
  • In the Fracture Live Event,Tilted Towers Battlefield was destroyed by The Herald.
