Launchpad was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 1 to the island Artemis. The landmark can be found west of Sanctuary, north of The Joneses, and directly south of The Ruins.

Overview

Overview[]

It is an island with two buildings and seven "launch-pads". Various other equipment can also be found around the location. It is currently operated by The Seven, and The Visitor currently can be found as a Character here.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Launchpad was added to the map. The Visitor can be found here as a Character.
  • Update v19.01
    • December 24th 2021 (~2 AM) - A Rocket has been prepped for launch at one of the launch pads.
    • December 26th 2021 - The Rocket has launched.
  • Content Update v19.01 - 2
    • January 11th 2022 - The Rocket has returned and the hatch has closed
  • Update v19.10
    • January 21st - A second Rocket has been prepped for launch at one of the launch pads.
    • January 23rd - The Rocket has launched.
  • Update v19.20
    • A visual glitch had occurred where the launch pad is both open and closed at the same time.
  • Update v19.30
    • February 25th - The third Rocket has been prepped for launch at one of the launch pads.
    • February 27th - The Rocket has launched.
  • Update v19.40
    • March 15th - The third Rocket has returned.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21.00: The Landmark was spray-painted vibrant colors to fit the theme of the season.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Update v22.00
    • Research Lab Pegasus has been created under the southern building in the location.
  • Update v22.20
    • A Floating Runway appeared hovering over the location.
  • Update v22.40
    • The Driftwood has floated over next to Launchpad in the air.
  • Fracture
    • Launchpad was destroyed by The Herald during the Live Event; A pod with a rocket could have been seen during the The Zero Point's reawakening.
