Overview

Puddle Pond was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 3: Season 1 to the island Artemis. The Landmark consists of a pond with a small fishing pier, along with some nature surrounding it. It can be found south of Coney Crossroads and west of Sanctuary.

History
