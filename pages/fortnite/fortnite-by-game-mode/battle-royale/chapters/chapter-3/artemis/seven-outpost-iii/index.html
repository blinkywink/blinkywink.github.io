Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Seven Outpost III was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale, added in Chapter 3: Season 1. It was located Northeast of The Daily Bugle.

Overview

Overview[]

It was one of the Seven Outposts, guarded by 2 Seven Sentries. A Vault was located at the basement of one of the huts. Three Rifts always spawned at this location.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Update v19.00: Added Seven Outpost III.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • Update v20.00: The Vault at Seven Outpost III is no longer accessible, The Imagined now spawns here as a character, Seven Sentries no longer spawn here.
  • Update v20.30: The Vault is now accessible again. A Choppa Funding Station has appeared
    • May 10th 2022: The Choppa at Seven Outpost III is available.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Update v21:00: The Choppa spawn has been removed, The Imagined no longer spawns here, The Origin now spawns here as a character.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • Fracture: Seven Outpost III, like the rest of Artemis, was destroyed by The Herald.
