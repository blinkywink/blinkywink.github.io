Overview

This article is about a location that has been removed from the map.

Butter Bloom was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Chapter 3: Season 3 to the island Artemis. Butter Bloom was a branch of the Reality Tree.

History

History[]

  • C3S3

Update v21.10[]

June 21st 2022

  • Butter Bloom replaced Butter Barn. A small reality bloom sapling is growing from the Reality Roots.

June 28th 2022

  • Butter Bloom bloomed, and the location rotated between being in the style of three previous locations:
    • Colossal Coliseum
    • Coral Castle
    • Neo Tilted

Update v21.50[]

August 30th 2022

  • Butter Bloom was renamed to Neo Butter Barn and has stopped rotating to stay in the style of Neo Tilted.
