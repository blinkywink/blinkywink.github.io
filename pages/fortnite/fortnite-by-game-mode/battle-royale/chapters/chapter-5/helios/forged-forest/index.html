Overview

The Forged Forest[1] is a biome in Fortnite: Battle Royale that appeared in Chapter 5: Season 4, in the north side of the map. It is very similar to the Grasslands, Upstate New York and the Boreal Tundra. It is the landscape of Latveria country from Reality: 616.
