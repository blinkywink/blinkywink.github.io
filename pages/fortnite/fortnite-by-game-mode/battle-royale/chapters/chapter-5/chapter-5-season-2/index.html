Chapter 5: Season 2, also known as Season 29, was the twenty-ninth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was the second season of Chapter 5. The season began on March 8th 2024 and concluded on May 24th 2024.

The season’s main slogan was Myths & Mortals (stylized as MYTHS & MORTALS) with a theme around gods and goddesses in Greek Mythology.

Delays

Delays[]

Chapter 5: Season 2 was originally going to start on March 8th, 2024, but there were technical issues that led to the downtime being extended.

The first delay was announced around 9:15 A.M EST, announcing that the downtime will be extended for 8 more hours. Then on 4:03 P.M EST, Epic Games announced that an updated version of v29.00 will be released at 9:00 P.M EST on March 8th. On 10:00 P.M EST, the servers came online, starting the season after an 18 hour downtime which is currently holding the record for the longest downtime in Fortnite history.

Previously, in Fortnite...

Previously, in Fortnite...[]

Odyssey arrived at Ruined Reels, having the Looper collect pieces of the Mosaic. Just a few days later, though, a giant, molten hand (referred to as the Titan Hand or just The Hand) had emerged left of Ruined Reels, with a giant chest attached to a chain: Pandora's Box. The entire community had to work together to break the chains, which had 5,000,000,000 HP. When the chains broke, the chest opened, letting out the chaos that was not meant to be released, leading us into Myths & Mortals!

Leadup to Season 2

Leadup to Season 2[]

After Pandora's Box was opened, the evil souls were unleashed onto the Island. Because of this, The Pantheon arrived on the Island and after they resealed away the evil souls Zeus became angry at humanity for opening Pandora's Box and vowed to eliminate all mortals. Thus the battle between Gods and mortals had started.

Chapter 5: Season 2 Storyline

Chapter 5: Season 2 Storyline[]

The Society's remnants, Nisha, Kavel, and Montague, battle against Zeus losing in the process marking the end of The Society. Faced with the threat of Zeus, wishing to annihilate all mortals, The Oracle made a new prophecy stating "Zeus's wrath is upon you. And doom is on the horizon. You must prove the worth of humanity to Zeus and the guardians of Olympus... Before the skies turn black..." . The Looper later challenges Hades and Ares, triggering a fight against Cerberus who was defeated, then later against Ares who was also defeated, which definitively signed the declaration of war between The Looper and the Gods. The Oracle then made another prophecy stating "Darkened skies...Choking dust... Hope lost, and found again..."

Meanwhile in The Underworld, Midas was chained in a prison cell by Hades. Midas later broke out as Ascendant Midas and reunited with his crew. Together they escaped the Island with The Marigold, Midas' yacht. Jules, Midas' daughter, also made gauntlets out of Underworld Essence to control the Golden Touch. An iceberg later arrives at Helios with Aang and his sky bison Appa trapped inside leading up to the Avatar: The Last Airbender collaboration.

The Looper then goes on quests from Aphrodite and The Oracle, revealing a prophecy by The Oracle stating "Bold Zeus calls out for vengeance, Aphrodite raises her shield... For his lost correspondence, keep your sharp eyes keenly peeled..." Artemis also gets recruited onto humanity's side by the Looper. Later on, Cerberus loses an artifact given to him by Hades so he blackmails the Looper into finding it, otherwise he will tell Zeus that the Looper is conspiring against him. The Looper finds the artifact and The Oracle instructs the Looper to find Medusa leading to the Medusa Snapshot Quests.

In Medusa Snapshot Quests the Looper retrieves Medusa's artifacts from Zeus and gives them back to Medusa. Later the Looper destroys Zeus's hourglasses, weakening Zeus. Finally, the Looper destroys Zeus's Stormforged Hourglass, defeating Zeus once and for all. The Oracle than reveals that her previous prophecy about darkened skies and choking dusts was actually talking about The Wanderer, and that Zeus was just the beginning. She quotes "You have won a great victory for humanity. But your war has not yet begun. Storm clouds gather. The Wanderer draws near. Even in the darkness... Hope will guide you." Hope also manages a new rule of freedom of Olympus.

Near the end of the season, a Mini-Event takes place called the Sandstorm Event. In the event, storm clouds gathered around the statue at Mount Olympus and the statue's eyes glowed and threw a lightning bolt at Pandora's Box using his sword. The box's orb charged up one-thirds of the way and the green water around the box turned yellow because of the boiling heat from the lightning bolt. The next day, the statue struck another lightning bolt at Pandora's Box, filling the orb up two-thirds of the way.

The next day, the statue threw a red lightning bolt at the box, imploding it. Something came out of the box, most likely The Wanderer, which bounced around to another island, creating a sandstorm that got closer and closer to the Island. It also speaks a message to us in morse code, saying "NO HOPE", "GET REKT", "WILDNESS, and "CASSATION". Eventually we can see a shark-shaped boat in the distance, which is owned by the Wasteland Warriors. The sandstorm then engulfs the southern part of the Island, starting the story for the next season...

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass costed 950 V-Bucks, with an additional 25-Level Boost offered for 1,850 V-Bucks available via the Item Shop. If you owned a Fortnite Crew subscription, you could unlock the Battle Pass for free. If you did not own the paid battle pass you wouldn't get all the rewards but you can still earn a few.

Each Battle Pass level requires 80,000 . Up to 200 levels can be bought for 150 V-Bucks each, with the first 25 being discounted.

Map Changes

Map Changes[]

Update v29.00 (March 8th 2024)

  • 5 new Named Locations added:
    • Brawler's Battleground
      • Ares spawns as a boss here.
    • Grim Gate, replacing Riviera Station.
      • Cerberus spawns as a boss here.
    • Mount Olympus, which replaced Hazy Hillside.
      • Zeus spawns as a boss here.
    • Restored Reels, a restored version of Ruined Reels.
    • The Underworld, which replaced Ritzy Riviera.
      • Hades spawns as a boss here.
  • 4 new Landmarks were added:
    • Charon's Crossing
    • Underworld Station, a new Train station.
    • Pantheon Path
    • Summit Temple.
  • Some smaller changes also occured:
    • The Marigold has been taken over by SHADOW replacing The Society.
    • Vaults at Lavish Lair, Fencing Fields, Grand Glacier & Snooty Steppes have been ransacked.
    • Rubble has blocked the entrance to the Underground HQ.
    • Hope has vandalized Grand Glacier and made it her home.
    • Stack of chairs added near Rebel's Roost.
    • Ruins near Reckless Railways have been repaired.
    • The Hand has been broken and Pandora's Box has closed.
    • Cargo Barge is no longer crashed into rocks.
    • Sand Castles on the western beach have turned purple.
    • Solar Panels have been added around the map.
    • Weapon Bunkers have been added around the map.

Update v29.01 (March 19th 2024)

  • The Marigold vault has opened, gold bars have been added around the Marigold, and Midas' Statue has been added to The Marigold.
  • Midas' Prison Cell has been added to The Underworld.

Update v29.10 Hotfix (April 2nd 2024)

  • The Iceberg with Aang and Appa frozen inside of it has been added to the map leading up to the Avatar: The Last Airbender collaboration on April 12th, 2024.

Update v29.20 (April 9th 2024)

  • The Marigold has vanished as Midas and his crew have left Helios.

Update v29.20 Hotfix (April 12th 2024)

  • Temples for Airbending, Waterbending, Firebending & Earthbending have been added around the Island.
  • The Iceberg with Aang and Appa has melted.
  • Cabbage Carts have been added around the Island.

Update v29.40 (May 3 2024)

  • Imperial Blockades have been added around the Island.
  • Darth Vader's Lambda Shuttle has been added on Piney Lane, with Darth Vader on it.
  • May 13th 2024
    • 4:00 AM ET - 2:00 PM ET: The god rays surrounding the statue at Mount Olympus slowly turn into storm clouds throughout the day.
    • 2:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue's eyes start glowing.
  • May 14th 2024
    • 1:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue's sword starts charging.
    • 2:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue raised its sword and threw a blue lightning bolt at the Pandora's Box, causing the orb to start filling up.
  • May 15th 2024
    • 1:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue's sword starts charging.
    • 2:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue raised its sword and threw an orange lightning bolt at the Pandora's Box, causing further destabilization.
  • May 16th 2024
    • 1:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue's sword starts to charge red.
    • 2:00 PM ET: The Mount Olympus statue raised its sword for the final time and threw a red lightning bolt at the Pandora's Box, unleashing a burst of energy that bounces across the water to hit another island and creating a sandstorm, thus attracting Megalo Don and his Wasteland Warriors to the island.
      • The statue became cracked.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Mark Rein's teaser word for Chapter 5: Season 2 was "Wings".[6]
  • On March 5th 2024, after Zeus, Thunderbolt of Zeus, Hades, and Chains of Hades were revealed through the season's first two teasers, tons of information about it were leaked.[7][8]
    • Cerberus' Modular Gatekeeper Shotgun.
    • Mount Olympus.
    • Bosses with multiple stages (although only Zeus has three).
    • Banana of the Gods recycled from Golden Apple (existence confirmed by Accolade icon shared by Toby Sperring).[9]
    • Minions and Shade Wolves summoned by Bosses.
    • Four Mythic weapons, likely referring to those that were obtained exclusively from Bosses (Modular Zeus' Huntress DMR, Hades' Modular Harbinger SMG, Cerberus' Modular Gatekeeper Shotgun, and Ares' Modular Warforged Assault Rifle).
    • Wings of Icarus item.
    • Poseidon's Breakshore Trident, but as a Pickaxe instead of a Weapon.
    • Ares' Modular Warforged Assault Rifle, but without firing in bursts.
    • Medusa, but as an Outfit and Character instead of a Boss.
  • The music used in the Battle Pass trailer is Human by Apashe ft. Wasiu.
