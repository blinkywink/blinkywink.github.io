Chapter 5: Season 1, also known as Season 28, was the twenty-eighth season of Fortnite: Battle Royale and the first season of Chapter 5. The season started on December 3rd 2023 and concluded on March 8th 2024. This is the first season to feature the Helios map. The theme features an european-inspired setting where the conflict of Society vs Underground took place.

The season’s main slogan is Underground (stylized as UNDERGROUND).

Previously, in Fortnite...

Previously, in Fortnite...[]

In a lush jungle under Asteria, an advanced ancient civilization had fled their villages from a ruthless vampire named Kado Thorne after predicting his arrival using The Apparatus, which was later used by Doctor Slone to discover the arrival of the vampire.

Doctor Slone paid Nolan Chance to assemble a heist crew. But, they are ambushed by the vampire himself, Kado Thorne. They successfully stole the Time Machine and John Jones used the Time Machine to take himself back to Athena during Chapter 1: Season 5.

Jones' mission is to slightly alter the Black Hole without modifying the future to create new realities alongside Apollo/Artemis so that the inhabitants of Asteria in the future have a reality to take refuge in, before the collapse of Asteria and the arrival of the enemies warned of by Optimus Prime. Jones carries out the mission, making it to Dusty Divot and installing the Time Machine on the Rocket. Finally on December 2nd at the following times, 2PM ET, 5PM ET, and 11PM ET, they launched the Rocket and entered the Zero Point and collided the unstable meteor with it, causing a second Big Bang in the Omniverse.

Welcome to Chapter 5

Welcome to Chapter 5[]

Main Article: The Big Bang

The Big Bang created multiple new galaxies with one of them containing a new world (Helios) where the inhabitants of Asteria could take refuge. Sometime later in that new world, Jones became a member of The Underground faction after learning that Peely had been kidnapped by The Society.

Storyline

Storyline[]

After The Big Bang occurred The Society was formed by Montague. The five leaders of The Society are: Montague, Nisha, Peter Griffin, Oscar, and Valeria who is the most notorious of the five. Later The Underground was formed by Hope, Valeria's younger sister, to defeat the Society. Valeria then kidnaps Peely in order to lure Jones in order to try to get information on Pandora's Box from Jones. However this backfired as Jones, now Vengeance Jones, had no information on Pandora's Box and decided to join The Underground in order to rescue Peely.

Later in the Takedown Quests Jones, Hope, and the Looper work together to defeat The Society once and for all. The Underground succeed and Jones rescues Peely from Fencing Fields. Near the end of the season Valeria sets up a camp in Ruined Reels to try to find Pandora's Box. A character called Odyssey comes to the Island to find the Mosaic saying that the Mosaic is the key to what is coming.

In Odyssey Quests the Looper uncovers the Mosaic revealing that it is a picture of Cerberus, Hades's three-headed guard dog, from Greek mythology. Odyssey states that she can feel an ancient power growing on the Island. She also states that the Society Medallions are fragments of this ancient power. Later Earthquakes start happening near Ruined Reels. Finally on March 2nd 2024 the Titan Hand comes out of the Island holding Pandora's Box in 6 chains. The Loopers work together to break the chains and then on March 3rd Pandora's Box opens and falls to the ground unleashing the souls of The Underworld to the Island. Valeria later goes missing.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass costs 950 V-Bucks, with an additional 25-Level Boost offered for 1,850 V-Bucks available via the Item Shop. If you own a Fortnite Crew subscription, you will unlock the Battle Pass for free. If you do not own the paid battle pass you wont get all the rewards but you can still earn a few.

Each Battle Pass level requires 80,000 . Up to 100 levels can be bought for 150 V-Bucks each, with the first 25 being discounted.

Map Changes

Map Changes[]

Update v28.00 (December 3rd 2023)

Introduced the Helios map featuring 11 new named locations including:

  • Classy Courts
  • Fencing Fields
  • Grand Glacier
  • Hazy Hillside
  • Lavish Lair
  • Pleasant Piazza
  • Rebel's Roost
  • Reckless Railways
  • Ritzy Riviera
  • Ruined Reels
  • Snooty Steppes

Five new bosses were also introduced:

  • Nisha at Fencing Fields.
  • Montague at Grand Glacier.
  • Oscar at Lavish Lair.
  • Valeria at Reckless Railways.
  • Peter Griffin at Snooty Steppes.

Loot Island has been brought back and has been redesigned.

Update v28.01 (December 6th 2023)

  • Loot Island has been changed to be made of Ice and have Crackshot's Cabin on it.
  • Hazy Hillside, Grand Glacier, Classy Courts & Ritzy Riviera have been updated with Christmas trees and lights.
  • Sewers have been updated with TMNT designs on them.
  • The boreal forest located on the northern part of the island is covered in snow for the Winterfest 2023 event.

Update v28.01 Hotfix (December 16th 2023)

  • Winterburg has been added.

Update v28.01 Hotfix (January 2nd 2024)

  • Winterburg has been removed along with Crackshot's Cabin on Loot Island and all the Winterfest decorations.

Update v28.10 (January 23rd 2024)

  • The snow around Rebel's Roost, Lavish Lair, half of Hazy Hillside, and half of Classy Courts has melted.
  • Several Unnamed Locations became Landmarks:
    • Catcher's Cove
    • Piney Lane
    • Slumberyard
    • Hazy Hillside Tunnel
  • The Zenith Wall has melted causing the plane to move down.

Update v28.20 (February 7th 2024)

  • The Zenith Wall has melted causing the plane to move down.
  • A car was added between 2 trees as a reference to a Reddit Post.
  • The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle Hideout was added.

Update v28.30 (February 22nd 2024)

  • Some exploration gear was added near the water west of Ruined Reels, including an opened book with an image of Pandora's Box and a telescope pointed towards the named location.
  • The Zenith Wall has melted more, causing the plane to drop into the water below it.

Update v28.30 Hotfix (February 27th 2024)

  • Cracks have bulged out of the ground at the Exploration site near the water west of Ruined Reels.
    • Small Caldera formed along with the earthquake.
      • Earthquakes are happening every hour.
    • February 29th 2024 (3:00 PM ET): A large explosion started happening with every quake.
      • The Caldera slowly started to grow with every quake.
    • February 29th 2024 (12:00 PM ET): The 1st crack has filled with lava.
    • March 1st 2024 (9:00 AM ET): The 2nd crack has filled with lava.
    • March 1st 2024 (6:00 PM ET): The 3rd crack has filled with lava.
    • March 2nd 2024 (3:00 AM ET): The 4th crack has filled with lava.
    • March 2nd 2024 (12:00 PM ET): The 5th crack has filled with lava.
    • March 2nd 2024 (1:00 PM ET): The Caldera has started to emit smoke.
  • (2:00 PM ET): After a series of earthquakes, The Hand finally emerged.
  • March 3rd 2024 (12:50 PM ET) Pandora's box was opened.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • This is the sixth season to start on a Sunday. The last five being Chapter 3: Season 1, Chapter 3: Season 2, Chapter 3: Season 3, Chapter 3: Season 4, and Chapter 4: Season 1.
  • Chapter 5: Season 1 reimplemented Hurdling after being vaulted for almost a year.
  • The Helios map looks similar to the Apollo map.
  • Wolves can be heard in the snowy forests despite being inaccessible.
  • Mark Rein's teaser word for Chapter 5: Season 1 was "#SavePeely".[5]
  • Chapter 5: Season 1 is the first season's Battle Pass that includes a Car Body.
  • The music used in the gameplay trailer is a remix of Unleashed Mayhem by Nicholas J. Tyler.
  • This was the first season with the Train, though it was not used much the entire chapter.
