Overview

“
Enemies that step into this trap get stuck for a short duration. Reactive to lead, so if an enemy gets stuck just give it a shot!
”
— In-Game Description

Tar Pits are a Rare to Legendary Floor Trap in Fortnite: Save the World that were introduced in Update v9.20.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Tar Pit traps immobilize Husks, and when the tar is damaged, it ignites, inflicting Fire damage to all trapped enemies. They have low durability and consume more of it when the flames are triggered. Like the Wooden Floor Spikes, each Husk that steps into a Tar Pit immediately reduces its durability by 1.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Tar Pits stop enemies in place similar to the Wooden Floor Spikes, except devastate them with an elemental blow of fire when triggered by shrapnel (not recommended).
    • Much like the Wooden Floor Spikes, each enemy that steps into the Tar Pit takes away 1 durability each. This is why it is recommended to run all Durability perks on it, since this trap has very low base durability.
  • As such, it’s recommended to not use Tar Pits in the middle of a tunnel, but at the end of one to stop any charging Smashers.
