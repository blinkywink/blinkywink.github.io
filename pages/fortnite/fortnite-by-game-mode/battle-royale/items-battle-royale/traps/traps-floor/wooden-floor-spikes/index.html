Overview

“
Damages and slows all enemies who are standing on the trap.
”
— In-Game Description

Wooden Floor Spikes are a Common to Legendary Trap in Fortnite: Save the World.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Wooden Floor Spikes slow down all enemies standing on them by 30% for a few seconds, or for the distance of ~2.5 tiles. Husklings that are slowed by this trap will temporarily lose their ability to leap towards the player. It deals decent damage to eliminate Husklings alone, and to chip away most HP of off Husks, Pitchers, Beehive Husks, and Zappers.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Wooden Floor Spikes are best used with Ceiling Gas Trap as both deal constant damage, especially in a trap tunnel.
  • It is recommended to place them at the beginning of your trap tunnels so Husks get slowed and spend more time in your tunnels, allowing your other traps to damage them more.
  • Always place a Wooden Floor Spikes trap one tile before a Floor Freeze Trap or Retractable Floor Spikes trap.
    • This will allow bigger targets to not dodge the latter trap because of the slow effect from the Wooden Floor Spikes trap.
