Overview

This article is about the Save the World counterpart. For the Battle Royale counterpart, see Chiller.

“
Freezes enemies in place for a short period of time, and makes them more vulnerable to damage.
”
— In-Game Description

Floor Freeze Traps are a trap that activates when enemies walk over it and it freezes all enemies in its range for short time. It has a somewhat higher reload speed so having more than one is recommended.

Obtain

Obtain[]

  • Floor Freeze Traps can be found in loot chests within a match.
  • Floor Freeze Traps can be dropped from glowing loot Animals.
  • Floor Freeze Trap schematics can be found from Llamas, and the Event/Weekly Store in the Llama Shop.
  • Floor Freeze Trap schematics can be rewarded from missions.
  • Floor Freeze Trap schematics can be researched.
  • Floor Freeze Trap schematics can be evolved into the next rarity.

Tips and Strategies

Tips and Strategies[]

  • This trap is similar to the Tar Pit, with the exception of not being able to stop charging Smashers in their tracks.
  • If perked correctly and used wisely, Floor Freezes can perma-stall enemies.
    • If one places Floor Freezes on the path facing the objective, this will cause husks to freeze for a short amount of time, then get knocked back the opposite direction.
      • If Ice King is in the support slot of your constructor build, it will make this strategy more helpful, as his B.A.S.E. snaring perk will increase stall time when B.A.S.E. is connected to Floor Freezes.
  • Enemies frozen by this trap are more vulnerable to damage from other sources. It's great when paired with Ceiling Gas Traps, and/or Broadsides.
