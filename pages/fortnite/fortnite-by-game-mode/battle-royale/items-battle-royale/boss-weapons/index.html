Boss Weapons

Overview

Since Chapter 2: Season 2, there have been many Bosses present on the map each season. They are typically very hard to defeat, with high amounts of HP and Shield, and when eliminated, they usually drop a Mythic weapon.

Here is a list of Boss Weapons:

  • Non-Collaboration
  • Collaboration
