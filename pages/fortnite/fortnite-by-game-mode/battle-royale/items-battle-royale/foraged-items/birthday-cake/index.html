Overview

This article is about the Foraged Item called Birthday Cake!. For the Back Bling introduced in Season 5, see Birthday Cake.

This page is split into multiple sections, as there are 2 different variants with the same name.

Chapter 1
OG
This item is currently vaulted in Battle Royale.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.
It is still available in Creative and UEFN.

The Birthday Cake! is a Foraged Item in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was introduced in Season 5.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Birthday Cake! heals the player for 10 Health and 10 Shield at the same time.

Birthday Cake! cannot be stored in the players inventory and must be consumed off the ground.
