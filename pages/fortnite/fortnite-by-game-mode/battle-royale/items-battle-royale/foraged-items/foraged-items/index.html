Overview

This article is about consumables, which include Apples. For the Jam Track by Charli xcx, see Apple (Jam Track). For the Emote featuring "Apple" by Charli xcx, see Bratty.

Foraged Items are a subclass of Consumable items in Fortnite: Battle Royale that were introduced in Season 4.

Description

Description[]

Foraged Items have a variety of uses, but are mainly used to increase the player's HP. Other items will apply to the player temporary movement effects (for example, Pepper Effect). Foraged items can be consumed off the ground without picking them up, and some can be stored in the inventory for later usage.

Foraged items are consumed by either pressing the Interact button whilst they are on the ground, or the attack button when stored in the inventory. Foraged Items stored in the inventory can be thrown to other players by holding the aim button. This will mark them for squad members upon landing.
