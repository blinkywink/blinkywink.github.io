Overview

Mythic Items are a special rarity in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They are often attributed to Boss Drops, upgraded versions of regular weapons, or completely unique items.

Description

Description[]

Mythic Items on introduction were unique items with no other existing rarities, often used to denote a completely unique item, or an advanced version of an existing weapon, separate to the actual one. In recent seasons however, the meaning of Mythic has slowly degraded, to the point where it is now considered an extra rarity for existing items, as well unique items.

Rules and Types[]

  • Unique Items - Some Mythic Items are unique, and are labelled as such to show how powerful they are.
  • Collaboration - Many Collaboration Items are Mythic, especially ones which are directly taken from existing media. Collaboration Items made specifically for Fortnite are not Mythic.
  • Boss Items - Many Mythic Items are Boss Drops, meaning they are used by Bosses and dropped by them when eliminated. These are usually more powerful versions of existing weapons, but still separate.
  • Extra Rarity - Some weapons have a Mythic version on top of their Legendary versions, gained through various methods such as Upgrading or a Reality Tree.
