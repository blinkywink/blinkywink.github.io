Overview

This article is about the weapon introduced in Fortnite: Ballistic. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 5, see Modular Drum Gun. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 1, see Drum Gun. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 7, see Drum Gun/Chapter 6.
This item is currently vaulted in Ballistic.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.
It is still available in Creative and UEFN.

The Drum Gun is a Submachine Gun in Fortnite: Ballistic that was added in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Drum Gun is a high damage, fast firing submachine gun with a large magazine capacity and bouncy recoil. It is equipped with a Vertical Foregrip.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, the Drum Gun cost 3,200 Credits.

The Drum Gun is a Hitscan weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Damage falloff:

  • Damage falloff begins at 15 meters (3 tiles).
  • Damage steadily decreases down to 80% of its maximum at 25 meters (5 tiles).
  • Damage reaches its minimum value of 76% of its maximum at 50 meters (10 tiles).

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • With the Drum Gun being the 2nd most expensive weapon in Ballistic, it is important to buy 2 ticks of Instant Shield for added surivability.
  • With its high magazine capacity and fire rate, the Drum Gun is an excellent weapon for suppressive fire, especially strong when combined with Recon Grenades and Smoke Grenades.
  • With its high fire rate and good damage, the Drum Gun can quickly melt through the Bubble Shield's dome or The Wall's segments.

Counters[]

  • Due to its high recoil, the Drum Gun can be very inaccurate, so ARs and accurate SMGs like Nemesis AR or Veiled Precision SMG can outperform it at longer ranges.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Drum Gun shares its model with the Modular Drum Gun, albeit with a different texture and animations. It also lacks a picatinny rail for mounting Optics.
    • Due to this, it resembles the M1 Thompson Submachine Gun.[1]
  • The Drum Gun is one of the 3 weapons in Fortnite: Ballistic that use Iron Sights. The others being Ranger Pistol and Hammer Pump Shotgun.
  • The Drum Gun was the first weapon in Fortnite: Ballistic to have an Underbarrel Mod.
  • Prior to Update v34.00, the Drum Gun would put the player into the First Person Camera when aiming down sights in Third Person.
  • The Drum Gun:
    • Deals the highest damage of any Submachine Gun.
    • Has the highest DPS of any weapon in the game.
    • Has the highest magazine capacity of any weapon in the game.
  • Despite not being a Modular weapon, the Drum Gun and other weapons featured in Fortnite: Ballistic still show what Weapon Mods they have when inspected.
    • Drum Gun uses a unique Drum Mag, internally called Mag Drum Feral in the files. This is why the said Weapon Mod doesn't show when inspecting the Drum Gun.
  • The Drum Gun has a total of twelve unique versions of itself: The basic Drum Gun, Midas' Drum Gun (OG), Jules' Drum Gun, Shadow Midas' Drum Gun, Snoop Dogg's Drum Gun, Modular Drum Gun, Midas' Modular Drum Gun, Midas' Gilded Eye Drum Gun, the Drum Gun added to the files in Chapter 6, Midas' Drum Gun (A hitscan version of Midas' Modular), and the Drum Gun (Ballistic).
