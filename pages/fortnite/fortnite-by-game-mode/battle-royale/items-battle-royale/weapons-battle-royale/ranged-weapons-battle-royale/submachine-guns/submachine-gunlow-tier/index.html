The Submachine Gun is a Submachine Gun in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Season 5.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Submachine Gun is a fast firing, short range, high hip-fire accuracy weapon, in line with most SMGs in the game. Its purpose is to deal high amounts of DPS in a close range environment. The weapon can function at longer ranges, but it suffers from high damage falloff and low accuracy.

Submachine Gun in action

The Submachine Gun is a Hitscan weapon without First Shot Accuracy

The Submachine Gun can be aimed down sights while reloading.

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • The Submachine Gun is ideal paired with a Shotgun, due to their close range abilities, if you do not kill the enemy with the Shotgun, switch to the Submachine Gun to finish them off.
  • The Submachine Gun can be very good at destroying builds, as it has a high DPS, good magazine size and does not lose Structure Damage at range.

Counters[]

  • Keeping distance between you and an opponent using a Submachine Gun can be beneficial, as even when ADS, the Submachine Gun has somewhat poor accuracy.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Submachine Gun has had a confusing life as a weapon in Battle Royale:
    • Before its release in Season 5, the Submachine Gun had existed before in Seasons 0-2, which was technically a separate weapon, but was vaulted after that.
    • Then before v5.00 it was added back as Submachine Gun PDW with an old P90 Icon.[2]
      • Description: Pistol: Machine Pistol. Fires at a very high rate, dealing great damage while consuming a lot of ammo. Good for run-and-gun combat, and accurate enough to fight at mid range when fired in short bursts.
    • Then in later seasons, the Compact SMG was added, which served as the Epic and Legendary versions of the Low Tier Submachine Gun, but was still a different weapon.
    • Finally in Update v11.00, the Compact SMG was vaulted, the Epic and Legendary versions of the Submachine Gun were added with the statistics of the Compact SMG given to them, in order to make them a single weapon.
    • After Update v13.00, the Compact SMG and the Epic and Legendary versions of the Submachine Gun were in the Loot Pool at the same time, with the Compact SMG having worse stats.
      • The Compact SMG was later vaulted in Update v13.20 and later converted into the Epic and Legendary rarities in Update v24.00.
