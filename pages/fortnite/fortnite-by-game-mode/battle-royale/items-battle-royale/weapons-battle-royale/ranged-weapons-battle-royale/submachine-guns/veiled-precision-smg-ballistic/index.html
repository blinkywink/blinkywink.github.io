Overview

This article is about the weapon introduced in Fortnite: Ballistic. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 6: Season 1, see Veiled Precision SMG.
This item is currently vaulted in Ballistic.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.
It is still available in Creative and UEFN.

“
Fully automatic scoped and suppressed SMG. Fires at a high fire rate with high accuracy, dealing moderate damage at close range while quickly consuming the short mag.
”
— In-Game Description

The Veiled Precision SMG is a Submachine Gun in Fortnite: Ballistic that was added in Chapter 6: Season 4.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Veiled Precision SMG is a high damage submachine gun with a medium fire rate and small magazine capacity. It is equipped with a Red Eye Sight and Suppressor.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, the Veiled Precision SMG cost 2,300 Credits.

The Veiled Precision SMG is a Hitscan weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Damage falloff:

  • Damage falloff begins at 10 meters (2 tiles).
  • Damage steadily decreases down to 91% of its maximum at 25 meters (5 tiles).
  • Damage reaches its minimum value of 80% of its maximum at 50 meters (10 tiles).

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Despite being an submachine gun, the Veiled Precision SMG's high damage, relatively low recoil and manageable damage fall-off make it surprisingly effective at medium to long ranges, easily beating out other weapons in its class.
  • Like the Drum Gun, the Veiled Precision SMG can eliminate fully shielded opponents with 2 headshots at longer ranges.

Counters[]

  • The Veiled Precision SMG has a noticeably longer time to kill for an SMG, due to its slower fire rate. This means that it will struggle in close range combat against faster firing SMGs and ARs.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Veiled Precision SMG shares its model with the Veiled Precision SMG, albeit with a different texture and animations.
    • Due to this, it resembles the PSA X5.7.
  • The Veiled Precision SMG was the second weapon added to Ballistic to be based on a Chapter 6 design, with the first one being the DL-44 Blaster Pistol.
    • Since the DL-44 was a temporary third-party collaboration item, the Veiled Precision SMG is therefore the first permanent weapon added to utilise a Chapter 6 design.
  • Despite not being based on a Modular weapon, the Veiled Precision SMG still has visible weapon mod slots and will sometimes show equipped mods when inspected.
  • The Veiled Precision SMG's Red Eye Sight and Suppressor disappear when the weapon is dropped on the ground.
  • The Veiled Precision SMG is the only Ballistic weapon to be rated higher than one star (typical for a Common item), instead being rated three stars (typical for a Rare item).
