Submachine Guns

Overview

Submachine Guns, or SMGs for short, are a type of weapon in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The purpose of these weapons is for short range to medium range combat. Most SMGs use Light Bullets, with a select few using Energy Cells and Medium Bullets.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Submachine Guns fill the role of a short to medium range weapon and are often used for up close engagements, due to their high damage per second. Some SMGs have first shot accuracy, but due to their low DPS rates and inaccuracy whilst aiming down sights, they are most useful for attacking opponents in close combat. On the contrary, SMGs do have excellent hipfire accuracy. This allows them to excel at close ranges and makes it very easy to hit consistent shots on a target. SMGs added in Chapter 5 fire fast-moving projectiles, while those added before and after Chapter 5 are hitscan.

Augments

Augments[]

The following Augments apply to Submachine Guns:

Icon Augment Name Effect Description
Rifle Recycle Weapons using medium ammo have a chance not to consume ammo. Each shot fired from a weapon that uses Medium Bullets has a 20% chance to not consume ammo. Applies only to the Drum Gun and its variants.
Light Fingers Weapons using light ammo reload faster. Decreases reload time by 25% for weapons that use Light Bullets. Applies to most Submachine Guns.
Medium Ammo Amp Medium Ammo weapons have increased magazine size. Increases magazine size by 20% for weapons that use Medium Bullets. Applies only to the Drum Gun and its variants.
SMG Signoff The last 4 bullets of your SMG magazines deals bonus damage. The last four shots from any Submachine Gun's magazine deal 25% more damage.
SMG Extended Mag SMGs have greatly increased magazine size. Increases magazine size by 20% for all Submachine Guns.
Reckless SMG Reload SMGs reload faster when reloaded with an empty clip. Submachine Guns are reloaded 33% faster when their magazine runs out of ammo.
Scoped Salvo Scoped weapons deal increased damage but have decreased fire rate. Submachine Guns with a scope deal 15% more damage but their fire rate is decreased by 15%.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • When the Submachine Gun was introduced, it did not come in Epic or Legendary rarities, however the Compact SMG existed in only those rarities. Due to this, the guns are separate, but as of Chapter 2: Season 1 the Compact SMG was vaulted, and an Epic and Legendary Submachine Gun were introduced with the same model as the Compact SMG, however it had a faster rate of fire but had a smaller magazine size then the Compact SMG.
  • The Compact SMG and high tier Submachine Gun share the same model, but use a different render for their icons.
  • The Machine SMG received a new look for Chapter 2, despite it not returning as of Chapter 2: Season 5's launch.
  • Although not included here, the Legacy Drum Gun was originally an SMG, but was re-classed as an Assault Rifle. Despite this, it is possible to complete SMG based challenges with a Legacy Drum Gun, as well as it sitting beside other SMGs in Creative.
  • The Machine SMG was originaly called the Machine Pistol but its name was changed in Chapter 4: Season 1.
  • Prior to Update v27.00, if you had the Submachine Gun in Epic or Legendary rarities in Creative Mode in an Item Granter or Vending Machine when the P90 variant was removed from the Creative inventory, it would have the same model and icon.
  • In Chapter 4: Season 3, you could obtain the Submachine Gun in Team Rumble, where it retained the P90 model.
