The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle is a Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Chapter 6: Mini Season 1.

The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle has a Mythic version called Enhanced A280-CFE Blaster Rifle.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle is a slow firing assault rifle, equipped with a custom scope, compatible with Canting. The weapon has infinite ammo but will overheat after firing a few shots and will need to cool down. The cooldown period can be manually entered by pressing the reload key after shooting for a bit.

The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle can continuously fire for 5.18 seconds or 22 shots before it overheats. This can be extended by waiting a bit between each shot.

The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle is a Hitscan weapon, with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Phasma's Blaster Medallion increases the amount of shots the A280-CFE Blaster Rifle can fire continuously from 22 to 28 and decreases its cooldown time from 1.83s to 1.46s.
  • The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle is best used in a similar manner to the Red-Eye Assault Rifle, acting as a sort of Assault Rifle/DMR hybrid, with a slow fire rate, average body damage, and a higher zoom scope.
  • The rangefinder on the scope of the A280-CFE Blaster Rifle can be very useful when playing in modes with teammates, as you can call out enemy positions to teammates and the exact distance without marking them. This is especially important when actively engaging with an enemy, as often you won't have a chance to live ping them for your team.

Counters[]

  • The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle, while hitscan, can struggle in accuracy at very far ranges, so using a more suitable weapon at farther ranges such as the IQA-11 Marksman Blaster Rifle, can be beneficial when engaged with an opponent wielding the A280-CFE Blaster Rifle.
  • The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle also struggles in close-quarters engagements due to it's slow fire rate, so using a more suitable weapon such as the E-11 Blaster or CR-2 Blaster can be beneficial against an opponent with a A280-CFE Blaster Rifle.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle is a modified AR-15 platform rifle, capable of being converted from a heavy blaster pistol into a blaster rifle.
  • The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle has an unused Prototype version in the files.
    • Its icon uses the Hammer Assault Rifle as a reference.
    • Its in-game description is Perhaps a scope will help Stormtroopers hit their target.
  • The A280-CFE Blaster Rifle's scope is considered a Weapon Mod, likely to allow Canting.
    • The scope's internal name is Jethro ACOG Optic and is based off the P2X Optic, even reusing its icon.
