Assault Weapons

Overview

Assault Weapons, alternatively known as Assault Rifles, are a type of weapon in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The purpose of these weapons is for medium range to long range combat with most weapons using Medium Bullets and some using Light Bullets. Some are suited better for close ranged and medium range.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Assault Weapons are typically an all-round class of weapons that are used for medium ranged combat and quickly destroying structures. Assault Weapons often have first shot accuracy, meaning they can fire their first bullet without any bloom. All of the Assault Weapons (except Chapter 5 Assault Rifles) are hitscan weapons, meaning the shots the player takes will instantly connect with targets. The medium to high capacity of Assault Rifle magazines means continuous fire can be laid down on targets or structures, allowing for the player to easily break structures or kill an opponent at closer ranges.

Assault Weapons do not fare well at close ranges due to their low hipfire accuracy and sometimes slow fire-rates. Due to this, other weapons such as shotguns or submachine guns fill the role better.

Assault Weapons also contain LMG-type weapons such as the Light Machine Gun and Drum Gun. The Drum Gun is unclear on what type it is so it acts as an SMG and Assault Rifle at the same time.

Augments[]

The following Augments apply to Assault Weapons:

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • At less than 50 meters, assault Weapons deliver maximum damage; however, as shot range increases, damage output decreases. At the maximum range of 100 meters, the assault rifle will only deliver 65% of its typical damage.
  • Players might consider aiming for the head with the first shot, as its accuracy is the best. Subsequent shots tend to be less accurate, so aiming for the body thereafter is suggested.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Drum Gun is unique among this class as it seems to be two classes at once. Although it is classed as an Assault Rifle by its in game classification icon, it will still count for SMG based challenges, and also is in the same place as the other SMGS in the creative menu, with the AR icon still present. This is because the gun was originally an SMG, but later updated to be an Assault Rifle, and so acts as a hybrid between the two.
    • It has a similar function to the Light Machine Gun since both guns have similar fire rates and higher magazines than traditional assault weapons.
  • There is an unobtainable Exotic version of the Striker AR called Hitscan AR.
  • The Infantry Rifle was originally a projectile based weapon like most Sniper Rifles. It would later be updated to hitscan to match the other Assault Weapons in Update v8.20.
