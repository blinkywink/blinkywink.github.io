The Enhanced Flapjack Rifle is an Assault Rifle available in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Chapter 4: Season 3.

Enhanced Flapjack Rifle is the Mythic version of the Flapjack Rifle.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Enhanced Flapjack Rifle is a light machine gun that features a large 45-round magazine, fast fire rate and bonus structure damage. It is suitable for use at close and medium range, but the bouncy recoil and harsh damage falloff make it hard to use at longer distances. In addition, due to the large magazine size, reloading takes somewhat longer than most assault rifles.

The Enhanced Flapjack Rifle is a Hitscan weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.
