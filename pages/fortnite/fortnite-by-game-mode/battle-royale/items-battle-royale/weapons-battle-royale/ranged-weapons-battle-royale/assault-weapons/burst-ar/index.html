Overview

This article is about the weapon featured in Fortnite: Ballistic. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 5, see Modular Striker Burst Rifle. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 3 and Chapter 6, see Striker Burst Rifle. For the weapon of a similar name, see Burst Assault Rifle.
This item is currently vaulted in Ballistic.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.
It is still available in Creative and UEFN.

The Burst AR is an Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Ballistic, that was added in Chapter 6: Season 2.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Burst AR is a high damage, 3 round burst assault rifle equipped with a Holo-13 Optic and Angled Foregrip.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, the Burst AR cost 2,300 Credits.

The Burst AR is a Hitscan weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Damage falloff:

  • Damage falloff begins at 10 meters (2 tiles).
  • Damage steadily decreases down to 90% of its maximum at 25 meters (5 tiles).
  • Damage reaches its minimum value of 84% of its maximum at 50 meters (10 tiles).

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Unlike the Nemesis AR and Enforcer AR, the Burst AR has damage fall-off. This means that at a range of 10 meters and above, the weapon will lose its ability to 3 shot enemies with extra 50 shield to the body. This is why when playing with the Burst AR, its recommended to always aim at head level past that distance.
  • Even with damage fall-off, the Burst AR's low recoil and 3 round burst still allows it to be effective at medium to long ranges.
  • The Burst AR is the second cheapest AR in the game, with the cheapest being the Enforcer AR, making this gun very good for early rounds.

Counters[]

  • When engaged with an opponent wielding the Burst AR, using a weapon with an easier recoil pattern and no damage fall-off, such as the Nemesis AR or Enforcer AR, can be viable.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Burst AR re-uses its model from the Modular Striker Burst Rifle, albeit with a different paintjob, different animations, no optic riser for the Holo-13 Optic and a left sided charging handle.
    • Due to this, it resembles the Steyr AUG-A3[1].
  • The Burst AR is the second weapon in Ballistic to use an Underbarrel Weapon Mod, with the first one being the Drum Gun.
    • It is also the first weapon to use the Angled Foregrip Weapon Mod.
  • In the Update v34.30 patch notes, Burst AR incorrectly uses the icon from the Modular Striker Burst Rifle.
  • Despite not being a Modular weapon, the Burst AR and other weapons featured in Fortnite: Ballistic still show what Weapon Mods they have when inspected.
