The Striker AR is an Assault Weapon in Fortnite: Ballistic that was added in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Striker AR is a medium damage, high fire rate assault rifle suited for close to medium range combat. It is equipped with a Red Eye Sight and Suppressor.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, the Striker AR cost 2,700 Credits.

The Striker AR is a Hitscan weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Damage falloff:

  • Damage falloff begins at 10 meters (2 tiles).
  • Damage steadily decreases down to 87% of its maximum at 25 meters (5 tiles).
  • Damage reaches its minimum value of 77% of its maximum at 50 meters (10 tiles).

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Due to the Striker AR being the 3rd most expensive weapon in Ballistic, it is important to buy 2 ticks of Instant Shield for added surivability.
  • Unlike the Nemesis AR and Enforcer AR, the Striker AR has damage fall-off. This means that at a range of 15 meters and above, the weapon will lose its ability to one-shot headshot enemies with 50 shield. This is why when playing with the Striker AR, its optimal to avoid long range parts of the map, like Mid or Long-A on Skyline 10, to keep that consistent one-shot headshot.

Counters[]

  • When engaged with an opponent using a Striker AR, keeping to mid-long range with a weapon such as an Enforcer AR, Nemesis AR or Reaper Sniper Rifle can be very beneficial, as all of them can deliver a 1 shot elimination to the head past the Striker's effective headshot range.
  • Within close range and combined with their jumping accuracy, the Hammer Pump Shotgun and Frenzy Auto Shotgun can be strong counters to the Striker AR.
