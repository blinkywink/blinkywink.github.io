List of Item Redesigns

Overview

A large number of weapons and items were updated to fit the new art style, or because they looked too similar to other items, potentially causing confusion. For example, the icon for the Modular Enforcer AR was changed because it appeared too similar to the Reaper Modular Sniper Rifle.
Accidental changes such as the Hop Rock Dualies' icon reverting to that of the default Dual Pistols are not listed.
Low Tier = Common, Uncommon and Rare.
High Tier = Epic and Legendary.
Below is a complete list of every gun and item that got their icon and/or in-game model changed.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Update v11.00 has the most icon changes (20) followed closely by Update v28.01.01 (10)
  • Balloons are so far the only item to ever have their icon and model reverted back to their original.
  • The icon for the new Midas' Drum Gun was changed before being officially added to the game.
  • The icon and model of the high tier Burst Assault Rifle were reused for the Striker Burst Rifle.
  • The Compact SMG received the new icon instead of the Submachine Gun.
