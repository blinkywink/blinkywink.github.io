Backpack Items

Overview

Backpacks are a subclass of Miscellaneous Items in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They are items that take the place of the player's back bling when picked up.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Like every other weapon and item, backpacks take up an inventory slot. You cannot carry more than one of some backpacks at a time (eg you can only carry one jetpack), and attempting to pick one up will swap it with the one you are currently holding.

While a Backpack is equipped, the player's Back Bling will be hidden. Their Back Bling will re-appear once the Backpack item is dropped.
