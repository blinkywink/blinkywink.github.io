Pistols

Overview

Pistols are a type of weapon in Fortnite: Battle Royale. These weapons can serve a variety of different combat purposes. Pistols typically use Light Bullets, but some use Medium Bullets or Heavy Bullets

Functionality

Functionality[]

Pistols are the most versatile weapons in Battle Royale, as they have a range of uses across all ranges. Some pistols are for closer ranges, have high fire-rate caps and good hipfire, whilst others are for long range, have slow fire-rates and deal high damage at distance. Depending on the Pistol in your hand, the uses it will have in combat have great variation across the board. All Pistols (except Chapter 5 Pistols) are hitscans, meaning the shot can directly damage their target on firing.

Augments

Augments[]

The following Augments apply to Pistols:

Icon Augment Name Effect Description
Rifle Recycle Weapons using medium ammo have a chance not to consume ammo. Each shot fired from a weapon that uses Medium Bullets has a 20% chance to not consume ammo. Applies to some Pistols, primarily revolvers.
Pistol Amp Pistols have greatly increased magazine size. Increases magazine size by 50% for all Pistols.
Light Fingers Weapons using light ammo reload faster. Decreases reload time by 25% for weapons that use Light Bullets. Applies to most Pistols.
Mythic Munition Receive a Mythic Tactical Pistol
Last Shots The last two bullets of your pistol magazine deals bonus damage. The last two shots from any Pistol's magazine deal 50% more damage.
Medium Ammo Amp Medium Ammo weapons have increased magazine size. Increases magazine size by 20% for weapons that use Medium Bullets. Applies to some Pistols, primarily revolvers.
Tracker's Armory Grants a Flare Gun and Shadow Tracker Pistol.
Go For Broke Grants a Flint-Knock Pistol and a Charge Shotgun.
Pistol Recycle Pistols have a chance to not cost ammo. Each shot fired from a Pistol has a 25% chance to not consume ammo.
Desperate Reload Pistols reload faster when reloaded with an empty clip. Pistols are reloaded 33% faster when their magazine runs out of ammo.
Heavy Headshots Heavy Ammo weapons deal increased headshot damage. Weapons that use Heavy Bullets deal 15% more damage on headshots. Applies to some Pistols.
Scoped Salvo Scoped weapons deal increased damage but have decreased fire rate. Pistols with a scope deal 15% more damage but their fire rate is decreased by 15%. Stacks with Pistol Salvo.
Pistol Salvo Pistols have reduced fire rate but increased damage. Pistols deal 15% more damage but their fire rate is decreased by 15%. Stacks with Scoped Salvo.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Both the Modular Monarch Pistol and Revolver used to consume Light Bullets before being changed to Medium Bullets to fit their damage profiles, which were too high for that of weapons typically consuming Light Bullets.
