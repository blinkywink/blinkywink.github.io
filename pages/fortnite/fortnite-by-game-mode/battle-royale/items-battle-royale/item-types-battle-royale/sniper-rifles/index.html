Sniper Rifles

Overview

Sniper Rifles are a type of weapon in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The purpose of these weapons is for long range combat. Most Sniper Rifles use Heavy Bullets, while some use Medium Bullets.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Sniper Rifles fill the role of a long range weapon and are often used to attack opponents at ranges where un-scoped weapons cannot easily see them. Most Sniper Rifles have a projectile bullet, meaning that the bullet does not instantly hit where the player aims. The projectile is affected by gravity, and different sniper rifles fire different speed projectiles. Exceptions include the Rail Gun and Zapotron. Sniper Rifles do not have fall-off damage, meaning they deal the same damage up close as they do at any range, allowing for most of them knock a player at any range with two well placed shots. Scoped Sniper Rifles, except for the Reaper Sniper Rifle equipped with an optic that is not a Sniper Scope, emit a Sniper Glare from the scope when aiming to warn others of a potential sniper shot.

Augments

Augments[]

The following Augments apply to Sniper Rifles:

Icon Augment Name Effect Description
Rifle Recycle Weapons using medium ammo have a chance not to consume ammo. Each shot fired from a weapon that uses Medium Bullets has a 20% chance to not consume ammo. Applies only to the Automatic Sniper Rifle.
Bloodhound Enemies hit by marksman rifle, bow and sniper shots are marked for a brief duration.
Sniper Surplus Sniper Rifles gain 1 extra ammo in their magazine.
Medium Ammo Amp Medium Ammo weapons have increased magazine size. Increases magazine size by 20% for weapons that use Medium Bullets. Applies only to the Automatic Sniper Rifle.
Heavy Headshots Heavy Ammo weapons deal increased headshot damage. Weapons that use Heavy Bullets deal 15% more damage on headshots. Applies to most Sniper Rifles.
Fire Armory Grants a Dragon's Breath Shotgun and Dragon's Breath Sniper.
Scoped Salvo Scoped weapons deal increased damage but have decreased fire rate. Sniper Rifles with a scope deal 15% more damage but their fire rate is decreased by 15%.
Sniper Striker Snipers give Siphon on hit against players. Sniper Rifles restore Effective Health when hitting opponents. Healing scales with damage dealt.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Zapotron was a hitscan laser type weapon and there is only one clip of its existence. It was removed shortly after. It is also the only weapon in Battle Royale outside of LTMs to use Energy Ammo.
  • Eliminating a player without aiming down sights with any of these weapons shows a 'NO SCOPED' message in the Message Feed. This is in reference to the concept of 'no-scoping' popularized by the Call of Duty franchise.
  • The Rail Gun and the Zapotron are the only two Sniper Rifles to fire a Hitscan bullet, meaning it will connect instantly with its target after the weapon is fired. All other Sniper Rifles fire a physical projectile with travel time.
  • In Update v22.00, all Sniper Rifles were buffed to have increased Headshot multipliers, and deal more damage, after many were nerfed to be unable to one shot players at 100 HP and 100 Shields.
  • Sniper Rifles have been fully Vaulted in only two seasons, being Chapter 2: Season 6 and Chapter 5: Season 4. This makes them the largest weapon pool to be Vaulted fully.
  • Most Sniper Rifles have had their scope updated to that of the Hunter Bolt-Action Sniper.
