Overview

Devices are a category of Objects in Fortnite: Battle Royale. These are stationary props which cannot be picked up and stored in the inventory, but perform a certain function.

Functionality

Functionality[]

When their conditions of use are fulfilled, Devices benefit the player in some way, normally by applying a movement effect. Devices cannot be destroyed by any means. Most of them have Infinite Durability and Health, but some go on a cooldown after being used.

Details[]

  • Reboot Van - Allows you to reboot eliminated players from your squad (Reboot Card Required).
  • Phone Booth - Disguises the player as a Henchman.
  • IO Scanner Console - Scans a very large area for a short time when interacted with. Found at Mole Teams (1 Minute Cooldown After Use).
  • Gas Pump - Used to refuel Vehicles. Found at Gas Stations.
  • Keycard Reader (No Image) - Opens the Vault once a Keycard is inserted. Found at Faction Bases.
  • Squad Lock - Get your Squad together to Unlock a Vault.
  • Rift - Teleports the player high into the air with Glider Redeploy.
  • Spire Jumpboots Spawner - Spawns Spire Jumpboots when a Runestone is inputted. Found at The Spire.
  • Hologram Pads - Applies a Movement Effect to the Player or Activates a Rift.
  • Sideways Anomaly - Shoots tentacles at players in the vicinity, then activates a Sideways Bubble.
  • Alien Launchpad - Launches the Player in a Certain Direction with Glider Redeploy Upon Entering it. Found at Abducted Locations and in The Mothership.
  • Spire Tower Launchpad - Launches the player forward with Glider Redeploy Upon Entering it. Found at Guardian Towers.
  • Corruption Vent - Launches the player upwards with Glider Redeploy upon entering it. Found in the large Sideways Bubble.
  • Hideout - Allows the Player to Jump Into it and Hide. Some of these Teleport you to Another Hideout.
