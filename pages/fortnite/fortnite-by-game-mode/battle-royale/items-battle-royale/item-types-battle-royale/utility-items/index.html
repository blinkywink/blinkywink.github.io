Utility Items

Utility Items

Overview

Utility Items are a type of item in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The purpose of these items is for ranged usage, but for more niche utility purposes.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Utility Items are usually items which are used for ranged combat or other purposes, but the definition of utility is far from strict. The items in this category are not all weapons, but some could have been defined as them given their specific characteristics. The term 'Utility' is not well defined in game, and some items do not fill this role, or could easily fit another role entirely.
