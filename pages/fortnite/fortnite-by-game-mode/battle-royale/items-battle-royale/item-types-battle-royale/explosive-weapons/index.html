Explosive Weapons

Overview

Explosive Weapons are a type of weapon in Fortnite: Battle Royale. The primary purpose of these weapons is to deal splash damage and destroy structures. Most Explosive Weapons use Rockets as ammunition, with a few exceptions.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Explosive Weapons fill the role of a variety of different weapon ranges, depending on which explosive weapon is being used and for what purpose. All explosive weapons deal Splash Damage, meaning that, from where the projectile lands, there will be a small spherical radius surrounding which will also deal damage. Explosive Weapons excel in Structure Damage, and many of them have different statistics for player versus structure damages. Players are also able to hurt themselves with Explosive Weapons, which limits their close range capabilities.

Some Explosive Weapons also have the unique mechanic of 'Rocket Riding', which allows a player to stand atop an explosive projectile and follow along with it until it detonates in the air or on a surface.

Other Explosive Weapons such as Kit's Shockwave Launcher and the Paint Launchers deal no damage to structures or players, as their uses are either for mobility or fun.

Strategy Guide

Strategy Guide[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Most Explosive Weapons are able to damage the player, so caution is advised when firing them near yourself.
  • Firing an explosive weapon, especially one with a slow projectile, can be used to destroy a wall and instantly attack those behind it. This is done by shooting a slow moving rocket or grenade, readying your other weapon, and firing as soon as the projectile destroys the wall.
  • Shooting most explosive weapons under a player will allow them to 'ride' the projectile. This can be used to move players from one area to another with great speed, such as out of the storm.
  • Explosive weapons are very useful in small storm circles, as the limited space makes it more difficult for opponents to avoid the blast, increasing the likelihood of landing a hit.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Explosives Weapon Class is the only weapon category without any suppressed weapons.
  • The Motorboat's rockets and the Saucer's Laser Cannon also count toward explosives-related Quests.
  • The Boom Bow and TNTina's Ka-Boom Bow were classified as Explosive Weapons until Chapter 2: Season 6, when they were reclassified as Bows.
  • The Common Rocket Launcher is the shortest-lived Explosive Weapon, being removed in a hotfix for the Chapter 2: Season 1 release update.
  • Although the Grenade Launchers fire grenades as projectiles, they still use Rocket Ammo for balancing reasons.
  • The Cluster Clinger, while being very similar to the Clinger, is considered a Utility Item.
