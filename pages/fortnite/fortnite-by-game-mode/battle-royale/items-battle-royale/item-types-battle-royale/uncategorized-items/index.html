Uncategorized Items

Overview

Every item in Fortnite has a category, even if the item itself doesn't function as its category specifies. However, many items in the game do not have a listed category, and instead are uncategorized. This page details those items and attempts to group them loosely in a way not defined in-game.

It is unclear why items do not get categories, as many of them could easily have their own category, or fill the role of others mentioned in game.

This page also includes items which may have a category, but due to them being inaccessible, are not known for categorization purposes.
