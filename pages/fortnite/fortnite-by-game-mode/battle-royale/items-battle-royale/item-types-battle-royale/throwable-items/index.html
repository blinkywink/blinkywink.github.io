Throwable Items

Throwable Items

Overview

Throwable Items are a type of item in Fortnite: Battle Royale that are used by throwing them. The purpose of these items varies greatly. It is not an official category in game.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Throwable Items are used in a variety of ways, from healing to explosive damage. All throwable items are thrown by either pressing the attack button and using the visual arc to guide where they will land, or doing the same with the aim button.

Many throwable items will not deploy when thrown into water.
