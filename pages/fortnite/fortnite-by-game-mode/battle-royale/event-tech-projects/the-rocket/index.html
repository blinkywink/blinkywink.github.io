Overview

The Rockets are spacecrafts built and used by The Seven. They were first introduced during Season 4.

Overview

Overview[]

Rockets are spacecraft created and used by The Seven to travel through space-time through Rifts. The very first glimpse was originally a simple set for the film Prepare for Collision, before being transformed into an actual functioning rocket by The Visitor after his escapade. Ironically, it turns out that the fake rocket in the film resembled the model used by The Seven, except for some missing elements.

History

History[]

Season 4[]

The rocket is built as a movie prop for the superhero film "Prepare for Collision". The Visitor later modified it into an actual rocket able to travel throught Rifts and seems to have used Hop Rocks in the process. It blasted off during Blast Off Event and ended to create the Sideways Rift.

Season X[]

In order to contain the Zero Point, The Seven (except The Paradigm) worked together across space-time to enact a plan. The Scientist and The Visitor would first work in Reality Zero in the extra loop was to take over Dusty Depot and create Rift Beacons to open 6 Rifts. In the meantime, a new rocket was built as well while 6 others were built elsewhere in the Omniverse. After The Visitor finished to study the B.R.U.T.E and left, The Scientist went in The Loop, in order for him to enact his plan, by triggering the "device", leaving himself tapes in order to remember in case he would lose his memories.

Then during The End, The Scientist launched his rocket, in a similar manner to The Visitor's, but entered a huge Rift in the sky, a Rift that activated the other six and created a seventh, from which emerged 6 other rockets as well as the Scientist's. Coordinating, the Seven used their rockets to undo the meteor's time-freeze and rift it away before one of them launched into Zero Point, while the other six, behind, accompanied the meteor to collide with Zero Point before rifting away.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

After The Seven settled on Artemis, at an unspecified time after Season X, they established the Launchpad, a location in the Sanctuary designed for rocket launches. Over the season, several of them were launched in an attempt to recover a weapon in space, but failed following Imagined Order sabotage.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

A rocket can be seen on The Ice Moon Base.

Chapter 4: Season OG[]

Travelling back in time and getting help from this timeline's version of The Seven, Jones build a new rocket at Dusty Divot a rocket that would this time be capable of traveling through time in a controlled manner, thanks to Thorne's time machine.

During this world version of The End, Jones would use the time machine of his rocket to travel in order to travel back in time and be Seven and undo again the time-freeze of the meteor, then using the temporal unstability effect caused the meteor to constantly change state, generating a new Big Bang, rather than a Black Hole.

OG Chapter 1: Season 4[]

A version of the Visitor arrived in an Unknown Reality similar to Zero. This version is notably owning a whole new armor but also remembers to have already visited locations such as Dusty Divot and Risky Reels, as if he already lived Season 4 or similar events.

Like his counterpart did in Reality Zero during Season 4, he plans to use the fake rocket of the set to transform it into a new real rocket.
