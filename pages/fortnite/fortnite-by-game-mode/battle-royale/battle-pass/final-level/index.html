Overview

The Final Level (mainly as Level 100, previously and most commonly known as Tier 100) is the final Level in a Battle Pass. Before Season X, it was not possible to progress further than Tier 100 in a Battle Pass. However, since Chapter 2: Season 1, it has been possible in order to unlock extra styles for the Battle Pass Outfits.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Officially, the name of Battle Pass Tiers were changed to Levels in Chapter 2: Season 1 as that system had merged with the XP/Levelling System.
  • From Chapter 2: Season 7 to Chapter 3: Season 4, the Level 100 has been a Collaboration cosmetic.
  • Some seasons did not have level 100 outfits. These seasons include:
    • Chapter 2: Season 4, containing the Tony Stark Awakening Challenges.
    • Chapter 2: Season 5, containing The Child pet and a style for Mandalorian.
    • Chapter 3: Season 1, containing a style for Spider-Man.
    • All seasons from Chapter 4: Season 1 and beyond.
