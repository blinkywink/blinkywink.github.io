Overview

This article is about the second season of Fortnite OG Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 2, see Season 2 (disambiguation).

“
Relive Battle Royale from its beginnings in Chapter 1 Season 2. Drop back in and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG seasons!
”
— In-Game Description

OG Chapter 1: Season 2 is the second season of Fortnite OG. It started on January 31st 2025 and ended on March 25th 2025.

The season has the slogan Go Full Tilt.

OG Pass

OG Pass[]

The OG Pass is a seasonal cosmetic item bundle in Fortnite OG, which grants the player rewards in exchange for .

OG Pass items are not exclusive to the season in which they are offered and are likely going to be added to the Item Shop sometime after the season ends. However, players are unable to access OG Pass from past seasons.

Map Updates

Map Updates[]

Update v33.30

January 31st 2025

  • Re-introduced the 5 locations from Season 2, which include:
    • Tilted Towers
    • Shifty Shafts
    • Snobby Shores
    • Haunted Hills
    • Junk Junction
  • Re-introduced the Unnamed Locations from Season 2, which include:
    • Convenience Store
    • Starry Suburbs Ruins
    • Sports Center
    • Motel
    • Umbrella Mine
    • Metal Llama
    • Yonder Yard
    • Outskirts
    • Blurry Billboards
  • Added new biomes:
    • Boreal Forest
    • Northern Houses
    • Forest
    • Swamp
  • Giant Chair is now bigger.
  • Tomato Tunnel now includes an emergency staircase.
  • Added some more huts around the Island.
  • Pleasant Manor was updated.
  • The elevated hut on a hill near Retail Row was added.
