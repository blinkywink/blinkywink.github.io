Overview

OG Chapter 1: Season 5 is the fifth season of Fortnite OG that started on August 7th 2025 and ended on October 2nd 2025[1] (formerly October 3rd 2025).

The season has the slogan Worlds Collide.

The Storyline of OG

The Storyline of OG[]

Season 4[]

In Season 4, The Crack in the Sky created by the Blast Off event connected Reality: Unknown directly to other realities, and within days before Season 5 began, rifts began forming around the island, rifting away objects from the island and bringing in new ones from other realities.

The Season Begins[]

At the beginning of the season, entire locations from different realities were brought to the island through the Crack in the Sky. Moisty Mire was replaced with a giant desert, along with a large resort town, a mexican-like town, scrapyard, and a cafe. The dirt racetrack to the east of Retail Row was revamped and upgraded to a full-scale concrete motorcourse track. Two more major changes occurred to the island. The farm Anarchy Acres was replaced with a luxury golf club called Lazy Links, and a large Viking Village was dropped on top of a mountain near Snobby Shores, complete with a large viking ship. Dusty Divot became overgrown with vegetation, and the research facility there started to fall apart and decay, same with the Hero Mansion and Villain Lair. The Durr Burger head that was transported into the real world was, again, sucked through a rift and transported back to the island, and could have been found on top of a hill near Pleasant Park. Around each of the affected areas, rifts could have been found, which transported loopers high up into the sky when entered.

This Season[]

After the Crack in the Sky brought the locations from different realities into the Island, it started shrinking and closing, and in the process, becoming deep red and sending lightning strikes in a mountain east of Fatal Fields in the Desert. When the Crack in the Sky was nearly done closing it sent it's last lightning strike, which was the longest, bringing in Revin the Cube, then finally closing.

Revin the Cube started rolling around the Island, burning runes into the soil of the Island with the purpose of gathering power, then sinking into the Loot Lake, where the Zero Point was, so it could start the next phase of it's plan in Season 6.

August 7th 2025

  • Moisty Mire was replaced by the Desert, bringing in a new city with it, Paradise Palms, located at the place of the Stagecoach originally rifted into Moisty Mire.
