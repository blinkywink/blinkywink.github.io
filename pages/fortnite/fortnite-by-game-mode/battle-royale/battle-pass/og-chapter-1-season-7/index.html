Overview

This article is about the seventh season of Fortnite OG Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 7, see Season 7 (disambiguation).

“
Relive Battle Royale from its beginnings in Chapter 1 Season 7. Drop back in and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG seasons!
”
— In-Game Description

OG Chapter 1: Season 7 is the seventh season of Fortnite OG that started on December 11th 2025 and ended on March 31st 2026 (originally March 18th 2026).

The season has the slogan You Better Watch Out.

The Storyline of OG

The Storyline of OG[]

Season 6[]

In Season 6, Kevin the Cube rose up with the Floating Island, and eventually exploded due to a sudden overload and loss of power, sending the loopers to The In-Between, where Revin the Cube could be found. After those events, an iceberg appeared south of the island, slowly approaching until the end of the season. It’s still a mystery as to why the iceberg appeared, but it is know that it broke off the ice moon surrounding the loop bubble. This comes from Issue #4 of the Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War comic book series.

Meanwhile in Reality: 783, a massive conflict took place, where Jones rifted Revin away from The In-Between to Oninoshima to help defeat the Dark Presence by blowing up. Later, in Reality: 266, the Battle Bus was destroyed by the Dark Voyager, causing Reality: Unknown to be temporarily inaccessible until the iceberg inevitably reached the island.

This Season[]

The Ice Moon arrives in Reality: Unknown just as it did on Athena. Hidden within Polar Peak and the surrounding area lies The Infinity Blade, the eggs of the Hybrids who would become The Brood, and the chains and cell of The Prisoner. As time progressed, the ice would begin to melt, exposing these ancient items and creatures. Meanwhile, Void Walker Lynx watches the Snow Biome and warns her trainee of the boss watching them.

The Ice King, looking with a vengeance, emerges into his Icy Sphere, preparing an Ice Storm as he grows and mixes his energy with a shard of The Cube. The clock ticking down, he breaks free, and utilizing a giant hologram, snow encompasses the Island with spawners coming up from the ground, allowing the Ice Legion to run amuck once more, all in an attempt to to preserve the Prisoner & all the artifacts he has collected. Meanwhile, just as Lynx forewarned, the Dark Voyager watches his plan succeed.

The Ice Legion plan is somewhat successful. Although The Prisoner succeeds in grabbing the Hybrid eggs and the formation of faults in the earth to create the Volcano and Lagoon, he is recaptured and placed back into ice alongside other captured foes of the Kingdom.
