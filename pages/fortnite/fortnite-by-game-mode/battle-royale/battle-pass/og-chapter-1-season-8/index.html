Overview

This article is about the eighth season of Fortnite OG Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 8, see Season 8 (disambiguation).

“
Relive Battle Royale from its beginnings in Chapter 1 Season 8. Drop back in and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG seasons!
”
— In-Game Description

OG Chapter 1: Season 8 is the eighth season of Fortnite OG that started on April 1st 2026 and will end on June 24th 2026 (originally June 17th 2026).

The season has the slogan X Marks the Spot.

Update v40.10

April 1st 2026

  • The northeastern quadrant of the island has been replaced by a jungle, with a large Volcano in its center.
    • Some of the missing eggs previously seen in the Polar Peak's dungeon and a throne can be found inside of the volcano.
  • Some new Named Locations have been added alongside the jungle, including:
    • Sunny Steps
    • Lazy Lagoon
  • Added new Unnamed Locations:
    • Pirate Camp - Pleasant
    • Pirate Camp - Greasy
    • Pirate Camp - Hill
    • Pirate Camp - Dusty
    • Pirate Camp - Volcano
    • Pirate Camp - Lazy
    • Pirate Camp - Paradise
    • Fork Knife Mine
    • Jungle Moai
    • X Marks The Spot
    • Star Shack
    • The Treasure
    • Expedition Outpost Digsite
    • Expedition Outpost Solar
    • Expedition Outpost Radar
    • Expedition Outpost Jungle
    • Expedition Base
    • Hot Springs
    • Scorched Containers
  • Tomato Temple is no longer a Named Location.
    • The only remaining building is the temple.
  • The Block has yet again landed incorrectly, ending up on a mountainside west of Motel.
  • The Tomato Tunnel was filled with lava with one of its exists completely blocked off.
  • Small Volcano Vents, that push the player upwards like the holes at the Corrupted Areas, were added.
  • A knife and fork shaped hole has appeared north of Fatal Fields, replacing one of the former Corrupted Areas.
  • The two houses south of Haunted Hills have been replaced by Abandoned Farm.
  • The Two Houses east of Salty Springs were changed.
    • One of them resembles the Hangar House in Northern Houses.
  • A new building is under construction in Tilted Towers.
  • Broken houses in Salty Springs and Pleasant Park were repaired.
  • A bit of snow south of Tilted Towers has melted.
  • The stone people have vanished.
  • Turtle was removed.
  • The Durrr Burger restaurant in Retail Row expanded and now includes a restroom.
    • The Uncle Pete's Pizza Pit restaurant has expanded. It has now more seating and the basketball court is gone.
  • Brick Pig was added east of Lucky Landing.
  • Wooden Rabbit was added north of Snobby Shores.
  • Hero Mansion and Villain Lair have decayed even more.
  • A Volcano Vent has appeared in the RV Park, causing one of the vehicles to flip over.
  • Most of the Expedition Outposts have been removed.
  • Primal Settlement didn't return for an unknown reason.
