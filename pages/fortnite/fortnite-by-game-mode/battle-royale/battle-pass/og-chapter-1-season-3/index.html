Overview

This article is about the third season of Fortnite OG Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 3, see Season 3 (disambiguation).

“
Relive Battle Royale from its beginnings in Chapter 1 Season 3. Drop back in and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG seasons!
”
— In-Game Description

OG Chapter 1: Season 3 is the third season of Fortnite OG. It started on March 25th 2025 and ended on June 7th 2025.

The season has the slogan Brace for Impact.

The Storyline of OG

The Storyline of OG[]

Season 3[]

About halfway through the season, a blue object appeared in the sky above Athena Island, which was known to be The Meteor approaching the island. As time went on, telescopes were set up on high vantage points, such as hills and mountains, to observe the meteor. Some players yet again began to theorize that the meteor would eventually strike Tilted Towers.

As the season started coming to a close, small camps appeared on a building in Tilted Towers, followed by televisions around the island broadcasting static with unintelligible audio. For the last week of the season, small meteor fragments would crash into the island starting towards the middle of matches, progressively getting worse as the match went on.

Map Updates[]

Update v34.20

March 25th 2025

  • Re-introduced the 1 location from Season 3, which includes:
    • Lucky Landing
  • Dancefloors were added in 3 points on the Island:
    • Pleasant Manor
    • Flush Factory
    • RV Park
  • Wooden Crab was added on a hill near Moisty Mire.
  • Brick Fox was added on a hill near Wailing Woods.
  • Lucky Bridge was re-designed to fit the theme of the newly added Lucky Landing.
    • Lucky Temple was added alongside Lucky Landing.
  • The elevated hut on Dusty Mountain is now correct.

Update v35.00

May 2nd 2025

  • The Meteor has appeared in the sky of the island.
    • It gets closer to the island as time passes
  • Telescope camps appeared around the island, looking towards the meteor.

May 16th 2025

  • A small camp appeared on a building in Tilted Towers with signs with a UFO drawing and text that read "Today Tomorrow".
  • Televisions around the island began broadcasting a test-pattern graphic of a Llama and unintelligible audio.
    • The TV broadcast contains morse code that says "It is happening ag..."

Update v35.20

May 30th 2025

  • Meteorites began falling from the sky during matches.
    • The amount of meteorites would increase as the match progressed.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • This season uses the slogan "Brace for Impact" even though that was the slogan for Season 4.
  • The use of Aerosmith's "I Don't Want to Miss a Thing" in its trailer is a reference to Armageddon, the film the song was made for, which involves an asteroid in collision course with Earth.
