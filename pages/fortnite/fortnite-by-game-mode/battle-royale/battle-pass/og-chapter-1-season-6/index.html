Overview

This article is about the sixth season of Fortnite OG Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 6, see Season 6 (disambiguation).

“
Relive Battle Royale from its beginnings in Chapter 1 Season 6. Drop back in and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG seasons!
”
— In-Game Description

OG Chapter 1: Season 6 was the sixth season of Fortnite OG that started on October 2nd 2025 and ended on November 29th 2025, as the Dark Voyager destroyed the Battle Bus and made the gamemode unplayable for the time being (alongside Fortnite: Reload and Blitz Royale). Due to this, Fortnite OG was shut down with the launch of Chapter 7: Season 1 due to the absence of a Battle Bus, with the OG Pass ending on December 11th 2025.

The season had the slogan Darkness Rises.

The Storyline of OG

The Storyline of OG[]

Season 5[]

In Season 5, Revin the Cube rolled around the island, printing seven runes before rolling into Loot Lake, sinking underneath the lake and deactivating underneath the central island. The entire surface of the lake was turned red in the process.

The Season Begins[]

At the begin of Season 6, instead of Revin, Kevin the Cube rises up from the Loot Lake, which becames the Floating Island and the former Loot Lake's place becomes Leaky Lake. With this change, all of the runes Revin printed reverted into the Kevin's rune. Revin's influence wasn't completely gone however, as instead of Kevin's Shadow Stones, they were now red and known as a Havoc Stone, giving people the power of the Nitro instead of transforming them into Shadows.

This Season[]

Over the course of the season, the floating island became more corrupted with each runes Kevin went to, and pieces of cubes appear throughout, most being purple with additions of red. After Kevin absorbed all the energy from the runes, they collapsed to the ground and the floating island returned to Leaky Lake, where it unleashed its energy to The Storm.

The floating island then exploded into several pieces, and Cube Monsters were unleashed across the island. From that day onward, as Loopers were fighting back, Kevin began to crack more and more, until it eventually shed it outer shell completely and began to drip nectar into the Vortex.

After the last drop of nectar leaked from Kevin, the Cube began to spin, gradually accelerating in speed. It eventually exploded into pieces, sent the Loopers to The In-Between, and sent the chunks of the Floating Island down to Leaky Lake, patching up the whirlpool in the process. While in the In-Between, Revin the Cube was seen altering the location in some form, having turned part of it red. After The Looper touched a Rift Butterfly, they were rifted back to the Island, now gazing upon what Leaky Lake had now become.

After that, an iceberg appeared on the south side of the Island, teasing what will happen in the next season...

Update v37.40

October 2nd 2025

  • The Cube has risen from the waters of Loot Lake, creating the Floating Island.
    • Loot Lake was renamed to Leaky Lake.
  • The seven runes The Cube imprinted into the ground corrupted the land around them, spawning Havoc Stones, with up to three chests around each rune, similar to the Moai Heads back in Season 5. Red rifts can be found around them.
    • All runes were reverted to the ones found in original Season 6.
  • Haunted Castle was added on a hill near Haunted Hills.
  • Four log cabins were added to Wailing Woods. Every one of the cabins has a secret room in the basement whose entrance is hidden behind a wardrobe.
    • The building in the hedge maze was turned into a secret underground laboratory.
  • Villain Lair and Hero Mansion started decaying.
  • The shack by Fatal Fields that was destroyed by the Cube is now under re-construction.

  • Depot Race Track was added around Starry Suburbs Ruins.
  • Wooden Wolf was removed.
