The Hideout

Overview

This page is about an item or mechanic that has been removed from the game.

The Battle Pass Hideout or simply known as the Hideout is the Battle Pass Menu. It was reworked in Chapter 2: Season 2 to fit the theme of each season and changed how Outfits progressed and how you could access other stuff. It was confirmed in the Doomsday event that the hideout was located under The Agency.

In Chapter 2: Season 3, the hideout moved to its new location at The Fortilla and its theme changed for the water and aquatics of the season.

In Chapter 2: Season 4, the hideout is now located on S.H.I.E.L.D.'s Helicarrier, which also now serves as the Spawn Island.

In Chapter 2: Season 5, the hideout has been reworked again to just be the Battle Pass.

Main Room

Main Room[]

The main room is where everything is accessed. It will show the Agents (BP Outfits) near the Challenge Table. It will also show your Battle Pass Level. Over time, wires for the Doomsday Device will start showing up. The first change happened on May 17, 2020, and more happened during Patch v12.60.

Agents

Agents[]

Agent Briefing Rooms were rooms where you could access which faction you want a Battle Pass Outfit you want to be on. You can also view your GHOST or SHADOW Loyalty Missions here. The choices people made here will be recorded in a poll and those results impact the Storyline.

Brutus' Room[]

Brutus' room contains Brutus himself, Deadpool’s Big Black Marker, a valve connected to a pipe, and a knife-throwing target. It is decorated with a large arsenal of weapons. There is some valves that can be interacted with that can be used to complete Agent Peely's Spy License.

TNTina's Room[]

TNTina lives in this room, and she lives with many explosives, missiles, and tools. You can overload a generator to complete Agent Peely's Spy License.

Meowscles' Room[]

Meowscles’ room contains Meowscles and one of Deadpool’s pistols. There are tons of fitness equipment, such as weights, a punching bag, and a ball of yarn. You can move a Hula Meowscles off of his bed, which can be used to complete Agent Peely's Spy License.

Skye's Room[]

Skye's Room holds Skye, her Ollie beanbag, and her caterpillar plushie. It is cluttered with books, posters, board games, and arts & crafts projects, and has a childish, adventurous vibe. You can click on the stars on the wall to make them glow, and on one of the board games to make the pieces move as if it's being played. She has a personal computer with a doodled Ollie as the lock screen, along with a retro game console and retro TV. Her night stand holds an Ollie-themed alarm clock. Her sword is mounted on the wall, and her personal space telescope sits on her shelf. She also has camping gear at the foot of her bed.

Midas' Room[]

Midas’s Room holds Midas, as well as a large collection of golden helmets of past outfits, including Black Knight, Alpine Ace, Lynx, Astro Assassin, Beef Boss, Chaos Agent, Carbide, Scratch, A.I.M, and more. Deadpool’s Shorts can be found there. This is also Midas’ Office. You can activate a gramophone to complete Agent Peely's Spy License. This may be a reference to Peely appearing when the jukebox had been activated in the Winterfest 2019 cabin. His room also contained The Doomsday Device, Which powered up during the last few weeks of Chapter 2: Season 2.

Battle Pass

Battle Pass[]

The Battle Pass room was a garage accessed by clicking on the Double Battle Star Sliding doors. Here, you can view the Premium and Free rewards.  You can also view the GHOST and SHADOW Allegiance Styles even before the Briefing is activated for that Outfit.

The Entrance will glow based on your current Pass. If you have the Free Pass, it will glow Silver with the Free Pass Logo. It will glow Gold and show the Premium Pass Logo only if you purchased the Battle Pass.

Upgrade Vault

Upgrade Vault[]

This is where you upgrade Gear Specialist Maya, where you can create a unique Maya out of millions of combinations. To obtain the ability to upgrade parts of her, you must complete her challenges.

?[]

? is accessed by clicking on the vent near Maya's Upgrade Vault. It will take you to a bathroom with a computer. Access the computer, and you will find Deadpool's Weekly Challenges. The room changes after each challenge completion.

Deadpool's Computer[]

You can click on Deadpool’s Computer, giving you access to the Deadpool Weakly Challenges. If you are a Free Pass owner, the challenges are not visible and it will take you to the Battle Pass Purchase Screen instead.

You can also play a secret mini game called “Pfreely,” in which you must ski around and avoid Peelys, which are trying to eat you. Peelys come out at night and disappear during the day. Avoid obstacles such as trees and lamp posts, collect food, and use ramps to avoid Peelys. It is based off of SkiFree.

Spy Games

Spy Games[]

Upon entering, the player is presented with a screen that resembles the Battle Pass, with unlockable tiers of 'tech' (which consists of weapons, perks, and items) that are available for use in Operations once unlocked. There are two sections of tiers, one for Shadow, and one for Ghost. When the player chooses an allegiance to either side, they can earn that side's respective tech by playing Operations and gaining intel in-game. Players can switch allegiances at any time through the Spy Games menu.

Every player starts out with 'Starter Tech', and has access to the other sides Starter Tech as well, no matter which side they are on. Players do not gain access to every perk or weapon right away, and can choose between them once in an Operation (choices are not permanent).

During The Device event, a limited version of the Spy Games was featured, nor it was never used for a single purpose. After the elimination goal reached its goal and kept going further, Tech will not be able to choose from until the event is over.

Daily Assignments

Daily Assignments[]

Daily Assignments are located at a bulletin board with the map right next to Skye. They will showcase all of your Daily Challenges and some pictures that point to the hidden challenges related to the Bears & Gnomes Challenges. If these are completed, they point to your daily challenges.
