OG Chapter 1: Season 4 is the fourth season of Fortnite OG. It started on June 7th 2025 and ended on August 7th 2025.

The season has the slogan Prepare for Launch.

The Storyline of OG

The Storyline of OG[]

Season 3[]

In Season 3, a meteor appeared in the sky of the Athena island. As the meteor approached the island, the inhabitants of the island began placing telescopes, camps, and signs around the island, all looking towards the meteor. During the final days of the season, small meteor fragments would crash into the island during matches.

The Season Begins[]

As the season began, the meteor from last season, as well as many meteor fragments, had finally crashed onto the island and damaging several areas on the island. The largest fragment crashed into the Factory and Dusty Depot, resulting in Dusty Divot. The smaller fragments landed around the island, hitting Tilted Towers, the Prison, Risky Reels, and the Motel.

The entirety of the Factory and half of Dusty Depot was destroyed from the largest meteor fragment. The fragment that landed at Tilted Towers destroyed most of the center building and the fragment that crashed into the Prison destroyed most of the prisons center. The fragment that landed at Risky Reels destroyed half of the cinema screen and landed amongst the densely packed cars and the final, tiny fragment, landed at the Motel, flipping over a bus and crashing into the motel roof.

This Season[]

The meteor left behind Hop Rocks in the crater of Dusty Divot, these rocks grant Loopers who consume them a low-gravity effect for a short duration. After the impact, The Government (also known as the the Unnamed Investigation Agency, or U.I.A) built a research facility at Dusty Divot to research and investigate a mysterious glowing rock which was left by the meteor.

While this was happening, a movie titled "It came from Moisty Mire" was being filmed near Moisty Mire when a meteor fragment crashed into the Prison. It was revealed that the supervillain O.M.E.G.A. Suit Alpha was working for The Government, Omega began to assemble an army of augmented soldiers in his Villain Lair. In response, the superheroes, led by Carbide, began to formulate a plan at the Hero Mansion. However, all of this was already known to be part of the movie as shown in the That's a Wrap! loading screen.

As time went on, The Government began filling in smaller craters and construction crews started rebuilding destroyed parts of Risky Reels and Tilted Towers. At the same time, the glowing rock in Dusty Divot began to crack and open, revealing a mysterious pod containing The Visitor (Reality Unknown), whom is thought to be the original Visitor. The Visitor broke out of the pod, fought O.M.E.G.A. Suit Alpha as revealed in the O.M.E.G.A Defense loading screen, trashed the research facility, and after escaping to the Villain Lair, transformed the fake rocket from the superhero movie into a real rocket by using Hop Rocks stolen from The Government.

After all of this, a hologram appeared in the Villain Lair which projected a three-day countdown for the Blast Off event on July 23rd, 2025, followed by the countdown being broadcast on all televisions on the island and then a siren began blaring at the Villain Lair which could be heard from everywhere on the island.

Event and Aftermath[]

Before the rocket launched, a red butterfly appeared and circled around the Looper before going into the rocket. Once the countdown ended, the rocket (piloted by The Visitor (Reality Unknown)) took off from the Villain Lair, leaving behind a thick trail of smoke as it ascended into the sky. It momentarily stopped mid-air to break off the rocket booster, before accelerating once more. Upon reaching a high altitude, the rocket shut off it's engines and stalled mid-air, before plummeting back down towards the island. As the rocket fell, The Visitor spoke, saying "Zero Point Oscillator on, setting coordinates. Engaging quantum field accelerator". The rocket then activated a laser that pointed to the center of Tilted Towers, and boosted towards the location. However, before the rocket hit, The Visitor activated a device which created a tear through space-time called a rift. The rocket entered the Rift and exited from one over Moisty Mire, flying across the island before entering another Rift near Snobby Shores. Finally, the rocket exited from a Rift at Loot Lake and accelerated into the sky, creating a giant rift in the sky, known as a crack in the sky. This time, the crack in the sky is red and is growing faster than it did in Season 4.

While the previous rifts created by the rocket closed, the crack in the sky did not, appearing to be permanent. As time went on, the crack in the sky began to crack more and more, causing space-time around the island to destabilize. As the season was coming to a close, small rifts began appearing around the island, rifting away objects such as the Tomato Town mascot, the Motel sign, and the Durr Burger head. As objects were rifted away, objects were also rifted onto the island from other realities such as a Viking ship anchor, dinosaur bones, and an ancient wagon.

Trivia[]

  • OG Chapter 1: Season 4 began at the same time as Chapter 6: Season 3. Coincidentally, both:
    • Are superhero themed seasons.
    • Are the fourth season of their respective chapter.
    • Were preceded by an object(s) striking the island. (Season 3 Meteor Strike and the ending of Death Star Sabotage)
    • Involve a collaboration that promotes an upcoming superhero film release. (Avengers: Infinity War and Superman)
    • Marked the debut appearance of a superhero team (The Seven and The Supernovas).
    • Begin and end on the same dates. (June 7th and August 7th)
    • Have an Outfit with a mainly black-colored attire as their Final Level Outfit. (Omega and Morgan Myst)
