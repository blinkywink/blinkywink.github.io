Overview

This article is about the first season of Fortnite OG Chapter 1. For other uses of Season 1, see Season 1 (disambiguation).

“
Relive Battle Royale from its beginnings in Chapter 1 Season 1. Drop back in and explore the OG map, collect OG loot, and relive OG seasons!
”
— In-Game Description

OG Chapter 1: Season 1 was the first season of Fortnite OG. It started on December 6th 2024 and concluded on January 31st 2025.

The season has the slogan The OG OG Returns.

OG Pass

OG Pass[]

The OG Pass is a seasonal cosmetic item bundle in Fortnite OG, which grants the player rewards in exchange for .

OG Pass items are not exclusive to the season in which they are offered and are likely going to be added to the Item Shop sometime after the season ends. However, players are unable to access OG Pass from past seasons.
