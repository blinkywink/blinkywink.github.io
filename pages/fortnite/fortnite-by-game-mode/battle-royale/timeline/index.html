The following is a full timeline of Fortnite: Battle Royale's Lore in chronological order.

Before Chapter 1

Before Chapter 1[]

  • The Nothing is born.

The Big Bang[]

  • The birth of The Zero Point, Reality Zero and The Omniverse.[1]

Unknown Time Period[]

  • The Last Reality is formed.
  • The Origin becomes the Cube King.[2]
  • Human Bill's father invents The Mothership, a spaceship that utilize the Cubes as fuel.[3]
  • The Cubes create the Chalice of Cubetastrophe.
  • The Herald makes a name for herself in quiet places.[4]
  • The Army of the Last Reality invades Fortnite: Save the World. They are defeated by ancient humans and their remains are buried deep underground, encased in ice around the world.[5]
  • Uncounted ages ago, The Ice King and his brave allies begin collecting items of power, things, and beings too dangerous to exist such as The Devourer and the Infinity Blade. He brings them all to The Ice Moon.[6]
  • The Prisoner, the Ice King's staunchest comrade, wants to use items of power to alter Realities to their liking. The Ice King is forced to freeze the Prisoner, his knights and all he had acquired within his fortress. He brings The Ice Moon to Reality Zero, a place he believes to be isolated and safe.[6]
  • The River Guard and the Fox Clan become historic rivals.
  • The River Guard, Fox Clan, and the Peace Syndicate come together to build MEGA City.
  • Mystica's home world is destroyed. She somehow ends up in MEGA City and decides to create The Luminaries.
  • The Unseen becomes a syndicate in MEGA City.
  • Dahlia, a member of The Unseen, bought the Chalice of Cubetastrophe into her possession.
  • The Foundation comes into contact with The Zero Point. He's been studying it ever since.[1]
  • The Foundation survives in The Nothing.[1]
  • The Paradigm comes into contact with the Reality Warriors in Reality: 659.
  • J.B. Chimpanski is abandoned by the astronauts.
  • Mari and Dennis are thrown into The Loop.
  • Midas and Marigold are cursed with the Golden Touch and both lose their left eye.
  • Midas accidentally turned his daughter gold. then, through unknown means, he bought her back to life.
  • Midas sets his sights on building an empire of espionage, intrigue, and conquest. He founds the SHADOW organization.[7]
  • Midas founds the GHOST organization.
  • Nolan Chance heists the Slap Juice Co. vault.

Billions of years ago[]

  • Eons ago, The Ancients jungle civilization of the Rumble Ruins use refined Kinetic Ore to create amazing technologies, so they create The Apparatus.
    • Optimus Prime came into contact with The Ancients.
    • The Apparatus made a prediction on The Eclipse and the arrival of the revenant vampire Kado Thorne.
    • The ancient residents quickly flee from their home.
  • Eons ago, the stars blink out, one by one. Until there were only a few left. Then, a "brilliant owl" helps a "clever fox" erupted a Big Bang, filling the vast emptiness with an eternity of new stars.[8]
    • The fall of the The Triarchs realm.[9]
    • Triarch Nox encounters a "decaying force" and became evil.
    • The other Triarch vanished, leaving Triarch Aurora as the last ruler of the fallen realm.
  • Within The Pantheon realm, mortals and immortals co-exist in peace. Until the day that Zeus changed his mind.
    • Medusa angers the immortals and becomes their target. Persesus saves Medusa from the wrath of the immortals.

Millions of years ago[]

  • The Zero Point creates Vibranium and it ends up in the Marvel Multiverse.[10]

Thousands of years ago[]

  • Geno founds the Imagined Order and imprisons The Zero Point.
  • In order to assist with their task of creating The Loop, the IO visit the universe of Fortnite: Save the World and steal Dr. Vinderman's technology, as well as harnessing The Storm and Blu-Glo. The survivors of The Storm become the first Loopers.
  • The Loop is formed by directly programming The Zero Point to create a pseudo-time loop that renders anyone inside mute and gives them amnesia, as well as rendering any supernatural or metahuman abilities useless and making everyone vulnerable to bullets.
  • The IO kidnap more and more people and put them in The Loop.
  • Geno achieves immortality, and a snapshot of him is created to rule the Oathbound empire.
  • The construction of IO Golems.[11]
  • The construction of The Bridge.[12]
  • The Imagined and The Order are born from Geno's genetic engineering.[6]
  • The Imagined and The Order are forced into The Loop by Geno himself. Geno adopts snapshots of The Imagined and The Order as his new daughters.[6]
  • Doctor Slone joins the Imagined Order at a very young age. She travels across the omniverse and receives doctorates from multiple realities.
  • The Foundation is trapped in The Loop.[1]
  • The Cube King realizes that the cubes are evil and defects from the Last Reality.[2]
  • The Cube King is captured by the Imagined Order.[2]
  • The Seven are formed.
  • The Imagined and The Order's files are deleted from the IO database by Geno.[13]
  • The Imagined loses her memory.[13]

Centuries ago[]

  • Jones leaves his family and his home reality to join the Imagined Order.[6]
  • Jones is tasked to jump into the Zero Point, creating a snapshot named Jonesy The First.[14]
  • The Shapeless Man adopts a young Rift Warden Stellan and made him join the Oathbound.[15]
  • The Sages are exiled from Oathbound after they make a disturbing prophecy on the Ageless Champion.[15]
  • Generations ago, an unknown individual manages to beat The Nothing's Last Reality warrior recruitment trials.[16]
  • The Spire appears in Raz's dreams.
  • Vengeful spirit Kondor is given to the emperor's family in Reality: 42, and is passed down for generations.[17]

Season 4[]

Season 4: Brace For Impact
Week Event Image
1 The mysterious comet has hit the island and crashes into the Factory, vaporising the facility. The resulting crater destroys most of Dusty Depot, and the entire area is renamed to Dusty Divot. Smaller comet fragments damage Tilted Towers, Risky Reels, and destroy the Prison. The Government arrives and sets up a research facility in the divot, examining a large and mysterious comet rock in the center, constructing a large building around it to shield it from prying eyes. Government cars and trucks also arrive at each of the smaller craters dotted around the island, and study the mysterious Hop Rocks found there.
2 Production of a movie begins in which the evil super-villain Omega found himself freed from the Prison due to its destruction from a comet fragment. The heroes led by Carbide begin formulating a plan at their Hero Mansion near Lonely Lodge. In his Villain Lair, Omega began to assemble an army of augmented soldiers for a final battle with the superheroes, taking place in Tilted Towers. A movie set was built at Moisty Mire for filming, and The Hero Mansion and Villain Lair were also constructed for filming. However, The Villain Lair itself actually contained what was thought to be a rocket prop for Omega's sinister intentions, but which played a major role in the lore in the later weeks of the season.
5 Production of the movie is complete.
6 - 7 The Government collect all the hop rocks and transport them to The Villain Lair. Not long after, they drill through the comet rock at Dusty Divot, which reveals The Visitor's pod. It seems as if the meteor was actually a method of transportation that The Visitor used to get to the island. The seemingly hostile individual Visitor broke out of his pod and trashed the research facility, escaping to the Villain Lair, which already contained all the hop rocks assembled by the Government. The Visitor proceeded to modify fake prop rocket in the lair to allow it to channel The Zero Point field, and began fueling it with hop rocks.
9 The rocket takes off from the Villain Lair, leaving behind a thick trail of smoke as it ascends into the sky. It momentarily stopped in the air to break off its rocket booster, before accelerating quickly. Once reaching a high altitude, the rocket shut off its engines and stalled in the air, before plummeting back down towards the island. The Visitor could have been heard saying 'Zero Point Oscillator on, Setting coordinates. Engaging quantum field accelerator.' The rocket activated a laser that pointed directly at Tilted Towers, and boosted towards the location. However, before it could hit, the rocket temporarily opened a 'sideways' rift 'between' two realities. The rocket went through the rift and boosted out of another one over Moisty Mires, flying around the island to enter another rift near Snobby Shores. Finally, the rocket boosted out of another rift at Loot Lake and accelerated into the sky, blasting through space-time and creating a gigantic rift in the sky, known as the Sideways Rift. This rift did not close, and instead slowly grew over the days until Season 5.
10 As the Sideways Rift continued to grow, smaller rifts began opening around the island, rifting different objects from the island. Most famously, The Durrr Burger rifted and appeared in the real world, in a remote part of the California desert, It was guarded by a group of unknown agents.

Sand started to leak from the Sideways Rift. Rifts begin bringing objects from different realities to the island, namely a ship wagon, dinosaur bones, and an ancient wagon.

The Zero Point was left exposed after the event, and it began destabilizing rapidly. Its outer shell was cracked by the mecha to obtain power, and Zero Point energy began to leak out and corrupt the surroundings.
