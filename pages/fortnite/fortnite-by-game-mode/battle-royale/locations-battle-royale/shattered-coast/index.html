The Shattered Coast, also known under the codename Hera v2, is the Fortnite: Battle Royale Island used in Chapter 7: Season 3. It is a version of the Golden Coast that was heavily altered by The Zero Point. The codename continued the trend of internally naming the map after Greek Deities beginning with 'H'.

Update v41.00[]

June 6th 2026

  • The restored Zero Point was sent into overdrive by the Dark Voyager, drastically warping the Golden Coast's landscapes and rendering it virtually unrecognizable.
  • New Named Locations were added, including:
    • The Battlewoods, a remixed version of Battlewood Boulevard, moved to the northwest side of the map and surrounded by trees in the forest biome.
    • Calamari Canyon, a remixed and dried up version of Squibbly Shores, found in the desert biome.
    • Chopped Shop, a small garage for modified cars, with a small race track nearby.
    • Cluster Coast, a new city centered around Cluster.
    • Frosted Flats, a small town in the autumnal biome with a new restaurant called FrosDeez, themed around the Ice King, and has remnants of Frigid Fortress nearby.
    • Golden Grove, an autumnal forest on the east side of the map with Sanctuary inspired buildings.
    • Heatwave Harbor, a new city found in the southwest corner of the island.
    • Lifty Lodge, a ski resort found in the far northwest corner in the snow biome, heavily inspired by Tiptop Terrace.
    • Shaken Sanctuary, an altered New Sanctuary, with changed buildings and its surrounding terrain now being tropical.
    • Sinister Strip, a merging of Sandy Strip and Dark Dominion, with the Strip now being controlled by the Dark Voyager, and its location being moved.
    • Sunken Shores, a flooded town in the southern part of the island.
  • New Landmarks were added, including:
    • Birder's Beach
    • Crashout Estates
    • Dine 'n Docks
    • The Bus Stop
    • The Quackaway
    • The Sleazy Snooze
    • Wettest-Bones Research Facility
  • Other Notable Changes:
    • Latte Landing is the least changed location on the island, but it is now in the autumnal biome and all of its changes from the previous season have been reverted. The crane is also gone.
    • Wonkeeland has moved to the far northeast corner of the map near Latte, and is also in the autumnal biome, however otherwise is unchanged.
    • Ripped Tides, Humble Hills, Sus Studios, Painted Palms, Fore Fields and Builder's Barracks were all completely wiped from the new island.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In Greek mythology, Hera was the queen of the gods and goddess of marriage, women and childbirth.
  • The Shattered Coast marks the first time an original Battle Royale island has been introduced during a Chapter outside of a mini season.
  • The Shattered Coast marks the first time since Chapter 3: Season 1 (not including Chapter 4: Season OG and Chapter 2 Remix) that more than 1 Named Location returned from a previous map, being Wonkeeland and Latte Landing.
