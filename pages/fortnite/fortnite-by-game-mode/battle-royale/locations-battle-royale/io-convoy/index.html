Overview

IO Convoy, also known as <Outpost 01> is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 8 to the island Apollo. It is a small IO Convoy of Abductor parts located near **REDACTED**. Doctor Slone can be found there as a Boss, along with two IO Guards.
