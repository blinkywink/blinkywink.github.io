Overview

The Last Stand of Llamamemnon was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was added in Update v15.00. It is located east of Salty Towers and Risky Reels, and west of Dirty Docks. It was an area in the centre of the coliseum with a giant wooden Crab and a giant Llama.

Chapter 2 Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00: The Last Stand of Llamamemnon was added.

Chapter 2 Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00: The Last Stand of Llamamemnon was replaced by Colossal Crops along with the rest of Colossal Coliseum.
