Overview

Gas Stations are scattered locations across the map in Fortnite: Battle Royale.

Overview

Overview[]

Gas Stations are marked on the map when driving Vehicles. Many Gas Stations were revamped in Update v13.30. The fuel provided at these stations is supplied by Spillexx, although the stations are owned by either Spillexx or the more common GAS.

Gas Stations can be used to refuel vehicles such as the Cars, Quadcrashers, Motorboats and X-4 Stormwings trough Gas Pumps. These can also be used to refill Gas Cans and Repair Torches.

Since Chapter 5: Season 3, Gas Stations feature Service Stations instead of Gas Pumps.
