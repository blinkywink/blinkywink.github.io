Overview

The Ancient Ship was an Unnamed Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Update v13.30 to the island Apollo. The spaceship crash landed north of Craggy Cliffs, and was piloted by Deo.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.30: The Ancient Spaceship was added north of Craggy Cliffs.
  • Update v13.40: The Ship has reappeared on the southwest part of the island, East of Rickety Rig, now open.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00: It was removed from the Island.
Challenges

Challenges[]

There were challenges to go alongside it during Chapter 2: Season 3:

Found Ancient Ship 0 / 1
Collect Missing Part 0 / 3 14,000
Install Missing Part 0 / 3 14,000
Begin Launch Sequence 0 / 2 25,000
Stop Ship Launch 0 / 1

If you finished all those challenges the spaceship would launch into the sky and leave Rifts on the mini-island.
