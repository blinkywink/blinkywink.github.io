Overview

IO Elevators are Unnamed Locations in Fortnite: Battle Royale that were added in Chapter 2: Season 5. Each match they had a chance to spawn, and would have 3 IO Guards in them.

Rare Spawns

Rare Spawns[]

Sometimes, an Elevator will not spawn IO Guards but something rare - usually an Easter egg, nods to past events, or even trolls.
These are all the rare objects that Elevators can spawn:

  • The GHOST and SHADOW Henchmen duo from the previous season, which seem confused about their whereabouts.
  • A Loot Shark, which will try and find the nearest body of water.
  • Midas' golden chair
  • The Stone Dog from Chapter 1.
  • A teddy Bear.
  • A Durrr Burger head
  • A Moai Head.
  • A triceratops from Dino Park.
  • The wooden bear statue from Lonely Lodge.
  • A gnome carrying a lamp.
  • A fish on top of a chest.
  • A holiday tree surrounded by presents and Chests - most likely only for Operation: Snowdown.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Surface Hub IO-F4, Surface Hub IO-E6, and Surface Hub IO-D1 all activate a PA system whenever an elevator has risen to the surface. The PA is louder when an Elevator has risen nearby, which can be useful for finding IO Guard spawn locations.
    • This means the elevators come from different elevators in The Bridge.
  • Whenever the Henchmen Duo appear, the elevators still count them as an IO Guard spawn. Even playing the klaxon and their combat music when they spawn.
  • The Female Announcer's voice lines are based on Jules' voice.
  • The Klaxon could occasionally be heard in the renovated Surface Hub IO-F4 during Chapter 2: Season 7.
    • The same Klaxon can also be heard in the Boreal Base Zone in the Frozen Fjords Ventures Season of Save the World.
