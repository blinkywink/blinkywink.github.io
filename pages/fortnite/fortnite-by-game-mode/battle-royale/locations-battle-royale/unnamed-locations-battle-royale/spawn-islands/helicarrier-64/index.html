Helicarrier 64 was a Location in Fortnite: Battle Royale that functioned as the Spawn Island for Chapter 2: Season 4. It later moved to serve as a platform at the start of The Devourer of Worlds Live Event, where it was destroyed by Galactus' fist and is likely never to be seen again.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Helicarrier 64 had a long runway for the 8 Quinjets that boosted up when the Battle Bus was ready. On the right side stood a tower, with a Vault on the lower floor that required a Keycard for access, although it remained inaccessible still. Near the top of the tower was a platform, and a hangar was located beneath it. The Helicarrier was supported by four large turbines. S.H.I.E.L.D. logos could be seen on it, indicating its affiliation. Helicarrier 64 featured a blue and black color scheme.

On the lower floors, the Battle Pass Tab was located, though it was not accessible.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Helicarrier 64's name was revealed in the Marvel Knockout LTM, where it was a playable location, instead of just being the Spawn Island.
  • In Update v14.60, a countdown appeared for The Devourer of Worlds Live Event.
  • An eyepatch possibly belonging to Nick Fury could have been found inside one of the rooms.
