Pandora was a Rift Zone in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Season X to the island Athena. It could have been found south of Paradise Palms and east of Fatal Fields. Entering it gave the player the ability to regenerate their shield by spending more than 5 seconds without taking any damage.

History

History[]

Season X[]

  • Update v10.20: Pandora was added to the map replacing Trucker's Oasis and the Pueblo.
  • Update v10.30: Pandora was removed from the map, and the Trucker's Oasis and the Pueblo replaced it again.
  • Update v10.40: A recreation of Pandora was built in The Block by Sunday.
  • The End (Chapter 1): The Pandora Block was sucked into the black hole.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Pandora and Gotham City were the only collaboration Rift Zones.
  • Sounds related to Starry Suburbs are codenamed Oak.
  • Pandora's mechanic replicates the way the shields from the Borderlands series work.
  • While Pandora was on the map, the desert face was changed to resemble Handsome Jack, the main antagonist of Borderlands 2.
  • Pandora was the shortest-lived landmark in the entire game; lasting for about 2 weeks only.
  • In the Arsenal LTM, Pandora would still recharge shields.
