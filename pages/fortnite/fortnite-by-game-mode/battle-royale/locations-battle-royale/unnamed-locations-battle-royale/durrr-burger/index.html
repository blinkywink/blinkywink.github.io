Overview

This article is about the company. For other uses, see Durrr Burger (disambiguation).


Durrr Burger is a fast food chain existing in multiple realities in Fortnite. They appear in Battle Royale, Save the World and Party Royale.

Durrr Burger is the rival to Uncle Pete's Pizza Pit.

Save the World

Save the World[]

Dennis, a Durrr Burger Employee comes take shelter of Homebase when The Storm ravages the population. After completing a quest in Stonewood, he has been making burgers for the survivors.

In the 2017 E3 Cinematic Trailer for Save the World and in the Build Off! Mission, the setting takes place in an abandoned Durrr Burger Resturant that is fortified by lots of traps and structures by Ramirez, Penny, Ken and A.C.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • At the Blue Steel Bridge, a small store can be found and the drain in the kitchen area is labelled “Durr-rain”, this could suggest that this store is owned by Durrr Burger.
  • Durrr Burger is the main sponsor of the Sports Center.
    • Some of the Durrr Burger's products were sold at the gas station at the Sports Center.
  • Some signs found in the Durrr Burger restaurants imply that Slurp Juice is sold in them.
  • The Arcade in Mega Mall seems to be sponsored by Durrr Burger, as there was a banner over the entrance reading "Vicdurrry."
  • The Durrr Burger appears on an ornament in the All Terrain Kart, along with Tomatohead.
  • In Save The World, there is canonically a "Durrr Burger Kingdom" amusement park including an attraction named "Sir Bun-Bun's castle."
  • A Real World parody of the Durrr Burger appeared at the FNCS Invitational known as MrBeast Burger; while also referencing MrBeast's fast food chain of the same name.
    • This was likely a subtle teaser for the MrBeast Set.
  • In Reality: 783, Durrr Burger doesn't exist. However, there is a similar restaurant chain named Durrr Sushi.
  • Most Durrr Burger buildings have architecture that resembles the 80-90s McDonald's restaurants.
