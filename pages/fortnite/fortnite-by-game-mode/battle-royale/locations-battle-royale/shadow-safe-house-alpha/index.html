Overview

Shadow Safe House - Alpha is a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 2 to the island Apollo. It is one of many SHADOW Safe Houses that were located around the map. It was located under the soccer field at Pleasant Park. SHADOW Henchmen could have been found here.

The location had three entrance points from above; a Porta-Potty behind the gas station, a Porta-Potty south of the blue house, and a Porta-Potty near the modern house, east of the soccer field.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

  • Update v12.00
    • SHADOW Safe House - Alpha was added to the map. At the time, the only way in was through Porta-Potties.
  • Update v12.20
    • A secret entrance has opened in the Pleasant Park soccer field, allowing for another way into Shadow Safe House - Alpha. It is now the landing spot for a Choppa.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00
    • Shadow Safe House - Alpha has vanished from below Pleasant Park.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00
    • Shadow Safe House - Alpha has returned from below Doom's Domain, now hijacked by Doctor Doom and co.. The landmark is now called Vault of Doom.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00
    • Vault of Doom (formerly Shadow Safe House - Alpha) has once again been removed from the map.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

  • Update v32.00
    • Re-added Shadow Safe House - Alpha along with Pleasant Park and the rest of Apollo. The only way in was through Porta-Potties and Dumpsters.
    • A Phone Booth was added underground inside the safe house.
  • Update v32.10
    • November 14th 2024: The secret entrance has been re-opened in the soccer field.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The SHADOW Henchmen located here likely had stripped the Crashed E.G.O. Plane and brought some of its supplies back to their base.
  • In Chapter 2 Remix, SHADOW Safe House - Alpha is the only of the safe houses to have Guards or ID Scanners. The other safe houses are on the map, but they either only have a non scanner-locked SHADOW Henchman Chest or nothing SHADOW related at all.
