Overview

Drop Shop was a Landmark in Fortnite: Battle Royale added in Chapter 2: Season 3 to the island Apollo. It is a boat store owned by A Lot O' Auto that replaced a bridge above Gorgeous Gorge. It contains five Motorboats, three of which that can be dropped into the river below. It is located inside Coordinate A4 - southeast of The Pyramid, and northwest of Lazy Lake.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Update v13.00
    • Drop Shop was added and it replaced a wooden bridge across Gorgeous Gorge.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

  • Update v14.00
    • The whirlpool was removed from the water below Drop Shop.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

  • Update v15.00
    • Drop Shop was slightly damaged from a rock created by The Zero Point.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Update v16.00
    • Drop Shop has been repaired.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Content Update v18.40 - 1 (November 28th 2021): The Cube Queen's corruption of the island has surrounded the forest around landmark, but not the landmark itself.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Drop Shop is one of the only landmarks from Chapter 2: Season 3 to stay in-game.
  • After the island's corruption spread over and around the location, some potted plants interestingly took on the corrupted overlay, despite them not being a part of the environment.
