The Death Star was an inaccessible location in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It's a gigantic space station belonging to the Galactic Empire, capable of destroying planets with a kyber crystal-powered superlaser. It was able to be accessed in the Death Star Sabotage live event before then getting destroyed.

    Trivia[]

    • Emperor's Command Loading Screen reveals that the Death Star's Throne Room is the one from Death Star II, seen in Star Wars: Episode VI – Return of the Jedi.

    Speculation Trivia[]
