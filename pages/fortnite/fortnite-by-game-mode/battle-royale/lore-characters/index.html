Lore Characters

Overview

This page features characters important of Fortnite: Battle Royale, those who affected the lore in game and in comics, whether as protagonist or antagonist.

In the case of Fortnite: Battle Royale, protagonists refer to the heroes and antiheroes of the storymine, those who act for good. The same way, antagonists refer to the villains and anti-villains of the storyline. Some protagonists appear in multiple seasons in big roles and have a higher status while others appear in multiple seasons in supporting/minor roles.
