Overview

The Forest Biome is a green forest in the northeast part of Athena and north of Slurpy Swamp on Apollo in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It was added in Season 1 and was removed after Chapter 2 ended.

History

History[]

Season 1[]

  • Half the island is in the Forest Biome.

Season 6[]

  • A Corrupted Area appeared temporarily.

Season 7[]

  • Wailing Woods was covered in Snow during Ice King event.
  • Wailing Woods starts to burn.

Season 8[]

  • The Volcano and a jungle biome replace Wailing Woods

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

  • With the new island, the Forest Biome can be found as Weeping Woods.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

  • Weeping Woods was flooded temporarily.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • A part of Weeping Woods is now Primal.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • The primitive part of Weeping Woods disappears.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

  • Half of Weeping Woods is corrupted and becomes Wasted Woods

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • The island has flipped over revealing Artemis, there is no forest biome on Artemis.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

  • In the Fracture Live Event, remaining Forests from Apollo were destroyed by The Herald offscreen.
