Overview

The subject of this article currently doesn't have an official name.

The Primal Biome was a biome that appeared in Chapter 2: Season 6. It consists of primal-themed buildings, orange land and autumnal trees. It was created by a Reality Wave from The Zero Point.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • The Zero Point created a reality wave that transformed Salty Towers, Hunter's Haven and Colossal Coliseum and created Primal Biome around them.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

  • The autumnal Biome was removed, however, several Primal-themed buildings remain on the map.
