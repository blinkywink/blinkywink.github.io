Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Apart from a Rift Zone in Season X, there has never been both a Swamp and a Desert biome accessible on the map at the same time.
    • One or the other of the above were consistently present on the map until Chapter 4.
  • Artemis is the only Island to not have a definite Farm biome.
    • However, Sanctuary can be considered a farm since the buildings are located next to some water rice farming rows. Additionally, some small soil patches are also present.
