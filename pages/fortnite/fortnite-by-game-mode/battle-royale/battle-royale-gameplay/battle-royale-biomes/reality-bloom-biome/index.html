Overview

The Reality Bloom Biome was a biome in Fortnite: Battle Royale that originates from The Zero Point at The Glow. It was added in Update v21.00.

Overview

Overview[]

The Reality Bloom Biome had blueish-green grass and giant mushrooms, alongside purple trees, exotic shrubs, pink flowers and an abundance of Rift Butterflies. Bouncy Slurpshrooms found within the biome can bounce the player and occasionally heal. Some places also have a healing pool of slurp.

During Chapter 3: Season 3, Many Reality Seeds spawn within the Reality Bloom.

During Chapter 3: Season 4, The biome was autumn colored, due to the environment dying and decaying.

The biome finally returned in Chapter 2 Remix, with Juice WRLD bringing it back with his landmark, WRLD's Point.
