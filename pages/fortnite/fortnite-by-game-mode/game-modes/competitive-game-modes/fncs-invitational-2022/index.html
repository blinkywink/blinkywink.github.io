Overview

Fortnite Champion Series: Invitational 2022 was a Tournament hosted by Epic Games, where the 50 best duos from around the world were invited to compete for their share of $1,000,000 (USD) in Raleigh, North Carolina, USA. The Tournament was hosted across 2 days with 12 games total, and having 6 games on each day.

Format[]

  • Day 1:
    • 3 hour limit & 6 match limit
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
  • Day 2:
    • 3 hour limit & 6 match limit
