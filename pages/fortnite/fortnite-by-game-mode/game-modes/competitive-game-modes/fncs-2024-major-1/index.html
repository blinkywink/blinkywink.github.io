Overview

FNCS 2024 - Major 1 (Chapter 5 Season 1) is the 18th season of the Fortnite Champion Series. This season continues the changes to the 2023 season, with 3 splits - Major 1, Major 2, and Major 3. This year brings a new qualifier system, with two open qualifiers (January 26th - February 4th) and an upper and lower bracket format for semi-finals (February 16th - February 18th). At its major tournaments, the best players from each region qualify for an international tournament that will determine this year's world champions.

Schedule[]

  • Week 1: January 26th-28th, 2024
  • Week 2: February 2nd-4th, 2024
  • Semi Finals: February 16th-18th, 2024
  • Grand Finals: February 24th-25th, 2024

Format[]

  • Week 1 - Round 1: January 26th, 2024
    • 3 hours limit & 10 matches limit.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 2000 duos of the Round 1 in every region will advance to Round 2.
  • Week 1 - Round 2: January 27th, 2024
    • 3 hours limit & 10 matches limit.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 2000 duos of the Round 1 in every region will advance to Round 3.
  • Week 1 - Round 3: January 27th, 2024
    • 3 hours limit & 10 matches limit.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 250 duos of the Round 2 in every region will advance to Weekly Finals.
  • Week 1 - Finals: January 28th, 2024
    • 6 set-lobby games.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Duos will be awarded Series Points based on their performance and results from that week.
  • Week 2 - Round 1: February 2nd, 2024
    • 3 hours limit & 10 matches limit.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 2000 duos of the Round 1 in every region will advance to Round 2
  • Week 2 - Round 2: February 3rd, 2024
    • 3 hours limit & 10 matches limit.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 1000 duos of the Round 1 in every region will advance to Round 3
  • Week 2 - Round 3: February 3rd, 2024
    • 3 hours limit & 10 matches limit.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 250 duos of the Round 1 in every region will advance to Finals.
  • Week 2 - Finals: February 4th, 2024
    • 6 set-lobby games.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Duos will be awarded Series Points based on their performance and results from that week.
  • Semi Finals - Upper Bracket: February 16th-18th, 2024
    • Teams have 3 hours and 5 games.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • The winners of the five games in Upper Rounds 1 and 2 advance to the Grand Finals.
    • Duos that place 6th-25th on the leaderboard in Upper Rounds 1 and 2 qualify to Upper Round 3.
    • Duos that place 26th-50th in Upper Rounds 1 and 2 will be relegated to Lower Rounds 2 and 3.
    • Top 25 Duos in Upper Round 3 advance to the Grand Finals.
  • Semi Finals - Lower Bracket: February 16th-18th, 2024
    • Teams have 3 hours and 5 games.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations.
    • Top 15 Duos in Lower Round 3 advance to the Grand Finals.
  • Grand Finals: February 24th-25th, 2024
    • 12 games spread over two days.
    • 6 games between the two days.
    • Scoring based on placement and eliminations, with points multiplied by 1.5x on the second day of competition.
    • Top Duos advance to Global Championship.
      • If a duo has already qualified, their spot will be transferred to the closest placing duo that has not qualified.
