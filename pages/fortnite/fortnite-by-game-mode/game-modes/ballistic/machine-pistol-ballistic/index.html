The Machine Pistol is a Pistol in Fortnite: Ballistic that was added in Chapter 6: Season 3.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Machine Pistol is a medium damage pistol with a fast fire rate but low accuracy and magazine capacity.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, Machine Pistol was a Sidearm that cost 500 Credits.

The Machine Pistol is a Hitscan weapon without First Shot Accuracy.

Damage falloff:

  • Damage falloff begins at 14 meters (2.8 tiles).
  • Damage steadily decreases down to 84% of its maximum at 25 meters (5 tiles).
  • Damage reaches its minimum value of 50% of its maximum at 50 meters (10 tiles).

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Machine Pistol is a great pair for long range weapons like the Reaper Sniper Rifle, Sovereign Sniper or Enforcer AR, as it can cover their weakness at close range combat.
    • It can also pair surprisingly well with the Hammer Pump Shotgun as a finisher tool. Do note that you will suffer at longer ranges when running both of them at once.
  • Thanks to its price and good DPS, the Machine Pistol can be used to get a quick elimination on an opponent to take their primary. This saves you some Credits for later rounds.
  • The Machine Pistol is one of the few weapons to still have an ADS accuracy penalty while moving after Update v37.10. Crouching and standing still are recommended when engaging at medium range combat with this weapon.

Counters[]

  • The Machine Pistol performs poorly at medium to long range, due to its lack of First Shot Accuracy and high damage fall off. Using a sidearm such as the Ranger Pistol or Hand Cannon can make up for said range issues.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Machine Pistol re-uses the model and is a singular version of the Dual Micro SMGs, albeit with a different texture.
    • Due to this, it resembles the PM-84 Glauberyt.[1]
  • Machine Pistol is the second weapon featured in Ballistic to not be based on a Modular weapon. The first one was the DL-44 Blaster Pistol.
    • It is also the first non-collaboration weapon to not be based on a Modular weapon.
  • Due to it lacking both iron sights or an optic of any kind, the player will cant the Machine Pistol while aiming down sight.
  • Machine Pistol is the cheapest available weapon in Ballistic.
  • The Machine Pistol has the lowest headshot damage of any weapon in Ballistic, which makes it unable to eliminate an opponent at full health with two headshots.
