Overview

This article is about the weapon featured in Fortnite: Ballistic. For the weapon introduced in Chapter 5, see Modular Ranger Pistol. For the weapon of the same name, see Ranger Pistol.
This item is currently vaulted in Ballistic.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.
It is still available in Creative and UEFN.

“
Automatic Pistol: Very effective at close range.
”
— In-Game Description

The Ranger Pistol is a Pistol in Fortnite: Ballistic, that was added in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Functionality

Functionality[]

The Ranger Pistol is a fully automatic pistol with a decent fire rate and damage.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, Ranger Pistol was a starting Sidearm and costed 0 Credits.

The Ranger Pistol is a Hitscan weapon with First Shot Accuracy enabled.

Damage falloff:

  • Damage falloff begins at 10 meters (2 tiles).
  • Damage steadily decreases down to 84% of its maximum at 25 meters (5 tiles).
  • Damage reaches its minimum value of 77% of its maximum at 50 meters (10 tiles).

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • The Ranger Pistol is a serviceable sidearm in early rounds or as a backup option for weapons that may struggle in close range combat.
  • In pistol rounds, the Ranger Pistol is the only weapon that a player can use if they choose to buy 1 tick of Instant Shield (unless they pick up a different sidearm from an eliminated player or a teammate offers them one). This increases survivability at the cost of potential firepower.
  • It is advised to aim for the head with the Ranger Pistol, as its body shot time to kill is noticeably longer than most other weapons.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Ranger Pistol shares its model and animations with the Modular Ranger Pistol, albeit with a different texture which lacks The Society styled paintjob.
    • Due to this, it resembles the Heckler and Koch MK 23.[1].
  • Ranger Pistol, Machine Pistol and Hammer Pump Shotgun are the only weapons in Fortnite: Ballistic to lack Weapon Mods of any kind.
  • The Ranger Pistol was the first item in Ballistic history to ever be Vaulted before the mode's removal, being replaced by the DL-44 Blaster Pistol for the duration of Galactic Battle.
  • Unlike other Sidearms in Fortnite: Ballistic, the Ranger Pistol will not drop from an eliminated player that had one in their inventory.
