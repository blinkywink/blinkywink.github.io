Overview

This article is about the weapon featured in Fortnite: Ballistic. For the original item, see Shield Bubble.
This item is currently vaulted in Ballistic.
It has been made unavailable to balance the loot pool, and may return in the future.
It is still available in Creative and UEFN.

“
Bubble Shield provides a short duration spherical shield against all forms of projectiles and damage.
”
— In-Game Description

Bubble Shield is an Explosive Weapon in Fortnite: Ballistic, that was added in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Upon landing, the Bubble Shield. creates a large transparent shield dome around its landing spot. The Shield is able to block projectiles and gunfire, and will block these from both in and outside of the bubble.

The Bubble Shield's dome has 600 Health and will completely vanish once its health bar reaches zero or when its 6 second duration runs out.

In Fortnite: Ballistic, Bubble Shield was a Flex Gadget, two were granted at the start of each round when selected.

Bubble Shield is a slow moving projectile affected by gravity.

Bubble Shield used to block incoming shots

Strategy

Strategy[]

This section outlines a strategy.
Feel free to add tips, tricks and general advice about the topic.
  • Bubble Shield is an excellent cover item for either planting or defusing a Rift Point Device, especially when combined with Smoke Grenades.
  • When playing aggressively, pair your Bubble Shield with close range weapons like Shotguns. This will make it harder for enemies to predict your steps and stop them for going for an aggressive play themselves.
  • Bubble Shields are effective against slow firing weapons like the Reaper Sniper Rifle and Sovereign Sniper, as they lack the sustained fire required to break a dome.

Counters[]

  • Even with its high health, a Bubble Shield can be very easily destroyed by certain weapons. This is especially true in the case of the Drum Gun.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Bubble Shield was based on the Shield Bubble Jr. from Battle Royale, albeit with a drastically altered appearance and shorter duration.
  • Bubble Shield and other throwable items featured in Ballistic, has a different indicator arc to the one seen on other throwables.
  • Before an unknown update, the Bubble Shield would turn into a Shield Bubble when thrown into water.
  • Bubble Shield shown in the promo image from Update v35.10 uses the old icon for the item.
