Overview

Respawn Rumble, formerly known as Training Grounds, is the Zero Build team deathmatch game mode in Fortnite: Battle Royale. It has been introduced as a core game in Chapter 6: Season 3, replacing Team Rumble.

Gameplay

Gameplay[]

Respawn Royale is a Zero Build team deathmatch game mode, centered on one Location (currently located at Dark Dominion). The first team to get the targeted number of eliminations (40) wins the game mode.

History

History[]

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

  • Update v36.10: Introduced Respawn Rumble (known as Training Grounds at the time) as a core game, replacing Team Rumble.
  • June 27th 2025: Training Grounds was renamed to Respawn Royale.
  • Update v36.30: Moved Respawn Rumble from Utopia City to Supernova Academy.

Chapter 6: Season 4[]

  • Update v37.40: Moved Respawn Rumble to Dreamy Domain.

Chapter 6: Mini Season 2[]

  • Update v38.00: Moved Respawn Rumble to Springfield Town Square.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

  • Update v39.00: Moved Respawn Rumble to InnoLoop Labs (later known as Lethal Labs and Dark Dominion).
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • For some reason, whenever players load a game of Respawn Rumble, it still appears Training Grounds in the top left corner of the loading screen.
  • Despite the temporary removal of the Battle Bus, Respawn Rumble was not taken down alongside Fortnite OG, Blitz Royale and Reload, and the Battle Bus remains intact in the mode.
    • This also leaks the new model for the Battle Bus, showing band-aids connecting the front and back.
