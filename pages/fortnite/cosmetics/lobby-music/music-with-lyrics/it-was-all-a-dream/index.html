It Was All A Dream was an Icon Series Lobby Music in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for 200 V-Bucks. It Was All A Dream was first released in Chapter 3: Season 3.

Following the release of Update v34.00, the item was removed from all player's lockers in favor of it's Jam Track variant.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for It Was All A Dream's appearances?
It Was All A Dream has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • To keep the song in line with the game's rating, several lines of the song are missing.
    • These lines reference Alcohol, Drug Usage and Terrorism as well as using Inappropriate Language. Furthermore, the last two verses of the song are cut off.
    • Differently from the Lobby Music, the Jam Track of the same song (Juicy) keeps those references uncensored in the lyrics, with only a few slurs and other swear words being censored.
