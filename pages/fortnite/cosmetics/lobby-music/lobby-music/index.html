Lobby Music

Overview

As of May 14th 2026, there are 221[1] Lobby Music In-Game
“
Choose your tunes.
”
— In-Game Description

Lobby Music are Cosmetic Items introduced in Season 6. They can be used in every mode.

Lobby Music change the lobby background music as well as the Victory Royale music. If equipped as a Moment, Lobby Music can also play at the beginning of a match when the player drops from the Battle Bus.

“
Press play and-
”
— Usual prefix for Descriptions

All Jam Tracks can be used as Lobby Music.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • After the introduction of Jam Tracks in Chapter 5: Season 1, Lobby Music was phased out of the game.
    • The last Battle Pass to feature Lobby Music was in Chapter 5: Season 4. Beginning with Chapter 2 Remix, only Jam Tracks were offered in the Battle Pass.
    • The last Lobby Music to be sold in the Item Shop was Find the Fury, which would later be taken out of the FNCS C5S2 Bundle.
      • Make It Known, a Lobby Music pack first sold during Chapter 5: Season 1, was also taken out of the FNCS C5S1 Bundle.
    • In Update v30.10, all Jam Tracks can be used as Lobby Music.
    • In Update v34.00, all Lobby Music that had a Jam Track variant were removed from players' Lockers.
    • Chapter 6 is the first chapter in Fortnite's history not to feature any new Lobby Music additions that were made available to players.
