Car Bodies

Overview

As of May 29th 2026, there are 109 Car Bodies[1] In-Game

Car Bodies are Cosmetic Items introduced in Chapter 5: Season 1. They can be used in Battle Royale and Rocket Racing. In Rocket Racing they serve as the player's car. In Battle Royale, they serve as an outfit for the Victory Motors Whiplash or the Grandeur Trailsmasher. When a player enters a Whiplash or a Trailsmasher, the vehicle will change to match the equipped car body.
