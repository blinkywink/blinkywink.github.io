Mashup Cosmetics

Overview

This article is about the fusion of cosmetics resulting from two original fortnite cosmetics. For the fusion of cosmetics resulting from collaboration, see Collaboration Mashup Cosmetics.

Mashup Cosmetics are a collection of Cosmetic Items in Fortnite, which are combinations of 2 or more already existing items. The concept of Mashup Cosmetics was introduced in Chapter 4: Season OG. To enter this category, a cosmetic must have the attributes of two or more outfits as well as a merged name.

Note: the fusions between a Collaboration Outfit and an Original Outfit are not included here. See Collaboration Mashup Cosmetics instead.

Trivia[]

  • From a storyline perspective, most of these cosmetics are explained by the Alternative Timelines of Reality Zero, in other words Realities very similar to Reality Zero but which had different events, such as Reality: UN-0-C4OG.
    • However, some such as Undercover Skye and Meowdas are respectively just an evolution (Skye disguised herself as Guff) and a completely different character (Meowdas is neither Midas nor Meowscles).
