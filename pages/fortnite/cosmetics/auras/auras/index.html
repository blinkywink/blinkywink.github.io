Auras

Overview

This article is about the Cosmetic Item Type. For the Outfit of the same name, see Aura (Outfit).
As of May 14th 2026, there are 44[1] Auras In-Game

Auras are Cosmetic Items introduced in Chapter 5: Season 1. They can be used in Battle Royale and Fortnite Festival.
