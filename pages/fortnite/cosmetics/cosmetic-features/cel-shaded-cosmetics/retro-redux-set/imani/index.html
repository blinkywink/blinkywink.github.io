Outfit[]

“
Embrace the past. Create the future.
”
— In-Game Description

Imani is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 3, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 2 Battle Pass. Imani is part of Retro Redux Set.
