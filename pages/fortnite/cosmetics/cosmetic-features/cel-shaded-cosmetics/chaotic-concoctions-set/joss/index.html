Outfit[]

“
It's not magic. It's chemistry.

Joss is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 3 of the Chapter 6: Season 2 Battle Pass. Joss is part of the Chaotic Concoctions Set.
