Masked is an Icon Series Emote in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with The Weeknd Combat for 1,800 V-Bucks or with The Weeknd Bundle for 3,500 V-Bucks. Masked was first released in Chapter 5: Season 1 and is part of the The Weeknd Set.

Masked is The Weeknd Combat's Built-In Emote and can only be used while wearing him, alongside his "White" Color style.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Masked's appearances?
Masked has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
