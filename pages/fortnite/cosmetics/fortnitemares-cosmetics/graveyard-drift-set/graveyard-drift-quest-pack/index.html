“
After falling into darkness, only the Driftwalker remains. Corruption is taking over and the Graveyard Drift begins! Complete Quests to earn V-Bucks with the Graveyard Drift Quest Pack.

The Graveyard Drift Quest Pack is a Legendary Limited Time Offer in Fortnite, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for $15.99. The Graveyard Drift Quest Pack was first released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and contains Driftwalker with his cosmetics from the Graveyard Drift Set along with the Driftwalker Quests.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Graveyard Drift Quest Pack's appearances?
Graveyard Drift Quest Pack has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
