Overview

The cosmetics in this Bundle have been chosen/created by a second/third party.
While most of the cosmetics in this Bundle are original to Fortnite, the contents were chosen through a special collaboration.

T-Pain's Locker Bundle is a Rare Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,600 V-Bucks. T-Pain's Locker Bundle was first released in Chapter 6: Mini Season 2 and contains cosmetics that T-Pain has picked out.
