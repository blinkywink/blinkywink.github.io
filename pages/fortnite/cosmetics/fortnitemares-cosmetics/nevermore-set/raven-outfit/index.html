Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Raven's appearances?
Raven has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

”
— Raven

Raven is a mysterious undead-looking creature, a poet as well as one of the fighters kidnapped by the Imagined Order and trapped in The Loop. While initially neutral, even an ally at times, he revealed to have been affiliated to the Storm and by extension The Last Reality since the beginning, being made from Cube/Storm energy.[1] In LEGO Fortnite:Odyssey, he was called back by The Storm King, and became his right hand man.

He is a major character in Fortnite, initially serving as a supporting character from Chapter 1 to Chapter 4 before turning as a major antagonist of Fortnite, serving as the Secondary Antagonist of LEGO Fortnite:Odyssey.

Personality

Personality[]

Raven is fascinated by everything dark and ominous as well as by the sky. As part of his personality, he will always say cryptic and often dark things, no matter the situation. He is also a poet in his spare time. However, while he remained relatively on the good side despite his claims for a time, he was actually a member of The Last Reality. He later joined The Storm King's army and began spreading corruption and destruction, thus becoming known as The Destructor.

Appearence

Appearence[]

Raven is an undead creature appearing in loose, dark clothing imbued with feathers similar to those of crows. He also wears a hood, revealing only his bright purple eyes.

In his Graven form, he reveals under his clothes that he is a skeleton, he however has wings allowing him to fly.

In his Storm-Wild form, Raven keeps exactly the same appearance, however the color of his clothes is altered by his powers, his eyes are even brighter and storm lightning emanates from his body.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Unknown Period of Time[]

Raven was trapped in The Loop alongside other Loopers.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

Raven enlists The Looper's help to destroy Cube Monsters in the Dark Skies Questlines. He then went on Beach Vacacion and became Graven.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

On Asteria Island, Raven is now in charge of a poetry club club. Phaedra excludes herself from him for her recent absence, due to a job she had to do.

LEGO Fortnite[]

Brick Life[]

Raven became a professor at the Mourndale Academy of Brick Bay.

Odyssey[]

Several people began to have strange dreams about a Storm and a mysterious creature. Raven was among them however, unlike the others, he was called to join them. He revealed to The Looper that a storm was coming, and that he could not stay, because "he" was calling him.

Storm Chasers[]

Raven joined The Storm King's army and became his right-hand man. While leading his master's Storm-Wild legion to destroy and consume the world, he earned the epithet of The Destructor. Raven was also entrusted the arcane lock to seal the Tempest Gateway that leads to The Storm King's lair.
