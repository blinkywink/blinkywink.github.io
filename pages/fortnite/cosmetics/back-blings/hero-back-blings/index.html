Overview

As of , there are 211 Hero Back Blings In-Game

Hero Back Blings are Cosmetic Items only usable in Fortnite: Save the World and are locked to specific Heroes. Some Hero Back Blings are also present in other modes such as Constructor Classic. Hero Back Blings are generally unlocked by evolving a hero to tier 3.

Hero Back Blings do not have official names and are instead listed as the Hero they come with.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • There are a total of 211 Hero Back Blings.
    • Soldiers have 63, 35 of those are available in other modes.
    • Constructors have 50, 19 of those are available in other modes.
    • Ninjas have 48, 17 of those are available in other modes.
    • Outlander have 50, 15 of those are available in other modes.
  • There are some Heroes that share the same Hero Back Blings, with the only diference being slightly different images:
    • Brainiac Jonesy and Skull Ranger Ramirez.
    • Chromium Ramirez and Diecast Jonesy.
    • Centurion Hawk and Special Forces Banshee.
    • Double Agent Evelynn, Undercover Vaughn and Support Specialist Hawk's Legendary style.
    • Demolitionist Penny and Saboteur Bull.
    • Archaeolo-Jess and First Shot Rio's Legendary style.
    • Pathfinder Jess and Shock Specialist A.C..
    • Ambush Buzz and Shockgunner Grizzly.
  • Dennis and Director Riggs were planned to have Hero Back Blings but were scrapped.
    • Dennis' ID is: BID_Ninja_040_Dennis.
    • Director Riggs' ID is: BID_Constructor_037_Director.
