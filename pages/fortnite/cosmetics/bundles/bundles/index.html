Bundles

Overview

This article is about the list of bundles. For the Outfit, see Bundles (Outfit).

Bundles (not to be confused with Item Shop Bundles) are Bundles that consist of cosmetics that are found through objects in real life. They usually don't become available again once the promotion they're dealing with has ended.
