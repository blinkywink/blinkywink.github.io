Overview

“
The youngest sworn knight of an ageless order.
”
— In-Game Description

Rift Knight Kieran is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during March 2023, as part of the March 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack. Rift Knight Kieran was released in Chapter 4: Season 1 and is part of the Embers of Empire Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Rift Knight Kieran's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Signature
Base


Rift Knight Kieran received a higher-detailed style in Update v30.20, which replaced the original one.
Players have the option to choose between both.

Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Rift Knight Kieran's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.
