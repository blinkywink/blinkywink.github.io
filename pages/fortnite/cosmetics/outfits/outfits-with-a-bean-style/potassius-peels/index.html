Overview

“
Et tu, Fruitus?
”
— In-Game Description

Potassius Peels is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks, with the Peely Party Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks or with SirSANX's Locker Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. Potassius Peels was first released in Chapter 2: Season 6 and is part of The Ides of Bunch Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Potassius Peels's appearances?
Potassius Peels has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Potassius Peels first appeared as a statue inside Colossal Coliseum.
  • Potassius Peels' description, "Et tu, Fruitus?" translates to "You too, Fruitus?" and is a reference to the phrase "You too, Brutus?" ,from the play Julius Caesar by William Shakespeare, which were Julius Caesar's final words before being fatally stabbed to death by Brutus.
  • Before being officially named, Potassius Peels was originally known by the community as "Peelius Caesar" in Chapter 2: Season 5.
