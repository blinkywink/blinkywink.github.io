Dahlia is a Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during May 2023, as part of the May 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack. Dahlia was released in Chapter 4: Season 2 and is part of the Unseen Genius Set.

Trivia[]

Dahlia is a member of The Unseen, and the saboteur responsible for corrupting Peace Syndicate's files on the Army of the Last Reality. She also instigated the events that nearly sparked a war between all of MEGA City's Syndicates during the Syndicate Quests.

She is a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main antagonist of Chapter 4: Season 2, and a minor antagonist of Chapter 4: Season 3 and Chapter 4: Season 4.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Background[]

During an unknown period of time, Dahlia joined The Unseen and began plotting in collaboration with The Last Reality alongside Triarch Nox and Styx, unbeknownst to the rest of The Unseen's members.[1]

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

Dahlia appeared near Crude Harbor in a small hut as a character. She became a wanted criminal in MEGA City following an Explosive Escape alongside Styx. However, her plans with the Last Reality were ultimately thwarted by Highwire and Renzo.[2][3]

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

Special dialogue with Aura strongly suggests that Dahlia is responsible for bringing the Chalice of Cubetastrophe to the island.[4]

Dahlia continued her schemes in MEGA City with Rian,[5] who was Lorenzo's guide in the rainforest, and would discreetly sell his secrets to her.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

Dahlia seemingly tried to steal Kado Thorne's Time Machine, however The Heist Crew managed to swipe the machine before her. Jones would later taunt her with a pun, combining "machine" and "machination".[6]
