Outfit[]

“
The future looks bright...
”
— In-Game Description

Brite Bomber is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks, with the Brite Bundle for 2,800 V-Bucks, with Ninja's OG Locker Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks, or with the Double Rainbow Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Brite Bomber was first released in Season 2 and is part of the Sunshine And Rainbows Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Brite Bomber's appearances?
Brite Bomber has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Brite Bomber has a male counterpart named Brite Gunner.
  • Brite Bomber is the most prominent outfit used in promotional material for LEGO Fortnite as she was the first character used in the initial reveal trailer, the main character of the mode's cinematic trailer, and has made an appearance in most of the mode's promotional images and keyart. Additionally, the outfit's icon was initally used as the profile image of LEGO Fortnite's official Twitter account.
  • Brite Bomber seems to have a close relationship with Drift, as shown in various Loading Screens.
