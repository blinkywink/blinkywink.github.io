Overview

“
Every good boy has a dark side.
”
— In-Game Description

Dark Doggo is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Darkest Grumblings Pack in the Item Shop for $4.49. Dark Doggo was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1 and is part of the Darkest Grumblings Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Dark Doggo's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Dark Doggo's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Dark Doggo's appearances?
Dark Doggo has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
