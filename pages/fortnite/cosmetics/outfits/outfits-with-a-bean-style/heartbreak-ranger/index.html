Heartbreak Ranger is a Lava Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Heartbreak Royale Pack in the Item Shop for $4.49. Heartbreak Ranger was released in Chapter 4: Season OG and is part of the Heartbreak Royale Set.
