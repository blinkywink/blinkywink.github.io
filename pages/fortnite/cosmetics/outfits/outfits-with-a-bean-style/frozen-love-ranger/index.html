Overview

“
Aim for the cold-hearted.
”
— In-Game Description

Frozen Love Ranger is a Frozen Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Frozen Legends Pack in the Item Shop for $16.99. Frozen Love Ranger was first released in Season 7 and is part of the Frozen Legends Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Frozen Love Ranger's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Signature
Base


Frozen Love Ranger received a higher-detailed style in Update v28.30, which replaced the original one.
Players have the option to choose between both.

Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Frozen Love Ranger's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Frozen Love Ranger's appearances?
Frozen Love Ranger has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
