Lizzik is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with the Sun & Scales Pack for $4.49. Lizzik was first released in Chapter 5: Season 4 and is part of the Sun & Scales Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Lizzik's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Lizzik's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Lizzik's appearances?
Lizzik has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Whenever Lizzik blinks, a nictitating membrane can be seen, though it retracts significantly slower than it's real life counter part.
  • If an Emote is played while using the outfit, Lizzik's tongue will instantly retract into his mouth and vice versa when the Emote finishes playing. Curiously, this does not happen with the additional player models that use the same Outfit the player is using for the demonstration of Synced Emotes in the Locker
  • Possibly due to a bug, when Sliding, Lizzik's tail will straighten out and clip into the surface during the start of the sliding animation, though it will reset to it's default animation if the player falls off a surface while sliding.
