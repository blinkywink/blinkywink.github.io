Outfit[]

“

Cuddle Team Leader is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks or with the Cuddle Team Bundle for 3,000 V-Bucks. Cuddle Team Leader was first released in Season 2 and is part of the Royale Hearts Set.

Cuddle Team Leader can also be obtained by purchasing the Rave Cave 77082 LEGO® Fortnite® Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Cuddle Team Leader's appearances?
Cuddle Team Leader has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader used to be in a "rare" Loading Screen telling the player that only they can prevent V-Buck scams, parodying the Smokey Bear posters from the U.S. Forest Service Wildfire Prevention Program.
    • With Chapter 5: Season 1's debut, she has since been replaced with Rapscallion.
  • Cuddle Team Leader and Raven are frequently paired together:
    • Cuddlepool and Ravenpool for Deadpool.
    • Curdle Scream Leader and Graven for Universal Monsters.
    • Raven Team Leader, a mix of the two.
    • Raven Team Syd, another mix of the two (with Syd being Cuddle Team Leader's real identity).
  • Cuddle Team Leader is seemingly inspired by the Care Bears from the franchise of the same name, as the symbol on her belly is reminiscent of their signature Belly Badges.

Syd (better known as Cuddle Team Leader and as Mecha Cuddle Master) is the leader of the Camp Cuddle, as well as the owner of Rave Cave's Baller coaster and Cuddle Cross Track.

Personality

Personality[]

Cuddle Team Leader is a very friendly person who can solve most of her problems by hugging it out. But when the problem is unsolvable, things can get a little violent. She helped Drift when he first arrived on the island in Season 5 Road Trip!. She also acts as a guide for new players in LEGO Fortnite as a tutorial character.

History

History[]

Chapter 1: Season 5[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader went on a Road Trip! with Brite Bomber, Rex and Drift.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader was thrown into an unknown futuristic reality after Zero Crisis Finale. She became Mecha Cuddle Master and fought her way back to the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader became the leader of Camp Cuddle.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader made a gigantic Cuddle Team Leader head on top of Rave Cave and turned it into a Baller coaster.

LEGO Fortnite[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader is one of the many characters rifted in the LEGO Reality as well as one of the first Looper encounters.
