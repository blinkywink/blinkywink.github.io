Overview

“
Brave the cold.
”
— In-Game Description

Frostbite is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Deep Freeze Bundle in the Item Shop in the Item Shop for $14.99 or with the Darkfire & Ice Bundle for $29.99. Frostbite was first released in Season 6 and is part of the Deep Freeze Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Frostbite's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Frostbite's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Frostbite's appearances?
Frostbite has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
