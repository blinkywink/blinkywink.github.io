Outfit[]

“
Conquer The Storm.
”

Magnus is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks. Magnus was first released in Season 5 and is part of the Norse Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Magnus's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Magnus is a character from an unknown universe, that has escaped The Loop. He joins a Rebel group with Bandolette, Renegade Raider, Bonehead and more. They get into The Bridge and find the room with The Zero Point. They turn the panel on and he goes back to his home universe.
