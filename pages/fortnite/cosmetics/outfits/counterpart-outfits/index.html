Counterpart Outfits

Overview

Counterpart Outfits are duos of male and female Outfits that share design, ID, theme, and/or lore characteristics. They tend to be in the same set, although it is becoming increasingly rare.

Notes

Notes[]

  • This page doesn't include characters made by creators other than Epic Games, except for Marshmello.
  • This does not necessarily imply any romantic or relationship connection between the characters; it refers solely to their status as counterparts within the game.
  • Some of the Outfits have multiple counterparts due to updates that introduced new Outfits or added styles to already existing ones, however only the "closest" counterpart has been chosen for this list.
    • For example, Wild Card was introduced alongside a counterpart outfit named The Ace during Update v5.30, however Update v21.50 introduced a new counterpart to Wild Card named Loveless, which is closer by design than The Ace. Loveless has therefore been chosen over The Ace.
