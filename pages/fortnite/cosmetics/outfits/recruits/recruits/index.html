Overview

Recruits, most commonly referred to as Default Skins or simply Defaults, are a collection of Outfits that are randomly selected if no outfit is selected in the Locker.

Ever since the Update v33.10, a specific Recruit can now be selected in the Locker from the selection (or be set to random), instead of just being selected at random.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Chapter 1 Recruits are based on the various Soldier Heroes from Fortnite: Save the World.
  • Only three Recruits stayed as Recruits in Chapter 2: Season 1 being Jonesy, Banshee and Ramirez.
    • Later, Wildcat would return, making it 4 veterans.
  • Jonesy and Ramirez are the only characters to be Recruits since Chapter 1.
  • Outfits based on the Chapter 1 (Origins Set) and first wave of Chapter 2 Recruits (Recruit Redux Set) are purchasable in the Item Shop.
  • The Chapter 2 Recruits have the logo of the Storm Shield from Save the World.
  • The Seven Sentries and Low Cards use the same model as Chapter 3 Recruits, albeit with helmets similar to those of The Seven and The Dealers respectively.
  • Since Chapter 3, no new Recruits were added, and existing ones were reused in later Chapters.
    • Chapter 5 introduced new Recruits, however they are only accessible in Fortnite Festival and Rocket Racing.
      • They were removed a Chapter later in Update v33.10.
