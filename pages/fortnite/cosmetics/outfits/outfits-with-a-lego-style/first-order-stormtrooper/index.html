Outfit[]

“
Wear the armor of a First Order stormtrooper.

— In-Game Description

First Order Stormtrooper is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be obtained by linking your myDisney account to your Epic Games account until October 31st. First Order Stormtrooper was first released in Chapter 6: Season 2 and is part of The New Trilogy Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, First Order Stormtrooper's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Trivia

Trivia[]

  • First Order Stormtrooper has an unreleased variant, added in Update v11.30, named Set_01_PA.
    • This makes the First Order Stormtrooper the Outfit with the longest distance between their addition to the game's files, and their official release, beating out both GHOST Enforcer and SHADOW Enforcer with nearly 5 and a half years of being hidden in the files.
    • The unreleased variant is also compatible with Kicks.

First Order Stormtroopers were hostile Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced during Chapter 2: Season 1.

Description

Description[]

They could have been found in First Order controlled POIs and will shoot at the player if they notice them undisguised.

All First Order Stormtroopers will be eliminated once all in the area are knocked, and they will attempt to revive each other if they remain alive.

First Order Stormtroopers were hostile Guards in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They were introduced during Chapter 2: Season 1.

Description

Description[]

They were first added into the game after the Live At Risky Live Event. 4-5 of them could spawn at all of the TIE Fighter Crash Sites around the island, protecting a First Order Banner in each Crash Site.

They would attack players on sight and will instantly be eliminated upon their health reaching 0.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The First Order Stormtroopers were one of the first human AIs added in the game.
    • They were also the first Star Wars Guards added.
    • As the First Order Stormtroopers were the first Guards created, their accuracy was extremely precise despite public notions of them having poor aim.
  • When eliminated by them during Chapter 2: Season 1, the Message Feed and the Guards' name displays the First Order Stormtroopers as The First Order.
  • The First Order Stormtroopers had a few unique animations that weren't carried over to the finalized Guard AI. Examples being looking around while patrolling or tapping their helmets to contact higher ups.
  • The First Order Stormtroopers were initially the only Guards to not have any visible pop up above their head when suspicious or alerted. A trait that would be added onto every human AI afterwards. This would be corrected with their re-appearance in Galactic Battle.
  • When they were re-added the First Order Stormtroopers reuse the voicelines of the Stormtroopers from Chapter 5: Season 2, but with a slightly lower pitch applied.
