Iron Man Mk 45 is a Marvel Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Iron Man Mk 45 Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks. Iron Man Mk 45 was first released in Chapter 5: Season 4 and is part of the Iron Man Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Iron Man Mk 45's appearances?
Iron Man Mk 45 has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Iron Man Mk 45's design takes heavy inspiration from the Marvel Cinematic Universe film Avengers: Age of Ultron.
    • Despite this, his description confirms that he is the same Tony Stark from Reality: 616, as it directly references The Devourer of Worlds Live Event.
    • Although the suit bears resemblance to the film suit, they are functionally different. This is due to the Fortnite snapshot being a nanotech suit, as stated in the Mark 45 Energy Blades Pickaxe's description, whereas the suit in Age of Ultron had full plating.

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a major character in Fortnite: Battle Royale during Chapter 2: Season 4.

History

History[]

Nexus War: Thor[]

As Galactus felt the reality shift during The End, and started to travel towards its source, The Zero Point, in order to consume it and become even more powerful. Thor arrived on the island through the Bifrost to warn its inhabitants of the upcoming threat, and summoned Tony Stark, Doctor Doom, She-Hulk, Wolverine, Storm, and Groot. S.H.I.E.L.D. also made an appearance, and provided Quinjets and Helicarrier 64 to aid in the mission.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

The Imagined Order allowed the superheroes to settle in on the island and fight back against Galactus, who was visible at-the-time as a red dot in the sky, as all of reality was at stake. Following Tony Stark's arrival, he got to work right away, establishing a small base of operations in a disused farm house and developing Rift Beacons. Stark placed the beacons around Frenzy Farm, and succeeded in his task, bringing a huge chunk of Upstate New York containing Stark Industries to the island. Stark utilized its high-tech research facilities and resources to upgrade the battle buses, fitting guns and gamma cells onto them. He then proceeded to use The Loop to clone the modified battle buses around 1 billion times. Stark also used the Rift Beacons to transport other superheroes to the island, such as Ant-Man and Black Panther (although the latter likely did not appear due to the passing of Chadwick).

The Devourer of Worlds[]

When Galactus finally arrived to the island, he used his powerful abilities to rip The Zero Point out from The Bridge and began to absorb its power. However, The Zero Point seemed to have fought back, and violently pulled the tendrils of energy Galactus was absorbing back into it. This process cracked The Zero Point, and it pulled the entire island into The In-Between. In the ensuing battle, Galactus was tricked into swallowing the battle buses, and the gamma cells were promptly detonated, injuring Galactus and sending him through a rift.

Following the event The Zero Point stabilized the island and reorganized the matter around itself into a desert, bringing in locations from the past as well. However, despite this, The Zero Point's outer shell was cracked, and it soon began to destabilize like in Season X. Following the battle, the superheroes left the island along with any of their traces.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

In the Chapter 3: Season 2 trailer, Tony Stark is seen in his Iron Man suit alongside Doctor Strange, entering the island through a portal.
