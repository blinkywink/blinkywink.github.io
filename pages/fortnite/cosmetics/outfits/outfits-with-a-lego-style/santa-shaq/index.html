Santa Shaq is an Icon Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Santa Shaq Bundle for 2,000 V-Bucks. Santa Shaq was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1 and is part of the Santa Shaq Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Santa Shaq's size is Slightly Bigger than other Characters. Similar to The Foundation and the Doombot Boss.
  • Santa Shaq wears the Santa Shaqpack as his Back Bling.
  • When aggravated, Santa Shaq will only use the Hittin' Mittens Pickaxe to fight.
  • Before Santa Shaq was decrypted, his Character's Outfit was Infantry Commander despite being available before the Outfit.
