Jess is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained as a reward from Save the World Quests. Jess was first released in Chapter 7: Season 2.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Jess's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Jess' appearance is based on Rare Pathfinder Jess from Save the World.
    • Unlike Pathfinder Jess, the Outfit Jess has her Hero Back Bling built into her model, along with the addition of her headphones from the Epic and Legendary variants.
    • Like Sarah, Jess' model was altered to use the Medium Player Model. Along with updating her head model.
