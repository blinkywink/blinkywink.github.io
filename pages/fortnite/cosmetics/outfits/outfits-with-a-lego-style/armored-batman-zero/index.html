Armored Batman Zero is a DC Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Armored Batman Zero Bundle for 2,100 V-Bucks. Armored Batman Zero was first released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and is part of the Batman: Zero Point Set.

Armored Batman Zero can also be unlocked by redeeming all six codes found in the Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Armored Batman Zero's appearances?
Armored Batman Zero has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Armored Batman is the Snapshot of Batman created when he left The Loop after Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 3.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 4[]

Right after Batman escaped The Loop, Armored Batman was created. This Snapshot of Batman later encountered a Snapshot of Catwoman in The Loop, and both decided to work together, much like their original counterparts did before them. Instead of focusing on escaping the Loop like their counterparts, Armored Batman and Catwoman focused on survival and upgraded their gear, with Batman crafting an armor, while Catwoman stole equipment from other Loopers, such as Lynx's helmet.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 6[]

He and Catwoman's Snapshots meet their original counterparts and they try to kill them but are distracted by Batman's bomb, allowing Batman and Catwoman return to The Bridge.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Around the same time The Last Reality's ship attacked The Spire[1] The Foundation created, ejecting the latter in Prime Earth[2], Batman escaped The Loop. However, unlike his original counterpart, he decided to stay on the Island to protect it. He joined the fight against the invading aliens and teamed up with a version of Beast Boy as well as The Looper in order to help another version of Superman who was trapped on the Island to recover his stolen memories[3]. After this he was approached by Doctor Slone about his explosive designs, the latter wanting to use it for the "Countermeasure Device", a device meant to destroy from the inside what the Imagined Order believed to be the alien's mothership. After The Looper warned Batman about the incoming crash, he disappeared, never to be seen again.
