Trivia[]

  • "Singularity" also refers to the center of a black hole that has infinite density.
  • Singularity has a male counterpart named Vendetta.
  • A popular theory was that she is The Paradigm, due to a few reasons:
    • The Paradigm shares the same armor design with Singularity's Upgraded style.
    • Donald Mustard said that Singularity will have a big impact on the story, and goes by many names other than Singularity.
    • This theory was disproved by Donald Mustard and the writers of the Zero War Comics on August 22th 2022.
  • Singularity was the first female Mystery Outfit.

The Singularity, is a former high-ranking agent of the Imagined Order, who was once tasked with protecting The Vault from within, watching over the Zero Point. She is the creator of Mecha Team Leader, Neo Tilted and Mega Mall.

She is a major character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as an minor character in Season 8, and one of the main protagonist of Season 9.

Timeline[]

Unknown time period[]

The Singularity appears to have had some connection to The Seven in the past, being listed seemingly as a member by AMIE in the database cataloging the status of The Seven's members. However, she joined the Imagined Order at one point, and later became the guardian of The Vault.

Season 8[]

During The Unvaulting, The Loopers who entered The Vault found Singularity's desk, directly in front of the Zero Point. Her helmet was on the desk, however Singularity herself was absent, for reasons unknown.

Season 9[]

After The Unvaulting, which culminated in the destruction of Tilted Towers and Retail Row during the eruption of The Prisoner's Volcano, Singularity oversaw the reconstruction of the two cities as Neo Tilted and Mega Mall respectively, and upgraded the Island's technology to something more futuristic, becoming a heroic figure on the island. In her honor, a statue in her likeness was erected in Neo Tilted.

Later, when The Devourer broke free from Polar Peak, she was tasked by the IO with building the Mecha Team Leader in order for The Paradigm to pilot it and defeat the kaiju before it could damage Zero Point. After two weeks of work within the Pressure Plant, which was transformed from a geothermal power plant into a mecha construction base, The Paradigm pilots a fully finished Mecha Team Leader in order to fight The Devourer during The Final Showdown.

In order to be able to finish off the beast once and for all, the Mecha Team Leader piloted by Paradigm pulls upon Singularity's statue and unsheathes it from the ground, revealing it to be a hidden Sword, which was then used to slay the monster.

The Singularity later left the Imagined Order, understanding Geno craved control and what he was becoming, she left the Imagined Order.

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

After AMIE replayed the downfall of The Paradigm, confirming her death to the Zero Fusion to The Seven, The Order lamented that she, The Foundation and The Visitor were all that was left of the Seven, and that it wouldn't be enough. However, The Visitor told her that might not be the case, before AMIE mentioned The Singularity. While The Order was saying that she was IO and wouldn't work with them, an annoyed AMIE repeated her name, before decrypting her status, revealing to the New Seven that she was on her way to Reality: 266, implying she might have received The Seven's distress signal. Upon learning of this, The Foundation acknowledges that she's late, but is appreciative of her assistance.
