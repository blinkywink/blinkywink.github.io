Outfit[]

“
The cold harbinger of fate.
”
— In-Game Description

Ragnarok is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Tier 100 of the Season 5 Battle Pass. Ragnarok is part of the Harbinger Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Stage 1 - Gain 35,000 Season XP
  • Stage 2 - Gain 75,000 Season XP
  • Stage 3 - Gain 125,000 Season XP
  • Stage 4 - Gain 250,000 Season XP
  • Stage 5 and No Shoulder Pad - Gain 500,000 Season XP

LEGO® Style
