Outfit[]

“
The arena's greatest champion.
”
— In-Game Description

Menace is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Level 83 of the Chapter 2: Season 5 Battle Pass. Menace is part of the The Vanquisher Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Menace has a female counterpart named Sica.
  • His Enlightened Styles don't affect his armor, but his head can be seen with the style on if an unmasked style is chosen.
  • In the Chinese version of Fortnite, the skull on Menace's Heavy Helmet was removed.
