Outfit[]

“
A wanderer whose road will lead him to glory.
”
— In-Game Description

Ronin is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 1, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 1 Battle Pass. Ronin is part of the Order of the Waning Moon Set.
