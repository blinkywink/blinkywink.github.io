Adamantium Claws is a Marvel Series Pickaxe in Fortnite, that can be purchased with the Adamantium Claws Pack in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks. Adamantium Claws was first released in Chapter 3: Season 3 and is part of the Zero War Set.

Adamantium Claws could have been obtained by redeeming a code found in either a copy of the Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War Hardcover Collection for $29.99 before December 31, 2024, a copy of Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War Issue 3 for $5.99 before December 31, 2023, or from digital issues of Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War Issue 3 via Marvel Unlimited.[1]

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Adamantium Claws's appearances?
Adamantium Claws has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
