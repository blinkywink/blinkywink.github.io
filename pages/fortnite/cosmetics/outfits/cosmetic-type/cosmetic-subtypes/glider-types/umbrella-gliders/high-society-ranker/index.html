High Society Ranker is a Rare Glider in Fortnite, that could have been obtained as a reward from Ranked Cups. High Society Ranker was first released in Chapter 5: Season 1.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Silver - Reach the Silver I Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1
  • Gold - Reach the Gold I Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1
  • Platinum - Reach the Platinum I Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1
  • Diamond - Reach the Diamond I Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1
  • Elite - Reach the Elite Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1
  • Champion - Reach the Champion Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1
  • Unreal - Reach the Unreal Rank during Chapter 5: Season 1

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • High Society Ranker is one of the few items, that shows the player's rank in Ranked.
  • High Society Ranker is a remix of Rebel Brella.
  • Before this feature was removed in Update v28.10, the High Society Ranker was the first Ranked Cosmetic to have selectable styles. They ranged from Bronze up to the highest Season Rank achieved by the player during Chapter 5: Season 1.
    • It was added back in Update v29.20.
