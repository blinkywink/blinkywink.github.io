Overview

“
Float into action with the first ever customizable umbrella.
”
— In-Game Description

Brella is an Epic Glider in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Level 5 of the Chapter 2: Season 3 Battle Pass. Brella is part of the Custom Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Tactical Scuba, Space Wanderer, Professor Pup, Ocean Rider, and Black Knight parts are named after the codenames of Scuba Jonesy, Siona, Kit, Ocean, and Eternal Knight.
    • In concept art, the Tactical Scuba and Space Wanderer parts use the colors of Advanced Scuba Jonesy and Siona.
  • Despite being a Forged Cosmetic, it does not have the Forged tag in-game, despite having the same mechanics as Gear Specialist Maya, who does have the tag.
