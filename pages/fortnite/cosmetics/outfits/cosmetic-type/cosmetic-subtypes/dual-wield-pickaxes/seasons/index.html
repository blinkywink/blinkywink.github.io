Overview

This article is about the Battle Royale seasons. For Save the World equivalent of this article, see Ventures.

In Fortnite: Battle Royale, Seasons are designated periods of time that features exclusive content and gameplay limited to that particular timeframe, which includes a themed Battle Pass, Events, and Limited Time Modes. Each season introduces new gameplay elements, map features, cosmetics, and Battle Passes, and this content is rarely revisited.

Seasons are grouped into what are known as Chapters. Since Chapter 3, Chapters are introduced annually, featuring new maps and revamped gameplay. The first six chapters are considered to be part of "Phase 1".[1]

Battle Passes, introduced in Season 2, are special offers that feature cosmetics that can be obtained by gaining XP and Levels through gameplay. Each Battle Pass has more than 100 unique rewards.

Timeline[]

Additions

Additions[]

There have been many additions to Battle Royale, including features and lore events.

Chapter 1[]

Pre-Season[]

This season took place before seasons were properly introduced to Battle Royale. It took place after the introduction of Fortnite: Battle Royale as a game mode, and ended a few weeks later at the start of Season 1. No cosmetics were available at this time, players had to use the Recruit Outfits and Default Cosmetic Items.

The Zapotron was introduced as a weapon at this time, but it was quickly vaulted after 30 minutes. The reasoning behind this is not clear.

Season 1[]

In Season 1, the Item Shop and Season Shop were introduced, making it possible for players to purchase Cosmetic Items. Some cosmetic items (such as Renegade Raider) could have been obtained by levelling up, similar to the concept of the Battle Pass.

This was the first official season of Battle Royale, and from this point Patch Notes were introduced, which meant that the game would be getting updated from time to time. The Ceiling Zapper and Wall Dynamo were completely removed from the games, and the rarities of several items such as Medkits changed.

Season 2[]

In Season 2, the Battle Pass was introduced, along with Battle Pass Tiers, which meant that if players purchased the Battle Pass, they would earn a Cosmetic Item every time they gained a Battle Pass Tier. The maximum tier was Tier 70, where players could unlock the Black Knight Outfit.

A number of locations and items were introduced in this season, including the Chug Jug, Small Shield Potion and Tilted Towers. Seasonal themes were also introduced, the season was medieval themed, and its tagline was "Fort Knights".

Season 3[]

In Season 3, the season got a whole new, unique Battle Pass, meaning that this concept was going to continue throughout all seasons. The theme for this season mainly revolved around space, space exploration, and meteors; its tagline was "Meteor Strike".

The Fortnite Storyline also started this season, with a meteor appearing in the sky around the middle of the season, as well as lots of small meteorites crashing into the island. New items and locations were also introduced, including the Guided Missile, and Lucky Landing.

Fortnite for iOS was released during Season 3, on April 2, 2018.[2]

Season 4[]

In Season 4, the location Dusty Depot was replaced with Dusty Divot after the meteor crashed into it. Hop Rocks were scattered around this location, and a movie studio appeared near Moisty Mire. The season was superhero-themed; its slogan was "Brace for Impact".

The first official Fortnite Collaboration appeared this season, with the introduction of the Infinity Gauntlet Limited Time Mode, as well as the first ever Mythic item, the Infinity Gauntlet (exclusive to the LTM).

Season 5[]

In Season 5, the location Moisty Mire was replaced with Paradise Palms, as well as a desert biome. The subtitle for this season was "Worlds Collide", and Rifts (portals which teleport you into the sky and allow glider redeploy) were introduced.

There were a lot of updates this season, and a lot of Cosmetic Items, Items, and Weaponry were introduced (i.e. Grappler, Llama Bell, Tomato Temple). The Getaway event took place this season.

Season 6[]

In Season 6, the Fortnite Storyline was at a high point. The Cube lifted a floating island, causing Corrupted Areas to appear throughout the map. The theme for this season revolved around Halloween/Darkness/Corruption, and was called "Darkness Rises".

Fortnitemares was introduced this season. Cube Monsters started to appear all over the map, and an exclusive LTM was created. Balloons, Quadcrashers and Quad Launchers were introduced this season.

Season 7[]

Season 7 was winter- and Christmas-themed. Thus, a snowy biome was added to the southwest quadrant of the island, which included Polar Peak, Frosty Flights, and Happy Hamlet. Fortnite's storyline was progressed throughout Season 7: Polar Peak served as prison to the Prisoner and home to the Ice King. Quickly, the prison began to thaw out, allowing the Prisoner to regain their power, destroying their shackles. The Prisoner used their power to cause fissures throughout the island.

Season 7 was also the first to have a Mythic item appear in Battle Royale (i.e., outwith an LTM), the Infinity Blade; the Infinity Blade spawned in a plinth in Polar Peak.

Fortnite: Creative, a whole new gamemode, was introduced in Season 7. This gamemode allows you to create your own island, building by placing individual objects, prefabs, galleries and devices. Island owners can tweak their islands' settings to change players' health, team sizes and more.

Season 8[]

In Season 8, theming revolved around pirates and adventure. A new tropical and volcanic biome was added to the northeast section of the map, along with Sunny Steps and Lazy Lagoon.

The first live event that players decided the outcome of took place this season, the Unvaulting Event. Players got the opportunity to pick one item that should be unvaulted, and they chose the Drum Gun. This weapon was vaulted again in Season X.

The Prop-O-Matic, Flashlight Pistol and other Creative-exclusive items were introduced.

Season 9[]

In Season 9, the theme was futurism, with the tagline: "The Future is Yours". Neo Tilted, Mega Mall and Pressure Plant were all locations added to the island. Slipstreams were also added across the map, as well as large, mechanical drones., a new type of cosmetic, were added. These allowed players to carry harvesting tools in both of their hands, instead of one. Pickaxes utilising this feature have been included in every Battle Pass since.

Season X[]

This season was the final season of Chapter 1. The theme for Season X was time travel, as lots of unexpected locations began to appear scattered across the map. The B.R.U.T.E., a new robotic vehicle was introduced to the game.

Rift Zones, a new form of location, were introduced. Each rift zone was unique, for example Retail Row had Monsters, Greasy Grove was taken over by Guaco, and Neo Tilted was replaced with Tilted Town and Gotham City.

The season and the chapter ended with The End event, when the Meteor impacted The Zero Point at Loot Lake, containing Reality Zero leaving a Black Hole.

The season's tagline was "Out of Time".

Chapter 2[]

Season 1[]

Chapter 2: Season 1 brought a whole new chapter to Fortnite, as well as a new Battle Royale Island, the Apollo island, a few days after The End event took place. Additionally, the whole Battle Pass concept got a complete revamp, allowing players to progress through an infinite number of tiers.[Citation needed] Fittingly, its slogan was "New World".

The loot pool got a revamp, with a lot of weapons and items being vaulted and unvaulted, as well as the Bandage Bazooka and Motorboat being introduced. This was the longest season of Fortnite, lasting from October 2019 to February 2020, 127 days total.

Season 2[]

Chapter 2: Season 2 – Top Secret revolved around spies and secret agents, as well as the conflict between rival organizations GHOST and SHADOW (both led by Midas. Five new locations appeared, each containing a boss with a Mythic item; each being a stronger version of the Legendary variant of that item.

Henchmen were introduced. They will appear at spy bases, spawning randomly and eliminating all players. Faction Locked Chests were introduced, as well as Secret Passageways and Disguise Boxes.

Travis Scott's Astronomical also took place this season.

The season ended with the live event, "The Device", in which Midas attempted to destroy the Storm, but instead destroyed the Agency and flooded the island.

Season 3[]

During Chapter 2: Season 3, the entire map was flooded in water thanks to Midas' attempt at destroying the Storm. New Mythic items became available, but this time each boss dropped two of them. Throughout the season, the water level lowered. Furthermore, there was a small live event that players could trigger near Craggy Cliffs.

Cars and radio stations were introduced. These allowed players to traverse the map, and listen to music that is either made by Epic Games, or by real life music artists.

Season 4[]

Chapter 2: Season 4 – Nexus War was the first ever crossover season, where Fortnite crossed over with Marvel. New bosses that dropped Mythic superpowers appeared, as well as a Marvel-themed Battle Pass. Frenzy Farm was crushed by Upstate New York and Stark Industries.

Marvel-themed LTMs, car racing, and more were appeared throughout this season, until The Devourer of Worlds event occurred, and the Zero Point became exposed.

Season 5[]

Chapter 2: Season 5 – Zero Point—Join the Hunt was themed around bounty hunters, and as such included the Mandalorian as the season's highest-level Battle Pass outfit. A desert biome appeared around the Zero Point. Different Characters started to appear around different locations on the island, with some selling Exotic items.

Reality Logs, IO Guards, Zero Point Portals and more features have been appearing this season. A significant number of collaborations occurred, such as Lazarbeam, The Grefg and more.

Season 6[]

Chapter 2: Season 6 brought crafting and tamable animals into Fortnite. A primal biome appeared at the center of the map, and new items such as the Makeshift Shotgun and the Primal Stink Bow became available to obtain.

Season 7[]

Chapter 2: Season 7 – Invasion was focused on a "war" between the Kymera (an extra-terrestrial speceis) that have invaded the island, and the Imagined Order. Saucers are added and are piloted by Trespassers, who also patrol select locations. Holly Hedges has been transformed into Holly Hatchery. Three named locations were abducted by The Mothership, removing structures and pieces of land from the Island.

Season 8[]

Chapter 2: Season 8 was the final season of Chapter 2. It revolves around The Cube, which returned after 12 seasons. After Operation: Sky Fire which destroyed the Kymeran mothership, puruple cubes crashed down onto the island, spreading corruption. Two new types of Cubes appeared in this season, the Blue Rebooted Cube and the Golden Cube. The Rebooted Cube was a Purple Cube until it was rebooted by loopers inside The Mothership. The Golden Cube awakened the Purple Cubes which spawned smaller purple cubes, and lead them to the center of the Island, forming The Convergence which turned into The Pyramid. The Cube Queen emerges from the Golden Cube, preparing to spead corruption and to contain the Island.

The Cube Monsters return and is known as Cube Legion with new monsters and a larger "boss" monster known as the Caretaker.

Chapter 3[]

Season 1[]

Chapter 3: Season 1 was the first season on the new Artemis Island (The Looper teamed with The Seven to flip over Apollo), with The Cube Queen & Cube Monsters presumed defeated.

Season 2[]

Chapter 3: Season 2 developed the war between The Seven and the Imagined Order with new vehicles like a drivable Battle Bus and new features such as Mantling and Sprinting.

Season 3[]

Chapter 3: Season 3, named Vibin', followed the conclusion of Collision. Doctor Slone, has been lost and the Resistance has won the war with help from the Paradigm.

Season 4[]

Chapter 3: Season 4 has the island progressively taken over by The Herald and Chrome, transforming POIs and turning players, objects, and weapons into chrome. The Nothing is directly introduced, its will done through the chrome.

Chapter 4[]

Season 1[]

Chapter 4: Season 1 followed the Fracture live event. The Zero Point combines elements of other realities to create the Asteria Island, under which the Zero Point lies dormant. A snapshot of Geno, The Ageless, reigns over The Citadel & has The Looper construct a portal which is later destroyed by The Looper in cooperation with AMIE.

Season 2[]

Chapter 4: Season 2 introduced Mega City to the map, along with new vehicles & grind rails. With The Ageless' disappearance, factions sprung up, each seeking power. The Peace Syndicate recruited The Looper to maintain peace between the factions and investigate missing files, thought to have been corrupted by Triarch Nox.

Season 3[]

Chapter 4: Season 3 introduced new Jungle biome as a result of collapse in the middle area of the Island with new Augments, rideable Raptors, new weapons and more. Slone has returned and is investigating the ruins of The Ancients.

Season 4[]

The season begins as the sun becomes eclipsed by the moon. Vampire Kado Thorne took up residence in Asteria, draining the island's wealth and treasures to finance luxury properties: Sanguine Suites, Eclipsed Estate, and Relentless Retreat. A team consisting of Nolan Chance, Piper Pace, Fish Thicc, Mae, Antonia, and Khaby Lame attempts to infiltrate the Eclipsed Estate and steal Throne's prized treasures to take back what belongs to the island and, more importantly, foil Thorne's ultimate plan. The fate of the island is at stake, and theft is the last resort.

Season OG[]

Chapter 4: Season OG - the Athena Island from Season 5 is brought back replacing the Asteria Island, The Athena Island brings back many old POI's such as Tilted Towers, Junk Junction & Fatal Fields, and bringing back many OG Named Locations and Unnamed Locations.

Chapter 5[]

Season 1[]

Chapter 5: Season 1 - The Big Bang created multiple new galaxies with one of them containing a new world (Helios) where the inhabitants of Asteria could take refuge. Sometime later in that new world, Jones became a member of The Underground faction after learning that Peely had been kidnapped by The Society and went to war with them alongside Hope. Weapon Mods, Wall Climbing, new improved animations, newer graphics, the Train and Sewers were introduced. Three new giant modes also released: LEGO Fortnite, Fortnite Festival and Rocket Racing.

Season 2[]

Chapter 5: Season 2 - The Underworld and Mount Olympus were brought to the island upon the opening of Pandora's Box along with Greek gods (Aphrodite, Ares, Artemis, Hades, Nike, Zeus) and monsters (Cerberus & Medusa).Zeus wanted to eradicate humanity, and it was up to The Looper to stop him. SHADOW Briefings, Weapons Bunkers and the Underworld Dash mechanic were introduced, and many Greek mythology-inspired items were also added.

Season 3[]

Chapter 5: Season 3 - Zeus destroyed the gemstone on Pandora's box which caused an encroaching sandstorm that transformed the south into a barren desert, with Megalo Don and his crew of marauders as well as The Wanderer arrving to sow chaos and eventually trying to raze the whole island down. Vehicle Modification and the Nitro were introduced.

Season 4[]

Chapter 5: Season 4 - after foiling Megalo Don's ship's ultimate refuel Doom absorbed the power of Pandora's Box and used it to transform the nothern part of the island into his home country of Latveria. Many heroes from Reality: 616 united to bring the despot down, with Heroic Hope being their leader. Many items from Marvel's reality belonging to different heroes that were similar to Powers were added.

Season Remix[]

Chapter 5: Season Remix - after a small concert hosted by Snoop Dogg and Ice Spice the island Apollo returned with POIs from the first three seasons of Chapter 2 (but with a musical twist: three Named Locations that belonged to Midas' Crew were redisigned to be owned by rappers Snoop Dogg, Eminem, and Ice Spice). Most weapon from that season also made a return, with the lootpool changing weekly. Lowrider Hydraulics were intorduced and Kicks were also added as a cosmetic. Some time later, Juice WRLD arrived to the island, creating WRLD's Point and hosting a massive concert with the other three rappers that ended Chapter 5. In parallel, Jones, Hope, The Looper, and a fragment of something else traveled to a new Reality to the Island Oninoshima.

Chapter 6[]

Season 1[]

Chapter 6: Season 1 – 鬼HUNTERS started with Reality 783's Zero Point's shard unleashing a wave of magic that allowed Demon Warriors to return to the island of Oninoshima. New movement mechanics (including ledge jumping), sprites, boons and magical masks, amongst other things, were introduced.

Season 2[]

Chapter 6: Season 2 – LAWLESS began with gold unleashing from the Frozen Portal and transforming the surrounding area into golden hills. Fletcher Kane arrived to become the sole owner of it and to earn even more, with the returned Midas forming a crew to get him out of his way so he can use the The Zero Point's Shard. The Gold Rush effect, Black Markets and Dill Bits, as well as a new way to break into vaults, were introduced with the season.

Galactic Battle[]

Chapter 6: Season MS1 – Galactic Battle—After the arrive of the Death Star in the skies of Oninoshima, an all-out war erupted between the Galactic Empire and the Resistance. Star Wars mythics, blasters, vehicles and abilities were added, and ammo and all the previous season's weapons were vaulted. After the fights between the two factions, the Death Star was sabotaged by Vengeance Jones, Hope and the Looper, before it could disintegrate the island.

Season 3[]

Chapter 6: Season 3 – SUPER—Following the explosion of the Death Star, a meteor hit the Spirit Realm Portal on Predator Peak, catching the attention of Superman. Hero ranks were introduced and the Star Wars weapons vaulted.

Season 4[]

Chapter 6: Season 4 – SHOCK 'N AWESOME—During the Super Showdown live event, Superman severed one of the Dark Presence's tentacles; it landed on the island and began to decompose, attracting flies. Bugs' nests began to form around the tentacle, a precursor to the bug infestation which occurs during Season 4. The season introduces four new named locations, including O.X.R. HQ, the home of the island's bug-fighting military.
