Ace pilot, military veteran, and trusted ally of Tony Stark.

War Machine is a Marvel Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 3, then spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 5: Season 4 Battle Pass. War Machine is part of the War Machine Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • War Machine's Iron Patriot style is based on the Iron Patriot armor worn by James Rhodes during the Iron Patriot storyline.
  • War Machine's wrist-mounted plasma blasters will be switched out for wrist-mounted machine guns if War Machine's Arsenal is equipped. Featuring a unique animation of the weaponry retracting and appearing.[1]
  • The shoulder-mounted gatling gun on War Machine will disappear if equipped with certain Back Blings.
    • The shoulder-mounted missile launcher will also disappear if either War Machine's Arsenal, War Machine's Auto Turret, or War Machine's Hover Jets are equipped.
  • There was a Legacy Achievement in Chapter 2: Season 4 that referenced War Machine: War Machine. It could have been obtained by earning 6 different weapon specialist accolades in a single match.

Trivia[]

  • War Machine wears the M.O.A.B.B. as his Back Bling.
  • When aggravated, War Machine will use War Machine's Arsenal to fight. But he will notably not have the back device replace his Back Bling, and not drop it when eliminated.
