Outfit[]

“
Founder of The Elites.
”
— In-Game Description

Elite Zadie is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks. Elite Zadie was first released in Chapter 6: Season 2 and is part of the The Elites Set.

Elite Zadie could have also been obtained for free by ranking high enough in the Elite Zadie Reload Cup or the Elite Zadie Ranked Cup.

Behavior

Behavior[]

In either of her spawn locations, Zadie will wander around the area. She will not attack the player on sight unless she is attacked, they aim their weapon at her for too long, or they get too close to her to slowly build up suspicion.
