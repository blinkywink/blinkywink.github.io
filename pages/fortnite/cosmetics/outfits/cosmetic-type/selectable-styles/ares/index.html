Phases

Phases[]

Ares is not initially patrolling Brawler's Battleground. Instead, he can be challenged by breaking his effigy thus triggering the fight.

Effigy of Ares
Icon

The encounter is split into three phases:

Phase 1: ELIMINATE THE MINIONS![]

Three Minions are summoned within the battle arena, all of them have to be defeated to progress to the next phase.

Phase 2: ELIMINATE THE MINIONS![]

Three more Minions are summoned with an additional one appearing when one is eliminated as only three Minions can be present at any time. All four of them have to be defeated to progress to the next phase.

Phase 3: DEFEAT ARES[]

A Rift cracks in the sky as Ares crashes down in flames at the ruined altar and attacks the player. Ares alternates between all of his weapons as the fight progresses, adjusting his fighting style based on which weapon is currently being held.

During this phase, Ares has the following abilities:

  • Explosive Landing
    • Ares crashes down onto the battlefield, dealing 10 damage in a small radius. This ability is only used when Ares enters the arena.
Explosive Landing
  • Summoning Minions
    • After landing into battle, Ares passively summons up to two Minions to aid him until he is defeated.

Trivia[]

  • The Icon of Ares' Elimination Accolade uses an older design of his helmet not present on his current model.

Ares is the God of War and the son of Zeus and Hera. He is one of the Gods of The Pantheon, and one of Zeus' closest allies.

He is a supporting antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a supporting antagonist in Chapter 5: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

Ares is constantly enraged and yelling, also known as the most brutal of the Gods. He is arrogant in his abilities and strength, believing himself undefeatable[6] until The Looper humiliated him in combat and stole his Medallion.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

God of War
Immortality
Due to being a God, Ares possesses immortality.
Pyrokinesis
Ares possesses the ability to manipulate red fire.
Weapon Enhancement
His Aspect of Combat allows him to have increased damage for ranged weapons.[7] It was taken by The Looper once Ares was defeated in combat.
Rift Opening
Ares seems to have enough strength to break the fabric of reality as seen in his boss fight's entrance.
Summoning
Ares summons his Minions to aid him in battle.
Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

A carving of Ares can be found on Pandora's Box.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

After Pandora's Box was opened, Ares came along with the other Olympian Gods to punish The Society for opening it and releasing the souls of The Underworld. While Ares did not engage with the Society, he opened the door for all mortal warriors to challenge him at Brawler's Battleground. After The Looper manages to defeat Cerberus, it attracts Ares' attention[8] and he challenges The Looper to a duel[9] which he ends up losing, as well as loosing his medallion to them. He reports back to his father, Zeus, about the Looper's strength and victory.[10] Thanks to Ares, Zeus now knows of the Looper's plans and would later ask Cerberus to look into them.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

Ares, humbled after failing to retain the order of Zeus' Pantheon, fled the Island to the infinite corners of reality with most of the other gods to find a new domain to reign over after Zeus's fall.
