Super Level Styles

Overview

Super Level Styles, also known as Enlightened Styles, are progressive styles that unlock past Battle Pass Level 100. They were first introduced in Chapter 2: Season 1, with 8-Ball vs Scratch's styles.

Chapter 2

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

Chapter 2: Season 1 introduced the first form of Super Level Styles, with 8-Ball vs Scratch's corrupted Scratch style, which progressed as you leveled up past Level 100. Starting from Level 115, the outfit slowly became more corrupted until reaching Level 350, where the corruption did not progress further.

8-Ball vs Scratch's Back Bling, Ball Bling also recived the Corrupted style.

Scratch Ball Bling

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

Chapter 2: Season 2 introduced Super Level Styles for each Battle Pass Outfit and their respective backblings except Deadpool, called Golden Agent styles. Every outfit would slowly become more gold until unlocking the next Golden Agent style, starting with Midas, then Brutus, Meowscles, TNTina, Skye, and finally Agent Peely. To fully complete all Golden Agent styles, players needed to reach level 350.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

In Chapter 2: Season 3, Eternal Knight received a Super Level Style, which progressed like previous styles. This style is simply a toggle. No other outfits in the Battle Pass had Super Level Styles in the season, but Eternal Shield did. Eternal Knight's Super Level style wasn't activated until Update v15.30.

Eternal Knight Eternal Shield

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

Foil Styles were Chapter 2: Season 4's Super Level Styles. Each Battle Pass outfit had them, and they unlocked every 5 levels. It would start with Silver Foil styles for every outfit, followed by Gold Foil, then Holo Foil. This was the first time that multiple Super Styles were given to a single skin. Oddly, Thor's Cloak also received Foil Styles along with Thor.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Crystal Styles were Chapter 2: Season 5's Super Level Styles. Reese, Mancake, Mave, Kondor and Menace had them, and they unlocked every 10 levels. It would begin with Sapphire styles for every outfit, followed by Topaz, then Zero Point styles.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Relic Styles are Chapter 2: Season 6's Super Level Styles. Tarana, Raz and Spire Assassin has them, and they unlock every 20 levels up until the Golden Styles, when each of those are unlocked every 10 levels. It would begin with Chromium styles for every outfit, followed by Runic, then Golden styles.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Rick Sanchez and Guggimon are the only collaboration Outfits to get Super Level Styles that aren't Marvel Series or Star Wars Series.
    • Spider-Man and Spider-Gwen are the only Marvel Series Outfits to get Super Level Styles outside of a Marvel-themed season.
  • 1-Ball, Undercover Skye, and Meowdas are the first Outfits that are remixes of Outfits with Super Level Styles to get Super Level Styles.
  • Depite the fact that the Enlightened tag was created for Super Level Styles, none of the Chapter 2: Season 4 cosmetics have the tag in-game, and neither do any cosmetics from Chapter 2: Season 7 onwards.
