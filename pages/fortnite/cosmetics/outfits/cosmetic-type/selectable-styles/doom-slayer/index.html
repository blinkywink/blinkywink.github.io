Doom Slayer is a Gaming Legends Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 5, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 1 Battle Pass. Doom Slayer is part of the Ultra-Nightmare Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Doom Slayer's description is a reference to the song of the same name from DOOM Eternal (2020).
  • Doom Slayer's design is based on his appearance in DOOM Eternal (2020), with his edit styles being taken from the various cosmetic armors from the same game.
    • The Ember Armor is incorrectly depicted without its flames.
    • The Phobos Armor is, by extent, a reference to Phobos from id Software's Quake series, who is also responsible for the DOOM series.
  • Doom Slayer was the first Gaming Legends Series Outfit to be released in Chapter 4.
    • He is also the second Gaming Legends Series Outfit to be featured in the Battle Pass, preceded by Lara Croft.
  • The Doom Slayer was teased in the Fracture event, as he appears floating in space alongside an attacking Cacodemon.
