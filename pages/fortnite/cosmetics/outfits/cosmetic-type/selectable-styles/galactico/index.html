Galactico is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks. Galactico was first released in Chapter 2: Season 5 and is part of the Kickoff Set.

Galactico could have also been obtained for free by ranking high enough in The Pelé Cup on January 20th, 2021.
