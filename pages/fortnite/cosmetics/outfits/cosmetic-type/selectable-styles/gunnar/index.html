Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Gunnar's codename is IO-Brute.
    • His Boss Outfit ID before Chapter 3: Season 2 was CID_TBD_Athena_Commando_M_OrderGuard.
  • Gunnar is the first Boss that could use Ziplines and slide.

Gunnar is the Imagined Order's highest-ranking enforcer and the right hand man of Doctor Slone.

He is a supporting antagonist in Fortnite, serving as a supporting antagonist in Chapter 2: Season 8, Chapter 3: Season 1 and Chapter 3: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

Little is known about Gunnar. He rarely speaks,[6] but he is said to be terrifying. Unlike most IO Field Agents, Gunnar relies heavily on brute force. He has a particular fondness for chainsaws and boasts a surprisingly large collection of them.[7] He is deeply obedient to Doctor Slone and unwaveringly loyal to the IO.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Gunnar is a man with blonde hair with shaved sides and brown beard. He has a very muscular physique and wears a battlesuit similar to the IO Patrols with additional platings on his shoulders and gauntlets.

Timeline

Timeline[]

The End[]

Gunnar was in Agent Jones' Cell in The Bridge when Doctor Slone arrived and told him to wake Jones up. He used the command panel and shocked him, then tortured him, following Slone's orders. The Foundation busted in and eliminated the IO Guard next to him, then smashed Gunnar’s face into the command panel.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Gunnar arrives at Artemis with his IO Guards and guards Covert Cavern; the IO's new HQ.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

Gunnar led the frontal assault on Artemis in the cinematic trailer and was seen going hand to hand with The Foundation once again. This time however, he is much more prepared to face him with similar arm powering capabilities of The Foundation.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

Gunnar and Doctor Slone guard an IO Outpost against The Resistance, blowing it up purposely to cut their losses.[11] They later ambush Agent Jones, The Foundation, The Imagined and Spider-Man at The Bridge, but they manage to escape into Reality: 616 using The Zero Point.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #5[]

He defends the surface against The Seven alongside IO's as The Order flies by him, destroying a lot of IO forces. Gunnar is called by Slone, telling him to find Doctor Doom.[12] Gunnar drops down and finds Doom, shouting at him to get to the battle and help the IO with The Ice King's weaponry.[13] Doom betrays the IO and blasts Gunnar, either killing or fainting him.
