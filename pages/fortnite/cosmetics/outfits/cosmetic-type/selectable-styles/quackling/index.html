Outfit[]

“
Quack in action.
”
— In-Game Description

Quackling is an Uncommon Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks or with DuckyTheGamer's Locker Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Quackling was first released in Chapter 2: Season 2 and is part of the Quackle Team Set.

Quackling could also have been obtained by ranking high enough in the DuckyTheGamer Mobile Cup on March 21st 2026.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Quackling's appearances?
Quackling has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
