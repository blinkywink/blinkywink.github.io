Outfit[]

“
Here comes the bride.
”
— In-Game Description

The Bride is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Chapter 7: Season 1 Battle Pass. The Bride is part of the The Bride's Revenge Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Bride uses the likeness of Uma Thurman, who portrayed the character in the Kill Bill duology.
  • The Bride was planned to be a Hunter in Chapter 2: Season 5.[1]
  • The Bride is the first Outfit to have drawn eyebrows and more realistic skin detail compared to other Outfits.
