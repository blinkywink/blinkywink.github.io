Sheev Palpatine, also known as his Sith alias Darth Sidious, is a Force-sensitive Sith Lord from Reality: UN-16 and Reality: UN-119. Having served as a Senator from Naboo and as Supreme Chancellor of the Galactic Republic, his mastery in politics contributed to his rise to power as Emperor of the Galactic Empire.

The Reality: UN-119 version of Palpatine was a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main antagonist of Galactic Battle.

Personality

Personality[]

Palpatine is a master manipulator whose personality is defined by a blend of cold calculation, ruthless ambition, and deceptive charm. On the surface, he presents himself as a calm, friendly, and charismatic leader, expertly using his apparent benevolence to gain the trust of others. However, beneath this mask lies a far more sinister nature. His thirst for power drives him to do whatever it takes to maintain control over the galaxy, using fear, manipulation, and deception to crush any opposition. He is not only willing to sacrifice others for his own gain, but he also delights in the suffering and fear that his power inflicts on those around him.

What truly sets Palpatine apart is his patience and long-term strategic thinking. Unlike many of his contemporaries, he is willing to bide his time, allowing others to play into his plans while he remains in the background. This long game enables him to maintain his grip on the galaxy with subtlety and precision, often pulling strings from the shadows. His cold and detached demeanor reflects his lack of empathy, seeing people as mere tools to be used and discarded at his will. His manipulation of Anakin Skywalker, for example, demonstrates just how far he is willing to go to shape the galaxy in his image. Above all, Palpatine is driven by an insatiable hunger for power, reveling in the control he exerts and the chaos he creates in his wake.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Palpatine’s appearance is a direct reflection of his loyalty to the dark side. His face is gaunt and pale, almost ghostly in its whiteness, marked by deep wrinkles and a twisted expression that suggests both age and corruption. His features appear permanently contorted in a sneer, and his sunken, yellow eyes further emphasize the toll that the dark side has taken on him. His physical form appears frail and fraught with decay, as if the very power that sustains him is slowly consuming his body.

Palpatine’s robes are dark and imposing, often in shades of black or deep purple, contributing to his mysterious and foreboding presence. The heavy, flowing cloaks he wears are designed to conceal much of his form, adding to his air of secrecy and power. His hood is frequently drawn over his head, casting shadows over his face, adding to his menacing aura. His posture, too, is hunched and frail, further highlighting his physical vulnerability. Yet, despite his weakened state, there is an undeniable sense of immense power that radiates from him, reinforced by his commanding presence and calculated movements.

Perhaps the most unsettling aspect of Palpatine's appearance is his eyes—yellow and glowing with the unmistakable mark of the dark side. These eyes serve as a visible reminder of his corruption, reflecting the malevolent force that has consumed him. His slow, deliberate movements and the rasping, sinister laughter that often accompany his speeches only deepen the sense of terror he inspires. This combination of frailty, darkness, and menace makes Palpatine both a terrifying and commanding figure in the galaxy.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

Live at Risky Reels[]

Emperor Palpatine is heard in Reality Zero as he delivers a threatening message to the galaxy, declaring that it is the day of the Sith.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

Fall of the Republic Quests[]

Palpatine orders the 501st troopers to "execute Order 66" against the Loopers.

Galactic Battle[]

An alternate version of Emperor Palpatine from Reality: UN-119 came to Oninoshima with the rest of the Galactic Empire. Palpatine was interested in the "junk" scattered across Oninoshima. While he oversaw preparations for the Death Star to annihilate the island, he in the meantime appeared in hologram form in various locations around the island to recruit Oninoshima's residents to his cause through his Rift Gate.

Death Star Sabotage[]

Palpatine confronted Jones and the Loopers, deflecting their blaster fire with his lightsaber before using the Force to take their weapons away. He then wiped out most of the group by throwing TIE Fighters at them, as well as choking and electrocuting them with the Force. Hope arrived with a Rift-To-Go and threw it at Palpatine's Force Lightning, saving Jones and the surviving Loopers by transporting them to the sky above Oninoshima. The Reality: UN-119 version of Emperor Palpatine met his fate when the Death Star exploded.
