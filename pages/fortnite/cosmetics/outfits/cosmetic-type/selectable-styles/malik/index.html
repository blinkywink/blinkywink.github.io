Overview

“
His destiny has been foretold for a thousand generations.
”
— In-Game Description

Malik is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 6, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 3 Battle Pass. Malik is part of the Age of Kings Set.

  • Auric Blaze Malik - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Malik appeared in the cinematic trailer for Chapter 3 Season 3 as he was heading towards Rave Cave with Evie.
  • Since Chapter 4 Season 2, Malik was used for testing special dialogue for characters. Since the test dialogues were not removed, they were able to be seen in-game when using his outfit when talking to certain characters.
  • Malik has a female counterpart named Jaina.
