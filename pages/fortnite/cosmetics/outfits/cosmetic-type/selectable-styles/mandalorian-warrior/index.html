Outfit[]

“
Become Mando'ade.
”
— In-Game Description

Mandalorian Warrior is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with Mandalorian Warrior for 1,800 V-Bucks or with the Champions of Mandalore Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks. Mandalorian Warrior was first released in Chapter 6: Mini Season 1 and is part of the Champions of Mandalore Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Mandalorian Warrior's appearances?
Mandalorian Warrior has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Mandalorian Specialists are the first instance of one Outfit being used for multiple different Characters.
    • The Imperial Stormtrooper and Clone Trooper Characters don't count in this regard as they still only took up one entry in the Character Collection Book, while the Mandalorian Specialists take up four separate entries.
  • The Heavy and Supply Mandalorians wear the Mandalorian Cape as their Back Bling.
  • Unlike other Heavy Specialist Characters, the Heavy Mandalorian will throw Thermal Imploders at his targets, though he will still drop two live Grenades upon being eliminated.
  • Because Ammunition isn't available in Galactic Battle, the Supply Mandalorian will often drop Cabbages if not Thermal Imploders.
  • All the Mandalorian Specialists have distinct color and pattern combos that aren't represented in their Character Collection Book entries.
  • "Su cuy'gar" is Mando'a for "Hello".
