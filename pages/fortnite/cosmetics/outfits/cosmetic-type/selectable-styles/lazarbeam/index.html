LazarBeam is an Icon Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased from the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Lazarbeam Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. LazarBeam was first released in Chapter 2: Season 5 and is part of the Lazarbeam Set.

LazarBeam could also have been obtained for free by ranking high enough in the Lazar & Fresh's Super Knockback on March 3rd 2021.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Lazarbeam's appearances?
Lazarbeam has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • LazarBeam's attire is a tribute to his former job as a construction worker on his family business prior to his YouTube career, as confirmed by him in a video.
  • LazarBeam's arm tattoo "Code Lazar" is a reference to his Support-A-Creator code, which he tattooed on himself.
  • LazarBeam's description references the Australian drinking song, which he likes singing while drinking a Chug Jug.
  • LazarBeam became a host on the Party Royale Radio Station on February 27th 2021.
  • On March 4th 2021, LazarBeam started teasing his set on @Fortnite's official Twitter Account by tweeting things like "hi mum im in fortnite" and "poggers".
  • LazarBeam is the third Australian to have an Outfit in-game.
  • According to LazarBeam on his twitter account, he chose the pose for both of his LEGO Style’s icons to make it show his “code lazar” tattoo.
    • In this same post, he accidentally revealed that Icons get to choose the pose for their LEGO Style, if they or LEGO choose to have one.
