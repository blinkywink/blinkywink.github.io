Chaos Agent, formerly known as Chaos Origins is a mad scientist, a secret agent and "The First Redeemed" and founding member of The First Shadows as well as the leader of SHADOW.

He is a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 1 and the unseen secondary antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 2.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Unknown Time Period[]

Chaos Agent, at the time known as Chaos Origins, attempts to attack Midas. Midas, seeing his potential, recruits him as the second member of The First Shadows. Him, The Burning Wolf and Sierra were the first members of SHADOW and Midas' first operatives.

At some point after this, Chaos Director, the director of SHADOW, delegated her authority to Chaos Agent for unknown reasons, leaving him as SHADOW's primary leader after Midas.[1]

Season X[]

He was on a movie poster in Gotham City named "The Final Reckoning", alongside Teef, Catrina, Willow and Blacklight.

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

As the leader of A.L.T.E.R, a small division of SHADOW agents, he lead the war against E.G.O. and created an army of Sludges out of Slurp to act as soldiers. He then sent them to hunt down Rippley, a rogue Sludge created in Slurpy Swamp. He watched the entire battle from his lair. He seems to have done an experiment on Scratch, corrupting his body.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

While Chaos Agent isn’t physically seen during this season, it’s assumed he continued to lead SHADOW during the infighting of Midas' Crew. He would later create Slurp-Human hybrids known as the Slurp Legends. The experiments go mad and get obsessed over Slurp, seeing it as a deity.[2]

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

He is not seen again until the release of the Double Agent Pack where his body is now golden. He infiltrated GHOST alongside Wildcard and Hush as double agents for SHADOW.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

As SHADOW and GHOST went dark, limited only to offering their mercenary services and acting in the shadows, Chaos Agent went into hiding.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

After the Cosmic Summer ends, Midas gives control of his Crew to Brutus. Chaos Agent continues to lead SHADOW.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

He and several other SHADOW agents were recruited as mercenaries to increase the ranks of the Imagined Order. In the Story Trailer, he attacks and gets eliminated by Kiara K.O.. After the end of the conflict with The Seven, he disappeared again without a trace.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

Chaos Agent reappears in the form of Chaos Double Agent as a member of the Cold Blooded syndicate; he protects the vault within Shattered Slabs. However after the vaults of the Cold Blooded Syndicate were robbed by the Most Wanted, he disappeared and was later replaced by Highcard. It's hinted that he was not loyal to Cold Blooded and was acting as a double agent for another organization.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

As Chaos Explorer he seemed to be searching for something in the deepest depths. He gave a mission to Stingray and Purradise Meowscles, two SHADOW agents, before going back into hiding.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Midas escapes from The Underworld and Brutus gives control of the Crew back to him. Chaos Agent remains in hiding.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

Chaos Director returns and takes back her authority from Chaos Agent, believing it was a mistake to grant her power to him in the first place.[3]

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The SHADOW logo resembles Chaos Agent's mask, which is strange considering that Midas had made the logo prior to meeting him. Although the resemblance could also just be a coincidence.
  • Big Chuggus, Sludges, Corrupted Scratch, Slurp Legends and Corrupted Legends are the results of his sinister experiments.
  • Another character named "Double Agent Rogue" should have been a Cold Blooded boss instead of Chaos Double Agent, however it was scrapped and replaced.
    • The name of the scrapped boss suggests that all of the Cold Blooded Bosses from Chapter 4: Season 1 except Cold Blooded Ace, must have actually been undercover agents for another organization.
