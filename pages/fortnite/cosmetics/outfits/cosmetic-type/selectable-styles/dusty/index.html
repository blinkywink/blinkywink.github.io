Overview

This article is about the Outfit. For the Loading Screen of the same name, see Dusty (Loading Screen).

“
Two-time Bruteball MVP (Most Vicious Player).
”
— In-Game Description

Dusty is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 7, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 1 Battle Pass. Dusty is part of the Night Game Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Boneyard Dusty - Page 8 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)
  • Away Game Dusty - Earn 10 Account Levels (Midseason Drops Quests)

  • Aeon - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
  • Halcyon - Page 6 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
