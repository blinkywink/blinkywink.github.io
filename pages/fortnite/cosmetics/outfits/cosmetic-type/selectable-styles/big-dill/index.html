Dillinger, known by his stage name Big Dill, is a criminal and freestyle rapper. Together with The Brat, they form the hip hop duo Tasty Bois, who released the single Runamok in 2025.

He is a major character in Chapter 6: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

Big Dill's ambitions generally lie in fame and wealth, stealing from others for his own personal benefit. As a criminal, he is known to rob large banks and criminal organizations.

Big Dill carries this fame-and-wealth mentality to his music career, but is a lot more aggressive, as evident by his beef with American rapper T-Pain. While the two were meant to collaborate together, Big Dill poisoned T-Pain with pickle juice and stole his laptop, using the saved beats to write a series of diss tracks aimed towards him.

Big Dill has also been known to resort to irrational violence. This was shown when he assaulted T-Pain after he noticed he was eating pickles in front of him, leading to T-Pain's hospitalization and charges being filed.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Big Dill is a giant pickle wearing two giant gold chains, one of which features his logo. As a criminal, he also dons a balaclava or occasionally a regular black mask.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 6: Season 2[]

After his birth at Kappa Kappa Factory, the strange pickle named Dillinger became a criminal and, becoming interested in music, became a rapper, forming a duo with The Brat. Together they released a song, Runamok, which was a hit.

Big Dill—T-Pain Feud[]

According to T-Pain, Big Dill was invited by him to collaborate on a song together. However, Big Dill poisoned him with pickle juice and stole his laptop. Using the saved beats, he alongside The Brat released a series of approximately 15 diss tracks targeted at T-Pain and openly bragged about the theft of the laptop on Instagram.[1]

The beef escalated when T-Pain started eating pickles in front of Big Dill (though according to him this was where the beef began). Taking a large offense to this, going as far as to call the pickles he was eating his "family", Big Dill assaulted him. T-Pain was later hospitalized and charges were filed.[2]

On February 20th 2025, Big Dill publicly released the diss track "Salty" aimed specifically at T-Pain.[3] T-Pain would respond on February 26th 2025 with the diss track "Pickle Juice".[4]

Pump & Dump[]

The Tasty Bois indirectly confronted Fletcher Kane, robbing his banks and working with Midas. Dillinger, now known as Big Dill, decided to create a cryptocurrency, in reality a massive scam that would line his pockets while also losing most of Fletcher Kane's fortune.

However following the release, Big Dill faced allegations saying he was purposely trying to do a run pull as the profit for Dill Bits has tanked. But, he ignored the complaints and with Midas, he set the plan in motion, before waiting for the right moment to carry out his pump and dump.

After Daigo's disappearance, the duo infiltrated The Thousands Face to steal anything of value, however they found a replica of Daigo's Kintsugi mask, attempting to wear it Big Dill had a vision of Daigo's corruption, before he appeared behind the duo.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

The Tasty Bois came into conflict with DJ Yonder and DJ Bop, while The Brat was loyal to his friend, Big Dill planned to abandon him and resume his solo career, using the funds he obtained from his scam to finance a secret album called Pump & Dump. At the same time, Big Dill is wanted by the authorities following his scam.

Unable to tell his "friend" he broke the Bois by himself, he recruited The Looper to tell him before asking them to hand deliver DJ Yonder and DJ Bop provocative invitations to Big Dill's concert. At the same time, Big Dill provoked DJ Yonder by recruiting someone to hack his phone to steal his beats, enraging the latter.

Yonder therefore called on the services of The Looper and asked to send a clear message to Big Dill by attacking his "family" at Kappa Kappa Factory. The Looper complied, before checking if Big Dill had understood the message, the latter being genuinely worried about his fate and wanting to make things right, realizing that Yonder was indeed furious.
