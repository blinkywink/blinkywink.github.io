Outfit[]

“
The galaxy's greatest monster hunter has arrived.
”
— In-Game Description

Spacefarer Ariana Grande is an Icon Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks or with the Spacefarer Ariana Grande Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks. Spacefarer Ariana Grande was first released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and is part of the Spacefarer Ariana Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Spacefarer Ariana Grande's appearances?
Spacefarer Ariana Grande has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia[]

  • There are numerous references to Ariana Grande's songs in both the Quest and Interaction dialogue, including 7 Rings (2000+ Bars), Nasty (first quest), Positions (second quest), Off The Table (third quest), Motive (fourth quest), and POV (fifth quest).
