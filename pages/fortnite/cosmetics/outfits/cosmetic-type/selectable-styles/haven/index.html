This article is about the Outfit. For the LEGO Character of the same name, see LEGO Fortnite:Haven.

Outfit[]

“
Raised by the Island. Born to defend it.
”
— In-Game Description

Haven is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 5, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 1 Battle Pass. Haven is part of the Kindred Spirit Set.
