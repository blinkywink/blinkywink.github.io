Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Deadpool and his cosmetics replaced what would usually be the Mystery Rewards for the Battle Pass.
    • In the Purchase Menu, he said "Shhh. I'm the secret Battle Pass Outfit!".
  • Deadpool's overall design is heavily inspired by his appearances in the Deadpool film trilogy.
  • Due to his presence in the Fortnite comic variants like other Marvel characters released in Chapter 2: Season 4, Deadpool was likely in development alongside them.
    • His codename also follows the same pattern as most of them (food).
  • Deadpool was the first Secret Battle Pass Outfit to be a Collaboration, and also the first Collaboration Outfit to be in the Battle Pass in general.
  • Deadpool appears for the April 3rd 2020 Item Shop reset, where he shows up to push out the April 2nd, 2020 Item Shop contents, unveil the special background, and pull in the new Item Shop contents.
  • Deadpool's X-Force icon contains an error in which it appears to be unevenly layered on top of the Default icon.
  • Deadpool was the first Marvel Series Outfit of Chapter 2.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Deadpool would attack the player if he noticed them undisguised. Doing small visual reactions for getting suspicious and spotting a target.

When knocked down, he can be shaken down to reveal nearby chests, his henchmen, Sentry Turrets and Sentry Cameras.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Deadpool was the first Collaboration Boss in Battle Royale.
    • He was also the first Marvel Series Boss.
  • Due to the music playing in The Yacht its normally much harder to hear the Combat music unless the music source was destroyed.
  • Despite his reputation and Outfit's description, Deadpool was the first Boss to not have any voicelines.
