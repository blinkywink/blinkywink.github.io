“

Kitbash is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks. Kitbash was first released in Season 7 and is part of the Boneyard Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Kitbash's appearances?
Kitbash has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kitbash is named after "kitbashing", the act of "making a new scale model by taking pieces from other kits".
  • Kitbash's icon was changed in Update v10.10.
  • Kitbash's model was reused for Kit's Henchmen during Chapter 2: Season 3. Their appearance is currently unobtainable.
  • In Update v16.00, Kitbash received a new style, Catbash.

SHADOW Henchman are hostile Guards in Fortnite. They were introduced during Chapter 2 Remix.

Description

Description[]

SHADOW Henchman can be found in Catty Corner and will shoot at the player if they notice them undisguised.

When knocked down, they can be shaken down to reveal nearby Chests, Guards, Sentry Cameras, and Kit if he were still neutral.

A player can disguise as them using various Phone Booths around Catty Corner.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Unlike the Kit's Henchmen from Chapter 2: Season 3, Chapter 2 Remix's version are called SHADOW Henchmen, use the original Kitbash Outfit instead of the Guard exclusive reskin and are affiliated with SHADOW.
  • SHADOW Henchmen reprise Kit's Henchmen's old Guard AI's voice from Chapter 2: Season 3. But with many new voicelines added with or replacing old ones, a new Full Alert Idle set, and simplified Idle voicelines.
