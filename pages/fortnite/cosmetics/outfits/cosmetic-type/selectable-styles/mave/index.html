Outfit[]

“
Wildheart defender of the Tempest Jungle.
”

Mave is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Tier 41 of the Chapter 2: Season 5 Battle Pass. Mave is part of the The Wildheart Set.
