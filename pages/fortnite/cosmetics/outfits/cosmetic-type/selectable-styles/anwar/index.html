Outfit[]

“
Burn with ancient knowledge.
”
— In-Game Description

Anwar is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks or with the Radiant Moon Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Anwar was first released in Chapter 5: Season 2 and is part of the Radiant Moon Set.

Anwar could also have been obtained for free by ranking high enough in the Lantern Fest Cup on March 8th 2025.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Anwar's appearances?
Anwar has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Anwar has a female counterpart named Noorah.
  • The texture file names for Anwar and Noorah show that their earliest working names were "Ameer" and "Layla", respectively. These names were eventually changed to "Prince Anwar" and "Princess Noorah" before being finalized as simply "Anwar" and "Noorah".
