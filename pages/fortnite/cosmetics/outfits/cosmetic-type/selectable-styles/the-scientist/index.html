Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Scientist was the first male Outfit in Battle Royale to use the Large male character model. With the default up to that point being Medium.
  • The Scientist was unlockable for free without owning the Battle Pass on his release. This glitch was patched the day after. The Outfit was not revoked from those who obtained him through said glitch.
  • The Scientist was originally going to be an Item Shop Outfit.[1]
  • Unlocking The Scientist's White style would've originally involved jumping in the Zero Point as him through a hidden quest called "UseZeroRift_WithSkin".[2]
  • The Scientist was originally called Visitor Volta and his description lacked "One of The Seven" prior to a hotfix on September 14th 2019.[3][4]
    • The word "volta" can mean "to turn" in Italian and "to return" in Portuguese. This implies that The Visitor and The Scientist would've been the same character.
      • An image tweeted by Fortnite's account on August 23rd 2019 (before The Scientist, then Visitor Volta, was added in Update v10.30 on September 11th 2019) shows The Visitor dismantling a B.R.U.T.E. at Dusty Depot, and four days later in Update v10.20, parts of it were added to said location.[5]
      • Gym equipments (including weights) were added there in Content Update v10.10, which could have been a teaser for The Visitor getting his Visitor Volta physique.
  • Unlike other members of The Seven, The Scientist, The Paradigm and The Visitor are the only members who don't have "One of The Seven" in their description.
    • It was removed from The Scientist's sometime during Chapter 2: Season 1.

The Scientist was the engineer of The Seven, who is a self proclaimed "brilliant engineer, eccentric and sarcastic mastermind". He also refers to himself as the "handsomest member of The Seven". He is the creator and "father" of AMIE.

He was a major character of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as one of the supporting, yet pivotal protagonists of Chapter 1, the tritagonist of Chapter 2, and the deuteragonist of Chapter 3.

Personality

Personality[]

The Scientist is extremely smart yet sarcastic, as he acknowledges in his Character Collection Book entry. He is lightly arrogant and sees himself as the handsomest one of The Seven and a brilliant engineer.

Appearance

Appearance[]

The Scientist has a much more bulky and darker colored armor compared to the rest of The Seven. It also has a white variant, which changes the dark blue to a white and lighter blue.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Season X[]

He arrives at Athena by meteor, frozen in time above Dusty Depot. He creates several Rift Beacons around the map one at a time, swapping areas for alternate reality counterparts. He eventually started work on a rocket, which he would eventually use to contain the Zero Point and save The Bridge during The End live event.

The End[]

The Scientist arrives at Apollo with The Visitor during the attack on The Last Reality, The Visitor covers him as he opens the Guava Fort Bunker to evacuate the Loopers, once done the Scientist tells to The Visitor to go on the "other side". He later accompanies the Loopers inside The Bridge to join The Foundation and Jones, however after joining them they are attacked by a Caretaker which caused the room to flood. The Scientist then made his way to Artemis.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

The Scientist arrives on the Island as a Character, and also has missions for The Looper to complete. In these quests, he employs the help of The Looper for various tasks such as spying on the colony of Jonesy snapshots and destroying signal jammers put there by someone working against the Seven. Audio logs made by the Scientist can be found detailing the Seven's relationship with The Paradigm. Throughout the weeks, The Looper listens to the Scientist's messages to the Paradigm, and the process of getting her to return to the Island.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

As the war with the Imagined Order breaks loose, The Scientist begins upgrading all the Battle Buses he can get a hold of, currently working on one in Synapse Station. He is secretly working with The Imagined and The Paradigm, the later of whom he and The Origin have had a disagreement regarding.

Fortnite X Marvel Zero War #1-4[]

The Scientist and the rest of The Seven attack an IO Outpost to access The Bridge. In another attack, The Resistance steal one of the IO's suttles and split into two with The Scientist, The Foundation, Storm and Thor going to The Ice Moon. They find the Mecha Team Leader and The Paradigm inside. They repair the Mech and return to the Island for the final battle.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: #5[]

The Seven regroup near Mighty Monument to plan an attack on The Collider. Triggerfish gives The Scientist the Collider's plans and they go to destroy the Collider's shield generators. As the Mecha is about to destroy The Collider, seismic charges create a giant sinkhole that the Mecha falls into. The Foundation, Agent Jones and the Mecha Strike Team destroy the Crystals and blow up The Collider.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

Scientist and The Looper work to discover the Reality Tree and do various experiments on it such as sending Bushranger in a seed next to the tree. They soon discover that The Zero Point was pulling powerful artifact from other Realities as well as trying to tell them something. Scientist creates an AI assistant named AMIE who has a weird attraction to her creator to help him and The Looper. While the duo was searching for relics, The Scientist tried to understand the Zero Point, as with each recovered relics, the Zero Point was doing energy shockwaves, each time more intense. At the same time, Foundation was chromed unbeknownst to everyone. The Scientist finaly discovered what the Zero Point was trying to tell, but was commissioned to study Chrome along with Origin and Visitor.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

The Chrome substance in The Scientist's lab breaks out and spreads on The Seven. While on her way escaping in another reality, Paradigm contacted The Looper, asking them to recover every notes from The Scientist, who discovered that the Zero Point was sending galactic distress signal and that if it don't get help it'll go dormant. After that, The Looper find The Scientist's audio log for Paradigm, the last one standing of The Seven, telling that he is sorry to left Paradigm alone again and that he is being taken somewhere he doesn't know and that he deserves whatever happens to him.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

The test Rift created by AMIE and The Looper locks onto The Scientist while he is suffering a fate worse than death, by having his existence forcefully rewritten by The Nothing.[1]

Chapter 4: Season OG[]

His alternate version arrived at Athena by meteor, frozen in time above Dusty Divot.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

In order to confront the Dark Voyager, The Order accessed an emergency database left by The Scientist for use in case of a crisis. There, they retrieved the blueprints for a device that would allow them to contact any remaining Seven members across the Omniverse.

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

During the status report concerning The Scientist, AMIE sadly revealed to The Seven that he was still lost in the Chrome, admitting she had failed to find him in time. She promised to make him proud, however.
