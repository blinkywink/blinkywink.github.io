Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Raz's Glyph Master style was briefly used during the Zero Crisis Finale. Where The Looper was turned into him upon being hit by a Reality Wave.
    • The Glyph Master Raz used is slightly different from the obtainable version. Having blue glowing black hair and no wraps covering his mouth.
  • As of Update v16.30, there are now two Raz AIs on the island. One being the Character that spawns at Colossal Crops and one being the Boss that spawns at The Spire. Making Raz the first Character to have two AIs present at the same time.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Glyph Master Raz's codename is "TinyBalloon".
  • Prior to being nerfed in a hotfix, Glyph Master Raz had the highest amount of health out of all the Bosses at the time. Totaling at 3000, by 1500 HP and 1500 Shield.
  • Glyph Master Raz was the first Boss to outright replace another Boss without moving them somewhere else. That Boss being the Spire Assassin.
  • If Glyph Master Raz is shockwaved away from The Spire, he will not use any of his abilities.
  • There’s no way to move Glyph Master Raz while he’s using his Crystal Health Siphon ability, even with a Harpoon Gun or a Shockwave Grenade.
    • It was possible to break this Ability by throwing a Boogie Bomb at him in a Limited Time Mode.

Raz was a researcher of The Spire, looking for it after seeing visions of the mysterious tower in his dreams since his childhood. He helped The Looper research The Spire for his own benefit and eventually found a piece of The Spire's Core that gave him powers, but consequentially corrupted him, turning him into Glyph Master Raz.

Glyph Master Raz was defeated by The Looper and had the artifact stolen from him. For reasons unclear, instead of returning to his normal form, he gained a blue, glitched-out appearance and lost part of his memories of when he was Glyph Master Raz, only recognizing that he had "messed up". His power soon attracted the attention of The Last Reality to the Island.

He is a major antagonist in Fortnite, serving as the main antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 6 and the posthumous antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 7 (as Glyph Master Raz).
