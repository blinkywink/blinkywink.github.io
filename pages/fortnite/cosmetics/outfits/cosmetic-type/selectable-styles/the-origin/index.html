The Origin is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 7, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 2 Battle Pass. The Origin is part of the The Seven Set.

The Origin is the field tactician and commander of The Seven[5] and the former Cube King of The Last Reality[6] that ran away due to the dark power of the Cubes.

He is a major character of Fortnite: Battle Royale serving as a minor, yet pivotal unseen character of Chapter 1 and Chapter 2, and a major protagonist of Chapter 3.

Personality

Personality[]

The Origin is strict and serious, commonly called old and grumpy by other members of The Seven. He is very experienced and earned his field tactician by watching countless Worlds end.[7] Origin suffers from a blatant lack of trust toward people because of Paradigm's apparent betrayal and his own past.[8][9] Because of this, The Origin has often tried to replace Paradigm with The Looper [10] and refused everything that could come from Paradigm, including weapons and battle plan showing how stubborn he was despite the whole reality being in danger. After Paradigm's case was resolved, Origin started to work to be more trusting. [11]

Appearance

Appearance[]

The Origin[]

As The Origin, His default style is the prisoner outfit that he wore during his time in the IO prison. The number "07" can be found on the outfit. Corruption Cubes and Golden Cubes emitting from his body.

His Seven style looks just like other members of the Seven, except for something that looks like his Cube King Crown floating near his shoulders. His armor also seem based on those of Trespassers and Kymeras.

The Cube King[]

As The Cube King, he has a similar armor to The Cube Queen with the addition of a flaming crown emitting cubes, flaming hands, and Sideways wings at his back.

History

History[]

Unknown Time Period[]

Main Article: Origin's Anthem

The Origin was the Cube King of The Last Reality. While they were invading realities, The Origin betrayed The Last Reality after realizing his actions. Soon after, he was captured by the Imagined Order. He formulated a plan to escape, and he tricked the IO making them think he had lost his mind to lower their vigilance and kill the guards in order escaping from their confines. After he escaped, he was saved from the prison by The Seven, joining them and becoming The Origin, the Seven's field general and tactician.[12]

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

The Origin arrived on the Island to help with the ongoing war against the Imagined Order and get his revenge. He arrived here with The Imagined and a few other characters, such as Doctor Strange and Black Widow.[13] He commandeers The Scientist's device [14] to get the help of The Looper, informing them that he believes that the Island's reality may be about to end [15]. He then sent The Looper on various field missions, and slowly started to trust them. However when he found out they helped Imagined to contact Paradigm he felt betrayed again [16], but soon forgot this knowing that The Looper is absolutely loyal to The Seven and did this for the common good [17]. After testing The Looper to become the pilot of the Mecha Strike Commander, or the co-pilot of Paradigm, he sent them urgently recoving keys that were meant to be used to activate The Collider to use them for reverse engineering. [18] After Jones and The Looper cut IO's power, they ordered The Looper to log-on into an IO holo-table to simulate the effects of the machine at Loot Lake, needing the two keys previously recovered. Discovering the purpose of The Collider, he orders the launch of the final attack as soon as possible and activated the Seven's emergency beacon [19], telling The Looper they'll win and that they'll see on the other side.[20]

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War[]

The Origin helps The Seven attack an IO Outpost to get acces to The Bridge. Spider-Man, Agent Jones and The Imagined get to Reality: 616 through The Bridge and recruit a group of Avengers to help The Resistance. In another attack, the Seven steal an IO shuttle and use it to get to The Ice Moon. Storm, Iron Man, The Foundation, Thor and The Scientist leave the Island, leaving only Origin, The Visitor and The Order.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War Issue 5[]

Origin doesn't join the battle on the ground and instead stays on the Moonbase to command the attack and deal with the Mecha's final repairs.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

After the Foundation and Order disappeared due to the chromium, Origin and the remaining members of The Seven gathered in a lab to study the Chrome [21][22]. Guessing that it came from The Herald, one of his former colleagues from The Last Reality, he prepared battle plans.[23]

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

While The Scientist was studying the sample of Chrome, it went out of control and Chromed The Scientist, then The Origin and the Visitor, leaving Paradigm as the only member of The Seven able to do something. Origin ordered her to escape before before being completely chromed.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

During AMIE's and The Looper's test of the portals, they accidentally found the location of where all the chromed people got sent.[24]. However, after sending fragments of AMIE through the portals, they discovered promptly after the fragments returned that everyone had their existences rewritten by "it".[25]
