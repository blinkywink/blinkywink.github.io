Outfit[]

“

Carter Wu is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 1 of Carter Wu's Set in the Chapter 7: Season 1 Battle Pass. Carter Wu is part of the Stunt Master Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Carter Wu's name in the Portuguese version of Fortnite is changed to Bruce Lindo, which is a pun and reference to the actor Bruce Lee, who was also a martial artist and stunt performer.
  • Carter Wu's Dare Devil Carter style is based on the Marvel Comics superhero Daredevil, who notably wore a red and yellow suit and included nunchaku-like billy clubs in his arsenal.
  • Carter Wu is the second male Outfit in the game to feature nipples (through his Black Dragon and Golden Dragon styles) after Tighty Whities Homer, and the first original Outfit to do so.
  • Carter Wu has a dojo in Breaker Beach on Surf City island.
