Overview

This article is about the Outfit. For the LEGO Character of the same name, see LEGO Fortnite:Bash.

“
When good llamas go bad.
”
— In-Game Description

Bash is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with Vegetta777’s Locker Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks. Bash was first released in Chapter 2: Season 1 and is part of The Leftovers Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Bash's appearances?
Bash has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Backstory

Backstory[]

On September 25th 2019, he lost against Ragsy, in the second Community Choice against Brite Blaster. He would remain in the files until later being placed with Brilliant Bomber as a bundle. However, in Update v11.30, the bundle was placed in the Annual Pass 2020 leaked along with Astra. Five hours later, Epic would cancel the Annual Pass. Bash finally made his first appearance two and a half months later on December 15th 2019, making him the first Community Choice loser to make it to the Item Shop.
