Outfit[]

“
No quarry can escape her... in any reality.
”
— In-Game Description

Shimmerdusk is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during October 2023, as part of the October 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack. Shimmerdusk was released in Chapter 4: Season 4 and is part of the Extraplanar Hunter Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Hunter's Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 1 additional month
  • Auric Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 2 additional month
  • Phase Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 3 additional months
  • Rimebound Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 4 additional months
  • Cosmic Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 5 additional months
