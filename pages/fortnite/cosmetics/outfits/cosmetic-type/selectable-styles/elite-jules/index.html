Jules is a master engineer, a member of SHADOW, as well as the daughter of Midas. She helped her father build The Device to break The Storm. She is also a former member of the Imagined Order and currently member of The Elites.

She is a supporting character in Fortnite, serving as the supporting character of Chapter 2: Season 2, Chapter 2: Season 3 and Chapter 7: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

Jules is a fan of technology and machines. After being left on her father's shadow for years, she learned how to rebuild the world the way she wanted to see it and is on track to be one of the greatest inventors of all time. Jules has had a complicated relationship with her father since the Golden Touch incident. However, despite their differing opinions and family problems, they worked together for a long time, supporting each other's goals. Although overshadowed by Midas, she managed to step out of his shadow and stand on her own two feet. When Midas finally returned from the Underworld, they managed to resolve their conflict and even found a way to seal his Golden Touch. However, their father-daughter relationship remains awkward in many ways.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Long ago, Jules was a princess and daughter of the golden king, Midas. After Midas received the curse of the golden touch, he accidentally turned Jules into gold killing her in the process. Midas eventually resurrected Jules but the cost was great, however Jules' trust was forever broken.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

A picture of Jules can be found in Midas' Hideout. Jules helps her father Midas build a Device that can break The Storm. The Device ends up failing and creates a huge flood on the island.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

Jules and her SHADOW Henchmen rebuild the remains of The Agency into The Authority, and build a water wall to keep it safe from the flood. She stays as the last remaining public leader of SHADOW until the end of the season.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Her voice can sometimes be heard near IO Elevators before they open.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

After SHADOW gets deactivated, Jules moves into Camp Cod. She befriends The Looper and tells them that "If we all work together, we can get out of here". She joins the Imagined Order as Scrapknight Jules.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

She participated in the Cosmic Summer Celebration as Beach Jules, with Midsummer Midas. Her, Marigold, and Midas are planning something, this can be seen through her special dialogue when talking to Marigold. After the Summer ends, Midas disappears.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

As Scrapknight Jules, she searches for Obi-Wan Kenobi alongside Gunnar and other IO troops.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

As Scrapknight Jules, she joined Oathbound in order to learn more about their technology. Her interaction with The Ageless reveals that she works with the Rift Warden. At the end of the season, special dialogue spoken by Scrapknight Jules heavily implies that her and Evie are the culprits behind the destruction of the Rift Gate, as she claims to have "the Island's best interests at heart".

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Marigold has suspicions that Midas is on the island, so she calls Jules and the rest of Midas' Crew back together. Jules wanted to stay out of family drama, but was roped into in nevertheless.

After Midas escapes The Underworld, he finds Jules and says he sees she's outgrown his shadow, and that he hopes she hasn’t left him behind.

In the Rise of Midas quests, Jules is tasked by a "Mutual Contact" to help Midas with his Golden Touch problem. She plans to build him Gauntlets, which she makes using mysterious "Essence" from the Underworld.

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

As Elite Jules, she joined The Elites. When the Dark Harvester was flying over Elite airspace, Jules boarded the ship when it landed briefly and made her way to the Golden Coast.
