Wanderer was a non-interactive Character introduced during Chapter 5: Season 3. He could have been found wandering around the island before Update v30.40.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Wanderer can be found in one of three locations, wandering around the area the entire match while phasing through destructible objects in his way including doors.

When the player gets close to Wanderer, he will vanish from their view. Only reappearing when they are far enough away from him. He is immune to all sources of damage.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Doombot's size is slightly bigger than the other Bosses, being the second Boss to have a different size than the normal Slightly Taller and Much Larger Boss Sizes after The Foundation.
  • When the Doombot is defeated, it will begin to spark up and malfunction, kneeling onto the floor while knocking nearby players away before exploding. This makes it the third Boss to have a unique defeat animation. The previous two being Peter Griffin and Darth Vader.
    • It is also the first Boss with a unique defeat animation to not use a Vindertech Drone or crumble into cubes.
  • Doombot has a unique footstep sound that is absent from the Doom Outfit itself.
  • Doombot wears Doom's Cloak as its Back Bling.
    • Despite this however it will not fuse with the Doombot. The cause of this is unknown as it does not use any Boss exclusive cosmetics.
  • Doombot's Foot Dive is a reference to Doctor Doom's aerial move from Marvel Super Heroes and made famous in the Marvel vs. Capcom fighting game series. Even performing the exact same movement as the ability is used.
  • Doombot's Arena Idle Instrumental is slightly shorter than the rest of its combat music layers.
  • Once The Storm hits Doombot, it will begin to turn on its Latverian Tactical Grunts, attacking them on sight. Why this happens is unknown but is likely rooted to how the Latverian Guards are coded.
  • Doombot is the only Boss during Chapter 5: Season 4 to drop two Mythic Weapons, being Doctor Doom's Arcane Gauntlets and Doctor Doom's Monarch Pistol.

Behavior

Behavior[]

DEFEAT DOCTOR DOOM!‎[]

After emerging at the start of the Absolute Doom Live Event, Doctor Doom will always appear at the center of the Island battling Captain Jones with a Legendary Rocket Launcher, Gwenpool with Epic Dual Micro SMGs, and Peelverine with an Epic Modular Combat Assault Rifle. He randomly targets anybody that is within the arena.

Doctor Doom's movement is slower due to his colossal size. He still possesses a headshot weakpoint and is able to be briefly staggered while in battle. Any player that gets too close to Doctor Doom will be pushed away without taking any damage. While he is active, an icon will display his current location on the minimap.

Doctor Doom's gigantic healthpool is shared across all servers. Once it is fully drained, Doctor Doom will stop spawning.

Doctor Victor Werner von Doom, Ph.D, commonly known as Doctor Doom and later as The Wanderer is a sorcerer from Reality: 616. He is a supervillain and ruler of Latveria that was recruited into the IO by Geno, and later assisted the Wasteland Warriors and Megalo Don.

He is a major antagonist in Fortnite, serving as one of the two secondary antagonists (alongside Doctor Slone) of the Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War comics, the overarching antagonist of Chapter 5: Season 3 and the main antagonist of Chapter 5: Season 4.

Personality

Personality[]

Doom is cold-hearted and selfish, with only aspirations for his own goals and seeking power, occasionally helping others if they align the same way. However, he will not hesitate to betray others and always put his self-interests as his top priority. His alignment with both the Imagined Order and the Wasteland Warriors both ended in betrayal, with him returning to his own reality and him locating and opening Pandora's Box respectively.

Doom also possesses a superior intellect, proven by his ability to understand how to use many of the weapons in The Ice King's weaponry with little to no experience. He was also described as a "wise friend" by Megalo Don and could harness the power of Pandora's Box without assistance.

As The Wanderer, Doom became a mysterious individual, only allowing himself to be seen from a distance and never showing his face, even to his allies.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Doctor Doom is a large and tall man who covers his whole body with titanium armor and wears a green tunic and a cape with a hood.

As The Wanderer he appear as a large and tall man in a white hood, appearing as dark smoke beneath a white cloak, surrounded by a blue aura that distorts the light, or possibly even the space itself around him and passing through the walls and disappearing like a ghost.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

Doctor Doom is called by Thor into Reality Zero because of the threat Galactus poses.[18][19] Doom takes over Pleasant Park, turning it into Doom's Domain and guarding it with his Henchmen.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

Doctor Doom was recruited by Geno into the Imagined Order's Inner Circle, being told to take care of The Seven and their allies.[20] Doom makes a plan to steal The Zero Shard by sending army of Hybrids to lure Jones, Wolverine, Spider-Man, and Shuri toward the High Evolutionary's domain. After a fight against Stegron and The High Evolutionary, the distraction allowed Doom to steal The Zero Shard, returning to Reality Zero immediately afterward.[21] Doom then made his way to The Ice King's castle on The Ice Moon to recover a doomsday weapon for the Imagined Order, but is followed by Jones and his allies. While Doom overpowers them with The Ice King's weapons, he is eventually knocked down by Shuri, who electrocutes him. Spider-Man retrieves the stolen Zero Shard and meets up with the rest of The Seven to power the Mech. Upon noticing this, Doom retreats to The Island.[22]


Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #5[]

Doom gets to the lower parts of The Collider, preparing to leave, but is found by Gunnar who shouts him to get to the battle and help the Imagined Order turn the tides of the war with The Ice King's weaponry. Doom betrays the IO and blasts Gunnar with his gun, opening a portal to Reality: 616's Latveria. Doom's true intentions are learnt, he thought the Order was not worthy nor capable of engineering the perfect society and that they were fools thinking they could do. He reveals that he only intended to use them, claiming he is the only one blessed with such vision. Afterward, he leaves with the Doomsday weapons from the Ice King's Castle, returning to Reality: 616 and abandoning Gunnar to die.[23]

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

After Zeus was defeated, The Oracle revealed the prophecy of darkened skies, choking dusts, and that our war has just begun. She reveals that "The Wanderer" draws near but that Hope would guide us. Zeus, not wanting to give up completely in his fight against mortals, forms a new plan.[24]

Sandstorm Event[]

During The Sandstorm event, Zeus used the Statue at Mount Olympus to strike Pandora's Box three times with lightning to completely fill the orb on the box. On the last strike from The Statue, the Nitro energy broke free from the gem on Pandora's Box, which fled The Island while leaving craters in its path, going far away onto the desert islands to the south of the Helios Island. Then, in a burst of energy a huge sandstorm was created, alongside a big leviathan-themed naval ship that slowly sailing closer to the Island day by day.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

Arriving alongside the sandstorm, the Wasteland Warriors, led by Megalo Don started to invade the Island, preparing to "raze this Island to the ground once my Leviathan awakes."[25] The Wanderer can be seen among his crew [26] as well as on sprays at Wasteland Warriors' owned locations.

Some time later, Islanders started to claim to feel observed or see a hooded figure in the distance observing them, before disappearing once they try to look at them.[27] The Wanderer started to appear around Placid Pumps, Coastal Comms and Estate Station.

After The Looper got the second Pandora gem and gave it to Hope, the gem gave Hope visions of The Wanderer and Megalo Don working together.[28] Hope then told us to be aware of The Wanderer. Shortly before all of the Pandora Gem's were reunited by The Looper, Hope and Jones, The Wanderer started to wander in the Wasteland, South of Lil 'Villa, Southwest of The Wreckmill and Cliffside Lodge.

Once Hope reunited all of Pandora's Gem, Megalo Don reveals in a transmission that he is aware of people watching at the pipeline's progress towards The Leviathan, and reveals that a "wise friend" told him that he would succeed in his plan. [29]

Refinery Event[]

As Megalo Don was finally fueling his Leviathan, to then launch his assault, Doom hacks into his system, with an evil laugh, causing the pipeline to explode, starting from Brutal Beachhead and reaching Redline Rig. In the process, he revealed the location of Pandora's Box.

Beware the Wanderer[]

Doctor Doom absorbs the powers of Pandora's Box, while Hope learn her fate with Pandora's Box, and how she is meant to defeat Doom.

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

Doom quickly takes over the Island, bringing Latveria and The Raft to the Island. He takes care of the Fantastic Four, his former arch-enemies and proudly displays their remains in his castle, transforming Mr. Fantastic into a rubber band ball, displaying Invisible Woman's deceased body in a glass case, using Human Torch's heart to power his fireplace and transforming The Thing into his throne at Castle Doom. Doctor Doom also killed and collected trophies from several people from Reality: 616: Ultron, Namor, Moon Knight, Thanos, Doctor Strange, Silver Surfer, Magneto and Galactus and "got rid" of his former ally Megalo Don. Despite his accomplishments, he feels empty and a vacant hunger due to the Box.[30] He wants to wield it again, but its power is too chaotic and uncontrolled[31], meaning that he won't be able to harness it properly until he prepares himself by improving his armor.[32] Before quickly disappearing from the public eye, Doom gives control over Castle Doom to one of his Doombots and orders his henchmen to dig around the Island for artifacts.[33] Doom's whereabouts is currently unknown.

Absolute Doom Event[]

Tony Stark had set up beacons aimed at Doom's Arcane Forge. Loopers who side with Hope, along with The Underground, the Avengers, and the X-Men, unite in an effort to rift both the Arcane Forge and Doom out of this reality. However, their plan falters as Doom shields his Arcane Forge, rendering the beams ineffective. Upon Doom's arrival, all but one of the rift beacons scatter to Brawler's Battleground, Grand Glacier, and Restored Reels. Doom then arrives on the Island and launches devastating attacks that kill several Loopers, with one that nearly kills Captain Jones until Hope intervenes. Using Jones's shield, she absorbs Doom's Pandora magic, enabling her to strike Doom in the eye, injuring him and damaging his armor. The Island's heroes manage to activate the final rift beacon, and the energy of Pandora's Box can be seen getting pulled out of Doom's Giant form and into the rift, before Doom himself is pulled into it. All that remains is his mask where he once stood.

Trivia[]

  • In one of the Pandora's Box concept art, a similar hooded figure as Doctor Doom (in the Wanderer's clothes) can be found on it.[34]
  • During Update v30.10, Megalo Don's Mysterious Transmission for The Wanderer was accidentally added early in Portuguese, Italian and other European countries dub.
  • The Wanderer's role as arch enemy of Hope is reminiscent the opposing nature between Elpis, the personification of hope, and Moros, the personification of doom and the force which drove men towards their fated death.
    • This may indicate he actually plays the role of Moros.
    • "Moros" can be translated as "Doom" or "Destined-Death", corresponding to The Wanderer's true identity: Doom.
  • In his prototype armor, Doom is referred as Ultra Doom.
    • Doom's Chosen is also referred as Ultra Doom, as they wear that armor.
  • In his Pandora's armor and with Pandora's Box power, Doom is referred as Absolute Doom.
