The Imagined was a chaotic member of The Seven introduced in Chapter 3: Season 2. She was one of The Sisters first mentioned by The Foundation during Zero Crisis Finale. She was the daughter of Geno, genetically changed to be "perfect" in her father's vision.

She was a major character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a major protagonist of Chapter 3.

Personality

Personality[]

The Imagined had a personality similar to that of a rebellious, free-spirited teenager who showed little restraint, standing in direct contrast to her sister. Although she had strong principles and ultimately sought to fight for what she believed was right, she often disobeyed orders and acted independently, sometimes recklessly.

Deep down, she harbored resentment toward her father, Geno, after he trapped both her and her sister in The Loop and effectively abandoned them, replacing them with snapshots when they failed to meet his expectations. This anger and sadness motivated many of her actions against the Imagined Order and Geno. In contrast, her sister was driven primarily by a sense of duty and a belief in doing what was right.

Despite this, the Imagined did not place that burden on others. When the time came to confront Geno, she asked Logan to ensure her sister's safety, choosing to face the consequences herself and accept to die alongside him.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Before Chapter 1[]

Geno created The Sisters and genetically modified them to be as perfect as possible, as the Imagined Order are on a hunt for the perfect civilization. Thanks to Geno's skills in science and biology, the Sisters and himself are immortal. The Omniverse was the playground of the IO, but Geno decided to throw the Sisters into The Loop, betting that they would be the first to escape. They weren't though, making Geno replace them with one of their more stronger Snapshots.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

She arrived on Artemis in Chapter 3: Season 2 along with The Origin, assisting the Resistance in the war against the Imagined Order. She employed the help of The Looper to destroy IO Building Jammers by having them steal IO documents, allowing The Scientist to build data chips that allows them to find the cloaked Building Jammers and ultimately destroy them. She appears to be one of the members of The Seven that trusts The Paradigm, with the other being The Scientist. Later in the season, she tricks Jones into giving her information on the Imagined Order's file system during a party, potentially in an attempt to find out more about herself. The files for both her and her sister have been wiped from the system by Geno.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

She joins Agent Jones, Spider-Man and The Foundation to infiltrate The Bridge, later being ambushed by Doctor Slone and Gunnar. They flee into Reality: 616 and Imagined gets in a fight with Captain Marvel. They are seperated by Spider-Man, who gets them to the Avengers Headquarters. Spider-Man comes up with the idea of getting help from Wolverine, leading the group to Madripoor. Wolverine joins them after Imagined tells him it will be the best fight he ever had. To find The Zero Shard, they travel to Wakanda. They fight against robot panther mechs that Shuri commands to stop. They go into the Vibranium vault, where Wolverine smells and tracks down the Shard into Serpent Valley. They are attacked by Hybrids and follow one to their base that gets blown up. They track more Hybrids and prepare a camp for the night. Imagined gets close with Wolverine, telling her backstory and origins to him. She says she likes who Wolverine is, giving him a kiss. Next day, they reach High Evolutionary and Stegron's laboratory. The group distracts Evolutionary and Stegron as Spider-Man sneaks into the lab to steal the Zero Shard. He meets Doctor Doom inside who escapes with a portal. They stop fighting and Evolutionary lets them get away to chase Doom. They end up on The Ice Moon near The Ice King's castle. They go inside but Jones accidentally triggers a hologram of the Ice King that congratulates them for reaching him. The holgoram tells them they are too dangerous to left alive so the group runs away. Imagined gets attacked by Doom and get in a fight with Doom. He beats them but Spider-Man steals the Zero Shard and uses it to power the Mecha Team Leader. Doom gets away and the Mecha carries the team to Reality Zero.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #5[]

As the attack on The Collider starts, The Imagined notices Geno on an upper platform, firing an energy beam at Iron Man, causing him to crash. She calls The Order to help her, telling that "daddy's home". The Sisters and Wolverine climb up The Collider and blast near Geno where they get into a fight. Geno doesn't kill the sisters and rather shows mercy, but The Order kicks him making all of them fall and hold on to the platform. The Imagined sacrifices herself and jumps into The Zero Point, taking Geno with her. They end up at "The Realm" and get discorporated, getting scattered across The Omniverse.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

Although unknown how she returned, she goes missing alongside the rest of The Seven. Her Blade was found at Rave Cave by The Looper.
