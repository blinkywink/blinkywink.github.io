Overview

“
Wrapped in rusty barbed wire, and ready to settle the score.
”
— In-Game Description

Raider's Revenge is an Epic Pickaxe in Fortnite that can be purchased in the OG Season Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks after earning 2,000,000 XP. Raider's Revenge was first released in Season 1 and is part of the Storm Scavenger Set.

Raider's Revenge could have also been purchased in the Season Shop during Season 1 for 1,500 V-Bucks after reaching Level 35.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Raider's Revenge's appearances?
Raider's Revenge has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
