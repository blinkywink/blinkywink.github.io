Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Meowdas is a fusion of Midas and Meowscles.
  • Meowdas's 'Midas Touch' exclusively used the Golden Grin Wrap prior to Update v33.00. Since Update v33.00, Meowdas has recived the option to pick between the Golden Grin and Golden Touch Wraps in as part of his selectable styles.
  • Meowdas's 'Street Art' Super Level style does not allow the outfit's reactivity to be used if it is equipped, regardless if it is turned on or not.
  • Meowdas's Midas Touch will not leave a item with a permanent wrap upon being dropped in-game, with the exception of Motorboats or the item being swapped with another item in-game.
    • This issue is also shared with Shadow Midas.
  • Meowdas is one of three Fortnite original characters featured in the Empty Out Your Pockets music video during Keep Dreaming With Juice WRLD.

Meowdas was a special Boss Character hybrid introduced during Chapter 2 Remix. He could have been found in The Yacht.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Meowdas will attack the player if he notices them undisguised. Though if disguised, he can be talked to for additional dialogue.

Shortly after getting knocked down, he will Self-Revive and join the team of the player who defeated him. Meowdas will replenish all of his health and act as a Medic Specialist Character, Providing Chug Splashes to teammates who are not full on health.

Besides this, he will grant the team hes following access to his SHADOW Chests, and neutrality from his henchmen, Sentry Turrets and Sentry Cameras.

Meowdas, when recruited, may also request a High Five or Hug from the player he is following. If ignored, he will cancel the offer and turn away.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Meowdas' Boss music is a remix of the Spy base combat music from Chapter 2: Season 2. Speeding up the previous season's Patrol music and combining the Threatened with Alerted music for his own Threatened music.
  • Meowdas reuses the voice of Meowscles with an added Full Alert Idle voiceline set and no Interrogation set.
  • Meowdas, along with Dynamo TNTina and Snoop Dogg are the first Bosses that can be recruited by the player.
    • The feature seems to be recycled from the Latverian Guards due to the Emote requests, now fully fleshed out.
    • Because of this, it is impossible to gain the Who's the Boss? Elimination Accolade when defeating them as they will immediately join the player's team after getting knocked.
  • If the player dismisses Meowdas, he will be eliminated instantly, as opposed to returning to his starting location like other Hireable Characters.
  • Along with other Remix bosses, Meowdas will not drop his items if eliminated by The Storm.
  • If the player that knocked Meowdas is eliminated before he Self-Revives, he will remain knocked and can be Revived manually by another player. However the popup that indicates the option will have no text.
