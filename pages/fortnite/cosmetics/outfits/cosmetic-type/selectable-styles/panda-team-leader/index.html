Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for P.A.N.D.A Team Leader's appearances?
P.A.N.D.A Team Leader has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In Update v24.20, P.A.N.D.A Team Leader received a new style, Syd.
  • P.A.N.D.A Team Leader's icon was changed in Update v10.10.
    • It was changed again in Update v24.20.
    • It was changed once again in Update v33.00.
  • Prior to the skin's official release in August 2018, Fortnite concept artist Jantrax_NL shared a Panda-themed Team Leader concept on Reddit. Due to the timing and visual similarities, the connection became a widely discussed topic within the community at the time. While there has been no official confirmation from Epic Games that the fan concept influenced the final design, the association remains a notable part of the skin's community history.[1]

Syd (better known as Cuddle Team Leader and as Mecha Cuddle Master) is the leader of the Camp Cuddle, as well as the owner of Rave Cave's Baller coaster and Cuddle Cross Track.

Personality

Personality[]

Cuddle Team Leader is a very friendly person who can solve most of her problems by hugging it out. But when the problem is unsolvable, things can get a little violent. She helped Drift when he first arrived on the island in Season 5 Road Trip!. She also acts as a guide for new players in LEGO Fortnite as a tutorial character.

History

History[]

Chapter 1: Season 5[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader went on a Road Trip! with Brite Bomber, Rex and Drift.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader was thrown into an unknown futuristic reality after Zero Crisis Finale. She became Mecha Cuddle Master and fought her way back to the island.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader became the leader of Camp Cuddle.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader made a gigantic Cuddle Team Leader head on top of Rave Cave and turned it into a Baller coaster.

LEGO Fortnite[]

  • Cuddle Team Leader is one of the many characters rifted in the LEGO Reality as well as one of the first Looper encounters.
