Joey is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 5, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 2: Season 7 Battle Pass. Joey is part of the Inner Vision Set.

Joey is an alien soldier from The Last Reality that secretly got into the Imagined Order's ranks, but later became a double agent for them through bribing by The Looper.

Personality

Personality[]

Joey has an interest in human culture and food, especially cat food. Unlike the rest of The Last Reality troops, they[1] are against the war on Reality Zero.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Joey settles at Dirty Docks, disguised as a human male. After Slone finds out about them[1], she and The Looper bribe Joey with cat food to double agent for the IO. Due to their[1] passion of human culture, Joey accepts the deal even though they later regret it.
