Overview

“
Here to see if life on dry land lives up to the hype.
”
— In-Game Description

Axo is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks, with Maisie Williams & Reuben Selby's Locker Bundle for 2,000 V-Bucks or with the Axo & Bryne Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. Axo was first released in Chapter 2: Season 3 and is part of the Axolotl Attack! Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Axo's appearances?
Axo has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Axo has a female counterpart named Bryne.
  • In Update v17.20, Axo received a new style, Toxo, which appears to be based on a poisonous species of salamander called "fire salamander".
  • Axo's tank top has a print based on an image of Otto, the pet of content creator ZackScottGames. The same image was previously used for his Banner Icon from Save the World's Search for Survivors event.[1]
  • Axo is based off an axolotl, a real life amphibian, this is also reflected in the name of his set and the cosmetics related to it.
  • If a player leaves Fortnite Festival during a performance or if No Fill is selected, Axo can appear as a substitute band member.
