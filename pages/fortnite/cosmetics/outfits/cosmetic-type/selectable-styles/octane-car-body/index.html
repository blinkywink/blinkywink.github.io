Overview

This article is about the Car Body. For the Vehicle of the same name, see Octane.
The media represented on this page has been created by a second/third party.
This content is not originally part of the game but has been introduced through a special collaboration.
Obtaining this cosmetic also makes it available in Rocket League.

“
Rocket powered perfection in a league of its own.
”
— In-Game Description

Octane is an Uncommon Car Body in Fortnite, that can be obtained as a reward from the Try Rocket Racing Quests. Octane was released in Chapter 5: Season 1.
