Outfit[]

8-Ball vs Scratch is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Tier 60 of the Chapter 2: Season 1 Battle Pass. 8-Ball vs Scratch is part of the Stripes and Solids Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Scratch - Complete 6 Alter Ego Challenges and eliminate an opponent while wearing the 8-Ball outfit.
  • Gold - Complete 9 8-Ball Vs Scratch Missions.
  • Corrupted - Reach Level 100.

8-Ball is an E.G.O. agent that was sent to Apollo by The Imagined Order to explore the island. He later joined The Resistance to fight against the Imagined Order.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

8-Ball is sent to Apollo by the Imagined Order. They discover the new island but are met with enemy agents. E.G.O. locates Scratch and 8-Ball went to fight against him.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-2[]

8-Ball joins The Seven's army and helps them attack an IO Outpost. Pirate Fishstick is killed in action, who 8-Ball seems to be close friends with. The Foundation tells him that Fishstick is a Snapshot and that the real Fishstick was killed a while ago. He once again helps the Seven attack another IO outpost and stays on the Island with The Visitor, The Order and The Origin.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War Issue 5[]

8-Ball helps the Seven in the attack on The Collider.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

8-Ball finds himself a new rival, 1-Ball, a member of GHOST, just like him. They compete in billiards matches at Faulty Splits.[1]

Chapter 2 Remix[]

Back on Apollo, 1-Ball challenges 8-Ball, whom he considers a legend, to a fight in order to prove he is the best.
