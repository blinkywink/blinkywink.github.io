Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Spire Assassin's codename is Inner.
  • Despite the name, the Spire Assassin Boss doesn't actually use the Spire Assassin Outfit in-game. She instead uses the Epic TBD Outfit used by the Spire Guardians.
    • The style used (NPC Helm B) looks very similar to the Spire Assassin Outfit's design. Though with a more open helmet and a few details either simplified or stripped down.
  • Despite being part of The Spire Cult, she will attack the Spire Guardians if one of them spots each other.
  • Spire Assassin, along with the Spire Guardians were the first human Bosses to have a visible Health bar above them when damaged. A trait that would carry over with every other Boss in the future.
  • Spire Assassin used to break her Primal Shotgun's fire rate. Causing it to fire at an impossible to achieve rate by the player.
    • This could be the reason behind her Mythic item being switched to her Recycler, as the fire rate would decimate any player caught within range.
  • Spire Assassin was the first Boss to be outright replaced after an update in the same Season she was introduced with no new spawn location.
    • She was also the first Boss to have their Mythic item replaced by another.

Spire Assassin is a member of the Spire Cult and the Imagined Order. She is the guardian of The Spire but was overthrown by Glyph Master Raz, leaving her fate unknown.

She is a minor antagonist in Fortnite, serving as the secondary antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 6.
