Outfit[]

“
Wander no more.
”
— In-Game Description

Trace is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 2, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 3 Battle Pass. Trace is part of the Luminary Rebel Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Rebel Trace - Page 3 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)
  • Hood - Up - Page 3 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)
  • Luminary Trace - Complete 12 Weekly Quests Weeks 1-2

  • Confetti Claw Trace - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
  • Regal Hunter Trace - Page 6 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
