The demon who makes trophies of men.
Throughout the galaxy, there are few species feared more than the Yautja. Fierce warriors that kill for honor and sport, this one followed Jones through a rift and is eager to sample all the new prey the island has to offer.
— Character Collection Book

Predator was a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 2: Season 5. He could have been found in Stealthy Stronghold after Update v15.21.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Predator dropped the following items when eliminated. He also dropped Bars.

Predator's Cloaking Device Slurpfish

Predator used the Predator Claw, a Melee Weapon reworking of the Yautja Wristblades Harvesting Tool to attack players or break obstacles.

Predator Claw
Behavior

Behavior[]

Predator roamed around Stealthy Stronghold in search of opponents to hunt, completely invisible until the opportunity arrived. When alerted, Predator would run towards his target and attempt to claw at them before retreating after a full attack to hide away, only to come back for another ambush.

Predator would not drop his claws when eliminated.

Special Abilities

Special Abilities[]

  • Super Speed
    • Like Wolverine, Predator had a significant speed buff which allowed him to close the distance between him and his target much easier.
  • Invisibility
    • Predator could use his Cloaking Device without the cooldown and while being in water. However, the invisibility would disappear when using his weapon.
  • Shockwave Leaps
    • Upon completing a full attack string in close proximity of his target, Predator would leap away with a similar effect to Shockwave Grenade. Destroying any nearby obstructions and allowing him to reactivate Invisibility before landing. It didn’t affect nearby players and Predator would not leap when breaking obstacles far from players.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Some of the noises Predator makes are taken from the 1987 Predator film.
    • Additionally, his Boss music pays homage to the music score in the film.
  • Predator is the first Boss to drop Bars. A trait that will carry over to every other Boss past Chapter 2: Season 5.
  • Predator used an unreleased variant of the Bio-helmet Online Emote. The difference is the Boss version doesn't have him take his mask off.
  • While retreating, it was possible for Predator to get stuck on the wooden bridges in Stealthy Stronghold. The boss was unable to break the obstruction whilst invisible until the nearby player got far away from him.

The Jungle Hunter, nicknamed Predator is a Yautja from Reality: 2053105-28 who was hunting Agent Jones until he came at Reality Zero.

He is a supporting antagonist in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 5, and a posthumous antagonist in Chapter 2: Season 6.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Since an unknown reality, Predator starts following Agent Jones in order to kill him. Jones tries to outsmart the Predator, but loses. Later Jones finds him and brings him to Reality Zero using his Portal Device to create a Pure Portal.

Zero Crisis Finale[]

During the chaos, he fights against Meowscles who punches him in the face, but fails to do any damage due to Predator's mask. Predator then aims his laser sight at Meowscles, and Meowscles looks at him with a worried face.
