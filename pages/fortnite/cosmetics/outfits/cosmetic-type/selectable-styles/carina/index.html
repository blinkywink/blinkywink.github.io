“
Secret places, abandoned places... That's always where things get interesting.
”
— Carina

Carina is a well-known streamer and conspiracy investigator in Reality: 266. She seeks to expose secrets and uncover the truth, whether it concerns InnoLoop, Classified Canyon's facilities, the Dark Voyager and even The Seven.

Although unfamiliar with the concept of alternate realities and everything related to The Zero Point, The Seven, and The Last Reality, her persistent investigations, hindered by Hope and Jones' warnings, led her to become more and more involved with the greater conflicts of The Omniverse.

She is a supporting character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main supporting character of Chapter 7: Season 1's story.

Personality

Personality[]

Carina is a curious, stubborn, bull-headed and fearless investigator driven by uncovering hidden truths. She believes information should be shared, not controlled, and actively involves others in her search through collaboration and livestreams. Skeptical of powerful organizations and official narratives, she prioritizes truth over rules or personal risk. Persistent and defiant, she refuses to back down when confronted, using confidence and humor as both a tool for engagement and a shield against intimidation. People view her as a annoying, stubborn, weird and nosy bull-headed girl who does not know what she's talking about.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Carina has tan skin and bright blue, shoulder-length hair styled in a straight bob, secured with a pink bandana tied at the top of her head. She wears a brown oversized T-shirt with the poster for Peelian movie on the front, light blue shorts, and a blue crossbody bag worn over her shoulder.

Her left forearm is wrapped in a pink cast or bandage, and she wears several bracelets on her right wrist. She has a knee pad on one leg and an adhesive bandage on her thigh. Her outfit is completed with long white socks with blue stripes and pink sneakers. A short necklace is visible around her neck.

History

History[]

Chapter Seven: Season 1[]

As The Looper is informed by Jones of the incoming war against the Dark Voyager, and trains themselves with the new weapons and gears, they also explore Lethal Labs' underground facility, where they find out The Order, now trapped in the rumored secret cell, surrounded by a cubic sealing that prevents her from rifting out. She asks the help of the hero to look for Carina who's been recklessly peeking around the labs, and to retrieve the keycard she found that would deactivate the cell's barrier. In exchange, The Looper gets to help Carina with looking for Bigfoot and Human Bill as well as collecting gold bars for her. After getting back to The Order, The Looper, Jones and Hope successfully free the spy, allowing her to rift out and look for the remaining members of the now scattered Seven—who will soon be needed for the fight to come—and triggering the cell's sirens, causing Skulldroid and NITE Unit Sniper to quickly show up to take care of the intruders, however, they fail and are defeated in battle.
