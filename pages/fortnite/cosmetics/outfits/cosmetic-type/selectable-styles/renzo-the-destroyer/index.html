Renzo the Destroyer or simply Renzo is a member of The Unseen, as well as Highwire's right-hand man. He is known to be a famous Doomfluencer in MEGA City.[1]

He is a supporting character in Chapter 4: Season 2, acting first as a minor antagonist before turning into a supporting protagonist.

Personality

Personality[]

Renzo the Destroyer is a prideful and arrogant robot who loves to destroy and cause chaos, just like the rest of The Unseen members. He is obsessed with rococo baroque. Renzo is also described by NeuraLynx as someone always giving his all.[2] According to himself and Triarch Nox, he seeks conquest, he wants to take over MEGA City[3] as well as "the light".[4] Renzo is also very proud of his title of Doomfluencer.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Renzo appears as a darkly colored, skeletal-like robot with blue flames on his head. He wears a classy outfit paired with a blue coat, damaged due his use of the Skull-a-tron.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Before Chapter 4[]

Renzo is created by an unknown person, and later mentored by Allan the Unmentionable.[8] After, he became a member of The Unseen within MEGA City, described like the rest of them as causing wreck and havoc without caring if innocent people were harmed. He also became a Doomfluencer at an unknown period of time.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

Following MEGA City's arrival on Asteria, its Syndicates are on the brink of war because of Dahlia - a member of The Unseen who was collaborating with Styx, Triarch Nox and secretly The Army of the Last Reality - causing trouble across the city and sabotaging the factions within it. After the Peace Syndicate managed, with the help of The Looper, to unite all the Syndicates and identify the saboteur as a member of The Unseen, Thunder and Evie place their trust in The Looper, hoping that Highwire and Renzo the Destroyer would listen to them and stop Dahlia's on-going scheme.

Using a P33LY disguise created by The Luminaries, The Looper manages to infiltrate The Unseen's territory, subsequently convincing Renzo and Highwire to put an end to their alliance with The Army of the Last Reality. Enraged upon learning that someone has made a move behind their back[9], they put an end to Dahlia's plan after a "talk",[10] before joining the alliance of MEGA City's Syndicates[11] and helping them cut Dahlia's contact with The Last Reality.
