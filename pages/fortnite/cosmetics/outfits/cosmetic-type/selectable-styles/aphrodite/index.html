Aphrodite is the Goddess of Love, an Olympian Goddess and the advocate for mortals among Gods.[1]

She is a supporting character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a supporting protagonist in Chapter 5: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

Aphrodite is caring about the mortals[4] and fascinated by their culture[5] and ideas.[6] She believes in the good inside the Gods and encourages them to care for the mortals too.[7] Even though she has the mortals' best interest at heart, those wronged by the Gods hesitate to trust her because she is one of them.[8]

Timeline

Timeline[]

Before Chapter 5[]

Aphrodite has been the advocate between Gods and mortals for eons, protecting them from the Gods' wrath.[11] She and her sister, Artemis were described as old friends.[12]

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

The Pandora's Box had a constellation symbolizing Aphrodite, in the form of a heart.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

After Pandora's Box was opened, Aphrodite came along with the other Gods who intended to punish mortals for opening it and releasing the souls of The Underworld. She was one of the first people who is against Zeus' senseless wrath and wanted to stop him.[4] After hearing about The Looper' cause, she grows sympathetic to it[13] and wants them to show their power, to prepare them against Zeus.[14] She wants them to help find a house, food and a car for Peely so that they can "prove their heart".[15] She then wants to recruit Artemis to help their cause[16] before Zeus can pull her to his side.[17]

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

Aphrodite has fled the Island into the infinite corners of reality in search of a new domain to rule over after the gods were humbled, yet able to go their own ways due to the fall of Zeus.

Chapter 6: Season 3[]

Aphrodite, determined as ever in her duty of finding the heart in herself and others, arrives in Reality: 783 to relax at its beaches, protecting herself from the sun and heartbreak as she does.


Chapter 7: Season 1[]

Seeking vacation, Aphrodite came to the Golden Coast for relaxation
