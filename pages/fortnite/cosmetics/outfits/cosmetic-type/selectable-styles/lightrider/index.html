Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Like most Supernova Academy Outfits, Lightrider's design has drastically changed from its concept art seen in a survey, likely to make them more distinct from each other.
  • Judging by her survey design and the Supernova Squad Loading Screen, Lightrider's powers were originally Rift-themed instead of Rezz-themed.
    • She retained her Rift-themed powers in Expeditions.
    • Reboot Man was likely intended to be the only Supernova Academy Outfit with Rezz-themed powers.
  • Lightrider has a male counterpart named Reboot Man.

Lightrider is a superhero, a member of The Supernovas on Supernova's Formation Tau and a graduated student from the Supernova Academy.

She is a supporting character of Chapter 6: Season 3.

Personality

Personality[]

Lightrider used to be an blunt, sarcastic, reckless, arrogant, snarky, and brutally honest with a tough exterior. With her deadpan remarks, she was a lone wolf who believed the only reliable teammate is herself. She also liked to show off her skills thinking she's the only one who is powerful. After the event of Chapter 6: Season 3, she finally started to rely on and work with her Supernovas teammates more and more.
