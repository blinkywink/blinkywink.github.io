Outfit[]

“
She's one chill kitty.
”
— In-Game Description

Meow Skulls is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 5, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 4 Battle Pass. Meow Skulls is part of the Cali-Cool Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Cloud Nine - Page 8 of the Battle Pass ( 8 Battle Stars)
  • Celestial - Page 3 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
  • Spectral - Page 4 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
  • Aurelian - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Meow Skulls was named after people were pronouncing "Meowscles" as "meow-skulls".
  • If a player leaves Fortnite Festival during a performance or if No Fill is selected, Meow Skulls can appear as a substitute band member.
