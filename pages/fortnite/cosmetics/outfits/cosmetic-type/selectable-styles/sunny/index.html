Outfit[]

“
Get me off of this rock.
”
— In-Game Description

Sunny is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 2, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 2: Season 7 Battle Pass. Sunny is part of the Escape Velocity Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Voyager Sunny - Page 3 of the Battle Pass ( 8 Battle Stars)

  • Sunny (Silver) - Page 3 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
  • Sunny (Golden) - Page 4 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
  • Sunny (Prismatic) - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
