Crew Legacy Styles

Overview

Crew Legacy Styles, also known as just Legacy Styles, are progressive styles that unlock as part of Fortnite Crew. They were first introduced in Chapter 4: Season 4, with Shimmerdusk's styles.

Just like other Fortnite Crew cosmetics, all cosmetics with Crew Legacy Styles are Crew Series. Starting with December 2024, Crew Legacy Styles are added during the month rather than before release.

Every Outfit as part of Fortnite Crew gets 5 Crew Legacy Styles that can be unlocked by subscribing to Fortnite Crew for multiple months. Back Blings and Pickaxes typically get Legacy Styles alongside their associated Outfit.

  • 1 month of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - A Legacy Style specific to the Crew Pack
  • 2 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Auric Legacy Style
  • 3 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Phase Legacy Style
  • 4 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Rimebound Legacy Style
  • 5 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Cosmic Legacy Style

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Similar concepts have existed previously, such as Super Level Styles and other Crew Legacy Sets.
    • Crew Legacy Styles are also similar to The First Shadows' styles, which required the player to own all 3 Outfits to unlock.
  • Huntress' Duskblade didn't get Legacy Styles, even though Shimmerdusk and Gothic Gearpack did.
    • Despite this, Huntress' Duskblade recieved the Crew Series rarity in Update v33.30, before other Crew cosmetics without Legacy Styles got the rarity.
