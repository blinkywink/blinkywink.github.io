Iron Man Zero is a Marvel Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Iron Man Zero Bundle in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks. Iron Man Zero was first released in Chapter 3: Season 4 and is part of the Zero War Set.

Iron Man Zero can also be obtained by redeeming all codes found in the Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War comic books.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Iron Man Zero's appearances?
Iron Man Zero has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
