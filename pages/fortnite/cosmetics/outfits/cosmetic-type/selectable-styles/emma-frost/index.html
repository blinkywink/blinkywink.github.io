Emma Frost was a neutral Boss introduced during Chapter 5: Season 4. She could have been found in The Raft.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Emma Frost dropped the following items when eliminated. She also dropped 12 Bars.

Emma Frost's Striker Burst AR Emma Frost's Reveal Medallion
Modular Hyper SMG Flowberry Fizz

Emma Frost uses a Rare Modular Striker Burst Rifle and Modular Harbinger SMG to attack players. She will not drop either weapon when eliminated.

Modular Striker Burst Rifle Modular Harbinger SMG

The Rare Modular Striker Burst Rifle is prefixed with the Red Eye Sight, Speed Mag, Speedgrip, and Muzzle Brake Weapon Mods.
The Rare Modular Harbinger SMG is prefixed with no Weapon Mods.

Behavior

Behavior[]

DEFEAT EMMA FROST![]

Emma Frost will be sitting on her throne inside her room at the end of the cellhall, patiently waiting for any interaction and leaning forward should the player get close enough to her. While she sits on the throne she will not get up and attack the player on sight unless she is attacked, they damage her throne, or they get too close to her as she sits.

Emma Frost sitting on her throne
Emma Frost leaning forward as a player gets closer
Emma Frost getting up off her throne

If Emma Frost is found patrolling she will not attack the player on sight unless she is attacked, they block her patrol path for too long, or they aim their weapon at her for too long.

Special Abilities

Special Abilities[]

  • Organic Diamond Form
    • Emma Frost hardens her entire physical form into an organic diamond body, granting 100% damage resistance while still being able to move and fight. This form will naturally wear off 17 seconds after casting.
      • Weakspots: Certain parts of Emma Frost's body will flash red when her diamond form is activated. Attacking these weak points will bypass her damage resistance and hitting it enough will force her out of her diamond form. Staggering her for a brief moment before she can fight again. There can only be one weak point present each time Emma Frost's Organic Diamond Form is active.
        • These weak points can appear on either of her knees, elbows, and head.
      • While in this state, Emma Frost will switch from using her Modular Striker Burst Rifle to her Modular Harbinger SMG.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Emma Frost's Boss Outfit ID is Character_PiperShelf_Pearl_NPC.
    • The Epic TBD Outfit itself switches her styles around. Having her Organic Diamond Frost style be the default while being unnamed, and her default style be the second style named non-diamond.
      • In Update v31.10, Emma Frost's TBD Boss Outfit's non-diamond style was given partial outlining. Likely an accidental holdover from the release Outfit getting Super Level Styles.
      • In Update v31.20, the outlining was removed.
    • Emma Frost's TBD Boss Outfit is the first to retain her release Outfit's unique Lobby pose.
  • Emma Frost's Organic Diamond Form can be broken without hitting the weakpoints by repeatedly hitting her with Captain America's Shield, Shuri's Black Panther Claws, or the Chainsaw.
  • Once The Storm hits Emma Frost, she will begin to turn on her Latverian Tactical Grunts, attacking them on sight. Why this happens is unknown, but it is likely rooted to how the Latverian Guards are coded.
