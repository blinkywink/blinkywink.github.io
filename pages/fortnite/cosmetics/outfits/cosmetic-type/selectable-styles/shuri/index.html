”
— Shuri

Shuri is the Princess of Wakanda from Reality: 616. She is the sister of T'Challa and a member of the Wakandan Royal Family, but also the new Black Panther.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War[]

In Wakanda, Jones, Wolverine and Spider-Man are attacked by Wakandan guards, whom Shuri commands to stop. The group enters Wakanda and The Imagined explains that her armor traced energy signals from The Zero Shard. Shuri takes them to the Vibranium vault, saying that if the Zero Shard is there, they're allowed to take it. Wolverine smells the Zero Shard, noticing that someone has recently taken it. They get to the Serpent Valley and are attacked by Hybrids. One of them escape and blow up a dome where the Zero Shard's smell comes from.

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

Shuri arrives on the island following the arrival of Doom to fight him and his kingdom. She has now also reunited with Captain Jones and is working with The Underground, along with other characters from her reality such as War Machine and Gwenpool. At the beginning of the season, Shuri sent Gwenpool to gather data at the Raft regarding some rift technology.

During the Absolute Doom Event, As the loopers were battling Doom, Hope and Shuri were working together to fix the final Stark Rift Beacon.

When Dooms Armour was damaged enough Shuri activated the rift beacon, stripping doom of his Pandora power and rifted him back to his own reality

After this , it is presumed that Shuri, along with the other Marvel heroes went back to their own reality
