Six String is a Legendary Back Bling in Fortnite that can be purchased with Power Chord in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks with the Volume 11 Bundle for 2,700 V-Bucks or with Italk's Locker Bundle for 2,100 V-Bucks. Six String was first released in Season 3 and is part of the Volume 11 Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Six String's appearances?
Six String has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia[]

  • In Update v12.41, Six String received a new style, Rogue.
  • The Six String is inspired by the real life Dean ML X guitar.
  • The design could possibly be in reference to Darrel "Dimebag" Abbots from Pantera as the item features his signature lightning pattern.
