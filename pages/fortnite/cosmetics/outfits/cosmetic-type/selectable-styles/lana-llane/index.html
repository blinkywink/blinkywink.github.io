Outfit[]

“
The Island's Weapon-O-Matic mastermind.
”
— In-Game Description

Lana Llane is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during February 2024, as part of the February 2024 Fortnite Crew Pack. Lana Llane was released during Chapter 5: Season 1 and is part of the Llana Origins Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Llamatic Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 1 additional month
  • Auric Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 2 additional month
  • Phase Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 3 additional months
  • Rimebound Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 4 additional months
  • Cosmic Legacy - Subscribe to the Crew for 5 additional months
