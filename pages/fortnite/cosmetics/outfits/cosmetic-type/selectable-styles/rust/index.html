Outfit[]

“
Shred the wasteland.
”
— In-Game Description

Rust is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 3, then spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 5: Season 3 Battle Pass. Rust is part of the Badlands Scavenger Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Corrosive Rust - Page 4 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)
  • Helmet - On - Page 4 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)
  • Oxide Rust - Complete 8 Weekly Quests : Week 3-4 (Quest Rewards)
  • Cold Fusion Rust - Page 4 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
  • Neon Hazard Rust - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
  • Digital Ghost Rust - Page 6 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)

Trivia[]

  • Rust's name as a Fall Guys Bean is changed to Metal Lord.
