Trivia

Trivia[]

  • According to Drew Hill, Major Mancake was Mancake's original design.[1]
  • One of his style's names, "The Cake With No Name", is a reference to the movie The Good the Bad and the Ugly, after a character called "The Man With No Name".

Mancake is a humanoid-pancake gunslinger and outlaw from Reality: 3258. He was brough to Reality Zero by Agent Jones to not let anyone escape The Loop.

Personality

Personality[]

Mancake is reminiscent of a typical Wild West outlaw. He mentions that he will respect anyone if they respect him. He seems to have a calm behavior and doesn't want any trouble with the sheriff.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Jones arrives at Butter Barn from Reality: 3258, and finds Mancake. After ordering a meal, he uses his Portal Device and bring Mancake to Reality Zero. While wandering in the forest, Kratos appears in front of Mancake, and eliminates Dire. After Kratos looks at him, Mancake runs away scaredly.

Zero Crisis Finale[]

Among the chaos between Hunters, Mancake spots Jones and tries to attack him, but misses. he is attacked by Kratos as the two get into a fight.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 6[]

Mancake's snapshot and a group of Loopers fight against Harley Quinn, who easily manages to defeat them.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Mancake builds a new Butter Barn on the new Island.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

Mancake joins The Resistance's Foodie Battalion led by Guaco. He spies on the Imagined Order in Rocky Reels.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

After the Butter Barn gets takenover by the Reality Tree and turned into Neo Butter Barn, Mancake fully moves to Rocky Reels.
