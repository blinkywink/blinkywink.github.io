Countess Daraku was an ancient vampire who pretended to be an international rockstar. She was partially introduced during Chapter 4: Season 1 before being fully introduced during Chapter 4: Season 4.

She is a minor antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale serving as the secondary antagonist of Chapter 4: Season 4.

Personality

Personality[]

The Countess appears at first glance to be a rockstar who appreciates her fans and is very friendly with them, however she hides a very cunning and manipulative personality, using her fans as meals. When her biggest fans, The High Stakes Club, discovered who she really was, she tried to break them psychologically one by one by showing them their respective weaknesses, showing a sadistic and cruel side.

History

History[]

Before Chapter 4[]

Countess Daraku is a very ancient cunning vampire who loves to play with her prey. Her last known activity was acting as an internationally renowned rock star. However, not knowing her true identity The High Stakes Club, a young group of vampire hunters became her biggest fans. At the same time, Victoria Saint, an experienced vampire hunter and Shimmerdusk's former apprentice, pursued her for a long time.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

After a short time meeting with Daraku, The High Stakes Club finally leaned the truth about her as well as the fact that they were her new targets. They became deeply disappointed and enraged, but Victoria Saint decided to train them in order to prepare them for the fight against her. A bit later, during Fortnitemares 2023, The Looper joined forces with the Club and Victoria Saint, showing their great fighting skills. During the final confrontation The High Stakes Club and Victoria Saint managed to eliminate The Countess for good thanks to the valuable help of The Looper.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kado Thorne and Countess Daraku were both eliminated thanks to The Looper.
