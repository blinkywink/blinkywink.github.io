Trivia

Trivia[]

  • When breaking obstacles, Oscar wields the Poacher's Regrets as his Pickaxe.
  • Oscar's use of the Knife Sharpener Emote and Cluster Clingers likely calls back to his Enragement Ability during Chapter 5: Season 1.
    • While Oscar still throws Cluster Clingers like when he was Enraged before, he lacks the Combat Music and voicelines he had prior, does not grant the Who's the Boss? Elimination Accolade when defeated, and isn't taller like other Bosses.
      • This is probably due to the fact that to trigger his fight and be rewarded with his Mythic weapon, he must be challenged to a duel, unlike other Character Boss hybrids.
  • Oscar is the first Character to have no shields. Possibly due to the loss of his Medallion after it was stolen by The Underground.
    • To compensate, his health equates to the total amount of health and shield he used to have during Chapter 5: Season 1. Not counting the shields regenerated after casting Enragement.

Oscar is one of the latest members of The Society who was introduced in Chapter 5: Season 1. He is a rich and wealthy tiger, a hunter, a gardener and the owner of Lavish Lair.

He is a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as one of the main antagonists of Chapter 5: Season 1.

Personality

Personality[]

Oscar is a ruthless and fierce tiger who doesn't hesitate to get his hands dirty to get what he wants. As noted by Nisha, he fight for nothing but himself. Although originally a riffraff, Oscar acts and speaks in a distinguished manner and enjoys luxury. Because of his pride Oscar is the type of person who wants to be respected, and will not give his interest to anyone if they do not know him. He also seems to believe that force prevails over all else. Following his belief, he accepted his own weakness and that of The Society in his defeat and doesn't hold a grudge against Hope and Jones for this, preferring to move on and focus on the future in order to regain everything he lost and more.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Oscar is a muscular and humanoid tiger in suit. His regular fur colour is orange with black stripes, but changes to white with black stripes in his Champion style and to dark blue with glowing blue stripes in his Shock Brawler form.

History

History[]

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

Oscar was originally a riffraff[8] who fought in the streets to get what he wanted[9]. He later went to do underground fights in a unknown underground fight club[9] for the same purpose, before becoming a hunter for The Society. Through The Society Oscar would make a name for himself and gradually gain more wealth and slowly climb up the ranks, before ultimately being offered a place as a boss of The Society. He became the owner of Lavish Lair now with his own Society Guards to command. Later, like the other bosses, he held Peely hostage for a while and blackmailed Jones so they could possibly get more information on Pandora's Box from him.[6] Later Hope and The Looper defeated him and would steal his medallion away.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

After Valeria opened Pandora's Box, Zeus and the Olympians arrive to the Island to punish the mortals. With Zeus planning to eliminate them all starting with the mortals who originally caused this mess, that being The Society Oscar would side with Montague and the other Society members as they fought against the gods, but ended up losing forcing them into hiding.[10] Artemis would continue to hunt down Oscar forcing him to constant run.[11].

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

With the gods having fled the island after the fall of Zeus, Oscar came out of hiding. Vowing to take back what he has lost once more.
