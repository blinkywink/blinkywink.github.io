Ziggy is a superhero, a member of The Supernovas on Supernova's Formation Theta, a professional napper, a graduated student from the Supernova Academy and the owner of Ziggy's Surf & Skate.

Ziggy is a supporting character of Chapter 6: Season 3.

Personality

Personality[]

Ziggy is the embodiment of laziness and the true master of slacking off. Ziggy may be useless in battles, but his sheer presence alone is a great morale booster for everyone in Supernova Academy. He is the go to guy to when you need someone to bring snacks into classes. Everyone loves Ziggy.
