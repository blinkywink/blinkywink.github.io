Back Board is an Epic Back Bling in Fortnite. Back Board was first released in Season 9 and is part of the Hang Time Set.

Back Board has been obtainable as a reward from Downtown Drop Challenges, Zone Wars Challenges, Creative Curse Challenges, Love & War Challenges, FNCS: Chapter 2: Season 2 Twitch Drops, The Spy Within Quests, Play Your Way Quests! and High Octane Quests.

How To Unlock?[]

Expand
  • Sweaty - Complete all Downtown Drop Challenges (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Banana - Find Jonesy near the basketball court, near the rooftops, and in the back of a truck (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Cuddle - Search O-N-F-I-R-E Letters (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Disco - Dance or emote at the basketball court (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Drift - Find 2 hidden shortcuts (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Fishstick - Jump through all 6 flaming hoops (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Food - Spray an ice cream truck (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Kevin - Hit any of the trickjumps on either the crane, elevated train, or fence (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Rex - Change the color of 1000 tiles (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Slurp - Collect a basketball, coin, and shoe in a single match (Downtown Drop Challenges)
  • Zone Wars - Complete all Zone Wars Challenges (Zone Wars Challenges)
  • Zombie - Complete all Retribution quests (Creative Curse Challenges)
  • Mastermind - Complete all Mansion of Power quests (Creative Curse Challenges)
  • Phantom - Complete all Gun Fright quests (Creative Curse Challenges)
  • Nosh - Complete the Infernum creative island and collect the pumpkin in the last room (Creative Curse Challenges)
  • Halloween - Complete all Creative Curse Challenges (Creative Curse Challenges)
  • Metal Masq - Complete 5 War challenges (Love & War Challenges)
  • Love - Complete 5 Love challenges (Love & War Challenges)
  • Henchman - C2S2 FNCS Twitch Drops
  • The Spy Within - Complete all The Spy Within quests (The Spy Within Quests)
  • Brite Boarder - Complete 1 Play Your Way Quests (Play Your Way Quests!)
  • Cuddle Kickflip - Complete 4 Play Your Way Quests (Play Your Way Quests!)
  • Goofy Fin - Complete 7 Play Your Way Quests (Play Your Way Quests!)
  • Llamasploded - Complete 10 Play Your Way Quests (Play Your Way Quests!)
  • Aerial Assist - Complete 2 Octane Quests (High Octane Quests)
  • Battle-car Blaze - Complete 4 Octane Quests (High Octane Quests)
  • 'Brella Beach - Complete 6 Octane Quests (High Octane Quests)
  • Fifty-Fifty - Complete 8 Octane Quests (High Octane Quests)
