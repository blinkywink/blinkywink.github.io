Outfit[]

“
All right, people, let's start at the beginning one last time.
”
— In-Game Description

Spider-Gwen is a Marvel Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 10, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 4 Battle Pass. Spider-Gwen is part of the Spider-Gwen Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Spider-Gwen's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Prior to the release of this LEGO® Style in Update v30.10, equipping Spider-Gwen in LEGO Fortnite would instead equip a random default outfit's LEGO® style.
