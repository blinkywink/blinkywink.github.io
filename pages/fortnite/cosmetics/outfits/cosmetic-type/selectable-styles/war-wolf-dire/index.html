Outfit[]

“
Howl into the Unknown.
”

War Wolf Dire is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 5 of the Season 6 OG Pass. War Wolf Dire is part of the Blood Moon Set.
