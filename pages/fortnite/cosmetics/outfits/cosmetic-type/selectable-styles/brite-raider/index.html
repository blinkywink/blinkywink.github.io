Outfit[]

“
Drive fast. Burn bright.
”
— In-Game Description

Brite Raider is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be unlocked by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 11, then spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 5: Season 3 Battle Pass. Brite Raider is part of the Sandstorms and Rainbows Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Pastel Brite Raider - Page 12 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)
  • Brite Raider (Helmet On) - Page 11 of the Battle Pass ( 9 Battle Stars)

  • Cold Fusion Brite Raider - Page 4 of the Bonus Rewards ( 20 Battle Stars)
  • Neon Hazard Brite Raider - Page 5 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
  • Digital Ghost Brite Raider - Page 6 of the Bonus Rewards ( 25 Battle Stars)
