Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Visitor is the first revealed member of The Seven.
  • According to a tweet from Donald Mustard, The Visitor was originally going to be called "The Stranger"[2].
  • This was notably one of the most difficult outfits to get in the game, requiring players to search 7 Chests in Risky Reels. The location was being flooded with players attempting to do the challenge, and the location had very few chests, along with not guaranteed chest spawns.
  • His back bling, Offworld Rig, along with The Scientist's back bling are possibly life-support systems with built-in flight components.
  • Unlike other members of The Seven, He, The Paradigm and The Scientist are the only members who don't have "One of The Seven" in their Outfit description.
    • Oddly enough he used to have it but it was removed sometime during Chapter 2: Season 1.

The Visitor is a member of The Seven, an explorer and navigational expert who helps chart the best course of action for the team.[1]

He is a major character of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the main protagonist of Season 4 and OG Chapter 1: Season 4, a supporting character of during The End (Chapter 2), and a major protagonist of Chapter 3.

Personality

Personality[]

The Visitor is a very quiet person, except with people he knows well such as The Seven. Unlike The Scientist and The Imagined, who sometimes do as they please and are willing to oppose The Origin, The Visitor acts according to orders and takes no sides, particularly regarding the case of Paradigm[2], although he wanted to end this infighting. Timeline

Timeline[]

Season 4[]

The Visitor first arrived in a Meteor that crashed near Dusty Depot. The Government built a research facility immediately within the crater. Over the weeks, the meteorite that housed The Visitor inside was slowly being excavated, which eventually opened up, allowing him to escape. He wrecks some of the facility's tech and leaves Dusty Divot. He went to Villain Lair and modified a movie prop rocket by using Hop Rocks.

Blast Off[]

The Visitor launched the rocket and it began to fly out of the island. After The Visitor coordinated the rocket, its lasers targeted Tilted Towers and opened a Sideways Rift above the location. The rocket then opened a rift above Moisty Mire and flew towards Wailing Woods before turning towards Greasy Grove and opening a rift near there. The rocket then opened a rift at Loot Lake and then out of the rift and blasted off into the sky, opening a massive Sideways Rift above Salty Springs, which overtime began to grow to extend to points above some locations.

Season X[]

The Imagined Order get help from The Seven to fix The Zero Point as The Scientist arrives in a new Meteor.[4]. The Visitor along with The Scientist settled their base in one of the Dusty Depot hangars, making it their laboratory[5]. The Scientist, presumably with the help of The Visitor started to build Rift Beacons, to open 6 Rifts that would allow the other members of the Seven to come with their Rockets in due time.

After building the Rocket, The Visitor left Reality Zero, while The Scientist entered The Loop, as he had to be inside to trigger the device[6]. The Scientist having left tapes for himself. When the time came during The End, The Visitor came with his own rocket from one of the Rift just like the rest of The Seven, except Paradigm. With the help of other Rockets he unfreeze the meteor and teleport it to the top of the Zero Point to make it collide with and contain the Zero Point's explosion before leaving via a Rift. However, something unexpected happened and the Zero Point became a Black Hole.[7]

The End[]

After the attack of The Last Reality starts and that The Foundation managed to free Jones and turn off the gyro system of The Islands, The Scientist and The Visitor fly near Guava Fort to save the remaining Loopers using an IO Hatch to get them inside The Bridge, which they believed safe. The Visitor guards the surface, eliminating the Caretaker forces before leaving as The Scientist and the Loopers go inside.[8]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

The Visitor was assigned to his post in Launchpad and assisted in launching and constructing retrieval rockets as the navigational expert of The Seven.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

The Visitor was still in Launchpad and was actively trying to track The Paradigm's position, wanting to stop the infighting within the team, stating that the team will be doomed if it continues.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

The Visitor helps The Resistance attack an IO Outpost to reach The Bridge. He comes up with the idea to use The Paradigm's Mecha against the Imagined Order. After The Seven attacks another outpost to steal an IO shuttle, he stays on the island alongside The Origin and The Order.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #5[]

The Visitor joins The Seven's ground forces and helps to destroy the shield generators.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

With the war against the Imagined Order won, The Visitor contemplates retiring from The Seven, wanting to settle down on the island as a Permanent Resident.[9]

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

The Visitor, The Origin, The Paradigm and The Scientist experiment on a Chrome sample that breaks out and covers the underground biolab. In order, The Scientist, The Origin and The Visitor all get Chromed but The Paradigm barely makes it out and flies over to The Zero Point.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

During the Looper and AMIE's attempt to test Stellan's Rift Gate by creating a smaller version based on Stellan's model, the place where AMIE's test subjects are sent, happens to be the place where all the Chromed people ended up after being Chromed, including the Scientist. Once returning, the AMIE fragment completely loses their mind as they discover that The Scientist's existence and that of all the others are being sculpting into something new.[10]

Chapter 7: Season 2[]

During the story, a holotable recording from the original Visitor can be found, where it is revealed that he was among the Seven members who were lost to the chrome.
