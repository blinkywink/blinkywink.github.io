Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Wild Card's appearances?
Wild Card has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Wild Card Suits, formerly known as Cold Blooded Suits, were neutral Guards in Fortnite. They were introduced during Chapter 4: Season 1.

Description

Description[]

Cold Blooded Suits could be found in Cold Blooded-controlled POIs and would not attack on sight unless the player damaged them, their Bosses, or had a Heat Level above 1.

In Chapter 4: Season 2, they were renamed to Wild Card Suits. Two would spawn at various locations mid-match alongside a Highcard Boss, patrolling behind him. Like the previous season, Wild Card Suits would not attack on sight unless they or their Boss was attacked.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Cold Blooded Suits' Epic Outfit ID is Character_BlingHearts_NPC. The Outfit has no name in the files.
    • As Hearts is in the codename, the Hearts style on the original Wild Card Outfit is the default style for this unreleased Outfit.
    • The Outfit uses the updated version of Wild Card's model used by GHOST Wildcard.
    • In Update v24.00, the Cold Blooded Suits' Outfit was given two new styles. A Red Diamond style used exclusively by the Highcard Bosses, and a Silver style that goes unused but was possibly intended for the Wild Card Suits.
  • Cold Blooded Suits originally spawned with all of the original Wild Card edit styles excluding the Lucky Card style during Chapter 4: Season 1. But during Chapter 4: Season 2 and Chapter 4: Season 3 as Wild Card Suits, they only spawn with the Diamonds style.
