Turk is a GHOST Agent part of the E.G.O. team. He was contracted by the by the Imagined Order to explore and map Apollo Island.

He is a Supporting Character of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as one of the main protagonists of Chapter 2: Season 1, a supporting character in Chapter 2: Season 6 and a minor antagonist alongside Riptide.

Overview

Overview[]

Turk is an GHOST agent and one of the main members of the now disbanded GHOST team: E.G.O. He and his team were contracted mercenaries hired by the Imagined Order to investigate the newly formed Apollo Island after the events of The End. However, independently, SHADOW, a "rival faction" (actually the second half of Midas' Crew), sent its own agents, the A.L.T.E.R. team, which would trigger a conflict that turned into an entire faction war.

Outside of this, Turk is a highly experienced survivalist (one of the best of E.G.O.) and fisherman, as well as the rival of Outcast, another GHOST agent who was part of E.G.O. and sent alongside him in mission.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

Turk is sent to Apollo by the Imagined Order. They discover the new island but are met with "enemy" agents from A.L.T.E.R. team who strangely look like E.G.O. members.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

As a GHOST Agent, Turk participated in the faction war between GHOST and SHADOW although his exact involvement is unknown.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

While GHOST and SHADOW are laying low, Turk is on the trail of The Big One: the fish that he believes will net him the title of Top Fisherman and beat his rival: Outcast.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Turk listens to Mari's radio broadcast on the road, and gives her suggestions on which animals might be aliens. He comes up with a name for the broadcast: Hot Saucers.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

Because of his loss of income, Riptide teamed up with his Alter-ego from long ago: Turk, to capture the Coral Buddies and sell them to a wealthy collector. This caused them to come into conflict with the people of Atlantis.[1]

The Looper would help Atlantis and enter into conflicts with them, resulting in a lost battle for both agents against The Looper, forcing them to reveal the location of the kidnapped Coral Buddies.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

With Midas' return, Turk (and Riptide) are presumed to have gone fully back to their spy and military activities by returning upon Marigold's call to reunite the entire Crew.
