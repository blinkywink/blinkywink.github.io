Lady Windfrost is a neutral Guard in Fortnite: Battle Royale. She was introduced during Chapter 7: Season 2.

Description

Description[]

Lady Windfrost will always follow behind the Ice King wherever he spawns. She will only attack the player on sight if they attack her or the Ice King, or they aim their weapon at her for too long.

When the Ice King is damaged, Lady Windfrost will occasionally throw a Chug Splash to heal him. She will be instantly eliminated once Ice King is defeated.

Loot[]

Lady Windfrost drops the following items when eliminated.
