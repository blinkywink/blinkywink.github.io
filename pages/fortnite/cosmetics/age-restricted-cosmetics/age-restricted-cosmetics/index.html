Age Restricted Cosmetics

Overview

With the release of the Update v27.10, every experience published in Fortnite received its own age and content rating. A small portion of items became not compatible with E or E10+ (or regionally equivalent) game ratings.

If a player equips one of these items and joins an island below the designated rating threshold, their equipped item will appear as an item that’s compatible with the island’s rating (For example, their Outfit will appear as a default Outfit, and Jam Tracks will play the instrumental instead).

Below is a complete list of every cosmetic item, that cannot be used in some Fortnite: Creative experiences and game modes made by Epic Games.
