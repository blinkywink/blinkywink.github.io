Contrails

Overview

As of May 14th 2026, there are 250[1] Contrails In-Game
“
Look stylish while in free fall.
”
— In-Game Description

Contrails are Cosmetic Items introduced in Season 4. They can be used in Battle Royale and Creative.

Additional features available for Contrails

Additional features available for Contrails[]
Selectable Styles Reactive
  • Holly and Divey was the first Contrail with styles.
  • Inversion was the first reactive Contrail.
  • Lightning Dropper was the first Contrail to play music.
