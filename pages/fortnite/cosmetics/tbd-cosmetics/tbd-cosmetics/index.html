TBD Cosmetics

Overview

This page is about an item that is likely to never be released as a Cosmetic Item.

TBD Cosmetics are Cosmetic Items in the game files that have been surfaced and obtained by experienced data-miners, from the in-game files of Fortnite. TBD Cosmetics generally use placeholder images and are unfinished. It is unknown if any of these Cosmetic Items will ever release. TBD Cosmetics are different to Scrapped Cosmetics and Missing Cosmetics as they are still present in the files.

NOTICE 1: Some of the Cosmetic Items shown use Unofficial Names and Unofficial Images as they are either named TBD, NPC or nothing at all and use Placeholder images. All Unofficial Names will be Italicized.

NOTICE 2: Some of the Cosmetic Items shown were already released as separate Cosmetic Items or as styles to existing Cosmetics.

Missing Cosmetics[]

Main Article: Missing Cosmetics

These are cosmetic items in Fortnite that exist beyond their concept stages, but are currently missing from the files, and thus unreleased (and in some occasions, scrapped).

Massive Datamining Incidents[]

Main Article: Massive Datamining Incidents

In Fortnite's history, a few large datamining incidents have occurred. These revealed a large amount of IDs for potential future cosmetics, some of which were scrapped and never likely to be added.
