Overview

This article is about YouTube Drops. For Twitch Drops, see Twitch Drops.

YouTube Drops are a category of cosmetic items which are acquired by watching a certain amount of Fortnite content on the YouTube website.

Acquisition

Acquisition[]

Tune in to Official Fortnite Channel during the competitions to earn Drops. To ensure you're eligible for Drops on YouTube, link your Epic account and Google account within 14 days of claiming your Drop reward to be processed.
