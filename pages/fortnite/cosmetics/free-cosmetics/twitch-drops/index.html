Overview

This article is about Twitch Drops. For YouTube Drops, see YouTube Drops.

Twitch Drops are a category of cosmetic items which are acquired by watching a certain amount of Fortnite content on the Twitch website.

Acquisition

Acquisition[]

Tune in to https://watch.fortnite.com, or any Drops-supported Twitch channel during the competitions to earn Drops. To ensure you're eligible for Drops on Twitch, link your Epic account and Twitch account within 14 days of claiming your Drop reward to be processed.
