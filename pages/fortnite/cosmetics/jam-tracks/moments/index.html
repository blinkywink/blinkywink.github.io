Game Moments are a feature in Fortnite that were added in Chapter 6: Season 2.

Functionality

Functionality[]

All Jam Tracks and Lobby Music can be used as a Game Moment. Players can select a track in the Locker to play when dropping from the Battle Bus as Intro Music, and another for earning a Victory Royale as Celebration Music. The chosen track plays only for the player, even when spectating. In split-screen, Player 1's track takes priority.

Musical Contrails will override Intro Music when the player is dropping from the Battle Bus, but not when the player is Storm Surfing.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • To accommodate for Moments, the deploy and ambient SFX volume of certain Gliders was reduced.
  • My entering straight into an area that forces glider redeploy from the bus (Springfield Tire Fire, the Floating Island's tornado, etc.), it's possible to loop the intro music indefinitely.
