Overview

Runamok is a Jam Track in Fortnite that could have been obtained on Page 9 of the Chapter 6: Season 2 Battle Pass.

Runamok could have also been obtained by purchasing it in the Item Shop for 500 V-Bucks.

Item Shop Appearances[]

  • February 28th 2025 - Part 1 - 464 days ago - Last Seen/Release Date
Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Runamok was the song used in the Chapter 6: Season 2 trailer.
  • Runamok was released on streaming platforms on February 21st 2025, making it the first Fortnite original song to be released on streaming.
    • The third verse is altered in the streaming version to remove all references to Fortnite.
  • Runamok was accidentally put into the Item Shop, but was removed from it on the same day. Anyone who bought it from the Shop had it removed from their account and refunded.
