Overview

This article is about Jam Track. For the Outfit of the same name, see Bones (Outfit).
The media represented on this page has been created by a second/third party.
This content is not originally part of the game but has been introduced through a special collaboration.
This Jam Track shares its music with another cosmetic.
The music used in this Jam Track can also be heard in Boney Bounce.

Bones is a Jam Track in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 500 V-Bucks or with the Imagine Dragons Jam Bundle for 1,100 V-Bucks. Bones was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Bones's appearances?
Bones has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
