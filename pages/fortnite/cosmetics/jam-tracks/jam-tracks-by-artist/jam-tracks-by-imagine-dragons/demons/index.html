Demons is a Jam Track in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 500 V-Bucks or with the Imagine Dragons Jam Bundle for 1,100 V-Bucks. Demons was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Demons's appearances?
Demons has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
