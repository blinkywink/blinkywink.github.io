Overview

This article is about a Jam Track. For a Chapter 2: Season 2 Battle Pass Loading Screen, see Eye of the Storm (Loading Screen). For a Save the World Resource, see Eye of the Storm.

Eye of the Storm is a Jam Track in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 500 V-Bucks or with the FNCS C6S3 Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Eye of the Storm was first released in Chapter 6: Season 3.

Eye of the Storm could also have been obtained for free by ranking high enough in the Champion Crystal FNCS Cup Tournament on July 19th 2025.
