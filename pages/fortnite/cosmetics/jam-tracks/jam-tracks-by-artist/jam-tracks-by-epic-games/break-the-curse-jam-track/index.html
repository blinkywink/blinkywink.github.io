Overview

This article is about the Jam Track. For the Jujutsu Kaisen Limited-Time Event, see Break the Curse!. For the unreleased Lobby Music, see Break the Curse (Lobby Music).

Break the Curse is a Jam Track in Fortnite that could have been obtained on Bonus Rewards Page 5 in the Chapter 6: Season 1 Battle Pass.
