Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics

Overview

Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics are a type of cosmetic that show a player's rank from a given season. They can be obtained through Ranked Urgent Quests, Ranked Cups, or the Item Shop, depending on what type of cosmetic they are.

These cosmetics tend to have styles that can be unlocked by ranking up within a given season. At the end of a season, new styles can no longer be unlocked.[n 1] The styles unlock based on your highest rank in any Ranked mode[n 2], including Battle Royale, Reload, Fortnite OG, their Zero Build variants, Rocket Racing, and Ballistic.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Starting with the release of Felina, ranked cosmetics now track your rank in all modes, not just Battle Royale.
  • Felina was shown in the Masked Victory spray over a month before she was added to the files.
  • Scrap Slicer was mistakenly said to show off your rank.
    • Blade of Ages however does actually show off your rank, despite the fact that it is also a pickaxe.
  • All Seasonal Ranked Umbrellas have been remixes of the current Season Win Umbrella.
    • Competitor's Time Brella, Ranker's Junker 'Brella, and Ranker's Doom Brella's names directly reference this.
    • Competitor's Skyblades is a remix of Snowblades, an Item Shop glider, which means all Seasonal Ranked Gliders are remixes of other Gliders too.
  • The style icons for Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics use the symbols for the Bronze III, Silver III, Gold III, Platinum III, Diamond III, Elite, Champion, and Unreal ranks.
  • Many Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics did not originally have styles.
    • High Society Ranker was the first to have styles until Update v28.20, when they were removed.
    • All existing Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics, except Burn Bright, gained styles in Update v29.20, and all new ones except Vanguard's Charge since have had styles from the start.
      • When High Society Ranker's styles were added back in Update v29.20, they used updated icons.

Release

  • Season 6 currently has the record for most Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics introduced in one season, with 6.
    • This is followed by Season 4, with 4.
    • Season 0 and Season 1 are tied for least, with only 1 each.
    • This is then followed by Season 3 and Season 8, with 2 each.
  • Chapter 4: Season 3 is the only Battle Royale Season since the release of Ranked to not release any Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics.
    • This is because it was continuing Season Zero from Chapter 4: Season 2.
  • Some cosmetic types have been getting released every season.
    • Every season since Season 1 has released a Back Bling available through Ranked Urgent Quests.
      • These used to be more difficult to unlock, but this was changed in Season 2.
    • Every season since Season 2 has released an Umbrella
      • Until Season 9, these were all available from a Ranked Cup.
    • Every season from Season 4 until Season 6 has released an Outfit of Epic rarity.
      • With the exception of Felina, thy were all Item Shop Outfits.
    • Burn Bright, Competitor's Skyblades, Blade of Ages, The Perfection, Felina's Claw, and Vanguard's Charge do not fall into a category.
      • Competitor's Skyblades is a Glider, but not an Umbrella.
      • Despite this, all of them were obtained from ways that other Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics have been.
  • Vanguard's Charge was the only Seasonal Ranked Cosmetic to be obtained from quests besides Ranked Urgent Quests, prior to the Felina Quests.
  • The Perfection and Burn Bright are the only Seasonal Ranked Cosmetics to be obtained from Ranked Urgent Quests that aren't Back Blings.
    • This was until Season 9, with the Ranker's Vaultbrella.
  • In Season 2, the ranked Glider you got depended on which session of a Ranked Cup you participated in.
    • The first sessions awarded Competitor's Skyblades while the second sessions awarded Competitor's Time Brella.
    • In later seasons, any session has granted the seasonal Umbrella.
  • Each Ranked Pickaxe has been obtainable in a different way.
    • Blade of Ages was available from the Item Shop.
    • The Perfection was available from Season 6 Ranked Urgent Quests.
    • Felina's Claw was available from Felina Quests.
