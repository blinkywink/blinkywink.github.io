Collaboration Mashup Cosmetics

Overview

This article is about the fusion of cosmetics resulting from collaboration. For the fusion of cosmetics resulting from two original Fortnite cosmetics, see Mashup Cosmetics.

Collaboration Mashup Cosmetics are a collection of Cosmetic Items in Fortnite, consisting of cosmetics resulting from a Mashup between a Collaboration character and Fortnite. The concept was introduced in Chapter 2: Season 2.

To enter this category, a cosmetic must be at least one of the following:

  • A Fortnite cosmetic with attributes of a character from another franchise, if not a pre-existing collaboration cosmetic.
  • A cosmetic based on a character from another franchise with attributes of a pre-existing Fortnite original cosmetic.
  • A character from another franchise with a Fortnite original design.

Super Level Styles don't count as a Fortnite original design, and neither do stylistic choices such as shading, or most outlines. Most Icon Series Outfits don't count either.
