Sidekicks

Overview

This article is about the "Pet" Cosmetic Items in Battle Royale. For the Back Blings, see Pets.
As of May 20th 2026, there are 45[1] Sidekicks In-Game

Sidekicks are Cosmetic Items introduced in Chapter 6: Mini Season 2. They can be used in Battle Royale, Save the World, Creative, LEGO Fortnite and Fortnite Festival.
