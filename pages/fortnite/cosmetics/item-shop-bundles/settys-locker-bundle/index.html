Overview

Setty's Locker Bundle is a Rare Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,800 V-Bucks. Setty's Locker Bundle was first released in Chapter 4: Season 4 and contains cosmetics that Setty has picked out.
