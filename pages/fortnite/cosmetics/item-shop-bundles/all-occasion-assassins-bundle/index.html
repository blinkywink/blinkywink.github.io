The All Occasion Assassins Bundle is an Epic Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,700 V-Bucks. All Occasion Assassins Bundle was first released in Chapter 4: Season 2 and contains various assassins and their matching Back Blings.
