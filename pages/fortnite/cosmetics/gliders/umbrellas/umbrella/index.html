Overview

The Umbrellas are a type of glider players may equip and use for Fortnite: Battle Royale. Umbrellas are cosmetic items, having no advantage. They function identically to all other Gliders.

Umbrellas were introduced during Pre-Season, the first being The Umbrella.

Obtaining[]

Players can obtain Umbrellas by achieving a Victory Royale, with a new Umbrella being released every season since Season 2, gotten from winning a match in Battle Royale or Zero Build, whether Solo, Duos, Trios, or Squads. If not already owned, The Umbrella will be unlocked alongside the current season's Umbrella.

Umbrellas can also obtained by earning 50 points during a Ranked Cup during a given season, with a new one releasing every season starting in Chapter 4: Season OG. Umbrellas earned this way show off your rank from the season they're from.

Occasionally, Umbrellas can also be obtained by participating in events, completing limited-time challenges, or other unlock requirements.

The Umbrella, The Rezzbrella, and Founder's Umbrella (if the player is a Founder) are the only Umbrellas that are always unlockable.

Trivia[]

  • Prior to Chapter 2: Season 3, all Umbrellas were Common Rarity
    • Since Chapter 2 Remix. all new Umbrellas are once again Common.
    • Still now, most Umbrellas are Common, with some exceptions:
      • Brella is Epic
      • Mighty Marvel 'Brella is Marvel Series
      • Beskar Umbrella is Star Wars Series
      • Cuddly Cloudcruiser, Chrome Cloudcruiser, and Rainbow Cloudcruiser are Icon Series
      • Beast Brella is Icon Series
      • Heist Flier is Uncommon
      • Time Brella and Rebel Brella are Rare
      • Competitor's Time Brella and High Society Ranker are Uncommon
      • The Olympian and Pantheon Ranker are Uncommon
      • Junker 'Brella and Ranker's Junker 'Brella are Uncommon
      • Doom Brella and Ranker's Doom Brella are Marvel Series
      • The Vaultbrella and Ranker's Vaultbrella are Epic
  • The Hadean Chariot glider used to be considered an Umbrella by the Locker.
    • This was fixed at some point after it's release.
    • This is also currently true for the Gothic Shadesoarer glider, however this is less likely to be a mistake.
  • Ranked Umbrellas used to be obtained by earning 75 points in the second session of any Ranked Cup in Chapter 4: Season OG.
    • This was changed to be any session in Chapter 5: Season 1.
    • This was lowered to 50 points in Chapter 5: Season 3.
  • Chapter 3 didn't release any Umbrellas that weren't Common.
  • Chapter 2: Season 4 was the only season to not release any Common Umbrellas until Chapter 4: Season OG.
  • The Rezzbrella was the first Common Umbrella since Chapter 4: Season 4.
  • The Vaultbrella and Ranker's Vaultbrella were the first Umbrellas since Chapter 5: Season 4 to not be common.
  • There are 8 unreleased and likely scrapped Umbrellas.
  • Chapter 2 introduced the most umbrellas out of any Chapter, with 15.
    • If the 8 unreleased Umbrellas are counted for Chapter 1, then it has the most with 20.
  • Chapter 2: Season 7 introduced the most Umbrellas out of any Season, with 4.
    • If the 8 unreleased Umbrellas are counted for either Pre-Season or Season 1, then that Season wins with 9 Umbrellas introduced.
  • It's inconsistent how Brella is stylized.
    • Some umbrellas do not shorten umbrella to Brella (e.g. Beach Umbrella).
    • Some umbrellas use a hyphen (e.g. MEGA-brella).
    • Some umbrellas use no seperator (e.g. Chromebrella).
    • Some umbrellas use a space, of which some use include an apostrophe while others don't.
      • From Chapter 2: Season 4 to Chapter 2: Season 6, an apostrophe was included.
      • From Chapter 4: Season 4 to Chapter 5: Season 1, as well as with the Beast Brella, an apostrophe was not included.
      • In Chapter 5: Season 3, it was included again, while in Chapter 5: Season 4, it was excluded again.
      • From Chapter 2 Remix onwards, it is once again included.
      • In OG, the apostrophe is included
