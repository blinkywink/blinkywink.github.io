Overview

September 2024 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. September 2024 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 5: Season 4 and contains the cosmetics from the Fuzzy Phenom Set.

September 2024 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, the Fuzzy Phenom's Legacy Styles and the Rocket Pass for the current Rocket League season.
