The Inferno's Quest Pack is a Legendary Limited Time Offer in Fortnite, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for $13.79. The Inferno's Quest Pack was first released in Season 8 and contains Inferno with his cosmetics from the Inferno Set along with the Inferno Quests.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Inferno's Quest Pack's appearances?
Inferno's Quest Pack has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Inferno's Quest Pack's price was changed from $19.99 to $11.99 and then to $13.79.
  • The name was changed from Inferno's Challenge Pack to Inferno's Quest Pack in Chapter 3: Season 3.
  • Formerly the description of this pack was "Take on Inferno's challenges and get yourself up to 1,000 V-Bucks, a pickaxe, and a wrap!"
  • This bundle requires you to do challenges for the vbucks, similar the save the world packs.
