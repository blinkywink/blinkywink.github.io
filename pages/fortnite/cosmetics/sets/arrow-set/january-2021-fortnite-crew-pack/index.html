Overview

January 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. January 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 2: Season 5 and contains the cosmetics from the Arrow Set.

January 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season and the Members Only Emote.
