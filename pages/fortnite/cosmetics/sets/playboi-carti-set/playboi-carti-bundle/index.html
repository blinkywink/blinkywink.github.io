The Playboi Carti Bundle is an Icon Series Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 3,200 V-Bucks. The Playboi Carti Bundle was first released in Chapter 7: Season 1 and contains cosmetics from the Playboi Carti Set.
