Overview

The The Pink Bandit Bundle is an Epic Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,600 V-Bucks. The The Pink Bandit Bundle was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1 and contains Akari with her cosmetics from The Pink Bandit Set.
