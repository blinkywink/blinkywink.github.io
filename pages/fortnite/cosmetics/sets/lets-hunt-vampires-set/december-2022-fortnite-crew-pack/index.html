Overview

December 2022 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. December 2022 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 3: Season 4 and contains the cosmetics from the Let's Hunt Vampires Set.

December 2022 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, the Photonic Legacy Set and the Chill-la-la-Llama Back Bling.
