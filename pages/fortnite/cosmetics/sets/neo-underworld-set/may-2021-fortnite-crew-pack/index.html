Overview

May 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. May 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 2: Season 6 and contains the cosmetics from the Neo Underworld Set.

May 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, 3 Months of Spotify Premium, Fortnite: Save the World, the Deimos Hero and 4 Loading Screens from The Returned Set.
