Overview

June 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. June 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 2: Season 6 and contains the cosmetics from the The Returned Set.

June 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season and 3 Months of Spotify Premium.
