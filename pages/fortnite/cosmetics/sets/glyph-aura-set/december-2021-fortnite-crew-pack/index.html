Overview

December 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. December 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and contains the cosmetics from the Glyph Aura Set.

December 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, the Golden Look Board Glider and 2 Banner Icons.
