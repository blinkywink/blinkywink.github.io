Overview

The Winter's Trickster Legacy Set is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. Winter's Trickster Legacy Set was released in Chapter 4: Season 3 and contains the cosmetics from the Winter's Trickster Set.

Winter's Trickster Legacy Set also provided the Winter's Trickster Banner.

Details

Details[]

For every month you’re an active Fortnite Crew subscriber, you’ll unlock a new stage of the Winter’s Trickster Legacy Set for up to five total stages. Stage 1 of the Set can be unlocked starting with the release of v25.11, and will remain unlockable until October 31, 2023, at 8 PM EDT. The next stages unlock at the same time you receive your monthly Crew Pack while subscribed (the last day of each month at 8 PM EDT / 7 PM EST).

  • Stage 1: Every active Fortnite Crew subscriber immediately unlocks a Winter's Trickster Banner Icon, the Tricksters Unite! Emoticon, and Trickster's Charge Spray.
  • Stage 2: Unlock The Underfox Lobby Track and Outfoxed Wrap. (The Underfox Lobby Track is featured in the trailer below!)
  • Stage 3: Unlock the Blizzard's Bite Pickaxe.
  • Stage 4: Unlock the Skulk Pack Back Bling.
  • Stage 5: Unlock the Volpez Outfit.
