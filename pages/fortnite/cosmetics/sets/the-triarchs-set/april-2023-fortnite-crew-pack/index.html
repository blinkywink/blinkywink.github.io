Overview

April 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. April 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 4: Season 2 and contains the cosmetics from The Triarchs Set.

April 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack also provides 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, the Photonic Legacy Set and the Rocket Pass for the current Rocket League season.
