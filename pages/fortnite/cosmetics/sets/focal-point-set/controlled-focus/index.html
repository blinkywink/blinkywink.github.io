Controlled Focus is a Rare Wrap in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 500 V-Bucks or with the Focal Point Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Controlled Focus was first released in Chapter 4: Season 1 and is part of the Focal Point Set.

Controlled Focus could also have been obtained as a reward from the Reboot Rally Quests.
