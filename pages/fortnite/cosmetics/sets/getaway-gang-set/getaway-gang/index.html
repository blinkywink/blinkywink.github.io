The Getaway Gang was a gang of criminals in Fortnite: Battle Royale that was introduced in Season 5. The gang later evolved into the Cold Blooded Syndicate.

Overview

Overview[]

The Getaway Gang is a gang of criminals, particularly robbers, based around card games, although some members do not follow this rule.

History

History[]

Season 5[]

Diamond Wild Card, alongside the gang's three other original members, appeared for a heist. However, they were thwarted by The Ace, who had rigged the Safe they were targeting with a Remote Explosive.[13]

Season 8[]

The four main Wild Cards, alongside The Ace, returned for a heist - this time working together. [14]

Season 9[]

Heist appeared during the summer, lounging and having fun at the beach with other characters present.[15]

Season X[]

Dark Wild Card - a Dark Reflection[16] of one of the four main Wild Cards - is created by Kevin the Cube. He later join the gang.[17]

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

Shark Henchmen were recruited by The Agency to protect The Shark.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

Diamond Wild Card joins Midas' crew as a member of SHADOW, he is however among GHOST agents as a double agent.[18]

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

Heart Loveless arrived on the Island. Her activities are unknown.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

Diamond Wild Card wanted to pull a heist of his own, though Heart Loveless, Heist, Midas and Sabina all refused to help him. He therefore recruited Diamond Diva and Aura. However, he later betrayed them after the gang - now reformed as the Cold Blooded Syndicate - called him in for a better heist offer. His former partners in Midas' Crew: Chaos Double Agent, and Double Agent Hush, were also recruited by the Cold Blooded.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The gang seems to get along well with Midas and his crew, as several of their members were recruited as Midas' agents - and Midas is one of Diamond Wildcard's contacts for heists.
