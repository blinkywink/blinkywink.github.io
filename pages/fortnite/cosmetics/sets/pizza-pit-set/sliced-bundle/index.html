Overview

The Sliced Bundle is an Epic Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite, that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,400 V-Bucks. The Sliced Bundle was first released in Chapter 3: Season 3 and contains Tomatohead and Crustina's and their cosmetics from the Pizza Pit Set and Pizza Pitfighter Set.
