Overview

August 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. August 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and contains the cosmetics from the Quest Summer Ever Set.

August 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, the Zen Legacy Set, the Rainbow Cloudcruiser Glider, the Skye up High Loading Screen and 4 Banner Icons.
