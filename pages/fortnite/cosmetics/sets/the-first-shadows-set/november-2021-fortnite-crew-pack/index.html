Overview

November 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. November 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack was released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and contains the cosmetics from The First Shadows Set.

November 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack also provided 1,000 V-Bucks, the Battle Pass for the current season, the Zen Legacy Set and extra style for the All-Weather Extractor Pickaxe, the Chaos Origins and The Burning Wolf Outfits.
