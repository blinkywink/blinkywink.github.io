Overview

The Corascana's Legacy Styles is an exclusive pack for Fortnite Crew subscribers in Fortnite, that could have been purchased in the Item Shop for $11.99. Corascana's Legacy Styles was released in Chapter 7: Season 2 and contains extra styles for the cosmetics from the Storm Dancer Set.

Details[]

Don’t just leave a legacy once, but several times per Crew Pack! Starting with the Corascana Outfit of the June 2026 Crew Pack, here are Legacy Styles you can unlock for your Crew Pack Outfits:

  • 1 month of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - A Legacy Style specific to the Crew Pack
  • 2 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Auric Legacy Style
  • 3 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Phase Legacy Style
  • 4 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Rimebound Legacy Style
  • 5 months of subscribing since receiving the Crew Pack - Cosmic Legacy Style
