Paper Plane is a Rare Glider in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks, or in the following bundles:

  • Hamuppi's Locker Bundle for 2,400 V-Bucks
  • Carlox's Locker Bundle for 2,300 V-Bucks
  • XxYjYxx's Locker Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks
  • Buyuriru's Locker Bundle for 2,100 V-Bucks
  • S1KE's Locker Bundle for 2,400 V-Bucks
  • D7boor's Locker Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks

Paper Plane was first released in Season 7 and is part of the Calculator Crew Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Paper Plane's appearances?
Paper Plane has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia[]

  • Paper Plane shares Glider_ID_104 with Glidurrr and Cuddle Cruiser. This was likely caused by a mistake.
  • Paper Plane has the phrase "My Name Jeff" written on it; a quote from Greg Jenko in 22 Jump Street, which became a popular meme on the internet.
