Balenciaga Unleashed is an Epic Item Shop Bundle in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,800 V-Bucks. Balenciaga Unleashed was first released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and contains Fashion Banshee and Shady Doggo with their cosmetics from the Balenciaga Fit Set.
