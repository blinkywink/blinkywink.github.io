Banners are the icons of players in Fortnite. Players can always edit and customize the icon and color.

Usage

Usage[]

The banner that a player chooses to use will be displayed in several places. These include:

  • In the matchmaking Lobbies of Battle Royale and Save the World.
  • The Party Menu during a game in Battle Royale and Save the World.
  • The Banner gadget in Save the World.
  • The explorable Homebase in Save the World.
  • Some challenges in Battle Royale, such as "Summit Mountaintops".
  • The Banner Brigade Set.
  • Kymera Outfit
  • Dive Bomber Wrap
  • Protocol Pack Back Bling
  • The Port-A-Bunker item in Battle Royale.
  • Capture Points in Battle Royale.
  • Spectra Knight Outfit
  • Spectra Protector Back Bling
  • Killswitch Outfit
  • The Skratch Company Set.

The following Emotes also display the player's selected banner:

  • Banner Wave
  • Card Drop
  • Stake Your Claim
