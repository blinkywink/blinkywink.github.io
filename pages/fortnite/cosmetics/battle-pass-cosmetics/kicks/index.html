Kicks

Overview

As of May 16th 2026, there are around 350[1] Kicks In-Game

Kicks are Cosmetic Items introduced in Chapter 2 Remix. They can be used in Battle Royale, Save the World, Creative and Fortnite Festival. Kicks can only be worn by compatible outfits.

Additional features available for Kicks

Additional features available for Kicks[]
Selectable Styles Reactive
  • Fluffy Buns was the first Kicks with styles.
  • UltraViolet Pulse was the first reactive Kicks.
