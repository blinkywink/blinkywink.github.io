History

History[]

Chapter 1[]

  • Season 4: Introduced the first Mystery Outfit: The Visitor.
  • Season 7: Introduced the first progressive Mystery Outfit: The Prisoner.
  • Season X: Introduced the first Bonus Outfit that is a remix of an existing Outfit: The Scientist.

Chapter 2[]

  • Chapter 2: Season 2: Introduced the first Collaboration and Marvel Series Bonus Outfit: Deadpool.
  • Chapter 2: Season 3: Introduced the first DC Series Bonus Outfit: Aquaman.
  • Chapter 2: Season 4: Introduced the first Bonus Outfit with a Built-In Emote & Super Level Styles: Wolverine.
  • Chapter 2: Season 5: Introduced the first Bonus Outfit with a Transformative Built-In Emote: Predator.
  • Chapter 2: Season 6: Introduced the first Icon Series Bonus Outfit: Neymar Jr.
  • Chapter 2: Season 7: Introduced the first Bonus Outfit with a Built-In Glider: Clark Kent.

Chapter 3[]

  • Chapter 3: Season 3: Introduced the first Epic Bonus Outfit: Indiana Jones.

Chapter 4[]

  • Chapter 4: Season 1: Introduced the first Gaming Legends Series Bonus Outfit: Geralt of Rivia.
  • Chapter 4: Season 2: Introduced the first cel-shaded Bonus Outfit: Eren Jaeger.
  • Chapter 4: Season 4: The first Star Wars Series Bonus Outfit was introduced; Ahsoka Tano.

Chapter 5[]

  • Chapter 5: Season 4: Introduced the first non exclusive Bonus Outfit: Doom.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Scientist is the only mystery outfit to be free, due to a glitch. The glitch was patched a day later but anyone who earned the outfit kept it.
  • There was no Bonus/Mystery Outfit during Chapter 4: Season OG, marking the first time since Season 4.
  • Despite not being Secret Outfits, several Bonus Outfits had Secret edit styles, revealed only when the skin was released.
    • These include: Deadpool, Wolverine, Clark Kent, Indiana Jones, The Herald, Geralt of Rivia, Solid Snake, Korra, Wastelander Magneto, Doom and Outlaw Midas.
  • Chapter 4: Season OG was the first Battle Pass to not have a bonus outfit, followed by Chapter 7: Season 1
