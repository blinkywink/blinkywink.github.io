Outfit[]

“
All will fall.
”
— In-Game Description

Ruin is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained as a reward from the Discovery Challenges. Ruin was released in Season 8 and is part of the Ruination Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Ruin's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Signature
Base

Ruin received a higher-detailed style in Update v30.00, which replaced the original one.
Players have the option to choose between both.
Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Ruin's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Ruin is The Prisoner's right-hand man.[1]
  • Ruin was originally called Thorax prior to his release.[2]
    • Ruin's description was also changed from "Looks aren't deceiving. He's not very friendly." to "All will fall." in Update v8.30.
  • Ruin's rarity was changed from Epic to Legendary in Update v8.30.
