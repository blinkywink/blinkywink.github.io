Frostnite Quests

Overview

“
Added several event quests that can be completed by playing Frostnite.
”
— v7.10 Blog

Frostnite Quests are a set of Quests in Fortnite: Save the World that were added on December 18th 2018. Available only during the Frozen Fjords Venture Season, they reward players with various Schematics, Heroes, Tickets, and Battle Pass XP for Battle Royale. Certain Frostnite Quests are time-limited, remaining available for only a week before vanishing for an entire year.
