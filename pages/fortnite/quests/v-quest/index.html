Mission[]

“
It's time for Ray to say goodbye to an old Friend. Help Penny build a memorial for Dr. Vinderman.
”
— In-Game Description

V is a Main Quest in Fortnite: Save the World. It is the 4th Quest of the Canny Valley part of the Main Questline, and unlocks the See You Later Quest.
