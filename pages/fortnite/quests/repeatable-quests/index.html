Overview

Repeatable Quests are a set of Quests in Fortnite: Save the World that can be repeated. They reward players with 200 Gold and 100 RE-PERK!.

History

History[]

  • S1
  • S4
  • S6
  • S8
  • S9
  • C2S2
  • C2R

Update v1.5

  • Introduced Repeatable Quests as a new way to earn Gold, with the Killer Storms Quest.

Update v4.00

  • Introduced the Epic "Mini-Boss Repeatable Quest.

Update v4.20

  • Introduced the A Watery Grave, Forces of Nature, and Set the World on Fire Repeatable Quests, along with the Perk Recombobulator.

Update v6.10

  • Introduced the Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest with the introduction of Beta Storms and the Retrieve the Data Beta Storm Mission.

Update v6.20

  • Retrieve the Data Beta Storm Mission has been rotated out, along with the Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest.

Update v8.00

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest has returned, and can be progressed by completing the new Eliminate and Collect Beta Storm Mission.

Update v8.10

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest can now be progressed by completing the new Test the Limits Beta Storm Mission.
    • Eliminate and Collect Beta Storm Mission has been rotated out.

Update v8.20

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest can now be progressed by completing the Resupply Beta Storm Mission.
    • Test the Limits Beta Storm Mission has been rotated out.

Update v8.30

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest can now be progressed by completing the new Lost in Transit Beta Storm Mission.
    • Resupply Beta Storm Mission has been rotated out.

Update v8.40

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest can now be progressed by completing the returning Test the Limits Beta Storm Mission.
    • Lost in Transit Beta Storm Mission has been rotated out.

Update v8.50

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest can now be progressed by completing the returning Resupply Beta Storm Mission.
    • Test the Limits Beta Storm Mission has been rotated out.

Update v9.00

  • The Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest has been removed, as Beta Storms have concluded.

Update v12.00

  • Removed the A Watery Grave, Forces of Nature and Set the World on Fire Repeatable Quests from the available pool.

Update v32.10

  • Renamed the Beta Storm Prospecting Repeatable Quest to Beta Storm Alert!.
    • It can now be progressed by completing the new Hunt the Titan Beta Storm Mission.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • As a result of Event Flags ending a day earlier than intended, the 2025 Flannel Falls Season departed a day early, along with Blockbuster.[1]
    • The Repeatable Quests Killer Storms and Danger in the Mist also disappeared from players' Quest Logs.[2]
