Quest[]

“
Retrieve some listening devices so we can intercept Kevin and Desiree's phone calls.

Ray on the Rooftop is a Main Quest in Fortnite: Save the World, on Page 9 of the Canny Valley Quests. It unlocks the Midnight Oil Quest.

Objectives

Objectives[]

  • Retrieve 15 listening devices in a 58+ Ghost Town or Thunder Route 99 zone.
