Unreleased Cosmetics

Overview

This article contains unreleased content that may be available in the future.
The content on this page is currently present in the game's files but is not accessible by normal means at this time.

Unreleased Cosmetics are Cosmetic Items in the game files that have been surfaced and obtained by experienced data-miners, from the in-game files of Fortnite. These cosmetics can include Outfits, Back Blings, Pickaxes, Gliders, Kicks, Contrails, Emotes, Emoticons, Sprays, Wraps, Loading Screens, Sidekicks, Reactions, Car Bodies, Decals, Wheels, Trails, Boosts, Lobby Music, Jam Tracks, Auras, Guitars, Bass, Drums, Microphones, Keytars, Builds, Decor Bundles and Item Shop Bundles that are not yet available in-game until they release.

These act as spoilers as to what cosmetics have to come. Although, bots, and developers are seen to sometimes use these cosmetics. #Epic Partner content creators are also able to obtain these cosmetics

Below are the cosmetics that have been leaked out by dataminers as of the latest update of Fortnite, cosmetics removed from the in-game files aren't listed below.

Missing Cosmetics

Missing Cosmetics[]

Main Article: Missing Cosmetics

These are cosmetic items in Fortnite that exist beyond the concept stages, but are currently missing from the files, and thus unreleased (and in some occasions, scrapped).

Massive Datamining Incidents[]

Main Article: Massive Datamining Incidents

In Fortnite's history, a few large datamining incidents have occurred. These revealed a large amount of IDs for potential future cosmetics, some of which were scrapped and never likely to be added.

TBD Cosmetics

TBD Cosmetics[]

Main Article: TBD Cosmetics

Unlike the usual unreleased cosmetic items or Unlisted Cosmetics, TBD cosmetics usually are used for AIs (such as Marauders) or used for items (such as Witch Broom). These differ from Scrapped cosmetics as they were never intended, at least not in their current state, to be accessible to the player, and many of them are still present in the files. Some of these, such as The Dealer, may become cosmetics in the future.
