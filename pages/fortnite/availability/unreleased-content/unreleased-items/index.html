Unreleased Items

Overview

This article contains unreleased content that may be available in the future.
The content on this page is currently present in the game's files but is not accessible by normal means at this time.

Unreleased Items are Items in the game files that have been surfaced and obtained by experienced data-miners, from the in-game files of Fortnite. These items are not yet available in-game until they release.

These act as spoilers as to what items have to come. Although, bots and developers are seen to sometimes use these items.

Below are the items that have been leaked out by data miners as of the latest update of Fortnite.
