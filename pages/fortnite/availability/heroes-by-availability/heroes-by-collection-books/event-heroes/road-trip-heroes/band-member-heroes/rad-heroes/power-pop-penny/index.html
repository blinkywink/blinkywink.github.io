Overview

“
Take center stage!
”
— Penny

Power Pop Penny is an Epic and Legendary Constructor in Fortnite: Save the World. She can be obtained from Rad Llamas during the Blasted Badlands Venture Season. She is a part of the Rad Heroes Set.
