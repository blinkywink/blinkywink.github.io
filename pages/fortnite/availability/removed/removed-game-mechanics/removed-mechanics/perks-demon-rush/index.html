Overview

This page is about a mechanic that has been removed from the game.

Perks were a Gameplay Mechanic in Demon Rush, introduced in Update v37.40.

Functionality

Functionality[]

Perks could modify gameplay in Demon Rush, enabling a choice between two Perks every round. If players do not like the selection of Perks initially presented to them, they were able to reroll them. Rerolling is free every first roll in a match, but reroll afterwards would cost 125 Monster Parts.

Unlike Augments, by opening the inventory after selecting a chosen perk, players could drop Perks and give them to other players (Cursed: Monster Part Influx is the only exception).
