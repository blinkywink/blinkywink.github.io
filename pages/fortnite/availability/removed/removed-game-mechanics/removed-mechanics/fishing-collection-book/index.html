Overview

This page is about a mechanic that has been removed from the game.


The Fishing Collection Book was a feature in Fortnite: Battle Royale, first introduced in Chapter 2: Season 4, that detailed all Fish you have caught throughout the season.

When viewing a particular fish's entry in the collection book, you can see the info on how to obtain it, how many of it you have caught; the longest length you have caught of it, and a leaderboard comparing the longest fish length of you and your friends.

Update v14.00[]

  • Added the Fishing Collection Book.

Update v14.10[]

  • Midas Flopper and Vendetta Flopper were added to the book.

Update v15.00[]

  • Rift Fish, Zero Point Fish and their variants were added to the book.
  • Midas Flopper, Hop Flopper, Spicy Fish and their variants were removed from the book.

Update v15.10[]

  • Snowy Flopper was added to the book.

Update v16.00[]

  • Cuddle Fish, Stink Fish and their variants were added to the book.
  • Snowy Flopper was removed from the book.

Update v17.00[]

  • Vendetta Flopper, Cuddle Fish, Stink Fish and their variants were removed from the book.

Update v18.00[]

  • Shadow Flopper and its variants were added to the book.

Update v19.00[]

  • Shadow Flopper and its variants were removed from the book.

Update v20.00[]

  • Thermal Fish and its variants were re-added to the book.
  • Spicy Fish and its variants were removed from the book.
  • Yellow Slurpfish, Pink Shield Fish, Silver Thermal Fish and Peely Jellyfish now spawn in desert areas.
  • White Slurpfish now spawn in mountainous areas.

Update v21.00[]

  • Thermal Fish and its variants were removed from the book.

Update v23.00[]

  • The Fishing Collection Book was removed.
