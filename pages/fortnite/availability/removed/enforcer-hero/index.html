Enforcer is a Specialty Outlander available in the game. The number of perks and abilities depends on the hero's maximum evolution and level.

Maximum evolutions

Maximum evolutions[]

  • Uncommon: 3
  • Rare: 4
  • Epic and Legendary: 5
Perks

Perks[]

Name Unlocked Description
Bear With Me 5 Increases the duration of TEDDY by 5 seconds.
Bearricade 15 Enemies damaged by TEDDY have movement and attack speed slowed by 30% for 6 seconds.
Bearserker 30 Increases the damage dealt by TEDDY by 25% and duration by 5 seconds.
Focused Acquisition Evo Increases chance to find double loot by 6%.
In the Zone Evo After 5 hits in a row with a pick axe, gain In the Zone which increases pick axe damage by 24%.
Overbearing 12 Increases the range of TEDDY's attack to 6 tiles.
Phase Shield 15 Phase Shift forces the shield to regenerate for 4 seconds. Your shield will regenerate even if you continue to take damage, but it will only regenerate at 20% of the regular rate.
Shield Boost 8 Increases maximum shield 20%.
Upgraded Bearings 25 Increases TEDDY's rate of fire by 50%.
Abilities

Abilities[]

Name Unlocked Description
Anti-Material Charge 1 Punch forward, destroying structures and harvesting their materials. Deals X Damage to enemies. Activate Heavy Attack with your pickaxe equipped.
Loot Llama 2 Consumes a Llama Fragment to deploy a Loot Llama. Whack the Loot Llama with a harvesting tool to get building materials and crafting ingredients before it disappears.
Phase Shift Evo Quickly dash through space, gaining 30% increased movement speed for 3 seconds.
TEDDY 2 Consumes a Charge Fragment to deploy T.E.D.D.Y. for 15 seconds. T.E.D.D.Y. will blast enemies within 4 tiles every 0.25 seconds, for a base of 13 physical damage.
Squad Bonuses

Squad Bonuses[]

When put into a squad slot on the Hero screen, an outlander can give either a passive support or tactical bonus. Some require a certain Primary Hero to use. Abilities that have percentages change depending on the star level.
