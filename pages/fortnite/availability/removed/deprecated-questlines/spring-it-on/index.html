Overview

This article is about a Questline in Save the World that is no longer available.

Spring It On! was an event in Fortnite: Save the World, that came out in Update v2.50. It introduced a 5-part story line, as well as new spring-themed heroes, the Heartbreaker Crossbow and new dragon weapons, with new Llamas.
