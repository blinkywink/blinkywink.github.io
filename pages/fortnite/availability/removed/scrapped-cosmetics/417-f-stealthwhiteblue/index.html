Overview

This page is about a cosmetic that is most likely scrapped.
The content on this page was never released and was removed from the files.

"CID_417_Athena_Commando_F_StealthWhiteBlue" was an Outfit in Fortnite that was meant for a PlayStation related promotional pack.[1][2] It is located between John Wick and Takara.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • "StealthWhiteBlue" was first discovered by Ali-A while he was redeeming the Neo Versa Bundle.[3]
    • In Update v9.40, Shadow Ops received the White style and the Prospect Back Bling, which are recycled from "StealthWhiteBlue".
      • In an unknown update during Chapter 6, the icon for Shadow Ops' White Style was replaced with the icon of "StealthWhiteBlue".
