Overview

This page is about a cosmetic that is most likely scrapped.
The content on this page was never released and was removed from the files.

“
Purchase the 2020 Annual Pass and receive all upcoming 2020 Fortnite Battle Royale: Battle Bundles and 7 Annual Pass exclusive cosmetics. Battle Bundles unlock 25% of content in each Battle Pass instantly upon release.
”
— In-Game Description

The Annual Pass 2020 was a leaked prototype in Fortnite. Annual Pass 2020 contained Battle Bundles for next 4 upcoming seasons, 3 Outfits, 3 Back Blings and a Pickaxe.

In a statement released to Eurogamer, Epic Games has revealed that they had "no plans" to release the pass, which means that it was cancelled and remnants of it remained in the game files. [1]

The idea of an Annual Pass was most likely replaced by the Fortnite Crew.
