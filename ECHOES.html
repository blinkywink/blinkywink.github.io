ECHOES OF DECEMBER

about

This song is probably like the 4th song I've made. First I made most of the instrumentals, and sent it to my Japanese friend Ryu Tanioka of Trse. I realized since none of my songs had vocals, it would be cool to have him sing on it. I asked him and he agreed; we spent months sending versions back and fourth and this isn't even the final version. The final version will be on the album. This song was a great learning experience considering I have never edited or mixed vocals before. 

Thanks Ryu, we made something I am very proud of.
