About the Fortnite Wiki Project

Content, licensing, and takedown requests

This is a personal fan project. I built it because I wanted a cleaner way to browse Fortnite lore — not to replace existing wikis or take credit for other people's work.

Most of the article text on this site comes from the Fortnite community's work on the Fandom wiki. Under Fandom's licensing terms, user-submitted text on Fandom wikis is released under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA) license, which allows reuse and redistribution when the original authors are credited.

I did not copy Fandom's HTML, CSS, templates, or site code. I scraped user-submitted article text and built my own viewer and design around it. Fandom wiki moderators are credited on this page below.

If you contributed content and would like something removed or changed, please contact me. I'm happy to take down or update specific pages on request — this site exists for fans, and I don't want it to cause problems for the people who wrote the material in the first place.

What is the Fortnite Wiki Project?

The Fortnite Wiki Project is an independent fan-made effort to create a cleaner, faster, and more modern wiki experience for fans of Fortnite.

While existing wiki platforms have done an incredible job preserving information, they often come with heavy ads, cluttered layouts, and slower performance. This project aims to provide the same information in a more streamlined and user-friendly format.

At its core, this project exists to make exploring Fortnite lore, characters, and episodes easier and more enjoyable.

Why was this project created?

This project was created out of a desire for a better browsing experience. Many fans rely on wikis daily, but the current platforms can sometimes feel overwhelming or intrusive.

The goal here is not to replace existing communities, but to offer an alternative that focuses on:

  • Speed and performance
  • Clean design
  • Easy navigation
  • A distraction-free experience

Where does the information come from?

The information on this site is based on publicly available content from the Fortnite fan community, including existing wiki pages and fan contributions.

The Fortnite community has spent years building a detailed and expansive knowledge base, and this project would not exist without their work.

This site simply presents that information in a more modern and accessible way.

Is this an official Epic Games or Fortnite website?

No. This is an unofficial fan project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Epic Games or the creators of Fortnite.

All trademarks, characters, and related content belong to their respective owners.

Will this replace the existing wiki?

No. The goal is not to replace or compete with existing platforms, but to provide an alternative way to view and explore the same information.

The original communities remain an essential part of the Fortnite fandom.

Can I contribute or suggest changes?

This project is based on information from the Fortnite Fandom wiki. If you contribute there, your contributions may eventually be reflected here as well.

Why does this site exist if another wiki already exists?

Because user experience matters.

This project focuses on:

  • Faster load times
  • Better readability
  • Less intrusive design
  • A more modern interface

It’s built for fans who want quick, clean access to information without distractions.

Final note

This project is built with respect for the Fortnite community and all the contributors who have spent years documenting the series. Their work is the foundation of everything here.
