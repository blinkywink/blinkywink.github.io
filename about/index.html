About

This site displays your Last.fm listening statistics in a clean, simple interface. Enter any Last.fm username to view their listening data, including:

  • Total plays, artists, albums, and tracks
  • Recently played songs with play counts
  • Top tracks, albums, and artists over different time periods
  • Detailed artist pages with your listening history

All data is pulled directly from Last.fm's API. The site focuses on visualizing your personal listening stats rather than music discovery.
