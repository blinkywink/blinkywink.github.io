Overview

“
Ransack every dimension.
”
— In-Game Description

X-Lord is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Season X Battle Pass. X-Lord is part of the Storm Scavenger Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Scavenger - Reach Season Level 35
  • Rust - Complete 7 Rumble Royale Prestige Missions
  • Crimson - Reach Season Level 15
  • Hunter - Complete 7 Smash & Grab Prestige Missions
