Valeria is the former highest leader of The Society and the older sister of Hope. She is a researcher, a flame manipulator with the burning ambition to know about everything.

She is a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as one of the main antagonists of Chapter 5: Season 1.

Personality

Personality[]

Valeria believes that knowledge is power and like all other Society members, power is everything.

Her house at Reckless Railways is themed around moths. In the Burning Ambition Valeria loading screen, She can be seen researching The Zero Point, The Big Bang, Jones and the Pandora's Box.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

Valeria's family would join The Society, with her becoming one of five leaders in the faction. She began her research on Pandora's Box after digging into her grandfather's ancient books, with her house at Reckless Railways having a personal library dedicated to finding the true powers of The Pantheon. She would convince the other Society leaders to help her in her search promising them greater power.

Valeria would use The Society to kidnap Peely in an attempt to get more info on the box from Jones, but the plan failed and ultimately led to the downfall of the Society with Peely getting freed from Fencing Fields. Despite this, Valeria continued her search for the box at Ruined Reels. A Titans hand emerged from the ground, holding the Pandora's Box that Valeria was looking for. After it was freed from his chains, it opened, letting a storm of Shades onto the Island.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Zeus and the Olympians appeared on the Island to punish mortals for opening the box. Zeus defeated the other Society bosses, forcing them into hiding and also marking The Underground's victory.[7], Valeria would flee along side Montague after realizing the consequences with opening Pandora's Box.[8]

Chapter 2 Remix[]

Planning to leave the Island after the dust settled, Hope would contact Valeria to talk about her own future as well as the Island's future. She encourages Valeria to meet up with The Underground and assist to rebuild things with the help of Nisha, saying that otherwise The Underground wouldn't stay gone. Despite the repeat calls, Valeria never picked up, leaving her sister on voicemail repeatedly.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In concept arts of the Burning Ambition Valeria Loading Screen, Valeria could be seen investigating on The Seven, notably The Foundation and The Order. She also owned books about The Seven.
    • However all of The Seven content was removed from the final version.
  • Valeria's fire based powers are a reference to several parts of Greek Mythology.
    • Valeria is known as a character who seek knowledge, in Greek Mythology knowledge is represented as the "fire" that Prometheus stole from the gods and gave to humans.
    • By opening Pandora's Box by seeking knowledge, Valeria played the role of Pandora, the first women in Greek Mythology who opened the Box out of curiosity. Pandora was crafted by the God of Forge and Fire, Hephaestus.
    • Valeria possesses a relic tied to the Phoenix, the fire bird capable of rising from its ashes in Greek mythology.
  • Valeria seems to be inspired by both Pandora and Pyrrha from Greek Mythology.
    • By seeking knowledge, leading her to open Pandora's Box, she played the role of Pandora in Greek Mythology.
    • Pyrrha, the daughter of Pandora and Epimetheus, was known to have flame-coloured hair, just like Valeria.
      • Valeria's appointed role to rebuild things on Helios by Hope, who is able to see the future like oracles, remind of the role of Pyrrha and Deucalion to rebuild humankind after the deluge, as they were told by Thesis, the Titan goddess of divine law and oracles.
