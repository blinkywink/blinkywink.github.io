Yuki Yubari is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be obtained by logging in between November 27th 2025 and December 1st 2025. Yuki Yubari is part of the O-Ren's Assassins Set.

Yuki was a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 7: Season 1. She could have been found after entering a Rift Gate.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Yuki dropped the following items when eliminated. She also dropped Bars.

Dual Micro SMGs Grenade
Behavior

Behavior[]

Yuki, alongside her sister Gogo, will rift infront of the player who entered a Rift Gate to obtain the Forsaken Vow Blade. Threatening them before attacking them on sight. Occasionally throwing Grenades at her target.
