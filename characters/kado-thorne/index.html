Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In the Brazilian version of the game, Kado's name has been changed to Kouji Datura, which is a pun on Conde Drácula, the Portuguese name for Count Dracula.
  • The Chapter 4: Season 4 cinematic trailer showed a then-unreleased Style for Kado Thorne with Lavish Kado Thorne's hair and glasses with the base outfit's clothes.
    • This style was later added in Update v26.10 as Collector Kado Thorne.
  • Unlike other vampire outfits, Kado Thorne is inspired by the vampires of European folklore, being essentially undead (also called Revenant).
    • He differs from the modern vampire, having no exaggerated canine teeth or other of their attributes. He nevertheless has a transformation roughly resembling a bat, a reference to Dracula.
  • There is a visual glitch where the Revenant Kado Thorne style would brighten when switching Pickaxes with an Emote.

Kado Thorne is a very ancient and powerful vampire and the leader of Eclipse. He is a space time traveler, a thief and a wealthy collector. He travels through space and time, stealing items from different realities and from time periods and leaving behind a calling card and a portrait of the Eclipse.

He is a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the overarching antagonist of Chapter 4: Season 3, the main antagonist of Chapter 4: Season 4, and the main antagonist of the second half of Chapter 4 overall.

Personality

Personality[]

Thorne is a voracious traveler with a hunger for all kinds of art forms, from ancient arts to modern arts. He will do anything just to get his hands on the things that interest him. If he couldn't buy it he would steal it. If it went extinct, he would time travel to get it.

It's shown in the Fortnitemares 2023 Quests that Thorne is very short-tempered, as he smashes some of his things out of rage. Additionally, his multiple portraits of himself imply that he is quite narcissistic.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Thorne in his human form appears as a very healthy and muscular man. He wears a robe that oddly resembles The Ancients statue. His default style shows him with mask and a very unique black hair cut that leaves room for his big Revenant ears. His Lavish/Collector style shows him with white hair and he also wears sunglasses.

In his Revenant form, he becomes a pale bat-like creature with no mouth. His robe is gone, his eyes turned blood red and he is covered in a light greenish aura with a mix of dark red aura.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

Revenant Vampire
Metamorphosis
Kado Thorne's true form as a Revenant, granting him various vampiric abilities as well as psionic powers. In this form his body is much more resistant. He can seal this form to have a human appearance using his tattoo, his necklace, his bracelet and his rings.
Flight
With his Wraith Wings, Thorne can fly at a terrifying speed. He can use them even in his human form.
Life-Force Absorption
His psionic vampirical powers allow him to siphon life out of any living beings near him in a certain range to restore his own vitality. He can also infuse his power into other objects like his Kinetic Blade.
Psionic Eclipse
Kado Thorne, as a psionic power user is capable of using Essokinesis (also known as Reality Warping) at least at a low level. It allow him to generate Solar and Lunar Eclipses even when the astronomical situation of a reality doesn't allow it.[8]
Telepathy
Kado Thorne, as a psionic power user is capable at least at a low level of manipulating the minds of his victims to trigger splitting headaches.[9]
History

History[]

Before Chapter 4: Season 4[]

Kado Thorne was a very old vampire traveling across Time and The Omniverse with his Time Machine in search of new artifacts for his collection. Eons ago, The Ancients encountered him on one of his travels. They feared him so much that they created The Apparatus, with the purpose to predict his arrival so they can make escape plans before it. They also left glyphs and murals to warn eventual future generations who would find their ruins. In one of his journies, Kado Thorne came across The Triarchs' broken realm and stole some of their relics for his collection. Thorne's havoc across space and time caused him to became target of many vampire hunters, including the dark fae known as Shimmerdusk.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

Kado travels back in time to steal Oro's head, TNTina's Ka-Boom Bow & Midas' Drum Gun.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

Kado travels back in time to steal Kit's Shockwave Launcher, Kit's Charge Shotgun, and Ocean's Bottomless Chug Jug.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Kado travels back in time to steal Zyg and Choppy's Ray Gun.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

Kado travels back in time to steal Gunnar's Stinger SMG, The Foundation's MK-Seven Assault Rifle and an artifact related to The Seven.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

The ruins of the Ancients were discovered after the central part of the Island collapsed, revealing a huge underground rainforest. The ruins revealed the arrival of Kado Thorne through murals, glyphs and The Apparatus announcing an Eclipse that should be astrologically impossible. The glyphs also mentioned his Time Machine, which Doctor Slone wanted to use to save the islanders from the arrival of the other enemies predicted by Optimus Prime and the impending collapse of Reality Zero.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

Kado Thorne arrived on the Island with his Time Machine and his faction, and began to drain all the island's resources to build his resorts, which are actually very well protected strongholds to house his collection and his Time Machine, which he wanted to use to collect items from Reality Zero's past as well as for a more sinister plan. He also took control of Rumble Ruins, and bought one of MEGA City's luxury restaurants as well as Slappy Shores' main Slap Factory, all of which he also transformed into highly protected strongholds. Additionally, he took control of the Mae Forecast Towers.

At some point, he traveled back in time to cut off Midas' left hand to add it in his collection.

Later, in an attempt to steal the Time Machine, Doctor Slone formed a Heist Crew consisting of Nolan Chance, Antonia, The Looper, Piper Pace, Fish Thicc, Mae, and Khaby Lame. While Thorne exited the Time Machine after his journey back to "2020 03 25" to get Oro's head for his collection, he finds the Heist Crew trying to rob his vault. After discovering the intruders in his estate, Thorne transforms into his Revenant Form and tries to kill them, forcing the crew to flee. After the Heist Crew's failure to steal the Time Machine, Thorne uses his Psionic powers to cause The Eclipse, which began with a permanent Super Blood Moon and Solar Eclipse. Around this time, Shimmerdusk arrives on the island to continue her hunt of him.

After that, Kado Thorne remained in his Revenant form to protect his Time Machine himself. He also established his next target, "2018 07 12" the period of Season 5, when Athena Island undergoes "Worlds Collide." However his plan wasn't about obtaining items for his collection, but something more sinister that would put the fate of the Island at stake. Later, the Heist Crew managed to successfully steal his machine and take it to their base before his plan could take form.

With the Time Machine stolen, Shimmerdusk was free to make her move onto her plan to assassinate Thorne. Throughout the Fortnitemares 2023 Quests, she enlists the help of The Looper to prepare. She has them stock up on ammo, eliminate Thorne's Guards, smash his valuables, etc. After weeks of preparing, she decides that they were ready and infiltrated his vault. She went to confront Kado , alongside The Looper, and they successfully take him down. With Thorne defeated, she declares that he will "no longer prey across time and space."

Chapter 4: Season OG[]

There are anachronisms present in Fortnite OG's Timeline, such as the book concerning the Chalice of Cubetastrophe, and a version of No Sweat Insurance depicting events that never happened in this timeline, suggesting Thorne already traveled here.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Thorne means 'thorn bush'.
  • Stolen paintings of Slone, Marigold, Princess Felicity Fish, Agent Peely, the Cubes and The Herald can be found in his estates.
  • Kado Thorne is technically one of the oldest characters in the game along with the Greek God of The Pantheon, as his first known chronological appearance was eons ago when he arrived in Reality: UN-74.
  • According to ItsUnusuaI on Twitter, a now-scrapped Marvel X Fortnite comic called "Order Restored" was supposed to tease Thorne's debut, and it would've featured him battling The Collector, a Marvel character similar to Thorne.
    • In that comics, Thorne was also meant to be one of the targets monitored and hunted by the Imagined Order, due to his dangerousness.
      • It explain why Doctor Slone recognized his signal once he was approaching the Island during Chapter 4: Season 3.
  • All stolen relics in Thorne's collection come from destroyed planets, galaxies or realities.
    • Apollo and Artemis were destroyed by The Herald.
    • The realities making up Asteria Island except MEGA City's Reality are all confirmed to be fractured by X (Peace Syndicate) and Day Zero loading screen.
    • Triarchs' realm has been destroyed.
    • His next target was Athena (during Season 5).
    • The objects that Kado possesses during Chapter 5: Season 1 almost all come from Asteria which was also destroyed.
