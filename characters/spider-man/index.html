Spider-Man is a Marvel Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 9, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 3: Season 1 Battle Pass. Spider-Man is part of the Spider-Man Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Spider-Man's Symbiote Suit style is based on the Black Suit from Amazing Spider-Man Vol. 1 #252 (1984).
    • The style also features slight blue highlights, referencing its early appearances in the comics.
  • Spider-Man's Future Foundation Suit style is based on the suit he wore as part of the Future Foundation in FF #1 (2011).
  • The Spider Emblem on the back of Spider-Man's Scarlet Blackout style isn't filled in, which could be a mistake since it isn't present on the other 2 Super Level Styles.
  • Spider-Man was the first Marvel Series Outfit of Chapter 3.
  • There was a Legacy Achievement in Chapter 2: Season 4 that referenced Spider-Man, Thwip!. It could have been obtained by eliminating an opponent right after pulling them with a Harpoon Gun.
  • In one of the Seasonal Quests, The Scientist reveals he knows Spider-Man's real name, referring to him as Peter.
  • The Disney Horizons Live from D23 Showcase featured a version of Spider-Man with thinner eye lenses.

Peter Parker, commonly known as Spider-Man is a superhero from Reality: 616 that got into The Loop alongside The Daily Bugle. He is a member of the Avengers and ally of The Seven.

Personality

Personality[]

Spider-Man is a talkative person, usually annoying some people around him. He is also very supporting and caring of his friends and family.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

  • Spider-Senses
    • A result of genetic mutation after being bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter can sense danger around him before it happens.
  • Superhuman Strength
    • Can carry extremely heavy items.
  • Wall-Crawling
    • Allows him to grab on to any surface or ceiling.
  • Web Shooters
    • Machines located below his palms that creates a web-like matter, allowing him to swing around by grabbing on to anything.
Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Spider-Man arrives on the island, along with the Daily Bugle building.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

Spider-Man allies himself with The Seven, where he tells them that they can grab allies from his reality to help them while The Seven fix the Mech. Before he leaves to head back to his reality, The Origin tells him of a fragment of The Zero Point that ended up in Spider-Man's reality, which they believe will allow them to power the mech. After breaking into the Imagined Order and reaching the bridge, Spider-Man, The Foundation, Jones, and The Imagined are attacked by Doctor Slone and Gunnar. In an attempt to escape, Spider-Man brings Jones and The Imagined to his reality, where they gather the rest of The Avengers to try and get them to join him on the island. Spider-Man then meets up with Wolverine to convince him to help them find the fragment. They travel to Wakanda and get attacked at the Border. The team is greeted by Shuri, who allows them to investigate Wakanda for the missing Zero Point fragment. They then learn that the fragment was stolen by lizards. They investigate the Serpant Valley, where they are attacked by lizard ninjas taken from the loop. Spider-Man and the others begin to head to the Savage Land, believing this to be the work of Stegron. After reaching the Savage Land, Spider-Man and the rest reach the domain of Stegron and the High Evolutionary, the real thief of the Zero Shard. After distracting him, Spider-Man heads inside his hideout to find the shard, only to discover Doctor Doom has already taken it. Realizing they were deceived, they head back to Reality Zero to go after him. They end up at The Ice Moon and are led to The Ice King's castle by Wolverine. They find The Ice King inside and Jones walks towards him. He triggers a pressure plate and activates a hologram of The Ice King. He tells them his backstory but tells them they are too dangerous to be left free, since they could survive The Ice Moon's cold temperature. The team runs away but are ambushed by Doctor Doom who easily overpowers them. Spider-Man snatches The Zero Shard and escapes the castle, heading towards to the Mecha Team Leader. He brings the Zero Shard to The Paradigm and they power the Mech, returning to the castle to defeat Doom. Doom flees into the Island and the team goes back to the island for the final battle.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #5[]

Main Article: Spider-Man Zero

Iron Man and Spider-Man wear special suits before heading near Mighty Monument to meet up with the Seven. They attack The Collider's shield generators and watch the Mecha head towards it. The Mecha falls into a sinkhole caused by seismic charges, leading The Seven to go inside by themselves. After The Collider is destroyed, Spider-Man goes near Jones, who has been looking at his family's pictures. He tries to conforts him, but eventually leaves and goes back to Reality: 616 alongside the Avengers.
