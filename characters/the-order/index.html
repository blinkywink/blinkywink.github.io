Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for The Order's appearances?
The Order has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Order was the last member of The Seven to be released.
  • In The Order's icon, her hair is shown to swoop away from her face, but in-game, her hair is static and cannot possibly be positioned as such.
  • The Order appears twice in the Armored Battle Bus Loading Screen.
  • The Order's icon originally included her Back Bling. It was later removed after Update v20.40.
  • The Order's Interrogator style shares a close resemblance to The Ageless's chest insignia.
  • In the Imagination Unlimited Loading Screen, The Order is displayed with what appears to be her own glider, a variation of The Imagined Wingspan (Glider). However, she doesn't have her own glider.

The Order is a member of The Seven introduced in Chapter 3: Season 2. She is the twin sister of The Imagined and one of the daughters of Geno.

She is major character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a supporting, yet pivotal character of Chapter 1 and a major protagonist of Chapter 3 and Chapter 7: Season 1.

Personality

Personality[]

The Order is a loyal, serious, and honorable member of The Seven. She is deeply caring of her sister, wanting the best for her so she can be happy and safe. She differs from her sister in how she acts and what drives her mindset as a hero. The Order wishes to protect those who are in similar situations to her, to save and prevent others from going through what she has. The Order is empathetic and caring, wanting the best for everyone, yet has the ability to retain a strict and grounded perspective, seen by her in-game dialogue with The Origin accompanying her role in The Seven, and how she confronts her sister during in-game dialogue with hard truths about her past. Although in the absence of her sister, it is made apparent that she shares similar reckless tendencies.

Appearance

Appearance[]

In The Order's original outfit, she is an adult woman with a short, black bob haircut and blue eyes. Her suit consists of red and silver plating, plum and grey padding, and a generally dark undersuit. She has a brown utility belt and thigh holster. In The Order (Reforged), she has a black pixie haircut. Her suit consists of a variety of reds and golds for armour, and a dark blue-green for partial armour and undersuit. The suit also includes several lights at her ankles, thighs, back, chest, and wrists. These lights are more prominently red at her feet, and brighten to a pink as they move further up the body. The suit also incorporates high heels into the design.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Before Chapter 1[]

Geno created The Sisters and genetically modified them to be as perfect as possible, as the Imagined Order are on a hunt for the perfect civilization. Thanks to Geno's skills in science and biology, the Sisters and himself are immortal. The Omniverse was the playground of the IO, but Geno decided to throw the Sisters into The Loop, betting that they would be the first to escape. They weren't though, making Geno replace them with one of their more stronger Snapshots.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

Whilst she isn't seen during this season, it is known from the Resistance Quests that Geno deleted all Imagined Order files relating to her or her sister just as The Imagined was about to access them. She was the first Seven member to find out about The Collider, seeing a hologram of it held by Doctor Slone when she infiltrated an IO base.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

She helps The Seven attack an IO Outpost and finds a Portal Device with two charges left. She gives it to Jones so that he can transport himself and his group across The Omniverse. As she hands over the Device, she hugs her sister and asks, "You sure you want to do this alone?", but The Imagined reassures her she'll return. The Order later goes with the rest of The Seven to attack another Outpost. She stays on the Island alongside Captain Marvel, The Visitor and The Origin.

Collision / Fortnite X Marvel: Zero #5[]

As the attack on The Collider starts, The Imagined notices Geno on an upper platform, being the one responsible for firing an energy beam at Iron Man previously, causing him to crash. She calls on The Order for help, telling her that "daddy's home". The Sisters and Wolverine climb up The Collider and blast through a Collider level near Geno, where they get into a fight. Geno doesn't kill the sisters and rather shows mercy, but The Order kicks him in the head, sending him over the edge of the platform, where all three leap at him as he holds onto the railing. The Imagined jams his flight jets and sacrifices herself, falling into The Zero Point, taking Geno with her. They end up in "The Realm" and get discorporated, getting scattered across The Omniverse. Wolverine carries The Order back to the surface, and The Order asks him which Reality he's from. She says that out of respect for her sister, she'll avoid it. She angrily walks away, advising Wolverine to avoid her, telling him, "If you ever cross my path again, I'll unmake you".

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

She goes missing alongside the rest of The Seven. She was last seen in Lazy Lagoon by Seven Sentry Svenja. Her Wings were later found by The Looper.

Chapter 7: Season 1[]

She arrives on the island of the Golden Coast in a Seven meteor after receiving a distress signal from Agent Jones during the events of Zero Hour. After leaving her escape pod, she records audio logs that are found around the island. Eventually, she ended up getting captured by the Dark Voyager and was imprisoned in a cell under Lethal Labs. However, she enlisted the Looper's help in getting her out her cell and setting up a satellite to contact any remaining Seven members.
