Outfit[]

“
Take your medicine.
”
— In-Game Description

Remedy vs Toxin is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Level 40 of the Chapter 2: Season 1 Battle Pass. Remedy vs Toxin is part of the Bad Medicine Set.
