Rift Warden Stellan is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks or with the Rift Warden Stellan Item Shop Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks. Rift Warden Stellan was first released in Chapter 4: Season 1 and is part of the Rift's Warden Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Rift Warden Stellan's appearances?
Rift Warden Stellan has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Stellan is the head alchemist of the Oathbound and the creator of the Rift Gate at Warden's Watch in Fortnite.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Stellan is a slim man with long blonde hair and golden eyes. He wears a white and gold armor decorated with creepy hands hugging the armor.

Personality

Personality[]

Stellan is a smart and well-mannered man who always works alone. After getting death threats from the Shapeless Man, He hired The Looper to help him finish the Rift Gate for the Shapeless Man as soon as possible.

History

History[]

Pre-Chapter 4[]

Centuries ago, The nameless young man came to the Oathbound at the order of the Shapeless Man. He received his name "Stellan" and the Warden's Oath as gifts from the Ageless Champion. After that he stopped having dreams.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

In Oathbound Quests Part 1, AMIE encountered Stellan in the Citadel studying her refined Kinetic Ore. He said to AMIE: "Your gift intrigues me... But do you truly know what you have given us?".

In Oathbound Quests Part 2, He explain to The Looper that he has been tasked by The Ageless Champion to create a Rift Gate, a bridge to travels realities. He then asks The Looper for help and later explains that the Rift Gate is the Ageless Champion's dearest wish but also that he has his own reason to built it. After further help from The Looper, he reveals something he told to no one: the existence of a being, a "Shapeless Man" who observes them with dark purpose and explains that it's necessary to finish the Rift Gate or else there will be terrible consequences.

In Oathbound Quests Part 4, Stellan can no longer sleep. The Shapeless Man's terrorize his dreams everyday since the day AMIE bought the Refined Kinetic Ore to him. Stellan activates the Rift Gate using the unstable energy from the Kinetic Ore. but someone sabotaged the gate and manipulated the coordinates causing it to overload and explode. The gate is destroyed, the sky is torn and The Shapeless Man disappeared. Stellan finds a prophecy from exiled sages of thee Oathbound. The texts prophesize a... "a beautiful monster, enabled by the hubris of a champion." and silence itself is the prophecy. Stellan thanked the Looper for assisting him in making of the Rift Gate and and bid farewell.
