Outfit[]

“
It was all fun and spacewalks... Until they left him behind.
”
— In-Game Description

J.B. Chimpanski is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 7 and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 2: Season 8 Battle Pass. J.B. Chimpanski is part of the Astronaut P-14 Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • J.B. Chimpanski has a Built-In Emote called Monkey Mosh.
  • J.B. Chimpanski is inspired by the Soviet monkeys sent to space during the Cold War.
    • This may be a reference to the 2008 animated film Space Chimps.
  • The text on his Loading Screen translates to "To the sun! To the stars!"
