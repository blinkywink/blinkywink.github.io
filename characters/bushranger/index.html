Outfit[]

“
He's one scrappy little tree.
”

Bushranger is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks, with the Brie Larson's Locker Bundle for 2,000 V-Bucks or with the Woodsy Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks. Bushranger was first released in Chapter 2: Season 1 and is part of the Woodsy Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Bushranger's appearances?
Bushranger has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 1[]

He was seen watching 8-Ball and Scratch dualing out in Weeping Woods

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

Disguised as a tree, he was called in by Midas for a meeting at The Agency

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

He attempted to adopt an Alien Parasite and ended up in a grave. Weeks later a small tree emerge from his grave.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

In Vibin' Quests, The Scientist brings Bushranger's newest form to talk to the Reality Tree to see what will happen. Bushranger thinks that the tree is darkness and big scary.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

In Paradise Quests, The Paradigm brings Bushranger to talk to the dead Reality Tree in preparation for the Fracture event. Zero Point energy began flowing back into the tree after the talk.

Fracture[]

He was helping everyone finding the Zero Fragments for the Zero Point.
