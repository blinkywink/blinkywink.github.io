Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Before Update v36.10, the names of Morgan Myst's Firebird and Darkfire styles were inverted.
  • In Update v36.30, Morgan Myst's Darkfire edit style was corrected after the texture of her clothes previously matched the texture of her raven decal.
  • Her powers names are Umbrakinesis (Darkness manipulation) and Avikinesis (Avian/Birds/Crows manipulation).
  • Although Morgan shares her name with Morgan le Fay, a witch of Arthurian legend, her design bears greater similarity to a war figure from Irish mythology, the Morrígan, or "Phantom Queen." Written as inspiring warriors while appearing as/transforming into a crow, a personal symbol, the Morrígan today is also closely associated with the arcane and occult circles.

Morgan Myst is a superhero, the leader of The Supernovas, a member of Supernova's Formation Tau and a graduated student from the Supernova Academy.

She is the main protagonist of Chapter 6: Season 3.

Personality

Personality[]

Myst is a very smart person but also timid and introverted. She may have lots of self doubts and anxiety, fears and insecurities, but when the time comes where heroes are needed, she will step up as proper leader who will help her friends through tough times.
