Overview

This article is about the Outfit. For the Save the World Hero and Character, see Cassie "Clip" Lipman.

“
Homebase's surefire munitions maker.
”
— In-Game Description

Clip is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Full Clip Pack in the Item Shop for $15.99. Clip was first released in Chapter 4: Season 3 and is part of the Full Clip Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Clip's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Signature
Base


Clip received a higher-detailed style in Update v30.00, which replaced the original one.
Players have the option to choose between both.

Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Clip's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Clip's appearances?
Clip has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
