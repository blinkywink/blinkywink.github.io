Outfit[]

“
Wear the armor of an Imperial Stormtrooper.
”
— In-Game Description

Imperial Stormtrooper is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks. Imperial Stormtrooper was first released in Chapter 2: Season 1 and is part of the Original Trilogy Set.

Imperial Stormtrooper could also have been obtained by purchasing STAR WARS Jedi: Fallen Order on the Epic Games Store before November 30th 2020.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Imperial Stormtrooper's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Icon Featured

This LEGO® Style was introduced alongside LEGO Fortnite in Update v28.01. As a result, it was not visible on Imperial Stormtrooper's detail page in the Shop or Locker starting from Update v28.00, even though it was usable immediately upon the mode's release.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Imperial Stormtrooper's appearances?
Imperial Stormtrooper has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia[]

  • Imperial Stormtrooper's reaction to Kylo Ren is similar to a quote from Watto to Master Qui-Gon Jinn in Episode I - The Phantom Menace, but with "Sith" in place of "Jedi."
  • Imperial Stormtrooper's reaction to himself (and the Sith Trooper) is a reference to their background conversations.

Description[]

They could have been found in Galactic Empire controlled POIs and will shoot at the player if they notice them undisguised, occasionally battling Chewbacca if he is also present.

All Imperial Stormtroopers will be eliminated once all in the area are knocked, and they will attempt to revive each other if they remain alive unless if Darth Vader is present.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Imperial Stormtrooper's Guard codename is SipTea.
    • This codename was previously used by the 501st Troopers.
  • Before Update v29.40, the Imperial Stormtroopers had the fewest number of guards spawned out of all the Guards released. Due to only being relegated as bodyguards for Darth Vader at the time.
  • Due to appearing in Chapter 5: Season 2, Imperial Stormtroopers are the first Guards that can be knocked down since the Wild Card Suits during Chapter 4: Season 3.
  • The codenames for the Imperial Stormtroopers' Galactic Battle voicelines suggest they were recorded during Chapter 5: Season 2, but didn't get added into the game with the voiceline set used during the season.
