Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Ageless Champion's Epic TBD Boss Outfit ID is Character_Knight_Boss_NPC and his codename is EmblemKnight.
    • The Ageless Champion's Noble Shroud is built into his Boss Outfit rather than being a separate Back Bling. It was initially exclusive to him until Update v23.20.
  • The Ageless Champion was the first Boss to gain an additional Mythic Weapon without replacing another in it's place.
    • Because of this The Ageless Champion is also the first Boss since The Mandalorian to drop two Mythic Items at once.
  • The Ageless Champion was the first boss of Chapter 4.
  • Added in Update v39.20 are numerous developer videos, one of which showcased unused Kinetic Ore styled visual effects once intended for The Ageless Champion, along with a "Machine" that would have been involved with him. Suggesting he would have been a more complex Boss than he was upon release.
    • Going along with the unused Statue Destroyed voiceline, it would appear The Ageless Champion had a scrapped ability that would involve statues (likely the later designation for the Machine) within The Citadel that projected a Kinetic field around him, needing them to be destroyed in order make battling the Boss easier.

The Ageless, real name Geno, is an ancient Snapshot of Geno[1] created during his youth. He is the ruler of the Oathbound empire, the former ruling faction on the Asteria Island during Chapter 4: Season 1.

Personality

Personality[]

The Ageless acts as a perfectionist[4] ruler, striving to bring order to Reality.[5] These traits were inherited from his original self. The Ageless' greatest desire is to explore The Omniverse. To achieve this, he actively sought to create a Rift Gate, a task he entrusted to Rift Warden Stellan. Following The Big Bang and the collapse of Asteria Island, he became an explorer on Helios Island.[6] Unlike Geno, The Ageless enjoys combat and dueling.[7] However, he remains arrogant and proud, ignoring warnings from sages about Stellan. When the sages made a prophecy about "a beautiful monster... enabled by the hubris of a champion", which seemed to implicate him as the cause of a future catastrophe, he dismissed their concerns, forbade their work and banished them.

Appearance

Appearance[]

The Ageless appear as a younger version of Geno with longer hair. His armor looks nearly identical to Geno but with "medieval knight" chain mail and the armor is in white and blue color instead of black and gold. The central orb symbol of the Imagined Order can be found at the back of his armor.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Before Chapter 1[]

The Ageless is created as a Snapshot of Geno[1], after Geno abandonned the Oathbound Empire to found the Imagined Order, The Ageless went to rule the Empire himself.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

The Ageless arrives on Asteria after his castle got pulled by The Zero Point. In Oathbound Quests, he has a duel with The Looper which he loses, and tasks Rift Warden Stellan to build a stable Rift Gate as bridge to new realities for him. Stellan reads ancient texts from Oathbound's Exiled Sages and warns that “A beautiful Monster” will be enabled by his hubris. The Rift Gate would eventually fail and bring MEGA City to the island.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

The Ageless hires Volpez's Winter Tricksters to take out Snowheart and the Frozen Red Knight[10].

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

As The Ageless Explorer, he ended up on Helios and began exploring this new reality[6], especially the Snow Biome, with the aim to uncover its secrets and rule over ice[11].

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • On Justin Holt's Artstation, The Ageless is named Geno, and considered as the same as the one who did appear in Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War and who led the Imagined Order.
    • Since Zero War's Geno never made an in-game appearance, it is possible that The Ageless was originally planned to be him, instead of a snapshot from a distant past.
