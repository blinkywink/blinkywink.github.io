Lil Whip is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Lil Whip Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks. Lil Whip was first released in Season 7 and is part of the Two Scoops Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Lil Whip's appearances?
Lil Whip has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Lil Whip has a chance to spawn in one of six different parts of the map each game, always staying put near his Ice cream truck doing emotes.

If a player gets near him he will throw down Ice Cream for them to pick up. The Ice Cream he drops will vary from 5 different flavors and all have different effects. Normal, Guzzle, Frozen, Spicy, and rarely; Lil'Whip's Special Serve.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

  • During the time of The Seven's preparation for war, not much happened to Lil' Whip as he is just an ice cream vendor and is just doing business as normal.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

  • As war with the Imagined Order broke out, he was found hiding inside the attic of the bookstore near his parlor. Relaying recon back to The Resistance like the rest of the Foodie Battalion. He and The Imagined did an unknown activity.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

  • Now that the war had ended Lil' Whip sought to enter the rapping game and aspired to become the hottest soft-serve rapper in the game. Unfortunately the music he produced isn't doing well in the industry, and with the surplus bars he borrowed from his benefactors he has now entered severe debt and is getting desperate to sell his album. After No Sweat Summer ended, Lil Whip disappeared.

No Sweat Summer[]

  • As the summer rolled out and his fear of his benefactor looming closer, Lil' Whip left his shop to go and hand out ice cream to Loopers, while leaving Beach Bomber with the responsibility of looking after the SoFDeeZ Ice Cream Parlor in his absence. Even if his method isn't helping him financially as he isn't earning any bars in return.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

  • Lil Whip appears as a Rift Encounter, once again trying to sell his soft serve in the SoFDeeZ Ice Cream Truck.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

  • In the Chapter 4: Season 2 cinematic trailer Lil Whip was crushed by a rifting SoFDeeZ building in MEGA City. This Lil Whip was in the Loop when it happened, as he breaks into small blue cubes on elimination.
