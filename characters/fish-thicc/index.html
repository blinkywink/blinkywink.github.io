Outfit[]

“
More ripped than the tide.
”
— In-Game Description

Fish Thicc is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 5, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 4 Battle Pass. Fish Thicc is part of the Buffer Fish Set.
