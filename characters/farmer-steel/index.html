Farmer Steel is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks. Farmer Steel was first released in Chapter 2: Season 2 and is part of the Farm Stand Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.

Trivia[]

  • According to Drew Hill, the idea for Farmer Steel came up when he visited Anarchy Acres while playing Battle Royale, which abides with his codename, AnarchyAcresFarmer.[1]
    • Based on this information, Farmer Steel was designed between Season 1 and Season 4.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

He asks The Looper to help him investigates IO Helicopter and aliens activities near his farm. He also asks Mari to help him look for volunteers to protect his corn.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

He was abducted by the The Last Reality and imprisoned in The Mothership. During Operation: Sky Fire event, he can be seen gliding down with his Chicken back to The Island.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

During Fracture event, he help Loopers finding the Zero Point Fragments so the Zero Point can fix his the farm.
