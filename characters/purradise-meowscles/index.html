”
— Meowscles

Meowscles is a humanoid muscular calico cat, a secret agent and a member of SHADOW. He has a son named Kit, a younger sister named Meow Skulls, and a "brother" (alt-reality counterpart self) named Phantom Meowscles. He is the owner of the cat food brand Meowscle's Morsels. He is one of Midas' agent and is a good friend of Skye.

Meowscles has 9 lives, and has lost at least three of them as of the events of Fortnitemares 2022.

Personality

Personality[]

Meowscles usually acts like a regular cat and has an interest in lifting and cat puns. He is said to not be great at expressing his feelings. He seems to be very supportive of his family. He can speak in both cat language and human language.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Meowscles is a muscular and humanoid calico cat in blue jeans. He has a heart with "Lynx" written inside of it tattooed on his right bicep.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

He is recruited as an agent of GHOST in The Agency. He guards The Yacht, but later switches sides with SHADOW. He is kicked out of the Yacht by Contract Giller and Deadpool. He goes to the Box Factory.

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

Meowscles rides a Loot Shark into The Fortilla and encounters many Loopers on the way. His son, Kit, builds an army of Henchmen and they convert the Box Factory into Catty Corner.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point[]

His SHADOW snapshot is seen in the fight near catwoman in Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 2.

Zero Crisis Finale[]

Meowscles appears on the route that John Jones followed to reach Zero Point, where he takes a swing at a Predator, only for the Predator to appear unfazed by the punch. The laser targeting reticle of the Predator's plasmacaster appears on Meowscles' forehead and kills him for the 2nd time[7].

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

The Last Reality use a cat food brand owned by Meowscles called Meowscle's Morsels and use it to install artwork that hijacks higher brain function.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

He moves in to Scratch Pad, to the west of Reality Falls. According to his Character Collection Book description, he has been practicing his speaking and elocution, but has been having some trouble with pronunciation. He reunited with Midas in one of his special dialogue.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

Meowscles' lack of ability to express his emotions slowly drifts him apart with Lynx. Kit helps him to reconnect with her using his playlist of 20-second videos by "SelfHelpCalicoPHD".

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

As Purradise Meowscles enjoying the summer, his special dialogue with Midsummer Midas indicates that he is napping on a covert ops mission. He is later contacted by Stingray, a SHADOW agent who tells him they have work to do, confirming that at the same time that SHADOW has not been disbanded and that Meowscles is working for them again.

LEGO Fortnite[]

At some point after Chapter 4: Season 4, Meowscles is taken to Reality: UN-85 by a Rift.

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

Meowscles gives Peter Griffin a physical test.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Marigold suspects that Midas is somewhere on the Island, so she calls Meowscles and the rest of Midas' Crew back together. When talking about Midas, Meowscles claims he's "Sweeter than you'd think."

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Meowscles has a heart with "Lynx" written inside of it tattooed on his right bicep, meaning he could possibly be in love with her.
    • This was confirmed in Chapter 3: Season 3, Kit being his and Lynx's son. In Chapter 3: Season 4 it was revealed that Meowscles and Lynx had broken up, and that Meowscles wants to get back together.
