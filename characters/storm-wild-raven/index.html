Overview

“
The haunted envoy of the Storm King.
”
— In-Game Description

Storm-Wild Raven is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Storm-Wild Raven Starter Pack in the Item Shop for $4.99. Storm-Wild Raven was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Storm-Wild Raven's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Storm-Wild Raven's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Storm-Wild Raven's appearances?
Storm-Wild Raven has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
