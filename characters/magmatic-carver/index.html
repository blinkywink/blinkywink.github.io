Ultima Carver was a hostile Boss exclusive to Fortnitemares 2025 introduced during Chapter 5: Season 4. He could have been found in either Mt Fetch, Pumpkin Pass, or Viney Shafts after Update v37.50.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Ultima Carver drops the following items when eliminated. He also dropped 12 Bars.

Pumpkin Launcher FlowBerry Fizz

Ultima Carver uses a Rare Pumpkin Launcher and Wood Stake Shotgun to attack players. He will not drop either weapon when eliminated.

Pumpkin Launcher Wood Stake Shotgun
Behavior

Behavior[]

In any of his possible spawn locations, Ultima Carver will attack players on sight. Alternating between using his Wood Stake Shotgun and periodically firing his Pumpkin Launcher while in combat.

Ultima Carver was a hostile Boss variant exclusive to the Demon Rush Limited Time Mode.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Ultima Carver will wield the following items, but not drop them upon elimination:

Pumpkin Launcher Wood Stake Shotgun
Behavior

Behavior[]

Ultima Carver has a chance to spawn on Round 2. Suddenly appearing in the very center of the Storm zone. While in combat he will alternate between using his Wood Stake Shotgun and periodically firing his Pumpkin Launcher.

Once eliminated, Ultima Carver will drop a bountiful pile of Weapons and Consumables.

Ultima Carver also has a chance to appear during the Score Attack Round.
