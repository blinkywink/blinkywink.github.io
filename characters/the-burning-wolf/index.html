The Burning Wolf is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during September 2021, as part of the September 2021 Fortnite Crew Pack. The Burning Wolf was released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and is part of The First Shadows Set.

History

History[]

The Burning Wolf was an extremely experienced swordsman before and throughout being recruited by Midas. He became a member of The First Shadows alongside Chaos Origins and Sierra.

Unknown Time Period[]

The Burning Wolf sneaks into the entrance of a castle in an unknown location, before going on a murder spree once he enters. Killing every guard in his path with ease, he makes his way to the throne room, placing his hand on his sword in preparation to unsheathe it and assassinate the person sitting in the throne (likely out of vengeance via the description of his Burning Fangs), however it turns around and instead reveals an invitational card left by Midas, who had been watching him the entire time.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

The Burning Wolf reemerges onto Apollo and reunites with the rest of The First Shadows.
