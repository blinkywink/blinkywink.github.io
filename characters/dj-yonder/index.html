DJ Yonder is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Season 6 Battle Pass. DJ Yonder is part of the Twin Turntables Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • DJ Yonder has a female counterpart named DJ Bop.
  • DJ Yonder is featured on the back of the Creative Phone.
  • If a player leaves Fortnite Festival during a performance or if No Fill is selected, DJ Yonder can appear as a substitute band member.
