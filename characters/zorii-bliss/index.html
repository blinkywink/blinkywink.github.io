Zorii Bliss is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks. Zorii Bliss was first released in Chapter 2: Season 1 and is part of the The New Trilogy Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Zorii Bliss's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Icon Featured


This LEGO® Style was introduced alongside LEGO Fortnite in Update v28.01. As a result, it was not visible on Zorii Bliss's detail page in the Shop or Locker starting from Update v28.00, even though it was usable immediately upon the mode's release.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Zorii Bliss's appearances?
Zorii Bliss has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
