Lynx, also known as the Void Walker, is an agent of The Last Reality who was first introduced during OG Chapter 1: Season 7 and Chapter Seven: Season 1 simultaneously.

She is an Alternate Reality Counterpart of the original Lynx who's been working for aeons under the Dark Voyager if we are to believe his own words.

She serves as the secondary antagonist of Chapter Seven: Season 1 and OG Chapter 1: Season 7.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

Source Ability
Cube Powered Combat Suit
An evolved combat suit created by Lynx with the power of the cubes from The Last Reality. Her outfit allows her to travel through space and the void, earning her the title of Void Walker.
Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter Seven: Season 1[]

Void Walker Lynx arrived alongside her master, the Dark Voyager in Reality: 266. Although her exact actions are unknown, she temporarily left to Reality: Unknown,[3] investigating about the strange reality resembling that of her own counterpart's past.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Void Walker Lynx is likely from the same reality as Dark Voyager (Reality Redacted) as they both have the same description of "\\REDACTED\\".
    • Although she looks very much like the original Lynx, Void Walker Lynx cannot be the same person because the Dark Voyager explains that the loyalty of his minions was acquired over a very long period, "aeons" according to him.
      • This version is also openly evil, unlike the original Lynx.
