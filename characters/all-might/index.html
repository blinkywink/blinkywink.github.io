All Might is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks or with the All Might Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. All Might was first released in Chapter 4: Season 1 and is part of the My Hero Academia Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for All Might's appearances?
All Might has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • All Might's matching Back Bling is U.A. Cape, which shares his Transform codename (despite being bundled with Principal Nezu instead).
    • Unlike Superman's Cape, the U.A Cape will be visible while using All Might's second style.
  • All Might was the first Legendary Outfit to be released in the Item Shop in Chapter 4.
  • When using the Symbol of Peace Emote with All Might, he does a unique laughing animation.
