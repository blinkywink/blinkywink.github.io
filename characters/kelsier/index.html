Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kelsier's appearance seems to take inspiration from how he is described in Chapter 5 of The Final Empire.
    • "Large and enveloping, the cloak wasn’t constructed from a single piece of cloth—rather, it was made up of hundreds of long, ribbonlike strips. [...] He pulled a pair of glass daggers from the bag, checked them for nicks, and slid them into the sheaths. He kicked off his shoes and stripped off his stockings, leaving himself barefoot on the chill stones. With the shoes also went the last bit of metal on his person save for his coin pouch and the three vials of metals in his belt."
      • This should be noted as Kelsier was never given a definitive appearance other than fan interpretations based on information gathered from the novels, and a few licensed artworks; though with varying interpretations, too.
        • Some aspects are incorrectly depicted, however. Such as the sheaths being around his thighs instead of his belt — which has a buckle, something the novel mentions it does not have.
  • Kelsier's three vials contain what appears to be Slurp instead of alcohol like it does in The Final Empire.
  • According to Brandon Sanderson, author of the Mistborn series, the idea of bringing Kelsier into Fortnite was brought up by Donald Mustard.[1]
    • In the same thread, Sanderson mentions that it takes atleast six months for a collaboration to be done, noting that it took five for Kelsier.
