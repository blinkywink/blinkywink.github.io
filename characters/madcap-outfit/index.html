Outfit[]

“
The master of mushrooms enters the fight.
”
— In-Game Description

Madcap is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Fungus King Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Madcap was first released in Chapter 3: Season 1 and is part of the Fungus King Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Madcap seems to have been in development as early as 2019.
    • Madcap's CID was first leaked in a massive datamining of IDs for upcoming or scrapped cosmetics; which occured on September 4th 2019, during Season X.
    • Madcap's CID was blacklisted between Outfits 609 and 611, located between Chapter 2: Season 1 Outfits.
  • A silhouette of Madcap could have been seen near a bridge in Misty Meadows on mobile devices.
  • Madcap recieved the Phosphorescent Style in Update v37.10.
