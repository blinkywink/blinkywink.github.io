Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Rap Princess Ice Spice's appearances?
Rap Princess Ice Spice has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Ice Spice will attack the player if she notices them undisguised. Though if disguised, she can be talked to for additional dialogue.

Shortly after getting knocked down, she will Self-Revive and join the team of the player who defeated her. Ice Spice will replenish all of her health and act as a Medic Specialist Character, Providing Chili Chug Splashes to teammates who are not full on health.

Besides this, she will grant the team shes following access to her GHOST Chests, and neutrality from her henchmen, Sentry Turrets and Sentry Cameras.

Ice Spice, when recruited, may also request a High Five or Hug from the player she is following. If ignored, she will cancel the offer and turn away.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Ice Spice's Boss music is Oh Shhh... by Ice Spice and Travis Scott.
  • Ice Spice's revive stinger is a portion of In Ha Mood.
  • Whenever the player performs an Emote as a follower, Ice Spice will choose between performing In Ha Mood and Oh Shhh, rather than just performing Dance Moves like other Bosses.
  • Unlike other Hireable Characters, Ice Spice will prioritize walking over running when moving around. Though she will still sprint and teleport to the player she is following if far enough.
  • Ice Spice is the only Snoop's Enterprise Boss to have her Throwable Item removed from her inventory. Due to community backlash from her sabotaging the player she would follow with her Chiller Grenades.
  • If the player dismisses Ice Spice, she will be eliminated instantly, as opposed to returning to her starting location like other Hireable Characters.
  • Along with other Remix bosses, Ice Spice will not drop her items if eliminated by The Storm.
  • If the player that knocked Ice Spice is eliminated before she Self-Revives, she will remain knocked and can be Revived manually by another player. However the popup that indicates the option will have no text.
