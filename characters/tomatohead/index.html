Outfit[]

“
Extra saucy.
”

Tomatohead is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks, with the Sliced Bundle for 2,400 V-Bucks or with HeyStan's Locker Bundle for 2,300 V-Bucks. Tomatohead was first released in Season 3 and is part of the Pizza Pit Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Tomatohead's appearances?
Tomatohead has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Tomatohead received the Crown style in Update v5.30, along with Quests associated with it to unlock it.
    • Tomatohead was the first cosmetic item to receive a new style after its release.
  • Tomatohead has a female counterpart named Crustina.
  • Similar to Beef Boss, Tomatohead's design is inspired by the logo of one of the in-game restaurants, Uncle Pete's Pizza Pit.
  • In Batman/Fortnite: Foundation, Tomatohead seems to have an actual tomato for a head instead of just wearing a costume.
  • Before Chapter 2 Remix, Tomatohead's style was obtainable via Tomatohead Challenges.
