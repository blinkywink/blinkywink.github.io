Outfit[]

“
Loyal captain of the First Order.
”
— In-Game Description

Captain Phasma is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Captain Phasma Bundle for 2,000 V-Bucks. Captain Phasma was first released in Chapter 6: Mini Season 1 and is part of The New Trilogy Set.

Captain Phasma also could have been obtained by purchasing the First Order LEGO® Pass.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Captain Phasma's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Captain Phasma dropped the following items when eliminated:

Captain Phasma's F-11D Blaster Phasma's Blaster Medallion Thermal Imploder

Captain Phasma used a special version of the Thermal Imploder in combat but wouldn't have dropped it when eliminated.

Thermal Imploder
Behavior

Behavior[]

There is no hope fighting against The First Order![]

Captain Phasma will attack the player if she notices them, throwing up to three Thermal Imploders at a time while in combat.

When her health is drained, she will be instantly defeated.
