Jonesy The First is an Uncommon Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks, with the Battle Classics for 2,000 V-Bucks or with The Joneses Bundle for 2,400 V-Bucks. Jonesy The First was first released in Chapter 2: Season 4 and is part of the Origins Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Jonesy The First's appearances?
Jonesy The First has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Jonesy The First is believed to be the very first snapshot of Agent Jones created by The Loop. He was first introduced in Chapter 2: Season 6, but was (probably) seen in Chapter 1.

Personnality

Personnality[]

Jonesy The First is very different from the Jones we know. Indeed as a fighter he is extremely arrogant and often believes himself superior to others, even to his original. He is obsessed with fighting and often underestimates his opponents. Just like Jones he sometimes acts like an idiot, however he knows how to act seriously when he really has to.

History

History[]

Before Fortnite[]

Jonesy The First was created before Bunker Jonesy, himself one of Jones' oldest Snapshots, having been created when the Imagined Order was experimenting on the Zero Point.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Jonesy The First plays a central role, when The Looper, searching for Agent Jones following the message left by The Foundation, started to seek all of Jones' snapshots. After discussions with a large number of Snapshot of Jones, Sash Sergeant and Slurp Jonesy revealed the existence of "The First Jones" to the Looper, leading them to seek him. Upon meeting him, The Looper explained the situation with The Spire, The Foundation and Agent Jones and Jonesy The First agreed to help them if they proved strong enough. He lost the duel and began to reveal some secrets about The Spire, warning them not to let anyone powerful be corrupted, as this might attract the attention of something. At the same time Raz seized a fragment of the Spire's Core making him extremely powerful. They asked The Looper to get the Spire Artifact back before Raz attract more unwanted attention however it was already too late.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Jonesy The First was released by The Seven for an unspecified period and with other Joneses founded The Joneses. An independent village against both the Imagined Order and The Seven. However later, the village finally agreed to ally with The Seven against The Imagined Order, thanks to the help of The Looper.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

He participated in the war against The Imagined Order as a member of The Resistance and leader of the Joneses.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

After the war ended, Jonesy The First sought a new occupation involving combat.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

With the arrival of The Herald, Jonesy the First and the other Joneses transformed The Joneses into Fort Jonesy to avoid Chrome altogether. He and the other Joneses were tasked by Agent Jones (having taken over command) to monitor The Herald.
