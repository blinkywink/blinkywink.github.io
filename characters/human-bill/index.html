Human Bill is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,800 V-Bucks, with the Alien Abduction Bundle for 2,500 V-Bucks or with the Human Bill Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. Human Bill was first released in Chapter 2: Season 7 and is part of the Hot Alien Summer Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Human Bill's appearances?
Human Bill has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Human Bill has a chance to spawn all around the Golden Coast at one of his locations, surrounded by two Henchfans praising him. He will not attack the player on sight unless he or his Guards are attacked, they aim their weapon at him for too long, or they get too close to him to slowly build up suspicion.

While in combat, he will occasionally throw Shockwave Grenades at his target.

In matches where players use the Battle Bus, Human Bill can be seen jumping out to where he will spawn.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Human Bill's codename is PAVBoss_Gamma.
  • Human Bill's combat music reuses Zyg and Choppy and the Trespassers' combat music from Chapter 2: Season 7. Altering the way the Idle and Base layers loop, and repurposing the Guard and Boss layers into Idle to Threatened and Defeated stingers respectively.
  • Human Bill, along with Beach Brutus and Hush, are the first Bosses to both drop out of the Battle Bus and thank the Bus Driver. As their names can be seen on the Message Feed before jumping.
