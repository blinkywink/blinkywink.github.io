“
All we want for Winterfest is Mariah!

Santa Suit Mariah is an Icon Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Mariah Carey Bundle for 2,800 V-Bucks. Santa Suit Mariah was first released in Chapter 6: Season 1 and is part of the Mariah Carey Set.

Santa Suit Mariah was a Character exclusive to Winterfest 2024 introduced during Chapter 6: Season 1. She could of been found at Giant Ice Block after the Defrosting Event.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Players who approach Santa Suit Mariah will find their current Emote Wheel replaced with a special Holiday Emotes section, featuring the All I Want For Christmas Is You Emote in all eight slots. Using the emote will cause Santa Suit Mariah to dance alongside the player while charging an EMOTE! meter. After 15 seconds, the player will receive Mythic Holiday Presents!.
