Greyscale guppy. Aspiring rainbowfish.

Toona Fish is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by spending 9 Battle Stars on Page 1 of the Chapter 2: Season 8 Battle Pass. Toona Fish is part of the Inkville Gang set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Toona Fish is the first Fishstick outfit to be featured in a Battle Pass. The second would be Fish Thicc in Chapter 4: Season 4.
  • Unlocking styles for Toona Fish counted as reward progression for the Chapter 2: Season 8 Battle Pass, allowing players to unlock Bonus Rewards without actually finishing the main pass and unlocking Carnage. This has since been patched.
  • Certain aspects of Toona Fish can be colored in unlockable styles that can't be changed with the basic colors, such as eye color and separately coloring bottom and top parts of the hat.
  • In Update v25.00, using the Full Googly Emote with Toona Fish would result in the googly eyes being notably oversized.
  • 22 colors (including default) to the power of 9 for the different parts makes 1,207,269,217,834 different possible variations of Toona Fish (While including the preset styles).
