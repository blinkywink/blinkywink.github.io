Nolan Chance is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Chapter 4: Season 4 Battle Pass. Nolan Chance is part of the Maverick Mastermind Set.

Nolan Chance is a selfish and self-confident professional heist mastermind. He is one of the leader of The Heist Crew and one of the main protagonist of Chapter 4: Season 4.

History

History[]

Pre-Chapter 4: Season 4[]

  • Nolan Chance helped Antonia in robberies and was her partner, but at one of them he betrayed her and went on the run. He is also known as the mastermind behind Slap factory heist.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

  • After the arrival of Kado Thorne, Nolan was recruited by Doctor Slone to carry out a heist and steal what he is hiding in Eclipsed Estate, without knowing what it is. He will then team up with The Looper, also recruited by Slone to assemble a team, first looking for new faces, but ending up having to recall his old heist crew.
  • The Looper managed to recruit Piper Place and with her help convince Antonia to forgive Nolan and join the crew instead of sabotaging him. Nolan and The Looper then set up a ruse to force Fish Thicc to join the Crew, forcing him to owe them a debt by asking them for a favor.
  • The Looper then managed to recruit Mae with the help of Fish Thicc and infiltration expert Khaby Lame joined the group.
  • Once Kado Thorne returned to the present, possessing Oro's head which he added to his collection, the Heist Crew attempted to rob him once, but it ended up going badly when Kado Thorne himself showed up and took his Revenant form to attack them, forcing the Crew to flee.
  • Jones, who returned to the Island following Slone's request for help, began working with her to set up the next part of the plan once the team manage to steal the Time Machine.
