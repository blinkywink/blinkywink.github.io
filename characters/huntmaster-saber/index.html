Huntmaster Saber was a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 3: Season 2. He could have been found at The Collider after Update v20.40.

Huntmaster Saber dropped the following items when eliminated. He also dropped a Healing Item and Bars.

Huntmaster Saber used a Legendary Thermal Scoped Assault Rifle to attack players. He will not drop the rifle when eliminated.

Thermal Scoped Assault Rifle

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Huntmaster Saber's Boss Outfit ID is CID_NPC_Athena_Commando_M_Masterkey and his codename is Thunder Meow.
    • In Update v20.10, Huntmaster Saber's TBD Boss Outfit received a new style, Order. This would be used for IO Hunter.
    • IO Hunter's codename also plays off Huntmaster Saber's as it is "Thunder Kitty" or "Thunder Meow Light".
  • Huntmaster Saber is immune to fall damage. This is likely to prevent him from accidentally dying by falling off the IO Airship he was patrolling in before being relocated to The Collider.
  • Huntmaster Saber's Scratch Spin ability is similar to Wolverine's Spin Slash. However, it deals slightly more damage.
  • When breaking obstacles, Huntmaster Saber wields the Adamantium Claws. A Harvesting Tool exclusive to Wolverine.
  • Despite the fact Huntmaster Saber has his own battle music, it does not play properly due to an error in the game's code. Causing priority to be shifted onto the IO Boss theme instead.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • IO Hunter’s codenames were "Thunder Meow Light" and "Thunder Kitty".
    • This plays off Huntmaster Saber's Boss codename which was "Thunder Meow".
  • It was possible to prevent IO Hunter from spawning by quickly eliminating the retreating IO Forces before Stage 3 started.
  • IO Hunter's appearance is an inverted recolor of Huntmaster Saber. Likely indicating he is a disciple trained by him.
  • IO Hunter reuses Huntmaster Saber's voicelines in combat.
  • Just like Huntmaster Saber, he:
    • Wields the Adamantium Claws when breaking obstacles. A Harvesting Tool exclusive to Wolverine.
    • Uses the standard Chapter 3 Boss music despite having another unique track.
      • Though in the setting he is found it, an argument can be made that its more appropriate than Huntmaster Saber's Boss track.

Huntmaster Saber, formerly known as Master Key, was an IO enforcer and former guardian of Sunny Steps.

He is a supporting antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale serving as a supporting antagonist of Chapter 3: Season 2.

Personality

Personality[]

Huntmaster Saber is described as a great tactician with well-documented tactics, while also being ruthless and merciless. He is feared by many and thought to be a legend.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Season 8[]

Huntmaster Saber, at that time known as Master Key, is tasked with protecting Sunny Steps and a number of keys. These keys get stolen by Hybrids sent by The Prisoner. After The Vault's discovery, Master Key joins Luxe, Blackheart and Sidewinder to take a look at it. He later goes into hiding and is portrayed as a feared legendary figure.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

He comes out of hiding and joins the Imagined Order. He guards their Airships and fights against The Seven in their attempts to reclaim POIs.
