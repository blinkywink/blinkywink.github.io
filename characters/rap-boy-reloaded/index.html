Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Rap Boy Reloaded's appearances?
Rap Boy Reloaded has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Rap Boy Reloaded is a remake of Rap Boy.
  • Rap Boy Reloaded's design takes inspiration from the music video for Eminem's song Houdini.
    • Rap Boy Reloaded's description is also a lyric from the same song.
  • Rap Boy Reloaded will lip sync to both the Real Slim Shady and Rap Monster Emotes when equipped.
  • Tubes will connect Rap Boy Reloaded's Shady Mask - On style to the Evil Twin Tanks Back Bling if both are equipped.
  • Owning Rap Boy Reloaded before Update v34.40 will gift the Air Jordan 3 SE 'Unite' Kicks for free.

Eminem was a special Boss Character hybrid introduced during Chapter 2 Remix. He could have been found in Spaghetti Grotto after November 7th 2024.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Eminem dropped the following items when knocked. He also dropped a Healing Item.

RG's Minigun Grotto Keycards

Eminem will also throw Grenades at his target, but will not drop any upon elimination.

Grenade
Behavior

Behavior[]

Eminem will attack the player if he notices them undisguised. Though if disguised, he can be talked to for additional dialogue.

Shortly after getting knocked down, he will Self-Revive and join the team of the player who defeated him. Eminem will replenish all of his health and act as a Heavy Specialist Character, using Grenades and his minigun against opponents in combat.

Besides this, he will grant the team he is following access to his GHOST Chests and neutrality from his henchmen, Sentry Turrets and Sentry Cameras.

Eminem, when recruited, may also request a High Five or Hug from the player he is following. If ignored, he will cancel the offer and turn away.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Eminem's Boss music and revive stinger is Rap God.
    • At 6 minutes and 3 seconds long, Eminem's Boss music has the longest length of playtime of all the Bosses in Battle Royale. Beating out Snoop Dogg who's Boss music is 4 minutes long.
  • Whenever the player performs an Emote as a follower, Eminem will perform Houdini, rather than just performing Dance Moves like other Bosses.
  • If the player dismisses Eminem, he will be eliminated instantly, as opposed to returning to his starting location like other Hireable Characters.
  • Along with other Remix bosses, Eminem will not drop his items if eliminated by The Storm.
  • If the player that knocked Eminem is eliminated before he Self-Revives, he will remain knocked and can be Revived manually by another player. However the popup that indicates the option will have no text.
