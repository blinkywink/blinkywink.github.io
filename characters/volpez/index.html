Volpez is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be obtained from the Winter's Trickster Legacy Set. Volpez was first released in Chapter 4: Season 3 and is part of the Winter's Trickster Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Judging by the IDs of Trickster's Charge, Tricksters Unite! and Winter's Trickster Banner, the Winter's Trickster Set was supposed to be obtained in a Refer a Friend event.
    • Spray_ReferFriend - Trickster's Charge
    • Emoji_S23_ReferFriend - Tricksters Unite!
    • BRS23_ReferFriend - Winter's Trickster
  • The name was changed from Winter Warrior Fennix to Volpez in Update v24.20.
    • Volpez's description has also been changed from Ready for a long winter. to The sly, wild leader of the Winter Tricksters..
  • Volpez is Fennix's younger brother as stated by Fennix NPC added in Chapter 4: Season 3.
  • Volpez is not part of Crew Series despite being a Fortnite Crew Outfit.
