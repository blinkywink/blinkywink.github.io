Outfit[]

“
Two-tailed trickster.
”
— In-Game Description

Nyanja is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Chapter 6: Season 1 Battle Pass. Nyanja is part of the Nekomata Rogue Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Nyanja's "Lucky Nyanja" style resembles a maneki-neko, or lucky cat.
  • Nyanja's name is a portmanteau of nyan (Japanese cat onomatopoeia) and ninja
