Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Peelverine is based on the Marvel superhero Wolverine.
    • Peelverine's X-Force Peelverine style is based off Wolverine's suit from Marvel's X-Force Comic Series.
    • Peelverine's Classic Peelverine style is based off Wolverine's suit from Marvel's Uncanny X-Men Comic Series.
  • Peelverine is notably one of the few Peely based outfits that do not feature his "head" slightly opened on either side, preceding outfits with the same notability include, Peelosopher Bananocrates, Unpeely, & P-1000
    • Peelverine is currently the only Peely based outfit that has a completely closed head, which could potentially be indirectly referencing to Wolverine's healing factor.
  • On August 10th 2024, players who visited the Disney Horizons Live from D23 and played for 10 minutes would obtain the Peelverine Plush Back Blings by August 16th.
  • In the Chapter 2: Season 4 cinematic trailer, Peely mimics Wolverine by replicating his Adamantium Claws. This would then culminate in the creation of the Nanamantium Claws and the idea of a fusion between Peely and Wolverine.
    • Although the Nanamantium Claws Pickaxe was originally planned for Chapter 2: Season 4, it was scrapped and was later reused as part of the Chapter 5: Season 4 Battle Pass.
  • Peelverine was teased in Chapter 5: Season 3, where a banana peel can be found in the Weapon X Lab.

History

History[]

  • C5S4

Update v31.00[]

August 16th 2024

  • Added Peelverine as a Character. He can be found at the entrance to Restored Reels.

Update v31.10[]

September 4th 2024

  • Peelverine's special dialogues for Shuri, Gwenpool and Captain Jones were added.

Update v31.20[]

September 17th 2024

  • Peelverine's special dialogues for Iron Man Mk 45, and Meowtooth were added.
  • Peelverine now has Super Speed.
  • Peelverine now wields a weaker version of the Pickaxe.

Update v31.30[]

October 1st 2024

  • Moved Peelverine Southwest of Reckless Railways.
  • Added Peelverine's Ask about... Rift Beacons dialogue.
    • Only available in the match when quest is completed.
  • Uncertain Period: Moved Peelverine back to Restored Reels.

Update v31.40[]

October 12th 2024

  • Peelverine was replaced by Peely Bone.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Peelverine wears the Meowtooth Plush as his Back Bling.
  • When aggravated, Peelverine will only use the Nanamantium Claws Pickaxe to fight.
    • After Update v31.20, being eliminated by Peelverine will display the Nanamantium Claws as named Pickaxe in Common Rarity.
  • Peelverine is the first Character to use a Traversal Emote to move around when left idle.
