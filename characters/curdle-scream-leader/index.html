Outfit[]

“
The ultimate monster mashup.
”
— In-Game Description

Curdle Scream Leader is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks. Curdle Scream Leader was first released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and is part of the Fortnite's Monster Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Curdle Scream Leader's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Icon Featured

Prior to the release of this LEGO® Style in Update v29.40, equipping Curdle Scream Leader in LEGO Fortnite would instead equip a random default outfit's LEGO® style.
Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Curdle Scream Leader's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Curdle Scream Leader's appearances?
Curdle Scream Leader has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Curdle Scream Leader can be considered an Universal Classic Monsters mashup since they share the same codename as their set's cosmetics and because of Universal Pictures being credited in their promotional material.
    • An unreleased bundle, Franken-Bundle, was going to include them and Frankenstein's Monster.
  • Curdle Scream Leader is a combination of following Outfits:
    • Cuddle Team Leader
    • Tomatohead
    • Raven
    • Fishstick
    • Rex
  • A Curdle Scream Leader entertainer was at Universal CityWalk in Hollywood to promote the Universal Classic Monsters collaboration.[1]
