Outfit[]

“
International man of mystery.

Bigfoot is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks, with the Bigfoot Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks, or with the Big Toes Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks. Bigfoot was first released in Season 9 and is part of the Bigfoot Set.

Bigfoot was a non-interactive Character introduced during Chapter 2: Season 5. He can be found wandering around the forest near Tiptop Terrace before Update v39.40.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Bigfoot can be found in the thick woods near Tiptop Terrace, wandering around the area the entire match.

When the player gets close to Bigfoot, he will vanish from their view. Only reappearing when they are far enough away from him. He is immune to all sources of damage and knockback.

Bigfoot is a neutral Boss introduced during Chapter 7: Season 1. He can be found at various locations around the Golden Coast after Update v39.40.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Bigfoot drops the following items when eliminated. He also drops a Healing Item and Bars.

Bigfoot's Flex SMG Bigfoot's Kit
Random Weapon FlowBerry Mist Grenade
Behavior

Behavior[]

Bigfoot has a chance to spawn all around the Golden Coast at one of his locations, surrounded by two Henchfans marveling at his presence. He will not attack the player on sight unless he or his Guards are attacked, they aim their weapon at him for too long, or they get too close to him to slowly build up suspicion.

While in combat upon taking damage, he will occasionally throw FlowBerry Mist Grenades at himself to heal lost Shields and obstruct his location.

In matches where players use the Battle Bus, Bigfoot can be seen jumping out to where he will spawn.
