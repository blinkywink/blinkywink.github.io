Behavior

Behavior[]

SURVIVE THE NITRODROME!‎[]

Ringmaster Scarr is not initially patrolling Nitrodrome. Instead the first player who enters the location will trigger a countdown timer and will need to survive for 2 minutes in order for her to reveal herself. This timer will be paused if no players are detected within the drome arena.

The timer can be reduced by either:

  • -5 Seconds by driving through rings inside the drome arena with a vehicle.
  • -20 Seconds by eliminating opponents inside the drome arena such as other players or the Wasteland Guards patrolling around.

BATTLE RINGMASTER SCARR FOR HER MEDALLION![]

Once the timer runs out, Ringmaster Scarr appears with fire sparks on the announcer platform overviewing the Nitrodrome.

She will not attack the player on sight unless she, or a Wasteland Guard that was not previously eliminated are attacked, they block her path for too long, or they aim their weapon at her for too long.

Ringmaster Scarr appearing in Nitrodrome
Note: The fire effects do not appear in the Replay Client.
Special Abilities

Special Abilities[]

  • Dynamite Scatter
    • Ringmaster Scarr spins and hurls three bundles of Dynamite at her target, scattering them around. She will only use this ability when she is affected by Nitro.
Dynamite Scatter
  • Nitro Splash Energize
    • When Ringmaster Scarr's Shield is depleted for the first time she will quickly consume a Nitro Splash. Increasing her movement speed and donning her hyena mask. The Nitro effect is permanently active until she is defeated.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Despite what is said in Update v30.00 Patch Notes, Ringmaster Scarr is the only Wasteland Warrior Boss that can't appear outside her Named Location.
    • Coding and voicelines in the files suggest she was going to be a possible War Bus Convoy Leader but likely doesn't happen in-game due to her requiring to be flushed out before being on the island.
  • When breaking obstacles, Ringmaster Scarr wields the Neon Reaver as her Pickaxe.
  • Ringmaster Scarr, along with the rest of the Wasteland Warriors are the first Bosses since The Foundation that can be aggro'd by simply blocking their patrol path.
    • They are also the first Bosses that can be aggro'd by aiming a weapon at them.
  • Like with all the other Wasteland Warrior Bosses, Ringmaster Scarr can be Nitro Energized early without depleting her Shield.
  • Whenever Ringmaster Scarr isn't on the field her voice can be heard in the speakers of the Nitrodrome commentating on the events that happen inside.
    • Because of this whenever she is on the field, her commentary will come from herself instead of the speakers. And once she is defeated the commentary will completely stop.
  • Its possible to completely prevent Ringmaster Scarr from Nitro Energizing herself by positioning her on top of the Fire Geysers in the Nitrodrome arena right before they set off after breaking her shields. She will not attempt to Nitro Energize once she is back on the ground from gliding.
