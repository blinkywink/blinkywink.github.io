Outfit[]

“
Buttered up and ready for batter.
”
— In-Game Description

Major Mancake is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with the Flap Jax Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks. Major Mancake was first released in Chapter 3: Season 3 and is part of the Flap Jax Set.

Major Mancake could also have been obtained as a reward from Crown Clash Challenges.

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Major Mancake's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Within Fall Guys experiences, Major Mancake's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.
