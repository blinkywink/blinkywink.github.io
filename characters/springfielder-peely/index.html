Professor John I.Q. Nerdelbaum Frink Jr., also known as Professor Frink or Peelfessor Frink is Springfield's mad scientist/professor and is extremely intelligent, through somewhat sociopath and mad inept. He became an humanoid banana by Homer Simpson during his mission to "turning Springfield into nirvana".

He is a supporting character of Chapter 6: Mini Season 2.

History

History[]

Chapter 6: Mini Season 2[]

At the Simpson residence, Frink examines Homer Simpson's Shard remote and informs him about the Zero Point, which is said to be the most dangerous force in the Omniverse, next of course love. Frink wanted to study the shard in order to receive fame, money and Nobel Prize by publishing a research document, but relents when Homer insists on keeping it, in part because Homer had turned his robo-caller into a "ladybot". Impressed by Homer's creation of a cloud turning into donut, Frink said he can keep it, although he has nothing to become a fame person. Hearing this, Homer turns him into an humanoid banana so that he can perform research on himself in hopes of obtaining Nobel attention.

Homer's actions catches to him as the townspeople of Springfield - including him, Krusty the Clown, Ned Flanders, Moe Szyslak and Mayor Quimby - gathered around at town square to put a stop to it, and sends multiple mails to The Looper in case to stop Homer.
