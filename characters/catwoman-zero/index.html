Outfit[]

“
The cat came back.

Catwoman Zero is a DC Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks or with the Catwoman Zero Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks. She was first released in Chapter 2: Season 6 and is part of the Batman: Zero Point Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Catwoman Zero's appearances?
Catwoman Zero has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Selina Kyle, commonly known as Catwoman is a burglar and villain from Reality: NF-1935 that got into The Loop by a crack in the sky over Gotham City.

She is a supporting character in Fortnite, serving as the deuteragonist of the Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point comics.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 1[]

She was lured into the Crack in the Sky by IO to give emotinal support to Batman. While fighting, Batman found her, despite not knowing who she was he helped her defeat the other combatants. They fight their way out until only her and Batman are left. They end their first Loop together, inside The Storm by slowly dying.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 2[]

Again, Batman finds her while fighting but this time, she strikes him. Batman then gives her a note on a Batarang saying "The Bat is a friend", instead of attacking her, then the Loop ends. Batman gets out of his shack and sees Catwoman in front of his door. They go to explore the island by water and land. She then gets shot by a combatant and dies. In the next Loop, her and Batman go to Steamy Stacks to investigate. They find Harley Quinn and try to team up with her too, but she is not interested in helping. She headbutts Batman into the Storm, then the Loop ends. They later do an experiment with Batman's GPS device, and find out non-living things stay in the same place after each loop ends. They turn get to the eye of the storm together to see if they can escape the loop, but nothing happens. Batman figures out there can only be one person that can escape the Loop per round. They go back to the center another Loop, but fail as the loop ends before only Catwoman is left. The next loop, they try again but this time, Batman gives her a knife so she can be the only one standing, by killing Batman. She then breaks free of The Loop, leaving Batman alone.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 3[]

She appears near the end of the issue, being held hostage by Deathstroke after Batman escapes The Loop.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 4[]

She then joins a Rebel group along with Batman that includes Bandolette, Magnus, Deathstroke and more. They get into The Bridge and split up. She and Batman find Fishstick, heavily damaged and about to die and Batman tells her to get water to help save Fishstick.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 5[]

They then team up with the others and find the room with The Zero Point, they turn the panels on then Batman accuses Deathstroke for being the traitor that killed Fishstick. They fight and make truce, then she ties him up. After sending everyone to their homeworld, Deathstroke cuts the rope and gets back to Gotham City using a Portal Device. She snatches his Portal Device using her Grappling Claw. Then her and Batman are left alone.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 6[]

They leave The Bridge and return to The Island to find Harley Quinn. They fight her and knock her out, and see her and Batman's Snapshots, then use the Portal Device to return to the Bridge. They open the panel and Catwoman says she wants to stay, because she is scared that they could have been enemies in their world, but they return to Gotham City. They remember everything, then Catwoman gets away, leaving Batman alone.
