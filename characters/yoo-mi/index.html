Outfit[]

“
An idol, on and off the battlefield.
”

Yoo-mi is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained after purchasing all the cosmetics on Page 3 in the Chapter 6: Season 4 Battle Pass. Yoo-mi is part of the O.X.R. Idol Set.
