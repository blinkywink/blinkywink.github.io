Outfit[]

“
The best at the beach.
”
— In-Game Description

Champion Surf Witch is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,000 V-Bucks or with the Fortnite Global Championship 2025 Bundle for 2,400 V-Bucks. Champion Surf Witch was first released in Chapter 6: Season 4 and is part of the FNCS Set.

Champion Surf Witch could also have been obtained for free by ranking high enough in the Champion Surf Witch FNCS Cup on August 31st 2025.
