Mother of Thorns was a special Boss Character hybrid exclusive to Fortnitemares 2025 introduced during Chapter 6: Season 4. She could have been found in either Freaky Fields, Gourdy Gate, or Thorny Throne after Update v31.40.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Mother of Thorns drops the following items when knocked. She also drops Bars.

Thorn Ripper Slurp Juice Wood Stake Shotgun

Mother of Thorns wields the Mythic High Stakes' Shotgun to attack players. She will not drop the Shotgun when eliminated.

High Stakes' Shotgun
Behavior

Behavior[]

Mother of Thorns will be sitting on her throne at any of the three locations she can appear, patiently waiting for any interaction and leaning forward should the player get close enough to her. While she sits on the throne she will not get up and attack the player on sight unless she is attacked, they damage her throne, or they get too close to her as she sits.

Mother of Thorns sitting on her throne
Mother of Thorns leaning forward as a player gets closer
Mother of Thorns getting up off her throne

If Mother of Thorns is found patrolling she will attack players on sight. Alternating between using her High Stakes' Shotgun and her Thorn Ripper to either swing at her target once or with a full swing combo when they get close, or to pull them toward her if they are far enough.

Shortly after getting knocked down, she will Self-Revive and join the team of the player who defeated her. Mother of Thorns will replenish all of her health and shield, acting as a standard follower Character.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Mother of Thorns' codename is FNM_AI_AxisThrow.
  • Mother of Thorns' Boss music is Demons.
  • Mother of Thorns wears the Scarlet Wings as her Back Bling.
  • Mother of Thorns is the third Boss to be fully voiced in a verbal language, though this time she only speaks English rather than the player's chosen language. The previous Bosses to have this occurrence were Doctor Doom and Orelia.
  • Mother of Thorns reuses all the throne animations of Emma Frost.
  • Mother of Thorns does not have an equipped Pickaxe.
  • Similar to the Bosses during Chapter 2 Remix, if the player dismisses Mother of Thorns, she will be eliminated instantly, as opposed to returning to her starting location like other Hireable Characters.
