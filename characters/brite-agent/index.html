Overview

“
Bold, brite, and ready for recon.
”
— In-Game Description

Brite Agent is an Uncommon Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Operation Brite Starter Pack in the Item Shop for $3.75. Brite Agent was first released in Chapter 5: Season 2 and is part of the Operation Brite Set.

Brite Agent can also be obtained by purchasing the 77070 Durrr Burger LEGO® Fortnite®, 77071 Supply Llama LEGO® Fortnite®, 77072 Peely Bone LEGO® Fortnite® and 77073 Battle Bus LEGO® Fortnite® Sets.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Brite Agent's appearances?
Brite Agent has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
