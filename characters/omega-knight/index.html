Omega Knight is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Omega Knight's Level Up Quest Pack in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks. Omega Knight was released in Chapter 3: Season 2 and is part of the Honor, Glory, Gold Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Omega Knight's appearances?
Omega Knight has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
