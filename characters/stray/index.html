Stray is the special ops expert of the Fox Clan and the former prince of the River Guard. He is the brother of Mizuki, who left his home behind for personal reasons, likely looking for the freedom that the River Guard could not offer.

Like all other River Guardians, he is an expert swordsman. His unique character dialogue suggests that he seems to be good friends with Drift.

History

History[]

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

In the cinematic trailer, he can be seen falling out of the Rift Gate's Sideways Rift before facing his sister, Mizuki, in MEGA City.

Leaving the River Guard behind for the Fox Clan seemed to only strain the pre-existent rivalry between the two - as well as his relationship with his sister. To Mizuki's disliking, Drift seems to take him under his wing.

In the Syndicate Quests, impressed by The Looper's swordsmanship, he, alongside the rest of the Fox Clan, agreed to meet with the River Guard for a peace negotiation over dinner.

During The Rumbles, he was one of the few islanders to notice the ongoing rumbling issues.

Chapter 6: Season 1[]

As the Fox Clan is brought into Reality: 783, Stray likely resides in Twinkle Terrace with the rest of the Clan in their hideout.

Stray seems to have become Drift's direct apprentice despite Mizuki's earlier warnings, though Vi has to encourage Drift to train him. He seems to have mended his relationship with Mizuki as well, as she advises him to keep his eyes sharp, warning him of possible dangers while looking out for him - and she notably expresses no hostility towards his apparent mentor.
