Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Like most Supernova Academy Outfits, Synthwave's design has drastically changed from its concept art seen in a survey, likely to make them more distinct from each other.
  • Synthwave's original half-colored hair design can be seen in the Supernova Academy student book.
  • Synthwave's power is Audiokinesis (manipulation of music and soundwaves).
  • Synthwave is a fan of deadmau5, owning posters and figurines of him in her dorm room.

Synthwave is a superhero, a member of The Supernovas on Supernova's Formation Theta and a graduated student from the Supernova Academy. She's also a DJ and a musician for Radio Stations.

Synthwave is a supporting character of Chapter 6: Season 3.

Personality

Personality[]

Synthwave is the most chill and most friendly member of the Supernovas. She will try her best to be friend with anyone and everyone, no matter if they like it or not.
