The Final Peel is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks or with the Peeltergeist Bundle for 2,000 V-Bucks. The Final Peel was first released in Chapter 6: Season 4 and is part of the Peeltergeist Set.

The Final Peel is a hostile Boss exclusive to the Demon Rush Limited Time Mode.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

The Final Peel wields an Epic Typhoon Blade to perform Iai: Demonrush. He will not drop the Melee Weapon when eliminated.

Typhoon Blade
Behavior

Behavior[]

The Final Peel will spawn at the start of Round 5 pounding his fist on the ground, serving as the final boss of the main rounds and attacking players on sight.

Once eliminated, The Final Peel will drop a bountiful pile of Weapons and Consumables along with ending the round.

The Final Peel also has a chance to appear during the Score Attack Round.

Special Abilities

Special Abilities[]

  • Ghoulish Void Warp
    • Vanishes through a green portal and emerges at another point around the arena. The Final Peel will prioritize utilizing this ability to move in-between attacks rather than simply walking.
  • Explosive Banana Faces
    • Hurls three ghoulish Peely faces that fly towards the target, dealing ??? damage in an AoE.
  • Molten Eruptions
    • Channels green flames around him and creates pits of fire on the ground, then raises his hand aloft, causing the fire to erupt in a violent burst, dealing ??? damage.
      • Weakspots: While The Final Peel is performing this ability, his hands will glow and become weak points. Shooting them will count as a headshot.
  • Iai: Demonrush
    • Sheathes his blade, then dashes towards the direction hes facing while drawing the sword, dealing 25 damage to anyone in his path.
      • Due to The Final Peel's size, this attack can be avoided by simply crouching or sliding under the slash. Though, given the varying terrain he can appear in, this might not always work.
Trivia

Trivia[]

  • The Final Peel's Boss Outfit ID is Character_NanaEternal_NPC.
  • The Final Peel is a reskin of Shogun X created for Demon Rush.
  • The Final Peel's size is Gigantic compared to other Bosses. Being as large as Shogun X and Daigo.
  • The Final Peel has a unique green crumbling effect when defeated.
