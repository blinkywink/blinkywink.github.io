Timeline

Timeline[]

Backstory[]

It is known from her collection book description that she's the sister of Bandolier, and that they used to be part of the same commando squad. The description also mentions that she's very skilled in making traps, alongside guerilla warfare.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 4[]

Bandolette and her team including Raider, Magnus, Bonehead and more meet with Batman and Catwoman. Batman come up with a plan to open up one of the IO Hatches, so the group looks on to fight items to find a way to get in. An energy frequency from The Spire opens the hatch, leading into The Bridge.

Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point Issue 5[]

The group fight against the hordes of IO Guards, until they find the room with The Zero Point. Batman accuses Deathstroke for being the traitor that killed Fishstick, Bandolette then wants to help Deathstroke but after Batman calls truce, she gives up. She then apologizes to Batman and goes back to her homeworld.
