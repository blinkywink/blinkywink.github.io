Update v23.00[]

December 4th 2022

  • Bunker Jonesy no longer spawns at Fort Jonesy. (The Joneses became Fort Jonesy during C3S4)

Bunker Jonesy is a conspiracy theorist and one of the earliest snapshot of Agent Jones created by The Zero Point.

He is a major character in Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as a major character in Season 9, and a recurring major character of the storyline from Chapter 2: Season 5 onwards.

History

History[]

Season 8[]

After Agent Jones, sent for a mission in The Loop, first encountered Peely at Lucky Landing's Bao Bros, the two became friend and started hanging out together. After some time, they found themselve trapped in the bunker to escape the Volcano eruption. However, they found themselves without food, and Jones had no choice but to devour Peely to survive.

Season 9[]

After being trapped for decades, if not centuries, Jones, who turned into Bunker Jonesy and Peely, who became a smoothie were freed by Sentinel, Vega and Rox and discovered Neo Tilted. However, during their imprisonment in the Bunker, the act of devouring Peely allowed Bunker Jonesy to see visions of both the past and the future. In one of these visions, he saw a fight between a Kaiju and a Mecha that would happen in the near future and tried to warn the inhabitants of The Island. At some point, Jones would escape The Loop, leaving behind the Bunker Jonesy snapshot.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

While inside The Loop, Bunker Jonesy encountered The Looper and revealed them the secrets of The Loop, trying to help them escape.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Bunker Jonesy conspirate about the Imagined Order and the aliens.

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Bunker Jonesy escaped the Loop thanks to the help of The Seven and along with other snapshots of Jones, founded The Joneses declaring their complete independence and hostility toward both Doctor Slone and The Foundation. However, The Seven thanks to the help of The Looper managed to rally them to their cause and fight the Imagined Order.

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

Along with the other Joneses, Bunker Jonesy participated in the war.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

After the war, Bunker Joneys became a survivalist, never wanting to be trapped again in a bunker.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

To escape the chrome, The Joneses transformed their village into a fort named Fort Jonesy. However, Bunker Jonesy now faces his greatest challenge: his roommates. He was also tasked by Agent Jones, along with the others Jones to monitor The Herald.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • While stated to be one of the very first Snapshots of Jones, Bunker Jonesy was created during Season 9.
    • The original Agent Jones himself is friend with the original Peely, meaning that the Jonesy that first encountered Peely in Bao Bros was in fact Agent Jones.
      • During Chapter 7: Season 2, Vengeance Jones confirmed he was the Jones who devoured Peely in the Bunker.
    • The Peelies are shown to have arrived on the island during Season 8 time period.
  • Several snapshots of Jones were created before Bunker Jonesy.
    • Mission Specialist first appearing during Season 3.
    • Sash Sergeant, first appearing during Season 4.
    • Rex and Grill Sergeant, first appearing during Season 5.
    • Sun Tan Specialist, first appearing during Season 6.
