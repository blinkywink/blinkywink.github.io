Outfit[]

“
Keep far away from Maki Master.
”
— In-Game Description

Fishstick is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks or with the Fishstick Bundle for 2,200 V-Bucks. Fishstick was first released in Season 7 and is part of the Fish Food Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Fishstick's appearances?
Fishstick has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

— Fishstick
Fishstick is a character from Reality: 7021 that escaped The Loop. He joins a Rebel group with Bandolette, Magnus, Bonehead and more. They get into The Bridge and split up, Batman finds Fishstick harshly damaged, he tells that one of them works for the "…", but before he could finish his sentence, he dies in the arms of Batman.
