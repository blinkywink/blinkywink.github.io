Outfit[]

“
No sword nor crown will keep her from her destiny.
”
— In-Game Description

Princess Lexa is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during August 2023, as part of the August 2023 Fortnite Crew Pack. Princess Lexa was released in Chapter 4: Season 3 and is part of the Ageless Royalty Set.

Section

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Princess Lexa's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Princess Lexa has a male counterpart named Prince Orin.
  • Unlike her original outfit version from Chapter 2 Season 5, Princess Lexa is not cel-shaded.
  • Princess Lexa's rarity was updated from Epic to Crew Series in Update v34.00.
  • Her original counterpart, Lexa, was a Hunter for the Imagined Order
    • However, Lexa didn't truly know who she was working for, and had The Looper hunt down IO Guards out of untrustworthiness.[1]
    • Princess Lexa, on the other hand, serves as Royalty for Geno's previous Kingdom, the Oathbound Empire.
