“
An elite stormtrooper with expertise in espionage.

AWR Trooper is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks. AWR Trooper was first released in Chapter 5: Season 2 and is part of the Original Trilogy Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for AWR Trooper's appearances?
AWR Trooper has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

AWR Troopers were hostile Guards in Fortnite. They were introduced during Galactic Battle.

Description

Description[]

One AWR Trooper could have been found in any active Forecast Tower, leading two Imperial Stormtroopers behind them. They will shoot at the player if they notice them undisguised.

All members of a Forecast Tower trio will be eliminated once all in the area are knocked, and they will attempt to revive each other if they remain alive.
