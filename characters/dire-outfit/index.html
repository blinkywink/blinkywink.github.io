Outfit[]

“
Leader of the pack.

Dire is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Tier 100 of the Season 6 Battle Pass. Dire is part of the Full Moon Set.

How To Unlock?[]

  • Half-Wolf - Gain 30,000 Season XP
  • Brown Werewolf - Gain 70,000 Season XP
  • Grey Werewolf - Gain 120,000 Season XP
  • White Werewolf - Gain 180,000 Season XP
  • Black Werewolf - Gain 250,000 Season XP
  • Blue - Complete 20 Weekly Challenges
  • Yellow - Complete 40 Weekly Challenges
  • Brown - Complete 60 Weekly Challenges
