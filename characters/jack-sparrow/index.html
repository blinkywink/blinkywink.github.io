Outfit[]

“
That's "Captain" Jack Sparrow.
”
— In-Game Description

Jack Sparrow is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 2,000 V-Bucks with the Jack Sparrow Bundle. Jack Sparrow was first released in Chapter 5: Season 3 and is part of the Pirates of the Caribbean Set.

Jack Sparrow could have been obtained by purchasing the premium rewards track of the Cursed Sails Pass for 1,000 V-Bucks.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Jack Sparrow's sheath serves as a sheath for the Jack Sparrow's Sword Pickaxe if both are equipped.
  • The Cursed Sails Pass (which contains Jack Sparrow) was accidentally released early in the Item Shop upon the end of downtime for Update v30.20. The pass was quickly removed, but players that purchased it were able to keep the Outfit.[1]
