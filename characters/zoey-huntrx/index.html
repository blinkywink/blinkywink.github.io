Outfit[]

“
The lead rapper and maknae of HUNTR/X.
”
— In-Game Description

Zoey (HUNTR/X) is an Icon Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the KPop Demon Hunters Bundle for 3,500 V-Bucks. Zoey (HUNTR/X) was first released in Chapter 6: Season 4 and is part of the KPop Demon Hunters Set.

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Zoey (HUNTR-X)'s appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Zoey (HUNTR/X)'s appearances?
Zoey (HUNTR/X) has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
