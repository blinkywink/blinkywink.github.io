Karl Keyes is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks or with the Urban Operator Bundle for 1,200 V-Bucks. Karl Keyes was first released in Chapter 6: Season 2 and is part of the Urban Operator Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Karl Keyes's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.
