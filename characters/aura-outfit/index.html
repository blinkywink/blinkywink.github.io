Aura is an Uncommon Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks, with the Reverse2K's Locker Bundle for 1,400 V-Bucks or with the Gilded Explorers Bundle for 1,600 V-Bucks. Aura was first released in Season 8.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Aura's appearances?
Aura has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Aura is a professional thief and treasure hunter who had been active on the islands since her debut in Season 8. She was hired for heist work in Chapter 4: Season 1, and worked with The Looper to investigate artifacts in Chapter 4: Season 3. She has positive - albeit mostly professional - relations with many other recurring characters in Fortnite, and has remained a mainstay in the revolving cast of people in the storyline. Aura's current whereabouts are unknown.

Personality

Personality[]

Aura is an inquisitive and determined hunter for treasure and valuables, whose skills are known and respected enough to warrant the attention of numerous Caper Crews. She has a conflicted moral compass between what she believes is right, in contrast to her criminalistic track record. She is a cooperative partner to those she works with, and is an ally and friend among those she has mutual respect with.

History

History[]

Season 8[]

Aura and her counterpart/associate, Guild, have appeared on the island Athena. For a long while, she is known as a treasure hunting deviant, often found working with Guild.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

Aura and Guild have split paths on her most recent job - a heist she invested in, and was hired for, by Wild Card. Against the advice of others, Aura is hired by Wild Card during a season of heists, only for Wild Card dismissing her out of hand[1]. After that she joined the Most Wanted to get revange on Wild Card, by robbing his faction's vaults

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

Returning into the limelight, Aura steps away from her history of thieving in search of hidden treasure. After picking up rumors of a powerful object Trace had found, she goes in search of the item with the help of The Looper. Throughout the season, Aura travels around the island, particularly in the newly uncovered Jungle Biome, charting maps and analyzing clues to uncover the Chalice of Cubetastrophe, in order to prevent it to find a wielder and destroy the island.

Chapter 4: Season 4[]

She has remained out of the heisting game due to her adventures with hunting artifacts.

LEGO: Fortnite[]

In a discussion with The Looper, she reveals that she often dreams of herself collaborating with another explorer to find a chalice whose name she doesn't remember. In her dream, she says that once they discover the chalice, they discover a "Golden Ghost Lady" living inside. This dream refers to her search for the Chalice of Cubetastrophe, however it seems that she has completely forgotten and that only scraps of memories appear through her dreams. This leaves us thinking about her true mental state when she searched for the Chalice as well as the true fate of the Chalice itself, which she had assured to entrust to one of her contacts to seal it back to Chapter 4: Season 3.
