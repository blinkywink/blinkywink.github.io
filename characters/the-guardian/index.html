The Guardian is a member of The New Seven and the Champion of the Sprites. He is a Water Sprite from Oninoshima who made his way to the Shattered Coast[2].

Timeline

Timeline[]

Pre-Chapter 7[]

Before Chapter 7, The Guardian was a regular Water Sprite on Oninoshima. After witnessing The Dark Presence's attempt to destroy Oninoshima, The Guardian became determined to protect all other realities.

Chapter 7: Season 3[]

Through unknown means, The Guardian made his way to Reality: 266. After proving himself a worthy fighter, he was titled "The Champion of the Sprites" and made a member of The New Seven.
