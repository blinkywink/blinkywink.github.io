Overview

“
Get in. Get out. Get paid.
”

Heist is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks. Heist was first released in Season 9 and is part of the Getaway Gang Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Heist's appearances?
Heist has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • In Update v13.20, Heist received a new style, Island.
    • The style was used as the model for the Shark Henchmen in Chapter 2: Season 2.
  • Heist and The Underwriter share the same outfit model with the only difference being that the mask of The Underwriter has the logo of No Sweat Insurance on it.
  • Heist's design is possibly inspired by the Payday (2011) series.
    • Interestingly, the official Payday Twitter account retweeted Heist's announcement, further supporting the connection.[1]
  • Heist's icon was changed in Update v10.10.
    • It was changed again in Update v25.20.
    • It was changed once again in Update v30.10.
