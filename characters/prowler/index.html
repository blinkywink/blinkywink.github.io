Outfit[]

“
Technologically enhanced world-class criminal and the most dangerous thief in America.
”
— In-Game Description

Prowler is a Marvel Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained as a reward from the Prowler Quests. Prowler was released in Chapter 3: Season 2 and is part of the Prowler Set.

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Prowler's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Prior to the release of this LEGO® Style in Update v30.20, equipping Prowler in LEGO Fortnite would instead equip a random default outfit's LEGO® style.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Prowler originally shared the codename Lurk with Mary Jane Watson, but his was altered to Sienna.[1][2]
    • Due to the fact that she was planned to have been a Character in the previous season, it can be assumed that he wasn't intended for the Chapter 3: Season 2 Battle Pass.[3]
  • Prowler came out 3 days earlier than the expected release. This also happened with The Cube Queen in Chapter 2: Season 8.
  • Despite having a Built-in Emote, he does not have a 'Built-In' tag assigned to the outfit in-game.
