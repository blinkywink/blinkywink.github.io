Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Gwenpool breaks the fourth wall in her description by referencing The Devourer of Worlds Live Event from Chapter 2: Season 4.
    • It also seems she's written most of the other descriptions for her set as a lot of them break the fourth wall too.
  • Gwenpool's Gwendolyn Poole Style is partially based on an attire she wore in Issue 16 of The Unbelievable Gwenpool, with some changes.
    • Gwenpool's Dark Gwenpool Style is based on an alternate version of herself from Earth-17217 who became a villain.
  • Gwenpool's Gwendolyn Poole Style features buttons on her jacket of Fishstick and of Deadpool wearing a crown.
    • The Deadpool button is likely based on the comic series where Deadpool becomes a king, and is left with Jeff the Land Shark.
    • Gwenpool has also been shown to be a fan of Fishstick.
  • Both the shirt on Gwenpool's Gwendolyn Poole Style and her accompanying wrap have an illustration of her pet Land Shark, Jeff.
  • In Fortnite, Gwenpool's bodysuit that she wears in most of her appearances is seemingly censored as she now wears shorts.
    • Despite this, her chibi drawing in the Fishstick Fangirl (Gwenpool Edition) YouTube Short has her normal comic outfit.
    • She has worn shorts at some points in the comics, such as in Gwenpool Strikes Back.
  • In Update v33.20 on the Quest Tab, a slightly extended version of Gwenpool's Gradient Shading Style Icon with a silloulette effect applied on top of it was used for the icon for when a Quest catagoty's icon hasn't loaded in.
    • In Update v33.30, this was changed to Vintage Ramirez's icon.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Gwenpool wears Gwenpool's Katanas as her Back Bling.
  • When aggravated, Gwenpool will use Rare Dual Micro SMGs to fight.
  • Gwenpool uses alot of Community Terminology and other out of lore terms in her dialogue and Collection Book entry. These include:
    • Mentioning that she might be the only person in the player's Locker who's aware that they're a playable character in Fortnite. Though this could be contested with other Outfits like MrBeast.
    • Referring to herself as an NPC as the generalized term for all the AI on the Island.
    • Mentioning Cranking 90s, a famous form of taking highground in a build battle and flex of one's Building skill.
    • Skill Issue, a general term for someone lacking in a specific skill or being lower in skill.
    • Camping, hiding in one specific place to hide and avoid contact from others. Being in the cell she was completely unreachable from players outside, which fits some definitions of camping.
    • "I'm trying to rank up to Unreal before the season ends.", in reference to Ranked's highest possible rank and how they reset after every season.
    • "I've got dailies to grind.", in reference to the Daily Quests.
    • Directly saying "come back next match", which is self-explanitory. Whether this indicates she knows about The Loop should be ignored due to the 4th wall breaking nature of Gwenpool and where she comes from.
  • Gwenpool's special dialogue with Deadpool, Ravenpool, and Cuddlepool references how her similarities to him were a mistake on her tailor.
