Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Marigold's appearances?
Marigold has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Marigold is named after the daughter of King Midas in Greek mythology.
    • Although this name did not appear in Greek mythology originally, it was adopted in retellings of King Midas's story, to emphasize the lesson of the story.
      • Midas's daughter is also named Zoe in some accounts.
    • The same name was previously used on The Yacht.
  • She is the female counterpart of Midas
    • She is the first and currently only non-Midas outfit to feature the golden touch effect.
  • In Update v24.40, Marigold received a new style, Phantom Code Marigold, for Global Accessibility Awareness Day (GAAD); matching the Access Core Back Bling.
    • Albeit, the aforementioned effect is disabled with this style equipped.
    • This style is internally known as CatBurglarDigital.
      • She is currently the only quest pack outfit to receive a new style.

Timeline[]

Unknown Time Period[]

Marigold became an ally of Midas who gifted her the Golden Daggers.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Marigold works with The Looper to recover Midas' gold.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

Marigold explores a mysterious cave and accidentally awakens Orelia.

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

As a member of the Imagined Order, Marigold is located at Lazy Lake. She looks for a getaway driver and plans something with Midas and Jules.

After Midas' disappearance at the end of the Cosmic Summer, Marigold begins searching for him.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Marigold finds out that Midas is somewhere on the Island, so she calls Midas' Crew to return. After doing some research with The Looper's help, she discovers that Midas had been trapped in The Underworld but managed to escape.

When Midas finds her after escaping, he says that it's "Good to see her again."

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Marigold is almost certainly not a Snapshot of Midas, as snapshots make exact copies of people in the loop, and Marigold has multiple differences from him.
  • Marigold is the name of Midas' daughter in Greek Mythology, but judging by Marigold saying "Your Father" to Jules and Midas saying that people never get the details in his Myths correct, this likely isn’t the case in Fortnite.
  • There are numerous things that hint Midas and Marigold have a positive relationship with each other:
    • Midas gifted Marigold her Daggers, which she treasures.
    • Midas' Yacht is named "The Marigold."
    • Marigold frequently acts in defense of Midas, such as reclaiming his Gold Bars and searching for him when he disappeared.
    • Midas says that it's "Good to see her again." After escaping The Underworld.
