Trivia

Trivia[]

  • According to Mike Diva's previz for Chapter 4: Season 4's cinematic, Battle Gamer Mae was going to be called Mae.EXE.[1]
    • This name could be a reference to the NetNavis from the Mega Man Battle Network video game series.
  • Battle Gamer Mae's description is a reference to O.X.R..
  • Chill Gamer Mae can be claimed without fulfilling the page completion prerequisite on Page 8.
    • This means this is the earliest method that Battle Gamer Mae can be obtained.
  • Battle Gamer Mae's "real" arms will move out of the way for her O-M-Gloves if they get close to her chest.
  • The Chill Gamer Mae style is possibly based on the original Mae's Deboot Dusk style.

Mae is a hacker, explosives expert and an engineer of O.X.R.. She is a former criminal as well as one of the members of The Heist Crew.

She is a supporting character of Chapter 4: Season 4 and Chapter 6: Season 4.

Personality

Personality[]

Mae is a bright and cheerful smart girl with impulsive thoughts and questionable humors. Above all, she loves having fun and creating weapons, being an expert in explosives in addition to her hacking skills. Her codebreaking skills and self-made combustibles got her kicked out of multiple hackathons and chemistry classes. Mae's morality is in a very gray area, having very little awareness of ethics and consequences. Even after her change from notorious criminal to working for a military organization, her thoughts did not change.
