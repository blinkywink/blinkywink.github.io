Outfit[]

“
One half of the Dynamic Duo and member of the Teen Titans.
”
— In-Game Description

Robin is a DC Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Chapter 6: Season 3 Battle Pass. Robin is part of the Teen Titans Set.
