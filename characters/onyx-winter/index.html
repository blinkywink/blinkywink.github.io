Outfit[]

“
O.X.R's fierce and fearless general.

Onyx Winter is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained on Page 11 of the Chapter 6: Season 4 Battle Pass. Onyx Winter is part of the O.X.R. Commander Set.
