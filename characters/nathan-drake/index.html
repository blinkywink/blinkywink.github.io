Nathan Drake is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Uncharted Bundle for 2,600 V-Bucks. Nathan Drake was released in Chapter 3: Season 1 and is part of the Uncharted Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Nathan Drake's appearances?
Nathan Drake has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Nathan Drake uses the likeness of Tom Holland, who portrayed the character in the film Uncharted.
    • This is the second Outfit to use his likeness, the first being Spider-Man (No Way Home).
  • Nathan Drake is not part of the Gaming Legends Series, even though he originated from a video game franchise.
    • This is likely due to his default appearance being from the film.
