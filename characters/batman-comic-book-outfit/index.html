Batman Comic Book Outfit is a DC Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with the Batman Caped Crusader Pack for $18.49. Batman Comic Book Outfit was first released in Season X and is part of the Gotham City Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Batman Comic Book Outfit's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.


Prior to the release of this LEGO® Style in Update v33.20, equipping Batman Comic Book Outfit in LEGO Fortnite would instead equip a random default outfit's LEGO® style.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Batman Comic Book Outfit's appearances?
Batman Comic Book Outfit has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Despite using Jonesy's face and being called an "Outfit" like Black Widow Outfit and Star-Lord Outfit, Batman Comic Book Outfit is seen alongside the actual Superman and Wonder Woman in the DC Trinity Loading Screen.
  • According to Donald Mustard, he convinced DC Comics into letting Batman join Fortnite by pitching them Batman/Fortnite: Zero Point.[1]
    • It's possible that Gotham City in Season X was originally intended to tie-in to the Rift seen in its original placement at Reality: NF-1935 on the first issue.
