Outfit[]

“
Protect the vaults.
”
— In-Game Description

Gold Blooded Ace is a Rare Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,000 V-Bucks or with the Gold Blooded Ace Bundle for 1,800 V-Bucks. Gold Blooded Ace was first released in Chapter 4: Season 1 and is part of the Getaway Gang Set.

Gold Blooded Ace could have also been obtained by reaching Tier 12 in the Most Wanted Event Pass.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Gold Blooded Ace's appearances?
Gold Blooded Ace has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Cold Blooded Ace was a neutral Boss introduced during Chapter 4: Season 1. She could have been found in Brutal Bastion.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Cold Blooded Ace dropped the following items when eliminated. She also dropped up to two or three Healing Items and Bars.

Heisted Explosive Assault Rifle Vault Keycard

Cold Blooded Ace will also throw Grenades at her target. But won't drop any.

Grenades
Behavior

Behavior[]

Cold Blooded Ace is initially neutral and will only attack players if they damage her, a Cold Blooded Suit or if their Heat Level goes beyond 1.

When eliminated, Cold Blooded Ace will get knocked and will be able to get revived by her Guards.
