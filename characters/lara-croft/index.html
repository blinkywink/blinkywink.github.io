Lara Croft is a Gaming Legends Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Level 15 of the Chapter 2: Season 6 Battle Pass. Lara Croft is part of the Lara Croft Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Lara Croft's default style is based on Tomb Raider's Survivor timeline.
    • Her 25th Anniversary style is based on the Legend timeline.
      • Her Gold Anniversary style is based on her death by the Hand of Midas in Tomb Raider: Anniversary.
        • Her Classic style is based on the Original timeline.
  • Lara Croft's description is a quote from Tomb Raider (2013).
  • Lara Croft was the first Gaming Legends Series Outfit to be in the Battle Pass.
    • She was also the first Gaming Legends Series Outfit to be a Character on the island, the second being Solid Snake.
  • Lara Croft (25th Anniversary) can be seen in the Zero Crisis Finale cinematic for a brief moment. Given that all the other collaboration characters present are Hunters (besides the Xenomorphs and T-800s, who came with Ellen Ripley and Sarah Connor), it can be assumed that she would've been one in Chapter 2: Season 5.
