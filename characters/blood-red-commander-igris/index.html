Blood-Red Commander Igris is a Gaming Legends Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop with Blood-Red Commander Igris for 1,800 V-Bucks or with the Solo Leveling:ARISE Bundle for 4,500 V-Bucks. Blood-Red Commander Igris was first released in Chapter 7: Season 1 and is part of the Solo Leveling:ARISE Set.
