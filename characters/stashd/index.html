Outfit[]

“
No one said being fresh was easy.
”
— In-Game Description

Stash'd is an Uncommon Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 800 V-Bucks or with the Stash'd Bundle for 1,300 V-Bucks. Stash'd was first released in Chapter 3: Season 1 and is part of the Fashion Forward Set.

Stash'd could also have been obtained for free by ranking high enough in the Summer Smash Cup 2022 on January 23rd 2022.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Stash'd's appearances?
Stash'd has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 1[]

Debuting on Asteria, he works as a freelance driver. At some point in his driving career, he lends his services to the Peace Syndicate.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

Most of his work at this time involves driving people around, mainly akin to that of a "really, really fast chauffeur". He years for assisting with heists as a getaway driver, and advertises his services around from Chonker's Speedway.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

Now an official member of the Peace Syndicate, he acts as the getaway driver for both Evie and Twyn. He voices his concerns at Evie's plans, and directs any of Twyn's misgivings towards her as well.

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

Stash'd appears to have been recruited into The Society.
