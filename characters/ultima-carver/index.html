Ultima Carver is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks or with the Ultima Carver Bundle for 1,500 V-Bucks. Ultima Carver was first released in Chapter 5: Season 4 and is part of the Knight of the Patch Set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Ultima Carver's appearances?
Ultima Carver has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Ultima Carver was a hostile Boss exclusive to Fortnitemares 2024 introduced during Chapter 5: Season 4. He could have been found in Brawler's Patch after Update v31.40.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Ultima Carver dropped the following items when eliminated. He also dropped 12 Bars.

Pumpkin Launcher Wood Stake Shotgun FlowBerry Fizz

Ultima Carver uses a Rare Pumpkin Launcher and Wood Stake Shotgun to attack players. He will not drop either weapon when eliminated.

DEFEAT ULTIMA CARVER!‎[]
