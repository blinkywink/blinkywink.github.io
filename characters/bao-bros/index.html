Outfit[]

“
Steamed up and ready for action.
”
— In-Game Description

Bao Bros is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,500 V-Bucks or with the Bao Bros Bundle for 1,600 V-Bucks. Bao Bros was first released in Chapter 2: Season 1 and is part of the Bao Bros Set.
