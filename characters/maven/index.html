Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Maven's appearances?
Maven has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

Maven was a corrupt IO agent that allied with The Last Reality. She was the 'mole' the Imagined Order had been looking for throughout Chapter 2: Season 7.

She is a major antagonist in Fortnite serving as the main antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 7 and a posthumous antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 8.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Chapter 2: Season 7[]

Maven is a mole in the Imagined Order since the start of the Alien Invasion, who has been telling IO's secret plans to civillians like Swamp Stalker, and causing chaos inside IO. She tried to activate the Mothership Countermeasure Bombs early to sabotage Operation: Sky Fire. Doctor Slone discovered this and sent The Looper to revert her work. She then sent them confront and eliminate her so she will learn "there is no room at the IO for traitors."
