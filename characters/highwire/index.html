Outfit[]

“
Chaos is a game, and she always wins.
”
— In-Game Description

Highwire is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 11, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 2 Battle Pass. Highwire is part of Chaos Artist Set.
