Cube Assassin was a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 2: Season 8. She could have been found in Sideways Encounters.

Boss Items

Boss Items[]

Cube Assassin used one of the following weapons to attack players and dropped it after being eliminated. She also dropped a Healing Item.

Sideways Rifle Sideways Minigun
Behavior

Behavior[]

During a number of Sideways Encounter variants, Cube Assassin has a 50% chance to appear. And will attack players on spawn.

Unlike Sideways Monsters, she can leave the Sideways bubble and she will only disappear when the Anomaly closes. Eliminating Cube Assassin will grant a large quantity of score and she will respawn as a Ghost for a short time when eliminated by the player.

Back Bling[]

  • The Cubist Back Bling on Cube Assassin is a weak point, hitting it will count as a headshot.
  • Her Back Bling has 3 possible colors: Purple, Orange, and Gold, which will indicate the rarity of the weapon that Cube Assassin will drop.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Cube Assassin's Boss Outfit ID is CID_NPC_Athena_Commando_F_CloakedAssassin.
    • The Boss Outfit, named U with the description of NPC, while being a near exact copy of the release Cube Assassin Outfit complete with the same introduction season, reactivity, and set placement, notably has a unique trail when moving. Which was not carried over to the release Outfit.
  • Cube Assassin's size is Much Larger than the other Bosses. Being the first Boss to establish the Much Larger size. This trait persists with her later reappearances.
  • If a player were to use a Sideways Scythe against Cube Assassin in a fight, she would only have health and no shields.
  • Before Update v18.20, the Shockwave Launcher had no effect on her.
  • Cube Assassin is the first humanoid Boss to have an extra vulnerability point.
  • Cube Assassin normally cannot be knocked when her health is drained. But with the help of a glitch that lowers her health to 100 upon spawn she can be knocked down. Complete with the Carry and Shakedown options.
    • Even more bizarre, She is still capable of using her Zero Crystal Dash while knocked.
    • If Cube Assassin is defeated during this she will not re-appear as a Character.
  • On rare occasions, Cube Assassin would sometimes wield a Rare Breacher from Save the World, but would not drop it when eliminated. This was a bug and was removed in an unknown update.
  • Cube Assassin was the last new Boss of Chapter 2.

Cube Assassins are a group of warriors who have pledged allegiance to the Cubes. They lead Sideways Monsters through the Sideways Anomalies.

They are supporting antagonists of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as the supporting antagonist of Chapter 2: Season 8.

Personality

Personality[]

Cube Assassins appear to be ruthless individuals not caring about the innocents killed and the realities destroyed in their countless invasions. They are very loyal to The Cubes and they'll stop at nothing to defend them. However they know how to admit when they are powerless against an opponent, such as The Looper. They do, however, have a sense of honor as warriors. Knowing how to recognize the power of their opponent and humbly admit defeat, while continuing to encourage them despite everything even if their opponent is an enemy.

History

History[]

Unknown Time Period[]

At some point in the past, the Cube Assassins swore allegiance to the Cubes and were empowered by them.

Chapter 2: Season 8[]

After the Mothership's downfall, the surviving Cubes that crashed on the Island began invading Reality Zero, opening portals leading to The Sideways. The Sideways monsters emerged from this dimension, along with the Cube Assassins themselves whose mission is to protect the cubes and to destroy Reality Zero.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • While initially believed to be a single Character, Horde Rush 2024 Quests revealed that there were in fact several Cube Assassins.
    • However, this was already implied before as Cube Assassin Boss could spawn in several Sideways Anomalies during Chapter 2: Season 8.
