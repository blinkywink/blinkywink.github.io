Overview

This article is about the Outfit. For the Emote of the same name, see Snap.

“
All assembly required.
”
— In-Game Description

Snap is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by spending 9 Battle Stars on Page 1 in the Chapter 3: Season 3 Battle Pass. Snap is part of the Spare Parts Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Pop It implies that Snap steals parts from other characters inside the loop.
  • The Match Up Loading Screen shows parts that Snap doesn't have in-game, which are likely early or scrapped designs. They are:
    • An early Reject torso.
    • A yellow Brute right arm.
    • Gold Low Key legs.
    • A purple Ripped torso.
    • A green Tenta-Classic left arm.
    • Turquoise Bonewalker legs; could be an early design for it.
    • A pink Frenzy torso.
    • An early Skelly right arm.
    • A yellow Trashblaster left arm.
    • Green Bonewalker legs.
  • Snap was going to have a baby doll head part.[1]
