Haylee Skye is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 3 of the Chapter 6: Season 3 Battle Pass. Haylee Skye is part of the Pep Monster Set.

Haylee Skye is a superhero, a member of The Supernovas on Supernova's Formation Theta and a graduated student from the Supernova Academy.

Haylee is a supporting character of Chapter 6: Season 3.

Personality

Personality[]

Haylee is an extroverted, airheaded and overly confident but also kind hearted and caring cheerleader who usually solves problem with brute forces.

She's known to drink coffee with poached eggs in it.
