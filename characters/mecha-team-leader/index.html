Mecha Team Leader is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,600 V-Bucks or with the Mecha Team Leader Bundle for 2,300 V-Bucks. Mecha Team Leader was first released in Season 9 and is part of the Final Showdown Set.

Mecha Team Leader can also be obtained by purchasing the Mecha Team Leader 77078 LEGO® Fortnite® set.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Mecha Team Leader's appearances?
Mecha Team Leader has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.

The Mecha Team Leader, later rebuilt as the Mecha Strike Commander was a large robot constructed at the Pressure Plant during late Season 9 by Singularity and piloted by The Paradigm.

It was constructed to defeat the Devourer in Season 9, after Paradigm volunteered to steer it in order to stop the Imagined Order from destroying Reality Zero with a Doomsday Device.

Mecha Team Leader was critically damaged during The Final Showdown, but still was able to kill The Devourer by absorbing The Zero Point's energy and then dealing a final blow to his head with the Neo Tilted statue, which turned out to be a massive sword.

History

History[]

Season 9[]

With The Devourer free, the Imagined Order worry that it might harm The Zero Point, thus they attempt to build a Doomsday Device to destroy everything in The Loop. The Paradigm, at the time locked up in an IO prison, instead volunteers to pilot a Mech to kill The Devourer. Only a few days after Pressure Plant received an upgrade, it was revealed that The Government had started their work on the Mecha. Another part of the Mecha was attached every two days.

The Final Showdown[]

The Devourer rises from the ocean and begins heading towards The Vault. Mecha Team Leader leaves Pressure Plant and attacks the monster, getting the two in a fight. The Devourer rips out Mecha's arm and heavily damages it, but the mech breaks into The Vault and pulls The Zero Point out. It goes for Singularity's statue and pulls it, revealing a giant sword underneath. The Devourer attacks back but the Mecha strikes the sword in his head, killing the monster with no sweat. It flies off into The Ice Moon, leaving the Island for a long while.

Post-Final Showdown[]

The Looped variant of Mecha Team Leader, who would gain their own consciousness, assuming they weren't already an existing being from The Omniverse, would arrive on the Island.

Fortnite X Marvel: Zero War #1-4[]

The Visitor comes up with a plan to use the Mecha against the Imagined Order, thus The Seven steal a shuttle from the IO and use it to get to The Ice Moon. Storm and Thor tame the weather as The Foundation, Iron Man and The Scientist look for the Mecha. They find it lying on the ice, with The Paradigm still inside. The Paradigm explains to The Seven why she helped the IO and they work together to rebuild the Mecha, but are missing a power source for it. Spider-Man steals The Zero Shard from Doctor Doom and bring it to The Paradigm, powering the Mecha. The Mecha attacks The Ice King's castle and forces Doom to retreat.
