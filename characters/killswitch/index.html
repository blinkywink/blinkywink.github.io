Killswitch is a superhero, a member of The Supernovas on Supernova's Formation Tau and a graduated student from the Supernova Academy.

He is a supporting character of Chapter 6: Season 3 and Chapter 6: Season 4.

Personality

Personality[]

Killswitch is a hardworking student at the Supernova Academy who aspires to become a hero, with Batman serving as one of his greatest role models. Seeing the Academy as everything he had trained his entire life for, he constantly pushes himself to improve and is known for training harder than anyone else.

Despite his disciplined nature, Killswitch is also highly extroverted and supportive of his teammates. He is always willing to encourage and help others, although he occasionally finds Ziggy frustrating due to his tendency to slack off.

Following Mask Maker Daigo's attack and the fall of the Academy, Killswitch blamed himself for the disaster and struggled with guilt over the failure to protect it. However, with the support of his team and mentors, he eventually regained his confidence and found the strength to move forward.
