Kit was a special Boss Character hybrid introduced during Chapter 2: Season 3. He could have been found in Catty Corner after Update v32.11.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Kit dropped the following items when knocked. He also dropped a Healing Item.

Kit's Charge Shotgun Kit's Shockwave Launcher Catty Corner Keycard
Behavior

Behavior[]

Kit will attack the player if he notices them undisguised. Though if disguised, he can be talked to for additional dialogue.

He will alternate between using his Charge Shotgun in close range and his Shockwave Launcher in long range while in combat.

Shortly after getting knocked down, he will Self-Revive and join the team of the player who defeated him. Kit will replenish all of his health and act as a Supply Specialist Character, providing Boogie Bombs and Ammunition to allies.

Besides this, he will grant the team he is following access to his SHADOW Chests and neutrality from his henchmen and Sentry Cameras.

Kit, when recruited, may also request a High Five or Hug from the player he is following. If ignored, he will cancel the offer and turn away.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Kit's Boss music is a remix of the Chapter 2: Season 2 Story Trailer music. Along with the Patrol theme utilizing samples from The Hideout when choosing between an agent's GHOST and SHADOW Styles.
  • Kit's voicelines reprise his old Boss AI's voice from Chapter 2: Season 3. But with many new voicelines added with or replacing old ones, a new Full Alert Idle set, and simplified Idle voicelines.
  • Kit is the first and only Boss from Chapter 2: Season 3 to once again return as a Boss.
    • He is also the only Boss to not be based on an old Boss from Chapter 2: Season 2, though he could also be filling in for Meowscles as he was relocated to the Box Factory during the season.
  • If the player dismisses Kit, he will be eliminated instantly, as opposed to returning to his starting location like other Hireable Characters.
  • Along with other Remix bosses, Kit will not drop his items if eliminated by The Storm.
  • If the player that knocked Kit is eliminated before he Self-Revives, he will remain knocked and can be Revived manually by another player. However the popup that indicates the option will have no text.
  • Kit was the last new Boss of Chapter 5.

Behavior

Behavior[]

Kit would attack the player if He noticed them undisguised. Doing small visual reactions for getting suspicious and spotting a target. Unlike the other Bosses present, Kit would use his secondary Mythic item if his target gets too far from his Shotgun's range.

When knocked down, he can be shaken down to reveal nearby chests, his henchmen, Sentry Turrets and Sentry Cameras.

Kit is the son of Meowscles, nephew of Meow Skulls and Phantom Meowscles, and it is theorized that he is also the son of Lynx. He wanted to be like his father, and with the help of Lynx, he built a suit to be bigger. He built many robots recycling parts from cars.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 3[]

In the Nexus War: Thor comic, Kit is seen relaxing with Brite Bomber when Thor arrives to warn them of the arrival of Galactus. He scampers off and returns on his mech suit, and was among the group defending themselves against Thor before Sif teleports the other Marvel universe heroes to the Island.

Chapter 2: Season 5[]

Kit was a character that spawned at Catty Corner. He had special dialogue with Meowscles and Lynx, further adding to the theory that he has some sort of relationship with Lynx, possibly that he is her son.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

Kit is a character who may spawn at Scratch Pad. It's revealed in special dialogue and his Character Collection Book bio that he is trying to patch things up between Meowscles and Lynx.
