Fresh Aura is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Minty Legends Pack in the Item Shop for $19.99. Fresh Aura was first released in Chapter 2: Season 8 and is part of the Minty Legends Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Fresh Aura's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Players have the option to choose between both.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Fresh Aura's appearances?
Fresh Aura has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
