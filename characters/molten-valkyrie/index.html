Overview

“
A fiery revelation.
”
— In-Game Description

Molten Valkyrie is a Lava Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased with the Lava Legends Pack in the Item Shop for $18.49. Molten Valkyrie was first released in Season 8 and is part of the Lava Legends Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, Molten Valkyrie's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Signature
Base


Molten Valkyrie received a higher-detailed style in Update v28.30, which replaced the original one.
Players have the option to choose between both.

Section

Bean Style

Within Fall Guys experiences, Molten Valkyrie's appearance is changed to that of a matching Fall Guys Bean.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?
For varying reasons, Epic Games may decide to add or remove items from the Item Shop before the usual rotation occurs. The Fortnite Wiki treats each change to the Item Shop within a single day as a separate 'part' that makes up the day's Item Shop History.
Why are there two different numbers for Molten Valkyrie's appearances?
Molten Valkyrie has, on at least one occasion, appeared in more than one shop in a single day. This has resulted in its count for appearances in the Item Shop being higher than the amount of days it has appeared in it. Both counts are listed by the Fortnite Wiki in order to be as accurate as possible, unlike other sources.
