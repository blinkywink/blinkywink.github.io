Nisha is a member of The Society who was introduced in Chapter 5: Season 1. She is a fencer and the wealthy owner of the Fencing Fields, FlowBerry farm that she inherits from her family[7].

She was a major antagonist of Fortnite: Battle Royale, serving as one of the main antagonists of Chapter 5: Season 1. However she turned a supporting protagonist, serving as one of the supporting protagonist of Chapter 5: Season 4.

History

History[]

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

Nisha's family was one of the first to join The Society. After her parents' death, she and her elder brother, Kavel, became the new owners of Fencing Fields, an estate that had been in their family for generations, with Nisha taking her parents' place as a leader of The Society. She was tasked with holding Peely hostage for the time being,[11] while the other bosses tried to extract information on Pandora's Box. Later, The Looper and Jones raided Fencing Fields to rescue Peely.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

After Valeria opened Pandora's Box, Zeus and the Olympians arrived on the island to punish the mortals, with Zeus planning to eliminate them all, starting with those who originally caused the mess: The Society.[12] Nisha, Kavel, and the other Society members tried their best to fight the gods, but they were ultimately forced into hiding.

Chapter 5: Season 3[]

After the gods left the island, Nisha and Kavel emerged from hiding just as the Wasteland Warriors arrived on the island. They took over the sand biome and all surrounding areas, including Fencing Fields. Ringmaster Scarr then constructed the Nitrodrome where Fencing Fields once stood, leaving Nisha and Kavel homeless and poor. Hope encouraged Nisha to join The Underground.[13] Nisha considered joining them but didn't buy into Hope's vision of a perfect world.[14]

Chapter 5: Season 4[]

Both Nisha and Kavel reluctantly joined Hope and The Underground, becoming official members and participating in the fight against Doom.[1] [15]
