Outfit[]

“
Street warrior, sushi chef, and the coolest lizard in the Peace Syndicate.

Thunder is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all of the cosmetics on Page 5, and spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 4: Season 2 Battle Pass. Thunder is part of the Street Rep Set.

Trivia[]

Thunder is a Japanese lizard chef, the most-trusted lieutenant of the Peace Syndicate and the owner of the Gila sushi restaurant in Mega City. His family also owns a farm[4].

Personality

Personality[]

Thunder is a chef at heart. He loves to talk about food recipes, his family farm and business. As a lieutenant of the Peace Syndicate, he takes good care of his co-workers, especially Evie. He is also shown to be caring, as he made a new signature menu item just for The Looper at his restaurant as a thank for helping the Peace Syndicate.
