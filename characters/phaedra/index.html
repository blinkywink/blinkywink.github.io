Outfit[]

“
A dark sea witch with unknowable power.
”
— In-Game Description

Phaedra is a Crew Series Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by becoming a member of the Fortnite Crew during July 2022, as part of the July 2022 Fortnite Crew Pack. Phaedra was released in Chapter 3: Season 3 and is part of the Gothic Nautilus Set.
