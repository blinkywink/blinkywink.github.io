Overview

“
Sovereign of the shifting sands.
”
— In-Game Description

Xander is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained as a reward from the Refer a Friend Challenges. Xander was released in Chapter 3: Season 3 and is part of the Immortal Sands Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • On September 20th 2023, Xander (along with his matching cosmetics) was granted to players who suffered from issues with the Refer A Friend 2022 program, which had previously suffered from issues on the website and had ended a month earlier than intended.[1]
