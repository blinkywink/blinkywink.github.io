Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Solid Snake's default style and Solid Snake (Goggles) style are based off his appearance in Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty.
    • His beard is inaccurate, but is reminiscent of Naked Snake in Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater. The Disney leaks (which consequentially include Solid Snake for being released alongside Peter Griffin, despite not being under the same corporate umbrella) show that this is the result of the 2015 Figma of Solid Snake being used as a reference for the Outfit.
      • It is also reminiscent of his appearance in the Super Smash Bros. series.
    • The goggles are based off the ones he wore in the title screen of Metal Gear Solid.
  • Solid Snake's Old Snake style is based off his appearance in Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots.
