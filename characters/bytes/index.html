Bytes is a warrior of The Army of the Last Reality chosen by The Nothing for completing their Trials. His lack of fear and hunger for power turned him into a soulless but powerful warrior.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

Source Ability
The Nothing's Gift
The Nothing gave Bytes chromed hands, and as he completed his trials, The Nothing made him more powerful by allowing Bytes to modify his hands into new weapons, with each time a weapon granted by successful trial.


History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

After choosing Bytes to become their Warrior, The Nothing granted him chromed hands and waited until he came to contact them. Once Bytes was ready, he went to contact The Nothing by himself through a Chromed TV. However The Nothing wanted Bytes to prove himself first, making him undergo trials to prove his strength, while making him feel hollow and grow a hunger, making him gradually lose his identity, his knowledge, and the beings he once was. Although Bytes was scared at first, he continued until the end. At each trial, The Nothing rewarded him by giving him more powers. At the end of the trials, once Bytes became a completely new being, the Warrior of The Nothing, the later told him that his journey was only beginning but that he must stay vigilant, as The Nothing will summon him when needed.
