This article is about the Outfit. For the Loading Screen of the same name, see Undercover Skye (Loading Screen).

Undercover Skye is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained by purchasing all cosmetics on Page 4, then spending 9 Battle Stars in the Chapter 2 Remix Battle Pass. Undercover Skye is part of the Mighty Questie Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Undercover Skye is Skye disguised as Guff.
  • Undercover Skye is uniquely downscaled in the Lobby and Locker in order to look shorter.
  • Undercover Skye's Midnight edit style is mainly inspired from Skye's SHADOW Edit style.
    • This is likely because Skye originally chose to stay with GHOST, so the SHADOW-themed style further ties in to her trying to blend in and be undercover.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Undercover Skye wields a Legendary Assault Rifle, but will not drop it when eliminated.
    • This choice of weapon likely calls back to Skye's Assault Rifle. A Mythic Assault Weapon wielded by Skye in Chapter 2: Season 2.
  • Undercover Skye is the first Character in all of Battle Royale that can be knocked and shaken down.
    • The reason for this is due to Undercover Skye having coding that suggests she was originally gonna be part of the Boss lineup before being scrapped.
  • Though the option is hidden with no texts or popups, Undercover Skye can be revived and recruited to the player's team. Similarly to the Bosses present during Chapter 2 Remix if the player that defeated them has been eliminated before they could Self-Revive.
    • This makes Undercover Skye the only Recruitable Character in Chapter 2 Remix that isn't Slightly Taller.
    • Unlike the Bosses, Undercover Skye is not a Specialist of any kind and will simply just shoot at her target.
      • Undercover Skye will also not request for a Bear Hug or High Five from the player upon being revived.
  • Everytime Undercover Skye has moved, it has been to tease an upcoming addition of some kind.
    • Her starting location teased Spaghetti Grotto.
    • She then moved near Lockie's Lighthouse to tease Ice Isle.
    • She was moved once again near Rainbow Rentals to tease WRLD's Point and Juice WRLD's arrival.
    • Finally, she was moved near The Doggpound to tease Remix: The Finale.
