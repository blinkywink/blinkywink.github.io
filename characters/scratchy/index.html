Scratchy was a hostile Boss introduced during Chapter 6: Mini Season 2. He could have been found at various locations around Springfield Island after Update v38.10.

Boss Loot

Boss Loot[]

Scratchy dropped the following items when eliminated. He also dropped a Healing Item, 100 Bars, and Materials.

Cartoon Chainsaw Random Weapon
Behavior

Behavior[]

Chainsawwwwwwww!!![]

Scratchy has a chance to spawn at the start of the 1st, 2nd, or 3rd Storm circle at one of his locations but is not patrolling the area. Instead, a TV playing the intro of The Itchy & Scratchy Show will be present at wherever his icon is. Once interacted with or damaged, Scratchy will suddenly appear by jumping out of the screen (unless if the TV is destroyed in which he will spawn where the TV was). Chasing after the player while attacking them with his Cartoon Chainsaw.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Scratchy's codename is FruitFire_TroopFactor.
  • Scratchy's Boss music is taken from the Season 36 Episode Estranger Things.
  • Similar to the other Springfielder Bosses, an animated Nelson Muntz will briefly appear and laugh at Scratchy when he is defeated.
  • Scratchy's mechanics are very similar to Billy. Except he does not patrol around after being summoned and isn't aggro'd, only standing at where his spawn location is after coming out of the TV.
