Outfit[]

“
She sees visions of a long-gone world.
”
— In-Game Description

Tarana is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Level 29 of the Chapter 2: Season 6 Battle Pass. Tarana is part of the Bone Punk Set.
