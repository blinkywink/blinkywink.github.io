Outfit[]

“
Elite member of the 501st Legion.
”
— In-Game Description

501st Trooper is a Star Wars Series Outfit in Fortnite that can be purchased in the Item Shop for 1,200 V-Bucks, with the Clone Trooper Bundle for 1,600 V-Bucks or with 501st & 212th Battalion Trooper Pack for 800 V-Bucks. 501st Trooper was first released in Chapter 4: Season 2 and is part of The Prequel Trilogy Set.

Section

LEGO® Style

Within LEGO Fortnite Odyssey and other LEGO experiences, 501st Trooper's appearance is changed to that of a LEGO® minifigure.

Icon Featured

This LEGO® Style was introduced alongside LEGO Fortnite in Update v28.01. As a result, it was not visible on 501st Trooper's detail page in the Shop or Locker starting from Update v28.00, even though it was usable immediately upon the mode's release.

Why are there shops labelled as 'parts'?

501st Troopers were hostile Guards in Fortnite. They were introduced during Chapter 4: Season 2.

Description

Description[]

When Order 66 began, 501st Troopers replaced every single Clone Trooper Character around the island in all Clone Checkpoints and in various Named Locations. Attacking any player in sight.

Two 501st Troopers would always appear alongside Darth Vader at the Imperial camp within Frosty Firs.

They could have been knocked and revived by other 501st Troopers. Once all 501st Troopers in the area have been knocked, they are all eliminated, unless if Darth Vader is present.
