Outfit[]

“
Who needs nine lives when one is enough?
”

Lynx is a Legendary Outfit in Fortnite that could have been obtained immediately after purchasing the Season 7 Battle Pass. Lynx is part of the Lynx Set.

Lynx is a member of Midas' Crew[6] and a former Expedition member. She was stationed at Frosty Flights and is tracked by the Imagined Order, alongside Jules and Midas;[7] showing she could have a connection to them.

Powers and Abilities

Powers and Abilities[]

Source Ability
Nano Tech Combat Suit
A nano tech suit made for combat created by Lynx, the suit forms around her upon activation. As the suit is used in battle against firearms and in extreme cold conditions, it is supposed to be bulletproof and temperature resistant.

Timeline

Timeline[]

Season 7[]

She was seen working for the Expedition and is stationed at Frosty Flights. She draws sketches of characters and built herself an armor.

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

She is called into The Agency by Midas while running away from Sledges. She activates her suit and shoots the Sledges.

Chapter 2: Season 4[]

After Thor's arrival on the island, Lynx and a group of other Loopers attack him. As they are approaching him, Lady Sif sends a group of heroes and villains. While everyone is praparing to fight, Thor tells them to focus on the real threat: Galactus.[10]

Chapter 5: Season 1[]

Lynx decides to do research on Helios to see if its a place where she can make home.[11] She then rigs up an inter-reality tether back to Katt to bring her to Helios.[12] [13]

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Panther, who is a Remix of Lynx and an IO member could have been Lynx herself, as the pattern on Lynx's hoodie can be seen on Panther's pants.
  • Vix is also based on the NeonCat model with many similar colors and patterns, but there has been no confirmed connection between the two yet.
