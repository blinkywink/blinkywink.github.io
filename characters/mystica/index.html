Mystica is the founder of The Luminaries. They are an explorer, inventor and a valiant warrior.

Appearance

Appearance[]

Mystica seems to be a humanoid plant with starry and flaming head. They wear a hologram oni mask and a japanese-themed armor.

History

History[]

Not much is known about their past but they knew what The Last Reality was capable of and that no one should be working with them. They became the last survivor of their kind after an unknown cosmic event and somehow ended up in MEGA City. Once there, they decided to start a syndicate: The Luminaries with the goal not to conquer, but to protect their new home in MEGA City.

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

In Syndicate Quests, They met The Looper and called The Unseen fools for working with The Army of the Last Reality. They agreed to help the Looper.

During The Rumbles, They hired the Looper to help them with the investigations on the earthquakes and The Explorers' excavation camps.

Chapter 4: Season 3[]

They gave their approval for Trace's jungle exploration project.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • Their plant like appearance is similar to The Herald who is also a plant creature with flaming head.
  • Remnant Blossom is the last remaining flower from their home world.
  • Mystica's gender is often debated. They look rather feminine, but use Male animations and sounds in certain emotes. In addition, the News page shown when unlocking the outfit refers to them as "their", meaning they might be genderless.
  • Being an Oni from Reality: 783, Mystica may have been a member of the Demon Warriors in the past.
