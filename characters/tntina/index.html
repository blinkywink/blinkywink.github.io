“

TNTina is an Epic Outfit in Fortnite that could have been unlocked by reaching Level 40 of the Chapter 2: Season 2 Battle Pass. TNTina is part of the Short Fuse Set.

Trivia

Trivia[]

  • TNTina was in development since between Season 8 and Season X.[1]
  • TNTina's Cartoon styles were the first cel-shaded Outfits.
  • TNTina has a male counterpart named Splode.
  • TNTina's shirt print is used in the Boom Sniper Rifle.
    • During its introduction in Chapter 2: Season 5, it was sold by her male counterpart, Splode.
  • In Chapter 2: Season 6, TNTina's Propane Tanks could have been found at the **REDACTED** bunker along with a vault door with the words "BOOM" printed on it in the comical yellow shaded font.
  • During Chapter 4: Season 2, there was a visual bug with TNTina where part of her head model was slightly off from the rest of her body model. This was fixed in Update v24.30, though this bug later returned during Fortnitemares 2024 with Update v31.40 before being fixed again in Update v33.10.
  • Parts of TNTina's skin would occasionally clip through her shirt above her right hip when performing certain Emotes and actions such as aiming with the Chains of Hades while walking. This was fixed in Update v30.20.

Behavior[]

When knocked down, she can be shaken down to reveal nearby chests, her henchmen, Sentry Turrets and Sentry Cameras.

History

History[]

Chapter 2: Season 2[]

TNTina guards The Rig with Rig Henchmen as a neutral agent. She later joins SHADOW with her henchmen, in turn, also converting to SHADOW.

Chapter 2: Season 6[]

During the Zero Crisis Finale trailer, TNTina attempts to hit Jones with a Bomb.

Chapter 5: Season 2[]

Marigold suspects that Midas is somewhere on the island, so she calls TNTina and the rest of Midas' Crew back together. She would use explosions to to cause a distraction, allowing The Marigold to escape the Island and The Pantheon.

Chapter 2 Remix[]

TNTina becomes Dynamo TNTina, a SHADOW operative residing at The Rig. She was contracted by Snoop's Enterprise along with other Midas' Crew agents.
