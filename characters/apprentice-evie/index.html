Evie is a member of the Peace Syndicate. She is stated to be their secret weapon, and works under Thunder.

Personality

Personality[]

Evie is a mischievous and cheerful girl. She is mischievous to the point where the leader of the Peace Syndicate, X, has to put her on probation and command Thunder to always keep an eye on her.

History

History[]

Chapter 3: Season 2[]

As an unseen character, she aided The Resistance after the destruction of The Collider, and is known for helping people through the Peace Syndicate.

Chapter 3: Season 3[]

She is first seen alongside Malik while the two are amazed about the rollercoaster in Rave Cave. She stations in Syndicate Shoals and begins familiarizing herself with those on the island, even offering to recruit a handful.

When talking to Mancake, Meowscles or Stash'd as her, they are shown to have worked with the Syndicate.

Chapter 3: Season 4[]

Evie receives a message from the Syndicate's elusive leader, "X"; it praises her progress and states that the next phase of their plan is set to be in motion. Twyn is then sent to assist her.

Chapter 4: Season 1[]

Following the creation of the new island, Evie is now found at the Shoal Shack. She seems to have taken an interest in The Ageless' work, specifically his castle and his "research project", presumably the Rift Gate. Whether she investigates out of her own curiosity or for the Peace Syndicate is unclear.

She is sent another transmission from "X", warning her that there's no time for "Plan A" and gives her permission to improvise. She then passes the message onto Twyn.

Dialogue from towards the end of the season heavily imply that her and Scrapknight Jules were the culprits behind the destruction of the Rift Gate, with her words stating whoever blew it up "[maybe] had good reasons to do it."

Chapter 4: Season 2[]

After catching The Looper in the act of hacking into the Peace Syndicate's servers, she recruits them, and introduces them to her superior, Thunder.

She and Thunder later instruct The Looper through several missions to unite the syndicates and stop an elusive saboteur.

Galactic Battle[]

Being force-sensivive, Evie became a Jedi Padawan under an unknown Jedi master. However, another version of herself became a Sith Apprentice under Darth Sidious and would invade the Island alongside the Galactic Empire.
